# Linen Laundry and Weekly Exchange Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 16:08:05.408-04
- Solicitation Number: W912QG26QA012

## Summary

This is not an invitation to submit offers or quotes. The South Carolina Army National Guard anticipates issuing a solicitation directly to at least three known suppliers and sources for fiber installation supplies and kits using the procedures outlined in Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) FAR 12.201-1(c)(2). The intended set aside is a Total 100% small business set-aside. The intended NAICS is 812332, and the intended PSC is S209. Anticipated Award Date: 06/03/2026. Anticipated Award Procedures: In accordance with RFO FAR 12.201-1(c)(2), the South Carolina Army National Guard intends to solicit quotations directly from at least three known competing companies and make an award to the quoter that represents the best value to the government at a fair and reasonable price. If interested in being sent a copy of the solicitation, please send an email to LTC Kevin Esber, Contracting Officer, at kevin.j.esber.mil@army.mil. However, the Government reserves the right to limit the contractors solicited to no fewer than three; therefore, including a capability statement is highly encouraged. Requests to be included in the solicitation must be received by the Contracting Officer no later than Friday, April 10, 2026 at 12:00PM Eastern Daylight Time. Best Value is anticipated to be defined as: a. Technical: Ability to meet or exceed the PWS requirements. b. Past Performance: The Government will utilize various methods to determine past performance: Completed Past Performance Questionnaires, Contractor Performance Assessment Reporting System (CPARS), Federal Awardee Performance Integrity Information System (FAPIIS) and Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS). Additionally, this agency may also utilize the Contracting Officers or Units personal knowledge of any past contracts to evaluate the contractor's past performance to ensure the best value for the Government. c. Price. Purchase Description: Refer to attached PWS. This posting accomplishes the notice requirements required by RFO FAR 12.201-1(c)(2) and RFO FAR 5.101. The point of contact for this notice is: LTC Kevin Esber Kevin.j.esber.mil@army.mil

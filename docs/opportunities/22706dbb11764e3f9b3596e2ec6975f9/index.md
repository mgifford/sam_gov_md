# Mobile Medical-Grade Refrigerator and Freezer System

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:22:21.896-04
- Solicitation Number: 75A50326Q00005

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products and/or commercial services prepared in accordance with Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) Part 12 Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. The purpose of this requirement is to procure five hundred eighty-seven (587) portable, mobile, medical-grade refrigerator/freezer units capable of operating primarily on alternating current (AC) power (standard electrical outlets) and equipped with rechargeable batteries for backup and mobile operation. See attched solcitiation for details of the requirement, specifications, and submission instructions. This amendment removes the Uniformity language from the solicitation, post the Questions and Answers received and extends the submission due date to Friday, March 20, 2026. See attached RFQ 75A50326Q00005 Amendment 00001 and 75A50326Q00005-Questions and Answers for details.

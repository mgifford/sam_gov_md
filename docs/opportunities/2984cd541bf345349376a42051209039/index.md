# Repair Generator Time Codes for Sole Source Brandywine Communications

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 11:09:11.73-04
- Solicitation Number: N6523626Q9324

## Summary

Naval Information Warfare Center (NIWC) Atlantic intends to award a Firm Fixed Price purchase order to Brandywine Communications 1513 E McFadden Ave., Santa Ana, CA 92705. The Government has a requirement for P/N: 001100X12 (Qty: 33); the repair Generator Time Codes for various Serial Numbers. The applicable NAICS code is 811210, PSC 5975. This action is conducted under the authority of FAR 13.106(b)(l)(i), soliciting from a single source under the Simplified Acquisition Threshold (SAT).This is a notice of intent and is not a request for competitive quotes. Interested firms must identify their interest and capability to provide the services identified no later than three (3) days after this notice posts. A solicitation will not be posted. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed order based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Information received will normally be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. Responses to this announcement may be submitted to Tenisha.b.hampton.civ@us.navy.mil.

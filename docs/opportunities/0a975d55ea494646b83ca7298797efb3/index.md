# HAFB & FAM Camp Grounds & Snow Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 14:41:21.817-04
- Solicitation Number: FA2835-26-1697

## Summary

This requirement is for services for grounds and snow removal maintenance on both Hanscom AFB and FAM CAMP. The contractor will provide all management, tools, supplies, vehicles, equipment, and labor to perform grounds maintenance and snow removal. The objective is to ensure safe access to facilities for base personnel and occupants in accordance with the Performance Work Statement (PWS). This contract is for a base period and four option years. All questions are due on or before 11 AM EST, Monday 27 April 2026. Answers will be posted to SAM.gov not later than 2:00 PM EST, Friday 1 May 2026. Offers are due on or before 12:00 PM EST, Wednesday 13 May 2026. Offers shall be valid for a period no less than 90 days. Please request read receipt on all submitted quotes.

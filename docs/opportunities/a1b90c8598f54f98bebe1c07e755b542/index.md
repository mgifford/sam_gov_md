Naval Surface Warfare Center Indian Head Division (NSWC, IHD), intends to award a firm fixed price contract on a noncompetitive basis to Thermo Electron North America, 1400 Northpoint Pkwy - Suite 10, West Palm Beach, FL 33407-1976 to provide the following service: Unity Lab Maintenance Service Plan This action is to be undertaken in accordance with FAR 13.106 (b) Soliciting from a single source, on the basis that the vendor is the only known source capable of meeting the government’s requirements.

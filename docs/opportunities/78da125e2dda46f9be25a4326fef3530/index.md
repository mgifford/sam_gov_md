# 61--Hoover Dam emergency Generator Maintenance and Rep

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 13:29:22.851-04
- Solicitation Number: 140R3026Q0002

## Summary

The basis for this cancellation is due to the following: Vague technical descriptions that prevented accurate pricing and technical planning. Inconsistent terminology across sections, resulting in conflicting interpretations. Insufficient detail regarding government-furnished equipment and expectations for emergency response timelines. To preserve the integrity of the procurement process and ensure all potential offerors have a clear and fair opportunity to compete, the Government is cancelling the solicitation in its entirety. A revised and corrected solicitation will be issued once requirements have been clarified and documentation properly updated.

## Summary

Specifications cover the production of 560,000 self-mailers, Quality Level 1, self-mailer packages requiring such operations as electronic prepress, proofing, printing, and delivery. All parts print 4CP plus 2 Pantone colors and coatings. Procurement for dated material. Electronic, content, high resolution proofs and stock sample are required. Delivery is f.o.b. Destination.

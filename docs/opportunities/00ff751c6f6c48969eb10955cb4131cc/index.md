# AGE Jack Trailer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 13:55:52.426-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1015

## Summary

The government requires a 4 Degree Trailer Company P/N 12600 (or equivalent) wing jack trailer to support Aerospace Ground Equipment (AGE) operations by providing a safe, efficient, and expedited method for loading, unloading, and transporting up to four 30-ton wing jacks or other related equipment IAW with attached Salient Characteristics.

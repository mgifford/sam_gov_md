# LOGCAP Industry Day Dallas

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

USG Update - LOGCAP Industry Day 02-04 March 2026: The General Session is a 1-day, 3hr event scheduled for Monday, March 2nd, from 0800 to 1100 CST. This event is intended to encourage meaningful group discussion on various LOGCAP VI acquisition strategy topics and the draft Performance Work Statement (PWS). There are not any additional General Session events scheduled thereafter. The maximum number of participants for each company remains unchanged at 3 and is limited to those who have already registered. There will not be any further allowable changes to attendees for the event. The remaining time on Monday, March 2nd, through Wednesday, March 4th, is reserved exclusively for the prescheduled 1 on 1 meetings. We appreciate your understanding and look forward to your participation in the General Session. We are providing the slide deck in advance of the industry engagement to prepare everyone for the General Session discussion, as well as the 1 on 1 topics. For those of you who did not receive a 1 on 1 invitation, we strongly encourage you to engage during the General Session. NOTE: Registration for this event has closed and no more registrations are being accepted. This update is for those that have a confirmed registration to attend the event. Please see the attachments portion of this posting for the slide deck, 1 on 1 discussion topics, and draft LOGCAP VI PWS.

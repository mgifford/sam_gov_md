# Intermediate Medical Acquisition Course (IMAC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 13:25:57.691-04
- Solicitation Number: HT942626RE011

## Summary

This requirement is for non-personal services to provide a Defense Acquisition University (DAU) Certified Intermediate Medical Acquisition Course (IMAC), as well as a truncated Sr. Leader (non-DAU) IMAC course. These courses are specialized training courses focused on acquisition in FDA-regulated medical product development. IMAC supports the Defense Health Agency (DHA) mission by equipping acquisition personnel with advanced knowledge beyond general DAU acquisition courses.

# Laboratory Services for Dredged Material Analysis, Blanket Purchase Agreement, US Army Corps of Engineers, New England District

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 15:10:43.542-05
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA058

## Summary

THE WORK CONSISTS OF: The Contractor shall furnish all labor, equipment, and supplies necessary to provide Chemical Analysis of environmental samples for dredged materials as needed for each BPA call under the agreement. The period of performance of the resulting agreement will be 36 months from the date of award. The BPA Master Limit is $350,000; where no single BPA Call may exceed $25,000 in total cost. In accordance with FAR 12.201-1(e)(3), the Government is obligated only to the extent of authorized purchases actually made under the BPA. THE GOVERNMENT INTENDS TO AWARD UP TO THREE (3) BLANKET PURCHASE AGREEMENTS FROM THIS ONE (1) SOLICITATION. The applicable NAICS code is 541380 with a Small Business Size Standard of $19 Million. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the New England District. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

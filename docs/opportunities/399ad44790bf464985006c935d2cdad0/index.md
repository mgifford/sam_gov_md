# 10--Rear Sight

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 10:42:36.014-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE7LX-26-R-0062

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) will issue a Request for Proposal on or about Septermber 8, 2022 at https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil/. The proposed contract will be solicited as 100% Small Business Set Aside. This is not a request for quote or proposal. The purpose of this prelisolicitation notice is to provide prospective vendors the opportunity to review the NSN: 1005-01-484-8000. The NSN listed on the request for proposal will have a contract minimum and maximum that is set at the NSN level. This will be used to determine if cost or pricing data would be required with any proposals, and if none of the exceptions to cost or pricing data were found. An indefinite quantity contract with one base year and four possible option years is anticipated. The total duration, base period plus option years shall not exceed 5 years. The solicitation for 1 NSN, determined as commercial of a type under FAR 2.101 (3) (i), will be solicited using FAR Part 12 Acquisition of Commercial Items. Items are all for DLA Direct (Stock) requirements. When solicited, the NSN will have quantity breaks. The solicitation will specify Inspection/Acceptance at Origin or Destination and whether Military Packaging or commercial packaging applies. Government required delivery will be annotated on the solicitation. While price may be a significant factor in the evaluation of offers, the final decision will be based on a combination of price, past performance, delivery and any other factors listed in the solicitation. If NSNs are a drawing, the drawings will be located on the cFolders once the solicitation is released. Drawings can be downloaded by authorized users from the cFolders website via the Internet at https://pcf1.bsm.dla.mil/cfolders. Problems with cFolders can be addressed by the cFolders Help Desk at (855) 352-0001 and/or via email at DSCC.ProdDataCustSvc@dla.mil. Do not call the buyer for Drawing Item Issues. The solicitation will be made available on the internet at https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil under Requests for Proposal (RFP) SPE7LX- 26-R-0062 on or around the solicitation date of April 29 2026. The solicitation will remain open for 30 days, allowing time for potential offerors to review the solicitation and related attachments and if applicable, submit proposals.

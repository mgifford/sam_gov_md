# Medical Detention Facility Services (South Carolina)

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-14 10:41:42.168-04
- Solicitation Number: PODMedicalDetentionFacility2026
- Awardee: RECOVERY SOLUTIONS OF SOUTH CAROLINA, LLC Nashville TN 37211 USA
- Award Amount: 18693720.93

## Summary

This sole source and fixed price contract action was to acquire medical detention services for individuals charged with federal offenses and detained while awaiting trial or sentencing, a hearing on immigration status, or deportation. The secured medical detention facility was a contractor-owned/contractor-operated detention facility with a capacity to accommodate 40 detainees at a single site to include special housing units. The requirement included secure transportation and guard services. The facility was located within the geographic boundaries of the primary district of South Carolina.

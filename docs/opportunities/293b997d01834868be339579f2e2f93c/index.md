# 4th Annual Buy Indian Industry Day & Matchmaking Event

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 19:02:58.633-04

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), in collaboration with the U.S. Department of the Interior (DOI) and APEX Accelerators, is hosting the 4th Annual Buy Indian Industry Day & Matchmaking event on April 27–28, 2026, at the OKANA Conference Center in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. This event will bring together federal acquisition professionals, Tribal enterprises, industry partners, and key stakeholders to strengthen engagement and advance implementation of the Buy Indian Act. The forum will focus on expanding opportunities within the small business industrial base and supporting economic growth across Tribal communities. Overview of the Event Day 1–2: April 27–28, 2026 – Conference & Matchmaking Participants will: Engage with federal agencies and Tribal organizations on acquisition priorities and partnership opportunities Participate in matchmaking sessions to foster connections between government and industry Explore strategies to strengthen Tribal economies through federal procurement Gain insight into small business opportunities aligned with mission needs Registration Registration for both the conference and matchmaking sessions is required and can be completed at: https://mbmgo.com/event/hhsbuyindianindustry Hotel Accommodations Attendees may reserve hotel accommodations at the event venue using the link below. Additional Information This event serves as a key platform to promote collaboration across government and industry while advancing procurement strategies that support Tribal businesses and economic resilience. DECLARATION THIS ANNOUNCEMENT IS FOR INFORMATIONAL AND OUTREACH PURPOSES ONLY. IT DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A SOLICITATION, REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL, OR COMMITMENT BY THE GOVERNMENT.

# Maintenance and Repair of Dining Facility (DFAC) and Mensa Kitchen Equipment at Caserma Ederle, at Del Din DFAC, and Italian Mensa at Livorno Camp Darby, Italy.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 05:06:45.15-04
- Solicitation Number: W912PF26QA024

## Summary

*SEE AMENDMENT #1 and updated Technical Exhibit 1_Inventory* This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number W912PF26QA024 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Maintenance and Repair of Dining Facility (DFAC) and Mensa Kitchen Equipment at Caserma Ederle, at Del Din DFAC, and Italian Mensa at Livorno Camp Darby, Italy. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. (ii) The solicitation includes 021 CLINS. There will be one (1) twelve-month base period and 2 option periods. A list of line-item number(s) and items, quantities, and units of measure (including option(s) is included in the attached solicitation (SF1449). (iii) The Government anticipates the award being a Hybrid contract with Firm Fixed Price and Time-and-Material (T&M) CLINs, to provide Maintenance and Repair of Dining Facility (DFAC) and Mensa Kitchen Equipment at Caserma Ederle, at Del Din DFAC, and Italian Mensa at Livorno Camp Darby, Italy. (iv) The base contract’s Period of Performance is estimated to begin 18 Jun 2026, with an end date of 17 Jun 2029. Place of Performance will be Caserma Ederle and Del Din, Vicenza and Camp Darby, Livorno, Italy (v) A list of solicitation provisions that apply to this acquisition is included in the attached solicitation (SF1449) (vi) A list of contract clauses that apply to the acquisition is included in the attached solicitation (SF1449) (vii) QUOTE DUE: Transmission of quotes in response to this RFQ, shall be submitted as an electronic version via email not later than 11:00 AM Central European Time on 20 April 2026 to the following email addresses: FRANCESCA.CANDIA.LN@ARMY.MIL and ANDREA.MORSOLETTO2.LN@ARMY.MIL. (viii) QUESTIONS: All questions concerning the RFQ shall be submitted via email to the following addresses FRANCESCA.CANDIA.LN@ARMY.MIL and ANDREA.MORSOLETTO2.LN@ARMY.MIL. no later than 03:00 PM Central European Time (CET) on 27 March 2026. Answers to questions will be posted to www.sam.gov in an amendment to the solicitation as needed.

## Summary

36C10D26Q0054 Page 1 of Page 61 of 61 Page 1 of This pre-solicitation is posted to contract opportunities as notification that the government intends to issue a sole-source firm-fixed price (FFP) contract to original equipment manufacturer (OEM) Delta T., LLC (dba Big Ass Fans) as allowed per RFO 6.103-1 Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. The OEM has issued replacements for warranted products (fan motors) for end-items installed at the St. Petersburg FL Veterans Affairs Regional Office (VARO). The requirement is to remove and replace warranted items (three (3) motors) such that they too will be covered by the OEM warrantee. Per the OEM point of contact, having validated the warrantee claim, an OEM project manager (PM) will be assigned to review availability of vendors with technicians certified and authorized to complete warrantee services within the St. Petersburg service area, determine which vendor will complete the work, and will then direct that vendor to contact the VARO POC to schedule the work. This is a pre-solicitation notice of the government s intent to sole-source this requirement. As such, no quotes or inquiries will be accepted, reviewed, or receive a reply. No award determination will be made based upon any communications received in association with this pre-solicitation notice.

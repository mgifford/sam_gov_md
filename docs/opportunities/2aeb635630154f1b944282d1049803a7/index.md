Introduction: The U.S. Army Medical Research Institute of Chemical Defense (USAMRICD) is seeking information on the availability of a commercial, off-the-shelf High-Density Microelectrode Array (MEA) system, along with a compatible training, warranty, and shipping package. The system is required for detecting, monitoring, and analyzing intracellular electrochemical activity and membrane potentiation in various biological models. Background: USAMRICD requires a complete system that enables high-resolution electrophysiological recordings from 3D biological models and excised tissues. This system is critical for research involving the functional imaging of neuronal activity and understanding complex cellular interactions under controlled environmental conditions. Requested Information / Minimum Essential Characteristics: We are seeking information on systems that meet the following minimum characteristics. Please describe your company's ability to provide a system that includes the following components and features: High-Density Microelectrode Array (MEA) System (Quantity: 1) Primary Function: Capable of detecting, monitoring, and analyzing intracellular electrochemical activity and membrane potentiation. Performance: * Electrode density greater than 1,000 electrodes. * Simultaneous recording, readout, and stimulation capabilities. * Minimum sampling frequency of 10 kHz per electrode. * Integrated stimulation from at least two (2) separate programmable channels. Compatibility: * Compatible with 3D tissue penetration technology (e.g., micropillars) for recording activity within spheroids, organoids, and tissue slices at depths greater than a single layer. * Suitable for use with excised tissue slices, including brain, heart, and skeletal muscle. Environmental Control: * Includes an associated mini-incubator or housing with active heating and cooling to maintain a stable environment of 37°C with 5% CO2 for long-term recording sessions. Compatible Sample Holders and Plates Sample Holders (Quantity: 1): A set of holders with various net sizes, including 1000/60, 1000/90, 300/60, and 300/90, to accommodate different test objects. Single-Well Microchip Plates (Quantity: 5): Plates incorporating 3D tissue technology with a well size of at least 25mm in diameter. Integration Components (Quantity: 1): All necessary components to integrate the sample holders, nets, and plates with the primary MEA device. Software and Workstation (Quantity: 1) Software: * Must be capable of sorting and analyzing spikes from all electrodes without requiring external support or additional programming requirements (e.g., Python). * Includes a perpetual license that is compatible with future software versions. Workstation: * A high-performance workstation operating on Windows 11. * Includes a 4TB Solid State Drive (SSD) and a GDDR5 graphics card. * TAA compliance is preferred. Monitor: A high-resolution monitor (>2000x1000) suitable for viewing imaging data. Interface: All necessary interfaces for the complete integration of MEA components, system, and software. Services and Delivery Training: A comprehensive training session on system function and use to be conducted no later than thirty (30) days after USAMRICD confirms receipt of all items. Training may be performed remotely or on-site. Delivery: All items must be delivered within 12 weeks post-award. Extended Warranty Please provide information on availability extended warranties.

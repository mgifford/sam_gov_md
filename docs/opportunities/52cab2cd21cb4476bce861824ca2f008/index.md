# Aircraft Maintenance Hangar at CSL Comalapa, El Salvador

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 21:46:19.648-04
- Solicitation Number: W9127826RA009

## Summary

See Advance Notice in PIEE for further details. Construct maintenance hangar at CSL Comalapa, El Salvador. The project will include construction of a single bay 2800 m2 aircraft maintenance hangar capable of supporting C-130 or P-8 aircraft. The hangar will include both a small admin space and material and tool storage spaces. Additional parking apron space is included to accommodate temporary parking of aircraft awaiting maintenance and provide swing space for the existing apron.

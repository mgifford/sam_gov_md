# Industry Day - 17th Contracting Squadron, Goodfellow AFB

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:35:11.143-04

## Summary

The 17th Contracting Squadron at Goodfellow AFB will host an Industry Day event to provide industry partners with insights into current and upcoming mission requirements. The Government will provide an overview of its programs, including technical specifications, anticipated solicitation requirements, and projected acquisition schedules. This event serves as a forum for industry partners to ask questions, provide feedback, and assist the Government in conducting market research to better understand current market capabilities. ? Event Details Date: Wednesday, May 13, 2026 Time: 0830 – 1530 CST Location: Powell Event Center, Goodfellow AFB 261 Scherz Blvd, Bldg 723, TX 76908 Format: In-Person Only. Briefings and materials will not be available virtually. ?? Registration & Deadlines Due to seating capacity limits, registration is restricted and will be processed on a first-come, first-served basis. Early registration is strongly encouraged. Registration Deadline: Monday, May 4, 2026, by COB (1700 CST). Late Policy: Failure to register by the deadline will result in incomplete registration; companies not registered by this date will be unable to participate. Registration form: https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/Ng69dehNf6?origin=lprLink ? Industry Capability Briefings (Networking) The Government will allot time for companies to reserve tables to network with Government representatives and other industry partners. If your organization is interested in reserving a table for these capability exchanges, please indicate your request during the registration process. ? Important Notices Updates: Additional information and registration links will be posted as subsequent modifications to this notice on https://sam.gov/. Cancellation Policy: The Government reserves the right to cancel this event up to five (5) business days prior to the scheduled date. The Government is not liable for, and will not reimburse, any travel or administrative costs incurred by participants.

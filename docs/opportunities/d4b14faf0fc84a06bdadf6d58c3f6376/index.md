# St. Louis District US Army Corps of Engineers 2026 Small Business Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-11 11:45:42.771-04

## Summary

Small businesses are invited to attend the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, St. Louis District Small Business Industry Day on April 16, from 2 to 5 p.m. at the Soldiers Memorial Military Museum in downtown St. Louis. This event offers a valuable opportunity to connect with USACE representatives, learn about upcoming procurement opportunities, and gain insight into how to do business with the agency. The program will feature presentations from the St. Louis District, Memphis District Corps of Engineers, U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Rock Island District, and U.S. Army Corps of Engineers St. Paul District, along with the U.S. Small Business Administration and APEX Accelerators. Attendees will also hear from a leadership panel and have time to network with key personnel and fellow small business owners.

# C-5 Ladder Actuator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 10:30:05.598-04
- Solicitation Number: SPRWA126R0011

## Summary

Solicitation This action is to establish a contract for the acquisition of the C-5 Crew Ladder Actuator, NSN: 1650-01-075-1690, P/N: 894244-02 The DLA/AWB Contracting Office intends to award a single definitive contract for the manufacture of the actuating linear cylinder assembly that contains a piston. Item is used on the C-5 crew entry door ladder. The quantity is 26. This sole source effort will be IAW FAR 6.302-1, Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements, under which the Government intends to solicit and negotiate only with the original equipment manufacturer for this item, Sargent Aerospace & Defense, LLC. (CAGE 78062). which maintains its configuration by part number and software version. The Government does not own any data, drawings, schematics, or any other releasable technical data in support of this requirement.

# Amendment 1 - Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 02:52:29.546-04
- Solicitation Number: FA560626QA015

## Summary

Amendment 1 - Solicitation has been amended to extend the due date for quotations to 13 May 2026, 1500 CEST. Amendment 2 will follow shortly with responses to questions submitted. This requirement is to establish a 5-year service contract with one base year and four option years to provide a comprehensive maintenance and repair program for all mission-essential Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems and associated battery strings supporting Spangdahlem Air Base. All UPS systems are provided as an attachment along with the PWS for the requirement.

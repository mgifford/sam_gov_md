# NLETS Secure Access

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-01 14:56:07.448-04
- Solicitation Number: DJA-26-AOIO-PR-0253

## Summary

The Government intends to negotiate a sole source award with National Law-Enforcement Telecommunications System (NLETS) located at 1918 W. Whispering Wind Dr. in Phoenix, AZ 85085. NLETS is a ‘501(c) (3) not-for-profit corporation owned and operated by the 50 States, districts and territories known as the principal membership and is administered by a Board of Directors elected from those state law-enforcement agencies. NLETS is the only vendor capable of satisfying this requirement. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS.

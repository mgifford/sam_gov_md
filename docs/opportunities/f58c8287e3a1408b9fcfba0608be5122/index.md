## Summary

PRESOLICITATION NOTICE (SYNOPSIS) The Indian Health Service, Navajo Area, Gallup Indian Medical Center, located at 1808 W Aztec Ave Gallup NM 87301 has an upcoming requirement for G180 Gomco OptiVac (brand name or equal) Portable Aspirators. The projected Period of performance will be 30 days from date of award. The North American Industry Classification System or NAICS code is 33912 – Medical Instrument Manufacturing with a small business size standard of 1,000. The Request for Quotations No. 75H71026Q00095 in accordance with 12.201-1 will be issued on or after April 7, 2026. Evaluation factors may include price and technical capability to meet the Government requirement. Quotes will be due on or by April 13, 2026. The acquisition is a 100% Indian Small Business Economic Enterprise (ISBEE) Set-Aside. In accordance with 52.204-7 the offeror shall have an active Federal Government contracts registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) when submitting an offer or quotation in response to this solicitation and at the time of award. For additional information, contact Shannon Seaboy, Shannon.Seaboy@ihs.gov. THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION.

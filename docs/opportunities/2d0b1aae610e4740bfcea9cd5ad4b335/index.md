The mission of the ENT department is to provide the best possible care with state of the art medical equipment. The current ENT microscopes have reached end of life and need to be replaced. 1.2 Objective The replacement microscopes need to be able to meet or exceed the specifications of the current Leica M400 and URBAN M703F. The contractor will need to deliver the equipment onsite and perform installation and training for staff. 1.3 Scope of Work The contractor shall provide all resources necessary to accomplish the tasks and deliverables described in this Statement of Work (SOW).

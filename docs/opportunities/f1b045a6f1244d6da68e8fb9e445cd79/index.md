# Pacific Amphibious Leaders Symposium (PALS) 2026 - Catering Opportunity

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 17:36:38.718-04
- Solicitation Number: M0031826Q0005

## Summary

The Marine Corps requires a catered networking event in conjunction with the Pacific Amphibious Leaders Symposium (PALS). The event will serve as an extension of the symposium itself and will require the deliverables of catered heavy hors d'oeuvres, alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages. The food will be served buffet style. The event will be at the Daniel K. Inouye Asia-Pacific Center for Security Studies (APCSS) on the 16th of June 2026 at 16:30 and will last for approximately 2 hours until 19:00. Buffet tables for food and high-top standing tables will be provided. Ice and simple sink facilities will also be available onsite.

# Multifunction Reference Thermometer Readout w/Temperature

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 15:19:54.111-04
- Solicitation Number: PANRSA-26-P-0000-033708

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command-Redstone, Operations, Services, & Training Division, plans to obtain information to serve as market research to help identify potential sources that can provide a brand name purchase of one (1) ADT286-TS-PKG-110V Multifunction Reference Thermometer Readout w/Temperature model or equivalent that will be a suitable replacement for the Hart Black Stack (20+years old) for Temperature Calibrations in comparison to Type-S thermocouples. This unit has a wide range of additional functions that will allow further use throughout the Temperature Laboratory. The ADT286-TS-PKG-110V Thermometer Readout w/Temperature shall include the following specifications or equivalent (if not brand name): Additel ADT286-TS-PKG—110V, Multifunction Reference Thermometer Qty: 1 Temperature Scanner Module 110 Voltage This RFI will utilize NAICS Code 334513.

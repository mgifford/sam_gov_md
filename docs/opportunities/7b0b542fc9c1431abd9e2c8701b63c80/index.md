# Small Business Event - GSA OSB - How to submit a GSA MAS Information Technology (IT) Offer - May 14th 2026 10:00AM PST

## Summary

Small Business Event - GSA OSB - How to submit a GSA MAS Information Technology (IT) OfferHow to submit a GSA MAS Information Technology (IT) offer - May 14th 2026 10:00AM PST Participants will learn the fundamentals of the GSA MAS program, including eligibility requirements, the structure of IT Schedule offerings, and how to navigate the application process. The course walks through key steps such as preparing a compliant offer, pricing strategies, required documentation, and understanding solicitation requirements. In addition, the training covers best practices for positioning IT services, maintaining compliance after award, and leveraging the GSA Schedule for long-term business growth. Attendees will also gain insights into common pitfalls, timelines, and strategies to improve approval success rates. By the end of the course, participants will have a clear roadmap for pursuing an IT GSA MAS contract and the tools needed to confidently begin the application process. Key Topics Include: Overview of the GSA MAS IT Schedule Eligibility and registration requirements (SAM.gov, NAICS codes) Preparing and submitting a GSA offer Pricing, labor categories, and compliance standards Post-award contract management and growth strategies Registration Link: https://gsa.zoomgov.com/webinar/register/WN_Sh46R3Q_R2Cgv_Hh895JQw#/registration

# Mowing, trimming, and general grounds maintenance at the Manchester Ranger District, Green Mountain National Forest, VT

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 15:45:49.879-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444326Q0014

## Summary

Description - The Manchester Ranger District requires groundskeeping services at two administrative sites and four recreation sites spread across the district in Rutland, Bennington and Windham counties. Lawns/open areas, roadway sides, campground sites, and designated surrounding areas shall be mowed or otherwise maintained as open, cleared spaces beginning in late April through mid-October. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12444326Q0014 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for GMF Manchester Administrative and Recreational Site Mowing. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 561730 The small business size standard is $9.5. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. SEE AMENDMENT 0001 ATTACHED.

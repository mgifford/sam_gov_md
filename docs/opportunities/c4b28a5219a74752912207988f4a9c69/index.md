# RFP 462624 - Power Control & Regulating System for AGS Motor Generator Set (Cycloconverter)

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 12:14:21.143-04
- Solicitation Number: 462624

## Summary

Brookhaven Science Associates, LLC (BSA), under a Prime Contract for the operation of Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL) with the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE), herewith solicits your proposal to furnish a Cycloconverter – Power Control and Regulating System for the AGS Motor Generator Set. This procurement is solicited as Full and Open Competition under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 335999 All Other Miscellaneous Electrical Equipment and Component Manufacturing. The business size standard for NAICS 335999 is 600 employees. BSA invites you to submit a proposal in accordance with the RFP letter and all supporting enclosures. Amendment 1 to RFP 462624: (1) provides all potential offerors with answers to questions received in response to the solicitation, (2) provides a Word version of Enclosure 1, Attachments D & E (see attached documents posted in this notice), and (3) extends the due date for proposals to May 8, 2026 (see attached revised RFP Letter posted in this notice).

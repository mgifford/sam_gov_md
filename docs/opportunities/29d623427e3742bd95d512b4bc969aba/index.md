# JBR050 Aircraft Maintenance Hangar

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 11:09:06.88-04
- Solicitation Number: W911KB
- Awardee: UNIT COMPANY Anchorage AK 99501 USA

## Summary

JBR050 is to construct a 52,047 SF Aircraft Maintenance Hangar and Aviation Operations Facility that provides consolidated multipurpose space for maintenance, repair, and major overhaul of miltary aircraft and may include maintenance bays, tech supply, production control, and quality control areas directly related to the maintenance and supervision of aircraft, component and assembly rebuilding, and quality control of aviation maintenance for AKARNG.

# Scalable Cloud-Based Lawful Intercept Platform

## Summary

Amendment 0001 Attached for review and updated information. If you require access to the controlled doucments, you must acknowledge the Controlled Information Notice below: Certain attachments associated with this Request for Information (RFI) contain Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) / Law Enforcement Sensitive information and are being provided solely for the purpose of supporting industry market research and response development. By requesting or accessing these documents, interested parties acknowledge that they will: • Use the information solely for the purpose of responding to this RFI • Safeguard the information from unauthorized disclosure • Not distribute, release, reproduce, or disclose the information outside their organization without prior written approval from the Government • Ensure any internal personnel accessing the information understand these restrictions Vendors requesting access to the controlled documents must acknowledge acceptance of these conditions in their request. Submission of an RFI response constitutes acknowledgement of these restrictions and agreement to protect the information accordingly. The Government is seeking information from industry on a scalable, cloud-based lawful intercept platform. This RFI is issued solely for market research and planning purposes. The goal is to modernize electronic surveillance capabilities with a secure, FedRAMP High cloud solution that can support domestic and international operations. The envisioned platform will enable authorized interception, processing, and analysis of communications data under lawful court orders, while adhering to stringent legal, privacy, and security requirements. Interested parties are asked to provide information on technical capabilities, innovative features, and approaches to meeting the requirements described below, in a vendor-agnostic manner that leverages current and emerging technologies.

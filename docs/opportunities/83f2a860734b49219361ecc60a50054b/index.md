# Executive Ride Program (FTC)

- Agency: FEDERAL TRADE COMMISSION
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 14:41:35.711-04
- Solicitation Number: 20251114

## Summary

The Federal Trade Commission has a need for the services of a qualified contractor to provide continuous weekday daily executive transport services in and around the Washington, DC metropolitan area. Most travel will originate from or return to 600 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW, Washington DC 20580, but this is not an exclusive starting point or destination. The contractor shall supply a six (6) passenger Sport Utility Vehicle, with driver, that is no more than five (5) years old.

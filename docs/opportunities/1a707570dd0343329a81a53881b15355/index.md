# RFI-Soda Ash Dispenser

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-19 13:57:51.882-04

## Summary

The U.S. Government is conducting market research to identify potential suppliers for two (2) volumetric dry screw feeder systems for the Shaw AFB facility. These systems are intended to replace existing soda ash mixers at our Well 4 and Well 6 locations and are critical for our water treatment operations. This procurement is being solicited on a Brand Name or Equal basis under NAICS 333914. The feeder system must be compatible with our existing USGI Chemical Feed equipment. Please note that this inquiry is for market research purposes only; no contract will be awarded based on this request. If your company can provide a system that meets these requirements, could you please provide the following information? Business Capability Statement GSA Contract Number CAGE Code Current SAM.gov Registration Status Point of Contact (POC) Information Please send your response no later than 10:00 AM EST on Thursday, March 26, 2026. Thank you for your time and consideration.

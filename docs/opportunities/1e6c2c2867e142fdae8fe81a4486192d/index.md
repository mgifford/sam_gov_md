## Summary

This is an Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to provide roof repair and replacement and various types of maintenance repairs and preparations associated with roof repair and replacement. Roof repair, replacement, and maintenance work will be performed at the three (3) Department of the Navy Sites, listed below: Philadelphia Naval Business Center (PNBC), 4921 South Broad St, Philadelphia PA 19112, Naval Support Activity Philadelphia (NSA-P), 700 Robbins St, Philadelphia PA 19111, and Naval Support Activity Mechanicsburg (NSA-M), 5450 Carlisle Pike, Mechanicsburg PA 17050

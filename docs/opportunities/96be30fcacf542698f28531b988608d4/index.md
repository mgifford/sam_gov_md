This is a Sources Sought Notice announcement; market survey for information only, to be used for preliminary planning purposes. No proposals are being requested or accepted with this synopsis. Medical Readiness Contracting Office-West, Fort Hood, TX, has an upcoming requirement to solicit for and award a contract to provide for Surgical Lense Intraocular Collamer Lenses (ICL) for Carl R. The Medical Readiness Contracting Office-West, Fort Hood, TX has an upcoming requirement to solicit for Surgical lntraocular Lenses that are FDA approved to be delivered on as needed basis for Carl R. Darnall Army Medical Center, Fort Hood, Texas. Salient Characteristics Lenss: a. Minimum clinical Performance Requirements: i. Must be FDA approved ii. Must be available/made from Collamer i. Must have a power range of For Nearsightedness (Myopia) between –3.0 to –20.0 Diopters with Astigmatism between 1.0 to

4.0 Diopters ii. Must be sterile with a shelf life of at least 8 months iii.

## Summary

The Department of Veteran Affairs is looking for a contractor to provide all labor, materials, tools, and equipment to perform the inspection and evaluation of the feed-water deaerator pressure vessel at the Malcolm Randall VA Medical Center. The inspection will follow guidelines as per NACE RP0590-96, National Board Inspection Code (NBIC/ANSI 23), VHA Directive 1810.

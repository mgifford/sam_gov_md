## Summary

****Answers have been posted ao 22 April 2026. The intent of this contract is to install Fiber-To-The-Desk (FTTD) within the rooms of B1109 (2nd floor) located at Hanscom AFB. The contractor must be capable of implementing TAA/NDAA compliant cabling solutions, strict security, performance, and compliance standards, identify compatibility with Cisco Room Kit-based VTC systems in classified/unclassified environments and understand labor and material sourcing timelines to determine if 60-day period of performance.

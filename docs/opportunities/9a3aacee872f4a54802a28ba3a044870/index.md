# Repair Service Roads Around H-Barracks #2, NAS Lemoore, CA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Award Amount: 971390.00

## Summary

At NAS Lemoore, the project requires repaving Parking Lots and Service Roads at H - Barracks # 2 and construction of new parking lots, widen the existing roads by Full Depth Recycling method, provide thermoplastic striping, new concrete wheel stops, new concrete collars, and new concrete sidewalks in accordance of project drawings and specifications. The Contractor must conduct a pavement survey to ensure proper drainage. The Contractor is responsible for utilizing all recycled materials, including grinding, milled, and pulverized materials, to correct low spots and ensure proper drainage. Additionally, the Contractor must dispose of any remaining recycled materials, waste materials, and debris from the project site to a disposal facility located outside the Naval Air Station Lemoore Base.

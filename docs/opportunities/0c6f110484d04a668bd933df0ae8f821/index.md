# FTQW235100 Repair Hanger Door B1340

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 16:18:14.963-04
- Solicitation Number: ftqw235100

## Summary

The purpose of this project is for the repair of one (1) existing tip-up aircraft hangar door at Eielson Air Force Base (AFB), Alaska. Construction includes, but is not limited to, repairs to existing mechanical operating systems, replacement of counterweight systems, hangar door insulation,cladding,installation of heated hangar door thresholds, and all incidental work necessary to restore full operational capability. The hangar will be partially occupied and operational during construction. All work will be coordinated with Government officials to maintain mission operations.

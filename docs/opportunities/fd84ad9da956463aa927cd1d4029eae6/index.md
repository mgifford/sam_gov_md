# Solicitation Repair of MSGR Shower

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 16:41:04.108-04
- Solicitation Number: 19PA1026Q0001

## Summary

This project consists of repairing the floor slope in nine (9) shower stalls located in nine (9) bathrooms at the Marine Security Guard Quarters. These bathrooms were constructed without proper slope in the shower stalls, causing water to flow out of the shower area and wet the bathroom floor during use. This condition creates both an operational inconvenience, requiring additional cleaning effort, and a safety hazard due to slippery floors that could lead to falls. Bathroom shower stall already fixed as a test. Eight (8) out of nine (9) bathroom shower stalls are pending for repairs.

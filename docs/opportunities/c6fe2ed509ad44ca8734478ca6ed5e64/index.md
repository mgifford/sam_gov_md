# Sources Sought for Mass Notification Remote Terminal Unit

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-17 17:28:35.138-04
- Solicitation Number: N6274224RTU26

## Summary

Notice of intent to use a brand name product. The Naval Facilities Engineering Systems Command, Pacific (NAVFAC Pacific) is performing a market survey to determine if other potential sources of supply can provide Mass Notification Remote Terminal Units (RTU). This product must have equal performance and listing/approval requirements as the Federal Signal Mass Notification RTU, UVRI-B Ultra Voice®. Fiscal Year 2024 Military Construction, Joint Region Marianas Project 678 (P-678), SATCOM Facility, Marine Corps Base Camp Blaz, Guam, requires products with the following characteristics: 1. Comply with National Fire Codes®, specifically NFPA 72. 2. Maintain electrical and operational compatibility with the Base Emergency Communication Control at Joint-Region Marianas Emergency Management Center. 3. Minimize potential for interference from jamming, spoofing, hacking, eavesdropping, or other malicious acts. 4. Provide the communication bridge to alert, notify and transmit emergency communications from the Base System to the new facility. 5. Broadcast live voice signals or activated prerecorded voice messages, tones, and other signals, to in-building mass notification system on Base. 6. Maintain flexible capability to interface self-amplified speakers and create additional notification directly from the RTU itself. 7. UL listed for service. This notification is for market research purposes only to identify if other potential sources of supply can meet the requirements of the Government. This is not a request for proposals, quotes, or an invitation for bid. The intent of this notice is to identify other potential sources of this product. To be considered, please submit detailed technical data in order for the Government to conduct a comprehensive evaluation of your product to ensure compatibility with the existing system. In accordance with FAR 15.201(e), a response to this notice is not an offer and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. The responder is solely responsible for the information it submits and any associated costs. The Government reserves the right to disregard any submittal that is incomplete or vague. Please do not submit company brochures. Please submit complete information via electronic mail to Mr. Shaun Bissen at shaun.m.bissen.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 31 March 2026 at 2:00 p.m. H.S.T. Information received after the due date/time will not be considered. Any information provided to the Government in response to this RFI will become U.S. Government property and will not be returned. All proprietary or classified information will be treated appropriately.

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. This solicitation is a Request for Quote (RFQ). The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2026-01, effective 30 January 2026. This requirement is a 100% small business set-aside. The associated NAICS code is 333310, which has a size standard of 1,000 employees. All interested parties must be registered as a small business under this NAICS code in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) to be considered for award.

1.0 Description of Requirement The 177th Aircraft Maintenance Squadron (AMXS) Weapons Flight requires the purchase of one (1) Ultrasonic Parts Washer. This equipment is essential for cleaning components during scheduled and unscheduled maintenance on the MAU-12 Bomb Rack, TER-9A Triple Ejector Rack, and the M61A1 Vulcan Cannon Gun System.

5.1 FAR 52.212-2 ADDENDUM, Evaluation-Commercial Products and Commercial Services (a) The Government will award a contract resulting from this solicitation to the responsible offeror whose offer conforming to the solicitation will be most advantageous to the Government, price and other factors considered.

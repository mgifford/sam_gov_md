# Easement Restoration and Emergency Watershed Protection – Seedbed Preparation, Seeding, and Post Seeding Clippings in South Dakota

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 13:37:52.182-04
- Solicitation Number: 12FPC126R0001

## Summary

Scope of Work. Work covered by this contract and respective task orders against this contract shall consist of furnishing all labor, equipment, operators, supervision/site superintendent, supplies and materials necessary to perform Easement Restoration and Emergency Watershed Protection services within the state of South Dakota (counties East of the Missouri River). This is anticipated to be awarded as a SINGLE Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Task Order contract. Contract Area: Location of the work to be performed shall be within the state of South Dakota (counties East of the Missouri River), to support Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS), U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) The contract consists of complying with applicable Federal, State, and local laws, regulations, and directives to include applicable publications. The Contractor shall perform all work in accordance with the professional licensing and certification laws of the respective state where the work is performed. Specific contract areas will be specified within each task order.

# Landslide Repairs in the North and South Region C2

- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 14:06:52.084-04
- Solicitation Number: 693C73-26-B-000009

## Summary

Sealed bids from certified Small Business Concerns only will be accepted for Landslide Repairs in the North and South Region C2, Project PR ER DOT PRMNT RPR(23), Solicitation Number 693C73-26-B-000009, located in the municipalities of Adjuntas, Aguas Buenas, Arecibo, Cayey, Coamo, Juana Díaz, Naguabo, Ponce, San Juan, and Yauco, Puerto Rico. The project consists of repairing landslides and washouts damages caused by Hurricanes Irma and Maria on PR-1 km. 111.6 - 111.8 (HWY-3), PR-1 km. 24.8 (HWY-4), PR-10 km. 52.3 (HWY-11), PR-10, km. 30.3-30.4 (HWY-12), PR-121 km. 11.65 (HWY-81), PR-128 km. 10.8 (HWY-142), PR-123 km. 24.2 (HWY-83), and PR-173 km. 24.7 (HWY-301), PR-184 km. 28.6 (HWY-315), PR-971 km. 6.3 (HWY-470). The work includes utility relocations, embankment reconstruction, slope stabilization and reconstruction, retaining walls, gabion walls, Mechanically Stabilized Earth (MSE) slopes, temporary shoring and bracing, drainage systems installation, pavement reconstruction, milling and overlay, pavement markings, signage, removal and replacement of guardrails, sediment and erosion control work, temporary traffic control, and other miscellaneous work. The project will be split into multiple work schedules/contract options, with the cost of the entire project expected to fall within the price range of $10,000,000 to $15,000,000. Schedule B (Government Option 1) cannot be executed until all permits have been received. Bid documents should be issued on or about March 27, 2026. Bid due date and location will be specified on the SF 1442, Block 13a of the Solicitation.

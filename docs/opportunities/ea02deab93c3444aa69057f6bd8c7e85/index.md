# Maintenance and Services for Integrated Commercial Intrusion Detection System (ICIDSIV), Version IV

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-04 15:05:18.798-05
- Solicitation Number: W56ZLW-26-Q-0005

## Summary

The U.S. Government desires to procure Maintenance and Services for Integrated Commercial Intrusion Detection System (ICIDSIV), which includes providing the needed management and personnel to effectively, efficiently and safely receive, store, and issue hazardous material. The contractor will provide services and preventative maintenance required to maintain all components of the Integrated Commercial Detection System; version IV (ICIDS-IV) system being utilized throughout the Fort Bliss Military Reservation in operational status and various devices throughout the installation. The Contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform preventive maintenance of ICIDS IV, to include Primary Monitoring Cabinet, Operator Work Station, Remote Monitoring Stations(RMS), on the Fort Bliss Military Installation, East Fort Bliss TX, Dona Ana, N.M, Oro Grande N.M. and McGregor Range N.M. Facilities as defined in the attached draft Performance Work Statement (PWS) except for those items specified as Government furnished property and services.

# Armory Sanitation Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Armory Sanitation Services Introduction: The Government is only conducting market research and attaining information to identify contractors that are able to provide this requirement. The Government may use the responses to this RFI for information and planning purposes. All information is strictly voluntary. Requirement: The 37th Training Support Squadron (TRSS) seeks remediation cleaning services to mitigate lead buildup, carbon residue, in the armory at Building 950, 900 Patrol Rd, Chapman Annex 78227. This process will include a comprehensive initial deep cleaning, followed by biannual maintenance cleaning to sustain proper conditions. Bi-annual maintenance shall take place every 6 months thereafter to ensure ongoing upkeep and operational efficiency. Response: Respond to this notice by submitting completed RFI (RFI_Armory Sanitation Services attachment) via email to Andrew Ng @ andrew.ng.4@us.af.mil. Telephone responses shall not be accepted. Responses must be received no later than Friday, 24 April 2026 at 4:00 p.m., Central Daylight Time (CDT).

## Summary

Request for Quote (RFQ)The Tennessee Army National Guard (TNARNG) Purchasing and Contracting Office for United States Property and Fiscal Office (USPFO) TN located at 3041 Sidco Drive, Nashville, TN37204 is issuing an RFQ for commercial single award contract in accordance with (IAW) the format in Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) subpart 12 as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. The North American Industry Classification System(NAICS) code for this requirement is 333924 Industrial Truck, Tractor, Trailer, and Stacker Machinery Manufacturing. This documentation incorporates provisions and clauses that are in effect through the Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2026-01 effective date 03/13/2026. The Government intends to award a single Firm Fixed Price contract. This solicitation is set-aside to Small Business vendors. Quotes are due in this office no later than Monday, 13 April 2026 at 10:00 AM Central Standard Time. Vendors must submit quotes on time. SUBMISSION OF QUOTE REQUIREMENTS: Offerors are encouraged to visit and familiarize themselves with PIEE website to avoid technical difficulties submitting QUOTES documents before the due date/time. Technical issues are not an excuse for late submission. The prime vendor must be the same as the Proposal Manager in PIEE when submitting a quote. A user guide on how to post an offer in response to this solicitation can be found at: https://pieetraining.eb.mil/wbt/sol/Posting_Offer.pdfA web link to a video demonstration on how to post an offer can be found at:https://pieetraining.eb.mil/wbt/sol/Posting_Offer.htmlTo verify your QUOTE has been delivered, you may e-mail sinamar.b.benauro.civ@army.mil .To be eligible for contract award, a firm must be registered with the System for Award Management (SAM) database. For instructions on registering with SAM, please see the SAM website at https://www.sam.gov/ .Submission of quotes are only through PIEE. Email, mail, or fax will not be accepted. Please refer to Evaluation factors for more information regarding evaluation of quotes. Questions are due on Tuesday, 6 April 2026 at 10:00 AM CST.

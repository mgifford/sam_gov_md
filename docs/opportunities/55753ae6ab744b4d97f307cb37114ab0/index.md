# Request for Proposal PR432386 RPF Telemanipulators

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 16:41:02.531-04
- Solicitation Number: PR432386

## Summary

Request for Proposal PR432386 RPF Telemanipulators UT-Battelle, LLC (the Company), is the Management & Operations contractor for the Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) located in Oak Ridge, TN. In support of a Department of Energy (DOE) funded project, the Company is seeking a quantity of two (2) telemanipulators of either of the two options, a three (3) piece design and a one (1) piece design, will be evaluated for functionality and cost. Please see Attachment C for exact specifications. Attachment a contains the minimum specifications. Size dimensions are based on a common unit in the commercial marketplace that will fit our allotted space to meet some of the minimum requirements needed. Suppliers must be able to meet the minimum requirements or to provide a functional equivalent. Any unit exceeding the size dimensions will be evaluated to see if it is technically acceptable.

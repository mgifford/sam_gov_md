## Summary

Solicitation number 1282A726Q0015 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Medical Services at Curlew Job Corp Center which includes examinations and assessments, treatment, issuance of prescriptions and immunizations, monitoring health conditions, recommending hospitalization, follow-up care, prevention planning and intervention, and all other services required by the Statement of Work (see Attachment 1 of Solicitation). The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 621111. The small business size standard is Q201 This solicitation is 100% Set-Aside for Small Business. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which shall be considered by the agency. Quotes that do not include all required information, including pricing and complete credentials for proposed candidates such as resumes, insurance, and licenses may be determined to be non-responsive.

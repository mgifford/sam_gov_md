# Lab Coat Services

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 13:15:26.939-04
- Solicitation Number: 75F40126Q00075

## Summary

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Office of Pharmaceutical Quality Research (OPQR) requires continued support to ensure a safe and efficient laboratory environment for its scientific staff. This requirement is specifically for laboratory coat services, including the provision, routine cleaning, maintenance, and replacement of lab coats used by OPQR personnel. Please refer to the attached Statement of Work (SOW) for the complete technical requirements. The anticipated Period of Performance (POP) for this requirement is structured as a one-year base period followed by four one-year option periods, exercisable at the discretion of the Government.

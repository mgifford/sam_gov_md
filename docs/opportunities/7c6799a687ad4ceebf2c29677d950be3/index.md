# Justification for Other than Full and Open Competition for Engineering Support Services

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-30 10:01:09.942-04
- Solicitation Number: 70Z02323F45900001

## Summary

In accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1), Justification for Other than Full and Open Competition, this notice is issued regarding a modification to award number 70Z02323F45900001 with Thor Solutions LLC. This modification extends Engineering Support Services for the U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) Assistant Commandant – Surface (CG-SURFACE) for an additional 12 months. The extension will provide continuity of services while a new solicitation for Engineering Support Services is conducted. This Justification and Approval (J&A) is being posted in accordance with the requirements of FAR 6.301(b). A copy of the J&A is attached to this notice

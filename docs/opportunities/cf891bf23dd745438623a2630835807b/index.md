# DDWG Generator Boxes

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-10 12:25:07.802-04
- Solicitation Number: SP330026Q0112

## Summary

This Request for Quotation (RFQ) is being issued to establish a firm-fixed-price (FFP) purchase order for Generator Boxes at DLA Distribution Warner Robins (DDWG). The Government intends to award one (1) contract as a result of this RFQ and will be issued on a Standard Form (SF) 1449. This acquisition is not a Defense Priorities and Allocations System (DPAS) rated procurement.

# MAINTENANCE OF FIELD TEST EQUITMENT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 13:08:13.764-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016426SNB33

## Summary

The Government is issuing this Sources Sought announcement as part of market research in accordance with FAR 15.201(d) to determine industry’s capability for the procurement of services and material support required to maintain operational condition of various Government Owned Vehicles (GOV), Non-Tactical Vehicles (NTV), and Government Furnished Equipment (GFE). The service support for the GOV’s, NTV’s, and GFE’s include performance of annual maintenance as recommended by the Original Equipment Manufacturer with emphasis on the electrical, breaking, and structure elements. The GOV’s require preventative maintenance checks and services for Mine-Resistant Ambush Protected (MRAP) military vehicles. Maintenance technicians should have similar qualifications as an Army MOS 91B (Wheeled Vehicle Mechanic). Contractors may obtain the Automotive Service Excellence certification standards via the internet at https://www.cool.osd.mil/army/moc/index.html?moc=91b&tab=cred. Maintenance should be performed as prescribed by the following service variant’s technical manuals: 9-2320-387-10 HMMVV operators manual, 9-2355-106-10 MRAPS and up armored vehicles, and 11202D-O1 Cougar MRAP Vehicle. MRAP specifications vary by vehicle type/model, but generally include: Height 159” (13 ½ feet) Length 260” (21 ¾ feet) Width 120” (10 feet) Weight 54,000 lbs. Fuel Diesel/ 70 gallons. USE DF-1 or DF-2 only. The NTV’s include all-terrain vehicles, side-by-side “Polaris” style vehicles, and a variety of trailers such as gooseneck, flatbed utility, and boxed/enclosed trailers with a max curb weight of 10,000lbs. None of the GOV’s or NTV’s are electric. Tire/wheel service, repair, and replacement for GOV & NTV shall be provided by the vendor at both local and remote locations. Local is defined within 50 miles of NSWC CRANE. In remote support cases (outside of 50 miles from NSWC CRANE), the vendor may subcontract the tire & wheel service requirements by coordinating with an accredited tire/wheel service provider in the vicinity of the GOV or NTV to meet the Government’s service completion timeline requirements. The Government will provide transportation of the GOV or NTV to and from the vendor location, except in remote service cases. Any maintenance or material support not considered part of the annual or preventive maintenance will be submitted as a separate quote for evaluation and consideration by the Government. GFE support for the installed/mounted equipment is required for local and remote locations, and includes but is not limited to: Small engine support for generators ranging from 2kw to 26kW. Antenna mounts and hoist systems. Trailer leveling components. Internal electrical wiring connected to the installed GFE (Electronic) is not anticipated under this effort. At the current time, NO FUNDING IS AVAILABLE for contractual efforts.

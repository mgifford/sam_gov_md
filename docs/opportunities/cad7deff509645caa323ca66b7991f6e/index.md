# Electrical Maintenance Contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-25 14:23:21.979-04

## Summary

The contractor shall provide personnel, services, test equipment, tools, materials, vehicles, and subcontracting necessary to perform non-personal services for, engineering, acquisition, operation, maintenance, installation, removal, and repair of electrical equipment and systems supporting RQ. Due to the dynamic nature of research and development, the contract will experience variations in workload. All tasks shall be accomplished IAW generally accepted engineering standards and practices. All government material handling vehicles and equipment used to perform the tasks under this PWS shall be operated IAW Wright Patterson AFB policies and procedures.

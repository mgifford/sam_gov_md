# Bldg. 907 WIFI Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-20 15:10:53.17-04
- Solicitation Number: F3PF1K6051AC01

## Summary

The 502d Contracting Squadron intends to issue a direct a firm fixed price purchase order to Spectrum/Charter Communications Operating LLC (SAM UEI: MX16CA896HK5), located at 12405 Powerscourt Drive, Saint Louis, Missouri, 63131-3673, for Wi-Fi services at Building 907, 1851 1st Street East, Suite 1, JBSA – Randolph, TX 78150. Market research determined that this requirement can only be fulfilled by Spectrum/Charter Communications Operating LLC. They are the sole vendor capable of providing Wi-Fi services to Building 907 due to the existing coaxial infrastructure, which is owned and maintained by Spectrum/Charter. A survey confirmed that the building’s ready-to-use coaxial cable system is proprietary to Spectrum/Charter, and no other provider can utilize or replace this infrastructure without causing unacceptable delays or incurring substantial duplication of costs. Based on these findings, a sole-source procurement is justified. This notice is NOT a request for proposals or an invitation for bids. The Government does not anticipate receiving responses to this announcement. Any party choosing to respond does so at its own expense. Costs associated with preparing or submitting information in response to this notice will not be reimbursed by the Government.

# Medical Linen Laundry Services-355th Medical Group, Davis-Monthan AFB, AZ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 15:36:40.83-04
- Solicitation Number: HT941026Q2023

## Summary

Defense Health Agency Contracting Activity (DHACA) Healthcare Contracting Division-West (HCD-W) requests responses from qualified sources capable of providing medical linen laundry services for the 355th Medical Group (355 MDG), Davis-Monthan Air Force Base (DMAFB), Arizona. The contractor shall provide medical linen processing, laundering, delivery and pickup services. Services include healthcare linen processing, curtain laundering, and hotpack services in accordance with the Performance Work Statement (PWS).

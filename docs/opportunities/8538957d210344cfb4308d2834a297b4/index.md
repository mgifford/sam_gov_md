# Cherry Creek Dam (CO) Rehab Gate Stems

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 16:23:54.402-04
- Solicitation Number: W9128F26QA016

## Summary

1 April 2026 - W9128F26QA016 AMD 0001 - Updated Performance Work Statement (AMD 0001) and Drawing Sheet (Attachment A - M103 AMD 0001) to reflect an updated scope to cleaning and polishing of the hydraulic cylinder main stem. The re-chroming requirement has been removed from the scope. Uploaded Site Visit Sign-In Sheet Updated Questions & Answers document (AMD 0001) See Solicitation Amendment W9128F26QA0160001 SF 30 ____________________________________________________ 26 March 2026 - Added Answers to questions recieved from the contractors. --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- The U.S. Army Corp of Engineers, Omaha District hereby announces it's intent to issue a solicitation for the Cheery Creek Dam Rehab Gate Stems #2 & #5. The solicitation will close on 10 April 2026 at 2 PM CDT. This solictation is set-aside 100% for Small Business. Description of Services: Following a previous incident involving the failure of the water packing gland on Service Gate Stem No. 1, a service contract was issued to replace the packing material and perform associated repairs. The scope of that contract included hydraulic hoist stem scoring repair, re-chroming, installation of a new wiper seal, and correction of a hoist brake leak. During contract modification, additional repair work was also completed on Gate Stems No. 3 and No. 4. After successful completion of these rehabilitation efforts, the Tri-Lakes Project Office has decided to initiate a similar comprehensive rehabilitation service contract for Service Gate Stems No. 2 and No. 5. This proactive measure is intended to ensure the continued safe and reliable operation of the intake service gates. This contract will encompass the rehabilitation of Service Gate Stems No. 2 (Base), and No. 5 (Option) loacted at Cherry Creek Dam in Aurora, Colorado. The detailed scope of work to be executed following the disassembly of each hydraulic cylinder in accordance with the terms of this service contract can be found in the Performance Work Statement and Attachment A (Annotated As Bulit). NAICS Code: The Primary North American Industry Classification Systems (NAICS) code anticipated for solicitation is 811310, Commercial and Industrial Machinery and Equipment (except Automotive and Electronic) Repair and Maintenance, with a size standard of $12.5 Million. Contracting Office Address: USACE Omaha District 1616 Captiol Ave, Omaha, NE 68102-4901 POC: All questions regarding thie solicitation should be made to the Contract Specialist, who can be reached by email: Tony.J.Manna@usace.army.mil

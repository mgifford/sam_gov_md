## Summary

DESCRIPTION: NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD A SOLE SOURCE CONTRACT TO Human Motion Technologies LLC. for the brand name only purchase of one (1) Prosthetic Device Emulator (BRAVO Lab) system for the Prosthetics Division to support clinical prosthetics care and research activities within the VISN 2 BRAVO Laboratory. This system will allow real-time simulation and evaluation of ankle-foot prosthetic devices prior to definitive prescription and fabrication.

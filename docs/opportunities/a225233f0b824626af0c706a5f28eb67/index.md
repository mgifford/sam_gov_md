# Tri-Service CAD/PAD Life Cycle Management Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-20 17:38:28.277-04
- Solicitation Number: N00174-24-RFPREQ-JPO-0086
- Awardee: CHEMRING ENERGETIC DEVICES INC Downers Grove IL 60515 USA
- Award Amount: 18192118.70

## Summary

The Naval Surface Warfare Center Indian Head Division (NSWC IHD) awarded a firm-fixed price, Basic Ordering Agreement on 08 April 2026, for Engineering Services to support Cartridge and Propellant Actuated Devices for the Joint Program Office requirements. The services under this agreement cover technology, development, product, improvement, qualification and prototyping support, quality evaluation and surveillance program support. As a result of N0017425SN0014, the Government awarded a Shared Ceiling Firm-Fixed Price agreement in the amount of $18,192,118.70 to Chemring Energetic Devices Inc., Downers Grove, Illinois, USA.

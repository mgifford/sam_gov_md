- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

This Sources Sought is strictly for market research purposes only. This requirement will provide essential Local Telecommunication Services (LTS) and Long-Distance services and features supporting Hurlburt Field. Site specific equipment connectivity requirements are identified in Appendix 1, along with required features. This LTS will support the installation 24 hours a day/seven days a week/365 days a year (24/7/365). The requirement is for IP trunking to the Hurlburt Field Call Manager.

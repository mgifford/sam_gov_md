## Summary

Combined Solicitation number W9127S26QA029 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ). The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular 2026-01. Set Aside, NAICS Code & Size Standard: This solicitation is being procured as a 100% Small Business Set-Aside. The NAICS Code is 561730, Landscaping Services, which has an applicable size standard of $9.5M. Description of requirements for the items to be acquired: Contractor shall perform maintenance approximately 50 miles per year of boundary line between Ozark Lake and Dardanelle Lake, Fletcher Bend, Springhill Park and Cherokee Park in accordance with PWS. The boundary line runs along diverse terrain, ranging from steep banks to flat agricultural lands. In some instances, hip boots will be required during line maintenance. Vegetation varies from open agricultural land to heavy underbrush areas. This should be a consideration when bidding on the contract. Place(s) and date(s) of performance: - The waters and lands of Ozark and Dardanelle Lakes and the McClellan-Kerr Arkansas River Navigation System under the Russellville Site Office, MKARNS Project of the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, located in Franklin County, Logan County, Sebastian County, Conway County, Johnson County, Faulkner Count, Arkansas. - Dates of performance will be as follows: date of award - 31 December 2026 1 January 2026- 31 December 2027 1 January 2027- 31 December 2028 SITE VISIT: NA If you have any questions regarding this solicitation, please contact John Kattengell at either 501.324.5604 or John.R.Kattengell@usace.army.mil Quotes are due no later than Thursday, April 23, 2026, at 10:00 AM CDT Please see attachment

## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT ONLY - Solicitations are not available at this time. This notice does not constitute a commitment by the Government. The information gathered from market research will be vital in the formation and set aside status of a future solicitation. Responses must be received no later than March 26, 2026 for proper consideration. The Bureau of Prisons has a future requirement for the removal, disposal, and replacement of a 25-ton Trane MCCA021 Air Handler Unit (AHU) that may result in a solicitation for a project entitled FDC Honolulu - Project 26Z6AN7 HVAC Repairs and Upgrades, located in Honolulu, Hawaii. The service will include all labor, materials, tools, equipment, supervision, transportation, and incidentals necessary to complete the removal, replacement, and restoration. The Government contemplates awarding of firm fixed priced purchase order resulting from a future solicitation. Further details concerning the required supplies and equipment will be part of future solicitation. A Government solicitation document is being developed, and a contract may or may not result. Market research is being conducted to determine interest and capability of potential sources for this requirement. All future information about this acquisition, including the solicitation and/or amendments, will be distributed solely through this site. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring this site to ensure that they have the most up-to-date information regarding this acquisition.

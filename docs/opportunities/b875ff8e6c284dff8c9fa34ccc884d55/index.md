# PORTABLE LATRINE, PORTABLE SINK, AND WASTE REMOVAL SERVICES

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 16:40:23.088-04
- Solicitation Number: W911SA26QA132

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to provide portable latrines, portable hand-washing stations, and waste removal defined in this Performance Work Statement (PWS). The objectives of this requirement are to provide, clean, and maintain portable latrines, portable handwash stations and waste removal through the use of 40-yard open-topped containers. The rental and service will take place in two (2) different locations across two states. The Government will not furnish any facilities, equipment, supplies, or services to the Contractor throughout the period of the contract.

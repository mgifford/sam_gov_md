# Sole Source Award, Rees Temperature Sensitive Medical Product Monitoring System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The East Services Branch, Health Readiness Contracting Office awarded a Firm-Fixed Price Purchase Order to REES SCIENTIFIC CORPORATION, 1007 WHITEHEAD ROAD EXT, EWING, NJ 08638 to provide the Ireland Army Health Clinic (IAHC) in Fort Knox, Kentucky with preventive maintenance, repair, validation/calibration, hardware, and system software support/upgrades for the Government-owned Temperature Sensitive Medical Product (TSMP) Monitoring System. Base plus four one-year option periods, total contract period of performance from 1 April 2026 through 31 March 2031.

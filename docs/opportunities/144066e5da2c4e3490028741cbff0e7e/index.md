# Willamette National Cemetery Grounds Maintenance Services

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF

## Summary

The National Cemetery Administration hereby announces the award of Contract 36C78626D50105 resulting from Solicitation 36C78626Q50019 for Grounds Maintenance Services at WIllamette National Cemetery. The total contract value, including the base period and all option periods, is $6,886,880.00. Award was made using a best value determination in accordance with the evaluation criteria stated in the solicitation. The evaluation factors included Technical Acceptability, Price Reasonableness, and Past Performance Recency and Relevancy. The selected contractor was determined to represent the best value to the Government.

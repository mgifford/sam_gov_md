# Cassville Cottage Asbestos Removal and Demolition and Clinton County Cottage Asbestos Removal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 10:56:58.207-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26QA019

## Summary

The United States Army Corps of Enginners (USACE) - Rock Island District has a construction requirement for Asbestos Removal and Demolition at Cassville Cottage in Cassville, WI and Asbestos Removal at Clinton County Cottage in Clinton County, IA. Additional information on the construction requirement will be included in the solicitation when released on SAM.gov. USACE - Rock Island District intends to advertise this requirement as 100% Small Business Set-Aside utilizing Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) evaluation procedures. A single Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract is anticipated to be awarded to the lowest price technically acceptable offeror. Any companies interested in this requirement please provide capability statements via email to contract specialist Carrie Holub at carrie.l.holub@usace.army.mil. The presolicitation notice is for information purpose only and is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government to procure any supplies or services, or for the Government to pay for the information received. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this presolicitation notice or any follow up information requested. Consequently, no solicitation is being issued at this time. Therefore, no award will be made as a result of providing this information.

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) from the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA)’s Tactical Technology Office (TTO) seeks to improve its understanding of the U.S. industry and supplier base for rocket propulsion technologies that can enable the development and production of low-cost missiles at speed and scale.

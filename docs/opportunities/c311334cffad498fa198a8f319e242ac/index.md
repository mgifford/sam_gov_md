# 2BCT Cranes

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-06 15:48:47-05
- Solicitation Number: W911RX26PA008
- Awardee: TECH O.R. LLC Redford MI 48239 USA
- Award Amount: 44000.00

## Summary

The contractor shall provide a Crane operator, a rigging crew, and one (1) Crane capable of hoisting an M1A2 Tank (73.59 tons) onto a rail car from the ground. The crane will also be used to load other non-mission capable equipment from the train onto the ground. Potential lift loads include: M1A2 Abrams Tank (73.59 tons), M1120A2 Truck (40,300 lbs), M1074A1 Material Handling Crane w/ winch (55,204 lbs), M88A2 Tracked Recovery Vehicle (70.8 tons), or the M2A3 Fighting Vehicle (74,400 lbs).

Incorporate Questions, Answers and Photos (See Attached, 17 pages) C Incorporate Wage Rate Determination CA20260026 dated 01.16.2026 to replace CA20250026 dated 12.12.2025 (See Attached, 23 Pages) No other changes at this time The Federal Bureau of Prisons (FBOP) is issuing solicitation 15BBNF26B00000006 for the award of a firm-fixed-price construction contract for a project entitled EMERGENCY REPLACE REFRIGERANT UNITS at the Federal Correctional Complex (FCC Victorville), located in Victorville, CA. FCC Victorville is a Federal Correctional facility located at 13777 AIR EXPRESSWAY BLVD., VICTORVILLE CALIFORNIA, 92394. The project consists of the following: To replace 4 (four) big condensing ground units and 14 evaporator coils with 5 remote condensing units and 14 (fourteen) evaporative coils located behind the food service outside warehouse at FCC Victorville. See the Statement of Work attachment included with this solicitation posting for the complete requirements of the work. The performance period for the project will be 163 Calendar Days from issuance of the notice to proceed.

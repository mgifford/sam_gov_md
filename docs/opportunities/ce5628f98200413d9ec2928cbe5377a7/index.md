# NGA HRM-II RFI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:53:39.731-04
- Solicitation Number: NGA_HM0476_HRM_II_RFI

## Summary

*** The purpose of this RFI Amendment is to; 1. Provide the official Government Response to Industry Questions, see HRM II Attachment 3- Government RFI Response Q and A 10MAR2026. 2. Issue a revised RFI document as a result of Government Response to Industry Questions. See Attachment- HRM II RFI Revision 1 10MAR2026. Industry Questions to HRM- II RFI are now closed. *** The National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA) is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) to conduct market research for the Human Resources Management II (HRM II) requirement. The purpose of this RFI is to identify qualified sources capable of providing a full range of human resources services to support NGA’s mission. The Government invites all businesses, including small businesses, to respond to this RFI. Please note: The Performance Requirements Summary (PRS) and Quality Assurance Surveillance Plan (QASP), referenced in Section 16.0 of the attached PWS, are currently under development by the Government and will be provided in a future solicitation, if any is issued. Response Requirements: Interested parties are requested to respond to this RFI by providing a white paper as described in the RFI document. The white paper must provide a response to all sections of the RFI and all questions listed in "Attachment 2 - Government Questions." Page Limit: The white paper shall not exceed twenty (20) pages. The cover page and table of contents are excluded from this limit.

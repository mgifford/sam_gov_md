# 472077 RFP - DUNE VD CRP-SST

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 14:08:05.832-04
- Solicitation Number: 472077

## Summary

Brookhaven Science Associates, LLC (BSA), under a Prime Contract for the operation of Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL) with the U.S. Department of Energy (DOE), herewith is soliciting proposals for Temporary Support/Contract Labor Services. This solicitation is posted for full and open competition under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 332312 – Fabricated Structural Metal Manufacturing; with a business size standard of 500 employees. This procurement is for sixteen (16) Charge Readout Plane Support Superstructures (CRP-SSTs) for the Deep Underground Neutrino Experiment (DUNE) Vertical Drift (VD) detector. This procurement also includes the following options: 1. Up to two (2) additional small CRP-SST frames. 2. Up to two (2) additional large CRP-SST frames. 3. Cost of cleaning/degreasing the frames prior to shipment. BSA invites you to participate in accordance with the RFP letter and all supporting enclosures. Please ensure to carefully read the RFP letter and all supporting enclosures in their entirety. Any questions concerning this RFP must be addressed via email tot he attention of Lisa Gonzales, lgonzales@bnl.gov.

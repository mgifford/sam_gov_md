# Gasoline Marine Generator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 12:39:37.031-04
- Solicitation Number: W912HN26S0017

## Summary

U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Savannah District. This is a pre-solicitation notice in accordance with RFO 5.1. This notice does not constitute a solicitation, request for proposal, or commitment by the Government. No contract will be awarded from this announcement, and all information is subject to modification and is in no way binding to the Government. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring the GPE for the official solicitation and any amendments. This acquisition is being conducted under Simplified Acquisition Procedures. The Government anticipates awarding a firm-fixed-price contract. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, which shall be considered by the agency. Offerors who wish to be provided further information on how to obtain the solicitation must submit all inquiries electronically through the PIEE module.PROJECT OVERVIEW: The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Savannah District, anticipates issuing a solicitation for the supply and delivery of one new gasoline-powered marine generator. The generator shall be delivered with all factory-supplied accessories and documentation for installation and operation. Delivery will be made to Savannah, Georgia. The required minimum specifications of the generator must be met to be eligible for award- The specifications are provided in the attached specification document.

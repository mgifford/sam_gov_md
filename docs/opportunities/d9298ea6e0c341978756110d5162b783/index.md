# Savannah CRTC - DFAC Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 14:04:37.888-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S7026QA002

## Summary

This is a non-personal services Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to provide full food service at the Savannah Combat Readiness and Training Center (CRTC). The Government shall not exercise any supervision or control over the contract service providers performing the services herein. Such contract service providers shall be accountable solely to the Contractor who, in turn is responsible to the Government. The Savannah CRTC Dining Facility is a full-service dining facility to include carry out service, box meals, and catering to on-base locations during scheduled events. The Dining Facility is only utilized on an as-needed basis to support visiting units exercises and does not operate on a full-time schedule. Notice will be provided a minimum of 1 week prior to DFAC services being required of the contractor. The contractor shall provide all personnel, uniforms, transportation, and supervision necessary to perform service as defined in this Performance Work Statement, except for those items specified as Government furnished property and services.

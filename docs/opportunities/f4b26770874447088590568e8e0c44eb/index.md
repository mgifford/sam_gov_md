# Refuse Collection and Recycling Services at the AFRC, Bristol, PA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-06 12:54:32.124-05
- Solicitation Number: W15QKN26QA065

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command – New Jersey, Warfighter Support Division has a follow-on requirement for Refuse Collection and Recycling Services at the Bristol Armed Forces Reserve Center (AFRC), 2501 Ford Road, Bristol, PA 19007 in support of the 99th Readiness Division. Refuse Collection and Recycling Services include, but are not limited to, the following: Refuse Pickup/Disposal: Collection from two (2) 8-cubic yard dumpsters, serviced once per week. Recycling Pickup/Disposal: Collection from one (1) 8-cubic yard dumpster, serviced once per week. Bulky Waste Disposal: Provision and servicing of one (1) 30-cubic yard roll-off container, serviced as needed (approximately once per year). The Government intends to award a single firm-fixed-price requirements contract as a result of the solicitation to be issued. The anticipated period of performance is one 12-month base period and four, 12-month option years.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The Department of the Navy is conducting market research to identify qualified sources capable of providing brand-name ORTEC Portable High Purity Germanium (HPGe) detectors, manufactured by Advanced Measurement Technology, Inc. (AMETEK), along with associated accessories. This RFI is issued solely for information and planning purposes in accordance with FAR Part 10. The Government intends to procure brand-name ORTEC HPGe detectors and accessories as listed in the attachment titled ?A2.2 RFI Product List.?

# US GOVERNMENT SEEKS SOURCES FOR LEASED RETAIL AND RELATED SPACE, HARLINGEN, TX

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-25 09:49:02.474-04
- Solicitation Number: 26-0030

## Summary

The U. S. Army Corps of Engineers is seeking to lease approximately 4,698 gross square feet of existing Class A or B retail space, in the Harlingen area specifically in zip code 78552. The ideal space would fit the following: building specifications in compliance with all federal, state and local ordinances; force protection standards incompliance with Department of Defense's Unified Facility Criteria; outfitted with either designated or open parking. An initial five year lease is intended. Space must be ready for occupancy by 1 September 2026. Expressions of interest should include the following: -Building name and address -Floor plan drawing -Digital photo of street view of property -Area (BOMA rentable square feet & BOMA usable square feet) -Rental rate per square foot rentable space Common area factor (s) for available space, if applicable interested parties may contact: Will Johnston at U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, ATTN: CESWF-RE-A (Johnston), 819 Taylor Street, Rm 2A06, Fort Worth, TX 76102-0300; or William.B.Johnston@usace.army.mil, no later than 1 September 2026. NOTE: this is a request for information only, not a solicitation for offers.

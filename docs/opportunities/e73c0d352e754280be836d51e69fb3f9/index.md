# M106 Canister Bodies

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 09:56:05.695-04
- Solicitation Number: 030840

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) is seeking sources for the manufacturing of 17,500 each of Canister Bodies for the M106 Grenade. This requirement is expected to result in a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract for the procurement of non-commercial item, Canister Bodies, under NAICS 322219 -- Other Paperboard Container Manufacturing. The Canister Bodies shall be in accordance with attachment 0001 - Drawing 13-19-1011C dated 4 March 2026. First article testing (FAT) will be required at a quantity of ten (10) each. FAT samples shall be delivered 30 days after contract award. Inspection and Acceptance/Delivery of Canister Bodies FAT and production quantity will be done at Destination. Freight on Board point is Destination to Pine Bluff Arsenal: US Army, Pine Bluff Arsenal Central Shipping Receiving Point (CSRP) BLDG 53-990, 506th Street Pine Bluff, Arkansas 71602 The Point of Contract (POC) for this action is contract specialist Austin Acree at austin.g.acree.civ@army.mil

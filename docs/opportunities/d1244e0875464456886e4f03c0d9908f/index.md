# Global Enterprise Mobility Services

- Agency: AMERICAN BATTLE MONUMENTS COMMISSION
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-30 10:04:17.301-04
- Solicitation Number: ABMC_GEMS_FY26

## Summary

ABMC is seeking information from vendors who can provide cellular services for locations identified in Appendix A. We are interested in solutions that can cover all locations, but proposals for some locations are also welcome. This is a sources-sought RFI only and no contract will be awarded at this stage. Vendors are asked to review the attached draft PWS and provide information on their ability to deliver services, including coverage, plans, pricing, and any value-added offerings. Please submit a brief capabilities statement outlining your ability to meet these requirements. Please clearly indicate which locations you are able to service and which you are not able to service.

## Summary

THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITIVE OFFERS VA Pittsburgh Healthcare System This is a Pre-Solicitation Notice for the Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office (NCO) 4, for preventative maintenance and corrective maintenance repairs to laboratory equipment, and software updates to the ACIST contrast injector. This services requirement is for the University Drive Campus located in Pittsburgh, PA 15240 . The full solicitation will be posted on the Contract Opportunities website at www.sam.gov on or about March 17th, 2026. The full solicitation will be unrestricted. The point of contact is Contracting Officer, Keri Hester, email: keri.hester1@va.gov.

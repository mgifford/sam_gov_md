# RFQ Casualty Surgical Simulation Kit

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 18:59:17.412-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124B26QA002

## Summary

Casualty Surgical Simulation Kit, for added clarity the below items shall be all inclusive to 1 kit. The vendor shall be trained and have prior experience in training for the usage of this equipment. The vendor shall travel to Fort Irwin CA, to provide Support for 3 NTC Training Iterations and train end users on this kit. Training support shall be conducted by a Cut Suit SME that has a background of 8404 Corpsman, 18D, or 68W with CMB. Part of this kit shall include 2 Day Hyper Realistic Moulage Kit based Makeup Course and a 3 Day TCCC Moulage support for up to 35 casualties. See RFQ.

## Summary

GENERAL INFORMATION Mowing, weed eating, & leaf/vegetation blowing landscaping services at the Darrington Ranger Station compound, Washington State. SCOPE OF WORK The Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest has a requirement for landscaping services on the Darrington Ranger District. The intent is to maintain approximately 3.6 acres located at the Darrington Ranger Station as directed by the USDA Forest Service. The Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, equipment, tools, transportation, and supplies to mow, weed eat, and blow plant material. PROJECT LOCATION The site is located at the Darrington Ranger Station within Snohomish County. See Attachment 3 - Project Location Map. The address is: 1405 Emens Ave. North, Darrington WA 98241. REQUEST FOR QUOTATION Refer to the attached RFQ for all requirments associated with this contract opportunity.

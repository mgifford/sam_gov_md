# Replace Triangle Pool Liner - Keesler Air Force Base

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-24 16:24:43.108-04
- Solicitation Number: MAHG251039

## Summary

This notice is NOT a solicitation for offers. No offers will be considered or accepted in response to this notice. The information requested is for market research only; responses are voluntary and will not be reimbursed. Nothing in this notice shall be considered to have obligated the Government to any agreement or expenditure of appropriated funds. Keesler AFB has a requirement to replace the pool liner, filter media, above ground pipes, and water heaters at building 7506. The work shall be done in accordance with the statement of work, specifications, and any other applicable documents. For more detailed information please review the attachments. PLEASE NOTE THAT THE ATTACHMENTS ARE DRAFTS. The government intends to issue a soliciation for a firm fixed price contract that will be set aside for small businesses. The market research results will assist in determining if further set asides should be used. The construction magnitude is between $250,000 and $500,000. Each response to this sources sought shall be a maximum of 3 pages and include the following information: Company name Address Point of Contact Phone Number Email Address Company's small business category (Small Business, Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB), Small Disadvantaged Business (SDB), 8(a), Historically Underutilized Business Zones (HUBZone), Service-Disabled Veteran Owned (SDVOSB). Brief statement of the company's interest in the project with details of the company's capability to complete a project of this type. Those interested shall respond to this sources sought no later than 1:00 PM CST on April 2nd 2026. Please email your response to Chance Daisy at chance.daisy@us.af.mil

# Bldg. 602 Access Control System (ACS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 11:11:32.252-04
- Solicitation Number: FA480026Q0024

## Summary

The contractor shall provide all management, transportation, tools, supplies, equipment, training and labor necessary to install, configure, and maintain a new Common Access Card (CAC)/Personal Identity Verification (PIV) card Access Control System (ACS) for ACC A589S, 204 Dodd Blvd Bldg. 602, Hampton, VA 23665 in a manner that shall ensure continuous and reliable service. Questions are due no later than 17 March 2026 at 1500, and responses will be provided by 1600 on 19 March 2026. Only one set of questions will be recorded and responded to per contractor so please ensure that your latest submission combines all of your previous questions (if *applicable). Site visit attendees must be prepared to submit the following: Full Name Date of Birth Citizenship ID Type State/Country of Issuance ID Number Real ID Compliance The Site Visit will take place on 16 March 2026. Please email the contract POCs for details. UPDATE 16 March 2026: The site visit has been canceled due to the impending severe weather in the area. The question and answer period remains the same; please submit your questions NLT 1600 on 17 March 2026; only the latest submission will be recorded so please ensure that succeeding emails also include your previous questions (if applicable).

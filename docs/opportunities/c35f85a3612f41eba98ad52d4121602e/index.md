# Grades 6-12 Reading Intervention Resources

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 11:04:58.739-04
- Solicitation Number: HE125426QE052

## Summary

DoWEA provides our learners in grades K-12 with an intervention program that (1) is research-based and aligned to the College and Career Ready Standards - Literacy (CCRSL), (2) provides college and career ready literacy skills via access to grade level appropriate complex text, appropriate blend of fiction and non-fiction material, and text dependent tasks, (3) integrates reading, writing, speaking and listening and (4) provides differentiated instruction to meet the diverse needs of students, DoWEA requires a 100% digital solution.

## Summary

COMBINED SYNOPSIS/SOLICITATION FOR MATERIALS AND TEXTILES LAB TENSILE TESTER This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; proposals are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. The Government reserves the right to award without discussions. This solicitation is expected to result in the award of a firm-fixed-price contract. The solicitation document, FA8606-26-Q-B0001 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ). This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The associated NAICS code is 334519. Small business size standard is: 600. This solicitation primarily incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. Notice to Offerors: The government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation, either before or after the closing date for receipt of quotations. In the event the government cancels this solicitation, the government has no obligation to reimburse an offeror for any costs. If funds are not available to fund the entire contract value, the Government reserves the right to re-evaluate quotations by unit price per item and issue an award on a SOME, NONE, or ALL basis for each individual item needed. Award will be made to the lowest price, technically acceptable, responsible offeror. Description of Requirement: The purpose of this combined synopsis and solicitation is for the purchase and delivery of the necessary labor, materials, equipment and tools required to provide and install one (1) Tensile Tester and remove the current tester (Instron 5584) in accordance with Attachment 1 – Salient Characteristics.

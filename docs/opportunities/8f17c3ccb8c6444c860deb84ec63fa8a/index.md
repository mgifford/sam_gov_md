# INTERNATIONAL REMEDIATION AND ENVIRONMENTAL SERVICES 3 (IRES3)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-13 15:37:21.597-04
- Solicitation Number: PANHES25P025184

## Summary

The objective of this Multiple Award Task Order Contract (MATOC) is for Contractors to perform Military Munitions Response (MMR) services involving Munitions and Explosives of Concern (MEC), Material Potentially Presenting an Explosive Hazard (MPPEH), Chemical Warfare Materiel (CWM), and Biological Warfare Materiel (BWM); Environmental Compliance and Remediation Services (ECRS) involving primarily Hazardous, Toxic and Radioactive Waste (HTRW) including Biological Waste and Munitions Constituents (MC); and Other Munitions- Related Services (OMRS) as necessary to permit lands and waters to be safely and efficiently used for their intended purpose.

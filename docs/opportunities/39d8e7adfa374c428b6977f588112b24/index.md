# Lava Fire Rangeland Fence Reconstruction

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 09:51:39.248-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LT26Q0030

## Summary

This project includes the removal and reconstruction of 3.5 miles of segmented interior Forest Service fence and Forest Service/BLM boundary fences on the Council Ranger District of the Payette National Forest. Fence construction will not be a contiguous 3.5 miles, but rather seven distinct segments of fence where either BLM lands border Forest Service lands, or interior Forest Service fences are needed. This project will include removal of all old fence material from the site and clearing vegetation and large woody debris as needed for the new fence line construction. The fence construction will consist of permanent, four strand, barbed wire, let down, livestock fence.

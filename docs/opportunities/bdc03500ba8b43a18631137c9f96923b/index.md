The Government is seeking to identify responsible vendors capable of providing a Matrix Assisted Laser Desorption Ionization Time of Flight Mass Spectrometry Biotyper System (MALDI TOF), brand name or equal, configured and supported as described below for use in organism identification in the Microbiology Laboratory at VANTHCS. II. Description of Requirement VANTHCS requires the purchase (outright equipment acquisition) of one Matrix Assisted Laser Desorption Ionization Time of Flight Mass Spectrometry Biotyper System (MALDI TOF) and associated operating software to support high throughput, rapid identification of bacteria, fungi, and mycobacteria in the Pathology and Laboratory Medicine Service Microbiology laboratory. The system will be installed on site and integrated with the existing Beckman Coulter Microscan 96 plus / LabPro MBT solution. Testing for bacteria must be FDA cleared for in vitro diagnostic use and classified as CLIA high or moderately complex testing.

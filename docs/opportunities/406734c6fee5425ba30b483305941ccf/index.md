# Army Intelligence Security Command (INSCOM) Mental Health Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 17:28:07.863-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0426RA018

## Summary

The Health Readiness Contract Office has a requirement from INSCOM to provide Mental Health Support Services. The contemplated contract is a re-procurement, previously awarded in fiscal year 2020. The resultant contract will provide specialized mental health support services to sustain the psychological readiness and operational effectiveness of personnel assigned to high-demand intelligence roles across geographically dispersed installations.

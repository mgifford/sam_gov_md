# Mobile Vehicle Barriers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 13:01:18.627-04
- Solicitation Number: FA441726Q0058

## Summary

The 1st Special Operations Wing at Hurlburt Field, Florida, is seeking quotations for the procurement of mobile vehicle barriers. This RFQ is issued to identify qualified vendors capable of providing reliable and effective mobile vehicle barriers to enhance security and control access across the installation. The required mobile vehicle barriers must be easily transportable, rapidly deployable, and meet the specific performance and technical specifications outlined in the attachments. Please refer to the attached documents for detailed information regarding the submission process, requirements, and all relevant deadlines. For comprehensive details on the statement of work, and terms and conditions, please see the attachments to this request.Funds are not presently available for the effort. No award will be made under this solicitation until funds are available. This solicitation is being posted in accordance with FAR Subpart 16.5, Section

16.505 Ordering. The Government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation, either before or after the closing date. In the event the Government cancels this solicitation, the Government has no obligation to reimburse an offeror for any costs.

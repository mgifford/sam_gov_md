# OIG parking in DC

- Agency: TREASURY, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 16:00:09.796-04
- Solicitation Number: SS-OIG-26-024

## Summary

INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE ABM INDUSTRY GROUPS, LLC - 40 law enforcement Parking Spaces w/24/7 Access SS-OIG-26-024 The Treasury Office of Inspector General (TOIG) Office of Investigations (OI) requires 40 monthly parking spaces for 40 Law Enforcement Vehicles with 24/7 facility access and the ability of unlimited overnight parking. The Bureau of Fiscal Service intends to make an award on a sole source basis to ABM Industry Groups, LLC, 14141 Southwest Fwy Ste 477 Sugarland, TX 77478-4630. The access and use of Forty (40) monthly parking spaces; forty (40) Gas/Diesel with the minimum Level 1 and Level 2 capabilities. To properly care and help maintain an operational safety level, the Fleet requires readily available and use of a Tire air station, which require a robust need of frequent preventive maintenance and checks/top-offs. Subject to final approval, The Treasury Common Services Center (TCSC), on behalf of the TOIG/OI, intends to negotiate a sole-source procurement in accordance with FAR 6.302-1(a)(2), to ABM Industry Groups, LLC, 14141 Southwest Fwy Ste 477 Sugarland, TX 77478-4630 for forty (40) parking spaces located at 1400 K Street NW, Washington DC 20005.

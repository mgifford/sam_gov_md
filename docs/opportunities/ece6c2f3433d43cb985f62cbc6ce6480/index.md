RFI Title: Hillrom Progressa+ ICU Beds for the Defense Health Agency (DHA) Medical Treatment Facility (MTF). LOCATION: Naval Medical Center Portsmouth, 620 John Paul Jones Circle, Critical Care Unit, Portsmouth, VA 23708. BACKGROUND: The Naval Medical Center Portsmouth (NMCP) Critical Care Unit has a need to replace 3 of their patient beds near life expectancy with an upgraded bed with features that the Hillrom Progressa+ ICU Beds and accessories can provide.

## Summary

**Amendment 4** The purpose of this amendment is to incorporate a revised FAR clause 52.219-14 Limitations on Subcontracting (FEB2023) (DEVIATION 2021-O0008) to the COMBO. As a result, the document "COMBO -Beluga Whale EOD Escort_Rev 2_20260306 now supercedes all previous versions. The key change replaces the previous subcontracting clause and clarifies at FAR 52.219-14(f)(1), that quoters must demonstrate how they will meet the required limitations on subcontracting by the end of the base period and again by the end of each subsequent option period. **Amendment 4 End** **Amendment 3** The purpose of this amendment is to incorporate a revised Performance Work Statement (PWS), which is included as Attachment 1 (EOD PWS REV 2 20260302). **Amendment 3 End** **Amendment 2** The purpose of this amendment is to add the clause DAFFARS 5352.201-9101 Ombudsman to the combo in full text. **Amendment 2 End** **Amendment 1** The purpose of this amendment is to publish the official Questions and Answers (Q&A) and incorporate the revised Performance Work Statement (PWS), which is included as Attachment 1 (EOD PWS REV 1 20260226). **Amendment 1 End** Please refer to the attached Combo and accompanying attachments.

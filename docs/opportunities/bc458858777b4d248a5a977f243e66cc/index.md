# O-Club CCTV

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 19:36:28.191-04
- Solicitation Number: FA520926Q0026

## Summary

This is a presolicitation notice for the purchase and installation of CCTV cameras for Yokota Air Base Officers Club. The scope of the requirement is as follows. Proccurement of 36 security cameras and peripheral equipment such as monitoring units, recording units and wall mounts . The contractor is responisble for procuring all nececassry items , components and wiring for the installtion of the CCTV's. Delivery of the items to the customers designated location at Yokota Air Base Japan. Removal of existing CCTV infrastructure. Installation of CCTV system User Training 10 year warranty All offerors are required to have a Japanese Cage Code for product maintenance purposes. Note 1: This project will be performed in its entirety in the country of Japan. The successful offeror must be licensed and registered to perform work in the country of Japan. Note 2: Funds are not presently available for this effort. No award will be made under this solicitation until funds are available.

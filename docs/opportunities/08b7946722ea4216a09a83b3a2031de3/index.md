# SOLE SOURCE AMERICAN STRENGTHS CENTER TRAINING FOR TRAINERS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 16:58:37.599-05
- Solicitation Number: N0040626QS175

## Summary

THIS IS A FIRM FIXED-PRICE SERVICE TYPE CONTRACT NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound is negotiating a SOLE SOURCE requirement for the procurement AMERICAN STRENGTHS CENTER LLC training for trainers. Commander Navy Installations Command (CNIC) Force Chaplain has vetted and approved a range of programs and curricula to provide a standardized product inventory for Chaplain's Religious Enrichment Development Operation (CREDO) programming. Commander Naval Forces Japan and Commander Navy Region Japan require a Training for Trainers event that complies with OPNAVIST 1738.1 and CNIC 1738.1 to certify Religious Ministry Teams to lead retreats and workshops with AMERICAN STRENGTHS CENTER LLC's strengths curriculum. This is a noncompetitive contract action lacking competition as AMERICAN STRENGTHS CENTER LLC is the only source per FAR 12.102(a). An offeror and its product and service must meet a qualification requirement in order to be eligible for award. The office from which the qualification requirement may be obtained is the Commander, Navy Installations Command (CNIC) Force Chaplain. AMERICAN STRENGTHS CENTER LLC is the only source authorized to perform this training per the CREDO program. See the attached solicitation. Closes on 10 March 2026 at 10:00 AM PST. The government will evaluate the offeror's price for reasonableness and past performance as part of the responsibiltiy determination. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. The anticipated award date is 12 March 2026. All questions regarding this solicitation should be submitted to Roger Bowers, roger.s.bowers.civ@us.navy.mil.

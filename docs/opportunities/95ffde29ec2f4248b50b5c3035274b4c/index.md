# Microwave Electronics Systems and Microsystems Engineering Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-06 14:06:48.205-05
- Solicitation Number: N0017326REM01

## Summary

The Advanced Concepts Group of the Radar Division of the Naval Research Laboratory (NRL) seeks contractor technical assistance in the highly specialized areas of Radio Frequency (RF) components and systems, electronic and microelectronics systems design and subsystems integration, digital signal processing and embedded microprocessors, related signal processing hardware and software, antennas and electromagnetics, wireless systems testing and demonstrations, and other engineering specialties. Tasks will be performed at the contractor's facilities, NRL-DC, NRL Chesapeake Bay Detachment at Chesapeake Beach, MD, NRL Blossom Point Test Facility, and at other field sites. Some work may be performed aboard ship or aircraft. The work to be performed is further described in the attached SOW, titled 26REM01 Draft SOW. Current Contract: N0017322F3003, Envisioneering, Inc. Contractor Response Format: NRL asks that interested parties, at a minimum, supply the following information via email: Contact Information a) Name of Company and Point of Contact (POC) with associated contact information b) Business Size/Socioeconomic Categories c) Unique Entity ID and/or CAGE Code Technical Information a) List of capabilities/resources relevant to the attached draft SOW b) Prior experience of similar scope/complexity of tasking in the attached draft SOW c) Status of your firm's current facility clearance d) Any questions/suggestions related to the attached draft SOW Responses should be emailed to the Contract Specialist, Elizabeth Maranto, at elizabeth.m.maranto.civ@us.navy.mil. No telephonic responses will be accepted. NOTE: THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION. This is a Sources Sought Notice (SSN) for planning purposes only, and is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government, nor will the Government pay for information solicited. Since this is a SSN, no evaluation letters and/or results will be issued to the respondents. The Government is not committed to issuing a solicitation or awarding a contract pursuant to this announcement. No solicitation package is available at this time. (End of SSN)

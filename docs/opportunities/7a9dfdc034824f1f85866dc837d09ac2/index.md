# Lease of one 25-ton heavy forklift for TLSC- E/A

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 08:57:44.513-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV26QA028

## Summary

The 409th CSB, Theater Contracting Center is publishing this presolicitation for the lease of one 25-ton capacity forklift, without operator, to support TLSC-E/ A and Ammunition Center Europe (ACE) operations at Miesau Ammo Depot, Bruchmuehlbach-Miesau (Germany). The forklift will be used for the safe handling, movement, and staging of heavy ammunition containers and related materials in support of ongoing logistics and training missions. All vendors who have questions, comments and are interested in providing the lease of the equipment as specified in the Attachment – Draft SOW Lease Heavy Forklift- TLSC- E 20 MAR, should submit their interest via email to: Ms. Coralia Kelly, Contract Specialist Email: coralia.a.kelly.ln@army.mil Ms. Judy Biboum, Contracting Officer Email: judy.i.biboum.civ@army.mil Please include the title " W564KV26QA028- Lease of one 25-ton heavy forklift " in the subject line of the request, and include the following information: Company name and address Cage Code Company business size by NAICS code Point of contact for questions/clarifications Name Telephone number email address The Government will not return any information submitted in response to this notice. All questions must be in writing.

# Air Force Reserve Officers’ Training Corps Instruction Support Services (AFRISS). Maxwell AFB, AL

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-23 11:49:49.598-04
- Solicitation Number: FA330026Q0320

## Summary

The 42d Contracting Squadron, Maxwell Air Force Base AFB, AL, is seeking a non-personal services contract to provide Air Force Reserve Officers’ Training Corps Instruction Support Services (AFRISS). All feedback and information received may be used to determine the appropriate acquisition strategy. The Government is seeking information from industry to assist in determining what types of products and capabilities are available in the market for this type of requirement outlined in the attached DRAFT PERFORMANCE WORK STATEMENT (PWS). NOTES OF IMPORTANCE: 1. A solicitation does not exist for this requirement. This is only a sources sought synopsis, so please do not submit proposals. The Government does not intend to award a contract on the basis of this sources sought. 2. If the Government decides to pursue a solicitation, portions of your responses to this sources sought may be incorporated into the final solicitation. Respondents are discouraged from submitting any information deemed competition-sensitive or proprietary. 3. Respondents will not be reimbursed for any costs incurred in responding to this sources sought. Information submitted will not be returned to the respective respondents. 4. Oral submissions of information will not be considered in response to this sources sought. To assist the Government in developing a potential acquisition strategy, please provide the following information: 1. GENERAL INFORMATION: a. Company name, address, point of contact, voice and fax telephone numbers and email address. b. Type of business, (i.e., small, small disadvantaged, woman-owned, Hub Zone, serviced-disabled veteran-owned small business, large business, etc.), under North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) 611699, which contains a size standard of $15.0M. CAGE Code and Unique Entity Identifier (UEI). c. Under which NAICS code does your company usually provide the objectives described above. Please provide rationale for your answer if different from NAICS 611430. d. Please describe any customary commercial contract terms or conditions that you feel would make any resulting contract more effective if applicable. 2. CAPABILITIES: The Government is seeking a non-personal services contract to provide Air Force Reserve Officers’ Training Corps Instruction Support Services (AFRISS). Please address the following regarding your capabilities in meeting the requirements: Can your company meet the requirement by providing a non-personal services contract to provide Air Force Reserve Officers’ Training Corps Instruction Support Services (AFRISS)? If so, please address the following in your company’s response. i. Provide documentation sufficient to demonstrate the capability to recruit and provide qualified Associate Professor of Aerospace Studies (APAS) Instructors. ii. Provide documentation sufficient to demonstrate the capability to perform the services as outlined in the DRAFT PWS para.’s 1.4.1-1.4.18. 3. RESPONSES: E-mail responses to this sources sought to the points of contact identified below. The due date and time for responses to this sources sought is at 12:00 P.M. Central Standard Time on 27 March 2026.

## Summary

Additional Info NAICS = 333517 FSC = 3408 Location of Work: Coronado, CA 92118 NO SET ASIDE EXPECTED DLA Aviation Richmond intends to issue a solicitation to procure (1) Computer Numerically Controlled (CNC) Horizontal Boring & Milling Machine. Complete technical data package VIBB 26-40-1005 including minimum requirements for the CNC Horizontal Boring & Milling Machine as well as training, technical data, IUID tags, shipping, foundation, removal of existing equipment, rigging, and installation will be included as a part of the solicitation. Request for Quote SPE4A8-26-Q-0021will be issued with this synopsis as a combined synopsis and solicitation for 45 days from April 24, 2026, and closing on June 8, 2026. FOB will be at Destination. Inspection at Origin/Acceptance at Destination. The solicitation will be Unrestricted with Other Than Full and Open Competition. Award shall result in a Firm, Fixed Price Contract. Please email the solicitation package along with the compliance statement and descriptive literature to Christina Gibbs at Christina.Gibbs@dla.mil. This solicitation and award will be made under FAR 13.5 procedures. A site visit is intended as part of the solicitation. Fleet Readiness Center- Southwest (FRCSW) Building 472, 1150 1st St. Coronado, CA 92118 *Site visit date/time will be available as part of the solicitation. A copy of the solicitation will be available via the DLA DIBBS website at https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil/Solicitations/ Solicitations are in Portable Document Format (PDF). To download and view these documents, use the latest version of Adobe Acrobat Reader. This software is available free at http://www.adobe.com. A paper or e-mail copy of the solicitation may be provided to requestors on a case by case basis if access to DIBBS is not available.”

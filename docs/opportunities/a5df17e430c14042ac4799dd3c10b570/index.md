## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD A SOLE SOURCE This Notice of Intent is not a request for competitive proposals. This is not a solicitation or request for offers. No solicitation package is available and telephone requests will not be honored. Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. FWS DIVISION OF MIGRATORY BIRDS intends to award a sole source purchase order to ORNITELA UAB to furnish: Ornitela Ornitrack 2.8, 6.0, and

10.0 GPS/GSM transmitters Requirements: Ornitela Ornitrack 2.8, 6.0, and

10.0 GPS/GSM transmitters are capable of recording locations with an accuracy of ~10m. Each transmitter collects locations, as frequently as once every minute (but programmable to be less often) and stores these data until the bird flies past a Cell Tower and then it uploads these locations for transmission back to the owner of the transmitter. All transmitters will have front and rear attachment tubes, and the 6.0 and 10.0 transmitters will also have a reinforced antenna. Deliverables: 60 Ornitela Ornitrack 2.8, 10 OrniTrack 6.0, and 10 Ornitrack

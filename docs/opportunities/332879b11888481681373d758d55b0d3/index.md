# FTQW261406 Replace Upper Dgst Compressors

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 18:25:12.689-05
- Solicitation Number: 261406

## Summary

The 354th Eielson Contracting Squadron is seeking qualification packages for A&E services for the project below: The work included in the scope of A-E Title I Services (Design Phase Services) for this design project encompasses design of the replacement of the upper digester compressors 1 & 2; assessment of the existing compressors, piping, and all related appurtenances and devices; collecting/reviewing project data and records; evaluation of alternatives; developing design concepts and cost estimates; attendance and participation in review meetings; and making revisions requested by the Government. The contractor is expected to submit intermediate design documents and ultimately produce a complete design based on specified requirements and comments received during the 65%, 95%, and 100% design review meetings. This information will be utilized to prepare comprehensive drawings, schedules, specifications, and cost estimates for all facets of work and disciplines/trades involved in the construction project. The primary objective is to develop a biddable set of construction documents tailored to address the unique challenges presented by the extreme Arctic conditions at Eielson AFB, AK. Architect and Engineering design services are not unique requirements only procured by the military. The contract schedule set forth in the statement of work requires a delivery of 100% in 125 calendar days. If this is something your firm is interested in and can accomplish, please submit a combined Instructions to Offers (Evaluation Factors Document), SF-330 (Architect-Engineer Qualification-Standard Form 330), and a PPQ (Past Performance Questionnare).

# Sole source award - OMAX

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-18 15:46:05.339-04
- Solicitation Number: M67004-25-Q-0100

## Summary

THIS IS A NOTICE OF SOLE SOURCE CONTRACT AWARD. The Government has awarded a firm-fixed-price, performance-based, non-personal services contract on a sole source basis for the inspection, maintenance, and repair of an Omax waterjet system in support of Marine Corps Logistics Command (MARCORLOGCOM), Marine Corps Logistics Base Barstow (MCLBB). The requirement includes periodic service to address system leaks and restore the equipment to full operational capability. All work is to be performed in Building B573, CWC 241, near Column F30. The contractor shall conduct diagnostics, repairs, and functional testing to ensure the system meets original manufacturer specifications. Upon completion of maintenance and successful operational testing, the contractor shall provide operator and maintenance training to designated Government personnel to ensure proper operation, routine maintenance, and basic troubleshooting of the system. The statutory authority for this sole source award is 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1), as implemented by FAR 6.302-1, Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements. This notice is provided for informational purposes only. No solicitation was issued, and no additional awards will be made as a result of this notice.

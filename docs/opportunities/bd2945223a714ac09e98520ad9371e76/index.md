# 2026 NSWC Port Hueneme Maritime Advanced Technology Accelerator Consortium (MATAC) OTA Virtual Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:40:11.379-04

## Summary

MATAC Members, NSWC Port Hueneme Division (PHD), is excited to announce the inaugural Maritime Advanced Technology Accelerator Consortium (MATAC) Virtual Industry Day event! Our consortium manager Advanced Technology International (ATI) will be hosting the virtual event with additional details to follow on matac.org. To become a MATAC member submit your request at matac.org Please mark your calendars for this unique opportunity to engage with PHD and collaborate with government requirement holders. Event: MATAC May Virtual Industry Day Date: May 20th, 2026 Location: Online, Virtual Event This event will feature topic presentations and discussions relevant to the MATAC technology areas. The registration, agenda, and specific topics will be shared soon, so stay tuned for more details! A highlight of the Industry Day will be virtual one-on-one sessions between industry representatives and government requirement holders, providing a valuable platform for direct engagement and collaboration. We look forward to your participation in this exciting event. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at MATAC@ati.org.

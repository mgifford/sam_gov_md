## Summary

The Bureau of Reclamation, Hoover Dam, has a requirement to inspect, test, clean, repair, rewind, resurface, and transport generator Armatures in accordance with the Electrical Apparatus Service Association (EASA) Standard AR100 2015. The work applies to static voltage regulator rotating exciters (“Armatures”) used on Hoover Dam’s generators. Work includes all required measurements, diagnostics, repair activities, rewinding, balancing, machining, commutator resurfacing, engraving/etching, testing, documentation, and reporting. This requirement Place of Performance • Pickup/Return Location: Hoover Dam Central Warehouse Nevada State Route 172 Approximately 8 miles east of Boulder City, NV (Escort and site access provided by Government) • Contractor Facility Requirements: Must be EASA-certified to perform rotating electrical apparatus services, including rewinding Armatures.

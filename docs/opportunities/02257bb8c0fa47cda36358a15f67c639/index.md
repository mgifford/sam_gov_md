# Tactical Radio Repair Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 14:31:43.914-04
- Solicitation Number: FA483026Q0017

## Summary

Moody AFB is seeking potential sources capable of the required evaluation and repair to restore ten (10) L3Harris Radios to full operational capability in accordance with Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) specifications for the following models: (2) PRC-117G 7 (3) PRC-152A (1) PRC-160 (4) PRC-117G Key Repairs: Broken screen Power cycle failure Failure to power on Failure to fill type 1 Back key fill Stuck on type 1 Crypto tamper alert Crypto alarm Required Certifications: Must be a L3Harris Certified Repair Technician to not void the manufacture guarantee. Firms that respond shall provide clear evidence that they can meet or exceed the specifications. The purpose of this post is to seek sources for market research purposes and no contract or obligation from the government will come of it. Please submit all information to ashley.castillo_flynn@us.af.mil. If you have any questions, you may email or call (229)-257-4717.

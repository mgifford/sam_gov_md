## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office (NCO) 21, has a requirement for Ice Machine Cleaning and Sanitizing Services. Contractor shall provide Ice machine Cleaning and Sanitizing Services for VA Central California Health Care System (VACCHCS) in accordance with the scope of work contained herein. The solicitation is set aside to SDVOSBs. The North American Industry Classification Systems (NAICS) is 811310, size standard $12.5M. Contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) at www.sam.gov to be eligible for award. Carefully read FAR 52.212-1 Instructions to Offerors and VAAR 852.273-73 Evaluation - Health-Care Resources in order to respond to this RFQ. Failure to do so may eliminate your response from being considered for award. IAW FAR 16.504, the Government intends to award a Single Award Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Contract as a result of this solicitation. Questions: Please submit any questions or inquiries regarding this solicitation to Lacy.Morgan@va.gov at least three (3) days prior to the end date of the solicitation with the subject line 36C26126Q0267 - Question. Questions will be answered via an amendment to the solicitation, which is posted on SAM.gov. Important: Vendors are responsible for checking sam.gov for amendments and must sign and acknowledge all amendments to the solicitation.

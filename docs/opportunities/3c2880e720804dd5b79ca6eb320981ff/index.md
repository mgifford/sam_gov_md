# K-12 PHYSICAL EDUCATION TEACHER RESOURCES

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 08:39:04.342-04
- Solicitation Number: HE125426QE027

## Summary

DoDEA requires 100% digital teacher resources, formative assessments, and related professional learning aligned with the national standards. Teacher resources shall include strategies that promote rigorous instruction, address potential challenges, and differentiate instruction. The formative assessments shall provide data on which to tailor instruction.

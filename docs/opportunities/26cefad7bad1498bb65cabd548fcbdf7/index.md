# United States Space Force (USSF) Human Resources Information Technology (HRIT) Guardian Automation Layer (GAL) Request for Information

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-08 12:41:32.267-04
- Solicitation Number: FA2553-HRIT-GAL-RFI

## Summary

The United States Space Force (USSF) is seeking information on how an interested contractor could provide the user interface and automation layer of a military Human Resources Information Technology (HRIT) system that is easy to use for both Guardians and Human Resource (HR) Practitioners. In support of this effort, USSF is interested in gathering industry feedback on a phased approach to incrementally deliver military HR and Talent Management capabilities in accordance with the Personnel Management Act. Interested parties are invited to respond to this request for information (RFI) by completing the accompanying “SRD_Response_Spreadsheet.xlsx” and an unclassified white paper as outlined in the attached HRIT GAL RFI document. Oral communications are not permissible. No telephonic communications will be permitted.

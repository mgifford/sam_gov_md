## Summary

This is a presolicitation notice only. The VA is currently NOT requesting proposals, quotes, or bids for this project. This solicitation will be 100% set-aside for small business. Solicitation 36C26026R0050 for project 663A4-26-001 AMLK Striping Base + 4 for the Puget Sound VA Medical Center American Lake campus is anticipated to be released to Contracting Opportunities on or about 2/24/2026. Drawings and specifications will be posted for review as attachments to the solicitation. Proposals will be due approximately 30-45 days after the solicitation is posted. Period of Performance: The intended contract period of performance is 365 calendar days after receipt of notice to proceed. Description of Services: The objective of this acquisition is for Restriping the Parking Lot Lines at the VA Puget Sound Health Care System American Lake campus. The Contractor shall clean and restripe all parking lots, roads, crosswalks, parking garages, speed bumps, hash marks, bus stops, and other painted road markings for the American Lake campus. IAW VAAR 836.204, the magnitude of this construction is between $1,000,000 and $5,000,000. NAICS Code: 237310, Highway, Street, and Bridge Construction and the small business size standard is $19 million. A solicitation is not currently available. This announcement is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government, implied or otherwise, to issue a solicitation or to award a contract.

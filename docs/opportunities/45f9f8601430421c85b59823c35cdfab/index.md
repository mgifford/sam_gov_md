# PREMISE WIRING B145

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 16:03:42.42-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462626Q0009

## Summary

Engineer, furnish, install, and test (EFI&T) a premises wiring upgrade to new plenum-rated Cat-6 and OM4 multimode (MM) fiber cabling for Building 145 (B145) at Malmstrom AFB, MT. The Cat-6 and OM4 MM fiber drop layouts are shown in Attachment A, Layout Drawings. The upgrade shall include installation and replacement of communications (comms) racks/cabinets, installation of new AC power with power distribution unit (PDU), and comms rack/cabinet grounding. The Contractor shall remove all existing outdated and abandoned-in-place (AIP) Cat-3/5 and MM fiber cabling, along with any residual materials, from the building and dispose of them accordingly. Amendment 0001: 1. A site visit has been scheduled for April 7th 2026 at 10:00 MST. The site visit will begin at the contracting office front lobby located 7015 Goddard Dr., Bldg. 145. Malmstrom AFB, MT 59402. 2. To Extend the Solicitation from April 6th 2026 to April 10th 2026 1:00 PM MST See Attached SOO for full details.

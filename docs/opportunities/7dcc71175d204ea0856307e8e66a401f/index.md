# National Laboratory of the Rockies - Landscaping Maintenance, Irrigation System Maintenance, and Irrigation System Minor Repairs

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 12:43:29.284-04
- Solicitation Number: RFP-2026029330

## Summary

The primary objective of landscaping and grounds maintenance at National Laboratory of the Rockies (NLR) is to preserve and maintain its campus open space areas to minimize the negative impacts on the environment and maximize the efficient use of resources to enhance the native and naturalized vegetation communities and wildlife habitat. The Subcontractor shall assist NLR’s Site Operations Team to provide various landscaping and irrigation services in the cultivation of both established and improvement project landscape areas. Landscaping and grounds maintenance activities shall be performed using environmentally responsible and sustainable practices, including water-efficient irrigation methods, integrated pest management, soil health preservation, and the use of native or climate-adapted plant species where feasible. See Attached SOW and RFP Exhibit A for responsive criteria and performance requirements.

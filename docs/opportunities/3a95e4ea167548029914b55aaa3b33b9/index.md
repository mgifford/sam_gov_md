# Purchase of Two Passenger Vehicles for U.S. Embassy Singapore

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 03:17:26.093-04
- Solicitation Number: 19SN1026Q0018

## Summary

This notice is for informational and planning purposes only and does not constitute a Request for Quotation (RFQ). The solicitation is not available at this time. The U.S. Embassy Singapore will post the RFQ on the System for Award Management (SAM) website when it becomes available. The U.S. Department of State, U.S. Embassy Singapore, intends to issue a solicitation for the purchase of two (2) passenger vehicles. Vehicles shall meet all applicable Singaporean regulatory requirements, which may include but are not limited to safety, emissions, and registration standards. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, which will be considered. The Government may award the contract based on the initial offer without discussion. The resultant contract will be a firm-fixed-price contract. SAM Registration Requirement: All contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) prior to submitting a quotation in accordance with FAR 52.204-7. Interested vendors are strongly encouraged to register as soon as possible. The guidelines for registration in SAM are also available at: https://www.fsd.gov/gsafsd_sp Legal and Regulatory Requirements: Performance of this contract will take place in Singapore. Prospective vendors must possess all required local licenses, permits, and authorizations to operate legally in Singapore. Contractors will also be responsible for complying with all applicable local labor, safety, and tax laws, and must ensure that any such obligations are satisfied prior to contract performance. If vehicles are imported, the contractor shall be responsible for ensuring that the vehicles meet all applicable Singapore regulatory requirements and are eligible for registration and lawful operation in Singapore at the time of delivery. This acquisition will include DOSAR clause 652.242-73 – Authorization and Performance (AUG 1999). Electronic Submission/Responses: Electronic submission requirements will be provided in the RFQ when issued. Other information Vendors must comply with all Singapore legal and regulatory requirements. The following links are provided for informational purposes only and do not constitute additional pre-registration requirements: www.acra.gov.sg/home https://www.mof.gov.sg/policies/taxes/goods-and-services-tax The Government reserves the right to cancel this notice and/or issue amendments at any time.

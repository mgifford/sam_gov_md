# Overhaul and Testing of OA-9243A(V)3/SR Antenna Tilting Group

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-16 15:49:12.872-04
- Solicitation Number: N4215826Q0011

## Summary

Norfolk Naval Shipyard (NNSY) is looking for a contractor to provide complete overhaul, upgrades, and testing of OA-9243A(V)3/SR Antenna Tilting Groups (ATG); completely dissemble ATG, perform repairs, paint/upgrade to powder coating components, provide upgraded parts for brakes, limit switches, gear box, bumper stops, and junction box as applicable. All powder coating / paint operations shall be in accordance with (NSI) 009-32 (2.3). The requirement will be solicited as full and open competititon. The Period of Performance for this acquistion is to be determined.

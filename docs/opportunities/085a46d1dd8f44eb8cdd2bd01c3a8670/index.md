# Façade/cladding washing of Embassy buildings and exterior window cleaning of Embassy buildings

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 02:11:42.711-04
- Solicitation Number: 19-AM10-26-Q-0005

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy Yerevan has issued a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the provision of cleaning services for stone cladding and exterior windows of Embassy buildings on April 14, 2026. The solicitation package is available for download on the Embassy website at Embassy services announcements - U.S. Embassy in Armenia (also attached). Quotations must be submitted no later than May 4, 2026, at 17:00 (local Yerevan time). Late submissions will not be accepted. All proposals must be submitted in English. Incomplete proposals will not be considered.

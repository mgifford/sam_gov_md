## Summary

Due to location conditions discovered during the site visit, this solicitation will be amended to include an updated PWS and pictures of the site are incorporated for those that were unable to participate in the site visit. The Contractor shall provide the 402 Commodities Maintenance Group (CMXG), Robins Air Force Base (AFB), GA all labor, materials, transportation (drop-off and pick-up), and oversight needed to support the lease of a 40-ton AC Unit located outside B185 to support the Conformal Fuel Tank (CFT) shop.

# Medical Carts with BCMA Mounting

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-23 11:34:53.891-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25526Q0352

## Summary

The Saint Louis VA Healthcare System (STLVAHCS) intends to enhance its Bar Code Medication Administration (BCMA) equipment system by adding additional equipment to meet their outpatient clinic orders. This includes purchasing new medication carts as integrated units with mounting capability for computers, monitors, keyboards, card readers, scanners, mice, and 5-year batteries.

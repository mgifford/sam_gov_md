## Summary

USDA-SEA DNA Extraction and Genotyping Services for Cotton This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0318 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for DNA Extraction and Genotyping Services for Cotton in support of United States Department of Agriculture – Southeast Area. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 541380. The small business size standard is $19 million. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

# Ground Maintenance and Tree Assessment USAG Wiesbaden

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 10:25:15.483-04

## Summary

****Amendment 0001, 15 April 2026, the solicitation has been amended to extend the solicitation deadline to April 27, 2026, provide the final copy of the PWS, provide updated pricebooks and respond to questions asked by contractors. Please refer to the attachments for updated documents. The Regional Contracting Office intends to competitively award a Firm Fixed price (FFP) contract for ground maintenance and tree assessment in USAG Wiesbaden, Germany. The Contractor shall inspect, service, maintain, and landscape grounds of USAG Wiesbaden. The contract will consist of a five (5) years Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Single Award. Any amendments related to the RFQ will be posted to www.Sam.gov. The previous contract number is W912CM-20-D-0002, awarded for the sum of $4,831,106.75. IMPORTANT: Quoters must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at time of submission of quotes. For registration instructions visit the following website: www.sam.gov NOTE: All dates and times refer to Central European Time.

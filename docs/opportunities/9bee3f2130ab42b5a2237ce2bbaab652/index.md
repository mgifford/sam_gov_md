## Summary

Amendment 1: The purpose of this amendment is to update the Salient Characteristics to remove "The Remote Chiller Evaporator and Install Kit" and to extend the quotes due date until Monday, 23 March 2026 at 11:00 am MDT. CLIN 0001 Trane Chiller Equipment Brand Name Only, Part #CU-1 Model CGAM - 4 EACH (includes delivery) CLIN 0002 Start Up Trane Rep - 4 EACH. Must have a value as Prime coordinates the start up after the chillers have been prepared by the Government (Start up occurs within a year of the delivery, depending on weather conditions). For example, if Chillers are delivered in October, it takes about 2-3 months to get the chiller prepared for installation. Once the Government installs with appropriate weather (likely March of the following year), then Prime coordinates the start up schedule with Trane. Contract will remain active until after all start ups are completed.

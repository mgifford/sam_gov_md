# Regional Military Protection (RMP) Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 09:51:25.77-04
- Solicitation Number: FA857626Q0001

## Summary

AMD01 09 March 2026: Addtional questions were added to the "GOVERNMENT QUESTIONS FOR INDUSTRY " section of the attached. The Air Force Life Cycle Management Center is hosting an Industry Day to actively investigate innovative methods for delivering Regional Military Protection (RMP) capabilities to the warfighter. This industry day will facilitate a discussion with interested industry partners to assess the feasibility of embedding this technology directly into M-Code compliant weapon systems—an approach that could offer a powerful, jam-resistant advantage. The objective is to foster a collaborative discussion, explore potential partnerships, and define a clear path forward by outlining the technical, logistical, and programmatic requirements for integrating this future capability. Event Details: Date: 17 March 2026 Address: Aerospace Facility / Teams 2310 E El Segundo Blvd, El Segundo, CA 90245 Time: 0900 PT All vendors intending to participate must submit their registration, including complete attendee details (name, email, phone number, company), no later than 5:00 PM EST on March 8, 2026 to the listed Government point of contact. To accommodate all interested parties and facilitate focused engagement, each vendor's attendance is capped at five (5) representatives, encompassing prime contractors and any associated subcontractors. Walk-in will not be accepted. We value your partnership and insights as we work to enhance our capability delivery processes. For any questions, please brookelyn.anderson.3@us.af.mil.

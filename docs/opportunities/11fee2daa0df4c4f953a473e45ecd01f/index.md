# Reline Fire Water Storage Tank 558

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-03-20 12:14:36.015-04

## Summary

SOLICITATION INFORMATION 1. This Request for Proposal (RFP) is being solicited in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) Part 19.502-2 Total Small Business Set-Asides and the Partnership Agreement between the Department of Defense and the U.S. Small Business Administration. Competition under the FAR part 6.2 - Full and Open Competition After Exclusion of Sources – this Requirement has been 100% Set-Aside for Small Business Concerns. 2. The purpose of the Request for Proposal (RFP) is to procure construction services to Reline the Fire Water Tank at Building 558 in the Ordnance Area, in support of Naval Weapons Station (NWS) Earle. Services are to be performed at Colts Neck and Leonardo, New Jersey. See Section 00 21 16 Instructions to Proposers for more details. 3. One (1) Site Visit is scheduled for Tuesday, 7 April 2026 at 1000 hours EST. All interested contractors must send a list of attendees NLT 1000 EST Friday, April 3rd per the instructions located in Section 00 21 16 and 00 21 00. PLACE OF PERFORMANCE: Naval Weapons Station Earle: 201 NJ-34, Colts Neck, NJ 07722

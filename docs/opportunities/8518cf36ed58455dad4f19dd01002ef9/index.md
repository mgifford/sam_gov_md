# ACC-Redstone PALT Session

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 10:30:24.223-04
- Solicitation Number: W58RGZ-ACCREDSTONE-PALTSESSION-RSVP

## Summary

Event Title: Army Contracting Command-Redstone (ACC-RSA) PALT Session Event Date and Time: Tuesday, 19 May 2026, 0900-1130 CST Session Type: Industry Collaboration and Procurement Strategy Discussion Attendees: US Army Procurement Officials Industry Partners (Contractors, Suppliers, Service Providers) Acquisition and Contracting Officers ACC-RSA has scheduled its next Procurement Acquisition Lead Time (PALT) session for Tuesday, 19 May 2026, starting at 9:00 am and ending at 11:30 am CST. This session will be a continuation of the ACC-Redstone Executive Director's intent to be transparent in our dealings with industry and to provide members of industry the opportunity to ask questions of the Senior Contracting Officials and ACC-Redstone's Mission Partners in support of the various portfolios supported by ACC-Redstone at Redstone Arsenal, Alabama. The session will include ACC-Redstone, PAE Maneuver Air, PAE Fires, SMDC and DEVCOM AvMC, ACC-Redstone Senior Leaders, and program management Senior Leaders. In an attempt to reach as wide of an industry base as possible, we are going to open this session as hybrid event (BOTH In-Person and Hybrid-MS TEAMS Option). It will be held in the Bob Jones Auditorium in the Sparkman Center on Redstone Arsenal. In order to attend, individuals must R.S.V.P. using the E-Invitation link NLT 8 May 2026. Please remember to identify whether you will be attending in person, or if you will participate via MS Teams. The MS Teams link will be sent to registrants 24-48 hours prior to the session. We are required to provide the list of personnel needing access to Redstone Arsenal at least 1 week prior to the day of the event, so if you don’t already have access to the installation, you MUST provide your First Name, Middle Initial, and Last Name with the registration. Directions/access information to the Sparkman Center/Bob Jones Auditorium will be provided after registration is completed. If you elect to submit an inquiry, we ask that you pose a specific question, vice simply listing a program acronym or name. This will greatly facilitate our Contracting Team in providing focused, accurate answers during the session. We will first address the questions we receive in advance, and with the remaining time, open the floor for questions. Thus, to ensure we do address your most pressing contract related questions, we recommend you submit them in advance. Information to be provided by the Government during this hybrid PALT Update Session will be limited to status updates pertaining to specific procurements. The Government will not provide any procurement sensitive information that would violate the integrity of the source selection process. Registration: https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/ffpjkLh4vB?origin=lprLink

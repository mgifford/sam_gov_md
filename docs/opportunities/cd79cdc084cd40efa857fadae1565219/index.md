# 4th Annual HHS/DOI Buy Indian Industry Day

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-13 10:50:24.535-04
- Solicitation Number: OSDBU004

## Summary

On behalf of the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), in collaboration with the U.S. Department of the Interior (DOI), please join us in Oklahoma City for the 4th Annual Buy Indian Industry Day and Federal Matchmaking sessions, taking place April 27–28, 2026, at the OKANA Conference Center in Oklahoma City, OK. This event will focus on “Strengthening Tribal Economies Through Federal Procurement: A Buy Indian Act Small Business Industrial Base Forum” and serves as a national forum to strengthen tribal economies through federal procurement and to advance effective implementation of the Buy Indian Act. It will bring together Federal acquisition leaders, program officials, and Native American-owned businesses to align mission requirements with qualified industry partners, reduce barriers to entry, and expand high-impact contracting opportunities. This event is co-hosted by all 6 Native APEX Accelerators, and will have one day of training sessions, and one day of matchmaking with HHS and DOI buyers! There is no charge to attend!

# Vehicle Wrap for Recruiting Vehicles 137th SOW

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 12:41:09.666-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8T

## Summary

The Oklahoma Air National Guard recruiting office is purchasing seven full-vehicle wraps, two for mini-vans and five for sedans, which will be used as a recruiting marketing tool to raise awareness of the Oklahoma Air National Guard as described in the Statement of Work. Amendment 0001- Clarify vendor information requirements. All vendors must self perform and no third party pass through is permitted for this requirement. Reminder that vendors must be within 50 miles of Will Rogers Air National Guard Base, OKC, OK, 73179. No other changes at this time.

## Summary

This is a special notice regarding Contract 47QFCA25C0002, which was awarded to Cognosante, LLC on May 28, 2025. This contract was issued by the General Services Administration (GSA) Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS) under Request for Proposal (RFP) 47QFCA23R0053 in support of the Office of the Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense for Military Community and Family Policy (MC&FP).

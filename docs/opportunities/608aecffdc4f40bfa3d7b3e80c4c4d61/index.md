# Repair of B-52 Stratofortress Multifunction Color Displays (MFCD)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 13:42:44.265-04
- Solicitation Number: FD20302502102

## Summary

The Air Force intends to award a Sole Source contract to Raytheon Company, Cage Code of 96JA0. Contract will be for the repair of B-52 Stratofortress Multifunction Color Displays (MFCD). The current acquisition strategy is to issue a non-commercial, Firm Fixed Price contract with a basic year and four 1-year options. Anticiapated award will be a Requirements IDV contract. The contemplated period of performance is Mar 22 2027 thru Mar 21 2032.

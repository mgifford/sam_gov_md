## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound (FLC PS) is posting a Sources Sought notice, as a means of conducting market research to identify any interested vendors for the procurement of OEM "K-Flex USA, LLC" - Insulation Pipe Part(s), in support of SRF-JRMC. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code assigned to this requirement is 238290, with a small business size standard of $22 million. The results of this market research may contribute to future procurement process decisions. NO feedback or evaluations will be provided regarding any submission against this Sources Sought notice. Please provide responses to this announcement no later than the posted closing date.

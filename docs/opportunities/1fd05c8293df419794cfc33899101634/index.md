# High Security NSA Regulated Shredders

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-28 11:36:58.077-04
- Solicitation Number: N00189260224

## Summary

The Naval Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) Fleet Logistics Center (FLC) Norfolk Contracting Department, Norfolk, Virginia, intends to procure three (3) High Security Level 6/P-7 NSA-regulated shredders. This requirement will be issued as a Small Business set-aside in accordance with FAR Part 12 procedures. The Government anticipates awarding a Firm-Fixed-Price supply contract utilizing FAR Part 12 Commercial Item procedures and Simplified Acquisition Procedures. All responsible sources may submit a quote, which shall be considered by FLC Norfolk. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 423420. Detailed specifications for the shredders are provided below. SHREDDER REQUIREMENT SPECIFICATIONS: Quantity: 3 Destruction : TS/SCI Level, paper, CD, Blu-Ray Security level: 6/P-7 Shred size: Paper: 0.8mm x 5mm, CD/Blu-Ray: 2mm x 2mm NSA Rating: 7 sheets/pass Sheet capacity: Up to 14 sheets/pass The required delivery date is 05/13/2026 or BEST POSSIBLE DELIVERY Requirement POC Vladimir Pierre-Charles Contract Specialist 1968 Gilbert street, Norfolk, VA. vladimir.pierre-charles.civ@us.navy.mil Shaina Sollenberger Contracting Officer shaina.m.sollenberger.civ@us.navy.mil

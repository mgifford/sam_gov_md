## Summary

Amendment 0003. Amendment 0002 updates 00 11 00, 00 21 16, 00 22 16 and provide a revised Statement of Work dated 9 Feb 2026.The proposal due date remains unchanged. Proposals are due 23 Feb 2026 at 1400 CST. Government POC is Melissa Hyslop at melissa.d.hyslop@usace.army.mil. Amendment 0001 is hereby issued to revise FAR 52.228-1; update Section 00 21 16; to Revise 00 22 16; add Programmatic AT-OPSEC. The proposal due date is extended. Proposals are due 23 February 2026 at 1400 CST. This is an Indefinite Delivery Contract (IDC) Job Order Contract (JOC). The capacity of this JOC is $14,000,000 for the base of two years and 3 one-year options in the amount of $7M each, total capacity of $35M. Task order limitations will range from $2,000 to $3M. Funding is available to meet the minimum guarantee for the IDC Contract award and task orders will be awarded with project funds. The FY26 JOC is an IDC and will be used for repair, maintenance, demolition and minor construction requirements on a variety of projects ranging from Sustainment, Restoration, and Modernization (SRM), simplified acquisition of base repair requirements, civil works operations and maintenance, small renovations, real property repair and maintenance with an estimated value more than the micro-purchase threshold (MPT) for acquisitions of construction. All facilities are located on McAlester Army Ammunition Plant in Pittsburg County, Oklahoma.

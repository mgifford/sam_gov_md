## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Malcom Randall VA Medical Center, Gainesville, FL, has a requirement for Deionized (DI) Water System maintenance services. The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, consumables, and supervision necessary to perform preventive maintenance, inspection, and consumable exchange services for two DI water systems. Services include tank exchange, system monitoring, and ensuring continuous operation in accordance with the Performance Work Statement (PWS). The Government intends to award a firm-fixed-price contract with a base year and four (4) one-year option periods. Please refer to the attached for complete details and submission instructions.

# B-2 Zonal Radar

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-01 11:47:57.704-04
- Solicitation Number: B-2

## Summary

The United States Air Force (USAF) is conducting market research and identifying, as potential sources, companies that may possess the expertise, capabilities, and experience to meet the requirements for a contract for the rapid procurement, sustainment, further development and/or modification of existing zonal radar capability to address obsolescence, new requirements, and/or unforeseen maintenance issues within the B-2 Weapon System.

# Notice of Intent to Sole Source Multifactor Authentication Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-23 13:32:33.38-04
- Solicitation Number: DC041020261453

## Summary

The Washington Headquarters Services Acquisition Directorate (WHS/AD), on behalf of the Defense Innovation Unit (DIU), intends to award a contract on a sole source basis to Lastwall Corporation, 4024 40th Street North, Arlington, VA 22201-4603, for Multifactor Authentication Support Services. The period of performance is estimated to be for a 12-month period. DIU requires secure identity, advanced authentication, identity-enabled data protection and encryption services that is compatible with DIU’s current infrastructure and security needs in the areas of identity verification, authentication, and enforcement, identity-enhanced artificial intelligence (AI) services, and identity-enabled data Protection. The primary North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) is 518210 – Computing Infrastructure Providers, Data Processing, Web Hosting, and Related Services. Small business Standard Size $40 million. Please see the attached for additional details.

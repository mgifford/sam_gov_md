## Summary

Pre-Solicitation Notice W9128F26QA052 Repairs for 54-inch Irrigation Conduit Pipe, Chatfield Dam, Littleton, CO On or about 24 April 2026, this office will issue Request for Quote (RFQ) for the repair of a 54-inch irrigation pipe located at Chatfield Dam in Littleton, CO. The solicitation will close on or about 15 May 2026. This solicitation is restricted to SMALL BUSINESSES ONLY. Site Visit: Site visit information will be issued with the solicitation. Project Description: The contractor shall provide material, and labor in order to complete required work to repair the 54" irrigation pipes located at Chatfield Dam, Littleton, CO. The work shall include but not limited to the following: 1. Excavation and dewatering. 2. Perform pipeline inspection. 3. Replace concrete or metal pipe sections damaged including joints seals. 4. Perform operational pressure testing. 5. Backfilling and soil compaction. Period of Performance: Complete within 90 Days after NTP The above project description is for information only. Project scope of work is subject to change. NAICS Code: The Primary North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code anticipated for solicitation is 237110, Water and Sewer Line and Related Structures Construction, with a size standard for Small Business of $45M. All contractors must be registered and have an active and verified account in the System for Award Management (SAM) in order to receive a contract award from any DoD activity. Contractors may access the Internet site at: https://www.sam.gov/ to register and/or obtain information about the SAM program. Contractors must ensure that the applicable NAICS code 237110 is included in their profile prior to submission of offer. Contracting Office Address: USACE Omaha District 1616 Capital Ave, Omaha, NE 68102-4901 Point Of Contact: All questions shall be submitted to the POCs listed on SAM.gov. Place of Performance: The work associated with this contract shall be at Chatfield Dam within the state of Colorado in Littleton, in Jefferson County.

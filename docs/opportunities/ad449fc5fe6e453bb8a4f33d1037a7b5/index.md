- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Brand name Mercury Marine Boat Engine Parts in support of the Seaborne Powered Target (SEPTAR) unit at the Pacific Missile Range Facility (PMRF), Barking Sands, HI. The NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center, Pearl Harbor, Regional Contracting Office requests responses from qualified sources capable of providing Mercury Marine OEM boat parts (see attachments 1&2). Please reference the attached three documents for this combined synopsis/solicitation. Attachment (1) Combined Synopsis/Solicitation N0060426Q4028 Attachment (2) Redacted Brand Name Justification Attachment (3) FAR & DFARS Provisions and Clauses As referenced in attachment 1, questions shall be submitted to lenin.a.moreira.civ@us.navy.mil or tyler.m.yamamoto.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 4:00PM HST on Tuesday, 28 April 2026. Emails sent to this address shall clearly reference RFQ# N0060426Q4028 in the subject line. Questions submitted after the allotted date and time will not be accepted. This announcement will close at 4:00PM HST on Thursday, 30 April 2026. Please submit quotes to Lenin Moreira (lenin.a.moreira.civ@us.navy.mil, 808-473-7531) and Tyler Yamamoto (tyler.m.yamamoto.civ@us.navy.mil, 808-603-7001). No other method will be accepted in response to this notice.

In accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 10 (Market Research), the Department of Veterans Affairs is conducting market research to ascertain if there are verified Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) concerns that have the capability to provide court reporting services to participating government facilities in Attachment 1 at a fair and reasonable price. This announcement is for informational and planning purposes only and is not to be construed as a commitment by the government, implied or otherwise, to issue a solicitation or award a contract. The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) has a requirement for court reporting services to the government facilities located in the RPO-Central Region (VISNs 9,10, 12,15 and 23).

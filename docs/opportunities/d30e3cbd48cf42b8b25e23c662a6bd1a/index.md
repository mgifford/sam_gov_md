# TechNet 2026 Cyber Conference-Baltimore MD

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 13:40:20.775-04
- Solicitation Number: 832674126

## Summary

Exhibit services for the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) Exhibit Booth at TechNet Cyber 2026 Conference. This requirement is to provide the following: rental background display structures with graphics, exhibit furniture, TV monitor mount(s)/stand(s), and flooring; installation and dismantling labor, and shipping and material handling for the DISA exhibit at TechNet Cyber 2026. All services required are for non-personal services. Rental, shipping, installation, and dismantling services for the DISA exhibit booth at the TechNet Cyber 2026 conference occurring June 2nd through June 4th, 2026.

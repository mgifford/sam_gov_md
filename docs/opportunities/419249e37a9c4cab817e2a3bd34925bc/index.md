# US Consulate Sao Paulo Warehouse Painting Project

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 13:14:45.859-05
- Solicitation Number: 19BR9326Q0007

## Summary

The U.S. Government intends to award a firm-fixed-price purchase order to the responsible offeror whose quotation is determined technically acceptable and offers the lowest price. The work site is located at the U.S. Consulate General, Rua Henry Dunant, #700, Santo Amaro, São Paulo, Brazil. The scope of work includes painting the warehouse interior walls, ceiling, and concrete floor in accordance with the statement of work. Amendment 01 In accordance with FAR 13.004, this amendment revises the solicitation in its entirety. The original solicitation is superseded and replaced by the attached revised pages. The site visit date has been updated. Offerors must refer to the revised solicitation for the correct date and time.

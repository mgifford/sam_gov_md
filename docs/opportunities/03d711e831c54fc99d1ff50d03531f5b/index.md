# Notice of Justification for Sole Source Award to Burnham Polymeric Inc. for ROV Fiber Optic Tethers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-01 18:03:46.427-04
- Solicitation Number: N66001-26-P-6080

## Summary

In accordance with FAR 6.301, the Naval Information Warfare Center (NIWC) Pacific is posting the Justification and Approval for a sole-source contract that has been awarded to Burnham Polymeric Inc. (BPI). The award was for the procurement of custom-wound Fiber Optic Cable (FOC) packs used to provide command, control, and data links for subsea Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROVs). The sole-source action was justified based on a multi-year market research and testing effort which concluded that BPI is the only source capable of manufacturing a fiber optic cable that meets the Governments essential technical and performance requirements for signal integrity under extreme subsea pressure. This notice is for informational purposes only.

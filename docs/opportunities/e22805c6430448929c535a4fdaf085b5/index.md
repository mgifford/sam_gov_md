Clear and Concise Description of Supplies or Services: Service Title: Sterilizer Equipment Maintenance Brief Description: 60th Medical Group (60 MDG) David Grant Medical Center (DGMC) requires a contracted service to continue critical preventive maintenance, to include intervening service calls, for Steris Brand government owned equipment. The critical need arises from the impending expiration of the current contract (FA442721C0007) on 31 March 2026, coupled with an unforeseen delay in DGMC’s strategic plan to transition these services to the mobile sterile processing department (SPD).

# Power Converter Maintenance, Fort Sill, OK

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 15:09:35.805-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124L-26-Q-A001

## Summary

Non-Personal Services - Power Converter Maintenance for Fort Sill, Oklahoma Amendment 0001 - The purpose of this amendment is to provide Government responses to industry questions received, please see Attachment 3 - Questions and Answers for more details. The Mission and Installation Contracting Command (MICC) Fort Sill has a requirement for preventative and corrective maintenance and repair of Solid State Frequency Converters (SSFCs) and Power Distribution Units (PDUs) for the 30th Air Defense Artillery (ADA) Brigade (BDE). The 30th ADA BDE School uses twenty (20) Hobart Ground Power Systems, PoWerMaster® ADV series 500049 180kVA AC SSFC, nine (9) ITW/GSE Hobart Ground Power Systems, Hobart PoWerMaster® 2400 60SX240, 60kVA Solid State Ground Power Units (GPU), one (1) FCX systems, Inc. Model PFCC048-v-40-AM-24P 200/115V, 400Hz, 60kVA SSFC, and forty (40) Wolf Power PDUs. The contractor shall provide personnel, equipment, materials, and supervision necessary to conduct scheduled preventive maintenance, unscheduled corrective maintenance to ensure continued availability of high frequency (400Hz) electrical power needed to prevent any disruption to ADA school training and operations, and reconfigure 400 Hz commercial power converters and PDUs as needed to meet 30th BDE’s future modernization of the Air Defense Artillery’s technical tactical equipment. North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) Code 238210 is applicable to this acquisition. The applicable size standard is $19,000,000.00. Pursuant to FAR 4.203-1(b), prospective contractors shall be registered in System for Award Management (SAM) database prior to award of a contract. Contractors may obtain further information on SAM registration at the following website: https://sam.gov/. This announcement does not obligate the Government to award a contract, nor does it obligate the Government to award a contract, nor does it obligate the Government to pay for any quote preparation costs. Request for copies of the solicitation package by any other means will not be honored. Potential offerors are responsible for monitoring the sam.gov website for release of the solicitation package or subsequent solicitation amendments. Any questions may be directed to Thomas Green (Contracting Officer) at thomas.m.green111.civ@army.mil and Sommer Roach (Contract Specialist) at sommer.p.roach.civ@army.mil.

The purpose of this solicitation, is to procure Drain Degreaser services for the 23rd Civil Engineering Squadron (23 CES) at Moody AFB. These services are essential to maintain servicing of facility drains, installation of industry-standard chemical injection degreaser equipment, and provision of associated non-hazardous enzyme-based treatment chemicals. Services will be performed at eleven (11) degreaser systems located across eight (8) facilities on base. The Contractor shall conduct monthly service visits to verify proper operation of chemical injection pumps and replenish treatment chemicals as required. All chemicals used under this requirement shall be non-hazardous enzyme treatments designed to prevent grease accumulation and ensure continuous, unobstructed drainage. The estimated annual level of effort is approximately 120 labor hours, equivalent to one part-time full-time equivalent (FTE). No significant changes in the number of serviced locations are anticipated during the period of performance. Performance shall begin on 1 October 2026 and will consist of a twelve (12)-month base period followed by four (4) twelve (12)-month option periods.

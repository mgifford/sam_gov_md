# Hydraulic Paper Cutter Purchase and Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-23 14:56:07.307-04
- Solicitation Number: SP700025Q1051
- Awardee: Print Finishing Systems Inc 7116 NW 79TH ST Oklahoma City OK 73132 USA
- Award Amount: 75551

## Summary

Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Document Services seeks to purchase the following equipment and associated services: One (1) 37? Hydraulic Programmable Paper Cutter w/ Air Table, and Touch Screen. This purchase includes delivery and installation of the equipment, warranties and repair of the equipment, and training on using/daily operations of the equipment, to include hardcopy User Manuals. The purchase will be for an up to 60-month contract with one-year base and four (4) option periods for maintenance, or a warranty plan. Device will be located at Tinker AFB, OK. This RFQ is a 100% Small Business Set-Aside. All information can be accessed utilizing the PIEE SOLICITATIONS App at: https://.piee.eb.mil/

# MESF High Risk Training- Only Seeking RFP Responses from Current Seaport-NxG Contract Holders

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 09:05:37.712-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926RL001

## Summary

MESF requires support services Subject Matter Experts experienced in specialized Maritime Expeditionary Security Force skills and operations in order to primarily execute High Risk Training in the course of training and creating curriculum for MESF companies, platoons, patrol boat crews, and other special purpose maritime security teams in Coastal Maritime operations, HVU protection, combat shooting techniques, tactical vehicle and convoy operation, Expeditionary Designated Marksman EDM), Ground Operations, Small Unit Tactics, Embarked Security Team (EST), and Aircraft Security Teams (AST), and critical time-intensive administrative functions. The Contractor will travel to off-site training ranges, training facilities, conferences and meetings, and other places as required in support of MESF and Navy Expeditionary Combat Command training requirements. In order to be eligible for award, the Offeror shall be a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), Seaport NxG contract holder under NAICS 541330. Please see attachments for additional information.

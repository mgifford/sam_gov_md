# B52-H Carriage Equipment Asset Management

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:26:30.11-04
- Solicitation Number: FA210326RB002

## Summary

AFNWC/PZNE is issuing a solicitation in accordance with FAR Part 12. This acquisition is being solicited as a small business set-aside. Interested parties who believe they can meet all the requirements for the services described in this solicitation are invited to submit, in writing, a complete proposal. Contract Details: Period of Performance: One-year base period with four (4) one-year options. Contract Type: Predominantly Firm Fixed Price Description of Services: This is a non-personal services contract to provide Asset Management for the B52-H Carriage Equipment (CE) program. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation/shipping, tools, materials and other items necessary to fulfil this contract. The items to be stored include the following: SUU-72 pylons (Pylon) married to ADU-490/Es or A/Es (PLA) Nuclear Store Interface Units (NSIU) Conventional Stores Rotary Launchers (CSRL) Loader Launcher Adaptors (LLA) Support and auxiliary equipment The contractor will be responsible for receiving, managing, securing, storing, tracking, and shipping approximately 60 CE assets. These assets have maximum dimensions of 50 feet in length, 15 feet in width, and 17 feet in height, with a weight of 20,000 pounds each. The assets have been cleaned and prepared for refurbishment and require storage in an environmentally controlled environment. Further details regarding proposal submission requirements and evaluation criteria will be provided in the forthcoming solicitation package. All responsible sources may submit a proposal which shall be considered by the agency. When requesting the documents, please provide an active DD Form 2345 if you have not done so previously for this notice.

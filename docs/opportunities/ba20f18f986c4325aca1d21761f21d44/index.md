# EQUIPMENT-REPROCESSING SINK-BATTLE CREEK

## Summary

This request is to replace the Endoscopy Reprocessing Sink at the Wyoming Clinic, a CBOC to the Battle Creek VAMC, for the Sterile Processing Service (SPS). The current equipment was original to the building and is noncompliant with VHA Directive 1116(2), and the Association for the Advancement of Medical Instrumentation (AAMI) standards set forth by the Joint Commission, AMMI ST91. This has been deemed a Priority 1 replacement (Mission Critical) for the Gastrointestinal (GI) clinic’s reprocessing area within SPS during the recent site visit and audit from the VISN 10 Chief Sterile Processing Officer (CSPO). The C&A requirements do not apply, and a Security Accreditation Package is not required. Required Specifications: Specifically designed for the cleaning of endoscopes Overall size 97.5” L x 30” W, height adjustable Three basins measuring 30” x 18” x 10” with telescoping drains Ergonomics—including 45-degree wrist rests one per basin for a total of three), adjustable height by push-button access, and 10” gooseneck swivel spout sprayer with ergonomic grip Sink Light Hood, one in 4’ L and one in 2’ L Flat-bottom support shelf that mounts to pegboard backing, 14” x 12 x 1.5” Water resistant programmable timer with alarm function, minutes and seconds, 9.75” L x 4.75” H x 3”D Task Halogen Light, 27” gooseneck arm with 2X magnifier, water resistant BACK Wall Pegboard, height adjustable with the sink, 97.5” L x 24” H, standard pegs throughout Additional Accompaniments: Volume indicator etching plate, one per basin Wall mounted sink facet, 8” swing spout and lever handles LED Basin lights Gallon bottle holder Scope Buddy Shelf mount system, 10” L x 11” D x 5” H at the back, 1” lip in front Stainless steel wire baskets: Front facing slant, 12” x 12” x 8-1/4” Stainless steel basket size 12” x6”x 6” Workflow plates: Leak Testing Wash Rinse Three Bay drain kit assembly

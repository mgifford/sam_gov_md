## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE / REQUEST FOR INFORMATION NOTICE: FA461326LCCCLeaningIDIQ - LCC Cleaning IDIQ PKB (Service) 1. THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT. NORTH AMERICAN INDUSTRY CLASSIFICATION SYSTEM (NAICS) OF 561720 – JANITORIAL SERVICES WITH A SMALL BUSINESS SIZE STANDARD OF $22M IS TO BE USED FOR MARKET RESEARCH ONLY TO DETERMINE THE AVAILABILITY OF POTENTIAL SMALL BUSINESS CATEGORIES FOR POTENTIAL SET-ASIDES. NO AWARDS WILL BE MADE FROM THIS SOURCES SOUGHT. NO SOLICITATION IS AVAILABLE AT THIS TIME; THEREFORE, DO NOT REQUEST A COPY OF THE SOLICITATION. ALL RESPONSES WILL BE USED TO DETERMINE THE APPROPRIATE ACQUISITION STRATEGY FOR FUTURE ACQUISITION. 2. F. E. Warren AFB, Wyoming is conducting market research to identify potential sources that market research to identify potential sources that may possess the expertise, capabilities, and experience to provide cleaning services at 15 Launch Control Centers (LCC) and associated Launch Control Equipment Building (LCEB) as a by-need basis. The amount of sites cleaned per year may vary from 3 to up to 15 in a year. The contract period of performance will be for a total of 5 years. The draft Performance Work Statement (PWS) is included. (Attachment 1 – PWS). The government intends to establish one Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ). 3. All interested parties are invited to send capability statement that will briefly describe how your company can satisfy the requirement described above to: 1st Lt Shane Yurkus at Shane.Yurkus@us.af.mil and Mrs. Donna Doss at Donna.Doss.1@us.af.mil. 4. Please provide (a) Company Name and Cage Code Number; (b) Mailing Address; (c) Point of Contact to include phone number and email; (d) website, if applicable; (e) State whether your company is small, 8(a), VOSB, SDVOSB, HUBZone small business, SDB, and WOSB, etc; (f) SAM registration. 5.

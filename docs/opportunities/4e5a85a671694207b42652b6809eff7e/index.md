# CMR Grounds Safety Improvement Project.

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-18 11:30:17.639-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15756088

## Summary

The US Embassy Banjul wishes to solicit bids for repairing the walkways, stairways, patio, and gazebo in the CMR compound. The repairs will include the removal of all existing pavers, stones, and sunken areas in the walkways and stairways, followed by their replacement with non-slip floor tiles. Additionally, the project will include repairing the storm drainage system and meeting safety requirements by installing handrails as per below scope of work (SOW). U.S. Government intends to award a contract to the responsible company submitting a technically acceptable quotation at the lowest price.

## Summary

AMENDMENT 01 Amendment 01, as attached, removes the requirement for the vendor to price supplies on the Schedule of Items. PROJECT TITLE & INTENT OF SERVICES Janitorial Services, Avery Office and Engine Bay The USDA Forest Service (USFS) is contracting for janitorial services for a potential 5-year period of performance. The contract type is firm-fixed-price (FFP) and will be awarded to one vendor. Janitorial services are needed for two (2) federally controlled buildings as described within the attached request for quotation (RFQ). PROJECT LOCATION The janitorial services location is forty miles outside of St. Maries, ID (Pop. 2,500) at 34 Hoyt Drive, Avery, ID 83802 which is on the St. Joe River Road, FH50 (Paved) mile marker 40.6 and about 6 miles before the town of Avery, ID (Pop. 25).

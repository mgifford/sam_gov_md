# Request for Information for OEM Replacement Parts for the Joint Light Tactical Vehicle (JLTV).

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-16 12:17:50.755-04
- Solicitation Number: W91247-26-R-0007

## Summary

The U.S. Army is seeking information from qualified vendors regarding the availability of Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) JLTV replacement parts. All parts must be brand new and not refurbished. Purpose: The purpose of this request for Information (RFI) is to gather information regarding the availability, production lead times, pricing and potential expedited delivery options for the parts listed in the attached Excel document. The government is conducting market research to identify industry capabilities and solutions to meet urgent mission requirements. Vendors are requested to review the attached list and provide detailed responses regarding their ability to supply these items, including estimated delivery timelines, production capacity, and any options for expediting delivery. Scope of Information Requested: Part Numbers and Descriptions: Provide details of the OEM parts available and production capacity, including part numbers, descriptions, and specifications. Pricing Information: Provide pricing details for new parts, including any volume discounts or pricing tiers. Lead Times: Indicate the typical lead times for delivery of new parts. Warranty Information: Provide details about any warranties or guarantees on the new parts. Company Information to include the following: Company Name: Full legal name of the business. Address: Physical and mailing address of the company. Point of Contact (POC): Name, title, phone number, and email address of the individual responsible for the response. DUNS Number or Unique Entity Identifier (UEI): The company’s identifier in the System for Award Management (SAM). CAGE Code: Commercial and Government Entity code assigned to the company. Business Size Classification: Indicate whether the company is a small business, large business, or qualifies under a specific socioeconomic category (e.g., 8(a), HUBZone, SDVOSB, WOSB). Submission Instructions: Please submit your responses via email to Mikel Hernandez at mikel.r.hernandeztrujillo.mil@army.mil no later than 5:00 pm EST on Friday 20MAR2026. Include all requested information in a clear and concise format. Disclaimer: This RFI is for informational purposes only, not a request for proposal, and does not constitute a commitment to issue a solicitation or award a contract. Contact Information: For any questions regarding this RFI, please contact Mikel Hernandez at (910) 396-1078 or mikel.r.hernandeztrujillo.mil@army.mil. Alternative contact is Cameron Wade at (910) 908-7156 or cameron.r.wade.mil@army.mil.

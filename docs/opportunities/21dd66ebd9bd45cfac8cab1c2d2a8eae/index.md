# Sources Sought - Ak-Chin Community Health Clinic Alarm and Surveillance System

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-25 19:01:32.435-04
- Solicitation Number: IHS1525595

## Summary

*THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT ONLY. DO NOT SUBMIT PRICING INFORMATION* The objective is to lease, install, and maintain an integrated security system for the Ak-Chin Community Health Clinic that can be maintained at the Phoenix Indian Medical Center. The system should include a remotely monitored alarm system, surveillance cameras, and associated monitoring and support services. The leased system will enhance the security and safety of facilities, personnel, and visitors, and safeguard federally owned property by providing continuous monitoring, timely detection of security incidents, and responsive support services in accordance with applicable federal standards, policies, and requirements *DO NOT SUBMIT A QUOTE OR ANY PRICING INFORMATION TO THIS NOTICE*

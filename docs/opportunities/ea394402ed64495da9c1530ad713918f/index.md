# Invitation to Bid for Scrap Mixed Steel located at Naval Air Station Lemoore (NASL)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 17:05:05.994-04
- Solicitation Number: T003-26-CD

## Summary

The Navy Region Southwest Qualified Recycling Program (QRP) is issuing an invitation to bid on the sale of scrap mixed steel located at Naval Air Station Lemoore (NASL) Lemoore, CA. This is a Government Sale. If interested, please open the attached announcement form for information and instructions. Bid must be mailed to our office using the bid sheet form provided with this email. Bid sheet must be sent in ink or typed, clearly legible, and signed by the person sending the bid. Please submit only the bid sheet. The other material in this packet, specifically the terms & conditions, is for your review and details what the job entails. Bids are due no later than 2 P.M. (Pacific Time) May 18th, 2026. If you have any questions regarding this bid, please contact our office at 619-556-9475 or email questions to cnrsw-qrp-sales@us.navy.mil

# Dell Federal Systems LP Master IDIQ Contract for Servers and Storage Contract Extension Awarded

- Agency: POSTAL SERVICE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 10:27:47.408-04
- Solicitation Number: 1CITHW-21-B-0003
- Awardee: DELL FEDERAL SYSTEMS L.P Round Rock TX 78682 USA
- Award Amount: 250000000.00

## Summary

The United States Postal Service has awarded a one-year non-competitive contract extension to Dell Federal Systems LP. The award also includes three options: two one-year options and a two-year final option. The master IDIQ contract covers servers, storage, IT Services, software, and other IT products. USPS requires Dell's ongoing maintenance and support of deployed storage and server systems.

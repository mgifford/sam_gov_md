# Purchase of a Government-owned Vehicle 19UK5626Q0022

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 13:19:22.848-04
- Solicitation Number: 19UK5626Q0022

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy London requires the supply, delivery, and registration of a new SUV?type vehicle in accordance with the specifications and terms and conditions set out in this document. The U.S. Government intends to award a contract/purchase order under this solicitation to the lowest?priced, technically acceptable (LPTA) responsible offeror/quoter, in accordance with FAR 52.212?1. The Government intends to make award on the basis of initial quotations, without conducting discussions, but reserves the right to hold discussions with companies within the competitive range if necessary. Vendors must be familiar with the procedures for registering a vehicle in the UK for use by Visiting Forces and must be able to provide support with this process as required. Only companies or individuals specializing in this field should apply. Interested parties should download the RFQ package attached to this advert and provide their quotation to Procurement_Box7@state.gov, referencing I.D. # 19UK5626Q0022 in the Subject Line before 1700 BST on April 13, 2026

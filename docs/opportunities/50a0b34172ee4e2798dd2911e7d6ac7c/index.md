## Summary

AWARD NOTICE: Dry dock inspection of the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers towboat M/V Wells due to suspected rudder damage. Vessel located at the Brennan Marine Dock, 818 Bainbridge St, La Crosse WI 54603. Contractor will raise vessel to dry dock, inspect and report, prepare estimate for repair. Contractor may not operate vessel under its own power. All work must be completed no later than April 24, 2026.

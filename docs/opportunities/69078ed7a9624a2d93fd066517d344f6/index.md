# ABS Nautical Systems human resources software renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:31:23.332-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102826R0032

## Summary

The Government intends to issue a sole source order to ABS Digital Solutions, LLC. for ABS Nautical Systems human resources software renewal for the United States Navy Military Sealift Command. The period of performance is May 01, 2026 through April 30, 2027. The redactd Brand Name Class Justification & Approval for Software Support is also posted along with the solicitation. Responsible sources may submit a completed and signed solicition in response. If you are a reseller, you must also submit a letter or email from the original equipment manufacturer confirming your reseller status along with your completed solicitation. Please submit by April 24, 2026 via email to alexis.n.corley.civ@mail.mil

# Preventative Maintenance of Illumina MiSeq Next-Generation Sequencing System

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 11:13:00.106-04
- Solicitation Number: 1333MG26Q0003

## Summary

The National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), National Centers for Coastal Ocean Science (NCCOS) has a requirement for Preventative Maintenance of Illumina MiSeq Next-Generation Sequencing System at the NOAA Hollings Marine Laboratory. All services rendered shall be in accordance with the Statement of Work. The estimated period of performance is April 13, 2026 - April 12, 2027. The Government will award a firm fixed price contract based on price. Quotes shall include all costs associated with each CLIN unless otherwise indicated on the vendor’s quote. This RFQ is unrestricted. The associated NAICS code is 811210, Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance Additionally, each offeror must provide the following information with the quote: The Request for Quote (RFQ) number; The Name, Address, Telephone Number, and Unique Entity Identification (UEI) of the Offeror; Fill in your quoted unit and total price/s in the space provided on attached SF1449 SOL_1333MG26Q0003 for each CLIN Per 1330-52.222-70, provide a statement that you understand and will comply with the sexual harassment prevention and response policy. Include a statement specifying the extent of understanding and agreement with all terms, conditions, and provisions included in the RFQ. Quotes that reject the terms and conditions of the RFQ may be excluded from consideration; Acknowledgment of Solicitation Amendments (SF-30), if any, All responsible sources may submit a quotation which shall be considered by the agency. Quotes must be submitted via electronic means (e-mail) by 11:00 A.M. Eastern on April 3, 2026; any award resulting from this RFQ will be made based on best value to the Government. Factors considered will be technical approach and capability, past performance, and price. Quotes must be submitted electronically via e-mail to scott.beliveau@noaa.gov . Telephone inquiries will not be honored. Contractors are encouraged to register with the Contract Opportunities Vendor Notification Service as well as the Interested Vendors List for this acquisition (see applicable tab within Contract Opportunities where this synopsis/solicitation is posted). The Government does not accept responsibility for non-receipt of quotes. It is the Contractor’s responsibility to request and receive a confirmation of the quote receipt.

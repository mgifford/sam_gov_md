# 1 year 50 Mbps Dedicated internet Renewal for PACOM Office (PAT INDO) at Medan Merdeka Selatan 5

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 04:40:19.272-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15894140

## Summary

The purpose of this firm fixed price purchase order is to procure – 1 year 50 Mbps Dedicated Internet Renewal for PACOM Office (PAT INDO). Period: June 1, 2026 – May 31, 2027. The bidders must operate in Indonesia and have an established business with a permanent address and telephone listing in Indonesia. Also, the companies must have experience in this service in this country. Provider must have the fiber-optic network infrastructure installed already in the Embassy area and provide multimode 50-micron Fiber-optic cable with SC connector to the Embassy OTB. In consideration of satisfactory performance of the services required under this contract, the vendor shall be paid a firm fixed price (FFP) per month as stated in the schedule below in Indonesian Rupiah. IMPORTANT NOTE: Central Contractor Registration For procurement or contracts over $30,000.00, the contractor must be registered within Central Contractor Registration, which is part of the System for Award Management (SAM). Prospective offerors are encouraged to complete the SAM registration process prior to the submittal of their proposals in response to this solicitation.

# Bloom software support renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 12:02:29.933-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102826R0027

## Summary

The Government intends to issue a sole source order to Point 3D, Inc. for Bloom software support renewal for Puget Sound Naval Shipyard & Intermediate Maintenance Facility. The period of performance is April 30, 2026 through April 29, 2027, plus one, one-year option period. The redacted Brand Name Class Justification and Approval for Software Support is also posted along with the solicitation. Responsible sources may submit a completed and signed solicitation in response. If you are a reseller, you must submit a letter or email from the original equipment manufacturer confirming your reseller status along with your completed solicitation. Please submit by April 20, 2026 via email to alexis.n.corley.civ@mail.mil.

## Summary

The 435th Construction and Training Squadron requires the acquisition of ventilation material to alter Satellite Fire Station 8 (bldg. 1058) at Vogelweh Family Housing, Germany. The request includes a ventilation unit, model: Reco-Boxx 1000 Flat-H-R / EV / EN (or equal), along with all necessary modular system parts and components listed below. These shall meet the exact requirements for air flow rates and technical specifications specified in Chapter 2. If an equal unit is proposed, the vendor must certify that it meets or exceeds the technical specifications listed in chapter 2. This unit must also include all the other parts listed below as compatible modular system equivalents.

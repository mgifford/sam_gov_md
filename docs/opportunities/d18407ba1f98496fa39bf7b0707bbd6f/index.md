## Summary

This is a sources sought notice. Please use the GSA Link to review the RFI, submit your responses to the RFI questions, and submit your Capability Statement. DHS - USSS Protective Intelligence Interview Training: This is a non-personal services contract/order to provide contractor services to instruct protective intelligence practical exercise interview training as part of the USSS protective intelligence basic and advance training program. This procurement is for a primary instructor (psychologist) and a secondary instructor (psycho dramatist). The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items of non-personal services necessary to perform lectures, demonstrations, and psycho dramatic roles at RTC facilities as defined in this Performance Work Statement except for those items specified as Government Furnished Property (GFP) and services. The contractor shall accomplish all lectures, demonstrations, and psycho dramatic roles on the dates scheduled, within the times allotted.

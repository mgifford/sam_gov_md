# Asbestos and Environmental Air Monitoring and Sampling

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 18:15:16.069-04
- Solicitation Number: RFP26-033-JR

## Summary

The Princeton Plasma Laboratory (PPPL) requires the service of an Asbestos and Environmental Air Monitoring Subcontractor on an as-needed, on-call basis. The Subcontractor may at times be called upon by PPPL to perform other air and surface sampling and analyses including, but not limited to, bulk asbestos, mold, organic and/or inorganic compounds. The Subcontractor may also be called upon to perform an asbestos location survey. The Subcontractor will coordinate with the Princeton Technical Representative (PTR), or designee, the required sampling and analyses, as needed. The Subcontractor shall provide a New Jersey licensed Asbestos Safety Technician (AST) to oversee all aspects of asbestos containing material (ACM) abatement projects performed at PPPL under a separate subcontract. The number and size of ACM abatement projects, along with asbestos, mold, and other organic and/or inorganic compounds analyses will vary depending upon the needs of PPPL.

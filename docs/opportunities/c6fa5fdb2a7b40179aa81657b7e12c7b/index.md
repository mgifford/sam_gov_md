# Cryogenic Interface Cans for EIC591 SC

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 14:24:11.293-04
- Solicitation Number: JSA-26-R449413

## Summary

Thomas Jefferson National Accelerator Facility (aka Jefferson Lab) is seeking to identify sources for fabrication and delivery of Cryogenic Interface Cans in support of the Electron Ion Collider (EIC) 591 MHz Single Cell Cryomodule (591 SC). Jefferson Lab is a nuclear physics research facility under contract to the U.S. Department of Energy.

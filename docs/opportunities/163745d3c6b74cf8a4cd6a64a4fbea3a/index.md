## Summary

THIS IS A NOTICE OF INTENT FOR REQUEST FOR QUOTE (i) This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; proposals are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. (ii) The solicitation number is 140P6426Q0033 and is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ). (iii) The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular 2026-01. (iv) This requirement is set-aside for Total Small Businesses. The associated NAICS code is 562991 and the small business size standard is $9.0M. (v) Please see the attached Price Schedule for a list of line-item number(s) and items, quantities, and units of measure (including options). (vi) Cuyahoga Valley National Park is seeking Septic Pumping Service in accordance with the attached Performance Work Statement. This will be a firm-fixed price contract expected to be awarded to a prime contractor in or around early May 2026. Service Contract Act (SCA) Wage Determinations for Cuyahoga and Summit Counties, OH will apply. (vii) Period of performance will be from July 1, 2026 to June 30, 2027, with government option to exercise up to four additional option years. Work will take place throughout Cuyahoga Valley National Park. (viii - xiii) Please see the attached Combined Synopsis-Solicitation for a complete list of provisions and clauses that apply to this solicitation. (xiv) Not applicable. (xv) Offers are due at 12:00 PM ET on Tuesday April 28, 2026 via email to jarrod_brown@ios.doi.gov. Please reference the solicitation number in email subject line. Please include the following items with quote package: 1. Price Schedule 2. Offeror Evidence of Technical Capability & Evidence of Prior Experience 3. Offeror Information (SAM Unique Entity ID, Point of Contact Email Address) 4. Acknowledgement of Solicitation Amendments via SF 30 (if applicable) (xvi) Contact Jarrod Brown at jarrod_brown@ios.doi.gov or 234-571-7515 for information regarding this solicitation.

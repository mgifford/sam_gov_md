# Vertical Transportation Equipment (VTE)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-06 10:52:34.42-04

## Summary

Holloman AFB, NM is seeking information of interested vendors for Vertical Transportation Equipment (VTE) Maintenance, Repair & Inspection Services, which are to be provided for a Base Year and Four (4) Option Years (OY). This requirement entails maintenance and inspection services IAW attached DRAFT Performance Work Statement (PWS). The contractor shall be capable of fulfilling this requirement in its' entirety. NOTE: Further revisions or changes may be made and sent out with any future solicitation (if and when a solicitation is issued). Responses to this sources sought are not offers and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. The Government reserves the right to determine how it should proceed as a result of this notice. Furthermore, those who respond to this sources sought should not anticipate feedback with regard to its submission. The Government will not pay any cost incurred in response to this sources sought.

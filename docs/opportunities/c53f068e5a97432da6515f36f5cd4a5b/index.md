## Summary

The 42d Contracting Squadron, Maxwell AFB, AL, is conducting market research to identify qualified sources capable of providing services of briefing a 1-hr session for the 2026 Chief Orientation Course to instill warrior ethos, mental toughness, accountability, and teamwork. All feedback and information received may be used to determine the appropriate acquisition strategy.

# 2026 South Dakota Military Youth Camp

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 15:49:51.051-04
- Solicitation Number: W912MM26QA011

## Summary

The South Dakota National Guard Child & Youth Program will be conducting the 2026 South Dakota Military Youth Camp for youth ages 9-14 on June 23-25th, 2026. This event provides the opportunity for military youth to connect with each other in a camp-setting with a programming focus on leadership, team building, resilience, life skills, personal development, and military values. Youth Camp is a core program element of the National Guard Child & Youth Program. The selected offeror will provide the required meeting space, food, lodging and outdoor activities for the event listed on this solicitation. This is a full and open solicitation. Questions must be submitted in writing NLT 8 April 2026 to shelly.j.thomson.civ@army.mil.

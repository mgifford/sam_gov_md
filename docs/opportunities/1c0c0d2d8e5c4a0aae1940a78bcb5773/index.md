# AS9100/AS9110 Certification

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-27 10:42:59.905-04
- Solicitation Number: FA813226Q0024

## Summary

The Air Force intends to solicit and award a firm fixed price, single source purchase order contract to Performance Review Institute (PRI) of Warrendale, PA ; CAGE Code 1N0B4, under the authority of RFO FAR 12.201-1, for a three (3) year contract for ISO 9001:2015 AS9100D:2016 initial certification and AS9110C:20116 recertification for the Oklahoma City Air Logistics Complex (OC-ALC)/QA (Quality Assurance) for the period of 1 May 2026 – 30 April 2029. The NAICS code for this requirement is 541380 with a size standard of $19 Million. Contractor registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) – which results in receiving a unique, active CAGE Code – is required prior to receiving any contracts or orders from the Government in accordance with RFO FAR 4.203-1(b). Contractors can register electronically at https://sam.gov/ . Interested persons may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement by submitting a quote and information. Electronic procedures will be used for this solicitation through SAM at https://sam.gov/. No telephone requests.

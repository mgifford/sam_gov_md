- Posted: 2026-04-08 05:37:09.474-04
- Solicitation Number: DJA-26-ALAB-PR-0197

## Summary

04/07/2026: Extend the solicitation closing date. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 15A00026Q00000042 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the procurement of reagents and supplies for ongoing need for DNA analysis at Forensic Science Laboratory – Wichita under a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA). Please see the “Statement of Work”, Attachment A and "SF1449_15A00026Q00000042 " for full description of the requirement and for the list of contract line item number and items, quantities, units of measures. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 339113. The period of performance is estimated to be: 04/30/2026 through 04/29/2031. Actual dates will be determined at award. See “SF1449_15A00026Q00000042” for a list of solicitation provisions that apply to the acquisition. See “SF1449_15A00026Q00000042” for a list of contract clauses that apply to the acquisition. See “SF1449_15A00026Q00000042” for the date, time, and place for receipt of offer. Steven Jaimes, steven.jaimes@atf.gov is the point of contact. All questions regarding this solicitation shall be submitted via email to Steven Jaimes at Steven.Jaimes@atf.gov and shall reference the solicitation, 15A00026Q00000042. All questions shall be received by 1000 EST, 04/03/2026. All quotes shall be submitted via email to Steven.Jaimes@atf.gov by the solicitation response date in Block 8, pg 1, of “SF1449_15A00026Q00000042” or the amended date (if applicable). Quotes received after the exact time specified are considered “late”.

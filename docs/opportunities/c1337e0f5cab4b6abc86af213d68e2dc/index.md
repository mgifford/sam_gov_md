# Dam Intake Debris Removal Services, Union Village Dam, East Thetford, VT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 14:07:02.263-05
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA057

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - New England District is issuing a Solicitation Notice for Dam Intake Debris Removal Services, Union Village Dam, East Thetford, VT. THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Furnish all labor, equipment, and transportation necessary to remove and transport, approximately one hundred (100) cubic yards of river debris from the intake (trash racks) at Union Village Dam. Material will be dumped onsite in an area designated by Technical Point of Contact (TPOC). All work shall be performed in accordance with the Performance Work Statement. This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 562119 with a Small Business Size Standard of $47 Million.

# Install Lift Station- FCI Bastrop

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 16:06:26.642-04
- Solicitation Number: 15BBNF26Q00000062

## Summary

Solicitation The Federal Bureau of Prisons (FBOP) is issuing solicitation 15BBNF26Q00000062 for the award of a firm-fixed-price construction contract for a project entitled Install Lift Station at the Federal Correctional Institution (FCI Bastrop), located in Bastrop, TX. FCI Bastrop is a federal correctional facility located at 1341 Highway 95 North, Bastrop, TX, 78602. The project consists of the following: Remove an existing septic tank and associated piping to enable the installation of a new fiberglass manhole with grinder pump and new duplex sewer lift station in a precast concrete sump. The sewer system shall then be extended to a Corix Utilities tie-in location. The Contractor shall coordinate all work with the Utility and shall be responsible for any costs associated with the tie-in. See the Statement of Work/Specifications attachment included with the solicitation posting for the complete requirements of the work. The performance period for the project will be 210 Calendar Days from issuance of the notice to proceed. The NAICS code for this requirement is 237110 with a corresponding small business size standard of $45 million. The solicitation will be distributed solely through the Contract Opportunity website at https://www.sam.gov. Hard copies of the solicitation will not be made available. The website provides download instructions. The "Sensitive /Secure package Upload" option will be selected for this procurement. If your firm does not have the NAICS code listed in www.sam.gov required for this solicitation, an email may be forwarded to your firm requesting an explanation as to why your firm should be granted access.

## Summary

NV-PAHRANAGAT NWR - Southwestern Willow Flycatcher (SWLF) Assessment SWLF is an endangered subspecies of Willow Flycatcher. This work is for Survey, Monitoring, and Banding and requires 2 permits. Due to the difficulty of obtaining the permits and the tight timeline, the vendor is required to already have the permits. Vendor will submit evidence of: 1. USFWS permit to conduct surveys and disturb nesting endangered birds. 2. Existing, valid permit from the USGS Bird Banding Laboratory (BBL) authorizing auxiliary marking of SWFL in Nevada. 3. A written narrative explaining their experience with the endangered subspecies and the work in the SOW. See SOW in solicitation packet for more details. Contact Robert_Sung@ios.doi.gov for questions.

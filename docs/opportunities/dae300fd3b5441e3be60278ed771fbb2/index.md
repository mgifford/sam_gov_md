# Coconino Portal Sign Installation

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-11 15:08:17.451-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAX26C0015
- Awardee: BDS SOLUTIONS LIMITED LIABILITY CO Clarkson KY 42726 USA
- Award Amount: 182190.00

## Summary

This project will consist of the removal and disposal of concrete and rock masonry portal sign bases in clear zones, the removal and disposal of existing wood portal signs, construction of new portal sign bases, and installation of Government-Furnished signs at 8 different sites across the Coconino National Forest, three (3) on the Flagstaff Ranger District, three (3) on the Mogollon Rim Ranger District, and two (2) on the Red Rock Ranger District. This project also includes optional work items for the removal and disposal of concrete and rock masonry portal sign bases at five (5) additional sites; the removal and disposal of a wood post base at one (1) additional site; the removal of existing wood portal signs at a total of ten (10) sites, four (4) additional sites; the installation of steel, square posts and aluminum signs at four (4) sites; and the installation of wooden portal signs at four (4) sites. Coordination with various government agencies will be required.

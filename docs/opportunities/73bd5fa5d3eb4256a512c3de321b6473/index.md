# INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY CLIENT SERVICES

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-13 11:59:03.82-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926

## Summary

The Naval Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) Fleet Logistics Center Norfolk (FLCN) is issuing this RFI as a means of conducting market research to identify parties having an interest in and the resources to support the requirement for a non-personal services contract to provide the Information Technology (IT) support in the Information Technology Services Division (ITSD) at the United States Naval Academy (USNA) and surrounding Annapolis Area Complex (AAC). The personnel will be working in the Client Services Department of ITSD with occasional work conducted for other departments within ITSD.

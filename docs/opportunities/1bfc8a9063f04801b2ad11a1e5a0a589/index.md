## Summary

This contract solicitation is for Serivce of Switch out the Manitex Model SC 97 Bed & Crane Assembly from old GSA Chassis to New GSA Chassis located at McAlester Army Ammunition Plant (McAAP). Please see below for details of the Statement of Work: Old GSA Chassis - G82-0536W International 7500 HT 570 New GSA Chassis - G81-C0047 Freightliner 114SD VIN: 1FVHG3FE4THWV5288, 11/2025 —54,600 GVWR Eaton Fuller 10 Speed Manual Transmission Key Service Tasks Remove and Install Old PTO Unit on new GSA Chassis Remove Crane and Install on New GSA Chassis Remove and Install Bed Assembly w/Outriggers on new GSA Chassis Replace and Replace any Hoses deemed unserviceable/leaking Remove and Reinstall Wiring Remove and Reinstall all Hydraulic Controls Remove and Reinstall Hydraulic Tank Similar Historical Contract Requirement NSN: AASSVC2101016R This solicitation is open to the Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) for this above described services/maintenence of Vechular Vehicles.

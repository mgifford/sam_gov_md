SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE Building 8 elevator repair VETERANS AFFAIRS MEDICAL CENTER SALEM, VA 24153 Secure the elevator in building 8 and once secured as appropriate, adjust and shorten the cable ropes for the elevator as appropriate.

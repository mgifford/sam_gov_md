# Tents for IDR 2026

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 17:03:36.405-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AR2026Q0013

## Summary

The requirement is for tents. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, which will be considered. The Government may award the contract based on the initial offer without discussion. The resultant contract will be a firm fixed price type contract. The Government is planning a pre-quotation conference and the date for this conference will be in the solicitation. All contractors must be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to submitting an offer pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. Therefore, prospective offerors are encouraged to register prior to the submittal of quotations/proposals. Electronic Submission/Responses: This opportunity allows for electronic responses. Please submit your offer prior to the response date and time. Please ensure your email with your offer does not exceed (30 MB), if it does then you might have to send more than one email. PLACE OF CONTRACT PERFORMANCE: Buenos Aires, Argentina

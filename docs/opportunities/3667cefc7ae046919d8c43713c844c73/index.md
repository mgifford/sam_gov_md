# Praia - Skydio X10D Drone Acquisition

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-25 13:56:17.567-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15818790

## Summary

Dears, The U.S. Department of State intends to award a contract for drone acquisition on a brand name basis. The required item is identified as Skydio X10D Drone. This procurement is being conducted under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b)(1) and FAR 11.105, as only the specified brand name items will satisfy the agency’s requirements for durability, functionality, and compatibility with existing equipment. A Justification and Approval (J&A) supporting this brand name procurement has been prepared in accordance with FAR 6.303 and FAR 6.305. The J&A details the rationale for restricting this acquisition to the specified brand name and is posted with this notice for public review.

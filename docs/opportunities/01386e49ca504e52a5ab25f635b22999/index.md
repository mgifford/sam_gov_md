# Aircraft Computer Maintenance Management System

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 14:23:37.769-04
- Solicitation Number: 12760426Q0005-2

## Summary

The intent of this solicitation and any resultant contract is to obtain an Aircraft Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) for the United States Forest Service (USFS) aircraft fleet. This product shall be a Configurable Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) Software and currently in aviation industry use and can be configured to support the USFS aircraft fleet.

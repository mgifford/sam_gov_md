# Vault Toilet Pumping Service for The Wallowa Whitman National Forest and Hells Canyon National Recreation Area

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:58:16.885-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240BE26Q0011

## Summary

The Wallowa Whitman National Forest requires Vault Toilet Pumping Service. Work scope is an estimated 76 pumps annually on 137 vault toilets in 92 locations throughout the Wallowa Whitman National Forest Baker City, Oregon and The Hells Canyon National Recreation Area, Riggins, Idaho. See ATCH-2_1240BE26Q0011 _PWS: Performance Work Statement, Vault Toilet Pumping for the Wallowa Whitman National Forest. Multiple awards may be made from this solicitation.

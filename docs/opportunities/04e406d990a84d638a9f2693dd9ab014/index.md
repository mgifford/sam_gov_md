## Summary

CONTRACTORS INTERESTED IN PARTICIPATING IN THIS INDUSTRY DAY MUST RESPOND PER THIS NOTICE. This announcement is released by the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Strategic Acquisition Center-Frederick (SAC-F) seeking contractors interested in providing services to the Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA), Medical Disability Examination Office (MDEO). VA anticipates conducting a series of general MDEO Industry Days with the goal of promoting engagement and learning about industry ideas and capabilities related to the work tasked to MDEO. These events are to be general in nature, not in response to a specific MDEO requirement or upcoming contract action. VA anticipates other market research and/or industry days for specific new/recompete contract actions. The first Industry Day is anticipated for Spring 2026 with future industry days planned at VA s discretion. All MDEO Industry Days are being conducted for market research purposes only. See attached Industry Day Notice for full details.

# Markland LD Mowing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 09:13:21.589-04
- Solicitation Number: W912QR26QA024

## Summary

This Request for Quote (RFQ) is for the Contractor shall furnish all necessary management, personnel, materials, supplies, tools, equipment, fuel, and vehicles, except as otherwise provided herein, required to perform mowing services at the Markland Locks and Dam project recreation and operational areas, as specified and in strict accordance with all Terms, Conditions, General, Specific and Technical Provisions, Drawings, Attachments, Exhibits, etc., contained herein or incorporated by reference. Quotes are due on 7 April 2026 at 1:00 PM Eastern Time.NOTE: THIS RFQ IS 100% SET ASIDE FOR SMALL BUSINESS. The NAICS code for this procurement is 561730. The size standard is $9.5M. The solicitation will be available on the web only. Telephone and Fax requests for this solicitation will not be honored. Project files are Portable Document Format (PDF) files and can view, navigated, or printed using Adobe Acrobat Reader. To download the solicitation for this project, contractors are required to register at the Federal Business Opportunities. Amendments will be available from the beta.sam.gov website by download only. Prior to submission of quotes all contractors must have an active registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) database. To register or update information go to http://www.SAM.gov. Questions may be addressed to justin.m.mcpherson@usace.army.mil. This announcement serves as the advance notice.

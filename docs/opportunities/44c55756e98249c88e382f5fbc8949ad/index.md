# CGC WAESCHE AERIAL TRAINING

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 14:22:35.757-04
- Solicitation Number: SFLCPROCUREMENTBRANCH70Z085

## Summary

Purpose and objective CGC WAESCHE seeks to purchase service for Aerial Lift Training to conduct maintenance by ETs, ITs, and EMs, as well as Hull Preservation by Deck. Scope Dates: May 27th, 28th and 29th 2026 People: Training for up to 10 Coast Guard Active Duty Member each day (30 people total) Classroom: Beginning at 0700, provide no less than one hour of Classroom training on Safety, Mishap Prevention, Operations, Emergency Response, and Best Practices. Hands On: Following Classroom Training on the same day with the same 10 people, provide no less than five hours of operations training with the Aerial Lift to include, Equipment Familiarization, Pre-Use Inspections, Safety Equipment, Moving Aerial Lifts around the pier and roadways on base, Working Aloft with the aerial lift, Emergency Response, Stowing Aerial Lifts, Post-Use Inspection, and Evaluation of Trainee’s ability to use equipment. A 30-minute lunch break will be provided at 1300 if additional time is needed. Post Training: Provide Proof of Training/Certification. Allow all presentation materials to be accessible by trainees after the training for reference. The contractor shall: Only provide the service outlined in the scope of work. The unit will not accept substitutions. Provide training on the Coast Guard Base Alameda’s Aerial Lift. Provide training for 30 people over three days at a pace of 10 people each day. Training will be held at Coast Guard Island, 1 Eagle Road Alameda, CA 94501 A classroom on base or on board WAESCHE will be provided with a TV and HDMI access. WiFi or wired internet will not be accessible. Provide names of instructors no later than May 22nd 2026 for Base Alameda and WAESCHE access.

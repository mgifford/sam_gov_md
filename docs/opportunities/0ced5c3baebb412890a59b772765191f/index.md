# Sole Source to Deep South Crane Rentals for Bottlefield Crane Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 08:50:35.877-04
- Solicitation Number: N61331-26-Q-JK11

## Summary

Sole Source to Deep South Crane Rentals, Inc. See attachment with Clauses. Synopsis NEDU BOTTLEFIELD CRANE SERVICE The Naval Surface Warfare Center Panama City Division, FL (NSWC PCD) intends to award a sole source, firm fixed price purchase order using simplified acquisition procedures to Deep South Crane, LLC for the crane rental service required to lift and move numerous 20 ton gas battles to a radius of up to 50 feet within the NEDU facility. This procurement is sole source for the following reason: Deep South Crane is the sole vendor with the equipment capable of executing the lift. Furthermore, Deep South Crane is uniquely qualified due to their experience with NEDU and NASPC requirements. As such, only Deep South Crane is capable of performing the required tasking. Destination to the Navy Experimental Diving Unit Panama City Division (NEDU), 321 Bullfinch Road, Panama City, FL 32407.

# USS SAMPSON (DDG 102) FY26 Depot Modernization Period (DMP)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-20 04:48:53.921-04
- Solicitation Number: N00024-26-C-4411
- Awardee: VIGOR MARINE LLC Portland OR 97217 USA
- Award Amount: 253195124.00

## Summary

Vigor Marine LLC, Portland, Oregon was awarded a $252,849,606 firm-fixed-price (FFP) contract action for maintenance, modernization, and repair of USS SAMPSON (DDG 102) Fiscal 2026 Depot Modernization Period. The scope of this acquisition includes all labor, supervision, equipment, production, testing, facilities, and quality assurance necessary to prepare for and accomplish the Chief of Naval Operations (CNO) Availability for critical modernization, maintenance, and repair programs. This contract includes options, which, if exercised, would bring the cumulative value of this contract to $253,195,124. This contract was not competitively procured, but in accordance with 10 U.S. Code 3204(a)(3)(Industrial Mobilization).

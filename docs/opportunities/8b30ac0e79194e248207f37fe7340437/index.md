## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number FA480326Q0053 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ) for the repair of one JLG Man Lift in accordance with the solicitation and all attachments. This acquisition is a total small business set-aside.This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The repairs must be deemed acceptable and complete within 14 calendar days from contract award. The point of contact for this solicitation is SSgt Braulio Ramirez at braulio.ramirez_galvan@us.af.mil All questions are due on 9 April 2026 at 12:00 PM EDT.

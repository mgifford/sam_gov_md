## Summary

Naval Surface Warfare Center, Port Hueneme Division (NSWC PHD), has awarded a sole source Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) contract to provide Oriole Rocket Motor Systems. Kratos Space and Missile Defense Systems, San Diego, California, is being awarded a contract with a total value (inclusive of all options) of $49,963,267.

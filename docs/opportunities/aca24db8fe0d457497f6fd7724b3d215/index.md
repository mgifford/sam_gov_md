# Platform Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-16 10:05:22.557-04
- Solicitation Number: JA25010MOD02

## Summary

This effort includes all personnel, management, and supervision necessary to provide operation and maintenance (O&M), management, security, and availability of the J6 IT environments. This includes server management and mainframe services; systems security support, storage services and specialized system management services such as space management; configuration management; and other operational support services located in the Pentagon and at the alternate site locations.

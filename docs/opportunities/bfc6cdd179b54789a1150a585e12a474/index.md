# USACE Rock Island District, Mississippi River Project Office Recreation Area Dumpster Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:53:44.205-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26MRPORADS

## Summary

Please see the attached DRAFT Performance Work Statement and associated Appendix A. The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers – Rock Island District is conducting market research to determine if there are qualified firms capable of providing dumpster services for several recreation areas within Rock Island District’s footprint. Please respond to this sources sought with the following information: Are you a small business under NAICS code 562111? Are you capable of providing dumpster services at ALL of the recreation areas listed in Appendix A? Provide examples of current or recent contracts for which you provided dumpster services across a geographical area similar to this requirement.

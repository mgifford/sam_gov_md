Type of Contract: Firm Fixed Price General Requirement: The Lebanon VA Medical Center (LVAMC) has a requirement for fourteen (14) Vital Sign Monitors and accessories to be utilized in the Primary Care unit. The Primary Care unit requires vital sign monitors to accurately address issues that may arise during a patient examination. The vital sign monitors that are currently used by the Primary Care unit are in need of life cycle replacement.

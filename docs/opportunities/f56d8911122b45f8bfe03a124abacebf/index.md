## Summary

INVITATION FOR BIDS (IFB) NOTICE 2-1-QSC-T-26-062 Mixed Scrap Metal NASA Marshall Space Flight Center Huntsville, AL 35812 OVERVIEW Commodity: DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap Location: NASA Marshall Space Flight Center, Huntsville, AL 35812 Contract Type: Term Contract (3-year base with up to two 1-year options) Estimated Volume: Approximately 1,200,000 pounds annually Disposal Requirement: Material must be recycled for material content only SOLICITATION ACCESS This solicitation is available for review and bidding on GSA Auctions. GSA Auctions Link: https://gsaauctions.gov/auctions/preview/360573 All solicitation documents, including the IFB and Appendices, are located in the Documents section of the GSA Auctions listing.

## Summary

Shelf Stocking, Receiving/Storage/Holding Area (RSHA), and Custodial Services at the Miramar MCAS Commissary. The Government anticipates issuing a solicitation for shelf stocking, receiving/storage/holding area (RSHA), and custodial services at the Miramar MCAS Commissary.

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0394 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the replacement of fire sprinkler heads at the USDA ARS BARC in Beltsville, MD. Quotes are due by 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time on May 4, 2026. Quotes shall be emailed to Michael.Sherlock@usda.gov. The magnitude of construction for this project is between $100,000 and $200,000. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 238220 The small business size standard is $19.0M This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. Please see the attachment for important information on how to RSVP for the site visit, site visit reporting instructions, and other important documents including how and where to submit quotes.

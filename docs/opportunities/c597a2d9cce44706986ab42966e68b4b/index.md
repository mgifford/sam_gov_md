# 1690 - AFNWC Fiber to the Desk

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 05:15:53.593-04
- Solicitation Number: FA853526Q0003

## Summary

***Answers posted as of 23 April The intent of this contract is to install Fiber-To-The-Desk (FTTD) within the rooms of B1109 (2nd floor) located at Hanscom AFB. The contractor must be capable of implementing TAA/NDAA compliant cabling solutions, strict security, performance, and compliance standards, identify compatibility with Cisco Room Kit-based VTC systems in classified/unclassified environments and understand labor and material sourcing timelines to determine if 60-day period of performance. Please see SOW and 1449 for additional information.

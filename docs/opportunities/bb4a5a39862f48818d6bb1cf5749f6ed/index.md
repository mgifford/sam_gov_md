# American Samoa Class II Research Vessel

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-24 16:24:25.395-04
- Solicitation Number: -2380-NMSAS-VESSEL

## Summary

This requirement is for one new and unused Class II research vessel for operations in American Samoa. Delivery of the vessel is required within fifteen (15) months of award. The vessel shall carry science and dive parties of up to nine persons total. The vessel will conduct the following missions: Monitoring, coral restoration, water sampling, mooring buoys, reef and fish surveys, partner missions, education & outreach, and emergency response. Please refer to the attached Sources Sought Notice and draft Statement of Work for additional details.

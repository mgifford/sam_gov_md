# Building 640 Furniture

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 17:26:31.82-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462626Q0014

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number FA462626Q0014 is issued as a Request for Quote for Building 640 Furniture. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The 341st Civil Engineering Squadron Unaccompanied Housing Management Office is seeking all of the following items OR EQUAL TO: - (144) XL Twin beds with frames - (148) Two drawer dresser/wardrobe - (148) Desks -(148) Dining Chairs -(76) Dining Tables This requirement will be used to furnish an on base dormitory. The mission partner is seeking a furniture in compliance with Statement of Work. All bedroom items must be matching sets. All items shall be not be upholstered nor made from manufactured wood (i.e. Particle board, MDF, pressboard). Contractor shall provide delivery of solid construction and fully assembled items, as well as install and remove previous items. All shipping/handling, installation, removal, and disposal costs must be included in total price. Questions pertaining to this requirement shall be submitted no later than 2:00 PM MST, 7 April 2026 to SSgt Perry at william.perry.27@us.af.mil and the Contracting Officer Charles McClintic at charles.mcclintic@us.af.mil.

# Disintegrator Repair Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 16:11:36.491-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226QA046

## Summary

The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, and OEM-certified technical services to perform one-time maintenance and repair on the SEM Disintegrator Model 23/4 located in Building 4486, Fort Drum, NY. Work includes installation of OEM V-belts, replacement of OEM cutting knives, and a full OEM maintenance inspection. All work shall be performed by SEM-certified or SEM-authorized technicians and completed no later than 30 April 2026.

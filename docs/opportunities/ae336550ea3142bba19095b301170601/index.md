# 19AQMM26N0033: Enterprise Physical Security Information Management (PSIM) Platform Global Integration, Engineering, and Operations Support

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-09 12:46:42.795-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AQMM26N0033

## Summary

The Government is seeking industry capability information from firms with demonstrated experience integrating and sustaining enterprise Physical Security Information Management (PSIM) systems within secure government or similarly controlled environments. The requirement involves engineering, integration, sustainment, and operational support of a globally deployed PSIM platform integrating multiple physical security subsystems including enterprise video management systems, access control systems, intrusion detection systems, GIS-based facility mapping, 3-D modeling environments, and advanced video analytics capabilities including facial recognition and license plate recognition technologies.

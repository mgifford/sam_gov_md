# Joint Standoff Weapon (JSOW)/Harpoon Block II+ (HII+) TacNet 1.0 Series Weapon Datalink Reconfiguration and Repairs for the U.S.Navy (USN)

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR), Precision Strike Weapons Program Office (PMA-201), awarded a Cost-Plus-Fixed-Fee (CPFF) Delivery Order (N0001925F2557) on 30 September 2025 to Rockwell Collins, INC, against Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) N00421-21-G-0005.This delivery order is for the repair and reconfiguration of Joint Standoff Weapon (JSOW)/Harpoon Block II+ (HII+) TacNet

1.0 Series Weapon Datalink radios. This DO was awarded on a sole source basis under the statutory authority of 10 U.S.C.3204(a)(1) as implemented by Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subpart 6.302-1(a)(2)(ii)(B) and FAR subpart 6.302-1(a)(2)(iii)(B), only one responsible source and no other services will satisfy agency requirements. Rockwell Collins, Inc is the only known, qualified, responsible source that can fulfill the requirements herein. Rockwell Collins, INC 400 Cedar Rapids IA, 52498-0505

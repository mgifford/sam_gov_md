# Construction of Curb Ramps

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 07:37:27.473-04
- Solicitation Number: 19L16026Q0001

## Summary

The contractor is required to construct curb ramps at the areas that do not have ramps and to increase the width of the Accessibility Parking by two (2) feet; NEC Cover Parking Lot. The length and width of the ramps are 4.43 ft x 4.43 ft respectively. The contractor must ensure the works meet the Architectural Barriers Act (ABA), and/or other relevant facility accessibility Codes, including slope, dimensions, landing, and railing requirements, and secure building permits, when required.

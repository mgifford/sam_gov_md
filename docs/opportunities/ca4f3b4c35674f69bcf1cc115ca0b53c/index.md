## Summary

This is a pre-solicitation notice. Please note that all attached documents, including the Statement of Work, are preliminary versions and are subject to change. All detailed requirements, technical specifications, and submission instructions, once finalized, will be provided in the formal solicitation. Interested vendors are encouraged to review these draft documents for a comprehensive understanding of the anticipated project scope.This project is a Design-Build requirement to construct a Mission Training Complex and renovate the associated access road at the Taukgolo Range Complex. The scope includes the new construction of an auditorium, classrooms, and a latrine facility, along with a multi-tiered improvement of the existing access road.

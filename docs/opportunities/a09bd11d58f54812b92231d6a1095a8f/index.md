# Sole Source Award to Phillips Corporation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 10:36:55.915-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700426Q0018

## Summary

Marine Depot Maintenance Command has a requirement for the ‘cartridge filter exchange set’ for the EOS M-400 machines is needed to maintain production readiness, provide the engineer techs the proper material to safely clean out each 3D Printing machine, and create/deliver items to customers in a timely manner. This item includes an F9 cartridge filter, transport container, PTFE seals, and 7 KG of quartz sand. The Government is awarding a purchase order to Phillips Federal (EOS division) on a sole source basis.

# Design Build IDIQ MATOC in Support of the DHA Program in the Western Region

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 15:02:25.131-04
- Solicitation Number: W9127826RA017

## Summary

This is a competitive, design bid build (DBB) solicitation for an Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ), Multiple Award Task Order Contract (MATOC) for military healthcare and medical research facility repair, new and minor construction in support of the Defense Health Agency?s (DHA) major medical centers, clinics, and laboratories throughout the United States Western Region (Alaska, Arizona, California, Colorado, Idaho, Montana, Nevada, New Mexico, Oregon, Utah, Washington, and Wyoming). The Western Region MATOC pool will consist of an Unrestricted pool with a Small Business (SB) reserve under North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 236220. This is Phase I of a Two Phase Solicitation. Phase I - Amendment 0001 (see attachment)

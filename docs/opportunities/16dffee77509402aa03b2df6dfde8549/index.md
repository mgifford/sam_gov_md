# Joint Planning and Execution Services (JPES) & Joint Capabilities Requirements Manager (JCRM) Software Sustainment Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Consolidate/(Substantially) Bundle
- Posted: 2026-03-25 09:18:36.791-04
- Solicitation Number: 622572376

## Summary

This procurement is intended to provide sustainment support for JPES and JCRM and optimize efficiencies of the mission by consolidating three contracts/orders into a singular task order. JPES, which has been recently modernized and will reach Full Operational Capability (FOC) in second quarter of the Calendar Year 2026, replaces the Joint Operation Planning and Execution System (JOPES), which is used to monitor, plan, and execute mobilization, deployment, employment, and sustainment activities associated with joint operations and exercises. JCRM is a tool that takes operational plans entered into JOPES/JPES and creates orders for those plans, which in turn are sent to the Secretary of the Department of Defense (DoD) for authorization. The JPES & JCRM Software Sustainment Services procurement is a new requirement; however, portions of this new requirement are currently being executed under the following: Contract #1: Task Order Number: 47QTCA15D001Q / HC104725F4032 Contract #2: Contract Number: HC104725C4001 Contract #3: Contract Number: HC104720D2045 See Attached Consolidation Determination and Findings for more information. This notification is related to Sources Sought Notice (SSN) 622572376.

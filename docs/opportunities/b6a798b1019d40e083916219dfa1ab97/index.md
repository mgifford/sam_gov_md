# Request for Information (RFI) - Audio Visual (AV) / Video Teleconferencing (VTC) Lifecycle Information Technology (IT) Support (AV LIT)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-20 14:50:05.448-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8204-26-D-XXXX

## Summary

The Air Force Nuclear Weapon Center (AFNWC) Intercontinental Ballistic Missile (ICBM) Systems Directorate (SD) team is conducting market research to define the scope and requirements of the Audio Visual (AV)/ Video Teleconferencing (VTC) Lifecycle Information Technology (IT) Support (AV LIT). For this RFI, Government is seeking input from industry on the capability to operate within a Special Access Program (SAP) environment. Please see attached RFI for additional information.

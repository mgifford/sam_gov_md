# CPR & AED Program Oversight and Training

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-03-17 10:53:37.903-04

## Summary

This Department of Justice solicitation (RFQ) is intended to support the Security Staff, Office of Administration, Criminal Division. The contractor should be able to provide AED, medical, and program oversight to satisfy the D.C. regulations, while providing technical support with periodic maintenance and troubleshooting/unscheduled maintenance of AED equipment, and to provide and track annual training requirements for CRM employees. The contract will be for one base year (12 months) and include four one-year options to extend the term of the contract up to a total of 60 months. This solicitation (RFQ) is 100% set-aside for small business. NAICS 611430 (Size Standard: $15 million) applies to this procurement. All contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) website to be eligible for award of contract. Please see the following attachments in reference to the subject RFQ: 1) Statement of Work 2) Level of Effort (LOE) Submission Instructions: 1) I am requesting that all quotations, using the spreadsheet provided, be returned to my attention by COB on Tuesday, March 19, 2026. Early submissions will be accepted and appreciated in this effort if possible. 2) Please also note that it is imperative that any and all questions regarding this effort are to be directed back only to the Contracting Officer (myself) to eliminate possible disqualification. Feel free to contact me with any questions or concerns regarding this request. I will also welcome negative replies of non-bidding.

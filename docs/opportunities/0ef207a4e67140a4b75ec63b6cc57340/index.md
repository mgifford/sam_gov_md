# Sources Sought: Nuclear Stores Interface Units (NSIU)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-09 07:38:14.599-04
- Solicitation Number: Sources_Sought_NSIU_AFNWC

## Summary

AFNWC/NDB is seeking information from Industry to assist with the establishment of a production contract in support of LRSO integration on the B-52 weapon system. The equipment to be produced in this build-to-print effort is the Nuclear Stores Interface Unit (NSIU) and will be integrated into the Suspension Underwing Unit-103 (SUU-103) pylons and Nuclear Rotary Launcher (NRL). Overview/Background: The NSIU is a Line Replaceable Unit (LRU) providing an electronic interface to allow communication between the aircraft platform and LRSO store. The NSIU is designed to update connections to meet current MIL-STD-1760 requirements and enhance nuclear hardness. AFNWC/NDB is looking for vendors with the capability to produce the NSIU to the provided specifications and quality standards as a build-to-print manufacturing effort, which will not require hardware installation.

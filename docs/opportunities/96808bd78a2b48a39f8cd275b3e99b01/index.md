## Summary

The Cincinnati VA Medical Center has a requirement for the replacement of the iDXA scanner, performing minor architectural renovations, installing a ceiling-mounted patient lift, and making necessary electrical and data upgrades in Room C211 located in building 1 within the Cincinnati VA Medical Center. The contractor shall furnish all supervision, labor, tools, transportation, miscellaneous materials, supplies and equipment necessary for the completion of this work. All services provided under this contract must be performed in conformance with all the applicable regulations, codes standards and specifications, including but not limited to VA, OSHA, NIST requirements, etc. All other materials and activities such as conduit, cabling, terminations and testing shall be provided by the contractor.

# H Dept Weapons Control and Integration Prototyping and Fabrication

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:51:43.316-04
- Solicitation Number: N0017827R4402

## Summary

This is a Draft RFP notice for release to industry comment/questions prior to final RFP being released. The Weapons Control and Integration Department (H Department) at Naval Surface Warfare Center, Dahlgren Division (NSWCDD) requires; design, fabrication, rapid prototyping, special acquisition, technology integration related to circuit board design, Radio Frequency (RF) distribution assemblies, mechanical fabrication, fabric assemblies, battery assemblies, and mechanical/electromechanical assemblies. NSWCDD intends to issue a base MAC IDIQ Contract lasting 5 years through issuance of FFP and CPFF Delivery Order(s) competed as a Competitive Full and Open requirement.

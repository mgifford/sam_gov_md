# Harris Mobile Radio Installation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 11:14:12.126-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U3048

## Summary

Aberdeen Proving Ground FES is seeking proposals from qualified upfitters/technicians to perform the installation of professional mobile radio systems from Harris (Model#14050-1150-01), Transceiver Model#X2-MPM1M) into two specific apparatus: one Command Unit and one Hazmat Unit. The goal is to ensure seamless communication capabilities within our emergency response fleet. Installation Requirements per Vehicle-? Mounting: Secure installation of one remote-mount transceiver and one control head unit.? Cabling: Routing of all power, ground, audio, and data cables.? Antenna System: Installation of a high-gain mobile antenna, including drilling/mounting and cable routing to the transceiver.? Audio: Installation of hand microphones and external high-output speakers for clear audio in high-decibel environments.? Testing: Comprehensive on-air testing to verify clear transmission and reception.***A more detailed Scope of Work is attached in the documents.

# Express Request for Vehicles - Expeditions, Tahoes, F250 and F350

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 14:26:26.949-04
- Solicitation Number: 47QMCA26Q0042

## Summary

Amendment 001 - See Amendment 001 PDF and Attachment B - Specifications_2026-03-19 This requirement is for the brand name or equal purchase of Ford Expeditions (QTY:23), Chevy Tahoe (QTY: 20) Ford F250 (QTY:12) and Ford F350 (QTY: 4), see Attachments for additional vehicle and solicitation details. This requirement is a 100% small business set-aside. The Government intends to award firm-fixed price delivery order(s) as a result of this solicitation, specifically Motor Vehicle Delivery Order (MVDO). The Government reserves the right to make one single award to one vendor or multiple awards to multiple vendors.

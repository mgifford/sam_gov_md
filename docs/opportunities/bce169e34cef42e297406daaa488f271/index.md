## Summary

Project No.: CL25342001 Classification Code: 2530 NAICS:, 336390 - Other Motor Vehicle Parts Manufacturing Title: Pump Assembly, Power DLA Land & Maritime is preparing to solicit the NSN(s) (see NSNs listed below) for a Commercial Indefinite Quantity long-term contract. These items are spare parts for the TRUCK, VEHICLE SYSTEM, 1 1/4-TON (HMMWV)and both AM General (34623) and P55 Limited (K6019) are the only approved sources. DLA Land & Maritime does NOT have any technical data with which to procure these items. The purpose of this synopsis is to locate sources interested in becoming approved sources. Please read about all of the information required before submitting an alternate offer. Responses must be sent to Michael Gallina via email at Michael.Gallina@dla.mil.

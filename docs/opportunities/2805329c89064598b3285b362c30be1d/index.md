## Summary

Please see attachments for more details. All attendees shall park in the gravel lot located in the front oval near the flagpoles on the Fayetteville VAMC campus, 1100 North College Avenue. Contractor parking is located at the back of the gravel lot, identified by red parking stops. VAPD has requested that all site?visit attendees use this lot, and they will be notified in advance to ensure there are no issues with parking. After parking, attendees shall proceed directly to the gravel lot contractor parking area, where the Government Point of Contact, Autumn Chappell, will meet the group. Autumn will be present at this location from 9:45 AM until 10:00 AM CST to check in attendees and assemble the group. At 10:00 AM CST, the site visit will begin. Autumn will escort the group on foot from the gravel lot to Building 8, Room 001A, and then to the required outdoor locations, including the roll?off container sites. Please note that this route involves a moderate walk across campus. Late arrivals may not be accommodated, and no secondary visits will be conducted. Attendees should allow sufficient time for parking and walking to the meeting point. Feel free to take pictures of the site or outside of structure as required to perform the work; veterans, patients, employees and visitors may not be photographed.

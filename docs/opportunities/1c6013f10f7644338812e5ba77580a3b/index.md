# Gardening Services for the Embassy of the United States of America in Rabat, Morocco

- Posted: 2026-04-16 04:43:09.262-04

## Summary

Enclosed is a Request for Quotations (RFQ) for Gardening services at the US Embassy Rabat.If you would like to submit quotation, follow the instructions in Section 3 of the solicitation, complete the required portions of the attached document, and submit the completed quotation electronically to the email address provided below. The Government invites qualified professional entities to submit a quote by following the instructions in Section 3 of the solicitation, complete the required portions of the attached document, and submit it to the email address provided below. The Government will consider an award to other than the lowest priced offeror or other than the highest technically rated offeror. All evaluation factors and significant subfactors that will affect contract award, and their relative importance shall be, when combined, significantly more important than proposal cost or price. We are using procedures outlined in FAR Part 15 and the Tradeoff Process described in FAR 15.103-1. In a tradeoff source selection process, the Government may make award to a qualified offeror without holding exchanges, although communications may be held if determined to be in the best interest of the U.S. Government with offerors before or after a competitive range is established. The Embassy intends to conduct a mandatory site visit, on April 7, 2026, at 10:30 AM at the US Embassy Rabat. Prospective offerors should contact: rabatgsocontracts@state.gov no later than April 3, 2026, with the names and ID card information for those wishing to participate in the site visit. Please be sure to bring the solicitation document with you to the site visit. No extra copies will be provided. Questions are due by April 13, 2026, at 16:00 local time, via rabatgsocontracts@state.gov Quotations are due by April 22, 2026. No quotations will be accepted after this time. Proposals must be in English and incomplete proposals will not be accepted. Your quotation must be submitted electronically to rabatgsocontracts@state.gov and not exceed 30MB.

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs has awarded a firm-fixed price, single award Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to Mother Daughter Contracting Services LLC, effective 04/22/2026 04/21/2031. The total value for this five-year IDIQ contract is $2,139,696.00. Only the awarding organization can place orders against this contract. Description of services: The Contractor shall provide temporary lodging and shuttle services to veterans (including individuals accompanying the veteran) that are authorized to receive overnight lodging under the Ralph H. Johnson VA Medical Center Hoptel Program in accordance with the performance work statement.

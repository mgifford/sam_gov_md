# N66001-26-Q-6106 - Notice of Intent to Award Sole Source Order to H3D, Inc.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 13:38:19.611-04
- Solicitation Number: N66001-26-Q-6106

## Summary

This synopsis is hereby issued in accordance with FAR (Overhaul) Part 5.101. The purpose of this synopsis is to announce the Department of the Navy, Naval Information Warfare Center (NIWC) Pacifics intent to award a Firm-Fixed-Price purchase order on a noncompetitive basis to H3D, Inc. Entity ID KF7VYKHNEVN5, CAGE Code 6BW68, of 812 Avis DR Ann Arbor, Michigan 48108-9649, United States. The requirement is for the procurement of the following brand name Cadmium Zinc Telluride (CZT) gamma-ray detector packages: Manufacturer: H3D, Inc. Description: T410 Package including: 19 cm^3 CdZnTe; Detector control hardware; Real-time data collection and spectrum visualization; Real-time imaging reconstruction including coded aperture for low energies; Real-time isotope detection and identification; Post-processing user interface; Two-year tech support; Two-year warranty Quantity: 5 each; The Government intends to negotiate solely with H3D, Inc. under the authority of 41 U.S.C. 1901 as implemented by FAR (Overhaul) 12.102. FAR (Overhaul) Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services, applies. However, all responsible sources may submit a bid, proposal, or quotation which shall be considered by the agency. A copy of the Request for Quote (RFQ) will be made available upon request. The applicable NAICS Code is 334519 ? Other Measuring and Controlling Device Manufacturing and the size standard is 600 employees. Comments, questions or concerns regarding this notice may be submitted via e-mail ONLY to stephen.a.oneill8.civ@us.navy.mil by 14 April 2026, 11AM Pacific Time. All responses received by this date may be considered by the Government for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. A determination by the Government not to compete this requirement based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. All responding vendors must be registered to the System for Award Management (SAM). Information can be found at https://www.sam.gov. Complete SAM registration means a registered UEID and CAGE Code. The authorities and procedures cited in this notice are compliant with the recent acquisition reforms commonly referred to as the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul.

# UK-Wide Consolidated CCTV BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 10:52:15.64-04
- Solicitation Number: FA558726Q1003

## Summary

The Contractor must provide non-personal services, to include all personnel, equipment, tools, supervision, and other items and services necessary to ensure that comprehensive CCTV support is performed at Royal Air Force (RAF) Lakenheath, RAF Mildenhall, RAF Alconbury, RAF Molesworth, RAF Croughton, Fairford, RAF Blenheim Crescent, RAF Menwith Hill, RAF Welford, and RAF Barford St. John and RAF Feltwell. The objective is to achieve a fully functional, integrated, and sustainable CCTV capability that meets the minimum performance standards established in the attached Performance Work Statement (PWS). Individual call orders will provide specific details, such as blueprints and quantities, but must adhere to the core standards of the PWS.

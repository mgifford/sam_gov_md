# EAGLE Advance Planning Briefing for Industry (APBI)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-21 07:21:17.119-04

## Summary

Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) and Army Sustainment Command (ASC) plan to hold an Advance Planning Briefing for Industry (APBI) for the Enhanced Global Army Logistics Enterprise (EAGLE) program. The intent of the event is to provide updates to industry on the current and future state of the EAGLE program on 04 June 2026 at the Vibrant Arena located at 1201 River Dr, Moline, IL 61265. This posting is to notify Industry of the Government's intent to hold the APBI to assist with planning and travel arrangements. An agenda and registration information will be forthcoming in a subsequent posting. Additionally, the Government plans to hold another Industry Engagement with 1-on-1 sessions specifically for future EAGLE following this APBI. The date and location of this engagement is currently unknown. Please continue to monitor SAM.gov for updates.

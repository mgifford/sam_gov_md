# Leroux East Stewardship IRSC- Region 3- Southwest Stewardship BPA- Coconino NF, Flagstaff RD

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 16:50:33.858-04
- Solicitation Number: 12363N26Q4052

## Summary

This procurement is restricted to the approved companies eligible for call orders under the R3 Southwest Stewardship BPA for the Coconino National Forest in the state of Arizona. This requirement may be awarded to one vendor as a BPA Call Order against BPA's previously solicited on the Government Point of Entry (GPE). The government will not provide feedback or evaluations to companies regarding their submissions in response to this notice that are not eligible for awards under the R3 Southwest Stewardship BPA.

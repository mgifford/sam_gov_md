# H312--5-YR Fire Safety Inspection & Kitchen Hood Service

## Summary

The Department of Veteran s Affairs will solicit for vendors capable of providing 5-YR Fire Safety Inspections and Kitchen Hood Cleaning/Maintenance services for the Western NC Veterans Health Care System/Charles George VA Medical Center, located in Asheville, NC. This will be awarded as a Firm-Fixed contract for One (1) Annual Base Year with Four (4) Annual Option Years. A Request for Quotation, RFQ, will be issued on March 10, 2026. To be eligible for the award, the prospective contractor must be registered in SAM database at http://www.sam.gov and comply with the Evaluation for Basis of Award at the time the quote is submitted. The prospective contractor must also have updated and correct information in the VA Financial Service Center s Customer Engagement Portal. If you are an SDVOSB, VOSB or Small Business and plan on subcontracting, please provide a plan to show how your company provides 51% of the requirement. The North American Industry Classification Code, NAICS, is 541990 (Inspection-Fire Control Equipment), Product Service Code is H312 (All Other Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services) and the Size Standard is $19.5 Million. Interested and capable contractors should reach out to Satasha Stewart via the information provided below. Point of Contact Satasha Stewart Satasha.Stewart@va.gov *Please direct all questions to the above email address. Phone calls will not be accepted.

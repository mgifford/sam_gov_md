# Amended-Colville National Forest -GAOA Sullivan Lake Swim Dock Replacement

- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 15:34:10.647-04

## Summary

Amendment 1 -3/19/2026 The purpose of this amendment is to cancel Solicitation 1240BD26Q0003 in its entirety effective 19 March 2026. The Government has determined that changes to the requirement are necessary and are substantial enough to warrant a new competition. No award will be made under this solicitation. A new solicitation incorporating the revised specifications will be posted to SAM.gov shortly. All interested parties, including previous quoters, are encouraged to participate in the new requirement. Requirement Overview The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Forest Service, Colville National Forest (CNF), has a requirement for the Great American Outdoors Act (GAOA). This is a firm-fixed-price, non-personal services contract for the removal and disposal of an existing swim dock and the furnishing and installation of a new replacement swim dock at the Sullivan Lake Campground in Metaline Falls, WA. Project Scope The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision to perform the following: Removal & Disposal: Complete removal of the existing swim dock (currently on dry ground) and off-site disposal at a legal waste facility in accordance with Washington State regulations. New Dock Installation: Furnishing, transporting, and anchoring a new swim dock designed for seasonal water level fluctuations and high-wind conditions. Salient Characteristics Dimensions: Minimum 15’x15’ to Maximum 16’x16’. Construction: Marine-grade aluminum or galvanized steel frame with encapsulated foam flotation. Decking: Non-slip composite (light grey or cedar tone) with black dock edging. Safety Features: 4-to-5 step detachable swim ladder; flush coupling systems with rubber covers (if delivered in sections). Anchoring: Flexible chain/fastener system for rocky/sand bottom (approx. 30ft depth) capable of withstanding peak wind events. Lighting: Solar-powered LED lights on each corner. Freeboard: 12” to 18” under dead load. Period of Performance Estimated Start: April 2026 Completion Date: No later than September 1, 2026. Coordination: Installation must be coordinated with the Contracting Officer’s Representative (COR) to align with optimal water levels. Place of Performance Sullivan Lake Campground, Metaline Falls, WA 99153. Solicitation Details This solicitation is issued as a Combined Synopsis/Solicitation. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which shall be considered by the agency. Vendors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) to be eligible for award. Invoices must be submitted via the Invoice Processing Platform (IPP). See attachments for details.

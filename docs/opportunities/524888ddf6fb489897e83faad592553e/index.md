# Replace Bowling Alley Sound System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 11:01:19.866-04
- Solicitation Number: FA850126Q0016

## Summary

The contractor shall provide tools, materials, personnel, labor, and services to replace and install a new Audio Music System at the Bowling Center (Bldg. 908), located at Ninth Street, Robins AFB, Georgia. The installation will require new audio speakers to be hung over the bowling lanes and a rack-mounted amplifier to be installed in the storage room adjacent to the front control desk. The system shall be controlled by an iPad tablet computer located at the front counter. The objective of this effort is to deliver a new audio system in support of the 78th Force Support Squadron (78 FSS/FSWB). This will be a total small business set-aside. Only offers from small businesses will be considered. The Robins AFB Bowling Center will accommodate site visits from 20 April 2026 through 24 April 2026, between 0900 and 1700 hours. To schedule a site visit, prospective vendors must contact the Government Representative, Danilo Bueno, at 478-926-2112.

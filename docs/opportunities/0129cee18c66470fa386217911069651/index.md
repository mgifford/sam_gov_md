# Marriage Enrichment Retreat (MER)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 15:38:31.685-05
- Solicitation Number: N0018926QB0050003

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide a 4-Star Rated (or higher) venue and supporting services for one (1) three-day, two night MERs consistent with the requirements set forth herein. The facility shall be suitable and conducive to the purposes described in Section 1(c). The facility shall be at a resort, hotel, boutique hotel, or inn located within 10 miles of Lake Geneva, Wisconsin.

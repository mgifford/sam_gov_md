# Sole Source notification for billboard advertising in Spokane, WA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-24 13:10:18.782-04

## Summary

This is a notification of a sole source acquisition for the purchasing of advertising time on specific billboards in the Spokane, WA area for the WA Air National Guard Recruiting Office. The billboards were selected to maximize viewing opportunities for the recuiting activities for the state. The advertising on those billboards are propriety through a specific company, Lamar Advertising.

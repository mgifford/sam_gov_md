# LTESM-C Expansion Powidz, Poland

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-09 09:45:14.853-04
- Solicitation Number: W912GB26RA026

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought announcement for market research purposes for the Long Term Equipment Storage and Maintenance-Complex (LTESM-C) Expansion project at Powidz Air Base, Poland.The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) anticipates a stand-alone, Design-Build (DB), Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) contract to construct several new support facilities, including a large-scale humidity-controlled warehouse and a vehicle maintenance facility. The anticipated magnitude of construction is between $100,000,000 and $250,000,000. This acquisition is open to all eligible firms registered and incorporated in a North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) member nation.This notice is a summary only. For complete project details, submission requirements, and response instructions, please download and review the full Sources Sought announcement attached to this posting.

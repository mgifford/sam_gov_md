# NAF Linen Cleaning

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 07:30:44.516-04
- Solicitation Number: FA560625Q

## Summary

This requirement is for non-personal Laundry and Dry Cleaning Services necessary to support the daily operations of the 52nd Force Support Squadron's Eifel Arms Inn at Spangdahlem Air Base. This acquisition will establish a new Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) contract structured with a base year and four (4) option years.

# Wisdom Ranger Station roofing, siding and windows

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 10:30:53.549-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LU26Q0046

## Summary

Beaverhead-Deerlodge National Forest Wisdom Ranger Station Roofing, siding and window replacements in accordance with the statement of work. Solicitation number 1240LU26Q0046 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Wisdom Ranger Station roofing and siding. The project consists of removing and replacing the existing roof(s) with new roofing. This will include installing underlayment materials, roof sheathing, metal roofing & associated trim, flashing, fascia, ridge cap, drip edge, vents, waterproofing, wall sheathing, metal siding & associated trim, demolishing roof structures, replacing windows, and all other incidental work to complete the project. Magnitude of Construction is between $100,000 and $250,000.

# F-22 Landing Gear Cataloging, Provisioning, & Depot Gap Analysis

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 10:46:30.274-04
- Solicitation Number: SPRHA1-26-R-2222

## Summary

This acquisition is restricted to qualified sources (6K4C8). The Government intends to award a sole-source, firm-fixed-price contract to the OEM for the requirements described in this solicitation. Award is contingent upon the Contracting Officer determining that the final negotiated price is fair and reasonable. To support this price analysis, the offeror shall be required to submit other than certified cost or pricing data as deemed necessary by the Contracting Officer to establish price reasonableness. The OEM is GOODRICH CORPORATION (CAGE: 6K4C8), located at 4630 N Beach St STE 100, Haltom City, TX 76137-3201. The requirement is for the procurement of non-commercial engineering services necessary to support the F-22 Landing Gear program. The contractor shall provide two main deliverables: 1. Cataloging and Provisioning Data: The contractor will deliver Logistics Product Data (LPD) and Engineering Data for Provisioning (EDFP) required for the complete cataloging and provisioning baseline of the F-22 Nose and Main Landing Gear systems (P/Ns: 5HL11001-101/107, 5HL11100-102/112, and 5HL11100-101/111). 2. Depot Gap Analysis: The contractor will perform a comprehensive gap analysis to support the establishment of an organic, Depot-level Repair and Overhaul Capability at Hill Air Force Base (HAFB). This includes assessing current capabilities, identifying skill and training gaps, and identifying all necessary equipment and tools. This acquisition is being conducted under the authority of Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.302-1, "Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements." Please see attached solicitation for more information.

Support under this contract will include the following: Technical, project, analytical, program, and supply chain management support of policy and compliance in the areas of OM&S, Plant PP&E and AM. All OM&S and PP&E/AM management functions including policy development, financial and physical accountability, training, performance monitoring, and process improvements Warehousing and delivery of materials and equipment. This is a new requirement. Responses to this RFI should include as much detailed information as possible.

# River Birch Road Asphalt Paving

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 14:48:37.057-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26QA015

## Summary

The United States Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) - Rock Island District has a service requirement to cinduct the milling of existing asphalt pavement, the stockpiling of millings, and the placement of hot mix asphalt pavement and tack coat for the repaving of River Birch Road to the River Birch Campground at the USACE Thomson Causeway Recreation Area. See Solicitation, Specifications, and Quality Assurance Surveillance Plan for additional information on requirement. Quotes are due via email to Contract Specialist Carrie Holub, Email: carrie.l.holub@usace.army.mil by 27 March 2026 at 2PM Central Time.

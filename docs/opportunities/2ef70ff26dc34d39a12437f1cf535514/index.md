# Reusable Medical Device Inventory Tracking System

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-11 12:51:40.496-04
- Solicitation Number: SSN-GSUT2-20260157

## Summary

Sources-Sought Notice The Indian Health Service, Shiprock Service Unit is issuing this sources-sought notice (SSN) to identify capable Indian-owned, economic enterprises – large (IEE) or small (ISBEE) – that can provide a reusable medical device (RMD) inventory tracking system. The system will monitor and track the location and movement of RMDs throughout the sterilization process from initial decontamination to assembly, sterilization, storage, and delivery to the point of use. The system should provide real-time visibility into the status and location of each RMD within the healthcare facility. This will include data collection from sterile processing equipment, integration with the RMD tracking system software allowing for comprehensive recordkeeping and workflow management. Other capable firms are also invited to respond to this SSN. The Contracting Officer will consider responses for Buy Indian Act (25 U.S.C. 47) opportunities first, then for set aside programs under the Small Business Act (41 U.S.C. 3104). The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code assigned to this requirement is 541512- Computer Systems Design Services with a small business size standard of $34.0 million.

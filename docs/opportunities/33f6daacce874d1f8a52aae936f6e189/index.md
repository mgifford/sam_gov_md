## Summary

36C25626Q0650 Intraoral Wireless Dental Scanner 3Shape Trios 6 with associated software and laptop (Brand Name or Equal) for the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Overton Brooks VA Medical Center (VAMC) located in Shreveport, Louisiana. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation (CSS) for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with (IAW) Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Overhaul Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services- FAR Overhaul. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested, and a written solicitation document will not be issued. The CSS number is 36C25626Q0650. It is issued as a Request for Quote (RFQ) for Intraoral Wireless Dental Scanner 3Shape Trios 6 with associated software and laptop (Brand Name or Equal) located at the Overton Brooks VAMC, 510 E. Stoner Ave, Shreveport, Louisiana 71101. The government anticipates awarding a Firm-Fixed Price contract resulting from this solicitation. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2026-01 (eff. 03/13/2026). The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 339114- Dental Equipment and Supplies Manufacturing with a small business size standard of 750 employees.

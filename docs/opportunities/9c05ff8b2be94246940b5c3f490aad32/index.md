# SACRAMENTO DEEPWATER SHIP CHANNEL (DWSC) DREDGING FY26

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 18:49:05.897-04
- Solicitation Number: W912P726BA004

## Summary

This Procurement will be conducted under FSC CODE: Z1KF, NAICS Code: 237990. The size standard for this code is $37,000,000. All questions should be directed to the Contract Specialist, J.Dennis Neal: james.neal@usace.army.mil. Estimated Cost Range is between $1,000,000 - $3,000,000. JOB DESCRIPTION: PROJECT TITLE: SACRAMENTO DEEPWATER SHIP CHANNEL (DWSC) DREDGING FY26 PROJECT LOCATION: SACRAMENTO and YOLO COUNTIES, CA The work includes annual maintenance dredging of the Sacramento DWSC to -30-ft (MLLW) plus 1-ft of paid over-depth. All dredge material is suitable for the upland beneficial reuse sites listed in Specification Section 00 10 00 PRICING SCHEDULE AND requirements in specification Section 01 11 00 SUMMARY OF WORK. Dredge material may be suitable for beneficial reuses at Contractor furnished sites in accordance with the permit requirements of the specific reuse site. This requirement is for FULL AND OPEN COMPETITION and will be issued as an Invitation for Bids (IFB) which will result in the award of a single Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract. The solicitation will be available on/about 30 April, 2026 for viewing/downloading. ALL BIDS will be due on/about 2 June 2026 at 2:00 PM PST. The specific date and time will be identified in the solicitation. BIDDERS ARE ADVISED THAT THIS PROJECT MAY BE DELAYED, CANCELLED, OR REVISED AT ANY TIME DURING THE SOLICITATION, SELECTION, EVALUATION, AND/OR FINAL AWARDPROCESS. Plans and specs will not be provided in hard copy or CD format. NOTE: In preparation for prospective to submit offers when the IFB is issued, it is imperative that all prospective Offerors (including their subcontractors) must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM - www.SAM.gov) and the Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) website in order to be able to view and download solicitation information, and to submit offers. Solicitation documents, plans, and specifications are only available to be download from PIEE, which may be accessed at https://wawf.eb.mil OR https://piee.eb.mil OR https://piee.eb.mil/xhtml/unauth/home/login.xhtml. Offers MUST BE submitted electronically through the PIEE website. Registration should be completed one (1) week prior to the anticipated solicitation release date. USACE will not notify Offerors of any changes to the solicitation; Offerors must monitor PIEE and SAM for the solicitation to be posted, and for any posted changes or amendments. See attachments -PIEE Solicitation Module Vendor Access Instructions-, -SOP PIEE Proposal Manager Offeror-, and -SOP PIEE Posting Offer- for instructions on getting access to the PIEE Solicitation Module and submitting Offeror proposals.

# Industry Day Notice - Projection System Upgrade of P-8A Aircrew Training Device (ATD) - Operational Flight Trainer (OFT)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 12:36:33.12-04
- Solicitation Number: N6134026R1010-1

## Summary

The Department of the Navy, Naval Air Warfare Center Training Systems Division (NAWCTSD), Orlando, FL, is seeking potential sources to design, fabricate, install, integrate, test and deliver P-8A OFT projection system upgrade solution to the United States Navy (USN) and the Royal Australian Air Force (RAAF). This is planned to be a Small Business Set-Aside Commercial Competition.

## Summary

MODOC NWR - GRANDMA TRACT WELL Repair The purpose of this project is to determine repairs required to the Grandma Tract Well, in order to provide a reliable source of irrigation water during dry seasons. The well had, and has, several maintenance issues including but not limited to: driveshaft shearing in half, motor and pump replacement, well casing repairs etc. This project includes a hydrogeological assessment of Grandma Tract well, a report including findings, recommendations and associated costs.

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Prisons, Federal Correctional Institution (Atlanta), intends to issue a solicitation 15B30126Q00000017 for the requirements of: QUARTERLY KOSHER/HALAL FOB Destination, Deliveries will be made to Federal Correctional Institution 601 McDonough Boulevard S. E.Atlanta, GA 30315. The contract type is a firm fixed price. The Government reserves the right upon award to make a formal single or multiple awards. The Government will award to the responsible offer(s) whose offer, conforming to the solicitation that are considered to be most advantageous to the Government. The solicitation will be made available on or about March 4th, 2026, and will be posted to the https://beta.sam.gov/. All future information about this acquisition, including solicitation amendments, will also be distributed through this site. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring this site to ensure they have the most up to date information about this solicitation. The bids are to be received no later than March 12th, 2026, at 12:pm CST. Delivery will not be accepted no sooner than April 1st, 2026. Orders over $15,000.00, payments will be made using Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) or via Government Purchase Card for awards of $15,000.00 or less. Each offer is required per FAR 52.212?3 to complete their representations and certifications via the On-line Representation and Certification Applications (ORCA) at http://orca.bpn.gov. Must include a letter of kosher certification with your quote. The point of contact for all information regarding this solicitation is R. Woods Contracting Officer. Completed offers may be emailed to the attention of R. Woods, Contracting Department, Field Acquisitions Office. Respectfully; R. Woods Contracting Officer R3woods@bop.gov

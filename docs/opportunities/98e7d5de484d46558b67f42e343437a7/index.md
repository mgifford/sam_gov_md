# USACE New Orleans Industry Day 2026 - Rescheduled for October 2026

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-23 10:11:02.675-04

## Summary

NOTE: USACE New Orleans Industry Day 2026 is being rescheduled for October 2026. Tentative date forthcoming. ***SAVE THE DATES!!!******SAVE THE DATES!!!******SAVE THE DATES!!!****** The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), New Orleans District, is conducting a one-day Industry Day event. The Industry Day will provide industry-specific information that focuses on active USACE projects in South Louisiana over the next 18 months, and is open to all interested contractors, large and small businesses, joint ventures, academia, as well as other Government stakeholders. Projects include: Comite River Diversion Project West Shore Lake Pontchartrain Project East Baton Rouge Flood Management Project Mississippi River Levee Construction Morganza to the Gulf, Humble Canal Preload Contract Southeast Louisiana Atchafalaya Basin Southwest Coastal Facilities & Physical Support Locks & Structures Dredging A-E Outlook Studies & Possible Future Opportunities DATE: TBD LOCATION: USACE New Orleans District Headquarters (Both Events) District Assembly Room 7400 Leake Avenue, New Orleans, LA 70118 The Industry Day will provide opportunities for industry to meet one-on-one with USACE technical personnel for the outlined projects above via breakout sessions on a First-Come, First-Served basis, allows Contractor presentation time slots on a First-Come, First-Served basis, and renders the opportunities for Vendors and Professional Organizations to setup table displays on a First-Come, First-Served basis, as well. These events are free of charge. All attendees will be required to pre-register for the event(s) they plan on attending. A tentative agenda will be uploaded to this announcement mid-March along with the registration form and link, so interested parties should check SAM.GOV for future updates to this notice.

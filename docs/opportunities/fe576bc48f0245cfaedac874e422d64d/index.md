# Rental - Compressed Air System High-Pressure Air

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-19 21:07:32.804-04

## Summary

Norfolk Naval Shipyard (NNSY) intends to award a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Single Award Contract for the rental of three (3) each self-contained compressed air system used to supply high-pressure air for ship service applications including breathing air. Period of Performance: Estimated June 2026 - May 2027 Location: Portsmouth, VA

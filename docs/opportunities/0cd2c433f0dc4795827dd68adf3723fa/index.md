# Thomson Causeway Recreation Area Park Attendant

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 21:50:12.444-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26QA027

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Rock Island District intends to award a firm-fixed-price services contract utilizing simplified procedures. This acquisition will be a total small business set-aside for park attendant services. The contractor is responsible for providing all labor, employees and/or subcontractors necessary to perform park attendant services for the Thomson Causeway Recreation Area. Park attendant services include but are not limited to collecting camping and other approved fees, selling interagency passes, registering campers, processing camping reservations, balancing funds and receipts, posting reservations and assisting visitors as needed. The employees and/or subcontractors are required to live-on-site during the duration of the periods of service for fee collection.

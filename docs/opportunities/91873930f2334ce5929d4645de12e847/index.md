# Shop Rags Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 11:35:16.601-04
- Solicitation Number: W9136426QA021

## Summary

The contractor shall provide direct-delivery service of bulk shop rags to the CSMS as identified in Section 1.4.1. Contractor will ensure quantities are divided up by the fourteen OHARNG maintenance facilities upon delivery based on the quantities and specifications identified in Technical Exhibit 2. During service delivery, Contractor will deliver quantities divided up by maintenance facility to the CSMS where each set of bulk rags will be placed in their designated drop off location for each maintenance facility. Contractor will also pick up used rags in the same location. Quotes and questions shall be submitted to the Contract Specialist, Alan J. Cooper, alan.j.cooper8.civ@army.mil. Questions submitted less than seven days prior to the solicitation closing date risk going unanswered. Please review PWS attachment.

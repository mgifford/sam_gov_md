## Summary

Adams National Historical Park, in the City of Quincy, Massachusetts. Utilizing two (2) National Park Service-owned buses also referred to as trolleys, the Contractor will operate a seasonal visitor transportation service and provide all maintenance, repair, and storage for the National Park Service vehicles. The 2026-year trolley service will operate five days a week Wednesday - Sunday during the period of May 1, 2026 - October 31, 2026. During that period of performance, the trolley shall operate from 9:10 am - 5:10 pm, with a 30-minute lunch break. In 2026, the trolleys will be stored by the Contractor during both the performance period and the non-performance period for a total of 12 months.

# Repair Fence Refueling Parking Area

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 17:16:50.579-04
- Solicitation Number: FA309926Q0007

## Summary

Laughlin AFB has a Design-Build requirement to repair by replacing the chain link security fence for the Refueling Parking Area. The Contractor shall be responsible for a complete 100% design, ready to issue for construction (IFC), and construction of the fence. The Contractor shall provide all materials, labor, tools, equipment, transportation, and all other incidentals to complete the work outlined in the Statement of Work (SOW) to design and install approximately 2,500 LF of new fence, with gates, that meets all security standards for fuels management and airfield fencing requirements. This project is in close proximity to an active airfield, and contractors will be required to adhere to strict safety and security protocols. This is a commercial acquisition conducted in accordance with FAR part 12. A site visit has been scheduled for 30 April 2026 at 0930. Complete Attachment 4 - Laughlin EAL Template and request a DoDSAFE Drop-Off link from the POCs listed. EALs must be submitted by end of day 23 April 2026 Please see the solicitation for complete details and Attachment 3 - Sections L and M for instructions and evaluation criteria. NOTE: For SOW Attachment A labeled CUI, please reach out directly to the POCs listed for access or request access in SAM.

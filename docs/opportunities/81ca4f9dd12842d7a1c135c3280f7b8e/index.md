# LLTM & Advanced Engineering UCA - Construction of four (4) Medium Landing Ships (LSMs) Block 1

## Summary

Marinette Marine Corp., Marinette, Wisconsin is awarded a $30,000,000 not-to-exceed (NTE) undefinitized contract action (UCA) for advance procurement of long lead time material (LLTM) and associated engineering and design activities in support of four (4) Medium Landing Ships (LSMs) Block 1. The statutory authority for this sole source award is in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(3) Industrial Mobilization; Engineering, developmental, or Research Capability; or Expert Services.

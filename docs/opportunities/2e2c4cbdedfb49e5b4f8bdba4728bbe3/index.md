# TW-26 Power Generation Rental Combined Synopsis Solicitation *Subject to Availability of Funds*

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 14:38:23.388-04

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for non-personal services to the Government with eight (8) 15 Kilowatts (15KW) generators with maintenance and refueling services to ensure continuous operations 7 days a week, 24 hours a day, with capability to power US equipment (110V) and European equipment (220V) at Camp Crabbs and Camp Blizzard, Antigua and Barbuda as specified in Attachment 1 in accordance with the format in subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes only solicitation; proposals are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. The Solicitation W912CL26-Q-Y003 is being issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ).

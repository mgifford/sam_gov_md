# Notice of Consolidation for Iron Bank Data Operations Requirement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-13 15:46:23.797-04
- Solicitation Number: ConsolidationNoticeIronBankDataOpsRequirement

## Summary

Data Operations Team Requirement This is a notice of intent to consolidate/bundle requirements iaw FAR Overhaul 7.107-3 Notifications. The AFLCMC/HNCK Platform One (P1) contracting office plans to solicit an offer for the Iron Bank Data Operations Team requirement. The acquisition team will seek a solution for Data Lakehouse Operations, Platform/Cloud/Data Engineering, AI/ML operations, Website operations, and Vulnerability Assessment Tool (VAT) or similar, and the overall Data Governance in support of Iron Bank. Iron Bank’s focus is to be the data provenance center for image vetting and supporting documentation for the DoW (Department of War).The contracting office has conducted market research that demonstrates the consolidation of four (4) existing Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) orders to be necessary, justified and in the best interest of the government. The four BOA orders that will be consolidated are: FA830723FB016 FA830723FB057 FA830723FB058 FA830723FB059 The government intends to solicit and negotiate with the vendor that provides the most technically capable solution under the current Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) on Platform One Solutions Marketplace (P1SM) in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3458 and DFARS 212.70. The resultant contract will consist of a one year base period and four (4) one-year option periods.

# Operations, Maintenance, Repair, Replacement, Rehabilitation (OMRRR) and Operational Testing (OT) Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 13:22:01.287-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EP26RA004

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Jacksonville District, is issuing this Combined Synopsis/Solicitation in support of a requirement for Operations and Maintenance (O&M) Operations, Maintenance, Repair, Replacement, Rehabilitation (OMRRR) and Operational Testing (OT) of features for ecosystem restoration, flood control, and other civil works projects. Refer to the Solicitation and Performance Work Statement (PWS) attached for a detailed description of the requirement. This will be an IDIQ MATOC with a one-year Base and Four (4) Option Years.

# Fermilab Giese Road Reconstruction

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 15:13:50.049-04
- Solicitation Number: 380034GMK

## Summary

Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (FNAL) is soliciting proposals for Giese Road Reconstruction as defined in the Scope of Work. The Subcontractor shall perform all work as described in the Statement of Work for the Giese Road Reconstruction and all exhibits. All work shall include transportation, overhead, bonding costs, insurance, safety oversight, quality control oversight, and supervision as required to construct the project described in the scope of work and all exhibits. A Pre-Proposal Meeting and Site Visit are scheduled for 1:30 PM, Central Daylight Time, Wednesday, April 8, 2026. Participants should meet (see instructions in Request for Proposal document) at the entrance to the Fermilab Welcome and Access Center (FWAC) before being escorted to Giese Road Reconstruction project site. For Access to the National Lab, all subcontractors are required to submit their information at least 5 days prior to the date of the site visit for approval. See RFP for detailed information. Please review the requirements of the solicitation. Questions Close on Friday, April 10, 2026 at 3:00 pm CST. All questions must be submitted in writing to ginakern@fnal.gov. The Request for Proposal (RFP) CLOSES on Wednesday, April 22, 2026 at 3:00 pm CST. Please email your proposals with all required documents to ginakern@fnal.gov.

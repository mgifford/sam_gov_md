# Research, Development, Test and Evaluation (RDT&E) Engineering and Technical Support (RETS) Presolicitation Notice

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 15:39:15.912-04
- Solicitation Number: MSW04032026

## Summary

PRESOLICITATION NOTICE Research, Development, Test and Evaluation (RDT&E) Engineering and Technical Support (RETS) For The Office of the Undersecretary of War for Research and Engineering OUSW(R&E) RETS Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) In accordance with FAR Overhaul Part 5.101, Pre-solicitation Notice, Washington Headquarters Services (WHS) Acquisition Directorate (AD) is issuing a pre-solicitation notice for the Research, Development, Test & Evaluation, Engineering and Technical Support Services (RETS). The Government will be issuing a solicitation in the near future to establish a multiple- award Indefinite-Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) vehicle using FAR Overhaul Part procedures under FAR Parts 12, 15 and 16. This acquisition will be competed as a Total 100% Smal Business Set Aside under NAICS Code 541715 Research & Development in the Physical, Engineering, and Life Sciences. The electronic copy of the solicitation will be issued be issued on SAM.GOV via Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE). See attached document of the Draft Solicitation, along with the IDIQ PWS and Task Order 0001 PWS.

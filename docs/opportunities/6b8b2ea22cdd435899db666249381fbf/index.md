# Request for Solutions (RFS) - Microelectronics Operations, Sustainment, Acquisition, and Infrastructure Capabilities Modernization (MOSAIC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-25 11:49:53.478-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016426SNB54

## Summary

The Department of War (DoW) released a new prototype project opportunity focused on modernizing microelectronics of fielded weapon systems through the digitalization of acquisition and sustainment. The effort, Microelectronics Operations, Sustainment, Acquisition, and Infrastructure Capabilities Modernization (MOSAIC), will leverage digital engineering tools, commercial best practices, and a scalable, cloud-based environment to enable co-design, data reuse, and lifecycle collaboration across programs. MOSAIC aims to accelerate the delivery of faster and affordable replacements for critical systems while strengthening long-term sustainment infrastructure.

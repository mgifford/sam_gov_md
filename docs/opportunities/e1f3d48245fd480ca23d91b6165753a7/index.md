# Common Tactical Truck (CTT) Industry Communications Forum

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 08:56:14.324-04
- Solicitation Number: CTT-006

## Summary

Product Director Common Tactical Truck (PdD CTT) Cordially Invites Government and Industry Leaders to Attend the CTT Industry Communications Forum The purpose of this Announcement is to inform industry that PdD Common Tactical Truck will be hosting a CTT Industry Communications Forum in Warren, MI. The engagement session will provide an overview of the CTT Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) and path forward for the CTT program. Date: March 20, 2026 Locations: Warren, MI OR Virtually on Microsoft (MS) Teams Note: Event address and/or MS Teams link to be provided post RSVP Time: 0900-1200 EST (Registration begins at 0830 EST, driver’s license or personal identification card will be verified during check in) Cost: None. No meals provided Note: In-Person Seating is limited to two people per company Registration Instructions: Please fill out your RSVP form at: https://forms.osi.apps.mil/Pages/ResponsePage.aspx?id=D9fm-kuVEUiStgUw1vhMQ7WsNIATvMROna0kw-CU0eBUNzdDQVY5Sk9QN0xOQU5QR1o5UFFINlpLUy4u Copy and paste the above link to your browser to complete the RSVP form. RSVPs shall be submitted by 5:00PM EST on March 18, 2026. Any changes to the time, date or location of this event will be posted to SAM.gov. Notice: This Industry Communications Forum is an informational event, not a request for bids or proposals. Your participation is voluntary, and the Government will not reimburse any costs associated with attendance or responses to this notice. No funding is associated with this announcement, and the session does not obligate the Government in any manner. Any future opportunities for contracts will be announced separately. All official documents and future announcements regarding CTT will be posted on the SAM.gov website. The information shared at this event is for planning purposes only and is subject to change.

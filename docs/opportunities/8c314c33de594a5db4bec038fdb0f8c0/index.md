# Westside Trail Bridge Replacements

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 11:47:50.624-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LP26Q0053

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1240LP26Q0053 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the purchase of three timber trail bridges to replace failed or poor condition bridges on trails within the Westside Ranger District. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 321999 The small business size standard is 500 employees This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. Questions Due: May 1st, 2026 by 1600 (MT) to Westley.Bisson@usda.gov Offers Due: May 8th, 2026 by 1600 (MT) to Westley.Bisson@usda.gov Delivery: Deliver within 45 calendar days from award. Coordinate delivery at least 7 days in advance with COR. Contracting Officer (CO): Westley Bisson (Westley.Bisson@usda.gov) Contracting Officer's Representative: Jonathan Wheeler (Jonathan.Wheeler2@usda.gov)

# Asphalt Paver Rental for Crestwood Annual Training

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-10 10:57:00.286-04
- Solicitation Number: W91SMC26QA005

## Summary

The 2118th Asphalt Detachment requires the short-term rental of one (1) commercial-grade crawler asphalt paver to complete the paving of a Physical Training Track during Annual Training at the Crestwood Armory in Robbins, IL. This is a “dry lease” requirement where the Government will provide the operator and fuel.

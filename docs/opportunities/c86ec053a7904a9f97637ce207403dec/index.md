# LRAFB Washer and Dryer Lease

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-20 11:28:38.966-04

## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE. NO SOLICITATION IS AVAILABLE AT THIS TIME. The purpose of this Sources Sought Notice is to identify potential sources capable of performing the effort described herein pursuant to FAR part 10, Market Research and FAR part 19, Small Business Programs. This is for planning purposes only. Any information submitted by respondents to this Source Sought Notice is voluntary. The Air Force does not intend to award a contract on the basis of this notice or to otherwise pay for the information solicited. Respondents should not construe this notice as a commitment by the Air Force for any purpose and should refrain from providing proprietary information in the response. 1. INTRODUCTION: This is the initial step in the planning process for WASHER AND DRYER LEASE. The 19 CONS/PKA at Little Rock Air Force Base seeks sources to procure WASHER AND DRYER LEASE. 19 CONS/PKA anticipates conducting a competitive acquisition for this effort. The effort is expected to result in a Firm Fixed Price. NAICS code is anticipated to be 532210, size standard $47 Million. 2. INSTRUCTIONS: All businesses (located in AR, TN or TX) capable of providing WASHER AND DRYER LEASE are invited to respond electronically. Responses shall include company name, address, UEI, CAGE code, point of contact with email address and telephone number, indicate business size (small business, small disadvantaged business, service-disabled veteran-owned small business, economically disadvantaged woman-owned small business, woman-owned small business, or HUBZone) using NAICS 532210, size standard $47 Million, and indicate any current government contract vehicles you are currently apart of. The Air Force reserves the right to consider a small business set-aside for any subsequent acquisition based upon responses to this notice. In the event Small Business contractors are not available for adequate competition for this project, it will be advertised as unrestricted. Responses must be received no later than Monday, 30 March 2026, and addressed to SrA Darius Roscoe at darius.roscoe@us.af.mil and A1C Lukas-Sebastian Ashley at lukas-sebastian.ashley@us.af.mil.

# Special Notice – Capability Program Executive (CPE) Mission Autonomy Industry Day announcement.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 15:10:34.796-04

## Summary

This is a special notice that the Government intends to annouce an upcoming Industry Day hosted by the National Adavnced Mobility Consortium (NAMC).The purpose of this event is to inform industry of key operational problems related to the CPE Mission Autonomy program, discuss forthcoming release of Request for Prototype Proposals (RPPs) and provide an opportunity for industry partners to offer feedback on autonomous standards and architectures. Draft RPPs can be made available by requesting through NAMC at Questions@NAMConsortium.org. Any question or input on the Draft RPPs must be received prior to Industry Day on 18 March 2026.

The Regional Contracting Office intends to competitively award a hybrid Firm Fixed price (FFP) and Time and Materials (T&M) contract for Pest Control Surveillance and Management Services in USAG Wiesbaden, Germany. This is a non-personal scheduled and On-Demand Pest Control and Surveillance services. The Contractor shall furnish all employees, supervision, equipment, supplies, tools and transportation necessary to provide comprehensive integrated pest control and surveillance services within the USAG Wiesbaden footprint. The contract will consist of a five (5) years Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Single Award. Any amendments related to the RFQ will be posted to www.Sam.gov. IMPORTANT: Quoters must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at time of submission of quotes.

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Office of Inspector General (VA OIG) is awarding a sole source contract, in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3204, 41 U.S.C. 3304 and FAR 6.103-1(b), One Responsible Source. This contract is negotiated with and awarded to: Gee Whiz Software, LLC 6540 Mulberry St San Diego, CA 92114-1505 The scope of work is for renewal of BankScan software and peripherals. The contract is Firm Fixed Price with a Base Period of Performance of twelve (12) months, followed by four (4) Option Periods of twelve (12) months each. The NAICS code is 541519. The size standard is $34 million to 150 employees. The PSC is DA10. In accordance with FAR 6.301, the Sole Source Justification is attached to this notice.

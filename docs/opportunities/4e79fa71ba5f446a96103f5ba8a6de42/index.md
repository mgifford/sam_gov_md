# Sources Sought/Rotary Screw Air Compressor Nonlubricated

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-25 16:29:02.999-04
- Solicitation Number: W25G1V009524507

## Summary

ROTARY SCREW AIR COMPRESSOR NONLUBRICATED replacement, installation of new unit and remove and dispose of existing unit. the requirement is for the removal and disposal of the existing air compressor and the procurement, delivery, installation and commissioning of a new Rotary Screw Air Compressor that meets the attached specifications. The Army Contracting Command, Tobyhanna Division, is issuing this sources sought synopsis as a means of conducting market research to identify parties having an interest in and the resources to support the requirement for ROTARY SCREW AIR COMPRESSOR NONLUBRICATED replacement. A determination by the Government not to compete this requirement based on this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Any responsible firms believing they can provide the complete requirements of the ROTARY SCREW AIR COMPRESSOR NONLUBRICATED replacement must contact the Contract Specialist identified below in writing within seven (7) days of this notice and submit a capability package which will be reviewed for consideration. Your response must be submitted through or via email to michele.a.dooley.civ@army.mil. See attachments for full specifications and details.

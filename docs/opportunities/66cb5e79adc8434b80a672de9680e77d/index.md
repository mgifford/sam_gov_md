# Touchless Identity Verification Units

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-24 15:18:17.976-04
- Solicitation Number: JA-2026-04-1301

## Summary

The Transportation Security Administration (TSA), Enrollment Services and Vetting Programs (ESVP), Contracting and Procurement (C&P) modified task order (TO) 70T02025F7503N004 under the General Services Administration (GSA) FSS that restricted the number of sources considered under FAR 8.4. The specific reference is GSAR 538.7104-3 (b)(iii), The order should be issued on a sole source basis in the interest of economy and efficiency because it is a logical follow-on to an FSS order already issued on a competitive basis. This in scope modification is for touchless identity verification units for biometric identity at airports to support the CMAP lanes, including the following: Equipment (Touchless Identity Units for CMAP) and associated warranty and shipping, Deployment activities to install, configure, and place the units into operational status at CMAP locations, Field support during deployment, No full-time equivalent staff positions are included; services are project-specific, and Will include Hardware and Helpdesk support in the option periods.

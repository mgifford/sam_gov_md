# AIRBOSS - Robins AFB, GA (78ABW) AIRSHOW - 2027

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 14:50:22.217-04
- Solicitation Number: F3Q0AC6055AW01

## Summary

The contractor shall provide support for the Air Show portion of the 2027 Open House at Robins Air Force Base (RAFB), Georgia. The Air Show portion includes all aerial and ground demonstrations and static displays. This requirement is inclusive of the AirBoss, Announcer team, and Ground Boss IAW PWS - Revision (Dated 19 March 2026). This is an amendment to the original solicitation to incorporate changes to the PWS (See attached 27 Airboss PWS - Revised). The revised PWS is current as of and dated 19 March 2026.

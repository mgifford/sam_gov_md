# R--Peace Corps Campus Recruitment

## Summary

DOI, IBC, AQD, on behalf of the Peace Corps, intends to procure Strategic Recruitment Services. The Strat Program expands Peace Corps recruitment resources by creating formal contract relationships between the Peace Corps and various universities. The Peace Corps intends to establish at least one Strat institution in each subregion, expected to range between approximately 1 and 8 Strat institutions per region, depending on competition, institutional capacity, and alignment with recruitment needs. This procurement is open to U.S. higher education institutions that meet at least one (1) of the following eligibility criteria: - United States higher education institutions with at least 10,000 undergraduate students; or - Institutions previously awarded a Strat contract with the Peace Corps. The anticipated period of performance is August 1, 2026 through July 31, 2027, with the possibility of four (4) option periods, not to exceed five (5) years total.

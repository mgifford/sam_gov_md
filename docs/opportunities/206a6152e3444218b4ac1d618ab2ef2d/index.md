## Summary

Combined Synopsis – Solicitation This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with RFO Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number FA460826QC033 is issued as a Request for Quote for two (2) Brand Name AERCO Benchmark BMK 2000 Boilers. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. Description of Requirement: This is a purchase and delivery requirement for two (2) brand-name AERCO Benchmark BMK 2000 boilers. This acquisition is on behalf of the 2d Civil Engineering Squadron (2 CES) located at Barksdale AFB, LA, and must conform to the customer's salient characteristics. Installation will be handled by members of the 2 CES.

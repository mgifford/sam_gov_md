## Summary

** THIS IS A PRE-SOLICITATION NOTICE. QUOTES WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED UNTIL THE REQUEST FOR QUOTE (RFQ) IS POSTED AT A LATER DATE. ** The Air Force and 76 CMXG intend to solicit and issue a sole-source contract to Hexagon Metrology Intelligence, Inc. under the authority of FAR 6.103-1(b) and 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1) for the following: Description: The following sets forth the minimum requirements for the procurement of (8) Leica Laser trackers, scanners, and accessories by Hexagon Metrology Intelligence, Inc.

## Summary

04 March 2026 1) Posting was updated to replace Attachment "DRAFT_52.212-1&2_DeploymentSolutions_2026.03.03.pdf" with Attachment "DRAFT_52.212-1&2_DeploymentSolutions_2026.03.04.pdf" 2) Added link to Related Notice 03 March 2026 Related Notice ID: HT003826RFIE001 The Defense Health Agency Contracting Activity (DHACA), on behalf of the Program Executive Office, Defense Healthcare Management Systems (PEO DHMS), intends to issue a solicitation for Deployment Solutions. Deployment Solutions is a non-personal services requirement to provide all services and materials necessary to support and execute the on-demand deployment of DHMS products, and related or supporting components, including site preparation, deployment, training, user adoption, change management, and post-installation support to ensure seamless integration and minimal disruption to existing healthcare systems. This announcement constitutes a draft Request for Proposal (RFP) for industry review and comment and includes the below artifacts. Draft Performance Work Statement (PWS) Draft 52.212-1 and 52.212-2 Draft Exhibit A003 Deployment Approach CDRL Draft Attachment 16, OCI Declaration and Disclosure, Notice of Organizational Conflict of Interest Draft RFP Deployment Solutions Feedback We are not soliciting proposals at this time, and none will be accepted. A separate formal solicitation is expected to be issued at a later date.

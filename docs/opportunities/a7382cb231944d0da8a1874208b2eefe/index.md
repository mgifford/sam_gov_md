## Summary

The 28th Contracting Squadron at Ellsworth AFB, is issuing this Request for Quote (RFQ) to procure mission-critical anti-exposure suits. This is a brand-name only requirement for the following specific item: Product: AeroStar OTS Suit Part Number (P/N): 601GBR006 Quantity: 29 Sizes: See Attachment 1 No substitutions or "or equal" items will be considered. Offerors must provide the exact part number specified above to be deemed technically acceptable. Basis for Award: The Government intends to award a single, Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) contract to the offeror whose offer conforming to the solicitation will be most advantageous to the Government, price and other factors considered. Technical and Price Factors will utilized. Delivery: Required delivery is FOB Destination to Ellsworth AFB, SD, no later than 60 days after contract award. Attachment: All information necessary to submit a quote is contained in the attached RFQ document titled FA469026Q0020. Offerors must read the attachment in its entirety before submitting a quote.

# Advantor System Additions

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-14 15:14:49.072-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S77-26-Q-0007

## Summary

This announcement constitutes a Notice of Intent to Sole Source. The 147th ATW intends to award a purchase order to support the procurement of Advantor System Additions. A protection of assets initiative includes a requirement for an Advantor Intrusion Detection system. Advantor is to provide and install CAC access systems for security of buildings and military assets. The specified access systems for security of building and military assets is unique because in order maintain workable and compatibility with existing systems requiring all parts and pieces to work together as a single unit. Mixing other parts and pieces of different manufactures is not an approved Air Force system. See attached JA document for full details.

# MCBCB Portable Toilets and Handwashing Stations

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 22:38:25.931-04
- Solicitation Number: N6264926QH015

## Summary

Naval Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) Fleet Logistics Center Yokosuka (FLCY) Site Marianas has a requirement to provide portable toilets, handwashing stations, and cleaning services in support of Marine Corps Base Camp Blaz (MCBCB). The scope of this requirement is to deliver, place, and service twenty-three (23) standard portable toilets and eight (8) portable handwashing stations at various locations on (MCBCB) as detailed in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). Interested parties are encouraged to review the attached PWS for detailed requirements and submission instructions, including deadlines before submitting a proposal.

# Provision of various European household appliances

- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 08:53:52.895-04

## Summary

PRESOLICITATION NOTICE Subject: Supply of various Commercial European household appliances for Naval Support Activity (NSA) Naples, Italy – Housing Dept. Classification Code: PSC: 7290 (Miscellaneous household and commercial furnishings and appliances) NAICS: 335220 (Major household Appliance Manufacturing) Contracting Office: NAVSUP Fleet Logistic Center Sigonella Naples, Italy Points of Contact: Mr. Luca Buonocunto, Contract Specialist E-mail: luca.buonocunto.ln@us.navy.mil Mr. David Biggs, Contracting Officer E-mail: david.a.biggs20.civ@us.navy.mil 1. ACTION This is a Presolicitation Notice of the Government’s intent to issue a solicitation for the procurement of European household appliances. This notice is for informational and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation or a promise to issue a solicitation in the future. 2. REQUIREMENT DESCRIPTION The NSA Naples, Italy – Housing Dept. has a requirement for the purchase and delivery of the following household appliances: 10 each REFRIGERATOR LARGE Class A or better / Color white/ Approximate dimensions: h 180/190 cm x w 80/85cm x d 70/75cm / AC: 220v 50Hz 15 each WASHER LARGE Class A or better / Color white / Approximate dimensions: W 62/68 cm x D 68/72 cmx H 100/110 cm / 220V Italian Plug (Schuko) 5 each GAS DRYER Class A or better / Color white / Approximate dimensions: w 65/70cm x d 70/72cm x h 100/110 cm / 220V Italian Plug (Schuko) 10 each Gas Range with Electric Oven Class A or better / Color white / Approximate dimensions: 90 cm W (+/- 2) x 60 cm D / AC: 220-240 volts 50 Hz 3. DELIVERY ADDRESS: All items shall be delivered to the following address: NSA, Housing Warehouse – Bldg. #2094 Via della Stazione, Teverola (CE) Activity TPOC: Mr. Gianni Lanzillo / Ms. Giovanna Fucci 4. DELIVERY DATE: 90 days from the date of Award 5. INTENDED ACQUISITION STRATEGY: This requirement is for a commercial product and falls below the Simplified Acquisition Threshold (SAT). In accordance with Revolutionary Far Overhaul (RFO) Part 12, "Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services," Clause 12.201-1(c)(2) “Simplified Procedures”, the Government intends to solicit quotes directly from a limited number of sources (at least three). The Government does not intend to issue a separate, public solicitation for this specific requirement. No formal solicitation document is available at this time. 6. DISCLAIMER: This notice is not a request for capability statements or competitive quotes. The intent of this announcement is for informational purposes only. Any information received within five (5) working days after the posting date of this notice will be retained as market research for any future similar requirement. For any inquiries regarding this action, please refer to the Points of Contact identified above.

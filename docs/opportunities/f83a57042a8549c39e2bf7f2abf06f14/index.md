## Summary

Sources Sought Notice: SPRDL125R0139 Item Name: Engine, Diesel, with container NSN: 2815-01-562-6787 Part Number: 57K6791 (CAGE 19207); kit consisting of the following parts: Part number 3695119 diesel engine manufactured by CAGE 75Q65 Oshkosh Part number 0-19980 container manufactured by Champion Corporation. 5-year Indefinite Delivery-Indefinite Quantity Contract 5-Year Minimum Order Quantity: 88/each End Item: Heavy Expanded Mobility Tactical Truck (HEMTT), ABT M977, M978, M983, M984, M984A1, M985 RESPONSE DATE: 5 MAY 2026 REQUIREMENTS DESCRIPTION: The engine is a critical component to the HEMTT fleet. The vehicle’s engine is required for all levels of function, and without engines, HEMTT vehicles are dead-lined and not mission capable. The engine functions by converting fuel into power which turns the driveshaft of the vehicle which will then turn the powered axles of the vehicle allowing for movement. The engine also serves to provide power to all auxiliary systems of the vehicle which assist in its overall function. This engine is specifically outfitted to fit to the HEMTT engine bay.

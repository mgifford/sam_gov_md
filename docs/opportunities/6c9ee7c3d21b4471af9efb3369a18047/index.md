# Campuswide Scrap Metal Recycling Services

- Agency: ARCHITECT OF THE CAPITOL
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 18:53:12.87-04
- Solicitation Number: AOCSSB26R0007

## Summary

The contractor will collect and recycle scrap metal from Architect of the Capitol (AOC) locations in a responsible and environmentally sound manner. The Architect of the Capitol has need to collect scrap metal at all their facilities from demolition and renovation activities. **Amendment 01 (AOCSSB26R0007_A01 and J.2 Instructions to Offerors and Basis for Award_A01) have been added to the attachments. Amendment 01 only extends the proposal due date from March 31, 2026 at 3:00 PM ET to April 14, 2026 at 3:00 PM ET** **Amendment 02 (AOCSSB26R0007_A02, Quoter Questions and Responses, J.4 Scrap Metal Pricing Spreadsheet_A02, Scrap Metal Recycling SOW_A02, and FAR 52.216-4 Economic Price Adjustment) have been added to the attachments. The revised Statement of Work (SOW) and J.4 Scrap Metal Pricing Spreadsheet_A02 supercede the previously published SOW and Spreadsheet**

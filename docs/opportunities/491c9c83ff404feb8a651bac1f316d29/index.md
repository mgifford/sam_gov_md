# Snow Blower Auger Components-M-B Companies

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-10 16:25:21.84-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S7E26Q0001

## Summary

**Request for Information (RFI)** **Snow Blower Auger Components** **1. General Scope** This RFI is issued to identify potential sources capable of providing auger attachment components as described in the detailed requirements below. The parts required are for an Oshkosh H293BCZ-GAFC Snow Blower. Parts must be manufactured by M-B Companies as noted below. **2. Detailed Requirements** The Government is seeking responses from vendors capable of providing the following components: A. **Tire**: Qty 1 - Nominal width in mm: 180 - Aspect ratio: 70 - Radial - Rim diameter in inches: 8 - 16 PLY WITH TUBE & FLAP B. **Skid Pad**: Qty 2 (MB attachment specific) - Mfg Part Number: 2159050W C. **Blade**: Qty 1 (MB attachment specific) - Mfg Part Number: 2159000 D. **Bolt**: Qty 12 - Special Plow Bolt - Diameter: 5/8 inch - 11 threads per inch (TPI) - Length: 2.25 inch - Grade: 5 - Dark finish - Mfg Part Number: 3620192 E. **Nut**: Qty 12 - Flange nut (Locking) - Diameter: 5/8 inch - 11 threads per inch (TPI) - Grade: 8 - Dark finish - Mfg Part Number: 3266311 F. **Spoke (LH)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - SPOKE, REMOVABLE, LH, APRT, WLDMT - Mfg Part Number: 5854034 G. **Spoke (RH)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - SPOKE, REMOVABLE, RH, APRT, WLDMT - Mfg Part Number: 5854035 H. **Bracket**: Qty 8 (MB attachment specific) - BRKT, AUGER SPOKE, WELDMENT - Mfg Part Number: 3557068 I. **Ribbon (RH, Outside)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - RIBBON FLIGHT, OUTSIDE, RH - Mfg Part Number: 3561865 J. **Ribbon (RH, Inside)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - RIBBON FLIGHT, INSIDE, RH - Mfg Part Number: 3561863 K. **Ribbon (LH, Outside)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - RIBBON FLIGHT, OUTSIDE, LH - Mfg Part Number: 3561866 L. **Ribbon (LH, Inside)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - RIBBON FLIGHT, INSIDE, LH - Mfg Part Number: 3561864 M. **Ring (RH)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - CURB RING, RH - Mfg Part Number: 1882760 N. **Ring (LH)**: Qty 4 (MB attachment specific) - CURB RING, LH - Mfg Part Number: 183610 O. **Plate**: Qty 8 (MB attachment specific) - PLATE 9.00X4.50X.250 - Mfg Part Number: 1929280 P. **Flat Head Cap Screw (Bolt)**: Qty 64 - Diameter: 5/8 inch - 11 threads per inch (TPI) - Length: 2 inch - Grade: 8 - 82-degree head angle - Plain black oxide finish - Mfg Part Number: 370-28984 Q. **Nut**: Qty 144 - Flange nut (Locking) - Diameter: 5/8 inch - 11 threads per inch (TPI) - Grade: 8 - Matte black finish - Mfg Part Number: 3970540005 R. **Flange Hex Head Cap Screw (Bolt)**: Qty 24 - Diameter: 5/8 inch - 11 threads per inch (TPI) - Length: 1.25 inch - Grade: 8 - Phosphate and oil finish - Mfg Part Number: 370-148014 S. **Square Neck Carriage Screw (Bolt)**: Qty 48 - Diameter: 5/8 inch - 11 threads per inch (TPI) - Length: 2 inch - Grade: 8 - Mfg Part Number: 3561867 **Warranty**: Manufacturer's standard warranty for at least 12 months. **3. Responses** Interested vendors are requested to submit a **Capabilities Statement** that provides sufficient detail to determine the organization’s ability to meet the Government’s requirements. Please include the following information: ### Product Information: - Availability of requested items or equivalent items. - Product description or catalog copy. - Manufacturer, model, and type. - Approximate lead time for each item. ### Company Information: - Company name. - Cage Code and Unique Entity ID (UEI). - NAICS codes registered in the System for Award Management (SAM). - Point of contact (name, phone number, email address). Responses should be submitted no later than **21 April 2026, 15:00 (Central Standard Time)** via email to the Government Point of Contact (POC) listed below. **Government POC** - Name: Lt Col Ann Feist - Email: ann.feist.1@us.af.mil - Phone: 612-713-2626 **4.

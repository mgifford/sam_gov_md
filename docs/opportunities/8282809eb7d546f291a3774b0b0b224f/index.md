# Airborne Noise Testing for Fan Coil Assembly

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 13:23:52.769-04
- Solicitation Number: N0040626Q0041

## Summary

This is a COMBINED SYNOPSIS/SOLICITATION for commercial services prepared in accordance with RFO Part 12, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. The solicitation number is N00406-26-Q-0041 and is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Airborne Noise Testing on one (1) Government Furnished Fan Coil Assembly in accordance with the Performance Work Statement (PWS) and MIL-PRF-23798E(SH). This acquisition is a 100% Small Business Set-Aside. This combined synopsis/solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The NAICS CODE is 541380 and the Small Business Size Standard is $19.0 Million. The Government intends to solicit and award a firm-fixed-price (FFP) purchase order to a responsible small business concern. Interested small business parties may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement and submit quotes. See the attached Solicitation document and PWS for full details, instructions, and applicable clauses.

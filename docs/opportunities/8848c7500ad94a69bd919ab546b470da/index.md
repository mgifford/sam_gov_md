# Scott AFB Commodity Sale - April 2026

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 16:55:14.156-04
- Solicitation Number: 02-Q326

## Summary

The 375th Civil Engineer Squadron (375 CES) sells scrap material direct to the public through the Qualified Recycling Program (QRP) via an Invitation to Bid. The QRP has 3 commodities available for bid: 12 bales of used cardboard, 4,000 lbs of palletized lead-acid batteries, and 450 boxes of palletized Onity Advance Door Lock Sets. If interested in one or more commodities, please open the attached Invitation to Bid and review all details. Fill out and submit the Invitation to Bid Sheet by end of bidding period. Please ensure the solicitation is completely filled out to include a SAM number and/or UEI number or CAGE Code. Send your bid to Michelle Engel by e-mail to michelle.engel@us.af.mil by 5 May 2026 End Of Business (EOB). Bid(s) will be awarded in writing on 6 May 2026 noon CDT. All bids are welcome.

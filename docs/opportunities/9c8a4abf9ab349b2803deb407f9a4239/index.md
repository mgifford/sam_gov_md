# TAFT Boss 6 Month EXT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 12:56:32.209-04

## Summary

Services addressed here are those required for broadcast operation support services for the American Forces Network Broadcast Center’s (AFN-BC) suite of 24/7 television channels. 1. The services require management and technical personnel to provide daily TV Traffic and Programming Products to support television broadcast operations from the AFN-BC. 2. Critical performance requirements include sensitive media handling, speed, accuracy and performance that adhere to DoD and broadcast industry standards. 3. The broadcast industry sells the service by billing hourly rates for the services of television broadcast managers and producers. The PWS is written to conform to industry expectations

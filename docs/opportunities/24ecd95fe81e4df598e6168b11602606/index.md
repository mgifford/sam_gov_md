# MSHA - ISIB Gas Analyzer

- Agency: LABOR, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 09:14:09.047-04
- Solicitation Number: 1605C4-26-Q-00041

## Summary

CAI Model 701LX NDIR Gas Analyzer or Equivalent The Government requires a California Analytical Instruments, Inc. Model 701LX NDIR gas analyzer. The Government is currently operating an intrinsic safety approval laboratory using outdated and/or deteriorated gas analyzing equipment. This equipment is used in the approval of mining equipment which is instrumental to the safety of miners.

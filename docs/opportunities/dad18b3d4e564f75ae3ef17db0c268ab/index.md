# Grand Canyon National Park Water & Wastewater Lab

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 13:03:37.58-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P1526Q0044

## Summary

The National Park Service (NPS) anticipates awarding a single, firm-fixed price Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract as a result of this combined synopsis/solicitation. Actual lab tests to be performed will be identified at the Task Order level. The purpose of this requirement is for Grand Canyon National Park (GRCA) and requires water and wastewater lab analysis services. The services required include sample analysis, recording and reporting of analytical data regarding drinking water, wastewater, reclaim water, chlorinated water discharge and biosolids. Offeror is to ensure that they hold the required licenses and certifications listed in the attached Statement of Work (SOW) and submit list with quote. This acquisition is a 100% Total Small Business Set-Aside. Questions shall be submitted electronically to lindsay_mclaughlin@ios.doi.gov no later than March 30, 2026 by 10:00am Mountain Time. A consolidated list of government responses to any questions submitted will be posted as an amendment to this RFQ. Follow all instruction to submit a quote to lindsay_mclaughlin@ios.doi.gov by the date and time set forth in this solicitation.

## Summary

Addendum 1 (March 19, 2026) This Addendum is provided to all Prospective Respondents for clarification of the subject RFI. The set of Questions and Answers (Q&A) for Addendum 1 is in the "Attachments" section of the SAM.Gov RFI posting. Should you have any questions, please address them to the undersigned by e-mail. - The Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL) is seeking information from qualified vendors regarding their capability to provide computer hardware to the laboratory’s end users. This RFI is for planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation or commitment to procure any services. Neither JPL nor the U.S. Government will be responsible for any costs incurred by you in furnishing any information. Responses received will be carefully reviewed by JPL and may be considered as part of a future solicitation. Prospective subcontractors are advised that any information provided shall be deemed to be furnished with unlimited rights to JPL, with JPL assuming no liability for the disclosure, use, or reproduction of such data. This RFI is to also gather information of potential qualified sources and to obtain your business size relative to the following NAICS code: 541513 “Computer Facilities Management Services,” with a size standard of $37 million dollars. Responses to this RFI may be used by JPL to make appropriate decisions regarding a Small Business Set-Aside procurement. All responses shall be submitted by 3/25/2026 electronically to the undersigned, per the instructions set forth in Request for Information Submittal (page 2). Below are some critical dates as it pertains to this Request for Information (RFI): Written RFI Questions are due by 3/12/2026 via submission to the undersigned by email. Questions after this date may be asked during an anticipated JPL-hosted webinar but no written questions will be accepted after 3/12/2026. JPL will respond to written questions by 3/19/2026. As stated above, all responses shall be submitted by 3/25/2026. Information about JPL can be found at: https://www.jpl.nasa.gov/who-we-are/. Also, JPL will host three webinars on the dates listed below to address questions live. Contract Technical Managers, the Subcontracts Manager, and representatives from the JPL Small Business Programs Office may be in attendance. Webinar registration links are provided below and in the “Attachments/Links” section of the SAM.gov RFI posting. March 10, 2026 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM PT (https://lp.constantcontactpages.com/ev/reg/yjp8qbb) March 12, 2026 10:00 AM to 11:00 AM PT (https://lp.constantcontactpages.com/ev/reg/zfuuuwr) March 17, 2026 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM PT (https://lp.constantcontactpages.com/ev/reg/ayuax3v) Thank you, Sara Tan JPL Subcontracts Manager 626-437-8710 Sara.W.Tan@jpl.nasa.gov

## Summary

Amendment 0001 attaches questions and answers received in response to the solicitation. The RFQ due date and time is extended to April 24 at NOON ET. No other amendments to the solicitation are anticipated. Contractor shall provide all labor, supervision, equipment, and material necessary to replace gutter systems and paint the exterior of the main building at USCG Station Oregon Inlet. See attached Statement of Work. This procurement is 100% set-aside for Small Businesses using NAICS code of 236220 and size standard of $45.0M. The Government contemplates award of a Firm-Fixed Price contract resulting from this solicitation. See 52.212-2 for evaluation criteria. All quotes must be submitted by NOON Eastern Time (ET) on APR 23, 2026 via email to frank.m.powers@uscg.mil. Ensure blocks 14 (Include UEI) through 20c of the SF 1442 and pricing are completed before emailing. Questions about this solicitation must be submitted in writing to the Contracting Specialist at the email address listed above, no later than NOON ET APR 16, 2026. An amendment may be posted to clear up any ambiguities and answer all questions at one time. No oral questions will be accepted. All potential quoters are strongly encouraged to visit the site to field verify any/all quantities before submitting quotes. A site visit will be held on APR 14, 2026 at 1000 ET at USCG Station Oregon Inlet, 8700 Coast Guard St, Nags Head, NC 27959. Interested parties must arrange site visits with MKC Thomas Roposa, 401-644-4986, Thomas.J.Roposa@uscg.mil a minimum of three (3) days in advance. All visitors must present a valid Real ID or Passport as a method of identification. Full name, date of birth, and last 4 of SSN must also be provided.

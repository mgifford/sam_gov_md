# USS TRUXTUN (DDG-103) FY26 Depot Modernization Period (DMP)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-14 11:16:50.491-04
- Solicitation Number: N00024-26-C-4410
- Awardee: METRO MACHINE CORP. Norfolk VA 23523 USA
- Award Amount: 183581496.00

## Summary

General Dynamics NASSCO – Norfolk (NASSCO), Norfolk, Virginia, is awarded a $183,228,722 firm-fixed-price (FFP) contract action for maintenance, modernization and repair of USS TRUXTUN (DMP-103) Fiscal Year 2026 DEPOT MODERNIZATION PERIOD (DMP). The scope of this acquisition includes all labor, supervision, equipment, production, testing, facilities, and quality assurance necessary to prepare for and accomplish the Chief of Naval Operations (CNO) Availability for critical modernization, maintenance, and repair programs. This contract includes options, which, if exercised, would bring the cumulative value of this contract to $183,581,496. This contract was competitively procured using full and open competition via PIEE. Competitive proposals were received in response to Solicitation N00024-25-R-4410. The Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA), Washington, District of Columbia, is the contracting activity.

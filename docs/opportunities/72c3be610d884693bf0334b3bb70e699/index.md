# Fiber Optic Equipment Upgrade - 42 OSS/OSM

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-17 09:25:46.938-04
- Solicitation Number: FA3300-032026

## Summary

The 42d Contracting Squadron, Maxwell AFB, AL, is conducting market research to identify qualified sources capable of supporting a Fiber Equipment Upgrade to connect Air Traffic Control radios to existing fiber communications equipment. All feedback and information received may be used to determine the appropriate acquisition strategy. The Government is seeking information from industry to assist in determining what types of products and capabilities are available in the market for this type of requirement outlined in the attached DRAFT Statement of Requirement (SOR).

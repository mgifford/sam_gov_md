# Recreational Center (WV) pool repairs

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 05:28:33.341-04
- Solicitation Number: 19CF2026Q0008

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy in Brazzaville requires the services of the contractor to remove existing wall sealant depth wall and bottom floor within the Swimming pool of Villa Washington. Bids should include all labor, materials, equipment required to prepare resurfacing and install new titles within the pool, correct piping leak, pool deck erosion control, debris/trash removal, transportation and services for completion of work outlined and shall be in accordance with local and US Standard construction industry practices.

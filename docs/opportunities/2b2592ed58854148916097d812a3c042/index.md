# Oracle Health Electronic Health Record Platform Advanced Training Services

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 11:35:12.282-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C10G26Q0045

## Summary

Sole Source vendor - Oracle Health Government Services, Inc., 8779 Hillcrest Road, Kansas City, Missouri, 64138-2700, United States Strategic Acquisition Center (SAC), on behalf of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Veterans Health Administration (VHA), Office of Informatics (OHI), intends to award a non-competitive, firm fixed price, sole source contract to Oracle Health Government Services, Inc. This procurement is for a platform specific training to provide informatics support for both the VistA-based and Oracle Health-based Federal Electronic Health Record (EHR) and associated tools to monitor VistA and Federal EHR use, configuration, and quality. This training is a discreet need from the VA-specific Oracle Health training provided to VHA staff at go live sites by VA’s Electronic Health Record Modernization – Integration Office (EHRM-IO). The estimated award date is April 15, 2026 The government shall not pay for any costs incurred by responses to this notice. Inquiries to this notice are due on or before April 13, 2026, at 12:00PM Eastern Time to (by the response due date shown above) to Lindsay.Gray3@va.gov and Deidre.Parker@va.gov. No phone calls accepted.

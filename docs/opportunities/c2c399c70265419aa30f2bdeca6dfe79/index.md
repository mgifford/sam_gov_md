# Sun Shade Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-09 14:07:26.41-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4801I0001

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide equipment, personnel, and labor to semi-annually inspect, maintain, and repair identified discrepancies for five existing aircraft sunshades, to include but not limited to: structure, metal roofing, lights, electric, bird nesting mitigation, lightning protection, obstruction lighting, safety reflective devices, fall protection, and any other unique attached features as applicable. At a minimum, the Contractor shall inspect for, report, document, and repair identified discrepancies to include: inherent structural weakness; corrosion; improper installation; design defect; electrical defect; weather or turbulence related damage; improper use; destruction; signs or evidence of electrical, structural or mechanical failure; severed item; evidence of stress, buckling, fatigue, etc.; missing hardware; loose hardware; wildlife intrusion; and/or other discrepancies not listed above. Please review Draft PWS for more information.

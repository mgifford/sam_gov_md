# USCIS FY26 3rd Quarter Engagement with Industry

- Posted: 2026-04-24 15:56:56.422-04

## Summary

4/24/2026: Updating the notice to provide an updated advance copy of the presentation slides (V.1.1), replacing V.1, providing subsequent updates, and populated with information currently available. ========================= 4/14/2026: Updating the notice to provide an advance copy of the presentation slides (V.1), populated with information currently available and subject to further revision. As noted below, questions are due by 3 PM (ET), on April 21, 2026. ========================= United States Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) Office of Contracting (OCON) will be hosting its Third Quarter Engagement with Industry for Fiscal Year 26 on April 28, 2026, starting at 1:00 pm (ET). The purpose of these quarterly engagements is to provide continued open and engaging dialogue between USCIS and industry on current and future requirements. The focus of this call will be to provide industry with the latest procurement related information for USCIS. To join as an “Attendee”, please use the link below to self-register: Microsoft Teams meeting https://events.gcc.teams.microsoft.com/event/7974b8fc-89a4-4f1a-805a-0f1b4863d55c@5e41ee74-0d2d-4a72-8975-998ce83205eb USCIS uses Microsoft Teams (MS Teams) as its platform to engage with industry. You will be muted upon entry and your video will be disabled. USCIS plans to record the session and post the recording on its YouTube channel after the event is concluded. USCIS will update this announcement with the presentation slides on or about April 14, 2026. The format of the call will remain the same. We will accept questions during the event and will answer those questions as best we can live. We encourage questions to be provided in advance. We have arranged the requirements by subdivisions. The presenter will present the subdivision’s requirements. We will then answer questions related to only those subdivision’s requirements. Once that subdivision requirements have been briefed, no further questions will be answered live. USCIS will collect all questions and provide a response post-call to an update to this announcement, along with the recording. USCIS is committed to providing industry with accurate and up-to-date procurement information. We continue to listen to the feedback from industry about these calls and have adjusted the calls format to better engage with industry. We strongly encourage industry to submit questions in advance so that we can properly provide you with the most accurate and timely answers. Please include the slide/APFS number when submitting your questions. Vendors, please submit your questions to the following email address by 3 PM (ET), on April 21, 2026: USCIS_Vendoroutreach@uscis.dhs.gov For awareness, here is the FY26 Quarterly Conference Calls with Industry Schedule 1st: October 28, 2025 2nd: January 27, 2026 3rd: April 28, 2026 4th: July 28, 2026

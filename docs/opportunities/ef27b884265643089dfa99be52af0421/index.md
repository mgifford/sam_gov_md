# MOBILE BLEACHERS - 316 CONS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-17 11:23:56.302-04
- Solicitation Number: FA286026Q4012

## Summary

Sources Sought: 316th CONS Bleachers Scope: The 316th CONS on JB Andrews has a need for bleachers that are 39’ Long about 10 rows, Highway towable for easy transport, Vertical Guard Rails for safety, Hot-dip galvanized guardrail, Heavy I-Beam main frame, Aluminum seats & walkboards, Full 24? row spacing for today’s taller people, LED Stop, Turn, & Clearance lights, Electric brakes all 4 wheels, Tongue telescopes in and can be easily removed, Stabilizing jacks operate from standing position, Hydraulic folding mechanism powered by 12 volt system (self-contained battery & charger). Must be towable and must be able to seat about 130 people. Please see attached Salient Characteristics Sheet for a copy of the above information. NOTE: This Sources Sought is issued solely for market research purposes and does not constitute a Request for Quote (RFQ) or a Request for Proposal (RFP). The Government is conducting market research for bleachers. The purpose of this RFI is to identify vendors capable of providing bleachers that meet the Government's requirements. Please provide detailed product information, including specifications and catalog numbers.

# Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) and Control Access System Maintenance - Ft. Bliss, TX

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 09:41:38.077-05
- Solicitation Number: W56ZLW-26-Q-0002

## Summary

The purpose of this amendment is to clarify/add the NTE amounts for the Cost No Fee CLINs and notify interested vendors that updated solicitation documents have been posted and are now available for review and use in preparing quotes. Quotes MUST use the amended solicitation titled "Conformed Amendment_W56ZLW26Q00020001_dated 6 MAR 2026.pdf". This requirement is a non?personal services contract for comprehensive maintenance and repair of the existing Avigilon security camera systems and DAQ Electronics access control systems located at facilities identified in the Performance Work Statement (PWS) on Fort Bliss, TX. The contractor shall provide all personnel, tools, equipment, materials, supervision, and other items necessary to perform: Scheduled preventive maintenance Unscheduled on?call repairs 24/7 emergency response in accordance with the mandatory response times outlined in the PWS Support for all system components identified in the PWS This amendment provides updated documents for vendor use in preparing complete and accurate quotes. Attachments Included in This Amendment The following documents have been uploaded and are available under the “Attachments/Links” section of the solicitation posting: SF 30 – Amendment of Solicitation/Modification of Contract Conformed Amendment Solicitation with Responses to Questions from Industry (incorporating all amendments) Wage Determination Limited Sources Justification (LSJ) Memorandum Vendors are advised to review all updated documents in full prior to submitting a quote. Instructions to Vendors All quotes shall be submitted in accordance with the instructions contained in the conformed solicitation. Vendors are responsible for monitoring SAM.gov for future updates or amendments.

# Fire Maintenance and Support IAW PWS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-10 11:26:58.606-04
- Solicitation Number: W912BU26RA00FireSpt

## Summary

**This is not a solicitation; this is a sources sought notice in support of market research.** The Contractor shall provide all qualified personnel, equipment, supplies, transportation, materials, and supervision necessary to perform inspection, maintenance and operation support services for the Operations Department, Infrastructure Division, NSWCCD as defined in Section 5 of this Performance Work Statement. This shall include inspections, maintenance, modification, and repairs to all fire alarm, protection, extinguishing, and notification systems. The contractor shall maintain a database of all equipment and maintenance records. The Contractor shall perform in accordance with (IAW) the terms and conditions of the contract and consistently with the established standards in this PWS. This is a six month IDC to support the acquisition of the five year service contract. With responses, please include past experience, related capabilities, and small business socio-economic information. This vehicle is to prevent a gap in service and expected to be under the SAT (Simplified Acquisition Threshold).

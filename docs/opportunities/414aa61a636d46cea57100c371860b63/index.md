# Supply and Installation for Wall-to-Wall Carpet Replacement, in Rome, Italy.

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 09:57:01.898-04
- Solicitation Number: 191T7026Q0021

## Summary

Please note this solicitation is not ready to be issued as of this date. The Embassy of the United States in Rome, Italy will launch the solicitation on the SAM.gov website when it is available. The solicitation covers the supply and installation of wall –to- wall Carpet replacement for an estimate of approximately 400 square meters for the U.S. Embassy in Rome, Italy. All responsible sources may submit a proposal, which will be considered. The Government may award the contract based on the initial offer without discussion. The resultant contract will be a firm fixed price type purchase order. All contractors must be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to submitting an offer pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. Therefore, prospective offerors are encouraged to register prior to the submittal of proposal. Electronic Submission/Responses: This opportunity allows for electronic responses. Please submit your offer prior to the response date and time. Please ensure your email with your offer does not exceed 25MB, if it does then you might have to send more than one email.

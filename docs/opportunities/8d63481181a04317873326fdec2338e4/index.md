# Notice of Intent to Sole Source to Pioneering Decisive Solutions, Inc for the Commander, Fleet Readiness Center to Procure Services to Support the Aviation Rapid Action Team

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-16 13:24:13.701-04
- Solicitation Number: N68520-25-RFPREQ-F000000-0234

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR), Commander, Fleet Readiness Center (COMFRC) Procurement Group, Patuxent River, MD hereby gives notice of intent to award a Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF) Contract with a Base and 4 Option Years to Pioneering Decisive Solutions, Inc (PIDESO), 22560 Epic Dr., Ste 100, California, MD 20619-3097, (Cage Code: 4LVQ7), on a sole source basis in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.103-1(b), only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. COMFRC has a requirement to procure Contractor Engineering Technical Services (CETS) for the Aviation Rapid Action Team (ARAT); to include engineering and technical support services, component repair services, and logistics support analyses that will be in the following areas: capability analysis; design and development; component testing and repair; component reverse engineering analysis; diagnoses and resolution of software and hardware failure modes; material obsolescence mitigation; technical documentation; workforce training; support equipment management; facilities and infrastructure planning; and logistics analysis of U.S. Naval Aviation maintenance and supply systems data. THIS NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITIVE QUOTES. However, all responsible sources may submit a capability statement to Contract Specialist, Lori Sinclair at lori.b.sinclair.civ@us.navy.mil within fifteen (15) days of this posting. Information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. A determination by the Government not to compete this requirement based on responses received as a result of this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. The Government will not reimburse interested parties for any presentation, marketing efforts, or data related to this notice.

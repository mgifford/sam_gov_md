# Sierra NF High Sierra & Bass Lake Reforestation

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 13:02:55.92-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAS26Q0017

## Summary

USFS PPS FPO SWZ CSA 2 is pursuing a contract to provide reforestation services for the High Sierra Ranger District and Bass Lake Ranger District, on the Sierra National Forest. Work includes auger planting conifer trees across multiple elevation bands in the spring months. Services shall be performed by the awardee in accordance with the specifications, terms, and conditions of this solicitation, including those found in the solicitation attachments (see Solicitation Section D). SET-ASIDE: This solicitation is set aside 100% for Small Business entities under NAICS 115310 - Support Activities for Forestry. PROJECT LOCATION: Sierra National Forest, High Sierra Ranger District and Bass Lake Ranger District. See solicitation and its attachments for location details. SOLICITATION POINT OF CONTACT: Matthew (Matt) Killian, Contracting Officer matthew.killian@usda.gov

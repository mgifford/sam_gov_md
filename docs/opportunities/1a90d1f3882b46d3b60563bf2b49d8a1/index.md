# Joint Base Andrews and Joint Base Anacostia-Bolling Simplified Acquisition of Base Engineering Requirements (SABER) Sources Sought

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-10 15:34:37.436-04
- Solicitation Number: FA2860SABER

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought notice for market research purposes only, not a request for proposal (RFP) or quotation. The U.S. Air Force at Joint Base Andrews (JBA) and Joint Base-Anacostia-Bolling (JBAB) is seeking to identify qualified and experienced 8(a) certified small businesses located within the mid-Atlantic region capable of performing work under a Simplified Acquisition of Base Engineering Requirements (SABER) contract. The mid-Atlantic region consists of the following states: Delaware, District of Columbia, Maryland, Pennsylvania, Virginia, and West Virginia. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) for this acquisition is 236220, with a size standard of $45,000,000. The Government does not intend to award a contract on the basis of this sources sought or to otherwise pay for the information solicited. Please see Attachment 1 - SABER Sources Sought Questionnaire for additional information and submission requirements. Note: Any responses provided that do not complete the sources sought questionnaire will not be recorded as interested vendors for market research purposes.

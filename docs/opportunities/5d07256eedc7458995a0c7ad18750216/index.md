## Summary

The Department of Commerce (DOC) Office of Acquisition Management (OAM), Shared Services Procurement Office is seeking potential sources who can Spectrum Repurposing Analyses Support in accordance with the draft Performance Work Statement (PWS) on behalf of the National Telecommunications and Information Administration (NTIA). This notice does not commit the Government to contract for any supply or service. This is not a Request for Proposal (RFP) and proposals will not be accepted. No pricing or cost data shall be submitted. If the information is submitted, it will not be considered. The Government will not make an award resulting from this Sources Sought Notification. Any information received in response will be used to develop a potential solicitation with the intent to make a Government contract award. This notice does not guarantee the release of a solicitation. Responses to the notice will not be disclosed outside the Government; however, Respondents shall consider information received may be used to shape a future public solicitation and therefore avoid providing proprietary information that could otherwise be disclosed through the solicitation process. Responses to this notice are voluntary and costs will not be reimbursed. No pricing or cost data shall be submitted. If the information is submitted, it will not be considered. Please review the attached documents to ensure an adequate response to this notice.

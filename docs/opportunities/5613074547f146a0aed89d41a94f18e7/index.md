# 5985 - Repair BOA - AN/USQ-167 Common Data Link System (CDLS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 16:21:13.371-04
- Solicitation Number: N0010426GBG01

## Summary

This is a sole-source presolicitation notice for a five-year Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) that will support the repair of the AN/USQ-167 Common Data Link System (CDLS), which is essential for fleet readiness. In accordance with FAR 6.103-1, the Government intends to solicit and negotiate with only one source, Cubic Secure Communications, LLC, which is the sole authorized repair source for the NIINs listed below. The Government does not own the data or rights to permit any other sources to perform the repairs. Applicable NIINs: • 015234824 • 015234850 • 015234852 • 015234855 • 015234681 • 015234827 • 016831743 • 016777534 Instructions for interested parties: This notice is not a request for competitive proposals; however, responsible sources may submit a capability statement in response. All responses received within 15 days of this notice’s publication will be considered by the Government. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed BOA based on responses is solely within the Government’s discretion. Parties who are not approved repair sources must obtain approval by submitting the information detailed in the NAVSUP Weapon Systems Support (WSS) Source Approval Process (SAR) Brochure – Maritime, available at: https://www.navsup.navy.mil/NAVSUP-Enterprise/NAVSUP-Weapon-Systems-Support/Business-Opps/. Approval of a source is required. Please note: award will not be delayed for the qualification of a new source.

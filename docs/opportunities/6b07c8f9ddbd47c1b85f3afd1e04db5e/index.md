# C-SUAS Equipment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 09:58:06.355-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4419-26-Q-0017

## Summary

The 97th Contracting Squadron, Altus Air Force Base, Oklahoma, intends to issue a Request for Quote (RFQ) for acquisition of a Counter-small Unmanned Aircraft Systems (C-sUAS) with a second UNFUNDED CLIN for the option to purchase a second C-sUAS by 1 Jul 26 should funding become available at Altus AFB, Oklahoma. Evaluation of pricing will include the total of BOTH CLINs. This is a brand name or equal requirement IAW FAR 52.211-6. The preferred products are DroneShield brand but a product that meets the characteristics listed below and in Attachment 1 - Salient Characteristics will also be considered technically acceptable. The items must comply with: 1. Immediate Response Kit (DroneShield Part # DS-119) 2. Magnetic Antennae Kt Wideband (DroneShield Part # DS-146) 3. Rugged Tablet for C2 Tactical (DroneShield Part #s DS-121) 4. C2 Tactical Configuration (DroneShield Part # DS-121A) Any questions shall be submitted via email to the below points of contacts by 6 May 2026 at 1100 CST (11:00AM CST). This acquisition will fall under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 334511. This is a 100% Small Business Set Aside. POC: Jarrod.Ford@us.af.mil, Scott.Swain.@us.af.mil.

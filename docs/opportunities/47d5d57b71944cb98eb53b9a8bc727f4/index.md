# 801 RHG Asphalt

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 19:37:05.349-04

## Summary

801 RHG requires the purchase and delivery of 1700 tons of 3/4" (19mm) 76-22 a high-performance polymer-modified asphalt mix and 550 tons of 3/4" clean rock, to be delivered in super 18s a 7-axle, high-capacity dump truck. Deliveries will be made incrementally upon request from date of award until August 13, 2026. Please see Attachments F4ATD16041AQ01_Combo and Attachment 1 - Item list included for more information.

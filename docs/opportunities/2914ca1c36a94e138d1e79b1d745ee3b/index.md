# Standardized Micro- and Nano- Plastics

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 12:46:05.407-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N992-26-SS-104

## Summary

The intent of this Sources Sought notice is to gauge industry interest, capabilities, and relevant experience to support Advanced Research Projects Agency for Health (ARPA-H) Health Science Futures Office (HSF) by providing standardized micro- and nanoplastics to enable technology research and development activities. Specifically, ARPA-H is looking for the capability to provide micro- and nanoplastic (MNP) materials with specific attributes. See attached for sources sought request and response instructions.

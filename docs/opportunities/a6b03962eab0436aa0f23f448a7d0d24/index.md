# BPA Call for Lease of Specialty Beds and Surfaces, Tripler Army Medical Center, Hawaii

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 15:08:14.5-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0226FA045

## Summary

Pursuant to FAR Subpart 13.106-1(b)(1), the Medical Readiness Contracting Office-Pacific intends to issue a firm-fixed price BPA Call under BPA W81K0224A0006 to Hill-Rom, Inc. in the amount of $75,000.00 for Leasing of Specialty Beds and Surfaces for Tripler Army Medical Center, Hawaii. This notice is not a request for proposal and a solicitation will not be issued. Any vendor that believes it can provide the stated lease for specialty beds and surfaces can provide supporting documents demonstrating their product's capability and specifications. Responses received may be used for future acquisitions. Information can be sent to the Contract Specialist: deborah.n.wada.civ@health.mil. No telephone or facsimile requests will be accepted. This procurement will be prepared in accordance with FAR Part 13, using Simplified Acquisition Procedures.

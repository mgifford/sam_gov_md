- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The contractor shall provide support for the Air Show portion of the 2027 Open House at Robins Air Force Base (RAFB), Georgia. The Air Show portion includes all aerial and ground demonstrations and static displays. This requirement is inclusive of the AirBoss, Announcer team, and Ground Boss IAW PWS.

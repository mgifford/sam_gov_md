## Summary

Aviation Devices and Electronic Components LLC, CAGE code 1NPE1 has been awarded a maximum $32,513,828.64 firm-fixed-price, Indefinite Quantity Contract under solicitation SPE7MX-25-R-0088 for 53 NSNs, gaskets and packing material items. This solicitation was issued under the statutory authority permitting other than full and open competition is 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1) as implemented by FAR 6.103-1 formerly 6.302-1. There was one response received. This is a five-year contract with a one-year base period and four one-year option periods. The performance completion date is March 31, 2031, if all options are exercised. The contracting activity is the Defense Logistics Agency Weapons Support Columbus.

# Award of DSMAC Phase III

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-30 15:10:09.42-04
- Solicitation Number: N00019-24-RFPREQ-WPM280-0040
- Awardee: RAYTHEON COMPANY Tucson AZ 85756 USA

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) awarded a new task order under the Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) N00019-20-G-0007 for Tomahawk Digital Scene Matching Area Correlator (DSMAC) Redesign Phase III with Raytheon Company 29 August 2025. This action was awarded under the authority of FAR 6.301.1 (a)(2)(III), only one responsible source, for Tomahawk (DSMAC) Redesign.

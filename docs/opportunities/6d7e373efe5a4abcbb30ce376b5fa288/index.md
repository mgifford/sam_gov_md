# Small Composite Cleanroom Modification

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 21:41:40.28-04
- Solicitation Number: SERV-C25-007

## Summary

** THIS IS A PRE-SOLICITATION NOTICE. NO QUOTES WILL BE ACCEPTED UNTIL THE REQUEST FOR QUOTE (RFQ) IS POSTED. ** Description of Services: The 76th CMXG request services to furnish and install an Environmental Control Air Handling Unit to be mounted on the floor outside and adjacent to the room. This unit is 54” wide x 31” deep x 74” high. It is a chilled water up flow, 25KW 3-phase, 460v unit that will include chilled water coil, belt drive blower, humidifier, reheat, filters, with controls and monitoring to integrate with Tinker AFB’s network. It will include a 15KW electric reheat, 1”, 3-way modulating chilled water valve, 1”, temperature/humidity sensor mounted on the unit, bottom return air, MERV 8 filters, 10 lb/hr steam humidifier, floor stand, and condensate probe. Furnish and install overhead ducted system with 6 diffusers Furnish and install low sidewall return Furnish and install one 10’ x 8’ high-speed, fabric, roll up door along 40’ side of room Furnish and install four process vacuum ports – TAFB to provide connection within 25’ Furnish and install two central vacuum ports – TAFB to provide connection within 25’ Furnish and install four compressed air ports – TAFB to provide connection within 25’ Furnish and install one drainless eye wash station Furnish and install BACnet devices and monitoring system to track room temperature, pressure, humidity, and particle count - TAFB to connect data collection equipment to network. Tinker Air Force Base (TAFB) to provide 460 v/3-phase power supply within 25’ for: AHU – 25kw, 3ph, 480 volt Door – 480 volt, 3 ph, 20 amp circuit The Contractor shall provide all labor, tools, equipment, test equipment, material, parts, quality control, transportation and inspection manuals necessary to perform the upgrade to the Small Composite Cleanroom located on TAFB. The Contractor shall be responsible for ensuring all services, parts and Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) components provided are in accordance with (IAW) the terms and conditions outlined herein, conform to the manufacturer’s equipment specifications and the Performance Work Statement (PWS). Contemplated Contract Details: Firm-fixed price (FFP) 100% Small Business Set-Aside, Competitive NAICS – 238220; Size Standard – $19M; FSC/PSC – J041 Eligibility for Award: System for Award Management (SAM) is required to receive any award. Vendors can register electronically at https://sam.gov/content/home.

## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE W912ER26RA025 Procurement of Shiplift Spare PartsThe U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Southwestern Division, Middle East District (SWM), is issuing this sources sought announcement to conduct market research, seeking to identify qualified parties with the capability to procure various spare parts identified within Appendix A and in accordance with the Performance Work Statement (PWS).As a result of this market research, the U.S. Government will gain a better understanding of industry capabilities for this potential future requirement.This is a sources sought announcement for market research purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation.

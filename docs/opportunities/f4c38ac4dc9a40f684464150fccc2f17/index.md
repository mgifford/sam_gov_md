# 43--Central Section Sump Pump Replacements-Hoover Dam

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-19 16:52:14.499-04
- Solicitation Number: 140R3026Q0054

## Summary

Hoover Dam is in need of two replacement HydroFlo Vertical Turbine or equal pumps. The scope of this supply is the purchase of 2 sump pump assemblies that are interchangeable, in whole or in part, with the currently installed sump pumps. Interchangeability of the pumps is essential to ease of maintenance of the entire sump pumps system and eliminates the need to maintain larger stock of wear parts while also removing the risk of incorrect part installation during maintenance. These two pumps will be installed by government personnel.

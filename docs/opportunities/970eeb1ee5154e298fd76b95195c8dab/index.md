## Summary

The purpose of this Sources Sought Notice (SSN) is to gain knowledge regarding the types of interested businesses and their qualifications. Information obtained through this SSN by the National Park Service (NPS) will be utilized to develop the acquisition strategy. The amount of interest, or lack thereof, will determine the possibility of a Small Business or other Socio-Economic Set-Aside for solicitation and award purposes. Analysis of the quality of responses to this SSN will be considered in the market research being conducted and in the making of any set-aside determination for this requirement.

# Anduril Enterprise Contract (EC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Anduril Industries, Inc., Costa Mesa, California, was awarded a Single-Award Enterprise Contract (EC) which is a 10-Year (five year base and five year option period), Firm-Fixed Price Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Contract valued at $20,000,000,000.00. This EC is for the complete full suite of Anduril commercial offerings, which include software, hardware, data and compute infrastructure, and related technical support services. The EC will be available for ordering by the Department of War (DoW) and all other authorized federal agencies.

# N66604- NUWC Newport Mastery of the Seas Office Hours

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-15 14:43:45-04
- Solicitation Number: N66604-MoS26

## Summary

Mastery of the Seas Industry Office Hours Announcement Naval Undersea Warfare Center, Division Newport-N66604 OFFICE HOURS: The Naval Undersea Warfare Center, Division Newport (NUWCDIVNPT) will be holding Mastery of the Seas Office Hours. During these office hours, Subject Matter Experts (SMEs) from the Mastery of the Seas Capability Focus Areas and other NUWC representatives will be available to discuss the strategic vision of the Center with a specific focus on how external industry can participate and contribute to the mission. This event will be held on a recurring bi-monthly basis with an in-person and online option. The first meeting will be held on Tuesday 21 APR 2026. TENTATIVE AGENDA: 1200 EST – Welcome and Discussion 1300 EST– Event Concludes 21 APR 2026: (In-person and Teams) 05 MAY 2026: (In-person and Teams) 19 MAY 2026: (In-person and Teams) IN-PERSON EVENT: In-person office hours will be available at NUWC Building 80 Conference Room 208 (second floor). To attend the in-person event, please complete and return the attached Registration Form to maria.f.gregory.civ@us.navy.mil. MS TEAMS PRESENTATION: A link will be provided via email to registrants prior to the event. Please email maria.f.gregory.civ@us.navy.mil with a completed registration. A link will be provided no later than one-hour prior to the event. A new link will be required for each day of participation. Please register each time to ensure the correct link is provided. REGISTRATION: Each person interested in participating in the Office Hours must complete and submit the attached Registration Form no later than the Friday prior to the Tuesday event. Please submit the completed registration form via email to maria.f.gregory.civ@us.navy.mil. Registration is required for both the in-person and online events. QUESTIONS: Questions are being accepted and should be submitted via email in advance of the office hours. Questions will be taken during the event. GENERAL STIPULATIONS AND DISCLAIMERS: Any information provided in response to this notice is not binding upon the Government. Industry will not be permitted to set up marketing material. Respondents are advised that the Government will not pay for any information or administrative costs incurred in response to this notice; all costs associated with responding to this notice will be solely at the interested parties' expense. Not responding to this notice does not preclude participation in any future event. It is the responsibility of the potential interested respondees to monitor SeaPort for additional information pertaining to the upcoming office hours. There will be no fee to attend, however, industry participants will be required to preregister no later than the Friday prior to the Tuesday event.

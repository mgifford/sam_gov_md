# GSA OASIS+ SB 10206 Router Solution for B-52H Crypto Modernization Production

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-09 13:21:14.917-04
- Solicitation Number: RFP1798904

## Summary

UPDATE 1: The subject contract opportunity's open period for proposal has been extended by 2 weeks. The new due date is 28 April 2026. This posting will only include Bidders’ Library documents and Fair Opportunity Proposal Request (FOPR). All other documents related to proposal submission are in the B-52 Crypto Mod Router Qualification posting on E-Buy (RFP 1798904 OASIS+ SB 10206). To access and review the bidder's library documents for the B-52 Crypto Modernization Router Qualification please submit the B-52 Crypto Mod Router Qualification Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) Restricted Access Request Form to lauren.vanhook@us.af.mil and michael.west.57@us.af.mil. The purpose of these documents is to provide potential offerors with information necessary to solidify their understanding of the requirement. Interested offerors need to have an active and verified DD Form 2345 on file to access unclassified technical data disclosing militarily critical technology with military or space application that is under the control of, or in possession of the U.S. Department of Defense. Offerors interested in obtaining access to the Bidders’ Library will need to send a request to the Contracting Officer, lauren.vanhook@us.af.mil and Contract Specialist, michael.west.57@us.af.mil, along with a copy of their verified DD Form 2345. For Questions on completing the DD Form 2345, offerors may view DLA’s Joint Certification Program page at https://www.dla.mil/logistics-operations/services/joint-certification-program/. The Government reserves the right to provide access to the documents to legitimate B-52 Crypto Mod Router Qualification bidders. Access to (CUI) Router Drawings_Wiring Interface will require restricted access request. PLEASE NOTE: The Procuring Contracting Officer and Contract Specialist are the sole points of contact for this acquisition and the documents within the Bidder's Library. All communication shall be routed through lauren.vanhook@us.af.mil and michael.west.57@us.af.mil.

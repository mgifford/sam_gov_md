# USS AUGUSTA (LCS 34) FY26 DSRA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 11:27:22.391-04
- Solicitation Number: N0002426C4420
- Awardee: BAE SYSTEMS SAN DIEGO SHIP REPAIR INC. San Diego CA USA
- Award Amount: 35705364.00

## Summary

BAE Systems San Diego Ship Repair Inc., San Diego, California, is awarded a $33,513,369 firm-fixed-price (FFP) contract action for maintenance, modernization and repair of USS AUGUSTA (LCS 34) Fiscal Year 2026 Docking Selected Restricted Availability (DSRA). The scope of this acquisition includes all labor, supervision, equipment, production, testing, facilities, and quality assurance necessary to prepare for and accomplish the Chief of Naval Operations (CNO) Availability for critical modernization, maintenance, and repair programs. This contract includes options which, if exercised, would bring the cumulative value of this contract to $35,705,364. This contract was competitively procured using full and open competition via the System for Award Management website, with three offers received. The Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA), Washington, District of Columbia, is the contracting activity (N00024-26-C-4420).

# Navigation and Ship Control System Engineering Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 09:51:13.721-04
- Solicitation Number: N6449826R3001

## Summary

The contractor shall provide technical, logistical, programmatic, training, field engineering, cybersecurity, and administrative services required for development, fielding, and maintenance of NSWCPD LCS, LPD, LSM, LHD, LSD, DDG, CVN, LHA, FFG, DDG1000 and CG Ship Control System projects. These services shall include support for land and shipboard based hardware and testing, program management support, shipboard installation, testing, and troubleshooting support (including at-sea troubleshooting and testing), waterfront technical, logistical and training support, and administrative support, as required to maintain and upgrade designated shipboard systems for the U.S. Navy.

## Summary

UPDATE: AMENDMENT 00003 IS HEREBY POSTED, ANSWERING QUESTIONS TO INDUSTRY. UPDATE: AMENDMENT 00002 IS HEREBY POSTED, EXTENDING THE SOLICITATION DEADLINE TO 28 MAY 2025 AT 3:00PM CST AND ANSWERING QUESTIONS TO INDUSTRY. UPDATE: AMENDMENT 00001 IS HEREBY POSTED, EXTENDING THE SOLICITATION DEADLINE TO 14 MAY 2025 AT 3:00PM CST. The Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) hereby issues a Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) Request for Proposal (RFP), Solicitation W519TC-24-R-2011, for various Contstruction Service Projects emerging at Pine Bluff Arsenal (PBA), in Pine Bluff, Arkansas. The BOA RFP will result in the execution of BOAs with offerors determined to be technically acceptable in accordance with the requirements in this Solicitation. Separate Task Order (TO) RFP's will be issued for each of the construction requirements emerging within the year; however, the BOA RFP will be the only opprotunity contractors will have to obtain an executed BOA in order to propose against the subsequent TO RFPs. BOA proposals will not be solicited nor accepted, and BOAs will not be executed, at any time other than during the annual BOA RFP, even for those emergent requirements not specifically identified herein; as such, if a contractor would like to have the opportunity to be able to participate in any future TO that may arise within the next year, it is imperative that the contractor submit a proposal in response to this BOA RFP. Contractors are advised to periodically review SAM in order to obtain the solicitation and any amendments which may be issued. Failure to obtain the solicitation/amendments and to respond to thme prior to the closing date and time of the BOA RFP may render the proposal nonresponsive and result in its rejection.

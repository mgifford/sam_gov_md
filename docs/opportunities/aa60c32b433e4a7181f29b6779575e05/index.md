## Summary

INVITATION FOR BIDS (IFB) NOTICE 2-1-QSC-T-26-054 DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap Term Contract NASA Wallops Flight Facility – Wallops Island, VA 23337 OVERVIEW Commodity: DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap Location: NASA Wallops Flight Facility, 34200 Fulton Street, Wallops Island, VA 23337 Contract Type: Term Contract (3-year base with up to two 1-year options) Estimated Volume: Approximately 185,000 pounds annually Disposal Requirement: Material must be recycled for material content only SOLICITATION ACCESS This solicitation is available for review and bidding on SAM.gov and GSA Auctions. GSA Auctions Link: https://gsaauctions.gov/auctions/preview/360492 All solicitation documents, including the IFB and Appendices, are located in the Documents section of the GSA Auctions listing. SUBMISSION INFORMATION Submission Method: Email (sealed bid submission) Bid Submission Deadline: May 19, 2026 – 5:00 PM Central Time Bid Opening: May 21, 2026 – Time provided via email notification KEY REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY) MANDATORY Pre-Bid Site Inspection Required (IFB Section 3.3) Submission must include: Appendix A – Site Verification & Bidder Certification Appendix B – Bid Sheet & SF 114 Appendix C – Removal Execution Plan Pricing is based on a percentage of a published market index Certified weight tickets are required; contractor-owned scales are not permitted Performance Guarantee of $15,000 required post-award All amendments must be acknowledged Failure to meet these requirements may result in the bid being deemed non-responsive SITE ACCESS / SECURITY NASA Wallops Flight Facility is a secured Federal installation Advance coordination is required for all site access Visitor registration and approval are required prior to arrival Access is restricted to U.S. citizens with valid identification REAL ID requirements may apply All inspection scheduling must be coordinated with the Government Site Point of Contact (POC): Henry Chew NASA Wallops Flight Facility Phone: 757-824-1331 Email: henry.c.chew@nasa.gov IMPORTANT NOTICE — READ ALL DOCUMENTS This notice is provided for summary purposes only The Invitation for Bids (IFB) and all Appendices located in the Documents section of the GSA Auctions listing are the controlling solicitation documents Bidders are responsible for reviewing all documents in their entirety.

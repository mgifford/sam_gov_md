# Mowing & Trimming Services at Thomson Causeway Recreation Area

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 14:21:51.944-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26QA025

## Summary

The United States Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) - Rock Island District intends to issue a solicitation for mowing and trimming services at Thomson Causeway Recreation Area in Thomson, Illinois. This is a pre-solicitation notice and is NOT a request for quotations. The contractor shall provide all labor, supervision, materials/supplies, transportation and fuel to perform mowing and trimming services at Thomson Causeway Recreation Area. Additional information on requirement will be provided when solicitation is released.

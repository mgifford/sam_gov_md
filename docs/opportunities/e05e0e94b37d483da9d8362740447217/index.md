# Maintenance & Repair of Fire Extinguishers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 08:51:01.922-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CM

## Summary

The Regional Contracting Office intends to competitively award a hybrid Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) and Time-and-Materials (T&M) contract for the maintenance and repair of fire extinguishers for USAG Wiesbaden, Germany. The Contractor shall furnish all employees, supervision, equipment, supplies, tools, and transportation necessary to provide comprehensive fire extinguisher maintenance and repair services within the USAG Wiesbaden footprint. The contract will consist of a three (3) year Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Single Award. Any amendments related to the RFQ will be posted to www.Sam.gov. IMPORTANT: Quoters must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at the time of submission of quotes. For registration instructions visit the following website: www.sam.gov NOTE: All dates and times refer to Central European Time.

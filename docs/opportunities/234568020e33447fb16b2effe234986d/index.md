# IFB 33-6003 HM Sales Used Pole Mount Transformers, Selfridge ANGB, MI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sale of Surplus Property
- Posted: 2026-03-10 14:38:19.305-04
- Solicitation Number: 33-6003

## Summary

A.3 Item Description: These transformers are classified as non-PCB per 40 CFR 761 due to: where analysis was performed, results show <50 PPM PCB, or where analysis was not performed, manufacture data plates show non-PCB. The purchaser will not be authorized to utilize a Statement of Intent (SOI) destination in a state which regulates PCB levels at a more stringent level than 40 CFR 761 unless the transformer PCBs levels are under the state regulated level. Example: CA, OR, and WA regulate PCBs if >5 PPM. SOI destinations in these states can receive transformers from these sales if the transformer PCBs levels are less than 5 PPM. These transformers are intact and unbroken. Should a leak be found at time removal, the Generator will fix the leak. Once leak has been verified fixed by Purchaser, Generator, and Disposition Field Site Representative, only then can the transformer be removed by Purchaser. Intent to export these transformers outside of the U.S. for recycling is not authorized under this sale. Potential high bidders intending to recycle the transformers outside of the U.S. will not be considered responsive bidders and their bids will be rejected. This is NOT a service contract

# KC-135 SIL Fuselage Wall

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 14:34:21.582-04
- Solicitation Number: FA813926Q0014

## Summary

Pictures have been added for the Cut End KC-135 SIL Fuselage Wall in accordance with PWS KC-135 SIL Wall Installation at Tinker AFB OK. The contractor shall provide all labor, tools, and expertise required to construct a wall enclosing the cut end of the KC-135 Fuselage. The Government will provide the primary construction materials as listed in Section 9.4 of the Performance Work Statement (PWS). There will be a Site Visit 10 April 2026 at 1300. Interested Parties should provide the following to the POC: First Name: Last Name: Drivers License #: State of Issue: *Must have a REAL ID to get on base.*

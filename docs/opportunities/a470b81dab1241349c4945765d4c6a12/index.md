# J--ASTORIA MX Maintenance contract

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE

- Posted: 2026-04-20 10:03:55.622-04

## Summary

This Sources Sought Notice is being published for the purpose of conducting Market Research to obtain information regarding the availability and capability of all qualified sources to perform a potential requirement. This is not a solicitation. All costs associated with providing information, as requested by this Sources Sought Notice, shall be the responsibility of the submitter. Submission is voluntary. The National Water Quality Lab (NWQL) uses seven (7) Astoria PacificA2 Segmented Flow Analyzer instruments, with Astoria Pacific 411-L autosamplers, for analysis of nutrients in various matrices. Timely and reliable instrument service and maintenance is critical to this analysis as these instruments analyze thousands of samples per year. The NWQL is located in building 95 at the Denver Federal Center, located in Lakewood, CO. Scope: The Contractor shall provide full on-site service, maintenance and software support, along with travel for the Astoria Pacific instruments and autosamplers

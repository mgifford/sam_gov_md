# Support for the Industrial Wastewater Treatment Plant_RFI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-23 15:33:24.365-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8201-IWTP-3_23_2026

## Summary

Hill Air Force Base requests information of sources capable of providing support for the Industrial Waste Treatment Plant’s (IWTP) control system. The goal of this work is to furnish a completely integrated Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) system to monitor and control industrial waste treatment processes located in the IWTP compound located at Hill AFB. Please review the attached documents.

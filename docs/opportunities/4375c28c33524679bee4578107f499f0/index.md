# SUNDAY PRIMARY MUSIC SERVICE, U.S. Fleet Activities Chinhae (CFAC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 01:16:31.271-05
- Solicitation Number: W90VN926RA046

## Summary

The U.S. Navy, Commander, Fleet Activities Chinhae (CFAC), Republic of Korea, intends to establish a fixed-price Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for an on-site Chapel Music Director. Services will include music support for Catholic Mass, Protestant Worship Services, and Special Religious Events conducted at the CFAC Chapel. This requirement is restricted to local contractors capable of providing a qualified Music Director in good standing who can perform in person on a recurring schedule at the U.S. Navy installation in Chinhae, Republic of Korea. Remote or virtual services will not be accepted. Individual BPA Calls will specify service dates, quantities, and pricing. Place of Performance: CFAC Chapel, Chinhae, Republic of Korea.

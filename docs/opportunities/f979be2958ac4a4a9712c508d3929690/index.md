## Summary

ID: NOI-NIH-26-PCA-ORS-02827 Post Date: April 3, 2026 Response Date: April 24, 2026 NAICS: 311119 Product Service Code: 8710 Forage and Feed This is a Notice of Intent, NOT a request for proposal. The National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID) of the National Institutes of Health (NIH) intends to negotiate on an other than full and open competition basis with Animal Specialties and Provisions, LLC (ASP) for this requirement to provide laboratory animal feed, bedding, produce, enrichment, and related dry goods. Description of need: The contractor must provide all labor, materials, transportation, logistics, and quality control necessary to supply and deliver laboratory animal feed, bedding, produce, enrichment feed items, and related dry goods to multiple NIH animal research facilities. These supplies include, but are not limited to: Standard rodent chow (maintenance, breeding, irradiated, and certified diets) Specialty research diets Nonhuman primate chow and biscuits Rabbit diets and agricultural species diets Bedding materials (corn cob, paper-based, hardwood, or approved alternatives) Fresh produce and enrichment feed items All products must meet strict quality, nutritional, traceability, and contamination control standards necessary for regulated research environments and must comply with applicable animal care regulations and accreditation standards. Period of Performance: 5/1/2026 – 4/30/2027 Place of Performance: Bethesda, MD 20892 Deliver to: 9000 Rockville Pike, Bethesda MD 20892-5520 The statutory authority for this sole source requirement is 41 U.S.C. 1901 (a) (1) as implemented by FAR 13.106-1(b) only one responsible source and no other supply of service will satisfy agency requirements. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL. THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION FOR COMPETITIVE QUOTATIONS.?

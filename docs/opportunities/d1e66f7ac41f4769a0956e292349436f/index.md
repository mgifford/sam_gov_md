# 18th Field Artillery FAB Ice Storage Boxes and Ice Delivery

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 11:20:08.082-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124726QA012

## Summary

The contractor shall prepare a technical and price proposal for three (3) portable ice storage boxes, electrical connection in order to operate the ice storage boxes and deliver sixty (60) 20 pound bags of ice monthly. Delivery of ice will be once every two weeks or upon request. The Contractor must ensure maintenance of the ice storage boxes is conducted for full operation and serviceability. The Contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform operations necessary for the 18th FAB request. The contractor shall remove all trash, debris, or surplus materials from the work sites and shall leave the work site clean of all debris when work is completed.

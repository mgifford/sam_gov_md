- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The purpose of amendment 01 is to make changes to salient characteristics for Line Item 001, and to change quantities to Line Items 001 - 003. See attached amendment. The contractor shall provide commercial, brand-name-or-equal components to support FPV Sprint 1 v2 systems, including solid-state lithium-ion drone batteries, chargers, battery checkers, XT60-to-XT90 connectors, CW/CCW propellers, toolkits, and fire-retardant battery bags. All items must meet or exceed the salient characteristics and must be compatible with FPV Sprint 1 v2.

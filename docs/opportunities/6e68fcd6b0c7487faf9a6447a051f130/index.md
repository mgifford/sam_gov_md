# Safety Assured Control and Security Architecture for Mission Autonomy

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-11 15:28:12.125-04
- Solicitation Number: RCI-2026-002

## Summary

CPE MA is conducting market research/sources sought to identify industry capabilities for software and hardware solutions to provide a safety assured control and security architecture for uncrewed air, ground and maritime platforms and their integrated lethal and non-lethal payloads operating under remote control and/or autonomous control. The goal is to provide an independent verifiable level of assured control with safety-critical functions of robotic autonomous systems under various levels of supervision. Supervision may range from an operator in-the-loop to on-the-loop, to complete local autonomy, all in a variety of operational domains.

## Summary

Per GSAR 570.203-2(b) and 570.402-1(a), sole-source actions at or below the SLAT do not require a formal Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition (JOTFOC). On behalf of the Tennessee Valley Healthcare System (TVHS), I am pleased to confirm execution of a fully signed lease awarded in response to VA Request for Lease Proposals (RLP) 36C24926R0001. This lease provides 7,360 ABOA / 7,360 RSF of office and clinical space located in Murfreesboro, TN, designated as the 250 Glenis Drive Lease and has been awarded to the incumbent Bass Ward II. The leased premises will continue to be occupied by the Department of Veterans Affairs to provide services to our Veteran beneficiaries in the Murfreesboro area. Lease Term: 10 Years (5 Years Firm; 5 Years Non-Firm) Lease Number: 36C24926L0002 Period of Performance: May 1, 2026 to April 30, 3036

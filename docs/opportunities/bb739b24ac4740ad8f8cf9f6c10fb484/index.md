# Phantom High-Speed Camera

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 12:01:09.183-05
- Solicitation Number: FA461026Q0012

## Summary

This amendment is issued to reopen the solicitation, revise technical submission requirements, and clarify the basis for award. Reopens the solicitation and extends the closing to 11 March 2026 @ 10:00AM. Requires offerors to resubmit or reaffirm their quotes in response to this amendment. Quotes previously submitted will not be considered unless resubmitted/reaffirmed by the revised closing date/time. Revises the Addendum to FAR 52.212-1 Revises the Addendum to FAR 52.212-2 Attch 1_Salient Characteristics has been removed Attach Solicitation Amendment SF50 This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number FA461026Q0012 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the purchase of Phantom high-speed cameras and related accessories. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. Please see the attached SF 1449 solicitation document for contract line-item numbers (CLINs), quantities, units of measure, description of requirements, applicable clauses and provisions, and other information pertinent to this acquisition. Offerors are required to utilize the SF 1449 solicitation document to submit their quote, following the instructions contained therein. The contract will be a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) per the CLIN structure reflected in the attached SF 1449 RFQ. All firms or individuals responding must be registered with the System of Award Management (SAM)(https://www.sam.gov). Lack of registration in the SAM will make an offeror ineligible for contract award. This requirement has a Brand Name Justification for Phantom High-Speed Digital Cameras, see Attch 1_Sole Source Justification. Quotation Due: 11 March 2026 @ 10:00AM PST If you have any questions, please contact: Jessica Rowe, Contracting Specialist, jessica.rowe.5@spaceforce.mil Scott Yeaple, Contracting Officer, scott.yeaple@spaceforce.mil

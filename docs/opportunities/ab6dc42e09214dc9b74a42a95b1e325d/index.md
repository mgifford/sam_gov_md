# Preservation, Hull, Mechanical, and Electrical/Electronic Repairs - VBSS 11MRX1101 and 11MRX1102

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 20:31:38.29-04
- Solicitation Number: N5523626R0006

## Summary

Southwest Regional Maintenance Center (SWRMC) is hereby issuing a Request for Proposal (RFP) No. N5523626R0006 to accomplish the Preservation, Hull, Mechanical, and Electrical/Electronic Repairs for the Visit, Board, Search, Seizure (VBSS) Boats 11MRX1101 and 11MRX1102. The Preservation, Hull, Mechanical, and Electrical/Electronic Repairs for VBSS Boats 11MRX1101 and 11MRX1102 will be from May 22, 2026 through May 20, 2027 (craft delivery date). The repairs shall be performed at a contractors facility within a 125-mile radius of Naval Base San Diego. The acquisition and solicitation-to-award process shall abide by Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR), Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) and the Navy Marine Corps Acquisition Regulation Supplement (NMCARS) guidelines. The Government anticipates awarding this effort as a supply contract whereas the supply equates to the procurement of a repaired, mission-ready VBSS Boats 11MRX1101 and 11MRX1102 to be returned to service to the Fleet. The scope of work includes all labor, supervision, production, testing and quality assurance necessary to prepare for and accomplish the Preservation, Hull, Mechanical, and Electrical/Electronic Repairs. Crafts 11MRX1101 and 11MRX1102 were built by United States Marine Incorporated (USMI). USMI considers construction drawings, referenced by the Statement of Work, to be proprietary. To receive drawings for bidding and repair services, bidding contractors must contact USMI directly to receive permission and establish any USMI non-disclosure agreements. SWRMC is unable to provide any USMI references included in REF 2.10-2.37 for repair and bidding purposes. All west coast area sources must be able to obtain the references and drawings listed in the statement of work from the VBSS boats original equipment manufacturer-(USMI) for bidding and repair services. The successful offeror will be required to have a SWRMC approved Quality System (IAW NAVSEA Standard Item 009-004), approved and in place at time of contract award. Offerors shall be registered in System for Award Management (SAM) in order to be eligible for award. The RFP will be a Total Small Business Set-Aside and the applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code is 336611, Ship Building and Repairing (a small business must have 1,300 or less employees). Award will be based on a best-value procurement utilizing past performance, eligibility, facilities, and price as evaluation factors.

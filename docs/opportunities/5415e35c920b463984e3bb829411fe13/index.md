## Summary

Notice of Intent to Sole Source Requirement Cepheid Reagents & Consumables The Network Contracting Office (NCO) 10, located at 260 E. University Avenue, Cincinnati, OH 45219-2356, intends to solicit and award a firm-fixed-price IDIQ contract with a five (5) year ordering period to one large business, Cepheid. The contract will be used as a procurement vehicle to acquire reagents and consumables for the Cepheid GeneXpert System currently used at the VISN 10, Cincinnati Veterans Affairs Medical Center (CVAMC), Pathology & Laboratory Medicine Service (P&LMS) at 3200 Vine Street, Cincinnati, OH 45220-2213. The analyzer performs Influenza A (Flu A), Influenza B (Flu B), Respiratory Syncytial Virus (RSV), Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 (SARS-COV2), and Group A Streptococcus (Strep A) testing. Only cartridges manufactured by Cepheid will work with this analyzer. It is proprietary in nature, and the configuration is such that only a specific cartridge will fit into the analyzer. The original equipment manufacturer (OEM) for this equipment is Cepheid. Cepheid 904 Caribbean Drive Sunnyvale, CA 94089-1189 The Contractor shall provide the following commercial reagents and consumables: XPRS4PLEX-10 - KIT,IVD,XPRESS,COV-2/FLU/RSV PLUS,10TEST Quantity of 426 Units Each XPRSTREPA-10 - KIT,XPRESS,STREPA,US-IVD,10 TEST- Quantity of 76 Units Each The associated North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this acquisition is 325413 In-Vitro Diagnostic Substance Manufacturing. The NAICS Size is 1,250 Employees. The Product Service Code (PSC) is 6550 In Vitro Diagnostic Substances, Reagents, Test Kits and Sets.

Statutory Authority This acquisition is being conducted under the authority of 41 U.S.C. 3304(a)(1), as implemented by FAR 6.302-1, Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements. Rationale / Justification The VA North Texas Histology section requires an additional IHC stainer to meet current and projected testing volumes and to maintain timely diagnostic services for Veteran patients. The required system must: Be a fully automated, random-access IHC/ISH staining platform capable of individual slide processing without interrupting workflow. Provide continuous loading of reagents, controls, calibrators, and samples without interruption. Include error-proof reagent and commodity design with built-in safeguards to help prevent reagent mix-ups, probe crashes, and unnecessary downtime. Offer modularity to add staining capacity as laboratory needs increase. Allow flexibility to optimize protocols, reagents, and timing to support best practices and varying tissue fixation conditions. Provide an IHC stain menu that includes, at a minimum, tissue staining options for a broad range of disease-related markers using the VENTANA assay portfolio. Be compatible with VENTANA specialized staining products currently used by the Government under existing Roche agreement. Fit within the existing laboratory footprint, with dimensions appropriate for installation in Histology Room 1B-328 at VANTHCS. Ventana Medical Systems, Inc. (Roche Tissue Diagnostics), as part of the Roche Diagnostics Division, designs and manufactures the VENTANA BenchMark ULTRA PLUS tissue staining platform and associated reagents. The BenchMark ULTRA PLUS system is an advanced evolution of the BenchMark series, providing fully automated IHC/ISH slide staining with optimized workflow, integrated touchscreen, and enhanced software and productivity features. It is specifically designed to work with the VENTANA family of tissue-based diagnostic assays, which are already in clinical use at VA and are supported under the existing Roche contract, thereby ensuring reagent compatibility and consistent, validated test performance. Substituting a different IHC platform would require extensive revalidation and correlation studies, adjustments to test protocols, and potential changes in reportable results, resulting in increased cost, extended implementation timelines, and risk of disruption to patient care. Ventana Medical Systems, Inc. (Roche Tissue Diagnostics) is therefore the only responsible source that can provide the VENTANA BenchMark ULTRA PLUS system with full compatibility to the existing VENTANA reagent menu, established workflows, and current Roche contractual framework while meeting all technical and space requirements defined in the SOW. For these reasons, only this source is considered capable of providing the required BenchMark ULTRA PLUS IHC/ISH stainer system in a manner that satisfies the Government s minimum needs without incurring unacceptable schedule, cost, and operational risk.

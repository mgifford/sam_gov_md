## Summary

Rivers Project Office (RPO) Variable Frequency Drives (VFD) Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) Supply and ServiceThis contract will replace an expired Variable Frequency Drives BPA that support Operations mission. This will require 24/7 technical support for the Allen-Bradley and Rockwell Automation VFDs. Many of these components are older and require authorized service centers to complete the work. This contract will be able to provide over the phone support when needed and have approved technicians come to the site for repairs/replacements.

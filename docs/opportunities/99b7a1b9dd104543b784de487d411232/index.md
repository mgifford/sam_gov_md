# ROOF INSPECTIONS AND MAINTENANCE @ WATERVLIET ARSENAL

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 11:51:01.349-04

## Summary

THIS EDIT IS FOR THE ATTACH OF THE FOLLOWING: - 'BID SHEET'; - REVISED 'BUILDINGS' DOC, AND - 'RFI RESPONSES' This solicitation is issued as a total small business set-aside for contractor to provide basic roof inspections and preventative maintenance for various roofing systems at the Watervliet Arsenal for a base plus two option years. See attached Statement of Work. Award will result in a Firm Fixed-Price Contract. Service Contract Labor Standards Wage Determination No. 2015-4143 Latest Revision applies and is incorporated. There will be one (1) site visit hosted for this requirement, on Wednesday 18 March 2026 at 11:00 am. See page 2 of the solicitation document for details.

# Fire Department Emergency Command Vehicle Upfitting

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 09:56:46.132-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8926QA010

## Summary

Nebraska Air National Guard is requesting quotes to fully upfit a new 2025 Ford F-450 Dually into a mission-ready Fire Department Command Vehicle. The project includes installation of multi-color emergency lighting, siren and control systems, custom center console and computer mount, radio and antenna integration, exterior warning lighting, a retractable bed cover, gooseneck hitch, storage solutions, external charging port, reflective fire-department graphics, and other NFPA- and FAA-compliant components. See attached Requirements Description for full details. Basis of award (evaluation factors) will be technical (capability of the products or services offered to meet the agency?s needs), price, and past performance.

# Intent to Sole Source - Autodesk Managed Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 16:51:51.223-04

## Summary

The Service Contract Division, C410, Norfolk, Norfolk Naval Shipyard (NNSY), Portsmouth, VA, 23709-5000 intends to negotiate a Firm Fixed-Price contract and will utilize procedures under FAR Part 8 - Required Sources of Supplies and Services for Autodesk Managed Services and Training as required by the Norfolk Naval Shipyard located in Portsmouth, VA. This notice follows FAR Part 8 ordering procedures under DoW ESI BPA N66001-19-A-0051. The recent FAR RFO is not applicable to this requirement. The NAICS code applicable to this procurement is 541512. The size standard is $34 million. This requirement is sole sourced to DLT Solutions. The solicitation, any documents related to this procurement, and all amendments issued will be in electronic form at www.sam.gov. Potential contractors are hereby notified that the solicitation and subsequent amendments to the solicitation will ONLY be available for download at the above site; this office will no longer issue hard copy solicitations. By submitting an offer, the vendor is self-certifying that neither the requestor nor the principal corporate officials and owners are currently suspended, debarred, or otherwise ineligible to receive contracts from any federal agency. Failure to do so may represent grounds for refusing to accept the offer. By submission of an offer, the contractor acknowledges the requirement that a prospective awardee must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database prior to award, during performance, and through final payment of any contract resulting from this solicitation, except for awards to foreign vendors for work to be performed outside the United States. Lack of registration in the SAM database will make a contractor ineligible for award. Contractors may obtain information on registration and annual confirmation requirements by calling 866-606-8220 or via the Internet at http://www.sam.gov. Please direct all questions regarding this requirement via e-mail to the POC identified in this synopsis.

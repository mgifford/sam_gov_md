# Baptist Camp Bridge Maintenance

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 14:10:14.865-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAX26Q0009

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 127EAX26Q0009 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Baptist Camp Bridge Maintenance This project will consist of repairing wearing surface, applying wood preservative, improving drainage, replacing timber posts, repairing asphalt and replacing object markers on the Baptist Camp Bridge located in the Tonto National Forest in the Payson Ranger District. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 237310 The small business size standard is $45M This acquisition is set aside for SBA Certified Women-Owned Small Businesses (WOSB). All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

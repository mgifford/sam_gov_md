# Broadband Wi-Fi Internet Access for McConnell AFB Unaccompanied Housing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-01 15:38:03.464-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462126Q0012

## Summary

The purpose of this notification is to garner interest in an upcoming requirement at McConnell AFB and to collect interested Vendor Capability Statements. Please review the additional information below and direct any interest or documents to the POCs listed. McConnell AFB appreciates our vendors' interests and time, and thanks you for your submissions. Description of Requirement: Establish Internet service for the Unaccompanied Housing, comprising four buildings with 444 rooms and common areas, using the existing infrastructure wherever possible (such as conduits). McConnell AFB requires download speeds of at least 100 Mbps and upload speeds of at least 20 Mbps or greater. Statement of Work (SoN) / Performance Work Statement (PWS): A SoN will be provided during the official Solicitation at a later date. At this time, McConnell AFB is only generating interest and possible vendors. Required Capabilities: The Vendors must follow industry standards and all federal, state, and local regulations, including regulations outlined in the Telecommunications Act established by the Federal Communications Commission.

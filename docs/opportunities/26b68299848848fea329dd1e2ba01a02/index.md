# FT LEAVENWORTH FIRE ALARMS AND MASS NOTIFICATION UPGRADE (SATOC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 15:42:58.992-04
- Solicitation Number: KP10281

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Kansas City District, intends to solicit and award a contract to replace fire alarms in multiple buildings at Fort Leavenworth, Kansas, in accordance with Department of Defense building standards and regulations. The Government proposes to issue a firm-fixed price request for proposal contract award for this project. This contract would have a base of two buildings and an option for seven more buildings with a square footage (SF) range of 3,165 to 43,056 SF (majority under 25,000 SF).

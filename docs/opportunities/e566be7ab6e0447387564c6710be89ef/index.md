# Lodging & Meals

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 17:36:10.612-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S8-26-Q-1BE5

## Summary

This requirement is to provide food, event space, and lodging for 100 soldiers and their families from the 62D Medical Brigade, stationed at Joint Base Lewis-McChord (JBLM), WA, during the Spiritual Readiness and Resilience Training (SR2T) event. The Government requires the hotel to provide guestrooms, catering, and conference space. The lodging and facilities shall be equipped to handle up to 60 adults and 40 children. All proposals and questions shall be submitted exclusively through the Unison Marketplace portal, accessible via the link provided in the 'Attachments & Links' section. Submissions that do not strictly adhere to the specified instructions will be deemed non-compliant and will not be considered for evaluation.

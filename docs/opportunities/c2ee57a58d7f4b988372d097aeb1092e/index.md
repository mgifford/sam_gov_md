# Subscription Services for the National Library of Medicine

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 13:15:23.64-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N97026R00001

## Summary

The purpose of this solicitation is to acquire subscription services and associated management support for access to biomedical literature published worldwide. This includes content produced by multinational commercial entities, governmental bodies, and non-commercial organizations, available in multiple formats and languages, for use by the National Library of Medicine. Please refer to the attached solicitation and supporting documents for additional details.

## Summary

The Veterans Health Administration, Network Contracting Office 02 intends to solicit from a single source IAW FAR 12.201-1 to obtain services for the ProCare Preventive Maintenance Agreement for Stryker medical beds at the Syracuse VA Medical Center. Anticipated Period of Performance: 5/1/2026 through 4/30/20278 with the provision of four (4) 12-month Options through 4/30/2031. Basic Description of The Requirement: This statement defines the service requirements for the ProCare Preventive Maintenance Agreement at the Syracuse VA Medical Center. The objective is to ensure operational readiness, regulatory compliance, and reliability of Stryker medical beds and surfaces. Place of Performance: Syracuse VA Medical Center 800 Irving Avenue Syracuse, NY 13210 NAICS Sode: 811210 (Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance) NAICS Code Standard Size: $34 Million Intended Source: Stryker Sales, LLC 1941 Stryker Way Portage, MI 49002 THIS IS NOT A NOTICE FOR COMPETITIVE QUOTES; however, any firm that believes it can meet the requirements above, may give written notice prior to the response due date and time. Supporting evidence must be furnished in sufficient detail to demonstrate the ability to perform the requirements. Responses will be evaluated; however, a determination by the Government not to compete the proposed procurement based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. If no responses are received, the Government will proceed with the award. Responses are due on 4/20/2026 at 4:00 PM CST. Responses shall be submitted via email to Contracting Specialist Lee Kern at lee.kern@va.gov. Telephone requests or inquiries will not be accepted.

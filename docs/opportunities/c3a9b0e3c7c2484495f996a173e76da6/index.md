# U.S. Army Uncrewed Aircraft Systems Marketplace Showcase- Amended 26 March 2026

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-26 19:54:37.108-04

## Summary

1. Introduction The U.S. Army's Capability Program Executive, Aviation (CPE, Aviation) in collaboration with the Project Management Office for Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (PM UAS) and Army Contracting Command (ACC), invites industry partners to participate in an upcoming Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS) Marketplace showcase. This event is scheduled for Tuesday, 14 April 2026 at Gaylord Opryland Hotel, 2800 Opryland Drive, Nashville, TN, 37214, Delta Ballroom D, 1330-1530. This event will continue collaboration between the Army and our valued industry partners to advance the development, integration, and sustainment of UAS capabilities. The key focus of this event will be the UAS Marketplace Storefront and a comprehensive update on the Army's UAS portfolio, including future opportunities. The Army is committed to transforming and accelerating how we acquire and field UAS technology. A strong partnership with industry is critical to the UAS Marketplace effort and ensuring Soldiers have the decisive edge on the battlefield. 2. Objectives The primary objectives of this special notice are to: Expand Collaboration: Continue engaging with a diverse group of industry partners, including traditional and non-traditional defense contractors, to explore innovative solutions. Showcase the UAS Marketplace: Continue providing information to industry on how to become part of the UAS Marketplace. 3. Registration Please note that there is a two-step registration process to attend the UAS Marketplace Showcase. Step 1: Register for the Army Aviation Warfighting Summit To ensure compliance with security protocols, all attendees must first be registered for the 2026 Army Aviation Warfighting Summit (15-17 April 2026). Proper badging from the summit is required for entry into the showcase. Summit Registration Link: https://s7.goeshow.com/aaaa/missionsolutions/2026/ Step 2: Register for the UAS Marketplace Showcase After registering for the main summit, please register specifically for the showcase event. Showcase Registration Link: https://www.jotform.com/260495306306051 Registration Deadline: The deadline for showcase registration is 8 April 2026. Due to space limitations, attendance is restricted to three representatives per company. You will need to submit and complete the registration process to attend. 4. Disclaimer This special notice is issued solely for information and planning purposes and does not constitute a solicitation. Responses to this special notice are not offers and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. Respondents are advised that the Government will not pay for any information or administrative costs incurred in response to this special notice. For further information or questions regarding this special notice, please contact: UASMarketplaceShowcase2026@army.mil We look forward to your participation in the U.S. Army UAS Marketplace Showcase and to exploring opportunities for collaboration in advancing UAS capabilities.

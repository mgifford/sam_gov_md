# Grounds Maintenance Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 14:53:01.675-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC26QA034

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) hereby issues this request for quote (RFQ) for Grounds Maintenance Services, in support of Pine Bluff Arsenal (PBA). In accordance with (IAW), Attachment 0001 Performance Work Statement. This award will be a Firm- Fixed-Price (FFP) contract for the purchase of scheduled routine grounds maintenance and special services to be performed at PBA in Pine Bluff, Arkansas. Amendment 0001- Questions and Answers, Updated Attachment 0003-Description of Services.

# U.S. Consulate Merida – Chillers and Water Pumps Annual Preventive Maintenance

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 17:25:58.734-04
- Solicitation Number: 19MX5226Q0009

## Summary

Enclosed is a Request for Quotations (RFQ) for the annual preventive maintenance of air-cooled helical-rotary chillers and chilled-water pumps at the U.S. Consulate General Merida. Please refer to the attached files for detailed information regarding the request, delivery, and payment terms. The U.S. Government intends to award a contract/purchase order to the responsible company submitting an acceptable offer at the lowest price. The award will be based on initial quotations without discussions; however, discussions may be held with companies in the competitive range if necessary.

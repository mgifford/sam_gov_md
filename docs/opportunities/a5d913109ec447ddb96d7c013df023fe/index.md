# SOLICITATION NUMBER 387858 “IMMOBILIZED LOW-ACTIVITY WASTE (ILAW) CONTAINER DESIGN OPTIMIZATION STUDY”

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-02 17:42:32.753-04
- Solicitation Number: SOLICITATION_NUMBER_387858

## Summary

Hanford Tank Waste Operations & Closure, LLC (H2C) requests proposals for Immobilized Low-Activity Waste (ILAW) Container Design Optimization Study. The requested work is in support of H2C’s Prime Contract 89303324DEM000096 with the U.S. Department of Energy, Office of River Protection. Proposals made under this solicitation shall be from small business concerns, including Disadvantaged, Women Owned, Veteran Owned, Service-Disabled Veteran Owned, Non-Alaskan Native American, and HUBZone small businesses. Proposals received from concerns that are not small businesses shall not be considered for this solicitation.

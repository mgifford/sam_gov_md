# Prime Vendor MAC-IDIQ to Provide Material and Logistical Support to the Southwest Regional Maintenance Center (SWRMC) Production Department Product Families

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 11:06:17.827-04
- Solicitation Number: N5523626R0003

## Summary

The Southwest Regional Maintenance Center, San Diego (SWRMC) intends to issue a solicitation for logistical support services to be provided to the SWRMC Production Department Families. The logistical services will provide U.S. Navy ship-related and SWRMC shop-related support to all of the SWRMC Product Family Shops, as well as SWRMC Facilities Division, Dive Locker, and Graving Dock through sourcing shop maintenance, materials, repair parts, and services. The normal place of performance will be at the Contractor?s facility, which shall be located within 15 miles of Naval Base San Diego (NBSD), as well as aboard NBSD for the purpose of deliveries and meetings. In addition, crane services may occasionally be required aboard NBSD, Naval Air Station (NAS) North Island, and Naval Submarine Base Point Loma. The offerors are responsible for the use of the latest revision of all references, specifications, and NAVSEA Standard Items. The successful offerors will be required to provide all labor, services, equipment, and materials in accordance with the specification package. This will be a multiple award indefinite delivery/indefinite quantity, firm-fixed price contract with one (1) base ordering year and four (4) option ordering years. This will be an 8(a) Set-Aside and the applicable NAICS code is 336611, Ship Building and Repairing (the small business size standard is 1,300 in number of employees). Award will be based on a best-value procurement utilizing past performance, technical, and price as evaluation factors. RFO FAR 52.216-27 Single or Multiple Awards (OCT 1995) applies. All responsible sources may submit a proposal, which shall be considered by this agency. Firms that are interested in this procurement may send an e-mail providing all point of contact information to Cindy Wong and Alisha Bloor at 410B_RFP_26R0003_Prime_Vendor@us.navy.mil to receive a courtesy notification when the RFP is posted.

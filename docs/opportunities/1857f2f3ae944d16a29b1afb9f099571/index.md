# Southwest Fisheries Science Center DNA Genotyping Services

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 13:05:55.752-04
- Solicitation Number: 143051-26-0036

## Summary

PRESOLICITATION NOTICE The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) intends to negotiate and award, on a sole source basis, under the authority of 41 U.S.C. 1901 and RFO FAR 12.102(a), a firm fixed price purchase order to the University of Georgia for the purchase of DNA Genotyping services to produce high-resolution Genetic Stock Identification (GSI) of Atlantic green and loggerhead turtles. This contract is expected to have a period of performance of one (1) base year and four (4) option years, with a total period of performance of 2026 - 2031. The purpose of this service is to generate data for a backlog of samples at NOAA’s Southwest Fisheries Science Center (SWFSC) Marine Mammal and Turtle Division (MMTD) in order to achieve current science and management objectives identified under the NMFS protected species Sock Assessment Improvement Plan (SAIP). No set aside is to be used for this sole source requirement. The associated NAICS code is 541380. This presolicitation notice is not a request for competitive proposals and no solicitation documents exist for the requirement. Sources interested in responding to this notice are required to submit a capabilities statement that includes technical data and cost information, sufficient detail, and convincing evidence that clearly demonstrates the capability to meet the requirement. All capabilities statements received by the due date of this notice will be considered by the Government. A request for documentation or additional information or submissions that only ask questions will not be considered as an affirmative response. A determination by the Government not to compete based on responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement or to proceed with a sole source procurement. Capabilities statements are due no later than Wednesday, 8 April 2026, by 1300 Eastern Time. Be advised that all correspondence sent via e-mail shall contain a subject line that reads “143051-26-0036 - SWFSC DNA Genotyping Services”. To ensure your response along with any and all attachments are received, attachment(s) must be less than 20 MB [in total]. It is the responsibility of the offeror to contact the government to confirm receipt prior to the offer due date and time. Capabilities statements shall be submitted by e-mail ONLY as a Microsoft Word or Adobe PDF attachment to the following POCs: Madison Vogeler at madison.vogeler@noaa.gov Aakanksha Bhargava at aakanksha.bhargava@noaa.gov

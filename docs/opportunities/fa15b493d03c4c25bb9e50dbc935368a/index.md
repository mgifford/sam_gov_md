# Isolation Rings for the TR-343 Transducer system

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 15:24:55.919-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016426RGN01

## Summary

NSWC Crane intends to award a sole-source contract to RE:Build CR, LLC, 485 Lakeshore Parkway, Rock Hill, MO 64802, Cage Code 8W9A9 under the authority of 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1) as implemented by FAR 6.103-1, only one responsible source. The estimated maximum dollar value over the 5-year ordering period is $2.5 million. ITEM DESCRIPTION- Naval Surface Warfare Center, Crane Division (NSWC Crane) has a requirement for Isolation Rings. The Isolation Rings are a component of the TR-343 Transducer system and under the cognizance of Naval Surface Warfare Center (NSWC), Crane Division. The TR-343 Transducer is part of the AN/SQS-53C Hull-Mounted Sonar Array, which is a component of the AN/SQQ-89(V) Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) System. The AN/SQQ-89(V) is a fully integrated Surface Ship Undersea Warfare (USW) combat system with the capability to search, detect, classify, localize, and attack submarine targets. Isolation Rings are critical components required to provide the required electrical isolation for the piezoelectric device in the TR-343 Transducer system. Naval Surface Warfare Center, Crane Division (NSWC-CR) requirement is for a 5-year firm fixed price (FFP) indefinite delivery indefinite quantity (IDIQ) contract for two different Isolation rings made from a resin These Isolation rings will be piezoelectric tested before delivery. These rings must be delivered in equal quantities for each of the two isolation rings. The required delivery time is a quantity of 250 each within 3 months of a delivery order issuance and an additional quantity of 250 each every 30 days thereafter. These units will be delivered to NSWC Crane.

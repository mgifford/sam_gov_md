# Protective Security Officer (PSO) Services throughout Utah and Wyoming

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-03 14:07:03.892-04
- Solicitation Number: FY26_0058

## Summary

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS), Office of Procurement Operations (OPO), Federal Protective Service (FPS), Acquisitions Division (AD) intends to extend the ordering period of task order 70RFPW25FRW800006 for up to three (3) months, with Diversified Protection Corporation (109 Rier Landing DR, STE 300, Daniel Island, SC 29492), for Armed Protective Security Officer (PSO) services throughout Utah and Wyoming on a basis other than full and open competition. This proposed extension is to provide for continued protective security officer services in the Utah and Wyoming area to allow time to award and transition the follow-on effort for these services. See redacted JEFO FY26-0058 attached below.

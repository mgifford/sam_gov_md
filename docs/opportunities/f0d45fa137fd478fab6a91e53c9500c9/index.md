# Motorola APX 6000 Series Handheld Radios

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-23 19:56:44.556-04
- Solicitation Number: N4432626PR00008

## Summary

HSM-78 requires reliable and mission-capable radio communications to support safe and effective flight line operations. Current handheld radio equipment used by flight line personnel, maintenance teams, and Squadron Duty Office personnel has reached end-of-life. Replacing these obsolete systems is mission-critical to ensure safe, effective, and interoperable fixed-base communications. Please review the Sources Sought document and the attachements before submitting your capability statements.

## Summary

This acquisition is for golf cart type vehicles rental in the Port Clinton (Camp Perry), Ohio area. Delivery (drop off) date July 6, 2026. Return (pick up) date August 12, 2026. Requirements: The contractor will provide: Quantity and style of Golf Carts 6 each – Box-T 2 passenger with cargo box golf carts 14 each – 6 passenger golf carts Golf Carts will meet the following specifications: • Gas Powered (gasoline engine) • Sun Canopy Top • Fold Down Windshield • Headlights • Taillights Quotes and questions shall be submitted to the Contract Officer, Alan J. Cooper, alan.j.cooper8.civ@army.mil. Questions submitted less than seven days prior to the solicitation closing date risk going unanswered. Please review PWS attachment.

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought notice only. This is not a request for proposals and does not commit the Government to award a contract, now or in the future. We are conducting research to identify interested parties to provide services for a beachhouse grill concessionaire contract at Blakeslee Air Force Base Recreation Area. The opportunity will be for a base year from October 2026 through September 2027, with the possibility of extending for up to four additional years. Please refer to the attachment for further information. Interested parties should email the primary and secondary contacts listed in this posting.

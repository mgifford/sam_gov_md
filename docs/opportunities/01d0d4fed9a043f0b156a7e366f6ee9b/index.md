## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - Tulsa District has a requirement for janitorial and lawn maintenance services at the powerhouse and contiguous exterior areas at Webbers Fall Powerhouse. The only authorized transmission methods for submitting quotes in response to this solicitation is through the PIEE Solicitation Module Website at: https://piee.eb.mil and/or submission through e-mail to the Contract Specialist. No other transmission methods *facsimile, regular mail, hand carried, etc.) will be accepted. Quote submissions shall be uploaded to the PIEE Solicitation Module website AND/OR e-mailed to the Contract Specialist by the identified date and time. Please NOTE this solicitation requires offerors to enter a National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) score in the Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS). The NIST score is a responsibility item. Those without a NIST score in SPRS will be deemed NON-RESPONSIBLE and will not be eligible for award. This is a 100% Total Small Business Set-Aside. NAICS 561720 - Janitorial Services PSC: S201 - Housekeeping- Custodial Janitorial (also includes CLINs for S208 - Housekeeping-Landscaping/Groundskeeping).

# Hotel Lodging BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 17:23:56.623-04
- Solicitation Number: FA489726QC029

## Summary

The 366th Fighter Wing at Mountain Home Air Force Base has a recurring requirement for commercial hotel lodging services to support personnel on temporary duty (TDY), for mission-essential travel, and to accommodate other surge requirements. The requirement is for lodging facilities capable of providing a minimum of 100 rooms, with amenities including high-speed internet, housekeeping, and maintenance that meet the criteria outlined in the Performance Work Statement. This requirement will be structured as a multiple-award Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) to create a simplified and flexible method for acquiring lodging, as the exact dates and number of rooms will vary. Calls placed against the BPA will be on a Firm-Fixed-Price basis. The requirement is commercial in nature as hotel lodging is a service offered to the general public.

# 60 MXS Industrial Laundry service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 17:25:05.294-04
- Solicitation Number: FA442726Q1043

## Summary

04/15/2026 Amendment 01: Attached Attachment 8 Questions and Answers The 60th Maintenance Squadron (MXS) at Travis Air Force Base is seeking proposals for weekly laundry services. The scope of this requirement includes the laundering of seven coveralls and fifty towels each week. The contract will consist of a one-year base period, with the possibility of four additional one-year options and a six-month extension. The selected contractor will be responsible for the weekly pickup and drop-off of all items every Tuesday from two designated locations on Travis AFB. For a complete understanding of this requirement, all interested parties should thoroughly review the solicitation document and its corresponding attachments. Response date 21 April 2026 12:00 PM PST. Offers of offer revisions received after the solicitation close date/time will not be accepted unless requested by the CO. All questions concerning this solicitation must be submitted to the Point of Contacts no later than 09 April 2026 at 4:30 PM PST. Any inquiry submitted past this deadline will not be accepted.

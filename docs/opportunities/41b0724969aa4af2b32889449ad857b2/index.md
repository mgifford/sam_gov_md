# Firing Range Services CBP Miami Area

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-13 07:28:02.142-04
- Solicitation Number: 20157155

## Summary

In accordance with In accordance with FAR 13.106-1(b)(1)(i) and (ii), only one source reasonable available, U.S. Customs and Border Protection, Miami Area intends to negotiate a Sole Source Award with Miami-Dade County Parks and Recreation, 275 NW 2ND ST FL 4 Miami, Florida 33128, for quarterly firing range services for 1,700 officers at the Trail Glades Range. Firing Range shall be located within 20 miles of the U.S. Customs and Border Protection, 6601 NW 25th Street, Miami, FL 33122 via MapQuest and available Monday to Friday, 6:00AM to 12:00PM. Services provided includes the following: Outdoor Firing Range, 48 lanes and up to 25 yards and shall accommodate pistol, shotgun and rifle ammunition. The period of performance for this requirement shall be from April 1, 2026 to March 31, 2027. The NAICS code for this procurement is 713990, small business size standard is $8M. This notice of intent is not a request for quotations. No solicitation will be made available. Interested parties who believe they can meet the requirements must submit information about their capabilities no later than 2:00PM ET, 2/11/2026. Information should be emailed to robert.j.kay@cbp.dhs.gov.

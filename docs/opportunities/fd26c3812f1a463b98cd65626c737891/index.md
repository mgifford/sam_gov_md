# Roman Catholic Priest Services at Naval Air Station Jacksonville, FL

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 14:39:01.713-04
- Solicitation Number: N6883626QS0042

## Summary

Secure the services of a Roman Catholic priest to provide faith-specific religious ministry to authorized Roman Catholic personnel, leadership and oversight to the Roman Catholic portion of the Command Religious Program, and advice to the Command Chaplain. The contractor shall perform services at the Chaplains' office spaces, chapel, and other places as determined by the Command Chaplain at Naval Air Station Jacksonville, FL. See Performance Work Statement in SF1449 for details.

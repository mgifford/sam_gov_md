# HAFB - 75th LRS – Coveralls and Laundering Services BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 11:16:21.027-04
- Solicitation Number: FA820125QC001

## Summary

The contractor shall provide coveralls and all management, tools, supplies, equipment, and labor necessary to perform laundry and/or dry cleaning service for the Vehicle Management Flight at Hill Air Force Base, Utah in accordance with customary commercial practices and this statement of work. Coveralls will be defined in this PWS as a 2-piece pant/shirt combination (short & long sleeves). The contractor is responsible for pickup of soiled coveralls and delivery of clean coveralls. The contractor shall provide clean coveralls for mechanics to the address below on a weekly basis. All dirty coveralls shall be picked up for laundering on a weekly basis. All mechanics must have clean coveralls daily. Billing for coveralls will be based on 4-5 coverall changes per week/per mechanic.

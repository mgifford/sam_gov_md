# Equipment Support Services Naval Base, Guam

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 16:24:55.254-04
- Solicitation Number: N3943026R0001

## Summary

*** AMENDMENT 0003 is hereby posted to provide responses to additional Pre-Proposal Inquiries. - AMENDMENT 0002 Posted 03/09/2026- AMENDMENT 0001 Posted 03/02/2026The Naval Facilities (NAVFAC) Engineering and Expeditionary Warfare Center (EXWC), Port Hueneme, California has issued a Request for Proposal (RFP) under solicitation number N3943026R0001 or a Firm Fixed Price, Single Award, Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ). NAVFAC EXWC has a requirement to support an Equipment Support Services (ESS) Contract that includes an Equipment Maintenance, Repair with Corrosion Prevention and Control (abatement and repair) program. Some of the services include operational repairs, maintenance (preventative and corrective) services, corrective repairs, washing, spot painting, corrosion prevention and repair for all Civil Engineering Support Equipment (CESE), Civil Engineer End Items (CEEI), Material Handling Equipment (MHE), Weight Handling Equipment (WHE), support vehicles, and Containerized Base Camp Facility items located at Naval Base Guam, Guam (NBG), or various locations off NBG, but still on the island of Guam. The closing date for receipt of proposals is 18 March 2026 as indicated on the solicitation. This acquisition is solicited as 100 percent small business set-aside. The Government will only accept offers from small business concerns. NAVFAC EXWC anticipates the award of an IDIQ with base ordering period of sixty (60) months, and an extension of services period not to exceed six (6) months. Individual task orders under this contract will be firm fixed price (FFP). The Government intends to issue a seed task order in place of the minimum guarantee. The seed task order will be issued concurrently with the award of the basic contract. The estimated award date is August 2026. The maximum value of all task orders placed under this contract will not exceed $17,000,000.00. The solicitation and attachments are available to view and/or download online via the Solicitation Module in the Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) at https://piee.eb.mil/, which can be accessed through the links included solicitation notice N3943026R0001. This will be the only method of distribution; therefore, it is the Offerors responsibility to check the solicitation module in PIEE periodically for any amendments to the solicitation. Offerors must be registered in System for Award Management (SAM) at http://www.sam.gov in order to receive an award under this procurement. All questions concerning this solicitation are due by 23 February 2026 and shall be addressed to Jake O. Blas via email at jake.o.blas.civ@us.navy.mil. Secondary Point of Contact is Crystal Brooks at crystal.s.brooks2.civ@us.navy.mil.

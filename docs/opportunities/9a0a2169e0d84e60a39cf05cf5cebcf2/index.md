## Summary

Presolicitation Notice Presolicitation Notice Page 3 of 3 *=Required Field Presolicitation Notice Page 1 of 3 This is not a solicitation announcement. This is a pre-solicitation notice only. The purpose of this notice is to announce that the Department of Veterans Affairs, Veterans Integrated Service Network (VISN 22), is seeking contractors interested in providing a quote for: The Northern Arizona VA Healthcare System (NAVAHCS) is seeking potential qualified contractors for a secure and reliable point-to-point communication circuit with the Arizona Department of Public Safety (AZ DPS) to access the Criminal Justice Information System (CJIS). The services shall comply with this Statement of Work (SOW), all VA, local, state, and Federal policies, and regulations. Solicitation shall be issued on or about April 09, 2026, with proposals due no later than April 24, 2026 at 5:00 P.M. MST. The following NAICS code: 517111 and FSC/PSC code: DG11 apply to this requirement. This will be posted as an unrestricted solicitation. All interested parties must include their SAM UEI and business size status in their response. All interested parties must respond to the solicitation announcement. A response to this notice is not a request to be added to a prospective bidders list or to receive a copy of the solicitation. Telephone responses will not be accepted. Point of Contact for this announcement is Jamie ElBedawi at Jamie.elbedawi@va.gov.

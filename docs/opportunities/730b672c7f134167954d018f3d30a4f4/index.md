# J--Repair of Tractor for Shiprock BOT

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-08 16:41:41.62-04
- Solicitation Number: DOIAFBO260036

## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD A SOLE SOURCE CONTRACT. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITVE PROPOSALS. The Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) intends to enter into a sole source Firm-Fixed Price contract with Watson Tractor and Implement Co. for the repair of a Holland tractor for the chain link fence for BIA, Shiprock Agency, Branch of Transportation, under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b)(1)(i), Contracting officers may solicit from one source if the contracting officer determines that the circumstances of the contract action deem only one source reasonably available (e.g., urgency, exclusive licensing agreements, brand-name or industrial mobilization). No solicitation is available and no technical questions will be answered for this requirement. The NAICS code for this acquisition is 811411. If a firm believes that the BIA should not pursue this procurement, it should submit a capability statement and pricing information for the requirement by 15:00 p.m. MDT on April 8, 2026. Please submit to Lynelle Benallie, Contract Specialist, via email at Lynelle.Benallie@bia.gov.

# AU Rustici License Renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 12:55:13.688-04
- Solicitation Number: F2XHA76083A001

## Summary

Air University (AU) operates a critical eLearning system on the Air Force's secure Cloud One platform, serving over 30,000 students on a 24/7 basis. The core of this system relies on "Engine" and "Dispatch" software. This annual software license renewal and technical support for software is to ensure the continued, secure, and uninterrupted operation of the Air University eLearning system.

# Morganza to the Gulf Hurricane and Storm Damage Reduction Project Sources Sought – Commercial Clay Borrow Source

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-22 07:40:44.537-04
- Solicitation Number: W912P826SS0008

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), New Orleans District is seeking sources for a project entitled Mississippi River and Tributaries, Morganza to the Gulf (MTG) Project located in Terrebonne and Lafourche Parishes, Louisiana. This may be used to establish a pre-approved list of commercial clay borrow sources that could be included in a number of construction contracts associated with construction of the MTG Project. Submit responses to Taylor Brandon at Taylor.H.Brandon@usace.army.mil. Your response to this notice must be received on or before 20 August 2026, 11:00 AM local time. To arrange to submit large files, send an email to Taylor.H.Brandon@usace.army.mil requesting access to DoD SAFE.

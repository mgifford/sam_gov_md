# Aquatic Plant Control - Raystown Lake

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 13:14:59.123-04

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued.Solicitation number W912DR26QA034 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ) for Commercial, non-personal services for aquatic plant control to manage and reduce the presence of an invasive aquatic plant species, Hydrilla verticillata, within designated areas of Raystown Lake.This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov.

# Call for Solution - GEO Antennae Systems (PdM SATCOM)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-18 19:50:49.227-04
- Solicitation Number: CFS-25-GEOANT-01

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command – Aberdeen Proving Ground (ACC-APG), on behalf of the Product Manager (PdM) for Satellite Communications (PdM SATCOM), is issuing this Call for Solutions (CFS) under the Army Open Solicitation (AOS). We are seeking innovative solutions for a Large Geosynchronous Earth Orbit (GEO) terminal capable of transmitting data with High-Throughput, Low-Latency (HT/LL) capacity and speeds. The full CFS document, which outlines the detailed problem statement, required solution attributes, submission requirements, and evaluation phases, can be found in the "Attachments" section of this notice. Key Dates: Questions Due Date: March 25, 2026, at 17:00 Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) Solution Briefs Due Date: April 13, 2026, at 17:00 Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) This CFS is issued under the authority of the Army Open Solicitation (AOS) Number W9128Z-25-S-A002, which can be found here: https://sam.gov/workspace/contract/opp/cbdd2d1829324ebf89f3ce15edb97bb2/view All interested parties are strongly encouraged to review the attachments to both this notice and the parent AOS posting for complete information.

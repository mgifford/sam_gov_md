# ALRE DAQ System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 12:44:05.379-04
- Solicitation Number: N6833526Q1113

## Summary

The Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division (NAWCAD) Lakehurst, NJ anticipates the award of a Firm Fixed-Price contract as a sole source to Bloomy Controls, Inc. (Cage Code: 1ZG80). This effort is to procure an upgrade to the ALRE DAQ System in support of the United States Navy. The upgrade will add new capabilities, including the ability to collect Telemetry over Internet Protocol (TMoIP) airborne data, remove obsolete NI-6071E data acquisition hardware and software, correct existing bugs and issues, and introduce additional features.

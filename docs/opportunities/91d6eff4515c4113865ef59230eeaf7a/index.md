# Genotyping Services

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 12:24:37.278-04
- Solicitation Number: 12639526Q0071

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) Animal and Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS) intends to issue a noncompetitive contract IAW FAR Model Deviation [Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO)] 12.102(a) to NEOGEN CORPORATION, 620 Lesher PL, Lansing, Michigan, 48912 (UEI: M2JMDW88MAQ9) for genotyping services because there is only one responsible source and no other services will satisfy agency requirements. The solicitation will result in a firm fixed-price, indefinite delivery, indefinite quantity, commercial services contract. The period of performance for this contract includes five ordering periods, with each ordering period being twelve (12) months in duration. The anticipated effective date of the contract is March 25, 2026. The solicitation is issued unrestricted. The solicitation package will be issued via email IAW FAR Model Deviation [Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO)] 12.201-1(c)(2). All responsible sources may submit a quotation for consideration. Questions concerning this presolicitation notice should be directed to Amanda Barbosa, Contracting Officer, by email only, at Amanda.Barbosa@usda.gov

# Moving Services IDIQ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-30 19:48:55.372-04
- Solicitation Number: 23-0066

## Summary

Moving will require the disassembly, assembly, repairs, and inventory tracking of furniture from the primary furniture manufacturers AllSteel, Steelcase, and Gunlock brands. There may be other modular systems furniture manufacturer’s products requiring similar services but these would be in limited quantities and isolated locations. Services will also include the movement of all other types of general office furniture, furnishings and equipment including filing safes weighing up to 5,000 lbs. Laboratory and testing equipment of various sizes and weights up to 5,000 lbs. shall also be moved from location to location or moved to a central location for disposal by the Government. All work shall be performed in accordance with the technical specifications described under other paragraphs of this specification.

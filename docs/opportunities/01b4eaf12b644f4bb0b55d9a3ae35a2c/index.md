# UPS HVAC MAINTENANCE SERVICES BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 09:36:37.528-04
- Solicitation Number: FA441726Q0047

## Summary

The purpose of this solicitation is to establish a BPA for HVAC Maintenance Services. The SOW attached defines the requirements for a contractor to provide comprehensive Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) maintenance services. The Contractor shall provide all labor, equipment, materials (except where specifically noted as Government Furnished), supervision, and transportation necessary to perform comprehensive HVAC maintenance services as described in the SOW. Questions are due on 27 Mar 2026 @ 1:30 PM CDT Please note that contractors must be within 200 miles of Hurlburt Field, Florida. See attached documents for the complete details of the requirement.

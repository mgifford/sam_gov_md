# 655-26-201 Repaint Water Tower

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 15:32:57.569-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25026R0062

## Summary

The project’s general scope of work is to furnish all tools, equipment, material, permits, labor, supervision, and all other necessary resources to accomplish the following work: Exterior surface of the water tower is to be thoroughly washed and overcoated, with the VA logo updated to the current design. The interior coating is to be fully blasted and recoated. The interior dry riser will require spot repairs and touchups as necessary. 655-26-201 Repaint Water Towers, Saginaw VA Medical Center, MI.

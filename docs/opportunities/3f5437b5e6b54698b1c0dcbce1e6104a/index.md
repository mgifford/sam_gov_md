## Summary

Grand Teton National Park (GRTE), Taggart Lake Area Renewal - PMIS 314711 Amendment 1 posts a document of questions and answers, updates specifications and drawings to correspond to the Q&A, and extends the offer due date. "B09 140P1426R0003 Amendment 1 Attachment 3 UPDATED DRAWINGS GRTE-314711-Taggart_CDs-Amend1_20260403" REPLACES previous document named "B08 140P1426R0003 Attachment 1 GRTE-314711-Taggart_Final Stamped Construction Documents". "B09 140P1426R0003 Amendment 1 Attachment 2 UPDATED SPECS GRTE-314711_Taggart_Specifications-Amend1_20260403" REPLACES previous document named "B08_140P1426R0003_Attachment_2_GRTE-314711-Taggart_Specifications_stamped". PROJECT DESCRIPTION: The requirement is for construction of Taggart Lake Parking Lot and Trailhead Area and Cottonwood Creek Picnic Area, Grand Teton National Park (GRTE, Park), Teton County, Wyoming. The Park entrance is located on Teton Park Road, northwest of US HWY 89 near Moose, Wyoming. The Taggart Lake Parking Lot and Trailhead Area are accessed by Teton Park Road, approximately 4.8 miles northwest of Moose, WY. The Cottonwood Creek Picnic Area is approximately 5.2 miles northwest of Moose, WY, or 0.4 miles northeast of the Taggart Lake Parking Lot. The work includes construction of a 140-vehicle paved parking lot for Taggart Lake Trailhead. Also included is the extension of a paved multi-use path to the trailhead area. The trailhead includes site furnishings like comfort stations, drinking fountain, three-panel kiosk, masonry seat wall, boulders, and benches. This project will improve well-used areas and increase the parking capacity and accessibility. Specifications and other supporting documents are attached and incorporated herein. Refer to SECTION J. SET-ASIDE TYPE: This procurement is being solicited as a Total Small Business (SB) - Set-Aside. NAICS CODE: 237310, Highway, Street, and Bridge Construction with a small business size standard of $45M. MAGNITUDE: In accordance with FAR 36.204, the project magnitude is estimated to be between $5,000,000 and $10,000,000. DURATION: See FAR 52.211-10. SITE VISIT: See FAR 52.236-27 for additional information. WAGE RATES: Davis Bacon wage rates apply and are included as part of this solicitation package. SYSTEM FOR AWARD MANAGEMENT (SAM): The successful offeror must be registered in SAM and have current online Representations and Certifications. Please access http://www.sam.gov to update your records prior to submitting final offer. INSTRUCTIONS FOR SUBMITTING OFFER: Refer to Section L and M. The contractor agrees that they will follow the plans and specifications provided. Offers must be provided electronically. QUESTIONS: Any questions regarding this solicitation shall be submitted in writing to Molly Lynam, Contract Specialist, at molly_lynam@nps.gov no later than ten (10) calendar days prior to the offer due date.

# Clark AB Terminal Rental

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-26 03:55:52.526-04
- Solicitation Number: FA527026CZ002

## Summary

This requirement is for the rental of Terminal 1 of the Clark International Airport in support of exercise Balikatan. A sources sought notice was posted for this requirement was posted. While one vendor expressed interest, they were unable to secure a rental agreement with the airport for the required facility as it had already been reserved by another party. The airport authority, LiPAD, was contacted to verify that STRA8 Logistics is the only company authorized to contract for Terminal 1 for the required dates; they confirmed that this is the case. This requirement is confirmed as a sole source as only one vendor is capable of fulfilling the required services during the required timeframe.

# MB-2 Vehicle Axle

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 10:42:37.118-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1045

## Summary

Amendment 1 - The purpose of this amendment is to extend the solicitation close out from 18 March 2026 to 01 April 2026 at 2:00 PM CDT. This is an official Request for Quote (RFQ) Notice (FA462526Q1045). The U.S. Air Force 509th Contracting Squadron at Whiteman AFB is posting this solicitation, Request for Quote (RFQ), for purchase of a Wollard MB-2 Aircraft Tow Tractor Rear Steer Axle Assembly that meets the specifications of the attached Salient Characteristics dated 25 September 2025. DESCRIPTION OF REQUIREMENT: A complete NAF Neunkirchener replacement rear steer drive axle Wollard part number 47325 for a 400MB2 model. Included with planetary geared hubs, king pins, proximity switches, steering cylinders and trunnion that fits a model M400. Twenty (20) 22x1.5mm wheel studs to mount wheels on vehicle. One (1) 1/16 trunnion model # M400 to connect the driveshaft to axle. Five (5) wire connectors to operate the Proximity switches. DELIVERABLES: The contractor shall deliver 120 days after receipt of the order. Delivery will be to Whiteman AFB, MO 65305. RESPONSES: Questions are due to both the Contracting Officer: James Bohnenstingel, at james.bohnenstingel@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 2:00 PM CDT, Wednesday, 18 March 2026. Questions received after that time may not be answered. To ensure all questions and responses are received, questions and responses must include the RFQ Number (FA462526Q1045) and title Wollard MB-2 Rear Axle Assembly in the subject line of the email submission and are to be sent via email to both the Contracting Officer: James Bohnenstingel, at james.bohnenstingel@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 2:00 PM CDT, Wednesday, 10 March 2026.

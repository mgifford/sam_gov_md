# Radio Suite Grooming and Preventative Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 18:35:37.181-04
- Solicitation Number: N0040626Q0050

## Summary

NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound intends to solicit and contract a firm fixed price, supply type contract to perform preventative maintenance on the radio suite of a USNS Supply class vessel with an OEM authorized repair facility on a Brand Name basis. Work location will be disclosed at award. See attached solicitation for requirements. Solicitation closes on 15 April 2026 at 10:00 AM PST. The Government anticipates awarding on or about 29 April 2026. All questions regarding this solicitation should be submitted to J. Brian Fergus, james.b.fergus.civ@us.navy.mil. Amendment 0001 changed the due date for quotations and is attached. Please note the requirement for evidence of technical ability as well as showing successful previous completion of similar projects.

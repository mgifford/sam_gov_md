# Maintenance and Repair of Emergency Alarm Systems

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 09:40:18.552-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CM26QA016

## Summary

The Regional Contracting Office at USAG Wiesbaden, Germany, is issuing a Draft Solicitation for a competitive, hybrid Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) and Time & Material (T&M) contract for the procurement of Maintenance and Repair of Emergency Alarm Systems throughout USAG Wiesbaden. The contract will consist of one (1) base year and two (2) 12-month option years. The anticipated performance start date is 31 May 2026. Submission Information: All responses are due: 27 April 2026 at 10:00 AM Central European Time All questions are due: 21 April 2026 at 4:00 PM Central European Time. Questions must be submitted via email to the points of contact listed below A formal solicitation is anticipated to be posted as an Amendment to this Posting on or before 17 April 2026. Important: Any responding party must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at the time of submission. For registrations, visit www.SAM.gov. All dates and times refer to Central European Time. For any inquiries, please contact Sebastian Gotzmer at Sebastian.Gotzmer.LN@Army.mil or Laura Lindsay at laura.k.lindsay6.civ@army.mil.

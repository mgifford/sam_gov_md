## Summary

Gettysburg National Military Park has a requirement for 30 Cubic-Yard Roll-Off Construction Dumpster Services. This requirement provides on-call delivery, placement, rental, haul-off, and disposal of 30-cubic-yard roll-off dumpsters to support construction, preservation, maintenance, and building cleanout activities across park facilities. Services will be provided on an as-needed firm-fixed-unit-price basis under a base year with four option years.

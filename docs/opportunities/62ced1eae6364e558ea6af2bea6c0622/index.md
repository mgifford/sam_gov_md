# Potable Water Testing Services, Buffumville Lake, Charlton, MA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 13:48:33.725-04
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA072

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - New England District is issuing a Solicitation Notice for Potable Water Testing Services, Buffumville Lake, Charlton, MA. THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, and transportation necessary to sample and test potable water at Buffumville Lake Park. All procedures will be in accordance with 310 CMR 22.00: The Massachusetts Drinking Water Regulations. The Contractor shall be the primary communicator between the Government and the Massachusetts Department of Environmental Protection (Mass DEP). The Contractor shall complete and submit all required documents and forms to allow for the public water supply to operate in accordance with the Mass DEP. This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 541380 with a Small Business Size Standard of $19 Million. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

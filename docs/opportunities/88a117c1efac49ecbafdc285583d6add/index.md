# Grounds

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-18 15:08:57.88-04
- Solicitation Number: FA302226Q0310

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide non-personal services, to include all personnel, equipment, tools, supervision, and other items and services necessary to ensure that grounds maintenance is performed at Columbus Air Force Base (CAFB) and Shuqualak Auxiliary Airfield, MS in a manner that will promote the growth of healthy grass, trees, shrubs, and plants and present a clean, neat, and professional appearance. This will be a five (5) year contract a base plus four (4) option years. This is a sources sought posting. No award will be made as a result of this post.

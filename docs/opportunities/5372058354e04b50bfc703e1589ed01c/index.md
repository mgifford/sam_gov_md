- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice

## Summary

The United States Air Force (the Government) intends to issue a Firm-Fixed-Price order to a single source; DDN Federal, LLC., on or about 1 April 2026. The period of performance (PoP) will run from 9 April 2026 to 8 April 2027. PSC: 7A21 NAICS: 541519 Size Standard: $34M Subscription Name: HAP Server Maintenance Supplier Name: DDN Federal, LLC. Product Description: -SEE REDACTED J&A This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quotations.

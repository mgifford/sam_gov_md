## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) awarded a sole source delivery order, N0001926F0221, under the Basic Ordering Agreement N0001921G0006 to The Boeing Company on April 2, 2026. This contract action procured the award of Research, Development, Test, and Evaluation (RDT&E) Phase 1 Structural Repair Manual (SRM) in support of the MQ-25 Stingray Air Vehicle.

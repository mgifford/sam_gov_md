# 2026 IUFRO Membership

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 08:36:10.725-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444026Q0042

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12444026Q0042 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the 2026 International Union of Forest Research Organizations’ (IUFRO) membership. The Government anticipates this being a sole source requirement due to only one organization offering membership into the IUFRO (see attached Justification and Approval for additional information). This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 813920 – Professional Organizations. The small business size standard is $23.5 Million. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. Only authorized membership providers of IUFRO will be considered.

## Summary

Southeastern Power Administration is looking for: Project Title: Conference Room Design and Technology Upgrade – Small and Large Rooms Purpose This Statement of Work (SOW) defines the scope, deliverables, timeline, and responsibilities for the installation of modern AV and conferencing technology in one small and one large-sized conference room. The goal is to enable high-quality collaboration and hybrid meetings through integrated hardware, networked audio, and video systems.

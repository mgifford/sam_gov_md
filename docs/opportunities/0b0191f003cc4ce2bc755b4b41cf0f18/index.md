# Catalog of U.S. Publications System Migration

- Agency: UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT PUBLISHING OFFICE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 14:38:38.66-04
- Solicitation Number: 040ADV-26-R-0025

## Summary

The Library Services & Content Management (LSCM) of the U.S. Government Publishing Office plans to replace its current Integrated Library System (ILS), Aleph, with a new ILS or Library Services Platform (LSP). This process will migrate the current Catalog of U.S. Publications (CGP) data, existing, custom indexes, services, and replace federated search funcionality for federal databases (MetaLib) The cut-off date and time for submitting questions is April 27, 2026 at 10:00 AM EST. A response to all questions received will be provided by an Amendment and will be posted on SAM.gov NLT April 29, 2026.

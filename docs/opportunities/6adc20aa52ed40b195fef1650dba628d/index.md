# RS Means Data Online

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 13:33:46.368-04
- Solicitation Number: W15QKN-26-Q-A075

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command- New Jersey (ACC-NJ) on behalf of the U.S. Army Combat Capabilities Development Command- Armament Center (DEVCOM-AC) intends to issue a Request for Quote (RFQ) for RS Means Data Online. This RFQ is being issued as a sole source requirement to The Gordian Group, Inc. as they are the sole provider. The redacted version of the limited competition justification is attached to this notice. The resultant purchase order shall be awarded as a Firm Fixed Price, commercial order, with a period of performance of a 12-month base period, including four (4) 12-month option periods.The SYNOPSIS DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A RFQ. Further, the synopsis does not guarantee for release of a future RFQ and should not be construed as a commitment of any kind by the U.S. Government. Responses to this synopsis are not considered proposals and/or quotes and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract.

# Kone Traction Elevator Preventive Maintenance Service

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 02:17:16.79-04
- Solicitation Number: 19Z11526Q0009

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy in Harare requires the Contractor to maintain the elevators identified in SOW in safe, reliable and efficient operating condition. The Contractor shall provide all necessary managerial, administrative and direct labor personnel, and as well as all necessary transportation, equipment, tools, repair parts, supplies and materials required to perform inspection, maintenance, repair, and component replacement as required to maintain the elevators in accordance with the manufacturer's specifications. The Embassy intends to conduct a pre-quotation conference at the site. The pre-proposal conference will be held on May 4 at 10.00am (Harare Time) at U.S. Embassy Harare, No 2 Lorraine Drive, Bluffhill. Prospective offerors/quoters should email the attached Pre-Quotation Conference/Site visit Registration Form to HarareGSOProcurement@state.gov by Wednesday April 29, 2026 to arrange entry to the building.

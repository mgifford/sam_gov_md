# Building Automation System (BAS) Installation & Maintenance Bridge

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-08 09:52:03.777-04
- Solicitation Number: 20260320_AASArmy_01

## Summary

This sole source contract was awarded pursuant to the authority of 41 United States Code (U.S.C.) 3304 as implemented by FAR 6.103-1 (GSA Class Deviation RFO-2025-06), on the basis that the proposed contractor is the only source that can provide the required services. In support of the National Defense Mission, DISA utilizes a BAS as one of its tools to successfully operate and report on its facility infrastructure. All facilities covered under this requirement currently have some level of BAS installed. The sophistication of the systems ranges from simple Direct Digital Control (DDC) systems to web-based systems running on virtualized hardware consisting of over 30,000 data points. This requirement is to provide BAS maintenance, installation, and modifications to BAS systems enterprise-wide in accordance with best industry practice and DISA BAS standards and in support of their overarching data centers and operational mission. The BAS systems are to be connected to future infrastructure information technology tools (provided as Government Furnished Equipment (GFE)) to assist DISA in its focus to provide managed, integrated, and reliable tools for data center management. Examples of the tools include Data Center Information Management (DCIM) and Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS). DISA Computing Services data centers provide routine and mission-critical computer processing resources and support to numerous government agencies twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.

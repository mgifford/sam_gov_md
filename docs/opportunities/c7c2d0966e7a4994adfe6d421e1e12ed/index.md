## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in RFO Subpart 12.2 as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. 500W Pulse Wave Laser Cleaning Machine, advanced industrial cleaning, designed for a wide range of applications, it effectively removes rust, paint, coatings, and contaminants across industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction. 500W Pulse Laser water cooled machine. This machine will have a 5m fiber working cable. Machine will operate on 220V. Additional items will be, 50 replacement lenses as well as 2 pair of safety glasses. Included will be Fume Extractor 220V a system that removes harmful particles from the air using a fan and filtration system 220v.

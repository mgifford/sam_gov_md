# Seed Orchard Herbicide Treatment Umpqua National Forest

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 16:52:11.163-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240BE26Q0035

## Summary

The purpose of this solicitation is to procure a contractor to chemically treat vegetation using ground or hand based chemical sprays. Areas to be treated include Seed Tree Orchards on the North Umpqua and Tiller Ranger Districts of the Umpqua National Forest. Please see attachments for details.

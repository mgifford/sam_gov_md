# one Gulmay 320 and two Comet 450 X-ray systems

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 11:56:48.745-04
- Solicitation Number: N4523A26Q1311

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subpart 12.2 as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; proposals are being requested, and a written solicitation will not be issued. This is a non-personal services contract to provide maintenance, troubleshooting and repairs services for Government owned (one Gulmay 320 and two Comet 450) X-ray systems utilized to perform radiography at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard. See attached Performance Work Statement (PWS) for full details of this requirement. This requirement is being competed on an unrestricted (full and open) basis, and the Government intends to award a Firm Fixed Price Commercial Service type contract. The period of performance will be 01 Jun 2026 – 31 May 2027 (base) Please complete pricing on attached combined synopsis solicitation document and submit required documents by 24 Apr 2026 , 9:00 am PST. Questions are due by 17 Apr 2026 at 9:00 am PST.

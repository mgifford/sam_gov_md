# DIVER LIFE SUPPORT SYSTEM MAINTENANCE SERVICES

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-03-19 14:51:47.285-04
- Solicitation Number: N0060426DLSS

## Summary

This Sources Sought/Request for Information (RFI) is strictly for market research purposes and is to identify interested and capable firms. It does NOT constitute formal solicitation, nor does it obligate the Government to any future action or contract award. This RFI is for diver life support systems (DLSS) maintenance support services in accordance with Attachments (2) and (3), in support of Explosive Ordnance Disposal Group One (EODGRU1), located in San Diego, CA. The anticipated period of performance will consist of one (1) 12-month base period, starting on or around 01 June 2026, and one (1) 12-month option period. The currently proposed 2022 NAICS code is 541380 (Testing Laboratories and Services) and the Small Business Standard is $19.0 million. The proposed Product Service Code (PSC) is J042 (Maintenance, Repair, and Rebuilding of Equipment- Fire Fighting, Rescue, and Safety Equipment; Environmental Protection Equipment and Materials), which has an Object Class Code (OCC) of 257 (Operation and Maintenance of Equipment). See attachments for further Sources Sought/RFI details.

## Summary

SPE7L326R0003 will be issued as a firm-fixed Price Request for Proposal (RFP) for NSN 2590-01-693-7794 for a quantity of 2 each and will include a 100% quantity option for an additional 2 each (a total of 4 each). LAKE SHORE SYSTEMS, INC. 34712, a large business, is the sole approved source for this winch parts kits, which has an AMC/AMSC of 3/C. Approved CAGE LAKE SHORE SYSTEMS, INC. 34712 Approved P/N: P/N P012781 Since competition is not anticipated from two small businesses offering the product of a small manufacturer, this solicitation will be issued unrestricted.

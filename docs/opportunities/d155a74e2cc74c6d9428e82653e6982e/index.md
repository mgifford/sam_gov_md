## Summary

This procurement is Fixed-Price with Economic Price Adjustment. The procurement was awareded as part of a Total Small Business Set-Aside. The program was created as a Total Small Business Set-Aside in response to small business being unable to compete under other programs. This program was created to allow small business firms to participate in DLA business for Medical/Surgical products. Through market research and vendor performance in the program, this was verified to be true.

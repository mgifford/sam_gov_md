# First Class Presort Services for IRS National Distribution Center (NDC)

- Agency: TREASURY, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 16:14:15.295-04
- Solicitation Number: 2043FY-26-Q-00012

## Summary

The Wage and Investment (W&I) Division has a requirement for contractor support to process and enter First-Class Mail letters and postcards into the United States Postal Service (USPS) mail stream from the National Distribution Center located in Bloomington, Illinois. The scope of services includes, but is not limited to, mail pick-up, sorting, processing, commingling, and preparation of mail pieces for USPS entry, in accordance with the Statement of Work (SOW).

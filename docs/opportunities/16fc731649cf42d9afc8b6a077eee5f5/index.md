# Cost Estimating Modeling Data Analytics and Research (CEMDAR) RFI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-23 01:31:05.482-04
- Solicitation Number: FA701426IDCEMDAR

## Summary

Air Force Cost Estimating Modeling Data Analytics and Research (CEMDAR) Industry Day The Air Force District of Washington (AFDW) Contracting Directorate in collaboration with the Air Force Cost Analysis Agency (AFCAA) program management team will host a virtual industry day on Thursday 26 March 2026 from 12 pm - 4 pm Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) via Microsoft Teams. The event will discuss the requirements for the anticipated AFCAA contract, act as a means of conducting market research to identify potential sources and provide vendors with an opportunity to engage with the AFCAA program management team. Interested participants are asked to do the following: Review the attached PWS in its entirety to ensure you have an overarching understanding of expectations and needs of this requirement. Prepare any questions, comments or concerns for the industry day. Interested vendors should email their interest to the points of contact noted in this announcement no later than 20 March 2026 at 5 pm EDT. ***Please note the cutoff date has passed. All interested vendors who emailed prior to cutoff will receive a TEAMS invitation for the Industry Day Briefing and for one of the breakout sessions by COB 24 March 2026. No further email requests to attend the briefing will be reviewed.***

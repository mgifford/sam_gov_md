## Summary

This is an Award Notice prepared in accordance with FAR Part 15 Contracting by Negotiation procedures. Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) awarded contract N0001926D0006 to Kratos Unmanned Aerial Systems, Inc. (KUAS), 5381 Raley Boulevard, Sacramento, CA 95838. KUAS will provide test, teardown, and evaluation (TTE) and repair services in support of the BQM-177A Subsonic Aerial Target System, a subsonic recoverable, aerial target system used in weapon systems test and evaluation and Fleet training. This effort was issued as a sole source to KUAS under NAICS Code 336414 (Guided Missile and Space Vehicle Manufacturing) as well as the Product Service Code (PSC) J014 (Maintenance, Repair, and Rebuilding of Equipment- Guided Missiles) which resulted in the placement of a Single Award Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract with five (5) one (1) year ordering periods. The contract consists of cost-plus-fixed-fee (CPFF) CLINs for TTE and Repairs and cost-reimbursable CLINs for Travel. This synopsis is for informational purposes only.

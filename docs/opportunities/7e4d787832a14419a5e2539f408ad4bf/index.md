- Posted: 2026-03-24 00:37:40.162-04

## Summary

Procurement of Japanese Domestic Market (JDM) Residential Appliances--Washers, Dryers, Dryer Stands, Refrigerators, and Microwaves for Naval Air Facility (NAF) Atsugi Family and Single Sailor Program Housing. Please see attached RFQ N6264926QJ007 for required specs and details. ***NOTICE TO QUOTERS*** This is a competitive action restricted to sources duly authorized to operate and do business in JAPAN, in accordance with Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) provision 252.225-7042, Authorization to Perform, listed in this solicitation within DFARS provision 252.204-7998, Alternate A, Annual Representations and Certifications (DEVIATION 2026-O0043)(FEB 2026). Prior to award, quoters may be required to submit additional documentation demonstrating their ability and authority to perform in Japan. This solicitation similarly includes DFARS provision 252.225-7035, Buy American--Free Trade Agreements--Balance of Payments Program Certificate, Alternate I, in said DFARS provision 252.204-7998. In accordance with this provision, the Government will give preference to U.S.-made and qualifying country end products. Only offers of U.S.-made or qualifying country-made items will be considered eligible for award without a price evaluation penalty. Quoters are reminded of the requirements at FAR 52.209-6, Protecting the Government's Interest When Subcontracting with Contractors Debarred, Suspended, or Proposed for Debarment. As with all FAR requirements, please ensure you are familiar with those requirements prior to submitting your quotation. Quoters are required to submit their quotation via E-mail to jon.r.miller17@us.navy.mil no later than 11:00 AM Japan Standard Time, Monday, 6 April 2026. A complete quotation must consist of the following: A signed and completed SF 1449 (Solicitation No. N6264926QJ007), to include: -Page 1 - Blocks 17a, 30a, 30b, and 30c filled out completely. -Pages 3-7 - Unit and total pricing for all line items (CLINs 0001 through 0006). Technical Information: For each line item, provide the manufacturer's name, part number, and official product specification sheet (or a direct link to the manufacturer's website with this information) for the exact model being quoted. This information is mandatory for the quotation to be considered technically acceptable.

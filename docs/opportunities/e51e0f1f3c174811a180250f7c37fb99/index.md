SYNOPSIS: Team Aberdeen Proving Ground (APG) will host the APG Advance Planning Briefing to Industry (APBI) from 21�22 April 2026, at APG, MD. Please note that details, including specific organizational briefing schedules, remain subject to change. The APG APBI is a critical tool for industry stakeholders, offering a glimpse into the organization's future contracting landscape and providing a foundation for strategic planning and partnership. By aligning potential contracting opportunities with the organization's key mission areas and strategic initiatives, the APG APBI supports the development of innovative solutions that address emerging challenges and drive long-term success. Looking ahead to Fiscal Years 2028-2029, the APG APBI will identify and showcase potential contracting opportunities that are closely tied to the organization's roadmap, enabling industry stakeholders to prepare and respond to emerging needs. By fostering collaboration and cooperation between industry and government, the APG APBI will play a vital role in shaping the organization's future and driving progress towards its goals and objectives. The APBI contract slides are anticipated to be posted electronically here in February 2026. The Government will not hear formal bids from industry, as the purpose of the APBI is for the Government to brief upcoming opportunities to industry to help it better understand Army capability requirements and shape its internal development efforts. In addition to the traditional contract opportunity briefings, strategic initiatives and small business highlights, this year�s APBI may include a selection of special interest sessions over at Mallette Training Facility (MTF).

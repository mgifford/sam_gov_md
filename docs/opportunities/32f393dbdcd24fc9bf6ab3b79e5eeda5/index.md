The Department of Veterans Affairs anticipates awarding a one-time, Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) Contract. Scope Summary: The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Police Service, Houston Division, requires a qualified contractor to securely retrieve, transport, and destroy obsolete VA Police firearms. VA policy requires that firearms deemed obsolete, unserviceable, or no longer authorized must be destroyed with documented witness certification and handled through accountable property procedures. The contractor must ensure complete destruction of all components so that no part is retrievable, salvageable, or reusable. Requested Information: 1. Please provide a positive statement of interest in submitting a quote based on the general scope summary above. 2. Please identify your firm s small business category. If your firm is either SDVOSB or VOSB, please state whether you are currently verified as an SDVOSB at sba.gov. 3. Please provide a capability statement. Describe your firm s in-house capability to accomplish the summarized scope/goal. 4. Please state your firm's name, address, SAM UEI and CAGE number, and primary point of contact, including telephone number(s) and email address. 5. If your firm has an active contract for this service, please respond and provide the contract number. A separate Sources Sought Notice will not be posted.

# USDA Forest Service, Alleghany NF, 2026 Tree Planting Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) BPA Call

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-01 17:05:05.299-04
- Solicitation Number: 12445026Q0014

## Summary

The USDA Forest Service (USFS) intends on awarding a competitive BPA Call Order against the LMIR BPA for 12445026Q0014 -2026 Alleghany National Forest Tree Planting. This notice is for a BPA Call against an existing Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) BPA, 1that includes tree planting services within the Alleghany National Forest . The LMIR BPA is a national BPA that consists of a large variety of work including Professional Services, Natural Resources Restoration, Engineering, Project Management, NEPA, Communications, and more. The BPA can be utilized on all Forests and Grasslands and is available for all federal agencies to utilize. This is not a solicitation. This synopsis notification of intent to award a BPA Call order does not constitute a request for quotations or submission of any information in response to this notification. This procurement is restricted to those approved companies eligible for call orders under the LMIR BPA. The LMIR BPA has continuous pre-solicitation sharing so that contractors may submit a proposal to join the BPA at any point in time while the BPA is active. If your firm would like to join, please contact the LMIR BPA Team at SM.FS.lmirbpa@usda.gov. Original documents pertaining to the LMIR Blanket Purchase Agreement can be found in the attachments located at https://sam.gov/opp/1e1dbb485706486c9aba63fab67f33e2/view under "Attachments/Links." Please send any questions to jocelyn.hayes@usda.gov no later than April 14th ,2026 11:59 pm, Eastern Time.

## Summary

Solicitation is being amended to correct the receiving party for bids. 1) This Synopsis/Solicitation Notice is to announce the U.S. Department of Interior (DOI), U.S. Geological Survey has a requirement for BOAT HULLS, MOTORS, & TRAILERS in accordance with the attached solicitation. 2) The synopsis/solicitation number is 140G0226Q0022 and is being issued as a Request for Quote (RFQ). 3) The award for this requirement shall be made using the policies in the Federal Acquisition Regulations (FAR) Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Items, and in conjunction with the policies and procedures for solicitation, evaluation, and award prescribed in FAR

13.5 Simplified Procedures for Certain Commercial Items, as appropriate for this acquisition.

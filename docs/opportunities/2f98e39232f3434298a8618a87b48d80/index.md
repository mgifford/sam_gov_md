# Annual Fire Hydrant Flow Testing

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 12:51:08.584-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C26026Q0474

## Summary

To provide contract services for the annual fire hydrant flow test at the White City VA Medical Center (VA SORCC). This test aims to ensure the proper functioning and compliance of fire hydrant systems in accordance with NFPA standards. The contractor will conduct annual flow tests for all fire hydrants across the facility for the contract period. The contract period shall be a base year and four option years. The facility is required to conduct annual flow tests for fire hydrants to comply with NFPA standards and ensure the operational readiness and safety of the fire protection system. Fire hydrant flow tests will be performed on all hydrants on the property annually (June). The contractor will be responsible for providing the necessary equipment, personnel, and documentation for these tests.

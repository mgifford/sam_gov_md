## Summary

Small Business Innovation Research Program Phase II Action Full and Open Competition after exclusion of sources This effort will modernize the 7,000?pound munitions lift truck by advancing the SuperJammer arm and integrating it onto a prototype SuperJammer vehicle, building on insights from SBIR Phase II contract FA864923P1050. The work includes refining arm compatibility with aircraft loading adapters, incorporating user feedback to optimize system integration, and developing a prototype vehicle for assessment by Air Force stakeholders. The integrated system will undergo demonstration and performance evaluation in a relevant operational environment to validate functionality, confirm load?handling capability, and ensure alignment with mission requirements.

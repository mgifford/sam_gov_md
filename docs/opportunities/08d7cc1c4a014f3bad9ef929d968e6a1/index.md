## Summary

Solicitation: NAFQJ3-26-B-0002. Title: NAF Outdoor Recreation/Equipment Rental Center, Kadena Air Base, Okinawa, Japan. Description: This project is to provide an adequately sized and configured Outdoor Recreation/Equipment Rental Center is required to support the operational functions of the Outdoor Recreational Center, Equipment Rental, and Information Office. The facility will provide classrooms, retail/rental area, office spaces, equipment storage and maintenance, and multi-purpose space.

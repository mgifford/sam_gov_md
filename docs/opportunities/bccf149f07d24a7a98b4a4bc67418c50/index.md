# MA-1 Aviation Breathing Oxygen Regulators

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-22 13:56:30.658-04
- Solicitation Number: FA440726OXYREG

## Summary

The 375 Contracting Squadron seeks to identify interested vendors technically capable of providing MA-1 Oxygen Regulators. The Government seeks to procure twenty (20) MA-1 Regulators from National Stock Number (NSN) 1660-00-782-4697, or a suitable substitute. The regulators connect to portable oxygen canisters (made to NSN 8120-00-515-2575 specifications), allowing for oxygen transfer to the breathing apparatus worn by the crew, particularly on aircraft without independent oxygen systems. Please see attached document for dates and specifications.

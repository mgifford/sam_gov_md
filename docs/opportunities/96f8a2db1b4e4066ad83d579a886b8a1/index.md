# Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) International Acquisition and Engagements.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-30 16:04:38.602-04
- Solicitation Number: 632673681

## Summary

This is a SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE to determine the availability and technical capability of small businesses (including the following subsets: Small Disadvantaged Businesses, Certified 8(a), Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses, HUBZone Small Businesses and Woman-Owned Small Businesses) to provide the required products and/or services. DISA is seeking information from potential sources for: the agency's International Acquisition and Engagements program. The focus for International Acquisition and Engagements program is to provide non-personal services to DISA supporting International Acquisition to include foreign military sales (FMS), engagements, and business process engineering activities, to include international agreements and technology release. Support is needed for the International Acquisition and Engagement office, Program Acquisition Executive (PAE) Offices, Program Management Offices (PMOs), and Resource Managers to fulfill partner nations' acquisition requirements. Please see the attached current requirement draft performance work statement (PWS) for details regarding required services. The anticipated Period of Performance is 18 October 2026 – 17 October 2032. The anticipated Place of Performance is DISA Headquarters, 6910 Cooper Avenue Ft. Meade, MD 20755.

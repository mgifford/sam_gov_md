# SPS Surgical Instrument Sharpening and Repair

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 17:53:25.706-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25726Q0348

## Summary

The objective of this soliciation is to establish a contract with a qualified vendor capable of providing comprehensive instrument maintenance services, including on-site laser sharpening, refurbishment of Reusable Medical Devices (RMD), and off-site repairs for instruments requiring extensive servicing.

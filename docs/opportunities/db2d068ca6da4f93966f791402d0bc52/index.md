# Dorm Internet Services at Whiteman AFB, MO

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-06 09:14:01.814-05
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1038

## Summary

The 509th Contracting Squadron is conducting market research to identify parties having an interest in and the resources to support a requirement for high-speed broadband internet services to a dormitory environment. This project will encompass a minimum of seven (7) facilities and over 700 individual rooms. The provided internet service must meet or exceed the Federal Communications Commission's (FCC) current broadband speed benchmarks. Please see attached Sources Sought document.

# Fabrication and Installation of Exhibit Components and Graphics

- Agency: LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 15:10:07.88-04
- Solicitation Number: 030ADV26R0023

## Summary

In September of 2026, the Library of Congress will open the Alive in Many Hands exhibition, in the SW Curtain Gallery of the Library’s Thomas Jefferson Building, to give annual onsite visitors a better sense of what the Library is, what it collects, and what it preserves, as the largest physical collection of human knowledge in the world. The Library of Congress Exhibits Office (EO) seeks a highly experienced exhibit fabricator production Contractor to provide services needed to fabricate and install exhibit components and graphics.

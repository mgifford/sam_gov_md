# Platoon Level Small Uncrewed Aircraft System (SUAS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 11:35:55.375-04
- Solicitation Number: W58RGZ-26-S-C001-0002

## Summary

ACC-RSA, in conjunction with PM-UAS, is proud to announce the release of the Platoon Level Small Uncrewed Aircraft System (SUAS) Call for Solutions (C4S) under the Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS) Marketplace Commercial Solution Opening (CSO) W58RGZ-26-S-C001. This Call will competitively acquire and/or onboard innovative commercial, Vertical Take-off and Landing (VTOL) Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS) in the Platoon Level SUAS classification. The Government reserves the right to alter or cancel this call at any time. The purpose of this effort is to increase current capabilities in the field, advance the Army’s requirements and accelerate the integration of cutting-edge SUAS technologies while driving cost efficiencies. This effort aligns with the Department of War's (DoW) strategic objective of achieving Drone Dominance. Through this Call, the Army ensures that industry remains informed of its changing needs, fostering ongoing investment and innovation in sUAS technologies to address future opportunities. Please read the Call and the Attachments, in their entirety, for detailed submission information.

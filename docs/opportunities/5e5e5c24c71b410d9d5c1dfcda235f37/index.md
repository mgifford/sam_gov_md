# Supply and Delivery of Vehicle for U.S. Consulate General Barcelona, Spain

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 09:04:13.282-04
- Solicitation Number: 19SP1026Q0001

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. The Consulate General of the United States of America in Barcelona, will be purchasing one passenger vehicle as per attached RFQ.This vehicle will be part of the Consulate's fleet and will be registered with diplomatic plates. Offerors must meet or exceed the following specifications and requirements: Mild - Hybrid automatic transmission system Standard Minimum features for EU Compliance 48V Mild-Hybrid SUV Automatic transmission Fuel: Gasoline Seats: 5 Color: Grey or light blue Engine Torque: 330 Nm Minimum: 200 horsepower Minimum Fuel Capacity: 65 liters Tire pressure monitoring system Climate control Parking Assistant Digital Audio Broadcasting (DAB) + Radio Wireless charging tray Vehicle maintenance service for 3 years

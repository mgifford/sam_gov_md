# Financial Services Electronic Subscription

- Agency: HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-14 09:26:18.155-04
- Solicitation Number: RCS-T-2026-00055

## Summary

Provide financial content covering: financial news and analysis; reviews of domestic and global banks; financial industry data; annual and quarterly regulatory filings; bank performance and peer analytics; earnings reports; and announcements and investor presentations. The contractor also shall provide a U.S. Bank Regulatory Custom Data Feed. This content will be delivered on-line as an electronic financial subscription service.

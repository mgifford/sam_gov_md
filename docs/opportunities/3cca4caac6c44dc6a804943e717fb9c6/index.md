# Basic Military Training (BMT), Dining and Classroom Facility (DCF) 4

- Posted: 2026-04-23 16:23:19.912-04

## Summary

PRESOLICITIATION NOTICE FOR: Basic Military Training (BMT), Dining and Classroom Facility (DCF) 4, Lackland Air Force Base (AFB), San Antonio, Texas This announcement is for an unrestricted, competitive, firm-fixed price, Design-Bid-Build acquisition to construct a BMT, DCF 4 at Lackland AFB, San Antonio, Texas. The solicitation will utilize the Best Value Trade-Off Process (See the Revolutionary Federal Acquisition Regulation Overhaul (RFO) 15.103-1). The project will be executed under a single construction contract. Project Scope: Construct a BMT, DCF, utilizing conventional design and construction methods to accommodate the mission of the facility. The construction includes a multi-story facility consisting of a drilled pier foundation, concrete floor slabs, structural steel frame, masonry walls, standing seam metal roof, and an elevator. Areas include kitchen, dining areas, and multiple classrooms for the residents of two Airmen Training Complexes. The project will include all site improvements, utilities, pavements, communications, and all other work necessary to make complete and useable facilities. Site improvements and environmental remediation will be addressed to ensure removal of asbestos, and any additional work needed to mitigate potential hazard. Facility will be designed as permanent construction in accordance with Department of Defense Unified Facilities Criteria 1-200-01, General Building requirements. The North American Industry Classification System code (NAICS) for this procurement is 236220 which has a small business size standard of $45 Million. The Product Service Code is Y1FD- Construction of Dining Facilities Please note this solicitation requires winning offeror to achieve, at the time of award, a level 1 Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC). Contractors are required to maintain a current (ie., not more than 3 years old) CMMC certificate at the specified level throughout the life of the contract. See DFARS clause 252.204-7021, contractor compliance with the CMMC level requirements. This solicitation requires the offeror to enter a National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) score in the supplier SPRS website: https://sprs.cds.disa.mil This project will be incrementally funded. See DFARS clause 252.232-7007, Limitation of Governments Obligation. The Target Price Range for this project is $195,500,000.00-$236,500,000.00. The solicitation is anticipated to be issued on or about May 2026 with an anticipated award date on or about September 2026. Anticipated project completion date is 800 days from Notice to Proceed. To be eligible for contract award, a firm must be registered with the System for Award Management (SAM} database. Register via the SAM Internet site at https://sam.gov/. No state level certifications will be accepted. After issuance of solicitation, contractors may view and/or download the solicitation and all amendments at https://sam.gov/. The use of the website requires prior registration at https://sam.gov/. The Government reserves the right to use the Internet as the sole means of notification for any amendments to this solicitation. It is the offeror’s responsibility to monitor the https://sam.gov/ website daily for updates. Accordingly, prospective contractors may view and/or download the solicitation and all amendments from the SAM website under solicitation number W9126G26RA028.

# TV Production of Malaysian Football

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 02:13:04.209-04
- Solicitation Number: 19MY3026Q0010

## Summary

The Embassy of the United States of America invites you to submit a quotation for the procurement of TV Production of Malaysian Football Students in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The Embassy intends to award a single purchase order / contract for the required services. Kindly refer to the attached scope of work (SOW) for further details. Your quotation must be submitted electronically to KualaLumpurDTO@state.gov marked “Quotation Enclosed – TV Production of Malaysian Football” to the Contracting Officer, General Services Office, Kuala Lumpur on or before 12:00 noon on May 20, 2026. No quotations will be accepted after that time. For any quotations above $40,000 USD, the Contractor must have valid and active registration in SAM.gov at the time of proposal submission. Direct any questions regarding this solicitation to the Procurement Agent, Prema by email at rmuthupillaip@state.gov during regular business hours.

# 6FL0628 - General Services Administration (GSA) seeks to lease space Warehouse Doral FL six months

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 07:04:06.049-04

## Summary

General Services Administration (GSA) seeks to lease the following space: State: FL City: Miami (Doral) Delineated Area: North: NW 74th St to Hialeah Expressway / State Road 934 East: Florida State Rd 9 (aka NW 27th Ave) South: FL-968 (aka W Flagler St) West: Florida Turnpike Minimum Sq. Ft. (ABOA): 125,000 Maximum Sq. Ft. (ABOA): 130,000 Space Type: 100,000 SF of Warehouse Space + 25,000 SF of finished office space Parking Spaces (Total): 240 Parking Spaces (Surface): 100 Parking Spaces (Structured): 140 (in warehouse) Full Term: 6 months Firm Term: 6 months Option Term: N/A Additional Requirements: Approximately 25,000 ABOA SF of office space and 100,000 ABOA SF of warehouse space that allows for the storage and maneuvering of vehicles Space should be available in existing, finished condition for immediate occupancy. If immediate occupancy is not possible, proposals must clearly identify lead times for delivering finished space. The Government does not anticipate using Tenant Improvements (TI) or Building Specific Amortized Capital (BSAC) for this procurement. The ability to meet all requirements listed in this advertisement and the RLP must therefore be priced into the shell and operating costs. If the market cannot deliver space that fully meets these requirements, the Government reserves the right to identify and consider the closest acceptable alternative. Offered space must meet Government requirements for fire safety, accessibility, and sustainability standards per the terms of the Lease. A fully serviced lease is required. Note: Entities not currently registered in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) are advised to start the registration process as soon as possible. ************************************************************************************ Expressions of Interest Due: April 24, 2026 Market Survey (Estimated): May 7, 2026 Occupancy (Estimated): July 15, 2026

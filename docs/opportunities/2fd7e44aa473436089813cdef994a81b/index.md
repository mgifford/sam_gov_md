## Summary

The James J. Peters (JJP) Veterans Affairs Medical Center (VAMC) Transplant Program relies on external laboratory partnerships to support critical transplant-related diagnostics and logistics. This requirement seeks a contractor that provides Courier Services for Tissue Typing and Immunogenetic Lab Services, Kidney Tissue Biopsies, and specialized medical equipment that ensure liability of organ and tissue transportation. The Contractor must provide timely, secure, and compliant transport of biological materials, organs, blood, and transplant-related equipment. The contractor must be available 24/7/365, this includes weekends and federal holidays. The contractor must also be capable of responding to both scheduled and unscheduled critical requests with no delays.

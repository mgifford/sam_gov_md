## Summary

The FY24 NDAA provided separate authority to sole source specific Energy Resilience and Conservation Investment Program (ERCIP) projects where the utility system has been conveyed under 10 USC Section 2688 and only provides services to a military installation. This notice is for the award for the construction contract of the Fort Hood Network Enterprise Center (NEC) Microgrid.

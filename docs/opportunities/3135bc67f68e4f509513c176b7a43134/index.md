## Summary

DLA Distribution San Joaquin located at Tracy, CA has a requirement to establish a Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) purchase order for annual preventative maintenance, inspection, and repair services for DLA Police's inventory of small arms. See attached solicitation. This RFQ is being solicited as a 100% Small Business Set-Aside. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this project is 811310 and the size standard is $12.5M. The Product Supply Code (PSC) for this acquisition is J010. Please direct any questions and email all quotes to John.Sproul@dla.mil. 1-Award will be made on an 'all or nothing' basis and the base and all option periods must be quoted. 2-Taxes do not apply as DLA is a Federal Agency and is tax exempt. 3-Payment will be made by Wide Area Workflow (WAWF). 4-All quotes shall be all encompassing of all costs for the requested services.

National Cemetery Administration - Water Features Cleaning (Reflection Pond and Frog Pond) - San Joaquin Valley National Cemetery -- Z1NE The San Joaquin Valley National Cemetery is in need of a water feature to be cleaned. The San Joaquin Valley National Cemetery is in need of a water features to be cleaned, that include a Reflection Pond and Frog Pond. This is a one time cleaning that shall be completed before Memorial Weekend. It is intended to be a SDVOSB set aside. Please note: debris and sediment removed from the water feature is not toxic, but is slick and has obnoxious smell. Statement of Work has full details. Interested contractors please submit a Capabilities Statement to Stacey.Lewallen@va.gov NLT 24 March 2026.

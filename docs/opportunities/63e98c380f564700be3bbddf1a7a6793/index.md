This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) and Defense Federal Acquisition Regulations (DFAR) changes to the notification clause. The United States Army Signal Activity (USASA)-Belvoir requires a non-personnel services contract to provide a full-scope, turnkey solution for the replacement, installation, configuration, and commissioning of its existing Physical Access Control System (PACS) and Intrusion Detection System (IDS). The facility requires a solution rated for a Nuclear Command, Control, and Communications (NC3) environment, demanding the highest levels of security, reliability, and compliance. The new integrated system must be fully compliant with all current Department of Defense (DoD), Federal, and Army regulations to protect critical national security assets, personnel, and information.

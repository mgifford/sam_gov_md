# Corrosion Control

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 11:49:21.637-04
- Solicitation Number: 47QFSA26R0003

## Summary

GSA Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS) Air Force Navy Space Force is issuing a Request for Proposal (RFP) 47QFSA26R0003, Corrosion Control. The scope of this requirement is to provide Organizational Level (O-Level) and Intermediate Level (I-Level) Maintenance Support for the Marine Aircraft Group 26 & Marine Aircraft Group 29. This RFP is issued by the GSA, Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS). See attached documents for additional details. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 488190 Other Support Activities for Air Transportation. The Product Service Code (PSC) is J015 Maintenance, Repair, and Rebuilding of Equipment – Aircraft and Airframe Structural Components. This acquisition is an 8(a) Competitive Set-Aside.

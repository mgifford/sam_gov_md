# National Laboratory of the Rockies Solicitation - Noxious Weed and Vegetation Control

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 13:36:44.739-04
- Solicitation Number: RFP-2026029331

## Summary

Subcontractor is responsible for decreasing the overall presence of noxious weeds across the site and managing vegetation around decorative rock areas and fire breaks through the application of pesticides. Subcontractor shall provide recommendations that offer approaches and remedies specific to plant species to the NLR Subject Matter Expert (SME) for review and approval. Please review the attached Statement of Work for additional solicitation requirements.

# 10 Bldg (422 Camera) CCTV System upgrade

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-20 18:24:39.796-04

## Summary

Contractor shall provide all equipment, material, parts and labor necessary to remove and replace Closed Circuit Television (CCTV) Cameras & Systems as required in the Statement of Work (SOW), to include Digital Video Recorders (DVR's), Cameras, Uninterupted Power Supply (UPS), Power over Ethernet (PoE) Switches, and Monitors in 10 Dormitory Buildings.

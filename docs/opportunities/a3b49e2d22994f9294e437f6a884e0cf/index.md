# HUD Technology Transformation Next Generation

- Agency: HOUSING AND URBAN DEVELOPMENT, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 11:43:29.599-04
- Solicitation Number: NOF2026-0005

## Summary

The purpose of thsi amendment is to revise the Sources Sought Notice and change the points of contact. The Department of Housing and Urban Development through the Office of the Chief Information Officer (OCIO) intends to issue a solicitation for Technology Transformation Next Generation (H2TNG) Multiple Award Contract (MAC) Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) vehicle. The H2TNG MAC IDIQ is intended to provide comprehensive IT Support Services and Technology Transformation Next Generation (ITSS and 2TNG) solutions across HUD Headquarters, regional and field offices, data centers, and other designated locations, spanning all OCIO functional areas. Through individually awarded Task Orders (TOs), contractors may be required to deliver enterprise-wide capabilities across the IT lifecycle, including governance, acquisition and financial support, enterprise architecture, systems engineering, cloud and data solutions, cybersecurity, IT operations and maintenance, and end user and infrastructure support. In addition to capability statements being due on Aprl 30, 2026, HUD will be hosting a pre-solicitation conference on April 22, 2026. Pre-registration is required and questions are due by April 16, 2026, not later than 11:00 AM EST. The location of the pre-soliciation will be provded.

## Summary

WE WILL ACCEPT ALTERNATE PRODUCTS IF PROVIDED. FULL DESCRIPTION, INCLUDING SALIENT CHARACTERISTICS, MUST BE PROVIDED. The Defense Health Agency Contracting Activity – Healthcare Contracting Division - Central intends to award a sole source requirement for reagents and consumables on two ImmunoCAP 1000 System Analyzers with service and maintenance provided at Wilford Hall Ambulatory Surgical Center (WHASC) located at Joint Base San Antonio, Texas, to Phadia US, Inc. (Cage Code: 1QNW5 ) located at 4169 Commercial Ave, Portage, MI 49002 under the authority of FAR Part 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 334516, Analytical Laboratory Instrument Manufacturing; the business size standard is 1,000 employees. The Product Service Code (PSC) is 6550 – In Vitro Diagnostic Substances, Reagents, Test Kits and Sets. This notice is NOT a request for competitive quotes. Responders are solely responsible for all expenses incurred associated with responding to this Notice.

## Summary

The USDA Forest Service (FS) Washington Office Law Enforcement Region 13 Subregion 8 has a requirement to secure a qualified vendor who can provide a UTV. All labor, parts and products installed must meet manufacturer’s specifications, this includes all non-OEM aftermarket items which may be necessary to meet specifications. These will be used on all types of terrain in the forest. The asset will be delivered to the location listed on the schedule of items.

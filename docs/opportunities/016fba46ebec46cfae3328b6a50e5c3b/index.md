# Dodd Hall HazMat Survey, F. 448

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-03-31 16:00:00.618-04

## Summary

Amendment 2 to change due date to 7 Apr 26 This is a Request for Quote (RFQ) to award a single firm-fixed-price contract for a comprehensive Hazardous Material (HAZMAT) survey of Dodd Hall, Facility 448, at JBLE-Langley, VA. The purpose of this survey is to determine the scope of asbestos and lead-based paint remediation required prior to the building's demolition. This is a 100% Small Business Set-Aside.

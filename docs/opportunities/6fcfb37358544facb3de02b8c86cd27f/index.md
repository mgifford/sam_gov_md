# Z2AA--Commercial Floor Replacement - Boise (347) RO

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 15:59:44.999-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C10D26Q0077

## Summary

The objective of this contract is to remove approximately 2238 square feet of existing resilient tile squares and replace it with commercial-grade Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) 20mil flooring, including the installation of a 6" cove base and thresholds. The 2000 sq ft includes two offices approximately 10x15 each, requiring carpet tile removal. This procurement is 100% set-aside for Service Disabled Veteran Owned business concerns. See solicitation for requirement details. Please reference Addendum to 52.212-1 Instructions to Offerors to ensure all requirements are met for a complete package.

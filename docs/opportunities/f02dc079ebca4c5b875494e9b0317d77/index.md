## Summary

NOAA Ship Reuben Lasker Non-forecasted Contractual requirements for services and supplies. Delivery of hydraulic oils to the NOAA Ship Reuben Lasker. Hydraulic oils need to be the following brands and types: PANOLIN HLP SYNTH 46 SHELL TELUS S2 V 68 SHELL OMALA S2 GX 220 ATLAS COPCO ROTO XTEND CHEVRON RANDO ISO 46 SHELL OMALA 150 These are the kinds currently used in our HPUs and gearcases, and we need the same oils for compatibility. Hydraulic oil is for required HPU and gearcase life cycle maintenance.

# Munising Falls Trail Rehabilitation

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 14:10:28.998-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P6426B0004

## Summary

SOLICITATION NOTICE: The National Park Service, Interior Regions 3/4/5, Ohio Major Acquisition Buying Office, is issuing an Invitation for Bids (IFB) for the Munising Falls Trail Replacement project for the Pictured Rocks National Lakeshore in Munising, Michigan. Providing all labor, materials, tools and other equipment necessary for renovating an approximately 650 foot long paved trail. The existing trail is asphalt pavement with three wood bridges. The trail traverses a variety of steep and gradual terrain and follows Munising creek terminating at an overlook deck at the base of Munising Falls. The existing trail is approximately 5 ft wide typical and the proposed trail is 6¿ wide typical. Existing bridges and portions of the existing on-grade trail will be replaced with elevated boardwalks. SOLICITATION NUMBER: 140P6426B0004 PROJECT MAGNITUDE: Between $1,000,000 and $5,000,000 TYPE OF CONTRACT: This will be a firm-fixed price contract. Construction Wage Rate Requirements for Alger County, MI will apply. North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) CODE CLASSIFICATION and SET ASIDE: The NAICS Code is 237990, and the size standard is $45 million. This project is set aside 100% for Total Small Businesses. SITE VISIT: See section L of the solicitation The contracting office will not provide hardcopies or email copies of the solicitation. REGISTRATIONS: In order to be awarded a contract, an offeror must have an UEI number. Also, the offeror must be registered and active in the System for Award Management (SAM), website at http://www.sam.gov, and have completed their Online Representations and Certifications at the time of the solicitation close date once the solicitation is posted. POINT OF CONTACT: Christopher Bauer, Contracting Officer, christopher_bauer@ios.doi.gov, 330-468-2500 x5.

# USGS Water Quality Instruments

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:52:06.121-04
- Solicitation Number: 140G0126Q0064

## Summary

This is an announcement directing interested parties to the FedBizOpps (FBO) web page at https://sam.gov/ where details of U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) Request for Quote (RFQ) 140G0126Q0064 may be viewed. This RFQ constitutes the only solicitation. Discounted Open Market Firm-Fixed Price quotation packages are being requested for one (1) water quality sonde and one (1) multiparameter water quality meter equal to or better than the YSI EXO3 water quality sonde and ProDSS multiparameter water meter. The posted RFQ contains a description of the Statement of Work, Instructions to Vendors, and applicable clauses. All vendors who can meet this requirement, have a valid Unique Entity Identifier (UEI) and are registered in SAM are invited to submit electronic quotes for this posted RFQ. All questions shall be submitted to the contract specialist via email at miranda_blankenship@ios.doi.gov, no later than 1:00 PM EST, Friday, March 13, 2026. Quotation Packages are due via email to miranda_blankenship@ios.doi.gov no later than 1:00PM EST, Tuesday, March 17, 2026.

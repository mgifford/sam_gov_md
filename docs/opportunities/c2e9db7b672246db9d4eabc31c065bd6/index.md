# DEA Course in Ubud - Venue & Student Lodging - May 2026

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 05:02:37.136-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15941335

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy Jakarta, invites qualified five-star hotels in Ubud, Bali to submit proposals for comprehensive venue and lodging services for a five-day training course scheduled for May 18-22, 2026. We seek a distinguished property managed by an international hotel chain that meets U.S. Embassy standards of health, safety, and security, located within 30 kilometers of Ngurah Rai International Airport, with spacious outdoor areas suitable for training exercises and no renovation work during the event period. The selected hotel will serve as a critical partner in creating an environment conducive to learning, collaboration, and professional excellence.

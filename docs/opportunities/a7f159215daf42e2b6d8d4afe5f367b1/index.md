# Maintenance and Repair of Emergency Alarm Systems

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 03:18:12.566-04
- Solicitation Number: PAN409-26-P-0000-026595

## Summary

The Regional Contracting Office at USAG Wiesbaden, Germany, is issuing a pre-Solicitation Notice for a competitive, hybrid Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) and Time & Material (T&M) contract for the procurement of Maintenance and Repair of Emergency Alarm Systems throughout USAG Wiesbaden. The contract will consist of one (1) base year and two (2) 12-month options. Proposed performance start date is on or about 31 May 2026. Submission Information: This notice will be posted for two weeks. All responses from interested parties must be submitted by April 2, 2026 at 10:00 am Central European Time A formal solicitation is anticipated to be posted to www.SAM.gov. The attached Draft Performance Work Statement are provided for informational and planning purposes and are subject to change. Important: Any responding party must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at the time of submission. For registrations, visit www. SAM.gov. All dates and times refer to Central European Time. For any inquiries, please contract Sebastian Gotzmer at Sebastian.Gotzmer.LN@Army.mil or Laura Lindsay at laura.k.lindsay6.civ@army.mil.

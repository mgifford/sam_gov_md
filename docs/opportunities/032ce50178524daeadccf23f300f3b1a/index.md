# Tecnau Roll Cart 18"

- Agency: TREASURY, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-08 14:18:21.434-04
- Solicitation Number: TecnauRollCart2026

## Summary

The IRS uses roll carts to provide easy transport of rolls weighing 1,000lbs from a pallet to the printing machine unwinder, eliminating manual lifting. Cart height matches the standard pallet for easy transfer. Rolls never touch the floor, keeping them clean and free from damage during movement. Tecnau 18” Roll Cart specifications: Height: 37 inches (94 cm) Length: 43 1/2 inches (110 cm) Width: 22 inches (56 cm) Cart Weight: 90 lbs. (41 kg) Qty: 75 carts Carts must be able to accommodate rolls that are 17” wide, 50” diameter and weigh up to 1,000lbs. Vendor must be an authorized Tecnau reseller.

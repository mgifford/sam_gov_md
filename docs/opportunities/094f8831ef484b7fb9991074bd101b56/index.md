# 732 AMS Airfield CCTV Camera Upgrades - Amendment 2

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 15:09:01.847-04
- Solicitation Number: FA500026QCMRA

## Summary

Amendment 2 - updates the Solicitation's CLIN structure broken out by building, Attachment 1 Salient Characteristics, and Attachment 3 Pricing Schedule (to reflect the updated CLIN structure). The Solicitation is further updated with the addtions of Attachment 5 (Questions & Answers) and Attachment 6 (As Builts/terminal COM photos). All updates are highlited in the amended COMBO attachment. The 732nd Air Mobility Squadron (AMS) requires a new, commercially available camera system to upgrade its current system. This upgrade will include 22 new cameras, servers, controls, and monitors to be installed in existing locations for monitoring the flightline, various ramps, and the interior of a freight warehouse in accordance with the attached Salient Characteristics and building schematics for Bldgs 15380, 15431, 15510, and 10364. Installation will be performed at JBER, Alaska and must be completed by 01 September 2026.

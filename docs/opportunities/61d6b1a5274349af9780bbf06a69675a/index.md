# FMCS Zoom for Government Combined synopsis solicitation

- Agency: FEDERAL MEDIATION AND CONCILIATION SERVICE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 15:35:23.018-04
- Solicitation Number: 93310026Q0005

## Summary

The Federal Mediation and Conciliation Service (FMCS) requires Zoom for Government (ZfG) licenses to support agency communication and collaboration needs. This is a brand name requirement for Zoom for Government (ZfG). No substitutes will be accepted. The account needs to be reactived the previous account number was 190002476. Vendors must be authorized resellers of Zoom for Government (ZfG) and provide proof of authorization with their quote. The Government intends to award a Firm Fixed Price contract to the lowest price technically acceptable offeror.

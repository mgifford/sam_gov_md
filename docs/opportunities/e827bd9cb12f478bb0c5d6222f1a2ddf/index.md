# Grounds Maintenance Services, Buffumville Lake, Charlton, MA and Hodges Village Dam, Oxford, MA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 14:47:56.501-04
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA071

## Summary

THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, equipment, and transportation necessary to perform grounds maintenance services at both the Buffumville Lake, Charlton, MA and Hodges Village Dam, Oxford, MA project areas. Grounds maintenance services include spring clean-up, spring fertilizer application, and fall clean-up at each project. Additionally, Hodges Village Dam requires annual planting maintenance to include mulch application and weed removal of landscaping beds. Buffumville Lake also requires landscape mowing of the hillside slopes at Pierpont Meadow Pond Dike, which requires specialized slope mowing equipment to mow slopes with an angle of incline up to 40 degrees. This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 561730 with a Small Business Size Standard of $9.5 Million. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

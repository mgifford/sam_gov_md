# Independence National Historical Park- Trees

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 13:40:35.123-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P4226Q0007

## Summary

Independence National Historical Park (INDE)- URBAN TREE PLANTINGS - PARKWIDE The National Park Service at Independence National Historical Park (INDE) has a requirement for a contractor to provide selected tree work, (i.e., tree sourcing, transport, planting, pruning, watering, replacements, stump grinding, and all related maintenance work), in accordance with the specifications, spreadsheet and plans stated herein, which includes one (1) base period and three (3) optional periods. This is a total small business set-aside acquisition. The resulting contract will be firm-fixed price base plus three options. Please send all questions to the contract specialist Ellie Delerme at ellie_delerme-velez@ios.doi.gov no later than April 17, 2026, 2:00 PM EDT.

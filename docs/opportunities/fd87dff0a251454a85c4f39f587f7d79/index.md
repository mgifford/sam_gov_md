# Local Civilian Range Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 13:03:46.198-05
- Solicitation Number: N0018926QL016

## Summary

The objective of this requirement is to establish an Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantify (IDIQ) contract for Local Civilian Range Support Services. EODGRU-2 requires a low to no impact all-inclusive training support solution to include the utilization of shooting and demolition ranges. All training ranges must be located within 50 miles of Joint Expeditionary Base Little Creek/FT Story, Virginia area. This PWS addresses the training range requirements and the contractor shall be required to work closely with designated EOD Group TWO Department personnel in the scheduling of training ranges defined above. The Government intends to award a single award, Indefinte Delivery Indefinite Quantifty (IDIQ) Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract for Local Civilian Range Support Services as a result of this solicitation in accordance with FAR Part 12, FAR Part Subpart 13.5, and FAR Subpart 16.5. The award will be made on a sole source basis to Academi Training Center, LLC. *Amendment 1- Provides clarification to questions asked during the solicitation. The solicitation is extended to 11 March 2026, 12:00PM.

# FY26 Capital Maintenance of Navy Owned Facilities at NIROP

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Strategic Systems Programs has awarded N00026C2038 on a sole source basis to Northrup Grumman Systems Corporation (NGSC), P.O. Box 98, Magna, UT 84044-0098 for the management and performance of the capital maintenance of equipment and facilities at the Naval Industrial Reserve Ordnance Plant (NIROP) Bacchus, located in Magna, UT. Capital maintenance is that work which is in excess of normal maintenance, that is major rehabilitation or repair work, which is not repetitive in nature and has a relatively long life span. Projects completed as part of this effort are limited to non-severable improvements, repair, or modifications of Navy-owned land, buildings, and support utilities. This synopsis is for information purposes only.

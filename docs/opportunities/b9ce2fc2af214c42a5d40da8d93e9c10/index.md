# Notice of Intent to Award Sole Source to Austin Insulators, Inc

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-09 18:24:23.094-04
- Solicitation Number: N66001-26-Q-6104

## Summary

The Department of the Navy, Naval Information Warfare Center Pacific (NIWC PACIFIC) hereby publicizes its intention to solicit, negotiate and award a Firm-Fixed-Price purchase order on a noncompetitive basis with Austin Insulators, Inc (CAGE L0755). This requirement is for the purchase of the following: Qty (2) Full Spare A-9600/USN TLIT Base Housing This notice of intent is NOT a request for quote. A determination by the government not to compete based upon responses to this notice is solely at the discretion of the government. The applicable NAICS Code is 335311 and the business size standard is 800 Employees per year. The point of contact for this announcement is teagan.m.murphy4.civ@us.navy.mil Be advised that the aforementioned information is anticipatory in nature and is NOT binding. This notice is not a request for quotes; however, any firm believing that they can fulfill the requirement may be considered by the agency. Interested parties may identify their interest and capabilities in response to this synopsis. Any comments, questions or concerns regarding this notice may be submitted via e-mail to teagan.m.murphy4.civ@us.navy.mil by 30 April 2025 @ 8:00am PST. Questions by phone or fax will not be considered. To respond to this announcement, you must be registered to the NAVWAR PIEE at https://piee.eb.mil/xhtml/unauth/home/login.xhtml and upload your inquiry under NIWC Pacific/Simplified Acquisition N66001-26-Q-6104. All responding vendors must be registered to the System for Award Management (SAM) website prior to award of contract. Information can be found at www.sam.gov. Complete SAM registration means a registered DUNS and active CAGE Code. Cognizant Contracting Office: NIWC Pacific, 53560 Hull St. San Diego, 92152 Contract Specialist: Teagan Murphy, teagan.m.murphy4.civ@us.navy.mil Contracting Officer: Bohdan Brozino, bohdan.z.brozino.civ@us.navy.mil

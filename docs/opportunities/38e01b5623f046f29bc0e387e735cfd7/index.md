# Modification to Contract ODT-5-C-0010 (Detention Services at Elizabeth CDF)

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-13 10:34:09.385-04
- Solicitation Number: ICE-DCR-2026-ENJ1

## Summary

The Department of Homeland Security (DHS), U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE), Office of Acquisition Management (OAQ), on behalf of the Enforcement and Removal Operations (ERO), New Jersey Field Office intends to modify contract no. ODT-5-C-0010 and extend the period of performance for an additional 2 months. This action would be executed on a sole source basis pursuant to FAR RFO 6.103-1 to the incumbent contractor, CoreCivic, Inc. DBA CoreCivic (physical address: 5501 Virginia Way STE 110, Brentwood, Tennessee, 37027-7684, United States; Unique Entity ID: HJGMJN1JKL46; and Cage Code: 3HAR6). Consistent with Federal procurement law, regulation, and other authority, CoreCivic provides comprehensive detention services for detained aliens at the Elizabeth Contract Detention Center (625 Evans Street, Elizabeth, NJ 07201), a contractor-owned and operated facility, under contract no. ODT-5-C-0010. The current period of performance of this contract will expire on March 31, 2026. This contemplated modification and extension will provide an additional 2 months of services while ICE completes awards under the completed competitive, follow-on procurement. Only the incumbent, CoreCivic, can meet the government’s needs by the required performance start date of April 1, 2026 and no other service or provider will satisfy the agency requirement. This notice is NOT a request for proposals or quotes.

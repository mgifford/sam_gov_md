# MOF Williams River at Tea Creek Bridge Replacement

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-04-22 08:30:01.18-04
- Solicitation Number: 12445526B0008

## Summary

Amendment 0001 - Posted 04/22/2026 Solicitation number 12445526B0008 is issued as an Invitation for Bids (IFB) for construction services for furnishing and installing one two span box beam bridge system on FR 135 Tea Creek Campground over the Williams River on the Monongahela National Forest in Pocahontas County, West Virginia. The work shall include, but not be limited to, construction survey and staking, E&S, clearing and grubbing, installation of cofferdams and dewatering the site, excavation for placement of riprap, removal of existing structure, installation of bridge structure, micro piling design and installation, roadway subbase and aggregate, asphalt cement, guardrail, and riprap armoring. Other construction activities to be expected are turbidity monitoring, in-channel perimeter control, fish relocation, fish removal, candy darter monitoring and capturing, and stream rehabilitation. Contractors are strongly encouraged to inspect the location before bidding. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 237310 - Highway, Street, and Bridge Construction. The small business size standard is $45,000,000.00 This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a bid which will be considered by the agency. Questions shall be submitted via email to Kevin.Brown2@usda.gov and are due no later than May 18, 2026, at 12:00 PM Eastern Time. This will ensure enough time to respond before the solicitation period ends. Please include the solicitation name and number as the subject line of the email. The public bid opening will be held via Microsoft Teams on May 21, 2026 at 1030 am ET. Microsoft Teams link: https://teams.microsoft.com/meet/27896891664507?p=FCsHu79IeV6TkWiZxP

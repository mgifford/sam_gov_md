## Summary

The Bureau of Reclamation, Missouri Basin Region, intends to issue a Commercial Request for Quote (RFQ) for the following supplies: A. Supply and delivery of the following buoy system and associated components: 1. Ten Float Collar Can Buoy with External Ballast. 2. The can diameter and height are 14¿x30¿. The float collar diameter and height are 28¿x12¿. Each buoy is to be a seamless one-piece ultra-strong high-density polyethylene plastic with UV stabilizers. No CAPS, completely foam filled with urethane foam meeting or exceeding U.S. Coast Guard requirements, self-righting without tackle, and impervious to chemicals including gasoline, solvents, or oils. 3. The buoys are to have top graphics that state ¿Boats¿. 4. The buoys are to have a symbol of a diamond with a cross. 5. The buoys are to have a bottom graphics that state ¿Keep Out¿. 6. Four (4) solar lights with kit to mount to top of buoy. 7. Thirty (30) ¿ Screw Pin Anchor Shackle Stainless Steel (T304) 3/8" ¿ Plain. 8.

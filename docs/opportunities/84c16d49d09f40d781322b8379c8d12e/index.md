# Request for Quote (RFQ) - EDCO 30 in Self-Propelled Gasoline Concrete Saw (ED31400)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 16:08:17.92-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U2926

## Summary

Amended to change NAICS EDCO 30 inch Self-Propelled Gasoline Concrete Saw, model ED31400. This is a heavy-duty, walk-behind saw designed for large-scale concrete cutting applications such as road and bridge repair, trenching, and joint sawing. It features a 30-inch blade capacity for a maximum cutting depth of 12 inches. The saw is self-propelled for improved operator control and reduced fatigue, and is powered by a gasoline engine.

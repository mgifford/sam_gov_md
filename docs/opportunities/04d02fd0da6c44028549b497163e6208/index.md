# All-Terrain Vehicles (ATVs) - RFI

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-21 16:19:19.806-04
- Solicitation Number: ATVs_PR20157892

## Summary

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is conducting market research to procure of a total of 285 All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV) units. This is Request for Information (RFI) released pursuant to the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul, Part 10 - Market Research. This RFI's purpose is to obtain market information on viable sources, answers to specific questions, and industry comments relating to the attached full RFI. Interested vendors must be an Authorized Manufacturer Distributor for the subject matter of procurement.

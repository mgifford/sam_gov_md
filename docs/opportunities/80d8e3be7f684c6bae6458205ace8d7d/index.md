## Summary

9 Mar 26 - We have received one question. The question and answer are posted as an attachment labeled, 'Question and Answer'. Thank you. The 97th Contracting Squadron, Altus Air Force Base, Oklahoma, intends to issue a Request for Quote (RFQ) for acquisition of 12 Each, LMC18M87 - Thrust Reverse Plug Set - PW4000 at Altus AFB, Oklahoma. Any questions shall be submitted via email to the below points of contacts by 9 March 2026 at 1100 CST (11:00AM CST). This acquisition will fall under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 336411. This is a 100% Small Business Set Aside. Prospective contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management Database (SAM) prior to submission of quotes. Offerors are further advised that failure to register in the DoD SAM prior to submission of proposal will render your firm ineligible for award. Registration requires applicants to have a UEI number. Recommend registering immediately to be eligible for timely award. Any questions regarding this notice must be submitted in writing via email to SSgt Daric Giles, Contract Administrator, (580) 481-7263, daric.giles@us.af.mil. Alternate contact, Mr. Scott Swain, Contracting Officer, (580) 481-6210, scott.swain.2@us.af.mil. See attachments for full details.

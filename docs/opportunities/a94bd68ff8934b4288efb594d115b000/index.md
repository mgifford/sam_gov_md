# Off-Site Tele-Radiology Services for the Asheville VA Medical Center

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 10:54:04.788-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24626Q0496

## Summary

Services Provided: The Contractor shall provide Board Certified or Board Eligible Radiology Physician Services in accordance with the specifications contained herein to beneficiaries of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) and the Asheville VAMC. The VA Radiology Services follow the standards & guidelines set forth by American College of Radiology (ACR) Practice Parameters and Technical Standards | American College of Radiology (acr.org). An existing National IT Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) must be in place and shall meet or exceed VA and the American College of Radiology Guidelines.

# Internal Vent & Pressure Valve Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 09:54:12.039-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S7F26Q0019

## Summary

The 148th Mission Support Contracting (MSC) Office, intends to issue a sole source contract utilizing FAR part 12.201-1 Simplified Acquisition Procedures to Aero Engineering Support Group (AESG) to perform diagnostics and overhaul or repair the Internal Vent and Pressure Valves for the F-16. The Vendor has an approved Source Approval Request (SAR) by the F-16 System Program Office (SPO). The initial period of performance for this requirement is estimated to be a No Later Delivery Date of July 31, 2026. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quote. All information received by requested date with sufficient technical documentation to meet the requirement will be considered by the 148th MSC. A determination not to compete this proposed contract action based upon response to this notice is solely within the discretion of 148 MSC.

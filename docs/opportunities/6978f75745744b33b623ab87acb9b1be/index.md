# SLICwave software support renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 15:22:09.833-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102826R0015

## Summary

The Government intends to issue a sole source order to Integrated Support Systems for SLICWave Software Support Renewal for the Fleet Readiness Center East. The period of performance is date of award (DOA) through February 02, 2027. The Attachment 1 - Product List and the Redacted Brand Name Class Justification & Approval for Software Support is also posted along with the solicitation. Responsible Sources may submit a completed and signed solicitation and completed product list in response. If you are a reseller, you must also submit a letter or email from the manufacturer confirming your authorized reseller status along with your completed solicitation. Please submit by 11AM CST on March 24, 2026 via email to alexis.n.corley.civ@mail.mil.

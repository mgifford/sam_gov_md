## Summary

PRESOLICITATION NOTICE: This solicitation is being issued to establish an Indefinite Quantity Contract (IQC) that will provide stock Replenishment coverage in support of military depots. The proposed procurement contains an option to extend the contract period one or more times at the discretion of the government. The total duration (1 base year plus 4 option years) and shall not exceed 5 years. This procurement will be solicited as Unrestricted on an Other than Full and Open competition basis in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 2304(c)(1) and FAR 6.302-1(a)(2). Currently Section B will contain one (1) NSN Item managed by DLA Weapon Support. See table below: CLIN 0001 NSN: 6110-01-506-8086 Nomenclature: REGULATOR,VOLTAGE Approved Source: C E NIEHOFF & CO CAGE CODE: 76761 P/N: N3207 Estimated Annual Demand Quantity: 748 EA The solicitation will be available on or about April 27, 2026 at http://dibbs.dscc.dla.mil/rfp. While price may be a significant factor in the evaluation of offers, the final award decision will be based upon a combination of Price, Past Performance, Delivery, and Surge and Sustainment, and other evaluation factors as described in the solicitation. All responsible sources may submit an offer/quote which shall be considered. Drawings/specifications will not be available through BSM DIBBS. This proposed procurement includes a family group of items within the Federal Supply Class 6110. Approved sources are: C E NIEHOFF & CO (CAGE CODE: 76761). TYPE OF SET-ASIDE: NONE

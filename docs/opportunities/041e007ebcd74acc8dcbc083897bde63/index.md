- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Award of a contract modification to increase the estimated value for the non-recurring services portion of Contract N40085-21-D-0002, HVAC Services IDIQ. The non-recurring services portion of the contract will be increased by an estimated $1,500,000.00 for the current year. The proposed action will increase the total contract value by an extimated $1,500,000.00 from from $2,028,825.00 to $3,528,825.00 (estimated)

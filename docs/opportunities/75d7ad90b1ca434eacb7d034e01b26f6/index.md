# MAGIS-100 Infrastructure Modifications

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 09:40:38.758-04
- Solicitation Number: ISD_373244_DA

## Summary

On 4/15/26, Amendment 01 was issued to provide the attendance sheet from the pre-proposal meeting/site visit held on 4/8/26 – see attached files for more information. Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (FNAL) is requesting proposals for the demolition, re-installation, and relocation of ducts and piping to allow future installation of the MAGIS-100 experiement in the MINOS Service Building. The project is located on the Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (Fermilab) Site, adjacent to the city of Batavia, Illinois. The project is in Kane County. Refer to the site map for the exact location of the Minos Building. The Subcontractor shall furnish all required supervision, labor, materials, tools, and equipment necessary to perform diligently and fully all work as described in the drawings and technical specifications. All work includes transportation, overhead, bonding costs, safety oversight, quality control oversight, and supervision as required to construct the project described in the drawings and technical specifications. For more detailed information, please review the attached SOW entitled, “Construction Services for MAGIS-100 Infrastructure Modifications.” Proposals are due 05/06/2026 at 5pm CT.

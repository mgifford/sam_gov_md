# 82 CS Bldg 1121 Floor Furniture

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 16:06:39.244-04
- Solicitation Number: FA302026Q0036

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with RFO 12.201-1. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation, excluding modifications. The solicitation is issued as Request for Quotes (RFQ) FA302026Q0036 to provide matching metro-series office furniture, mobile pedestal files, privacy panels, and room dividers to delivered within 30 days. Offerors may propose products that meet or exceed the functional, performance, and quality characteristics referenced in the specifications atttachment. All items are to delivered within 30 days after contract award to 221 3rd Ave. Bldg 1121, Sheppard AFB, TX 76211. This requirement is a total woman owned small business set-aside under NAICS 337214 (size standard: 1,100 employees). All solicitation documents and amendments are available through this notice, and contractors are strongly encouraged to monitor for updates. The solicitation closes at 12:30 PM CST on Wednesday 22 April, 2026. Quotes must be submitted via email to the Primary Contact listed in the notice. All questions must be submitted in writing by 12:30 PM CST on 17 April, 2026. The 82d Contracting Squadron anticipates awarding a single Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract. The Government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation at any time; in the event of cancellation, the Government is not obligated to reimburse any offeror for costs incurred.

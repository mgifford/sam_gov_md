# MCT-GSO-P & S Waste Management for Warehouse

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-29 07:04:19.086-04
- Solicitation Number: 19MU3026Q0011

## Summary

The purpose of this contract is to provide skip container rental and waste collection services for the U.S. Embassy Warehouse in Ghala. The Contractor shall furnish all labor, equipment, transportation, materials, supervision, licenses, permits, and management necessary to provide a 10 cubic meter (10 m³ / 10MP) skip container and associated waste removal services in accordance with this Statement of Work.

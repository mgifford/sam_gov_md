- Posted: 2026-03-11 06:29:06.434-04

## Summary

Enclosed is a Request for Quotations (RFQ) for Legal Services. If you would like to submit a quotation, follow the instructions in Section 3 of the solicitation, complete the required portions of the attached document, and submit the completed quotation electronically to the email address provided below. The U.S. Government intends to award a Firm Fixed Price Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity Contract (IDIQ) to the responsible company submitting an acceptable offer at the lowest price. We intend to award a contract based on initial quotations, without holding discussions, although we may hold discussions with companies in the competitive range if there is a need to do so. The Embassy does not intend to conduct a pre-quotation conference at the site. Questions are due by March 24, 2026, at 16:00 local time, via rabatgsocontracts@state.gov Quotations are due by April 9, 2026, at 16:00 local time. No quotations will be accepted after this time. Proposals must be in English and incomplete proposals will not be accepted. Your quotation must be submitted electronically to rabatgsocontracts@state.gov and not exceed 30MB.

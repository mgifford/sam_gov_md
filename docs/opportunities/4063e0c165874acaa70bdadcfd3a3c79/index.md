## Summary

This is a notification that an award has been made IAW10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1), Justification for Other than Full and Open Competition. This contract was awarded to US Foods Los Angeles (Cage code 1TZ63), to continue to provide Prime Vendor services to Southern California Land Region and surrounding areas . This contract, SPE300-26-D-3013, will have a period of performance from March 22, 2026 for 322 days, until February 6, 2027, or until the award of a new contract. This is a Justification and Approval (J&A) for full line distribution and is being posted IAW requirements of FAR 6.305. A copy of the J&A is attached to this notice.

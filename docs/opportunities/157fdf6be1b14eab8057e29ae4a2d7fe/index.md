- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-03-24 17:26:52.827-04

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT), Maritime Administration (MARAD), Suisun Bay Reserve Fleet (SBRF), located in Benicia, California, has a requirement for profiling, manifesting, transportation and disposal of hazardous waste and non-hazardous waste generated at the facility. Refer to Sections 10 and 11 of the Combined Synopsis/Solicitation for submittal instructions and evaluation criteria.

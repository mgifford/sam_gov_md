## Summary

Iowa National Guard is conducting market research for portable latrines (14) and hand washing(4) stations near Solon, IA. From 29MAY2026 to 7JUN2026. Review PWS draft for more details. 5.1.1 Provide QTY 3 Portable toilets and QTY 1 hand-washing station delivered NLT 5:00 p.m. on 29 May 2026 near Sutliff Bridge, Solon, Ia. 5.1.2 Provide QTY 7 Portable toilets and QTY 2 hand-washing station delivered NLT 5:00 p.m. on 29 May 2026 near Malinda Reiff Reilly Fen & Prairie Park, Solon, Ia. 5.1.3 Provide QTY 4 Portable toilets and QTY 1 hand-washing station delivered NLT 1700hrs on 29 May 2026 at end of parking lot, North side at Kent Park Youth Group Campsite. 5.1.4 Portable toilets and hand-washing stations must be secure for inclement weather.

5.2 The vendor will provide one cleaning and resupply of each portable latrine and hand-washing station on 1 and 3 June 2026 near Malinda Reiff Reilly Fen & Prairie Park; on 1,3, 5 June 2026 near Sutliff Bridge, Solon, Ia; and on 1, 3, 5 June 2026 near Kent Park Youth Group Camp.

5.3 The vendor will pick-up all latrines and hand-washing stations. 5.3.1 Pick up no earlier than 9:00 a.m. on 4 June 2026 near Malinda Reiff Reilly Fen & Prairie Park. 5.3.2 Pick up no earlier than 9:00 a.m. on 7 June 2026 near Sutliff Bridge, Solon, IA. 5.3.3 Pick up no earlier than 9:00 a.m. on 7 June 2026 near Kent Park Youth group Camp.

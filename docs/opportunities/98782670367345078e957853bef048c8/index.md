# Airshow 2026 Tents, Tables, Chairs Rental

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 10:50:07.258-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4897QC039

## Summary

Mountain Home Air Force Base has a requirement for the rental of tents, tables and chairs. Please see the attached Solicitation, Statement of Work, and Wage Determination. ATTENTION: Please review Amendment #2 to this solicitation, issued by the 366th Contracting Squadron, Mountain Home AFB. This amendment revises the required quantity of 8' x 30" rectangle tables from eighty-seven (87) to one hundred twenty-two (122). The required quantity of chairs has also been increased from five hundred fifty (550) to six hundred twenty (620). Due to these quantity changes, the solicitation deadline has been extended from Thursday, April 2, 2026, to Friday, April 3, 2026. For details, please see the revised documents, "COMBO - Tents Tables Chairs Rental - Amednment 2" and the Performance Work Statement, "PWS - Tents_Tables_Chairs Rental - 12 MAR 26 - Amendment 2," in the attachments. _______________________________________________________________________________________________________ ATTENTION: Please review Amendment #1 to this solicitation, issued by the 366th Contracting Squadron, Mountain Home AFB. This amendment revises the required table size from (10' x 30") to (8' x 30"). As a result, the required quantity of (8' x 30") tables has increased from twelve (12) to eighty-seven (87). For details, please see the revised documents "COMBO - Tents Tables Chairs Rental - Amednment 1" and the Performance Work Statement, "PWS - Tents_Tables_Chairs Rental - 12 MAR 26 - Amendment 1," in the attachments.

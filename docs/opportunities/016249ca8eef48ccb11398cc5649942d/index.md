# Yakima Training Center Grounds Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 18:02:32.004-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S826RA011

## Summary

***SOLICITATION AMENDMENT 0001; 12 March 2026*** See attachment titled "Solicitation Amendment - W911S826RA011-0001," Conformed Solicitation - W911S826RA011", and "Consolidated Questions and Answers". *********************************************************************************************************************************************** This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number W911S8-26-R-A011 is issued as a request for proposal (RFP) to provide comprehensive grounds maintenance at the Yakima Training Center (YTC) in Yakima, Washington. This acquisition is 100% Small Business Set-Aside. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, and supplies necessary to perform grounds maintenance for Improved and Fire Break areas as defined in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). Key services include mowing, trimming, edging, servicing planter beds, seasonal irrigation start-up and winterization, pruning trees and shrubs, removing green debris and leaves, and providing season-long weed and firebreak control.

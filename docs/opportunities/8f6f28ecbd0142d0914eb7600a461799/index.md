## Summary

Firm-Fixed-Price award issued for mastication of multiple acres of brush to make way for reforestation efforts within the coming years. Most of the project area consists of brush that can be mulched by most industrial mowing implements. For the purposes of this contract the terms “mow” and “mastication” are used interchangeably. Brush out Roads and ditch per specifications to improve driving conditions and reach units with ease.

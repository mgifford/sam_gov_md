# Troubleshooting and repair of K400XL test stand– B1600

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 18:24:20.832-04
- Solicitation Number: K400XL

## Summary

Troubleshooting and repair of K400XL test stand– B1600 412th Maintenance Group (412 MXG) The contractor shall provide all supervision, labor, equipment, and materials to perform troubleshooting, test and repair on the K400XL test stand, to include requisite operational checkouts. Equipment that is part of the test stand inventory will be provided by the 412 MXS/MXMAE, Electrical Element, as required. The proposed source, Testek Solutions, is the sole authorized distributor for the required products in the continental United States. Testekhas proprietary control over K400B-XL's integrated system which includes hardware, controlled-access software, and partnership-derived data. The original equipment manufacturer (OEM), Testek Solutions, has confirmed in writing (see attached letter/email) that it does not sell directly and exclusively uses the proposed source for all government sales. Therefore, no other source can provide these supplies.

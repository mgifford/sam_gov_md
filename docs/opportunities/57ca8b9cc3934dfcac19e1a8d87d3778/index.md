# DRAFT RFP - NASA Flown Space Vehicle Multimodal Transportation Multiple Award Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity Contract

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 16:02:14.122-04
- Solicitation Number: 80JSC026R0014

## Summary

This is a Draft RFP for the NASA Flown Space Vehicle (FSV) Multimodal Transportation (MMT) Multiple Award (MAC) Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract. The Government is soliciting feedback from industry partners to refine and clarify the Final RFP. Your comments are requested on the clarity of the tasks, the evaluation criteria, and the completeness of the proposal preparation instructions. This feedback will be instrumental in creating a fair and competitive environment for the final solicitation. This DRFP has a Question and Comment period ending April 9, 2026, and NASA intends to hold a Pre-Solicitation Conference in late April 2026. NASA may hold additional conferences or exchanges depending on industry feedback. This posting will be updated with finalized dates once they have been set.

# Media and Social Media Analysis Support Tool Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 14:26:32.985-04
- Solicitation Number: FA701426R0020

## Summary

The contractor shall provide web?based assessment tools capable of monitoring, aggregating, and analyzing large?scale external media data. The tools shall support analytical reporting and visualizations that provide timely insight into media narratives, trends, and sentiment relevant to SAF/PA mission objectives. The system shall monitor thousands of domestic and international media outlets across print, web, broadcast, and social media platforms.

# JBSA Lackland Remove and Replace Golf Cart Bridge

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 11:14:37.071-04
- Solicitation Number: FA301626Q0043

## Summary

3 April 2026: Solicitation Amendment - FA301626Q00430001 attachment has been amended to reflect updated Request for Quote due date from 9 April 2026 to 14 April 2026 at 10:00am CST. ______________________________________________________________________ This is a Solicitation for the removal and replacement of: Hole 18 Golf Cart Bridge (B490) at Joint Base San Antonio (JBSA) Lackland, in a manner that will provide a structurally sound pre-fabricated bridge to allow the safe conveyance of pedestrians/golf carts. Vendors must refer to the attached Request for Quote (RFQ) and Performance Work Statement for all information, to include special notes and instructions, pertaining to this requirement. Vendors shall respond to this RFQ by reviewing and filling out the attached RFQ Document and submit via email to Miranda Garza; email: miranda.garza.2@us.af.mil and Lauren Macias; email: lauren.macias.1@us.af.mil by the date and time noted within this Notice. Questions/Concerns shall be submitted to the above POC's NO LATER THAN Thursday, 2 April 2026 at 12:00PM CST. Questions and/or concerns submitted after the date and time noted above will not be accepted, nor considered. Please refer to ALL attachments pertaining to this Solicitation Notice. The attached Request for Quote (RFQ) must be filled out and returned. All interested vendors must provide response by the due date and time displayed in this notice.

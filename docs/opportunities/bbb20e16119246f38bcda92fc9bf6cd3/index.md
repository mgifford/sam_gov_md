## Summary

UPDATE 16 APRIL 2026: The B-52 CERP Production Modification Kit & ICS acquisition team is hosting an Industry Days event to provide industry partners with information on the upcoming acquisition. The Government will provide an overview of the program, including technical requirements, solicitation requirements, and the anticipated acquisition schedule. This event is an opportunity for industry to ask questions, provide feedback on the draft Statement of Work (SOW), and for the Government to better understand the current market capabilities. The briefing and all materials presented during this event will be in-person only. When: Tuesday May 12 and Wednesday May 13, 2026 (0800 – 1700 CST) Where: Rose State College 6420 SE 15th Street Midwest City, OK 73110 Building: Student Union 213B Registration: If you are interested in participating in Industry Day, register by navigating the attached link: https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/WSPCBhLhPg Attendance is limited to a maximum of four (4) representatives per company. Due to limited seating capacity, registration is on a first-come, first-served basis. Early registration is strongly encouraged. The registration and question deadline is Monday, May 4, 2026. Failure register by 4 May 2026 at COB will result in incomplete registration and your company will not be able to participate. One-on-One Sessions: The Government will be holding one-on-one sessions with interested vendors. If you are interested in a one-on-one session, please indicate this in your registration. Additional information regarding these opportunities will be posted as subsequent modifications to this notice on www.sam.gov. Note: The Government reserves the right to cancel these Industry Days up to 5 days before the event and will not be subject to reimburse participants for any travel costs. ________________________________________________________________________________________________________ This draft solicitation is being posted in accordance with RFO 15.101(b). When submitting questions, please cc the Contract Specialist, Kacie Cram at kacie.cram@us.af.mil. Please submit any questions NLT COB 30 April, 2026. All questions will be answered here on Sam.gov at a later date.

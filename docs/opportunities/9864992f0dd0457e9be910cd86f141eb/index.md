## Summary

This notice is provided for informational purposes only. The U.S. Department of State, U.S. Embassy Singapore, previously awarded a sole source purchase order for lodging accommodations and workspace in Singapore in support of official U.S. Government personnel during a temporary mission. The period of performance was from 01 March 2026 through 12 March 2026. The award was issued on a sole source basis pursuant to FAR 13.106-1(b)(1) as only one source was reasonably available due to operational and security requirements requiring personnel to be located at a specific facility.

# Linen and Mat Rental and Cleaning Services at the Anchorage and Eielson Commissaries in Alaska

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-22 15:18:10.987-04
- Solicitation Number: HQC00526QE021

## Summary

The Defense Commissary Agency (DeCA) is seeking interested firms that can provided Linen and Mat Rental and Cleaning Services for Anchorage and Eielson Commissaries in Alaska. The Statement of Work (SOW) for this requirement is attached to this Request for Information (RFI). This RFI is for market research purposes only. The anticipated period of performance is October 1, 2026 through September 30, 2031 with four option years. Quoters must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) in order to be considered for award. The NAICS code for this requirement is 812332. The contractor will supply the commissaries with linens/mats/mops and then service them by cleaning them weekly. The contractor will provide a weekly service for pickup and drop off based on the scheduled dates provided by the store. Interested firms are asked to provide a short synopsis of the services you offer to express your interest. Emails must be sent to Fatia Broussard at fatia.broussard@deca.mil.

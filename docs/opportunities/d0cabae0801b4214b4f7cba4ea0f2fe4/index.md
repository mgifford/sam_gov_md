# Airshow Vehicle BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-06 13:30:35.713-05
- Solicitation Number: FA489726QC028

## Summary

The 366th Fighter Wing at Mountain Home Air Force Base requires a multiple-award Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for non-personal, commercial vehicle rental services. The BPA shall provide a variety of vehicle types, including sedans, trucks, SUVs, and vans, to support mission-essential travel and temporary duty assignments for authorized personnel. However this is a sources sought, some information may be subject to change and no award will come at this time. We are currently conducting market research.

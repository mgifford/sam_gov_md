# NCTAMS LANT Telecommunication Support Services- Only Seeking RFP Responses from current Seaport NxG Contract Holders

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 18:25:53.771-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926R3103

## Summary

Note: Only seeking RFP Responses from current Seaport NxG Contract Holders. Naval Computer and Telecommunications Area Master Station Atlantic (NCTAMS LANT) requires telecommunications corrective and preventive maintenance for Navy-owned Avaya and Nortel telephone switching platforms, switch remote units, and adjunct equipment (inclusive of Voice Mail and the Administrative Switch Maintenance Terminal) to NCTAMS LANT and DET Cutler Base Communication Office (BCO) locations during peacetime, exercise, natural disaster, and conflict. This is a follow-on requirement to Seaport Task order N0018921F3024.

# Brand-Name Microwave and Network Test Equipment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:53:53.732-04
- Solicitation Number: W52P1J21C0004P99999

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with FAR Part 12 and the procedures of FAR Part 13 (FAR 13.105). This notice constitutes the only solicitation; a written solicitation will not be issued separately. The Government intends to award a firm-fixed-price contract for brand-name only commercial test equipment from Keysight Technologies, Anritsu, and Fluke Networks. This equipment is required to maintain, repair, and certify communications and navigation systems, including a Nokia Microwave System, in support of the NAVAIDS Division at Fort Rucker, Alabama." Brand Name Only Items (no substitutes): Qty 3 – Keysight Technologies 53230A Frequency Counters (Model 53230A) Qty 3 – Anritsu Site Master Test Sets (Model MS2090A) Qty 3 – Anritsu Site Master Test Sets Cable Sweeper(Model S820E) Qty 1 – Fluke Networks Fiber Optic/Network Test Set (P/N 4982240) See and complete the highlighted sections in Attachment 0001- Price Matrix RFQ - Fort Rucker, which outlines specific required product upgrades/options. Delivery: FOB Destination. Delivery required within 30 days after receipt of order/award. Ship To: Thai Tran Cairns Army Airfield BLDG 30801 Fort Rucker, AL 36362 The Government intends to award a single firm-fixed-price contract to the responsible vendor whose quote, conforming to the solicitation, is most advantageous to the Government. For evaluation purposes, preference will be given to vendors who provide a quote for the total requirement (all line items). However, the Government will consider and may accept partial quotes. The Government reserves the right to make multiple awards if it is determined to be in its best interest. The following factors shall be used to evaluate offers: (i) Price Quoters shall submit pricing (unit and extended), confirmation that the offered items are the brand name products identified above (manufacturer and part number), delivery confirmation, warranty terms, and point of contact information. Offeror must be actively registered in SAM.gov. Questions: Submit in writing to jalen.a.jones11.civ@army.mil and Shelly Hitt, shelly.a.hitt.civ@army.mil no later than 20 April 2026, 1200 CT. Quotes due: 22 April 2026, 1200 CT, via email to Jalen Jones, jalen.a.jones11.civ@army.mil and Shelly Hitt, shelly.a.hitt.civ@army.mil. The solicitation incorporates applicable provisions and clauses in effect through the current FAC and DFARS change notices. Full text may be accessed at https://www.acquisition.gov/.

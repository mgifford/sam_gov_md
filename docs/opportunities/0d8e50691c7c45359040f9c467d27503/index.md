# Maintenance and Repairs on Shredder Plant

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 10:24:32.961-04
- Solicitation Number: W912PB26QA012

## Summary

The U.S. Army Regional Contracting Office (RCO), hereby issues a solicitation to award a commercial, single award, Firm Fixed Price (FFP), Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract for Maintenance and Repairs of Refuse Shredder Plant in support of USAG Bavaria located in Grafenwoehr, Germany. The Period of Performance is expected to start on 01AUG2026 and includes one (1) base period and four (4) option period IAW FAR Clause 52.217-9 for a total of five (5) years. The Product Service Code (PSC) for this requirment is J036 and the North American Classification System (NAICS) code is 811310. Please see attached Solicitation.

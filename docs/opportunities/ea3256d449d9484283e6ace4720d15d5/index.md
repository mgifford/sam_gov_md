# Renovate Base Chapel

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:04:43.037-04
- Solicitation Number: FA489726QSS07

## Summary

This post is for market research purposes only. THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION FOR OFFERS AND NO CONTRACT WILL BE AWARDED FROM THIS SOURCES SOUGHT. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this notice or any follow up information requests. The 366th Contracting Squadron (366 CONS) at Mountain Home Air Force Base (MHAFB), Idaho is gauging the interest of total small business set aside with further potential set aside into socioeconomic categories. Contracting requires the renovation of the Base Chapel to address multiple failing building components. The following work shall be IAW with manufacture instructions in conjunction with the SOW. Installation of a new fire suppression system. Demolish and dispose of Masonry Wall. Provide concrete landing with ABA compliant metal railings. Disposal of entire existing composite roof and replace with new metal roof. Replace various windows, doors, and floor coverings. Room 111, demolish and dispose of all existing steam boiler. New emergency lights throughout facility. Full restroom renovation. All IAW the statement of work (SOW). The magnitude of this project is between $5,000,000 and $10,000,000 In order to reduce chances of missed emails, the Subject line shall be "Renovate Base Chapel, B2606 Sources Sought Response" Each company’s response to this Sources Sought shall be a maximum of 3 pages and shall include the following information: Company name CAGE code Address Point of Contact Phone Number E-mail address Company's Small business category (Small Business, Woman-Owned Small Business, Small Disadvantaged Business [SDB] to include the 8(a) Program, Historically Underutilized Business Zones [HUBZone], Service- Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business [SDVOSB]). A brief statement of the company's interest in bidding on the project as well as details of the company's capability to perform a contract of this type, magnitude, and complexity

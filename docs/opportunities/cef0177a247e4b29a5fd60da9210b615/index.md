# USDA Forest Service Pike Zone Tree Planting Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR)BPA Call

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-10 15:07:56.107-04
- Solicitation Number: 1284LM26Q0036

## Summary

This notice is for a BPA Call Order to be issued against an existing Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA). To respond to this solicitation, contractors must have an existing LMIR BPA that includes Tree Planting Services within Jefferson County, Colorado, Platte Ranger District. Please see the attached BPA Call Order Form for the schedule of items and submittal instructions. Please send any questions to joanne.meiers@usda.gov no later than 3/17/26, COB. NOTE: Original documents pertaining to the LMIR Blanket Purchase Agreement can be found in the attachments located at https://sam.gov/opp/1e1dbb485706486c9aba63fab67f33e2/view under “Attachments/Links.” There is an open and continuous BPA with a ceiling amount of $50M per contractor that allows USDA Forest Service to accept new responses for the life of the BPA (LMIR BPA on SAM.gov). The LMIR BPA has a continuous pre-solicitation sharing that allows contractors to join the BPA at any point in time while the BPA is active. If your firm would like to join, please contact the LMIR BPA Team at SM.FS.lmirbpa@usda.gov.

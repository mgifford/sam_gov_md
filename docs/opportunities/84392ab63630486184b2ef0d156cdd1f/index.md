# CubeTec Inspection Scanner

- Agency: LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-13 14:07:16.385-04

## Summary

This is a public notice prepared in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 5.2 – Synopsis of Proposed Contract Actions. The Library of Congress, Contracts and Grants Directorate, intends to award a sole-source firm fixed price contract to: Cube Tec North America, LLC (192 Edgewood Road, Asheville, NC 28804-3342). The work to be performed under the contract will be performed at the following location: Contractor's facility as well as the Library of Congress facility. The period of performance of the intended contract shall run as follows: 1-year with three 1-year option periods. It is valued at an amount below the simplified acquisition threshold (defined as $250,000 and below), but above $25,000 (the public notice threshold). The work shall consist of the following: The contractor shall provide an inspection scan system with the following additional modules: Quadriga QC View and Dynamic Report Generator. This announcement constitute the only public notice, proposals are not being requested from other parties and a written solicitation will not be issued. This public notice accomplishes the following: Notice of the LC’s intent to sole source this requirement to the business listed herein; Synopsizing the requirements in accordance with the FAR; and Publically posting the approved Justification and Approval (J&A) to the public in advance of the contract award rather than after award. As a result of this, a second posting of the J&A is not required after the award has been made. As a result of this public posting, this shall be the only notice issued. A separate award notice and a separate J&A posting shall not be performed, as all public information is being accomplished via this public notice. The following attachments are included in this notice: Attachment 1: Statement of Work Attachment 2: Sole Source Justification (Redacted for Public Release) No further notices shall be pursued at this time in regards to this contracting action.

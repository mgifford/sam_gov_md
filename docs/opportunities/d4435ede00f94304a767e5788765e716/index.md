# Wood Packaging Components

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 14:55:42.549-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC-26-Q-A101

## Summary

UPDATE: Solicitation W519TC-26-Q-A101 is hereby amended to answer questions from industry, incorporate attachments, and extend the solicitation closing date. Please see the amendment and attachments for more information. Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) has a requirement for Wood Packaging Componenets in support of various programs at Pine Bluff Arsenal. This solicitaiton will result in a Firm Fixed Price Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity contract with a base year plus four ordering periods. This solicitaion is competed as a 100% Small Business Set Aside under NAICS 321920 - Wood Container and Pallet Manufacturing. This solicitation is posted in accordance with FAR part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Products and Services. Award will be made to the most responsive, responsible, and lowest priced offer. Please see the solcitation and corresponding attachments for more information.

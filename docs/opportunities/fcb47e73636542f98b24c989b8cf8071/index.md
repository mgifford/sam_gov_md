# CADIQ Software Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-17 14:57:19.238-04
- Solicitation Number: N68520-26-SIMACQ-JC00000-0122

## Summary

The Commander Fleet Readiness Centers (COMFRC) Procurement Group, Patuxent River, MD 20670 hereby gives notice of their intent to award a purchase order to International TechneGroup Incorporated; 5303 DuPont Circle Millford, Ohio 45150 (Cage Code: 74722) on a sole source basis in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subpart 6.103-1(b), only one source reasonably available and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. Fleet Readiness Center East (FRC East) has a requirement for CADIQ Software Maintenance Subscription in support of 3D model inspection and integrity when converting file types to 3d models. Software Subscription includes license, maintenance, and support. International TechneGroup Incorporated is the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) of CADIQ Software and Maintenance and retains all proprietary rights. International TechneGroup Incorporated does not partner with any resellers for this software support. THIS NOTICE OF INTENT IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITIVE QUOTES. However, all responsible sources may submit a capability statement, which, if received within five (5) calendar days after the date of the publication of this synopsis, will be considered. Information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. A determination by the Government not to compete this requirement based upon responses received as a result of this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Point of contact for this requirement is Jessica Franceschini at jessica.d.franceschini.civ@us.navy.mil

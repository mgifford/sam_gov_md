# RFI - Bloomberg Licenses/Terminals

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 07:52:42.789-04
- Solicitation Number: U-26-0568_CFOAC

## Summary

The Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA) is seeking information from authorized resellers regarding the procurement of Bloomberg licenses and terminals. This Request for Information (RFI) is intended solely for market research purposes and will not result in any award. The objective of this RFI is to gain a better understanding of available solutions, pricing structures, and licensing options from qualified vendors. Responses, including questions and answers submitted during this process, will be used to inform and strengthen the Statement of Work (SOW) for any potential future solicitation. Businesses responding to this RFI should carefully review the attached documents, which provide additional context and specifications relevant to the DIA’s requirements. We encourage all interested authorized resellers to submit their responses and any clarifying questions to support DIA in gathering comprehensive market information.

# Unit Training & Certification (UT&C) in support of the Japan Annual Service Practice (ASP) Custodial Service Contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-01 17:40:39.608-04
- Solicitation Number: PANMCC-26-P-0000-037243

## Summary

THIS IS A Sources Sought Notice ONLY. The U.S. Government desires to procure services for non-personal custodial services to include cleaning, sanitation, and annual waxing/stripping for Fort Bliss, Texas at building 1008, and 1009, concerning (1) base, (2) option years, and a 6 month optional extension as defined in the Performance Work Statement except for those items specified as government furnished property and services on a small business set-aside basis, provided 2 or more qualified small businesses respond to this sources sought notice with information sufficient to support a set-aside. Be advised that the U.S. Government will not be able to set aside this requirement if 2 or more small businesses do not respond with information to support the set-aside. We encourage all small businesses, in all socioeconomic categories (including, 8(a) Business Development Program, Small Disadvantage Business, Historically Underutilized Business Zone, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned, Women-Owned Small Business concerns), to identify their capabilities in meeting the requirement at a fair market price.

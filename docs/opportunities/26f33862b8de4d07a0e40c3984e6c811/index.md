# Patch-Clamp Amplifier System

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 13:48:43.852-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N98026Q00195

## Summary

The National Center for Advancing Translational Sciences (NCATS) is issuing this combined synopsis/solicitation for a commercial item in accordance with FAR Parts 12 and 13. The Government requires one (1) dual-channel patch-clamp amplifier system to support high-resolution electrophysiological recordings of human pluripotent stem cell (hPSC)-derived cell types. The system must meet the minimum salient characteristics outlined in the attached Purchase Description. This requirement is being competed on a brand name or equal basis. Award will be made on a Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) basis. Please refer to the attached Combined Synopsis/Solicitation and Purchase Description for complete details, including submission requirements and evaluation criteria.

# JB CHS Giant Voice System Maintenance and Repair Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-16 15:01:39.797-04
- Solicitation Number: A060290

## Summary

THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION ANNOUNCEMENT. THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT ONLY. THE PURPOSE OF THIS ANNOUNCEMENT IS FOR MARKET RESEARCH PURPOSES ONLY. RESPONSE TO THIS ANNOUNCEMENT WILL BE USED BY THE GOVERNMENT TO MAKE APPROPRIATE ACQUISITION DECISIONS. A SUBSEQUENT SOLICITATION ANNOUNCEMENT MAY FOLLOW. RESPONSES TO THIS ANNOUNCEMENT WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED AS AN ADEQUATE RESPONSE TO ANY FUTURE ANNOUNCEMENT. Joint Base Charleston (JB CHS), located in Charleston, SC seeks a contractor to provide Preventative Maintenance Inspections (PMIs) and emergency/on-call services for an exisiting Giant Voice System. The contractor shall provide all management, tools, supplies, labor, transportation, supervision, and any other items and services necessary to perform two (2) semi-annual Preventative Maintenance Inspections (PMI) on 16 Giant Voice sirens and three (3) control points for the system located across Charleston AFB and Charleston NWS. The contractor shall provide SiRcom certified technicians. Interested parties should provide the following information: Company Name, Company Address, Point of Contact, Phone Number, Email Address, and CAGE Code. Capability Statement and/or information you wish to provide about your business. Your small business size representation for NAICS 811210 - Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance (e.g., is your business small or large? If small, is your business VOSB, SDVOSB, WOSB, EDWOSB, HUBZone, 8(a), etc.?). The Size Standard for NAICS 811210 is $34M. Do you believe this acquisition should be assigned a different NAICS other than 811210 - Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance? If so, which one? Do you believe this acquisition should be assigned a different PSC other than J063 - Maint/Repair/Rebuild of Equipment—Alarm, Signal, and Security Detection Systems? If so, which one? The government is seeking responses from all qualified sources. Those wishing to identify their capability to provide the aforementioned services to any or all of the required locations should respond to this announcement via email to madison.nerenberg@us.af.mil and taylor.mcdaniel.1@us.af.mil no later than Monday, 23 March 2026 at 1400 PM EST. THIS REQUEST FOR INFORMATION OR "SOURCES SOUGHT" SHALL NOT BE CONSTRUED AS A REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL. INFORMATION IS TO BE PROVIDED ON A VOLUNTARY BASIS. NO ENTITLEMENT TO PAYMENT OF DIRECT OR INDIRECT COSTS OR CHARGES INCLUDING POSTAGE AND SHIPPING WILL ARISE AS A RESULT OF THE SUBMISSION OF INFORMATION.

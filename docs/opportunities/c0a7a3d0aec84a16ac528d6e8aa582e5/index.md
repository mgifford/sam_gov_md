# B26 Hot Water Recirculation

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 18:14:07.802-04
- Solicitation Number: 1333ND26RNB190010

## Summary

The National Institute for Standards and Technology (NIST), Boulder Campus, is seeking a contractor to provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision necessary to install a hot water recirculation line and two associated recirculation pumps in Building 26 (Daycare Center). Work includes mechanical piping modifications, electrical connections to existing panelboards with new breakers and disconnects as required, and complaince with current NEC and NIST site standards. Due to the facility's daycare operations, work will require close coordination and may include off-hour activities. The project will also include an optional line item for furnishing and installing a new water heater associated with piping in Building 111. Important Dates: List of Individuals due for Site Visit: Friday, April 24, 2026 Site Visit: Tuesday, April 28, 2026 at 9:30am at Building 26 Questions Due: Thursday, May 7, 2026 Proposals Due: Thursday, May 14, 2026

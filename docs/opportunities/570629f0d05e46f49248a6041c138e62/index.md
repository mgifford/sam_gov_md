## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE: The Department of Veterans Affairs Network Contracting Office 6, 100 Emancipation Drive, Hampton, VA 23667, intends to award a sole source Firm-Fixed-Price contract to Veterans Healthcare Supply Solutions, Inc for the W. G. Hefner Medical Center, 1601 Brenner Ave. Salisbury, NC 28144. This contract action is to replace the TempTrak Sensors for Medical Refrigerators for the W. G. Hefner Medical Center, 1601 Brenner Ave. Salisbury, NC 28144; for which the Government intends to solicit from only one source under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b), Soliciting from a Single Source. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) for this requirement is 339113 with a size standard for NAICS is 800 Employees. THIS NOTICE OF INTENT IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITIVE QUOTATIONS. Responsible sources may identify their interest and capability to meet this requirement by submitting such information by 08:00 A.M., EST. on 03/30/2026. Supporting evidence must be furnished in sufficient detail to demonstrate the ability to comply with the above requirement. If your firm is a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned or Veteran Owned Small Business, you must be CERTIFIED in Dynamic Small Business Search. DSBS (see internet site: https://dsbs.sba.gov. An authorized dealer/distributor letter must be submitted with supporting evidence. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed contract based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Information received will be used to determine acquisition strategy. The Government will not be responsible for any costs associated with the preparation of responses to this notification. All inquiries and submittals must be sent via email to kenya.robinson@va.gov. Telephone inquiries will not be accepted.

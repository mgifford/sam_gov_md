# Joint Base Charleston AGE Corrosion Prevention and Paint Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:41:44.238-04
- Solicitation Number: FA441826Q0001

## Summary

The Purpose of this Amendment is to: Update AGE Priority Projection Pricing (Attachment 3). FY29 was missing the month of December Solicitation number FA441826Q0001 is issued as a Request for Quote (RFQ) for a Indefinite Delivery Requirements Contract to provide the 437th Aerospace Ground Equipment (AGE) Flight with surface preparation and painting services of 368 AGE pieces at Joint Base Charleston (JB CHS). The Contractor shall refurbish various support equipment, property of the 437th MXS, AGE Flight, based on available funding. This includes providing all necessary labor, management, facilities, equipment, tools, materials, transportation, and supervision to clean, paint, and protect metal surfaces of all support equipment over a five (5) year period.

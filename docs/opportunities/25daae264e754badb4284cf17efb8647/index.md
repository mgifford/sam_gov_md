## Summary

**Important Notice to All Interested Parties: This is a presolicitation notice. Quantity: 54 each Delivery Point: FOB Destination Estimated Date of Solicitation: April 15. 2026 SAM Notice: In order to be eligible for award, the successful offeror's System for Award Management (SAM) Registration must be active and include representations and certifications. Failure to obtain any said amendments and to respond to them prior to the date and time set for receipt of offers may render your offer nonresponsive and result in the rejection of same. Technical Data Package: Instructions will be available in the solicitation on how to access the associated technical data package.

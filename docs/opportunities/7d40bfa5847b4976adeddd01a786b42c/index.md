# Green River Lake Area Asphalt Blanket Purchase Agreement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 16:30:05.82-04
- Solicitation Number: W912QR26QKD51

## Summary

***AMENDMENT 0001 START***Solicitation W912QR26QKD51 is hereby amended as follows:NOTE: For pricing purposes for CLIN 0001, offerors are instructed to assume 1,000 linear feet of crack fill and 39,350 square feet of seal coating.NOTE: For pricing purposes for CLIN 0002, offerors are instructed to assume 200 linear feet of crack fill and 8,500 square feet of seal coating.***AMENDMENT 0001 END***This is a Request for Quote (RFQ) that will result in a Blanket Purchase Agreement for asphalt services in the Green River Lake Area (Green River Lake, Barren River Lake, Nolin River Lake and Rough River Lake). NOTE: The enclosed project is a SAMPLE project that will be used for price evaluation ONLY and will NOT be awarded. The pricing submitted will be used to determine fair and reasonableness and to establish a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) Pool of contractors for Asphalt Services in the Green River Lake Area (mentioned above). Selection will be based on lowest price and up to four BPAs will be awarded. Future projects will be competed among the BPA Pool of contractors only and individual calls will be awarded to the lowest price offered. NOTE: Contractors MUST be located within 125 miles of the Nolin River Lake Office, 2150 Nolin Dam Road, Bee Spring, KY 42207, to be considered for the Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA). Contractors located outside of the 125 mile radius will not be considered. NAICS: 237310 Quotes are due: 13 March 2026 at 11:00 AM EST. Please see solicitation for details. The solicitation will be available on the web only. Telephone and Fax requests for this solicitation will not be honored. Project files are Portable Document Format (PDF) files and can view, navigated, or printed using Adobe Acrobat Reader. To download the solicitation for this project, contractors are required to register at the Federal Business Opportunities. Amendments will be available from the beta.sam.gov website by download only. Prior to submission of quotes all contractors must have an active registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) database. To register or update information go to http://www.SAM.gov. Questions may be addressed to ProjNet. This announcement serves as the advance notice.

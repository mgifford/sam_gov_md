# AC Solid State Power Source

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 12:07:57.056-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1051

## Summary

This is an official Request for Quote (RFQ) Notice (FA462526Q1051). The U.S. Air Force 509th Contracting Squadron at Whiteman AFB is posting this solicitation, Request for Quote (RFQ), for purchase of an AC Power Source that meets the specifications of the attached Salient Characteristics dated 22 April 2026. DESCRIPTION OF REQUIREMENT: A new functioning 1.5kVA, Three Phase, Solid State AC Power Source with Limited Feature Set in accordance with the attached salient characteristics. DELIVERABLES: The contractor shall deliver 90 days after receipt of the order. Delivery will be to Whiteman AFB, MO 65305. RESPONSES: Quotes are due to both the Contracting Officer: Tiffany Driskill, at tiffany.driskill@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 12:00 PM CDT, Wednesday, 29 April 2026. Quotes received after that time may not be answered. To ensure all responses are received, responses must include the RFQ Number (FA462526Q1051) and title AC Power Source in the subject line of the email submission and are to be sent via email to both the Contracting Officer: Tiffany Driskill, at tiffany.driskill@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 12:00 PM CDT, Wednesday, 29 April 2026.

## Summary

Naval Surface Warfare Center (NSWC) Crane has a requirement for Instron test system, Thermal Analysis System DMA/SDTA The proposed contract action is for the supplies or services for which the Government intends to solicit and negotiate with one source, Mettler-Toledo LLC.1900 Polaris Parkway Columbus, Ohio 43240-4035; under the authority of FAR 13.106-1. This is being sole sourced because (Proprietary Information). All responsible sources may submit a capability statement, which shall be considered by the agency.

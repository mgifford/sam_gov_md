# Carousel Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-30 10:07:53.529-04
- Solicitation Number: M00263JL0021

## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT SYNOPSIS ONLY. This is NOT a Request for Quotation and in no way obligates the Government to award any contract/purchase order. The purpose of this Source Sought Synopsis is to seek out interested businesses capable of providing maintenance to White Carousel Conveyor Systems aboard MCRD Parris Island. Requirement is a five (5) year ordering period. Reference the Statement of Work for a more detailed description. Regional Contracting Office Parris Island is performing market research to determine available sources that can provide maintenance to White Carousel Conveyor Systems in support of locations aboard MCRD Parris Island. In addition, interested vendors shall provide the following information: Is your business a small business under this NAICS code? Refer to the attached draft Statement of work and respond based on the ability to provide those technical requirements. Are the services available under a GSA/FSS contract? If so, please provide the contract number. Cage code and DUNS number. Cage code and DUNS number must be registered in System for Award Management (https://www.sam.gov) Provide a contractor capabilities statement and electronic business card. The capability statements are due NLT 8 April at 4:00 PM EST POC is SSgt Loftis Jaydian.Loftis@usmc.mil All Questions will be due no later than April 6th at 2:00 PM EST POC Mr. Wentworth randy.wentworth@usmc.mil

# Tionesta Dam Security Fence Installation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 06:45:34.24-04
- Solicitation Number: W911WN26QA027

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Pittsburgh District, has a need for a Commercial Construction Contract for installation of Security Fences at Tionesta Dam, Tionesta, Forest County, Pennsylvania. The scope of work includes removing and replacing existing security fencing at two locations, as well as installing new fencing at one additional location within Tionesta Dam property. At the Control Tower Bridge, the new security fence will be 12 feet high and will include a barbed-wire apron mounted on extension arms, along with gates matching the height and configuration of the fence. New fencing at the Control Tower Tunnel and the Visitor Information Center (VIC) ? Fuel Tank Area will consist of 8-foot-high security fencing. This acquisition will be procured as a 100% Small Business Set-Aside. The resulting award will be for one (1) Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) Contract. This is a Request for Quote (RFQ) and will be submitted through Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) which posts the opportunity to SAM.gov. Therefore, requests for hardcopies will not be honored. The North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) for this procurement is 238990 ? All Other Specialty Trade Contractors, and the Small Business Size Standard is $19,000,000.00. Information regarding the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers process for settling requests for equitable adjustments and duration goals to achieve definitization of equitable adjustments for change orders under construction contracts can be found by going to https://www.usace.army.mil/Business-With-Us/Partnering/ then clicking on View FFP Contract Changes Playbook Here.

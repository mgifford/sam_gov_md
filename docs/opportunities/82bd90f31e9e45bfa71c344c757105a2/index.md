# DLA Energy Ship's Bunkers Program – Ponce, Puerto Rico (F76/JP5)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-25 22:39:28.72-04

## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE-MARKET RESEARCH PURPOSES. This Sources Sought Notice is for information and planning purposes only and does not constitute an Invitation for Bids (IFB), Request for Proposals (RFP), or Request for Quotes (RFQ). This notice should not be construed as a commitment by the Government to issue a solicitation or ultimately award a contract. Responses will not be considered as proposals or quotes. No award will be made as a result of this notice. The Government will not be responsible for any costs incurred by respondents in furnishing this information. Defense Logistics Agency - Energy is seeking to identify businesses that are interested and capable of supplying ships bunker fuels requirements for DLA - Energy. This notice covers the ships bunkers requirements for DLA - Energy customers located in Ponce, Puerto Rico for delivery of: F76 - Naval Distillate Fuel JP5 - Turbine Fuel, Aviation The deadline for responding to this Source of Sought Notice is April 1, 2026 3:00 PM EST. Responses and/or questions can be sent via email to: DDFBunkers@dla.mil

## Summary

SDVAHCS has an urgent requirement for a contractor to provide Non-Emergent Medical Transportation (NEMT) Services to eligible veteran beneficiaries twenty-four (24) hours a day, seven (7) days a week, to include holidays. This requirement is for a new, short-term, firm-fixed-price emergency bridge contract.

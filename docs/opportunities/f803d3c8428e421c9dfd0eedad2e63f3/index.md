# Multiple NSNs for OEM Pratt & Whitney (Cages 52661/77445) for addition to sole source long term contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-13 15:01:43.918-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE4AX24D9000ADD5

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency Weapons Support Richmond issues this Sources Sought for potentially adding a population of 381 NSNs identified as sole source via modification. This agency proposes adding 381 items to the existing TF33 Pacer Phoenix Contract. These will be added to the contract SPE4AX-24-D-9000, awarded to Pratt and Whitney/CAGE 52661 in accordance with the originally approved J&A. However, the NSNs identified here were not included in the list of original items synopsized for this effort. The contract has 8 potential years remaining. This contract requires supply of parts, as well as other functions including but not limited to planning and forecasting, reliability improvements, engineering maintenance, and design control affecting these parts. A Letter Request for Proposal (RFP) will be issued to the awardee to request addition of the 381 items as sole source. The NSNs on the attached list are currently under technical review. Any item identified as competitive will be removed. Contact the Contracting Officer listed to obtain a copy of the Letter RFP.

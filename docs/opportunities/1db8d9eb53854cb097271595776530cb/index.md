# DRAFT Request for Proposal (RFP) for Fairchild AFB Optimized Remediation Contract at Fairchild AFB, Washington.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 09:42:33.343-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8903-25-R-0035

## Summary

Providing the DRAFT RFP allows potential Offerors to submit questions to the Government on the subject requirement. The Government is also open to feedback to alternative approaches that can provide the best services to the Air Force. The purpose of this effort is for Optimized Remediation Services at Fairchild AFB, Washington (State). The requirement shall be completed in accordance with all applicable technical and regulatory requirements and the draft Performance Work Statement (PWS) dated 16 September 2025, Attachment 1. The Period of Performance (PoP) will be approximately 10 years from date of award. One (1) base year, and eight (8) option years. This draft RFP is anticipated to be issued as a competitive Section 8(a) Set-Aside. It is the responsibility of the interested party to check frequently for any changes to the solicitation. See the attached DRAFT RFP Cover Letter and all attachments.

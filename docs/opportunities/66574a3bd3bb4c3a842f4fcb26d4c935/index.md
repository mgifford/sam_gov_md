# Dehumidifier Purchase

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:11:21.563-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124M26QA009

## Summary

This solicitation is for the purchase of 200 personal dehumidifier. Dehumidifiers shall meet or exceed the following specifications and salient characteristics which are similar or equal to a TABYIK model CS50-SR dehumidifier: 1. Usage: Units will be utilized in a standard military barracks room. 2. Portable: Units shall be compact in design with a minimal over-all size and footprint that meet the requirements as spelled out in this document. They must be easily relocatable with little or no effort. Casters are preferred; however, wheels are acceptable. 3. Physical Features: The height of the unit should not exceed 21”. The units should be white or black in color. 4. Power Requirements: Units must be 110/120 VAC and work in a standard wall outlet with an American standard three prong outlet with a ground. 5. Required Operational Features: Extracts at least 35 pints of water per 24 hours Must have an external drainage connection and included drainage hose of at least 10 ft Must have an internal pump and capability to automatically drain the collected water into an elevated sink Auto shutoff that prevents overflow Handle(s) and wheels for easy portability ENERGY-STAR certified Questions: Kindly please confirm if the government will accept the dehumidifiers made in China? The government will accept dehumidifiers made in China. Kindly please confirm if the government will accept the dehumidifiers made in Trade Agreement Act designated country ? The supply purchase does not meet the dollar threshold to TAA and is thus not applicable The deliver address is: Building 1152 328 Italy Street Fort Stewart, Georgia 31314 * Between the referenced model (TABYIK CS50-SR) and the listed specifications (35-pint capacity, internal pump), which should take precedence? – The minimum capacity required is 35 pint/hr. The internal pump is a strict requirement to allow the dehumidifier to automatically drain water through a hose and into an elevated sink (above ground level). * Is the 21” height requirement strict, or would a unit around 23.25” be acceptable? – 23.25” is the max height requirement. Shorter is preferred. * Are you specifically looking for a unit with an internal pump and a 10 ft drainage hose, or would a unit with continuous gravity drainage (hose connection) be acceptable? – An internal pump is a strict requirement. A drainage hose of at least 10’ is also a strict requirement. * Is the 10 ft drainage hose requirement mandatory, or can a different length be provided if the unit otherwise meets performance requirements? - An included 10’ drainage is a strict requirement. * Is ENERGY STAR certification required, or preferred? – ENERGY STAR certification is preferred.

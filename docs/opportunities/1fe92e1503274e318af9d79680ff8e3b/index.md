# Maintenance Services for Anesthesia Machines for NH Rota

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 14:52:37.23-04
- Solicitation Number: 12422039

## Summary

This requirement is for preventive and corrective maintenance on anesthesia machines located at Naval Hospital Rota, Spain. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform preventive and corrective maintenance to ensure dependable and reliable equipment operation as defined in the Performance Work Statement for equipment listed below. The scope of work performed under these specifications includes the furnishing of all labor and parts to perform all repairs on equipment listed, to ensure continued operation at their designed efficiency and capacity. Defense Health Agency (DHA) intends to negotiate on a sole source basis in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 2304(c)(1) and FAR 13.106-1(b)(1) soliciting from a single source. The proposed supplier is Drager Hispania SA, Calle XAUDRAO, 5, Madrid 28034, Spain. See attached for Notice of Intent.

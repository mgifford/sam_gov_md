# Hand-Pulling Eurasian Watermilfoil, North Hartland Lake, North Hartland, VT and North Springfield Lake, Springfield, VT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 09:31:35.738-04
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA076

## Summary

THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Furnish all labor, equipment, and transportation necessary for hand-pulling Eurasian watermilfoil at North Hartland Lake, North Hartland, VT and at Stoughton Pond, North Springfield Lake, Springfield, VT. No snorkeling or SCUBA diving will be permitted. The Government may choose to exercise additional options for hand-pulling. All work shall be performed in accordance with the following Performance Work Statement and schedule. This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 541990 with a Small Business Size Standard of $19.5 Million. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

## Summary

The purpose of this RFP is to fulfill an Office of Secure Transportation (OST) requirement for Medical Support Services as it relates to the Human Reliability Program (HRP). Contractor support is required to ensure Federal Agents are continuously evaluated against HRP standards in order to maintain an elite level of medical readiness.

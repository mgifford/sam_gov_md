# Lot 4 SOW/CDRL Update Supply Chain BOM

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 09:04:19.053-04
- Solicitation Number: N00019-26-RFPREQ-WPM280-0098

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) intends to issue a sole source modification to Delivery Order (DO) N0001923F2616, under contract N0001920D0008 to Raytheon Company, (Raytheon), 1151 E Hermans Road, Tucson, AZ 85756-9367 to procure supply chain management data deliverables. This BOM is critical to adequately illuminate the supply base, support demand, capacity, and capability modeling as well as enable standard and efficient aggregation of common materials across the weapons industrial base to promote critical defense industrial base needs and mitigate supply base disruptions.. This contract modification is being pursued on a sole source basis under the statutory authority of 10 U.S.C. § 3204(a)(l) as implemented by Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subpart 6.302-1, only one responsible source and no other services will satisfy agency requirements. Raytheon is the sole designer, developer, and manufacturer of Tomahawk, and as such, is the only known source available with the requisite technical knowledge, experience, and tooling to produce the requirements described herein to satisfy the Government's requirement. Unless otherwise stated herein, no formal request for proposal or additional written information is currently available. Request for same will be disregarded. This notice is not a request for competitive proposals. All responsible sources may submit a capability statement which shall be considered by the agency. Subcontracting opportunities should be sought directly from Raytheon at the following address: (a) Raytheon Company, Raytheon, 1151 E Hermans Road, Tucson, AZ 85756-9367 (b) Attn: Ms. Melissa Arvayo (c) melissa.m.arvayo@rtx.com. IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FOR INTERESTED PARTIES: There are no commitments by the Government to issue any solicitations, make any award or awards, or to be responsible for any monies expended by any interested parties before award of a contract for the efforts mentioned above. Information provided herein is subject to change and in no way binds the Government to solicit for or award a contract. No telephone responses will be accepted. Point of contact for this synopsis is Jordan Sabedra. Responses to this synopsis should be sent by email to jordan.m.sabedra.civ@us.navy.mil.

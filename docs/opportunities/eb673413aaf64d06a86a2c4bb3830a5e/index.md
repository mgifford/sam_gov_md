# Southeast Regional Architecture Engineering 2 (SERAE 2) Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 13:41:23.409-04
- Solicitation Number: 80KSC026R0004

## Summary

This acquisition is for a regional multiple award, indefinite-delivery indefinite-quantity (IDIQ) contract for Architecture and Engineering services for the design and other professional services necessary to rehabilitate, modernize, and/or develop new systems for facilities and Ground Support Systems (GSS) across all of the following locations: Kennedy Space Center (KSC), including Cape Canaveral Space Force Station (CCSFS); Stennis Space Center (SSC); Johnson Space Center (JSC); Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC), including Michoud Assembly Facility (MAF), with the possibility of performance at other NASA assets and Centers nationwide.

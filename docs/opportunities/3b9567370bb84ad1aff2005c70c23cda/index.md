# MU26 & MU27 Aircraft Fire Extinguisher Cartridges

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 15:42:26.401-04
- Solicitation Number: PANROC26P0000030328

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command, Rock Island intends to award a Sole Source Contract t Pacific Scientific Energetic Materials Company (PSEMC, AZ) . in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements, for the procurement of Aircraft Fire Extinguisher Cartridges, Department of Defense Identification Code (DODIC) MU26 and MU27. This notice is intended for subcontracting opportunities only; it is not a request for competitive proposals. Detailed specifics of the technical and quality requirements will be provided in the Solicitation. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code applicable to this announcement is 325920 with a size standard of 750 employees. This synopsis does not constitute an RFP and shall not be construed as a commitment by the Government for any purpose. All questions and comments regarding this notice shall be directed to Emily Kundel at emily.a.kundel.civ@army.mil, Meghan Newman at meghan.p.newman.civ@army.mil, and Caitlyn Smith at caitlyn.l.smith3.civ@army.mil.

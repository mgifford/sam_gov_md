## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1240LU26Q0028 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) and consists of all labor, materials, equipment and subcontracted services for replacing and/or repairing the shingle roofs of several buildings at the Plains Ranger Station in Plains, MT. North American Industry Classification (NAICS): 238160 Roofing Contractors: Size Standard is $19.0 M. Set Aside: 100% small business set-aside. Project Magnitude –$100,000 - $250,000 Bid Bond/Offer Guarantee (FAR 52.228-1): 20% required if quote/offer in excess of $150,000 Performance & Payment Bonds (FAR 52.228-15): Both required at 100% of original contract price if value in excess of $150,000, due 10 days after award. Only payment protections required for contracts exceeding $35,000 but not exceeding $150,000, irrevocable letter of credit or payment bond allowed per FAR 52.228-13, due within 10 days of contract award. Provide quote by: 12:00 PM, Mountain Time on March 18, 2026. DOCUMENTS TO RETURN: The following documents shall be submitted by the quoter for evaluation to be considered responsive: Schedule of Items A document demonstrating recent and relevant experience and past performance in respect to the size and scope of this particular project. See pages 12 - 14 for specific information to include for evaluation. Acknowledge Amendments (per the instructions in Block 11 on the SF-30) if applicable. Send quote to: Forest Service, Property and Procurement Services, Intermountain Zone, CSA #9 Attn: Alisha Knaub E-mail: alisha.knaub@usda.gov For solicitation questions call Alisha Knaub at 406-438-2981 or email alisha.knaub@usda.gov. Questions are due no later than March 11, 2026, at 4:00 PM Mountain Time. This will ensure enough time to respond before the solicitation period ends.

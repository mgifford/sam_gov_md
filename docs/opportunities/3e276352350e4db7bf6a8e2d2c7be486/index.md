## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all management, tools, supplies, equipment, and labor necessary to perform all work in accordance with the Statement of Work. Work includes the design and installation and removal of fencing and replace the fences at the youth center, Building 348, and Building 916, the Education Center, on Columbus AFB., Ms. All materials will be contractor furnished. This general outline of work does not, in any way, limit the responsibility of the Contractor to perform all work as specified herein. NO ADVANCED PAYMENTS WILL BE MADE FOR THIS CONTRACT.

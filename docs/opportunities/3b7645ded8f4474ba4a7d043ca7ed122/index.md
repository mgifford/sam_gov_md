## Summary

Request for Information ***Amendment Two*** Staff Scheduling Software Access and Support Service FY26 (VA-26-00027047) Market Research Feedback Loop The purpose of this Request for Information (RFI) amendment is to provide the Government s Acquisition Strategy for Staff Scheduling Software Access and Support Service to industry. Based on market research, there is reasonable expectation that two or more Service-Disabled Veteran Owned-Small Businesses (SDVOSBs) are capable of meeting the requirement. Therefore, it has been determined by the Contracting Officer that the subject Request For Quotation (RFQ) will be released as a SDVOSB set-aside on SAM.gov under NAICS code 541519 Please continue to monitor System for Award Management (SAM.Gov) as a RFQ (solicitation) shall be forthcoming).

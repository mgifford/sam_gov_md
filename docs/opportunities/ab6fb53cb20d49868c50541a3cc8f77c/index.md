# Air Target Utilization by US Navy Ship (DDG)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 04:12:15.724-04
- Solicitation Number: N6817126QT006

## Summary

The Department of the Navy, Naval Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) FLC Rota, Spain; hereby publicizes its intention to solicit, negotiate, and award a Commercial, Firm-Fixed-Price purchase order on a noncompetitive basis with Aviation Defense Service from Saint Gilles, France, and CAGE code FA2B6. The requirement is for transportation, deployment, towing, and recovery of air target for engagement by a USN Guided Missile Destroyer (DDG) off the coast of Toulon, France in May 2026. A determination by the government not to compete based upon responses to this notice is solely at the discretion of the government. However, all responsible sources may submit a capability statement which shall be considered by the agency. The applicable NAICS Code is 481219 - Other Nonscheduled Air Transportation, and the size standard is $25M. The point of contact for this announcement is julius.j.arrascue-pastor.civ@us.navy.mil Be advised that the aforementioned information is anticipatory in nature and is not binding. A copy of the Request for Quote will be made available upon request. Any comments, questions or concerns regarding this notice may be submitted via e-mail to julius.j.arrascue-pastor.civ@us.navy.mil by 30 MAR 2026 at 11am local, Rota, Spain time. Questions by phone or fax will not be considered.

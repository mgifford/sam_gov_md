# Lighting installation for B704

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 12:50:41.747-04
- Solicitation Number: FA449726Q0020

## Summary

The 436 Civil Engineer Squadron, Dover Air Force Base (DE), requires a contractor to provide all labor, materials, tools, and equipment necessary for the removal and replacement of existing lighting with LED luminaires in Building 704. The requirement includes installation, disposal of existing fixtures, and associated electrical services in accordance with the Statement of Work (SOW). This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code associated with this requirement is 238210 – Electrical Contractors and Other Wiring Installation Contractors, with a small business size standard of $19,000,000.00.

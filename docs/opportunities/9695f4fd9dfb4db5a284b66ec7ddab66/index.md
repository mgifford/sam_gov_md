# Procurement of Dynamic Waterjet Cutting Head Upgrade, Components, Software, Installation, and Training

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 04:37:29.423-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV-26-Q-A037

## Summary

The contractor shall furnish and deliver all equipment, components, software, and materials required for the Dynamic Waterjet Cutting Head Upgrade, as detailed in Attachment 1 – Salient Characteristics. The contractor shall also provide installation and operator training to ensure full integration with the Government’s existing waterjet system. Quoters are required pay special attention with the instructions in FAR 52.212?1 and FAR 52.212?2 as stated in the solicitation document.

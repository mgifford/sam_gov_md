# Flagpole Installation Services and Materials

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:50:33.205-04
- Solicitation Number: PAN41026P0000025651

## Summary

The 410th Contracting Support Brigade (CSB), Regional Contracting Office (RCO) – Miami, in support of the Joint Interagency Task Force South (JIATF South) located at Truman Annex, Bldg 4180, Key West, FL 33040, will be issuing on a competitive basis 100% Total Small Business set-aside, Firm Fixed Price Purchase Order. This requirement is for the furnishing of all labor, materials, tools, and equipment necessary for the installation of two (2) new flagpoles, each approximately 25-30 feet in height. The flagpoles must match the existing flagpoles in material, finish, and style to ensure uniformity. Installation must meet or exceed hurricane wind load requirements for Monroe County, Florida, and comply with the Unified Facilities Criteria (UFC) and all applicable local, state, and federal codes. All work planning and coordination must begin within 5 days of contract award, and all work must be completed no later than 180 days after award. This announcement constitutes the synopsis. The complete Solicitation with the Performance Work Statement will be released on or around 21 April 2026. Vendors should refer to the solicitation for details regarding the requirements.

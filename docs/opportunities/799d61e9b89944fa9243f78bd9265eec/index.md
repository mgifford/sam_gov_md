## Summary

SALIENT CHARACERISTICS Item: Decon Tent Essential: Decon Tent is used for mass decontamination, Mass Decontamination, Infrastructure Protection, Mass Casualty Triage Significant Physical: +Articulating external frame with locking straps allows rapid deployment with high system integrity + Aircraft-grade aluminum frame with highly corrosion-resistant alochrome treatment and polyester powder coating + Frame can function with 30% of structure missing or broken + Portable and light weight; shelter can be relocated while deployed + Extremely flexible, UV stable, fire retardant, tear and chemical-resistant fabric on interior; protects exterior frame from contaminants and expedites post-use clean up + Spray jets on interior fabric saturate patients with 360° of spray; individual on/off capability allows users to scale up or down based upon operational needs + Ergonomic hand sprayers enable personnel to assist for decontamination of both ambulatory and non-ambulatory patients + Removable active divider curtains with integrated spray jets provide for thorough decontamination while also separating the shelter into three lines one non-ambulatory center line flanked by two ambulatory lines for privacy between males and females Functional: Decontamination Shelter provides two shower lines for the effective decontamination of multiple ambulatory or non-ambulatory casualties as well as larger emergency response teams. Small undress and redress areas at each end accommodate the pre-shower doffing of contaminated clothing and post-shower donning of clean clothing inside the shelter. The shelter can be set up by only two to four people in just minute.

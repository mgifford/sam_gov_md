# Rapid Response Munitions Testing and Engineering Services Follow-On

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 09:09:26.909-04
- Solicitation Number: N0017426R1010

## Summary

NSWC IHD and other Navy and DoD requiring activities require engineering services and rapid response testing support for a variety of programs, which protect and support the warfighter. The engineering services and rapid response testing support are a major requirement of the life-cycle process. Much of the testing support will require specialized test facilities with the capability to handle live ordnance and fixturing, which is able to accommodate extreme size and weights, or underwater. This support shall include engineering services and infrastructure for characterizing, testing, and documenting the performance of weapons, ammunition, munitions, and energetic systems, components and related systems.

# Sterile Bags for FSIS Laboratories

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 11:02:23.281-04
- Solicitation Number: 123A9426Q0014

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 123A9426Q0014 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for sterile bags for all U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Food Safety and Inspection Service (FSIS) laboratories. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 541380 - Testing Laboratories and Services. The small business size standard is $19 million. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

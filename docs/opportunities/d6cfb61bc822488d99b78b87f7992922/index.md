# Two 6-Seater ATV Purchase SN/BK 26 Philippines

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 22:30:18.292-04
- Solicitation Number: W91QVP-26-Q-A003

## Summary

The Government intends to award a Firm-Fixed Price Contract to provide two (2) 6-Seater All-Terrain Vehicles (ATV) for the 25ID. These ATVs will support mobility, personnel transport, and operational movement during SN/BK 26, Philippines. This procurement is a competitive requirement. The expected delivery of the supplies will be within ten (10) days of contract award.

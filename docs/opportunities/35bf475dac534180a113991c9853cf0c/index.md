# Third Party Support for Servers and FC Switches

- Agency: LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 13:35:04.724-04
- Solicitation Number: 2026-CIO-0119

## Summary

Amendment 0002: The purpose of this amendment is to updated sections B and L of the solicitation to remove option year CLIN's and option year pricing. In addition, extend the due date for submissions to 03/25/2026. Amendment 0001: The purpose of this amendment is to update section C and L of the solicitation and extend the due date for submission to March 23, 2026 at 12:00 PM EDT. The Library of Congress (Library) requires a Support/Maintenance services contract for several hardware components the Library owns. The Library has older servers and MDS Fiber Channel (FC) switches that are no longer supported by the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) due to their age. The servers and MDS switches are functional and support critical applications such as Copyright and loc.gov. The Support services will provide a consistent reliable source of parts replacement for failed hardware. See attached solicitation for full details.

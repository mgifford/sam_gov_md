# Chemical and Hazardous Waste Collection, Transportation and Disposal Services

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 14:34:53.101-04
- Solicitation Number: (15DDHQ25R00000017)

## Summary

This solicitation is a Request for Proposals (RFP) for a Base Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to provide Chemical and Waste Disposal Services for the Drug Enforcement Administration (DEA), Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) and the Authorized Central Storage (ACS). The services are required for the Geographic __________ Region. The Government intends to award seven individual five year firm-fixed-price IDIQ-type contracts through negotiations to cover the seven geographical regions across the United States. Offerors are invited to submit proposals that demonstrate their capability to meet the requirements outlined in this solicitation.

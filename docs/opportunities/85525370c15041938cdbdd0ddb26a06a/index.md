# FiberStar Welding Station and on-site Intro to laser welding

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 12:40:33.884-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016726Q1072

## Summary

Naval Surface Warfare Center Carderock Division (NSWCCD) intends to award a firm fixed price (FFP) purchase order using Simplified Acquisition Procedures under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b) the Contracting Officer has determined only one brand to be reasonably available. The Government intends to solicit for: FiberStar 8900 Series 600W CNC Industrial Welding Station package and on-site Intro to laser welding, to include all shipping/freight costs, as detailed in the attached solicitation. This solicitation is a brand name, small business set-aside (justification attached). This announcement constitutes the only solicitation and a written solicitation will not be issued. Quotes are being requested under Request for Quotation (RFQ) no. N0016726Q1072. The NAICS code is 333992 and the business size standard is 1,250 Employees.

# NYLON-TUNGSTEN GRANULAR

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 15:18:47.582-04
- Solicitation Number: N4523A53577171

## Summary

This is a notice for market research purposes only. This is not a solicitation for proposals or quotations. The purpose of this notice is to obtain information regarding the availability and capability of qualified sources to provide the following item: 1. Requirement Details: Product: Nylon/Tungsten Granules Description: Granules, Thermoplastic Composite Material. Material is Tungsten/PA 12 Composite at 11 g/cc. Manufacturer: ECOMASS TECHNOLOGIES Manufacturer Part Number: 1700TU96 NAT Quantity: 5 Each (Unit of Issue is one 50 lb container) The Government has identified ECOMASS TECHNOLOGIES P/N: 1700TU96 NAT as a technically acceptable part for this requirement. 2. Purpose of Notice: The Government is conducting market research to identify potential sources capable of providing this material. We are seeking responses from all interested and qualified businesses. 3. Instructions for Response: Interested parties are requested to submit a brief statement of interest and capability. Please do not submit quotes or pricing information at this time. Your response should include the following: Company Name, Address, and Point of Contact Information. CAGE Code and Unique Entity ID (UEI). A statement confirming your company's ability to supply the specified part number. 4. Disclaimer: This notice is for informational and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation or a promise to issue a solicitation in the future. This notice does not commit the Government to contract for any supply or service. The Government will not pay for any information or administrative costs incurred in response to this notice. Please submit your statement of interest via email to: carolyn.j.george.civ@us.navy.mil

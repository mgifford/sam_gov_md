# OUSD(R&E) Seeks Advanced Manufacturing, Prototypes and Materials (AMPAM)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 12:50:07.396-04
- Solicitation Number: HQ003425BOTA1_Amd_1_CFA1

## Summary

Capability Focus Area 1 (CFA1) is issued against BOTA HQ003425BOTA1 to solicit submissions that address concepts and technologies to enable Multi-Modal Agile Manufacturing in support of improved Contested Logistics Technologies. CFA1 for HQ003425BOTA1 is managed by Office of the Undersecretary of War Research and Engineering (OUSW(R&E)) Manufacturing Technology program (ManTech). The OUSW(R&E) is the principal advisor to the Secretary and Deputy Secretary of War for all matters regarding the Department of War Research and Engineering (DoW(R&E)) enterprise, defense R&E, technology development, technology transition, developmental prototyping, experimentation, developmental testing activities and programs, and unifying defense R&E efforts. The OUSW(R&E) also serves as the Chief Technology Officer for the DoW with the mission of advancing technology and innovation within the DoW.

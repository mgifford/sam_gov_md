## Summary

PRE-SOLICITATION NOTICE: THIS SOLICITATION IS EXPECTED TO RESULT IN A FIRM FIXED PRICE CONTRACT WITH ONE, 1-YEAR, 100% OPTION. NOUN: Valve, Butterfly NSN: 4810-01-153-9363 BASE QTY: 44 EA OPTION QTY: UP TO 44 EA PART NUMBER: 2248440 CAGE: 75Q65 PRESERVATION: MIL PACKING: B INSP/ACCP: Origin/Origin FOB: Destination This butterfly valve used on the M978 HEMTT Tanker for the purpose of controlling the flow of fluids. In the case of a butterfly valve, fluid flow is controlled by a disc that can be found in the middle of the pipe. Technical data is not available for this procurement. Solicitation release to web will be approximately 1 May 2026; approximate closing date is 1 June 2026. Once posted, see the solicitation for actual closing date which will be available for downloading from https://sam.gov.

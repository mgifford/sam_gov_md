# Request for Information: Graphene Production and Manufacturing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-16 11:07:27.171-04
- Solicitation Number: DARPA-SN-26-62

## Summary

The Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) is seeking information on the current global capabilities and the latest technological advancements in graphene production. This research is to evaluate its potential for use in manufacturing load-bearing structures for aerospace applications.

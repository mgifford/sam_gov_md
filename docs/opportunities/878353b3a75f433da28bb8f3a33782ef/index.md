## Summary

24 APRIL 2026 UPDATE: Q&A 1-8 posted. The closing date remains unchanged. ************************************************** This solicitation notice is issued to inform industry that the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) / Defense Information Technology Contracting Organization-Pacific (DITCO-PAC), Building 77, Room 129, 477 Essex Street, Pearl Harbor, Hawaii 96860 has released solicitation no. HC1019-25-R-0007 to procure, install, program and test two new video walls, two video processors, and miscellaneous connected commercial information technology (IT) products located at DISA-PAC, Pearl Harbor, Hawaii. This solicitation directly replaces HC1019-24-R-0003 which was awarded under HC1019-25-C-0002, then canceled shortly thereafter. The Government was unable to amend the original solicitation, thus HC1019-25-R-0007 replaces it. The due date of proposals is Wednesday, May 6, 2026 NLT 10:00 am HST. ATTACHMENTS INCLUDE: (1) Solicitation-HC101925R0007 (2) PWS Attachment-Product List (3) Price Quote Worksheet (4) Quality Assurance Surveillance Plan (QASP) (5) Brand Name Justification (6) RFQ HC101925R0007 Q+A 1-8_ 4.24.26

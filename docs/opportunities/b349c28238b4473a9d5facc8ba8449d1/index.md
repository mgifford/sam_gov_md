## Summary

NOTICE: This is not a solicitation but rather a Sources Sought Synopsis to determine potential sources for information and planning purposes only.

1.1 The purpose of this Sources Sought is to conduct market research to assist in determining if this effort can be competitive and/or a total Small Business Set-Aside. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL (RFP). I The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 334111, a corresponding category for manufacturing Computer Terminal and Peripheral Equipment (i.e. keyboards). The Government will use this information to determine the best acquisition strategy for this procurement. The Government is particularly interested in all small businesses to include 8(a), Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned, HUBZone, and Women-Owned small business concerns. The Government requests that interested parties respond to this notice if applicable and identify your small business status to the identified NAICS code. Additionally, please provide any anticipated integrators along with a description of similar manufactured items offered to the Government and to commercial customers for the past three years. Any responses involving teaming arrangements should delineate between the work that will be accomplished by any integrators and work accomplished by any/all teaming partners. Responses due no later than 1700 MST. on 9 April 2026 Please see attachment for more details. Questions relative to this market survey should be addressed to Justin Raleigh, (justin.raleigh.1@us.af.mil)

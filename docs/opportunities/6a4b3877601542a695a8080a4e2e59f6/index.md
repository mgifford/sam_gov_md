## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR 12.6/13.303, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. The solicitation number is 47QMCA26R0030, a request for proposal. The Government intends to establish a Blanket Purchase Agreements (BPA) in Guam for auction and marshalling services in support of the General Services Administration’s Fleet Program Office. This effort encompasses receipt, inspection, preparation, assignment/termination of new, exchange, or reassignment for Government owned or leased vehicles. The BPA will be effective for a 12-month base period with three 12- month option periods. Base Period: June 1, 2026 – May 31, 2027 Option Period 1: June 1, 2027 – May 31, 2028 Option Period 2: June 1, 2028 – May 31, 2029 Option Period 3: June 1, 2029 – May 31, 2030 This solicitation is full and open under NAICS 811111. The small business size standard is $9M. If sales services are offered, the NAICS code is 425120. The small business size standard is 125 employees. The Service Contract Act does apply and the following DOL Wage Determination applies to the established BPA; 2015-5693 REV 26. The annual estimate for the number of vehicles to be marshalled is 1130. The specific work requirements are detailed within the attached Statement of Work (SOW). Potential offerors are hereby notified that the solicitation and subsequent amendments to the solicitation will only be available by downloading the documents at the beta.SAM.gov website at https://sam.gov/ . This office will not issue hard copy solicitations.

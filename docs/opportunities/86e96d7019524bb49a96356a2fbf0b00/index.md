# Commander's Conference Room Upgrade

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 07:56:11.3-05
- Solicitation Number: FA857126Q0048

## Summary

3/4 Amendment: Solicitation extended until 3/16 at 4:30 PM EST to allow a scheduled site visit scheduled for Tuesday, March 10, 2026 at 9:00 AM EST. You must make it known by Friday, March 6th 12:30 PM that you intend to attend the site visit. Please view the Site Visit Notice attachment for instructions to sign up for the site visit. Solicitation FA857126Q0048 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ). Quotations should be emailed to patrick.madan.2@us.af.mil. Vendors are required to complete and sign solicitation document and return it with their quote. Requirement Description: The Contractor shall provide all transportation, materials, parts, travel, delivery, tools, training, and labor necessary to provide an upgrade to the Commanders Conference Room in Building 125, Robins AFB, Ga. Rewire audio visual equipment in the conference room, supply and install approved camera system, supply and install microphone system, supply and install speaker system, supply and install rack and amplifier, and tune system for better audio in room and on the Polycom phones. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with the format in subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; proposals are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued.

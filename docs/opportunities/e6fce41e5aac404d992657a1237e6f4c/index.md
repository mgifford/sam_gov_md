# Commercial Data Program (CDP) Microwave Sounder (MWS) Data Buy

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 14:15:25.4-04
- Solicitation Number: 1332KP26R0007

## Summary

*Updated 04/02/2026 to include the Question & Answer (Q&A) Form attachment for ease of submission for questions. **************************************************************************************** This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1332KP26R0007 is issued as a request for proposal (RFP) to acquire commercial, near-real-time MWS radiance data, including optional hyperspectral observations, from on-orbit MWS systems. This acquisition is a full and open competition, RESERVED for one small business concern. Proposals are required to be received in the contracting office no later than 2:00 P.M., Eastern Time on April 22, 2026. Questions related to this RFP are due no later than 2:00 P.M. Eastern Time on April 10, 2026. All proposals must be emailed to the attention of Suzanna Espinoza, Contracting Officer at: suzanna.espinoza@noaa.gov and Cherron E. Bennett-Pettus, Contract Specialist at: cherron.bennett-pettus@noaa.gov. See Attachments for further details.

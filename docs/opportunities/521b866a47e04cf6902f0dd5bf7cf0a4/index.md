# C-5 Honeycomb Contract Strategy

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-04 10:31:13.559-05
- Solicitation Number: C-5-Honeycomb-Contract-Strategy

## Summary

The C-5 Galaxy aircraft relies on varius adhesive bonded honeycomb panels to provide aerodynamic contours and adds structural integrity to airframes. Manufacturing bonded panels requires strict control in facility contamination levels, building temperature/humidity, tracking adhesive life, autoclave operation and bond line surface preparations. C-5 bonded panel manufacturing follows detailed processing specifications, and in the case of highly contoured areas, requires an experienced technical staff to interpret aircraft loft data.

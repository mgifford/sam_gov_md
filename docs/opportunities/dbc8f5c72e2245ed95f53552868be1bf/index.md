## Summary

Description This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 127EAX26Q0036 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Carson National Forest to furnish & install (1) Electronic sliding gate at the existing entrance to the El Nogal Campground, on the Camino Real Ranger District. Additionally, furnishment & installation of (1) electronic sliding gate at the entrance to the Canon Administrative Site, on the Camino Real Ranger District is included in this contract. With an additional option for the Fabrication and Delivery of (3) Swing Gates to the Canon Admin Site. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 236220 The small business size standard is 45.M This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. A formal site visit will be held on May 12, 2026 at 10:00AM Pacific Time. All questions are due no later than May 15, 2026 at 12:00 PM Pacific Time. All question shall be sent to Paula Sales, paula.sales@usda.gov. Items to return: 1. Signed 1442 2. Attachment 2 Schedule of Items 3. Bid bond if applicaple 4. Technical Proposal 5. Past Performance

## Summary

In accordance with FAR/ RFO 19.108 and R-DFARS 219.108, this is a sole source directed 8(a) fixed -price contract for the continuation of non-personal services providing daily operations, maintenance, sustainment, modifications, and security support for the Command Human Resources Intelligence System (CHRIS). CHRIS is a critical, web-based Human Resource (HR) reporting tool that integrates data from multiple DoD and Air Force systems to provide workforce management reporting capabilities to Air Force Materiel Command (AFMC) and the Air National Guard (ANG).

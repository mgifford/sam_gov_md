# RFI-Female Anatomy Medical Training Models

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-24 10:16:45.811-04
- Solicitation Number: FA3016-26-Q-0100

## Summary

This is a request for information (RFI) and a sources sought notice. The purpose of this notice is to conduct market research and determine commercial market availability of female anatomy medical training models, market factors that affect availability, cost and shipping of these medical training goods. Addtionally, the government is researching the commercial market for vendor availability and business size to determine a path forward to solicit and quote medical training markets identified in this notice. Businesses who are interested in participating in this market research are highly encouraged to read the RFI and salient characteristics in their entirety before filling out the attached survey and responding to this notice.

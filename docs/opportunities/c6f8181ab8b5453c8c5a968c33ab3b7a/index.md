# Wrapping Machine for Laundry Plant

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 00:03:13.706-04
- Solicitation Number: FA520926Q016A

## Summary

This notice is prepared IAW RFO FAR 5.1 and 12.201-1(c)(2). THIS NOTICE IS NOT A REQUEST FOR QUOTATIONS. The Government intends to solicit this requirement directly from suppliers IAW RFO FAR 12.201-1(c)(2) for firm-fixed price supply contract to purchase Wrapping Machine for Laundry Plant in support in Sagami General Depot, Japan. The anticipated award date is on or about 30 May 2026 (subject to change). Description of the requirement: The contractor shall provide Wrapping Machine including installation, transportation and any additional equipment or materials necessary to deliver/install the required products. Note 1: This procurement will be performed entirety in the country of Japan. The successful offeror must have valid licenses work in the country of Japan.

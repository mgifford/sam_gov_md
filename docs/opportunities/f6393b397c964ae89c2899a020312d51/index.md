## Summary

4/20/2026 - As required, posting to provide notification that all classes housing off Center for March 2026 have been placed. 2/4/2026 - As required, posting to provide notification that all classes housing off Center for February 2026 have been placed. 1/2/2026 - As required, posting to provide notification that all classes housing off Center for January 2026 have been placed. 11/26/2025 - As required - posting to proivde notification all classes for December 2025 have been placed. 10/26/2025 - As required - posting to provide notification all classes for Oct 28 to 31 and November 2025 have been placed. 10/3/2025 - Posting Amendment 0001 and Questions and Answers. Please see the attached Request for Quotes for Off Center Lodging. The Government has a requirement for an estimated 2754 rooms for the time period of October 28, 2025, through March 31, 2026, for lodging students. This number is subject to change based on needs of the Government due to class changes, including but not limited to cancellations; additions; etc. The number is total rooms and will be broken down by class. At this time the Government does not know the specific arrival dates or departure dates because classes are still registering. Specific classes with number of students, arrival dates, and departure dates has not been finalized. Classes will arrive at different dates throughout the months of October 28, 2025, through March 31, 2026. Rooms will be ordered for the number of students in one (1) class. Thus, multiple awards are expected.

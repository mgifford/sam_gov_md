# Engineering Support for Building B210 Optimization

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-17 12:22:42.944-04
- Solicitation Number: 55555

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) is being issued solely for information and planning purposes and does not constitute an Invitation for Bids (IFB), a Request for Proposals (RFP), a Request for Quotations (RFQ) or an indication that the Government will contract for any of the items and/or services contained in this notice. In accordance with FAR 52.215-3, Request for Information or Solicitation for Planning Purposes, the Government will not pay for any information/items submitted in response to the RFI. No determination as to the viability of this potential requirement has been made at this point and there is currently no solicitation for this effort. Building B210 requires engineering support to update its current documentation, optimize space, and standardize various operational processes. The building houses several major organizations, and a coordinated effort is needed to ensure the layout meets current standards and stakeholder needs. The selectee will work closely with the WR-ALC/EN Point of Contact to achieve the outlined objectives. Please see document attached

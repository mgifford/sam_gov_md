ALL DELIVERIES WILL BE MADE TO AVON PARK AIR FORCE RANGE (ZIP - 33825). Solicitation for Graded Crushed Aggregate Base Course This is a solicitation for the provision and delivery of Graded Crushed Aggregate Base Course (GCA) for the Avon Park Air Force Range in Avon Park, Florida. Summary of Requirements The contractor shall provide all plant, labor, material, design, and supervision necessary to deliver a base quantity of 16,410 tons of Graded Crushed Aggregate Base Course (GCA). The GCA will be used for the construction of Taxiway Hotel and the C-17 Assault strip. The government holds an option to purchase up to an additional 1,000 tons of GCA, in accordance with FAR 52.217-7. Important Instructions for Offerors All interested parties must carefully review the full solicitation package.

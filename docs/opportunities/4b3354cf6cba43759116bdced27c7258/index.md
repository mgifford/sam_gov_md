- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-08 18:37:21.088-04

## Summary

The Government intends to make a sole source award to Alfa Laval, Inc. as they are the OEM for the Aqua Blue Single Stage Freshwater Generators aboard the USNS HENRY J. KAISER (T-AO 187). Any parts ordered from any entity worldwide will ultimately be purchaed through Alfa Laval.

# Ground Maintenance and Tree Assessment USAG Wiesbaden

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 08:42:55.238-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CM26QA014

## Summary

The Regional Contracting Office intends to solicit and competitively award a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract for Ground Maintenance and Tree Assessment in USAG Wiesbaden, Germany. The contract will consist of a five (5) years Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Single Award. Proposed performance start date is on or before February 2026. Solicitation W912CM-26-Q-A014 will be issued before or after April 01, 2026. The RFQ and any amendments will be posted to www.Sam.gov. Intended Quoters should note that the DRAFT Performance Work Statement (PWS) is attached. This Document is being provided for planning purposes only and subject to change. All documents in the issued solicitation, and subsequent amendments, if applicable, will supersede this draft document. IMPORTANT: Quoters must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at time of submission of quotes. For registration instructions visit the following website: www.sam.gov NOTE: All dates and times refer to Central European Time. FOR ANY ENQUIRIES CONTACT NANCY TAGU, EMAIL: NANCY.N.TAGU.LN@ARMY.MIL

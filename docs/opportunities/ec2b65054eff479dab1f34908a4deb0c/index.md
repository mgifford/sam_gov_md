# 1 Faraday Tent

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 11:03:08.273-04
- Solicitation Number: 15F06726Q0000156

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) intends to issue a Request for Quote (RFQ) for the one (1) Faraday Tent FOB Destination to Washington, DC. This notice is not a request for proposals. The solicitation will be issued as a 100% Small Business set-aside. The North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code is 314910- Textile Bag and Canvas Mills, and the applicable Small Business Size Standard, as established by the U.S. Small Business Administration, is 500 employees. The magnitude of this acquisition is estimated to be less than $25,000. In accordance with Revolutionary FAR Overhaul FAR 12.201-1(c)(2), the Government may solicit quotations directly from suppliers for this acquisition at or below the simplified acquisition threshold. Vendors that respond to this notice and provide a valid point of contact MAY BE issued the solicitation directly via email, however, response to this notice does not guarantee that your firm will receive the solicitation package. The Government reserves the right to determine the number of sources to be solicited. It is the vendor’s responsibility to notify the points of contact below of their interest in this requirement. The solicitation (RFQ) will be issued on or about March 27, 2026. Telephonic inquires will not be honored. If adequate competition is not received, the RFQ will be posted to SAM.gov. Interested vendors are encouraged to email the Contracting Officer, Alexa Moates at acmoates@fbi.gov and the Contract Specialist, Lakeita Wilson at lrwilson3@fbi.gov. Ensure the subject of your email contains the words, “Faraday Tent”.

# FA480326Q0025 - LRS Shelving Units

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 13:56:33.838-04
- Solicitation Number: FA480326Q0025

## Summary

The contractor is responsible for the installation of a pallet rack shelving system in the existing warehouse located in building 216 and in building 1518. The contractor will adhere to safety regulations and manufacturer specifications during the installation process, which includes securely anchoring the shelving to the floor to enhance safety and accessibility, as well as the removal and disposal of any outdated shelving.

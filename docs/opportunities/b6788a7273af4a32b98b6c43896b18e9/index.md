# Shipyard Overhaul of the S/V RECONNAISSANCE

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 12:47:24.874-04
- Solicitation Number: W912BU26QA010

## Summary

***The purpose of Amendment 0001 to W912BU26QA010 is to extend the solicitation closing date to 19 March 2026 at 1:00 PM EST.*** The U.S. Army Corps of Engineer (USACE), Philadelphia District (NAP), intends to award a firm fixed-price purchase order to procure services to furnish all engineering, design, labor, services, equipment, parts and materials necessary to perform drydocking and repairs for the Survey Vessel RECONNAISSANCE in accordance with the Statement of Work. BASIS FOR SELECTION OF AWARD: Award shall be made to the offeror who is has the lowest price meeting the technical requirements. Quote must meet or exceed the statement of work and delivery schedule to be considered for award. QUOTE SUBMISSION: Offerors shall submit their quotes no later than 1300 EST on 12 March 2026 as follows: The Offerors quote shall be submitted electronically via Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE), as described below. The Offerors quote shall not contain classified data. The use of hyperlinks in bids is prohibited. NOTE: The only authorized transmission method to this solicitation is electronically via PIEE Solicitation Module online submission. NO OTHER TRANSMISSION METHODS (EMAIL, FACSIMILIE, REGULAR MAIL, HANDCARRIED, ETC.,) WILL BE ACCEPTED. ADDITIONAL INFORMATION: Specific information pertaining to this procurement is available in the Solicitation. The Solicitation documents are posted on the SAM.gov website. It is, and will continue to be, the responsibility of all potential Offerors to monitor the SAM.gov website for any amendments, updates, responses to industry questions/comments, etc. The Government will not be maintaining a mailing list. POINT-OF-CONTACT: The Primary Contract Specialist for this Solicitation is Katie Sayers at katie.l.sayers@usace.army.mil. Any communication regarding this notice should be made in writing sent via email and must identify the Solicitation number, company name, address, phone number and point of contact.

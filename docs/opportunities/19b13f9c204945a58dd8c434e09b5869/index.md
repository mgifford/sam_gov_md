# AN/USC-28(V) Satellite Communication Sets (SCS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-10 10:12:12.936-04
- Solicitation Number: PANAPG-26-P-0000039405

## Summary

Purpose: Request for Information – Sources Sought, The US Army Communications-Electronic Command (CECOM) is conducting a Market Survey to identify potential sources with the capability to provide maintenance support for the AN/USC-28(V) Satellite Communication Sets (SCS) and the Charon Merged Emulator. The intent of this RFI is to gauge industry capabilities, and relevant experience to support the AN/USC-28(V) Satellite Communication Sets (SCS) and the Charon Merged Emulator. The scope of this requirement includes, but is not limited to: • Depot-level repair and overhaul of Line Replaceable Units (LRUs), Shop Replaceable Units (SRUs), and end-items for both Airborne and Ground-based systems. • Providing Field Maintenance Technician (FMT) support. • Testing, inspection, and certification of equipment.

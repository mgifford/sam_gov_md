## Summary

This solicitation is to integrate Intrusion Detection System for the 353 SOW to the main Intrusion Detection System IAW the SOW provided. Questions on this solicitation are due 13 FEB at 0800 JST. Answers will be provided by COB on 17 FEB JST. This solicitation is due 21 FEB 2026 at 1000 AM JST. Amendment 1: The solicitation was extended to 16 March at 12 pm JST (or 15 March at 7pm PST) due to outstanding unswered questions by the government causing hardship on the vendors ability to quote. Concerning the period of performance, we ask that you propose a period of performance and start date that you find realistic. Amendment 2: This amendment incorporates Contractors' questions and answers provided by the Government. Quotes are still due 16 March at 12 pm JST.

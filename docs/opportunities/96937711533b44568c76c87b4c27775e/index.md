# 2026 HAFB Air Show Crowd Control and Barricades

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 12:52:34.759-04
- Solicitation Number: FA820126Q0016

## Summary

THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR QUOTE (RFQ). THE GOVERNMENT WILL NOT PAY FOR ANY COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH RESPONDING TO THIS PRESOLICITATION. The Government intends to issue a Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) sole source solicitation/contract to Interstate Barricades, LLC (Cage Code 4FXP6). The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, transportation, delivery, installation, maintenance, and removal necessary to support traffic management and crowd?control operations for the 2026 Warriors Over the Wasatch Airshow at Hill Air Force Base (HAFB), Utah. All work shall comply with federal, state, local, and Department of Defense (DoD) regulations, including MUTCD standards. Type: Service Estimated Delivery Schedule: 24 June 2026 to 28 June 2026 Anticipated Award: 03/31/2026 Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) Contract

# Jet/AGE Wash Service, Misawa AB, Japan

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 03:40:09.898-04

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number FA520526Q0014 is issued as an RFQ. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. This solicitation is for the Jet & AGE Wash/Corrosion Prevention Services for the F-16 and F-35 Aircraft at Misawa Air Base, Japan. This will be a service contract with a base year and four (4) option years. All specifications and clarifying information will be found in the Performance Work Statement and the attachments.

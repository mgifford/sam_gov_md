# SYNOPSIS U.S. Army West Point Temporary Billeting, Training, Shower Structures, and Equipment Rental

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 14:27:58.148-04
- Solicitation Number: W911SD-26-Q-A028

## Summary

The Mission and Installation Contracting Command Installation Contracting Office, West Point, NY (MICC-West Point) is issuing this SYNOPSIS for the Service of temporary structures for billeting (weatherproof housing), training structures, latrines (showers, sinks, toilets), systems need to accommodate for a 24/7 support staff, power, sewer, energy, and gas set up for Engagement Skills Trainer (EST) II, normally held at Camp Buckner on a base plus two (2) one (1) option years contract. The period of performance will be for April 30, 2026 – April 03, 2029 (estimated). This requirement will be 100% set aside for Small Business concerns holders under NAICS code 721310 - Rooming and Boarding Houses, Dormitories, and Workers' Camps, size standard is $14,000,000.00 Million per year and FSC/PSC of J054 Maintenance, Repair and Rebuilding of Equipment: Prefabricated Structures and Scaffolding. In order to be eligible for award, business must be registered as Small Business concern under the appropriate NAICS code in the System for Award Management (SAM) https://sam.gov. Please verify that both your SAM UEI # , CAGE Code, are current and valid in the System for Award Management (SAM), Offerors are reminded to carefully review solicitation documents issued as criteria is stated in the solicitation and shall govern. The solicitation will be issued on or about the week of March 02, 2026, in electronic format only and will be available on the Internet at: https://sam.gov. It is the Offeror’s responsibility to check the SAM.gov site; https://sam.gov provided as necessary for any posted changes to this solicitation and all amendments.

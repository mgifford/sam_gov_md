# National Forests in Mississippi, Homochitto Ranger District –Clear Springs Mowing Services

- Posted: 2026-04-07 09:26:29.165-04

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12444026Q0052 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Clear Springs Mowing area . The contractor shall provide all materials, labor, equipment, tools, transportation,permits, licenses, supervision to successfully complete grass cutting, blowing, and weed trimming for an estimated 6 acres in the Clear Springs recreation area and .2 acres lake edge as to maintain healthy grass, trees, shrubs, and plants. All landscaping etc, is to be professionally done at the Clear Springs Campground, 3888 Clear springs Rd, Meadville, MS 39653. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 561730 The small business size standard is $9.50 This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

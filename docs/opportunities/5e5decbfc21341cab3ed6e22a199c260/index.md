# Laundry and Dry Cleaning Service Amendment 3

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 18:11:21.794-04
- Solicitation Number: W911SF-26-R-A010

## Summary

The Contractor shall operate and manage the Laundry Distribution and Collection Point(s) for the issue, receipt, requisition, exchange, warehousing, and turn-in of linens IAW AR 210-130, Laundry and Dry-Cleaning Operations; AR 710-4, Property Accountability; and other regulatory requirements. The Contractor shall maintain a contractor developed accountability system for linens and other items submitted for L&DC services IAW AR 710-2, Supply Policy Below the National Level; AR 710-4, Property Accountability; and other regulatory requirements. Amendment 0003 is to update the following: (A) Update solicitation attachments 5,7, and 8, (B) Remove attachment nine verbiage from each CLIN, (C) Remove CLINs 3, 5, and 8, (D) Answer questions (E) Remove clause 52.219-30.

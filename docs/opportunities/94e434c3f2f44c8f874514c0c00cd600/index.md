# Guardrail Removal Services, Mansfield Hollow Lake, Mansfield Center, CT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 09:57:41.09-05
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA067

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - New England District is issuing a Presolicitation Notice for Guardrail Removal Services, Mansfield Hollow Lake, Mansfield Center, CT. QUESTIONS REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT WILL NOT BE ANSWERED DURING THE PRESOLICITATION PHASE. SUBMIT QUESTIONS TO THE CONTRACT SPECIALIST AFTER THE SOLICITATION AND ASSOCIATED DOCUMENTS HAVE BEEN POSTED. THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Contractor shall furnish all equipment, materials, and labor for the removal and disposal of approximately 10,600 linear feet of steel cable and wooden post guardrail at Mansfield Hollow Dam in accordance with the requirements and Performance Work Statement. This is not a request for quotes at this time. The solicitation requirements and contract documents, to include instructions for submission, and the response date will be available on or about March 19, 2026. Neither telephonic, mailed nor faxed requests for hard copies of this solicitation will be accepted. The solicitation will only be available online; in order to download the solicitation offerors must access the Contract Opportunities website (www.SAM.gov). This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 561990 with a Small Business Size Standard of $16.5 Million. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

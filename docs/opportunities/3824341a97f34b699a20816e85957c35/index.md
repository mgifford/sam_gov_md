# ACRU Thermal Management System Hoses

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 09:41:30.764-04
- Solicitation Number: 26-41-0153

## Summary

The Naval Surface Warfare Center Philadelphia Division intends to procure Advanced Carbon Dioxide Removal Unit (ACRU) Thermal Management System Hoses on a sole souce basis from McGill Hose & Coupling. The proposed contract action is for supplies or services for which the Government intends to solicit and award with only one source under authority of FAR 13.106(b). Any questions regarding this notice should be emailed to: zachary.f.mccurry.civ@us.navy.mil Phone calls will NOT be accepted.

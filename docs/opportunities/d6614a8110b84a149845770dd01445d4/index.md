# Z--FOG SEAL AND RESTRIPE 9.6 MILES OF PINTO BASIN RD

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 18:14:53.942-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P8126B0001

## Summary

The National Park Service, ConOps West, has a requirement to fog seal and re-stripe 9.6 miles of Pinto Basin Road in Joshua Tree National Park. The acquisition is subject to the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code of 237310 with a size standard of $45 million. This acquisition will be handled as an IFB prepared in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subparts 14 and 36. This project will be solicitated as 100% small business set-aside. A firm-fixed price contract award is contemplated. The project magnitude is estimated between $350,000 and $450,000. Place of performance will be Twentynine Palms, CA. The official solicitation and its attachments will be issued on/or about March 26, 2026, and will be posted to SAM.gov. The contracting office will not provide hardcopies of the solicitation. REGISTRATIONS: In order to be awarded a contract, an offeror must have a Unique Entity ID (UEI). Also, the offeror must be registered and active in the System for Award Management (SAM), website at http://www.sam.gov, and have completed their Online Representations and Certifications at the time of the solicitation close date. POINT OF CONTACT: Caroline Bachelier, Contracting Officer, caroline_bachelier@nps.gov

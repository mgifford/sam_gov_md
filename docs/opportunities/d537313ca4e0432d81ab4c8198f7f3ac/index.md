# Topside Equipment Repair and Maintenance Aboard the Dredge Wheeler

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 14:36:12.764-04
- Solicitation Number: W912P826RA022

## Summary

Work consisit of inspection, maintenance, and repairs to hull, steel structures, dredging equipment, propulsion system, and auxiliary systems aboard the Dredge Wheeler. The contractor must provide all necessary labor, supervision, tools, supplies, materials, equipment, machine shop facilities, and transportation (except those items identified as government-furnished in Section C-5 of the PWS), to perform the scope of work, as described.

## Summary

Synopsis: All correspondence should reference Notice ID 80NSSC26930623Q in the subject line. NASA/NSSC has a requirement for ITSM Module ServiceNow Staff Augmentation & Impact Guided Renewal. NASA/NSSC intends to issue a sole source contract to Carahsoft Technology Corp 11493 Sunset Hills RD STE 100 Reston, Virginia 20190-5230 United States under the authority of FAR 12.102(a). The Government intends to acquire Commercial items or services using the provisions, clauses and procedures prescribed in FAR Part 12 for Commercial The NAICS Code for this procurement is 513210.

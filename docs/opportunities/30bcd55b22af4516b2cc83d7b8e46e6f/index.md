## Summary

Federal Bureau of Prisons, Federal Correctional Institution (FCI) Schuylkill located in Minersville, Pennsylvania intends to make a single award for a quantity of four (4) AERCO SPDW42 WWP91A SmartPlate Double Wall Water-to Water Heater, with SP 150 psi kit and Communications Board. Domestic Water Pressure Drop 8 PSIG @ max. rated flow, Ambient Operating Temperature 23F to 113F, Electrical Requirements 120/1/60 Hz 2 Amp, 220/1/50 Hz 2 Amp, Standby Amperage Draw 2 Amp, High Limit "Tripped" Amperage Draw 2 Amp, Max Continuous Water Flow Rate 90 GPM, Max Boiler Water Pressure & Temperature 150 PSIG @ 250F, Max Domestic Water Operating Pressure 200 PSIG, Adjustable Temperature Control up to 180F, Adjustable High Limit Control up to 200F. This award is for supply of equipment only. Installation is not needed. Please attach completed SF1449 and quote cover sheet. Please submit quotes to Stephanie Sandler via email to ssandler@bop.gov.

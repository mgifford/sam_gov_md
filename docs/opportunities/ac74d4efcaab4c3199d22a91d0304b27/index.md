# Technology Test and Evaluation Division (TTED) Technology Evaluations Support Services

## Summary

This is a Combined Synopsis / Solicitation for Commercial Items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6 – Streamlined Procedures for Evaluation and Solicitation of Commercial Items – as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This solicitation is a Request for Quotations (RFQ). The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular 2026-01, effective March 13, 2026. The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 541990 – All Other Professional, Scientific, and Technical Services with a small business size standard of $19.5 Million. This acquisition is being procured as a 100% small business set-aside; all qualified small business concerns capable of providing the required services are eligible. FAR Part 12 and acquisition procedures will be utilized. The Government intends to award one or more Indefinite Delivery/ Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract(s) to provide Technology Evaluations Support Services for the Technology Test and Evaluation Division (TTED) at the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST). The maximum cumulative amount of all orders placed under the awarded IDIQ contract shall be $7,400,000.00. See attached SF1449 and its attachments for pertinent details of this solicitation. Please note that this is a draft solicitation and the final solicitation is expected to be issued on or around April 23, 2026.

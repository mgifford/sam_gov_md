# Request for Information (RFI) for Industrial Non-Regulated Wastewater and/or Stormwater Containment and Temporary Storage Tank Types (High Capacity)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-24 09:32:52.835-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC26PFRAC

## Summary

Request for Information (RFI) for Industrial Non-Regulated Wastewater and/or Stormwater Containment and Temporary Storage Tank Types (High Capacity) Response Requested Not Later Than 09 April 2026 @ 1600 CST Introduction: McAlester Ammunition Plant is seeking information from industry on Frac Tanks and/or similar tank types to understand of market availability, capabilities and identify qualified vendors. Example Frac Tank Description and Specifications included below. All interested and qualified businesses (i.e., Small Business, Other than Small) are encouraged to submit your information. Requirements: McAlester Ammunition Plant is seeking industry businesses and information on tank types, descriptions, and specifications, with consideration to alternative solutions to meet conditions presented. Industrial Non-Hazardous Wastewater and/or Stormwater Containment Tank Types (High Capacity) holding tank option types that are portable, examples such as, polyethylene tanks, steel skid-mounted tanks, or modular units designed for temporary storage, pumping, and hauling. Potential solutions, design, tanks for large-scale storage, ensuring compliance with non-hazardous environmental regulations during industrial processes. Present and Identify Multiple Pricing Structures available for Frac Tanks, Alternate Tank Types, and/or Potential Equal Alternative(s) not listed. Include solutions, conditions or considerations not identified. Identify Multiple Pricing Structures: Frac Tank/Alternative Purchase; Frac Tank/Alternative Lease to Purchase; Frac Tank/Alternative Rental Agreement, Other Solution Types. Examples such as: Quantity (8) – New, Delivery, set-up, direct purchase. Quantity (8) – New, Delivery, set-up, base, plus four option years with one 6-month extension. Quantity (8) – New, Delivery, set-up, base, plus four option years with one 6-month extension duration and option to purchase at the end of the performance period. *Optional Add-On’s: *Warranty *Option to increase/decrease quantity of tanks as needed. *Maintenance/Service, identify and perform "normal and routine preventative maintenance and repair. *Other not identified Specifications and Features: Minimum Capacity: 500 BBL / ~21,000 Gallons Structural Material: Corrugated or Smooth steel walls Design: V-bottom or Round-bottom for easy clean-out and complete drainage Top: Closed Top Mobility: Tires, Air Brakes, Fifth-Wheel Kingpin, Skid-Mounted or Axle-Mounted. Valves & Piping: Configuration for high-flow liquid transfer and easy maintenance in industrial settings. Safety: Pressure/Vacuum Relief Vents, Interior Coatings, and Chemical Resistance. Submission Requirements: Company Overview: Brief overview of the company, including its experience and expertise. Products or Services Overview: Detailed description of the proposed product(s) or services the company offers, including technical specifications. Pricing: Identify purchase option(s) and pricing breakdown structure, term, warranty, and options. Performance Data: Past performance and capabilities. Submission should be organized in accordance with the instructions in this document to permit a thorough and accurate review. Responses to this RFI must be submitted via electronic mail no later than 09 April 2026 at 4:00PM, CST. Points of Contact: The point of contact for this submission is: Andrea Jones, andrea.jones36.civ@army.mil Security: This RFI is unclassified. Do not include any classified information submitted in response to this RFI. Proprietary information should be clearly marked. Disclaimer: THIS REQUEST FOR INFORMATION IS SOLELY FOR INFORMATIONAL AND PLANNING PURPOSES AND DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A SOLICITATION. In accordance with FAR 15.101(c), Requests for Information. The Government does not currently intend to award a contract, but wants to obtain delivery, market, or capabilities information for planning purposes. Responses to RFIs are not offers and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. The Government does not intend to award a contract on the basis of the RFI or otherwise pay for the information requested; and Responses will be treated as information only and not as a proposal. Information received in response to an RFI must be safeguarded adequately from unauthorized disclosure. Contracting officers shall mark the information with date and time of receipt and provide the information to designated officials.

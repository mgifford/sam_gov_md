# Health and Life Insurance service for LE staff

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 05:54:02.45-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AL6026R0001

## Summary

Please note this solicitation is not ready to be issued as of this date. The Embassy of the United States in Albania will launch the solicitation on the SAM.gov when it is available. Post anticipates awarding a contract for Health and Life insurance services for locally hired employees of the Embassy of the United States of America located in Tirana, Albania. Premiums will be based on the number of individuals covered. Health Insurance: There are approximately 250 Locally Hired Employees with approximately 435 eligible dependents for Health Insurance. Life Insurance: There are approximately 250 Locally Hired Employees Please note that these are only estimated quantities. The anticipated performance period is for a base year, with four one-year option periods, if exercised by the Government. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, which will be considered. The Government may award the contract based on the initial offer without discussion. The resultant contract will be a firm fixed price type contract. The Government is planning a pre-quotation conference and the date for this conference will be announced in the solicitation. All contractors shall be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to submitting an offer pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. Therefore, prospective offerors are encouraged to register prior to the submittal of quotations/proposals. This opportunity is subject to 52.229-12 - Tax on Certain Foreign Procurements Electronic Submission/Responses: This opportunity allows for electronic responses. Please submit your offer prior to the response date and time. It is important to make sure the submission is made in specific size and format; in MS-Word 2007/2010 or MS-Excel 2007/2010 or Adobe Acrobat (pdf) file format. The file size must not exceed 30MB. If the file size should exceed the 30MB, the submission must be made in separate files and attached to separate emails with less than 30MB each.

# Radio Redesign Integration and Test Effort

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 11:25:03.967-04
- Solicitation Number: N00019-26-RFPREQ-WPM280-0124

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) intends to issue a sole source Delivery Order (DO) to Raytheon Company, (Raytheon), 1151 E Hermans Road, Tucson, AZ 85756-9367 for the integration of the redesigned Integrated Single Box Solution (ISBS) radio, original part number 3123AS5000-1 into the Tomahawk All-Up-Round (AUR), under Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA), N0001925G0009 as a Cost-Plus-Fixed-Fee (CPFF) procurement. The scope includes hardware and software development, changes to meet new requirements and the addition of alternate parts to drawings where applicable, test, qualification, Special Test Equipment (STE) updates, Automated Test Equipment (ATE) updates, release to production, and National Security Agency (NSA) Information Assurance (IA) certifications. Additionally, support of the prime contractor’s and Government’s platform integration within scope. This contract is being pursued on a sole source basis under the statutory authority of 10 U.S.C. § 3204(a)(l) as implemented by Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subpart 6.302-1, only one responsible source and no other services will satisfy agency requirements. Raytheon is the sole designer, developer, and manufacturer of Tomahawk, and as such, is the only known source available with the requisite technical knowledge, experience, and tooling to produce the requirements described herein to satisfy the Government's requirement. Unless otherwise stated herein, no formal request for proposal or additional written information is currently available. Request for same will be disregarded. This notice is not a request for competitive proposals. All responsible sources may submit a capability statement which shall be considered by the agency. Subcontracting opportunities should be sought directly from Raytheon at the following address: (a) Raytheon Company, Raytheon, 1151 E Hermans Road, Tucson, AZ 85756-9367 (b) Attn: Mr. Patrick Marchant (c) email: patrick.j.marchant@rtx.com. IMPORTANT ADDITIONAL INFORMATION FOR INTERESTED PARTIES: There are no commitments by the Government to issue any solicitations, make any award or awards, or to be responsible for any monies expended by any interested parties before award of a contract for the efforts mentioned above. Information provided herein is subject to change and in no way binds the Government to solicit for or award a contract. No telephone responses will be accepted. Point of contact for this synopsis is Jordan Sabedra. Responses to this synopsis should be sent by email to jordan.m.sabedra.civ@us.navy.mil.

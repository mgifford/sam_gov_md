# Generator Repair

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 16:30:40.279-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAT26Q0026

## Summary

This solicitation is for the repair of a Kohler Diesel Generator, Model Number: 230RE0ZJE. The scope includes the removal and replacement of the blown circuit board and fuse, necessary to programming and testing to ensure proper functionality of the unit. The project is located at: Acton Work Center 33708 Crown Valley Rd. Acton, CA 93510 A pre-bid tour for this project is not scheduled, however the site is open for to visit and is encouraged. Questions should be submitted by 3/27/26 to christopher.malley@usda.gov Quotes are due by 7 April 2026 @ 5:00 PM PT and may be e-mailed to: Government Point of Contact: Christopher Malley, CS at christopher.malley@usda.gov

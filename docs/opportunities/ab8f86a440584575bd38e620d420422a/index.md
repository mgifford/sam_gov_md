# Destruction of SAP hardware

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:05:56.284-04
- Solicitation Number: 2026OL01

## Summary

HNJT-OL requires the destruction of 8,000 lbs of SAP IT Hardware. Facility must be a Department of the Air Force approved IT sanitation facility Minimum standards to meet sanitization requirements IAW DAF SAP IT Sanitization Standard Operating Procedure, minimum standards to meet sanitization requirements are: Incineration: 670 degrees Celsius with two exceptions. Solid State Drives (SSD): 500 degrees Celsius Optical storage device: 600 degrees Celsius Smelting: 1204 degrees Celsius Disintegration to particles that are nominally 2 millimeters (mm) in size.

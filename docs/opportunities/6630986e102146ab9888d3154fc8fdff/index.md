## Summary

The U.S. Coast Guard intends to award a sole source contract to NW Propeller Operations INC. This procurement is being conducted under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b), which permits sole source awards when only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. NW Propeller Operations INC is the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and possesses proprietary knowledge, technical expertise, and specialized capabilities that are essential to meet the requirements of this procurement. No other vendor has been identified that can provide the required goods/services with the same level of compatibility, quality, and performance. Key Details: Vendor Name: NW Propeller Operations INC NAICS Code: 336611 This Notice of Intent is not a request for competitive proposals.

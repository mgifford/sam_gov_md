## Summary

Modified posting to compete full and open. This is a Brand Name requirement for Modicon M580 Programmable Logic Controller (PLC) Equipment. This procurement is full and open competition. All correspondence should reference Notice ID 80NSSC26929072Q in the subject line. NOTE: Hardware purchased must be identical to the make and model of equipment currently in use (listed below), is new (never used) in the original packaging and not refurbished. Schneider Electric only sells PLC automation hardware through authorized distributors who can guarantee the source, condition, safety, security and warranty of the PLC automation hardware. NOTE: The vendor shall be Authorized/Certified through the Schneider Electric Alliance Industrial Automation Distributor (IAD) program. Verification in SE’s IAD and verification as an authorized distributor can be confirmed at https://www.se.com/us/en/partners/distributors/industrial-automation/. See attachments for complete details for the RFQ, Revised Statement of Work, and Redacted Brand Name Justification.

OBJECTIVE: The objective of this notice is to conduct market research to determine if there are Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) or Veteran Owned Small Business (VOSB) sources, or any other Small Businesses capable of providing HVAC System Controls & Services for the Omaha VA Medical Center (OMVA), 4101 Woolworth Ave, Omaha, NE 68105 DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE: HVAC System Controls & Services. PLACES OF PERFORMANCE: Omaha VA Medical Center, 4101 Woolworth Ave, Omaha, NE 68105 POTENTIAL SCOPE OF WORK: Examination, cleaning, and recalibration of sensors/thermostats, humidity controls, temperature controls, pressure controls, relays, damper actuators, instrumental and accessories directly pertaining to the automatic temperature control system. Check and verify performance of temperature controls equipment in accordance with manufacturer's specifications, original design criteria, and proper maintenance practice. Check and confirm control system sequence of operation to ensure optimum system efficiency and economy. Lubricate as required air compressors, control valve packing glands, damper bearings and linkages as well as other accessories directly pertaining to the control system. Inspect automatic temperature control valves for proper stroke, close off, and packing conditions. POTENTIAL CERTIFICATIONS AND LICENSURE: The Contractor shall be an authorized service provider of the entire existing Schneider Electric EcoStruxure System throughout the period of performance. The Contractor shall provide written proof of status as an authorized service provider from the corporate headquarters.

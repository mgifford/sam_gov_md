## Summary

SEE ATTACHED FOR RESPONSES TO INQUIRIES SUBMITTED PRIOR TO THE QUESTIONS DEADLINE ****Under this notice, the United States Army Corps of Engineers - Chicago District is issuing a Request for Quotation (RFQ) the supply and delivery of approved armor stone materials of the specified gradations, rock types, and sizes to Manistee, MI. The Contractor will deliver the stone materials acquired by this purchase by means of truck delivery or marine transportation. The contractor will also supply a stone dock meeting USACE Fleet Requirements for the delivery and storage of these materials. ****GOVERNMENT REQUIREMENTS: In this acquisition, the Government is seeking the supply and delivery of approved armor stone materials of the specified gradations, rock types, and sizes to Manistee, MI. The Contractor will deliver the stone materials acquired by this purchase by means of truck delivery or marine transportation. The contractor will also supply a stone dock meeting USACE Fleet Requirements for the delivery and storage of these materials.

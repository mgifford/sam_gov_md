# NAF Pest Control

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 12:06:04.416-04
- Solicitation Number: FA468626NAFPEST

## Summary

Amendment 002: The purpose of Amendment 002 is to incorporate Q&As via Attachment 6. RFQ Q&As NAF Pest Control. Amendment 001: The purpose of Amendment 001 is to extend the solicitation due date from 10 April 2026 at 10:00 AM PDT to 16 April 2026 at 10:00 AM PDT. All other terms and conditions remain unchanged. The 9th Force Support Squadron (9 FSS) at Beale Air Force Base, California, is issuing the attached Request for Quote (RFQ) to establish a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for Nonappropriated Fund (NAF) Pest Control Services. The Government requires a qualified contractor to provide pest control services, including routine management, emergency response, and specialized treatments for various facilities at Beale AFB, in accordance with the attached Performance Work Statement (PWS). Contract Vehicle: The Government intends to award a single BPA with a total performance period of five (5) years. Individual Call Limit: $25,000.00 Total BPA Ceiling: $300,000.00 Guarantee: This BPA does not guarantee a minimum amount of work and the Government is only obligated to the extent of authorized calls placed against the agreement. Quotes are due no later than 10:00 AM PST, 16 April 2026. All questions and final quote submissions must be sent electronically to Cali McVay, Contracting Officer, at cali.mcvay.1@us.af.mil and Contract Specialist, Cedric Mejia, at cedric_ayvan.mejia@us.af.mil.

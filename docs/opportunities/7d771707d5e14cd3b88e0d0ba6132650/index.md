# Amendment 1: Call 001_Air Force - Small UAS & Asymmetric Capabilities - Commercial Solutions Opening

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 16:20:45.838-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8629-25-S-C001(Call-001)

## Summary

This is Amendment 1 to the initial announcement. This purpose of this amendment is: Clarify the anticipated contract vehicle. The Government anticipates the award of a Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) based contract as a result of this Call. This Call will not result in an award of an Other Transaction (OT) agreement for prototype. All offerors should prepare their submissions with the understanding that any resultant award will be a FAR-based contract. Extend the Solution Brief Submission Deadline. The due date for responses is hereby extended from 20 April, 2026 to 23 April, 2026. Provide a Revised Solicitation Document. A conformed copy of the CSO Call, incorporating the changes noted above, is provided as an attachment. All other terms and conditions of the original Call 001 remain unchanged. This notice supplements the Air Force Small UAS & Asymmetric Capabilities – Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO), Notice ID: FA8629-25-S-C001, available at [https://sam.gov/workspace/contract/opp/b14ba8e84f1543bdba1f0169cdee211a/view]. It announces the solicitation of Call 001, seeking innovative commerical solutions and technologies to advance small UAS and asymmetric mission capabilites. Details are provided in the attachment.

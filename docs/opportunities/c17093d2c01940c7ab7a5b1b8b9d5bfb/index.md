# PSNS & IMF SIOP Move B147/367 Warehouse & B445 Classroom/Office

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 18:00:38.506-04
- Solicitation Number: N4523A26Q1305

## Summary

The Regional Maintenance Center at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard & Intermediate Maintenance Facility (PSNS&IMF) intends to award a standalone, Firm-Fixed Price contract to remove equipment and material from buildings to provide space for construction activities. Noun Name: Moving services Description: The Navy is undertaking an effort to revitalize and optimize industrial infrastructure at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard and Intermediate Maintenance Facility (PSNS&IMF). To support upcoming military construction projects, PSNS&IMF must remove equipment and material from buildings to provide space for construction activities. This requirement will relocate Government equipment and materials in two main efforts. First, relocate warehouse equipment and materials from PSNS&IMF CIA Building 147 and/or 367 to a government controlled leased storage facility. Second, relocate classroom/office furniture and materials less than 2-miles from Building 445 to Building 502 located on Naval Base Kitsap Scope: Contractor will relocate all equipment and materials identified by Contracting Officer Representative (COR) up to the maximum quantity outlined in the approved vendor quote. Contractors will provide suitable transportation vehicles, lifting and handling equipment, and all packing and moving supplies required to relocate equipment and materials. A full description of the project scope is available in the attached Performance Work Statement (PWS). Period of Performance - 7 May 2026 – 31 Dec 2026 Please complete pricing on attached combined synopsis solicitation document and submit required documents by 16 Apr 2026, 9:00 am PST. Questions are due by 10 Apr 2026 at 9:00 am PST. This is a 100% Small Business set-aside under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) 484210, size standard $34M. All offerors must be registered with the System for Award Management (SAM) to receive award of government contracts. offerors are advised that SAM representations and certifications can be completed electronically via the System for Award Management website at https://www.sam.gov

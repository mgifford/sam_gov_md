## Summary

U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) is conducting market research to procure of a total of 285 All-Terrain Vehicle (ATV) units. This is Request for Information (RFI) released pursuant to the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul, Part 10 - Market Research. This RFI's purpose is to obtain market information on viable sources, answers to specific questions, and industry comments relating to the attached full RFI.

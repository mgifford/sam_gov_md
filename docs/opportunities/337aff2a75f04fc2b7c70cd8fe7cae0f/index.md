# Document Destruction Services

- Agency: NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS BOARD
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 15:41:10.297-04
- Solicitation Number: 63NLRB26Q0003

## Summary

The RFQ Questions and Answers have been posted as an attachment. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. The NLRB’s Office of the Chief Information Officer has identified a requirement for document destruction services at NLRB Headquarters and its Field Offices located throughout the United States to include Hawaii and Puerto Rico. Secure paper destruction and recycling ("shredding") is a prominent practice among businesses and Federal agencies concerned with the proper shredding and recycling of sensitive information, such as Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

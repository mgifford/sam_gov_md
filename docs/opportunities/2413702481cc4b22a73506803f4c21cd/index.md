## Summary

The Office of Naval Intelligence (ONI) has a requirement for evaluation-driven, mission-aligned artificial intelligence (AI) applications and systems that accelerate analyst workflows in areas critical to national security. Submit responses to Ryan Hodges, Contract Specialist, via email to ryan.t.hodges.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 31 March 2026 at 1500 CT. Telephone inquiries will not be accepted or acknowledged, and no feedback or evaluations will be provided to companies regarding their submissions. Please reference N61331-26-SN-Q18 as your email subject line. Review the attached document in its entirety for information pertaining to this notice.

# Cut Log Removal - Bird/Animal Strike Hazard (BASH)

- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 16:13:12.536-05

## Summary

SEE BELOW FOR UPDATE - SITE VISIT The government is seeking log removal services to support aircraft safety requirements, outlined in Department of the Air Force Instruction (DAFI) 91-212, on Selfridge ANGB, MI. Selfridge ANGB has log piles from previous tree cuttings that have become environmental and safety hazards. These piles not only create visual blight but have also become habitats for various local species, posing a Bird/Wildlife Aircraft Strike Hazard (BASH) concern near the airfield. The Contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and quality control necessary to remove all logs located within a designated area to support Selfridge Air National Guard Base (ANGB), Michigan, flying safety as defined in this Description of Service (DOS).

# Sprint 1 FPV

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 16:17:02.224-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226QA061

## Summary

The contractor shall provide commercial, brand-name-or-equal components to support FPV Sprint 1 v2 systems, including solid-state lithium-ion drone batteries, chargers, battery checkers, XT60-to-XT90 connectors, CW/CCW propellers, toolkits, and fire-retardant battery bags. All items must meet or exceed the salient characteristics and must be compatible with FPV Sprint 1 v2.

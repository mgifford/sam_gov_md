## Summary

Solicitation Number: THERE IS NO SOLICITATION AT THIS TIME Notice Type: RFI / Sources Sought Requisition Number: RFISS-169- KB Dock/Keyport Piers Ladder Replacement Synopsis: The Naval Undersea Warfare Center Division, Keyport, is issuing this sources sought notice as a means of conducting market research for the removal of thirteen (13) existing, twenty-foot ladder extensions that are shackled to existing pier ladders, and the installation of twelve (12) new, twenty-foot aluminum ladders to be shackled in place. The new ladders should be constructed from 6061 marine-grade aluminum, with material comparable to Schedule 40, 2-inch square tubing. This request for capability information does not constitute a Request for Proposal (RFP); submission of any information in response to this announcement is purely voluntary and the Government assumes no financial responsibility for any costs incurred.

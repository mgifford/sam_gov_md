# Security Services, Cape Cod Canal, Bourne, MA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:57:49.986-04
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA087

## Summary

THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Furnish all management, supervision, labor, materials, and equipment necessary to provide armed security guard services for the Cape Cod Canal Field Office in Bourne, Massachusetts. Proof of Contractor's "Watch, Guard, or Patrol Agency" licensure from the Massachusetts State Police Certification Unit is required. This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 561612 with a Small Business Size Standard of $29 Million. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

## Summary

This notice replaces notice no. 19GE5026Q0027, which is hereby cancelled. The Regional Procurement Support Office, Frankfurt provides the following Pre-Solicitation Notice regarding the issuance of a Request for Quotes (RFQ) for: New ABA compliant exterior elevator at the Bilateral CMR - U.S. Embassy Vienna, Austria The Regional Procurement Support Office in Frankfurt, Germany, intends to issue a Firm Fixed-Price purchase order during the second quarter of calendar year 2026. The successful contractor shall provide complete construction services, project coordination, and supervision, including but not limited to the management and construction necessary to meet the requirements of the purchase order within the established timeframe. The U.S. Embassy Vienna, Austria has a requirement for a new exterior elevator at the Bilateral CMR to ensure ABA compliant accessibility to both floors of the building. The work includes site preparation, structural modifications to the CMR, installation of a code-compliant elevator system, weatherproofing, electrical and safety system integration, and all necessary finishes to ensure seamless access and operation. The floors served by the elevator will be: - Exterior patio level - First Floor lobby - Second Floor hallway The work includes site preparation, excavation and foundations, structural modifications to the CMR, construction of a steel and glass enclosure, installation of a code-compliant elevator system, weatherproofing, mechanical, electrical and plumbing work, safety system integration, and all necessary finishes to ensure seamless access and operation. The magnitude of this project is anticipated to be between $500,000 and $2,000,000. Companies interested in the RFQ should express their interest by written correspondence, no later than April 13, 2026, at the following email addresses: baltaa@state.gov and gallonea@state.gov - and must include the following information: Name of Firm, Name, Telephone, Email Address of Point of Contact and Complete Physical and Mailing Address of Firm. Please do not telephone. Firms shall be knowledgeable in written and spoken English. The RFQ package/documents shall not be placed on SAM.gov.

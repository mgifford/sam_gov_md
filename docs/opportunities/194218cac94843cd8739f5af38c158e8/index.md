# DroneGun Mk4, Repair Kit, Immediate Response Kit, & Magnetic Antenna Kit

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 13:32:38.518-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4497-26

## Summary

The Security Forces Squadron on Dover Air Force Base intends to award a brand name, firm-fixed-price contract to Droneshield for 4 Items: Drone Gun Mk4 5865-66-167-4802, RfPatrol Mk2 5865-66-167-4795, Immediate Response Kit 5865-66-167-4802, and Drone Gun Repair Kit, or equal. Based on market research conducted, the government has determined that only one responsible source is available to meet this requirement. If you believe your products meet the specifications of the listed items above, please sumbit fully comprehensive specifications sheets or capabilities in PDF format. Please provide information if the NAICS/PSC is correct or if a different NAICS/PSC is a better fit. Please specify the different NAICS/PSC, if applicable, and explain why. The type of solicitation to be issued will depend upon the responses to this pre solicitation notice. The Government must determine and ensure there is adequate competition among the potential pool of interested, capable companies or if this a brand name requirement. All responses will be due by May 29, 2026 by 1:00 P.M EST. Responses are to be emailed to SSgt Christine Parsons at christine.parsons.1@us.af.mil AND CC 436CONS.PKB.ORG@us.af.mil. Negative responses are not required.

## Summary

(T&E) sea turtle species within the project footprint of the St. Johns County (SJC) Shore Protection Project (SPP) (St. Augustine Beach). The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code assigned to this procurement is 541620, Environmental Consulting Services. The Florida Fish and Wildlife Conservation Commission (FWC) implements a program regulating permits for marine turtle-related activities. Only one marine turtle permit is issued per beach to manage impacts on turtle habitats. This approach ensures responsible management of marine turtle populations in Florida. Coastal Conservation Group LLC is the only Marine Turtle Permit Holder for the St. Johns County locations listed in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). Therefore, this requirement will be solicited using other than full and open competition in accordance with the procedures of FAR 13.106-l(b)(l). No solicitation will be posted for this requirement.

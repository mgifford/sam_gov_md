## Summary

This is a source(s) sought notice only and it is not a solicitation announcement. The purpose of this sources sought notice is to gain knowledge of qualified potential contractors and their socio-economic size classification in accordance with NAICS code 811310 - Commercial and Industrial Machinery and Equipment (except Automotive and Electronic) Repair and Maintenance. The Nebraska / Western Iowa VA Health Care System at Omaha, NE and Grand Island, NE is seeking a Contractor to provide UST-AST (Underground Storage Tanks and Above ground Storage Tanks) Corrections. Responses will be used by the Government to make appropriate acquisition decisions. Response to Request To respond to this source(s) sought notice please return via email to the Contract Specialist, Evan Beachy, Email: evan.beachy@va.gov your company information (including DUNS Number and SAM Unique Entity ID) and capability statement by 12:00PM (Noon) Central Time March 13,2026. Please also include information if you company is a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), Veteran Owned Small Business (VOSB), Small Business, Small Disadvantage Business, Women-owned Small Business, Economically Disadvantaged Small Business, HUBZone, 8(a) Certified or Large Business. Please identify if this procurement may be procured through your GSA Contract, FSS Contract, SEWP Contract or any other Contract that allows orders to be places against it. This is not a solicitation and in no way obligates the Government to award any contract. Additionally, a response to this source(s) sought notice is not a request to be added to a prospective bidders list or to receive a copy of the solicitation. The purpose of this sources sought notice is strictly for market research purposes. This source(s) sought posting is issued solely for information and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation. All information received in response to this source (s)sought that is marked as proprietary will be handled accordingly. In accordance with FAR 15.201(e), responses to this notice are not offers and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. Responders are solely responsible for all expenses associated with responding to this source(s) sought.

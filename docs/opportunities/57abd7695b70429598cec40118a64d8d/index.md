# DFAS Rome HVAC Maintenance and Repair Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:59:30.809-04
- Solicitation Number: HQ042326QE014

## Summary

Perform Heating, Ventilation Air Conditioning (HVAC) unit maintenance service, and provide HVAC repairs on Trane brand equipment, including the Trane Tracer Building Automation System and Trane Programmable Controllers. These systems require Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) authorized, factory certified technicians with access to proprietary Trane diagnostic tools, software platforms, and system controls, to properly perform maintenance, troubleshooting, configuration, and repair in accordance with manufacturer requirements.

# PN: 636-26-306 Replace HVAC Systems Building 6 & 8

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 13:51:56.376-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C26326Q0456

## Summary

The VA Nebraska-Western Iowa Medical Health Care System, Omaha VA Medical Center, needs to replace the existing HVAC R-410A split system in building 6 with a new R-410A system and in building 8 replace the HVAC unit with a new R-454B system, to meet A2L requirements. Additionally, a new mini-split system will be installed in the basement of building 6 to provide cooling in the mechanical room.

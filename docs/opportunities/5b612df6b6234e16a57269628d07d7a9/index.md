# MATOC IDIQ Catering Food Services- Philippines

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 03:22:47.991-04
- Solicitation Number: W91QVP26QA001

## Summary

The 413th Contracting Support Brigade (CSB), 921st Contracting Battalion intends to award a multiple Firm Fixed Price Indefinite Delivery/ Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract for Catering Food Service in Philippines. The NAICS code is 722320 – Caterers. This competitive procurement is full and open competition. The service period of performance (POP) will be five years including five 12-month ordering periods.

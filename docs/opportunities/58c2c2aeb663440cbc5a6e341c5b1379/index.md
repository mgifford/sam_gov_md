STATEMENT OF WORK (SOW) FOR Allied Dual Point Davit Flood Light parts Flood lights are obsolete on the WMSL’s dual point davit system and LRE/Allied need to identify and validate a suitable replacement that meets operational, safety, and NVIS-friendly lighting requirements. The objective is to ensure continued mission readiness and maintainability of davit operations without disruption to cutter boat launch/recovery capability. OBJECTIVE The goal is to procure six (6) complete replacement kits for the dual point davit (DPD) flood lights on the remaining six WMSL cutters. The kits must meet all operational, safety, and NVIS (Night Vision Imaging System)-friendly lighting requirements as specified below. Item Description Part Number/ ID Number Kit, Replacement; NVIS Lighting 103929 Kit comes with 2 white, 2 NVIS lights, mounting brackets, and wire harness. The company shall supply and deliver six (6) Allied Dual Point Davit Flood Light Replacement Kits, part number 103929. Each kit must include the following components: 2 White Flood Lights 2 NVIS-Compatible Flood Lights Mounting Brackets (sufficient for installation of all lights) Wire Harness (suitable for integration with existing davit system) All components must be new, unused, and fully compatible with the WMSL dual point davit system. The NVIS lights must be compliant with Coast Guard NVIS requirements and suitable for operations involving night vision equipment. Delivery address is below.

# Janitorial Services for San Salvador Embassy Compound and Official Residences

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 16:25:44.759-04
- Solicitation Number: 19ES6026R0203

## Summary

Dear interested vendor, The US Embassy in San Salvador has a requirement to procure Janitorial services for its Compound in Santa Elena, Antiguo Cuscatlan, La Libertad Este, and for two official residences, one located inside the compound and the other one in San Benito, San Salvador, San Salvador Centro. It is the Government's intention to issue a Firm-Fixed price type contract. The Contractor shall provide labor, personnel, supervision and any other items necessary to perform these services. The proposed acquisition is a full and open competitive solicitation. Request for Proposal (RFP) 19ES6026R0203 will be posted on or about April 16, 2026 and made available via the Contracting Opportunities web site at sam.gov.

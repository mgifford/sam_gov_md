## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, North Florida/South Georgia Veterans Health System (NF/SGVHS), requires document destruction services for multiple medical centers and associated facilities. The contractor shall provide secure collection, handling, and destruction of sensitive paper records, including provision of locked containers and scheduled servicing in accordance with applicable VA and federal requirements. Services will be performed at locations throughout North Florida and South Georgia. Additional details are provided in the attached combined synopsis/solicitation.

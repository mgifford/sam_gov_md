# Synopsis for Project Manager Mounted Armored Vehicles Logistic Fielding Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-08 14:24:33.621-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CH-25-R-0182-Synopsis

## Summary

DISTRIBUTION STATEMENT A. Approved for public release; distribution is unlimited. Synopsis: ACC-DTA proposes to award a new five-year, single-award, IDIQ contract to provide PM MAV Logistic Fielding Support Services (LFSS) for the Armored Multi-Purpose Vehicle Family of Vehicles (AMPV FoV), Armored Personnel Carrier M113 FoV, Bradley Fighting Vehicle (BFV) FoV, Multiple Launch Rocket Systems (MLRS), and Combat Recovery systems (CRS) M88 platforms. This service acquisition provides Field Service Representatives (FSR), Vehicle Maintenance/Repair, De-processing, Total Package Fielding, New and Displaced Equipment Training, Modification Work Orders installations, and Program Management. These efforts support the production and deployment and sustainment portion of the acquisition life cycle for both contiguous United States and Outside the contiguous United States locations, including Foreign Military Sales (FMS) cases. The estimated release date of the Request for Proposal, W912CH-25-R-0182, is April 2026. The primary contacts for this RFP are darlamae.d.lasky.civ@army.mil and jill.t.wagner.civ@army.mil. Contracting Office Address: U.S. ARMY CONTRACTING COMMAND, DETROIT ARSENAL, MICHIGAN 48397-5000

## Summary

Join us on Tuesday, May 12 at 1 p.m. ET, to learn more about DAF PAE C3BM! The Department of Air Force Portfolio Acquisition Executive for Command, Control, Communications and Battle Management (DAF PAE C3BM) welcomes you to our quarterly DAF PAE C3BM Engagement Day. This unclassified session will provide valuable insights into our mission and how you can collaborate with us.

# Strong-Bonds Retreat

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 18:11:38.018-04
- Solicitation Number: FA460826QS016

## Summary

To ensure military couples receive the opportunity to grow, strengthen, and renew relationship bonds through guided workshops at local retreat venues. Additionally, ensure single military members receive the opportunity to grow personally, strengthen relationship skills, and develop healthy foundations for sustainable long-term relationships, while also building self-confidence, emotional awareness, and personal resilience as individuals. The three (3) retreats will support holistic well-being and equip participants with practical tools applicable to both personal and professional contexts.

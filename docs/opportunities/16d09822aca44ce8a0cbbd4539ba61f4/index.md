# Anticipated Sole Source Award to Driven Technologies, Inc

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 12:58:34.731-04
- Solicitation Number: F1S0AX6065A001

## Summary

This is a presolicitation notice; this is not a solicitation for a Request for Proposal, Invitation to Bid, or a commitment, implied or otherwise, that AFTC/PZZG will take procurement action in the matter. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this posting or any follow-up information requests. The purpose of this notification is to inform industry of the USAF’s intent of contract action and possibly gain knowledge of other potentially qualified sources and their size classifications (small business, large business, etc.) please do not request a copy of solicitation, as one does not exist. The AFTC Contracting Directorate (AFTC/PZZG), Edwards AFB intends to issue a sole source, Firm-Fixed Price Contract under the authority of 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1) as implemented by RFO 6.103-1, to Aerospace Driven Technologies, Inc, 2807 Catherine WAY Santa Ana, California, 92705-5708, United States. The applicable NAICS is 336413; Other Aircraft Part and Auxiliary Equipment Manufacturing, with a size standard of 1,250 Employees. The following is a brief description of the acquisition requirement for existing F-35 Simulators: P/N: 800878 F-35 Panoramic Center Display (PCD) P/N: 800857 F-35 Standby Flight Display (SFD) The delivery terms will be: FOB Destination/ 5 months After Receipt of Order (ARO) There are no solicitation documents; however, any vendors that believe they can meet the requirements described should submit a complete technical package, which provides clear and convincing evidence that they can meet the Government’s requirement. The response should include any special requirements for a commercial contract (i.e. Commercial Financing, Warranty Provisions, Delivery Information, etc.). All information furnished shall be in writing and must contain sufficient detail to allow the government representative to evaluate and determine if it can meet the requirement. The Air Force Test Center has appointed an Ombudsman to consider, and facilitate, the resolution of concerns from offerors, potential offerors, and others for this acquisition. Concerned parties should address their concerns, issues, disagreements, and/or recommendations to the Contracting Officer for resolution prior to consulting the Ombudsman. Additionally, Department of the Air Force Federal Acquisition Regulation (DAFFARS) 5352.201-9101 – Ombudsman, will be included in any potential solicitation and contract for this acquisition. The AFTC Ombudsman contact information is as follows: Air Force Test Center Vice Commander 1 South Rosamond Blvd, Edwards AFB, CA 93524-3784. Phone Number (661) 277-2810, Facsimile Number (661) 277-7593, Email: aftc.workflow@us.af.mil. Responses must be received no later than (NLT) 10 April 2026. Responses should be submitted via email to: Contract Specialist jamil.minosa.2@us.af.mil and the Contracting Officer anne.beach@us.af.mil.

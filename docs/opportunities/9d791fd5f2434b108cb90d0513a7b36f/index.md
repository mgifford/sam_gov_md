# DIA 2026 Industry Day Announcement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 15:11:23.959-04
- Solicitation Number: DIA_2026_Industry_Day_Announcement

## Summary

The Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA) Senior Acquisition Executive cordially invites you to our upcoming Industry Day. This live event offers our industry partners an opportunity to obtain information on DIA's acquisition direction and the business opportunities that are potentially on the horizon for 2026-2027. This event will be held at the SECRET/NOFORN level, therefore registration is limited to properly cleared US citizens. Event Highlights: Date: Thursday, April 23, 2026 Time: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM Location: DIA Reston 1 Conference Center 12310 Sunrise Valley Drive Reston, VA 20191 Attendees: Limit two (2) per Company Clearance Requirements: Attendees must at a minimum possess an active SECRET security clearance. Reservation: Attendees should email DIAIndustryDay@dodiis.mil and provide: Company Name CAGE Code Small Business (Y/N)? Currently Work with DIA (Y/N)? Last Name First Name Middle Initial IC Badge (Y/N)? If No IC Badge: SSN, DOB, POB Reservation requests containing PII should be password protected, or sent via DoD SAFE. Submissions will be acknowledged via return email with parking, access and more detailed agenda information. Please also be advised that there is a 200-person room capacity limitation so do not delay in responding. Registration will close when the event reaches capacity, or at COB Wednesday, 15 April 2026. If seating remains available after 15 April, only personnel with an IC badge may be added. We look forward to your participation in advancing our shared mission! Best Regards, Defense Intelligence Agency Senior Acquisition Executive

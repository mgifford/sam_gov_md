## Summary

This award is a 10-year, indefinite-delivery indefinate-quantity (IDIQ) contract for Community-Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC) services for eligible Veteran beneficiaries. Services provided in clinicl to be located in Pierre, South Dakota. Award is made under a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) set-aside. Period of Performance: Base Year: 01 July 2026 - 30 June 2027 Nine option years available. If all are exercised, final performance expiration would be 30 June 2036.

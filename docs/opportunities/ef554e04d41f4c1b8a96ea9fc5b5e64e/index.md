# 12 MXG Laser Engraver System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:30:11.633-04
- Solicitation Number: FA301626Q0045

## Summary

The 502d Contracting Squadron, 395 B. Street West, JBSA-Randolph, TX 78150, is issuing a solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with FAR Part 12 and in conjunction with FAR Subpart 8.104. NAICS: 333517 Machine Tool Manufacturing PSC: 3433- Gas Welding, Heat Cutting, and Metalizing Size Standard: 500 Employees The Government intends to award a Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) delivery order for a Laser Engraver. Quotes shall include the unit, extended and total prices. Total CLIN pricing must include shipping and handling. Award will be made IAW the basic contract ordering procedures, the procedures specified in the solicitation and based on the lowest priced quote meeting or exceeding the acceptability standards for non-price factors. The Government will evaluate the three (3) lowest priced quotes for Technical Acceptability. Qoutes shall be valid for a minimum of 30 days.

# Insurance Brokerage Firms

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-16 18:34:59.649-04
- Solicitation Number: 376560

## Summary

Fermi Forward Discovery Group, LLC (FFDG) is a Delaware not-for-profit limited liability company whose sole purpose is to manage and operate the Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (Fermilab) with the United States Department of Energy (DOE). FFDG is seeking sources of a qualified insurance broker to access the best and most competitive markets for commercial insurance coverage. The firm will provide an assessment of the market conditions that will affect the insurance placement of required coverage throughout the year. These coverages include but are not limited to: Commercial General Liability Business Auto Workers Compensation Umbrella / Excess General Liability Builder’s Risk Owner’s Interest Cybersecurity Directors and Officers Fiduciary Employment Practices Property Crime Kidnap & Ransom Fine Arts Drone / Aviation Pollution Liability Other specialty coverages as required The selected firm may also be required to advise FFDG, and provide administrative support for, one or more “wrap-up” insurance programs. If your firm is interested, please email Monica Schultz at mschultz@fnal.gov, include your Company name, point of contact and any email address.

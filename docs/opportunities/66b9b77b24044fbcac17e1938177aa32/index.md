# Z--Repair Fence for BIA, Western Navajo Agency, BOT

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:43:55.007-04
- Solicitation Number: DOIAFBO260034

## Summary

Repair Fence for BIA, Western Navajo Agency, BOT NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD A SOLE SOURCE CONTRACT. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITVE PROPOSALS. The Bureau of Indian Affairs (BIA) intends to enter into a sole source Firm-Fixed Price contract with Buffalo Fence Co. Inc, for the repair of the chain link fence for BIA, Western Navajo Agency, Branch of Transportation, under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b)(1)(i), Contracting officers may solicit from one source if the contracting officer determines that the circumstances of the contract action deem only one source reasonably available (e.g., urgency, exclusive licensing agreements, brand-name or industrial mobilization). No solicitation is available and no technical questions will be answered for this requirement. The NAICS code for this acquisition is 238990. If a firm believes that the BIA should not pursue this procurement, it should submit a capability statement and pricing information for the requirement by 15:00 p.m. MDT on March 27, 2026. Please submit to Lynelle Benallie, Contract Specialist, via email at Lynelle.Benallie@bia.gov.

Solicitation (RFQ) Scope & Requirements Scope Summary: The Puerto Rico Facility serves as a secure location for storing supplies and equipment that support APHIS mission in Puerto Rico and is a staging site for African Swine Fever mission in the Caribbean, The facility holds animal handling equipment (chutes, panels, gates), depopulation carbon dioxide cart, supply trailer, spray pump trailers, utility task vehicle, and supplies/equipment that cannot be shipped directly by vendors to overseas locations. Vendor must provide 1600 SF of warehouse storage (inside and outside) for USDA-VS Fiops D1 PR for equipment for normal operations as well as emergency operations in the Caribbean area. Facility must be secure and not exposed to the public. Must have 24 hour security.

# Regions 01 and 02 A&E Services

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-18 19:13:38.522-04
- Solicitation Number: 1165974

## Summary

The purpose of this notice is to obtain information regarding: 1) Availability and capability of qualified sources 2) Availability of capable firms to provide the contemplated requirement listed below The information provided herein is for informational purposes only. THIS NOTICE IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL. This notice is issued for information and planning purposes only and does not itself constitute a Request for Proposal (RFP). The Government does not intend to award a contract based solely on responses to this Sources Sought Notice. This notice acts as fulfillment of public synopsis & GPE posting requirements set forth in both FAR Part 5 & 13.501. The USDA Forest Service Regions 01 and 02 anticipate awarding contracts for procuring Architecture and Engineering Services which include, but are not limited to architecture, civil, structural, mechanical, electrical, electrical, electrical, geotechnical and landscape survey and design, inspection services, and construction management services. These services are being sought under NAICS codes 541330 and 541310; Firms may be registered under either one or both of these codes. Response Format: The Forest Service is conducting this Sources Sought to identify small business contractors who possess the capabilities to provide services as specified above and to determine their availability, capability, and adequacy. Interested small businesses under the referenced NAICS codes are invited to submit their interest by 4PM Eastern Time on April 02, 2026.

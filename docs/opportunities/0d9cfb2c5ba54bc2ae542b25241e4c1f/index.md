# Touch Labor Support Services for Ultra High Pressure Operators

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-26 12:30:22.861-04
- Solicitation Number: N3904026UHPNOTICE

## Summary

Portsmouth Naval Shipyard is seeking to procure touch labor services for Ultra High Pressure Operators, along with On-Site Supervisors and a Program manager, to assist with the execution of Fast Attack Submarine overhaul, modernization, and repair work. PNS will require 10 experienced, skilled, and qualified UHP operators, 2 supervisors, and 1 program manager. The period of performance for this requirement is Jun 1, 2026- September 25, 2026. Please see the statement of work, and manning table for more details. The Government intends to solicit this for 14 days. All interested parties should start preparing their respective proposals now.

# C-130 Ground Power Unit Spares

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-23 08:14:31.543-04
- Solicitation Number: N6833525Q0173
- Awardee: TRIMAN INDUSTRIES INC Morristown NJ 07960 USA
- Award Amount: 50038.07

## Summary

The Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division Lakehurst, NJ awarded a Purchase Order type contract to Triman Industries Inc. (CAGE: 0ZBE8) for the procurement of various spare items in support of the KC-130 Ground Power Unit under FMS Case KU-P-GGY. This requirement was awarded on a limited competition basis between the Original Equipment Manufacturer, Essex Electro Engineers (CAGE: 21269), and its authorized distributors in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1), FAR 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. This requirement was synopsized under Notice ID N6833525Q0173.

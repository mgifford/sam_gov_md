## Summary

DLA Disposition Services LAMS Tent Maintenance This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items/services prepared in accordance with FAR Part 12. The required equipment is per the attached specifications, including its delivery timeframes. The procurement is under NAICS 811310 with a size standard of $12.5 million. The requirement is a small business set-aside. Quotes are to be received no later than 2:00 p.m. Eastern Standard U.S. Time on the above specified date, via email only to the designated Contracting Officer. See Attachment for the Price Schedule and Specifications/Additional Terms. All contractor performance shall be in a safe manner, and in compliance with all applicable Federal, state, and local laws and regulations, and all installation policies. Any/all required documentation and service-related requirements including installation access are the responsibility of the contractor, with coordination as necessary with the site POC.

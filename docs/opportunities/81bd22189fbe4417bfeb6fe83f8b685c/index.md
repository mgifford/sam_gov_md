# Advanced Networking Infrastructure A Kits

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-13 08:37:37.457-04
- Solicitation Number: N00019-24-RFPREQ-TPM265-0477

## Summary

The F/A-18E/F and EA-18G Program Office (PMA-265) is seeking capable sources to provide Advanced Networking Infrastructure (ANI) A Kits for the Royal Australian Air Force (RAAF) AF/A-18F and AEA-18F Aircraft required for ECP 6522. This is a Sources Sought notice for market research purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation or a promise to issue a solicitation in the future. This notice does not commit the Government to contract for any supply or service whatsoever. The Government is not seeking proposals at this time and will not accept unsolicited proposals. Responders are advised that the Government will not pay for any information or administrative costs incurred in response to this notice. All costs associated with responding to this notice will be solely at the interested party’s expense. Not responding to this notice does not preclude participation in any future Request for Proposal (RFP), if any is issued. Any information submitted by respondents to this notice is strictly voluntary. The information submitted will be used for informational purposes only to help the Government determine the appropriate acquisition strategy. Under this effort, the Contractor will design, develop, fabricate, and deliver ANI A-Kits and associated technical drawings. Interested parties are requested to submit a capability statement of no more than five (5) pages in length. The capability statement should demonstrate the ability to perform the work described. Responses shall be limited to submitted electronically to the e-mail address provided below. Responses are requested by 03/27.2026. Earliest possible response is encouraged. Responses will be reviewed, and additional information may be requested from individual sources, if desired by the Government.

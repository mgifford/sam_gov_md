# Short Term Office Rental in Edinburg, Texas

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-02 12:02:57.762-04
- Solicitation Number: 12FPC426Q0026

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA),Farm Service Agency (FSA), Rural Development (RD), and Natural Resources Conservation Service (NRCS) is conducting market research to define sources and gain interest from vendors with capability to fulfill the requirement to provide a short-term office rental in Edinburg, TX to deliver uninterrupted service to United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) service center customers. This Request for Information (RFI) is being issued for information, market research and planning purposes ONLY and does not constitute a Request for Proposals (RFP) or a Request for Quotations (RFQ). This notice does not constitute a commitment by the Government. All information submitted in response to this announcement is voluntary, and the Government will not pay for information requested nor will it compensate for any cost incurred in developing information provided. No determination as to the viability of this requirement has been made at this point and there is currently no solicitation for this effort. Any solicitation resulting from this RFI will be announced separately. The purpose of this RFI is to: 1. Obtain information regarding availability and capability of qualified vendors; 2. Obtain business size of qualified vendors- small (including socio-economic status) or other than small (large); 3. Identify size classification status relative to the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for the proposed acquisition is 531120- Lessors of Nonresidential Buildings (except Miniwarehouses), size standard of $34.0 Million; and 4. Gain industry comment/feedback on the draft Statement of Work. Responses from interested vendors to these questions above will be used by the Government to make appropriate acquisition decisions.

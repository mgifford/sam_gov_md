# FEDLINK Books & Non-Print Materials and Related Support Solicitation

- Agency: LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 14:08:34.291-04
- Solicitation Number: LCFDL26R0007

## Summary

Amendment 0003 posted on March 9, 2026 shares Questions and Answers 3. Amendment 0002 posted on March 9, 2026 shares Questions and Answers 2. Amendment 0001 posted on February 24, 2026 extends the closing date/time to March 12, 2026 at 12 pm ET. Amendment 0001 also updates section Pricing Schedule Requirements; includes LC52.211-1 Deliveries (APR 2015); updates Section L - Instructions to Offerors; updates Attachment J.3 - Pricing Pages Template to allow for editing in previously locked cells; and shares Questions and Answers 1. The Library of Congress (LOC), Federal Library and Information Network (FEDLINK), is seeking proposals from vendors for Books & Non-Print Materials and Related Support. This solicitation will result in a multiple-award Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract with a 5-year base with no options. Questions regarding this solicitation are due by 12:00pm Eastern time on Thursday, February 19, 2026. Answers to all questions received will be provided via an amendment to the solicitation. Proposals are due by 12:00pm Eastern time on Thursday, March 5, 2026. Please reference this solicitation number in all emails regarding this opportunity. Offerors are advised to closely review Sections L & M for instructions on proposal format and the evaluation factors for award. Proposals will be evaluated using a best value tradeoff analysis to select the offeror or offerors whose proposals represent the best value to the government. All future information about this acquisition, including solicitation amendments, will be distributed through SAM.gov. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring SAM.gov to ensure that they have the most up-to-date information about this acquisition.

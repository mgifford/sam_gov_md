# 432 SFS Light Towers

- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 18:06:55.087-04
- Solicitation Number: F3G3JE6091AG01

## Summary

------------------------------AMENDMENT----------------------------------------------- Amendment 1 - This requirement has been amended as of 13 April 2026 to incorporate the answers to the questions. Please see Attachment 2 - Questions and Answers for more details. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- The 432 Contracting Office, Creech Air Force Base, Nevada, is issuing this Request for Quote (RFQ) for 432 Security Forces Light Towers. This requirement is for CLIN 0001- Alaska 3kW Portable Light Towers, 60Hz, tan Qty: 6 MFR: Alaska Defense P/N: AKS-BM293-2. Please see the attached COMBO being utilized for the RFQ and supporting documents for more information. Any questions shall be submitted via email to the below point of contact by 13 April 2024 at 10:00 AM PDT. Prospective contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management Database (SAM) prior to submission of proposals. Offerors are further advised that failure to register in the DoD SAM prior to submission of proposal will render your firm ineligible for award. Registration requires applicants to have a Unique Entity ID Number. The government recommends contractors register immediately to be eligible for a timely award. This solicitation will be available ONLY via the Internet at the following address: http://sam.gov. No paper copies of the solicitation will be furnished. Any questions regarding this notice must be submitted in writing or via email to SrA Ethan Clark, Contract Administrator, 702-404-2374, ethan.clark.7@us.af.mil. Alternate contact,Capt Taylor Peeples, Contracting Officer, 702-404-1112, taylor.peeples.1@us.af.mil

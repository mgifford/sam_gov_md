## Summary

Justification and Approval to increase the shared ceiling on Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts N3220521D4105 (P00007) to MAN Energy Solutions Middle East, LLC and N3220521D4106 (P00008) to Fairbanks Morse, LLC.

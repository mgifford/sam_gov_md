# River Birch Road Asphalt Paving

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 11:02:18.64-05
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26QA015

## Summary

The United States Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) - Rock Island District has a service requirment to conduct the milling of existing asphalt pavement, the stockpiling of millings, and the placement of Hot Mix Asphalt (HMA) pavement and tack coat for the repaving of River Birch Road to the River Birch Campground at the USACE Thomson Causeway Recreation Area. Additional information on the requirement to include the Performance Work Statement (PWS) and Specifications will be provided when the solicitation is released. USACE - Rock Island District intends to advertise this requirement as 100% Small Business Set-Aside utilizing Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) evaluation procedures. A single Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract is anticipated to be awarded to the lowest price technically acceptable offeror. Any companies interested in this requirement please provide capability statements via email to contract specialist Carrie Holub at carrie.l.holub@usace.army.mil. The presolicitation notice is for information purposes only and is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government to procure any supplies or services, or for the Government to pay for the information received. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this presolicitation notice or any follow up information requested. Consequently, no solicitation is being issued at this time. Therefore, no award will be made as a result of providing this information.

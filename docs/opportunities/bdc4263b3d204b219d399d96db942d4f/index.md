# METES & BOUNDS SURVEY FOR CAFRL

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 14:40:10.497-04
- Solicitation Number: 140G0126Q0085

## Summary

The U.S. Geological Survey S.O. Conte Research Laboratory requires services to perform a metes and bounds survey, in turner Falls, MA with the final deliverable due July 31, 2026. There are no option periods. Competitive Request for Quotations (RFQ) 140G0126Q0085 is a total small business set aside under NAICS 541370 Surveying and Mapping (except Geophysical) Services. The associated size standard is $19 million. The anticipated award date is April 15, 2026. Vendors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) at https://www.sam.gov to be eligible for award. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, which shall be considered by the agency. Quotations shall be submitted to Mindie Dixon at mindie_dixon@ios.doi.gov.

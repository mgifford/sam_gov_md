# SOURCES SOUGHT - Brand Name or Equal 100K PlaneSkate Aircraft Flat Tire Dolly for JBER, AK 99506

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-21 09:24:05.664-04
- Solicitation Number: FA500026Q0014

## Summary

The 673d Contracting Squadron at Joint Base Elmendorf-Richmond (JBER), Anchorage, Alaska, seeks feedback/sources of contractors capable of providing a Brand Name or Equal asset comparable to the 100K PlaneSkate aircraft flat tire dolly that meets the salient characteristics as outlined in the Salient Characteristics list attached to this notice. Response Guidelines: Only electronic responses will be accepted. Please send responses by email to the primary POCs not later than 22 May 2026 at 10:00 AM Alaska Daylight Time. Earlier submission is highly encouraged. *This Request for Information (RFI) notice is issued for planning purposes only. Responses to this notice shall assist the Government in market research and identifying potential sources. This notice does NOT serve as a formal request for proposal or request for quote. No pricing information is requested at this time. *Funding is not currently available and may not be made available to solicit for a requirement based on this information. This RFI is not intended to serve as an official notice to obligate the Government to award a contract. All information submitted in response to this announcement is voluntary, and the Government will not pay for the information requested nor will it compensate any respondent for any costs incurred in developing the information provided. The Government requests that interested parties respond to this notice in writing, providing the following information: 1. Whether your company is willing and able to provide 100K PlaneSkate aircraft flat tire dolly to JBER, Anchorage, Alaska. 2. Company Name and Address. 3. Cage Code. 4. SAM UEI Number. 5. Company business size by relevant NAICS code. 6. Small Business Type(s), if applicable. 7. Point of Contact for questions and/or clarifications. 8. Telephone Number and Email Address. 9. Web Page URL (if applicable). 10. Teaming Partners (if applicable). 11. Industry standard on warranties. 12. Product Country of Origin / TAA Compliance. 13. Provide any recommendations and/or feedback. 14. Identify the appropriate number of calendar days required to respond to a solicitation. 15. Identify ambiguous or confusing language in the Salient Characteristics. Please do not submit pricing information in response to this Sources Sought

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 05:50:30.909-04

## Summary

Procurement of a turnkey, all-inclusive FM radio broadcast service in the Naples, Italy vicinity utilizing a U.S. Government provided Studio-to transmitter Link System (STL) and broadcast through contractor owned and maintained equipment. The purpose of Amendment 0001 to Solicitation N6817126QN031 is to provide answers to questions, revise Performance Work Statement and extend Solicitation due date to 27 April 2026 at 11.00 am Central European Time.

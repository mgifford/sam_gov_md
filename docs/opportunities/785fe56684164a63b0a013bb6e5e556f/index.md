## Summary

For added clarity the below items shall be all inclusive to 1 kit. The vendor shall be trained and have prior experience in training for the usage of this equipment. The vendor shall travel to Fort Irwin CA, to provide Support for 3 NTC Training Iterations and train end users on this kit. Training support shall be conducted by a Cut Suit SME that has a background of 8404 Corpsman, 18D, or 68W with CMB. Part of this kit shall include 2 Day Hyper Realistic Moulage Kit based Makeup Course and a 3 Day TCCC Moulage support for up to 35 casualties.

# WBAMC Washer Disinfector Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 12:49:28.418-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0026QA102

## Summary

This is a synopsis only and not a request for quote (RFQ). The RFQ W81K0026QA102 is forthcoming. This synopsis is for commercial services prepared in accordance with (IAW) the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This requirement is for maintenance of six (6) government-owned Steris washer/disinfector units for William Beaumont Army Medical Center (WBAMC), Fort Bliss, TX. The resultant contract (purchase order) will be a base plus one (1) option, the periods of performance of which are: Base: 1 May 2026 through 30 April 2027 Option 1: 1 May 2027 through 30 April 2028

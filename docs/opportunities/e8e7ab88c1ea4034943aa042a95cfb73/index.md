- Solicitation Number: TRACENBirmingham-SupportServices-2026

## Summary

This is a Request for Information (RFI) for the purpose of conducting market research, analysis, and screening of potential capable parties to provide base operations support services for a newly established training center located in Birmingham, Alabama. Services required will include housing maintenance, pest control, grounds maintenance, custodial, and other general support services. All information contained in this RFI is preliminary and is subject to change if, and when, a solicitation is issued. See attachments for additional details.

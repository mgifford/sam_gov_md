# Replace Medium Voltage Cutoff Switches at US Coast Guard Air Station Cape Cod Buzzards Bay, Massachusetts

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 09:47:16.756-04

## Summary

THIS IS A PRESOLICITATION NOTICE ONLY. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL. The United States Coast Guard Civil Engineering Unit (CEU) Providence has a requirement for a contractor to provide construction services to Replace Medium Voltage Cutoff Switches at USCG Joint Base Cape Cod, 5201 Lee Rd., Buzzards Bay, MA This solicitation is being advertised as a 100% Total Small Business Set Aside. Only one award will be made. The Invitation for Bid (IFB) will be issued on or about 4 May 2026. The procurement method is Sealed Bidding, FAR Part 14. The Northern American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this project is 238210 (SBA Size Standard: $19.0M annual revenue). The Product Service Code is Z2NZ, Repair or Alteration of Other Utilities. The solicitation and any amendments will be posted on this website, www.sam.gov Contract Opportunities. This notice is for informational purposes only. The Government is not obligated to issue a solicitation as a result of this notice. No other information is available until issuance of the solicitation. All interested parties should register on this website. It is the offerors responsibility to check the web site periodically for any amendments/notices to the solicitation. Prospective contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database in order to obtain access in SAM.gov Contract Opportunities. Offerors who submit a quote or proposal in response to a solicitation must have an active SAM.gov registration at time of quote submission and throughout the evaluation and award phase. Contractors please note: Representations and Certifications can be maintained, online, in the System for Award Management (SAM), at https://www.sam.gov. Project Description: The project consists of (1) base bid item. Base Bid Item: Replace Medium Voltage Transformer Cutoff Switches: Contractor shall furnish all design, labor, materials, equipment, supervision, transportation and disposal necessary to provide a complete operational 5KV distribution system that meets or exceeds the National Electrical Code. Work includes Replacing two medium voltage transformer cutoff switches and their cabinets. All work shall be accomplished in accordance with the project specifications (PSN 16677450), project drawings, all local, State, and Federal Codes and all required permitting Period of Performance: The period of performance for the project is 270 calendar days from issuance of Notice to Proceed. Contract Magnitude: The magnitude of this construction project is between $250,000 and $500,000 IAW FAR 36.204. QUESTIONS REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT WILL NOT BE ANSWERED DURING THE PRESOLICITATION PHASE. SUBMIT QUESTIONS TO THE CONTRACT SPECIALIST AFTER THE SOLICITATION AND ASSOCIATED DOCUMENTS HAVE BEEN POSTED. NOTE: The Government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation, either before or after the closing date. In the event the Government cancels this solicitation, the Government has no obligation to reimburse an offeror for any costs.

# Intent to Sole Source - Clark AB - Terminal 1 Rental

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-22 21:32:09.921-04
- Solicitation Number: FA5270_BK26_01

## Summary

DESCRIPTION: The USAF intends to award a sole source contract for the rental of terminal space and selected surrounding parking areas at Clark Air Base (AB), Philippines. This contract is in support of exercise operations to ensure USMC objectives are met. This sole source procurement is justified under the authority of FAR 6.302-1, "Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy requirements:. The required facility is currently available for rental through STRA8 Logistics, who possess the unique capabilities and facilities necessary to meet the needs of this requirement. REQUIREMENTS: The contractor must have all appropriate credentials, licenses, and authorizations to do business with the Airport Authorities at Clark AB. This includes, but is not limited to: - Valid business licenses and permits to operate within the jurisdiction of Clark AB. - Authorization from the Airport Authorities to provide access to the requested facilities. - Compliance with all local, national, and international regulations governing airport operations and facility rentals. PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE The anticipated period of performance for this requirement is one month as a base period, with two additional month-long option periods to be executed as necessary. CONTACT INFORMATION: Interested parties who believe they can meet the above requirements and provide the necessary services may submit a capability statement for review by the contracting authority. Responses must be received by the Response Deadline stated in this posting, and should be addressed to: Nicholas Cripe Contracting Officer nicholas.cripe@us.af.mil DISCLAIMER: This notice of intent is not a request for competitive proposals. However, all responses received by the deadline will be considered by the Government. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed contract based upon responses to this notice is solely at the discretion of the Government.

# Roofing, Repair, and Replacement Follow-on (R3F) Multiple Award Task Order Contract (MATOC) Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 14:15:24.353-04
- Solicitation Number: FA890326R0009

## Summary

The Department of the Air Force, 772d Enterprise Sourcing Squadron, is issuing a solicitation for the Roofing, Repair, and Replacement Follow-on (R3F) Multiple Award Task Order Contract (MATOC), an Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract vehicle providing roofing, repair, and replacement construction services across multiple geographic regions. The R3F MATOC will establish multiple awards under a single five?year ordering period. Task Orders will be issued on a firm?fixed?price basis in accordance with FAR Part 15 and FAR Part 36 procedures. Any further questions, concerns, or clarifications shall be submitted in accordance with the criteria outlined in the solicitation. Proposals will be evaluated in accordance with the criteria outlined in the solicitation. The solicitation, attachments, and all subsequent amendments will be made available electronically on SAM.gov. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring the site for updates. Note: Due to the size of this solicitation, some clauses may appear more than once.

## Summary

Please note this solicitation is not ready to be issued as of this date. The Embassy of the United States in Pristina will launch the solicitation on the SAM.gov website when it is available. The U.S. Embassy in Pristina anticipates issuing a solicitation for garbage collection in support of its operations. The anticipated performance period of this BPA is for a period of 5 years. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, which will be considered. The solicitation is expected to be issued on May 5, 2026, as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) under solicitation number 19KV4226Q0004. The Government may award the BPA (blanket purchase agreement) based on the initial offer without discussion. The result of this BPA agreement will be a firm fixed price. All contractors must be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to submitting an offer pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. Therefore, prospective offerors are encouraged to register prior to the submittal of quotations/proposals. Electronic Submission/Responses: This opportunity allows for electronic responses. Please submit your offer prior to the response date and time. Please ensure your email with your offer does not exceed (5MB), if it does then you might have to send more than one email. or No electronic offers will be accepted. Please mail your offer to the contracting office listed in this notice prior to the response date and time.

## Summary

Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Network Contracting Office (NCO 21) intends to award a sole source, firm fixed price contract to L1 Enterprises Incorporated. The procurement will result in a purchase order for an Automated Linen Dispenser lease for supplies and services currently in use at VA San Francisco Healthcare System. The solicitation for this action will be posted to SAM.gov, and all responsible sources may submit a capability statement, proposal, or quotation, which shall be considered by the agency. After consideration, the Government will determine whether to proceed with the sole source acquisition.

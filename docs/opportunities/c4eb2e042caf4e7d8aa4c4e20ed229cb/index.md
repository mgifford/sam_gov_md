- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The contractor shall provide all equipment, tools, materials, and associated labor necessary to provide vehicle lease and maintenance services for Combined Joint Task Force Horn of Africa. The vehicles will be used to transport personnel, equipment, and cargo throughout Djibouti. The place of performance is Camp Lemonnier, Djibouti.

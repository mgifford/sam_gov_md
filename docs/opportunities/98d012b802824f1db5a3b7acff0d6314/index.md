## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCE SOUGHT NOTICE ONLY. This is NOT a Request for Quotation and in no way obligates the Government to award any contract/purchase order. The Regional Contracting Office – Parris Island is seeking vendors who can provide 1st Marine Corp District (MCD) with privately-owned vehicle (POV) parking spaces to support the following recruiting location indicated below. All feedback and information received is for Market Research Purposes Only. Location: Recruiting Substation (RSS) Empire State Address: 165 Chambers Street, Manhattan NY 10007 REQUIREMENT The parking facilities maintained by the contractor must meet or exceed the following requirements: Six (6) POV parking spaces Parking must always be available and accessible: 365 days/year-24 hours/day. Premises must be monitored by security cameras. Parking may be indoor or outdoor surface lot but must always have lighting for security purposes. Parking must be reserved for the Marine Corps ONLY. Parking spaces are reserved for use by the Marines regardless of whether they are being used every day. Parking must be within a 0.5 mile (walking distance) of location indicated above If key cards or other items are needed to gain access to the parking garage and/or parking spaces, it must be provided with your pricing.

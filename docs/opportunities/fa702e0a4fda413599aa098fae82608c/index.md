# Ellex Absolu Ultrasound Console

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-19 09:50:15.9-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C241-26-AP-1996

## Summary

INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION OR REQUEST FOR OFFERS. NO SOLICITATION PACKAGE IS AVAILABLE AND TELEPHONE REQUESTS WILL NOT BE HONORED AND NO SOLICITATION WILL BE POSTED TO CONTRACT OPPORTUNITIES. PROCUREMENT TITLE: Ellex Absolu Ultrasound Console INTENT TO AWARD: The Department of Veterans Affairs (NCO 1), Brockton Healthcare System 940 Belmont Street, Brockton, MA 02301-5596; intends to award one (1) Contract to SDVOSB Corp Medical Supply, LLC, for the Ellex Absolu Ultrasound Console system. BACKGROUND AND DESCRIPTION: The VA Boston Healthcare System Surgical Department, Optometry Section, located at the Brockton Campus, is requesting a replacement of their Ellex Absolu Ultrasound. The Ellex Absolu Ultrasound Console is a critical tool in optometry, particularly for monitoring intraocular tumors and retinal detachments in patients with poor media. See enclosed Statement of work (SOW). NAICS: 334510 Electromedical and Electrotherapeutic Apparatus Manufacturing, 1,250 Employees PSC: 6515 Medical and surgical instruments, equipment, and supplies AUTHORITY: The authority for this procurement will be via FAR Part 12 - Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed award upon response to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Contracting Officer. INTERESTED PARTIES: However, any interested party who believes that they have experience and capability to provide all items described in the enclosed Statement of Work (SOW) may express interest by contacting the VA’s Contract Specialist Pearl Wray, at pearl.wray@va.gov, by 14:00 EST, by 3/24/2026.

# Recondition Hubtex MSL Drive Units and Provide Spares

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 16:20:56.771-04
- Solicitation Number: N0040626QS230

## Summary

NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound intends to solicit and contract a firm fixed price, supply type contract for OEM Hubtex MSL Drive Unit Reconditioning and Spares with only one source, Hubtex North America, Inc, on a One Source basis. See attached solicitation for requirements. Solicitation closes on 15Apr2026 at 10:00 AM PST. The Government anticipates awarding on or about 27Apr2026. All questions regarding this solicitation should be submitted to J. Brian Fergus, James.B.Fergus2.civ@us.navy.mil.

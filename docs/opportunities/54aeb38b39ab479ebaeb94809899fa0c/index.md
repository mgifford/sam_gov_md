# Lease for Retail/Office Space in Gillette, WY - 1,997 SF Required

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 14:11:41.361-04
- Solicitation Number: DACA455270012000

## Summary

The US Government currently occupies office and related space in a building under lease in Gillette, Wyoming, that will be expiring. The Government is considering alternate space if economically advantageous. In making this determination, the Government will consider, among other things, the availability of alternate space that potentially can satisfy the Government’s requirements, janitorial services provided in the lease, as well as costs likely to be incurred through relocating, such as physical move costs, replication of tenant improvements and telecommunication infrastructure, and non-productive agency downtime.

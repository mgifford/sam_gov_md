# XENEX DIFINFECTION AGREEMENT FOR ROBOTS AT TVHS SITES

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-04-02 16:17:28.289-04

- Awardee: XENEX DISINFECTION SERVICES INC. San Antonio TX 78216 USA

## Summary

The Tennessee Valley VA Medical Centers in Murfreesboro, Nashville, and Chattanooga have a Base Plus 2 or 3 (1) year Ordering Period requirement to provide service and maintenance for their Xenex sanitizing robots. These robots are used to sterilize rooms from germs and infectious diseases. Market research was unable to identify any small business entities for this project. Due to the proprietary needs of this project, a sole source award will be made to the OEM, Xenex RFO 6.103-1

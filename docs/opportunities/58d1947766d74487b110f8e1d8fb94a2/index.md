# Structures WS Post 4 Vehicle Barrier Net FY26

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 14:49:17.476-04
- Solicitation Number: FA441826Q0050

## Summary

This requirement is for the procurement of a replacement Global GRAB-300 vehicle barrier net. The contractor is responsible for providing labor, materials, and travel to and from Joint Base Charleston (JB CHS), SC in order to meet the requirements specified within this solicitation. Please be advised that the full requirements, including Instructions to Offerors (FAR 52.212-1) and the basis for evaluation (FAR 52.212-2), are detailed in the solicitation attachments. A thorough review of all attached documents is required for a compliant submission. Items shall be delivered to 2482 Red Bank Road, Bldg. 37, Goose Creek, SC 29445 30 Days ARO. Access to the installation must be made via the Weapons Station commercial entry gate on Remount Road. Authorized delivery hours are 0730 to 1530, Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. This is a delivery-only requirement; no installation is required.

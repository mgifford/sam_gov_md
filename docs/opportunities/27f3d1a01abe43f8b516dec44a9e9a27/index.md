# Wrong Way Sensor Replacement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-07 13:26:14.975-04
- Solicitation Number: FA480326Q0059

## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE ONLY. The 20th Contracting Squadron is conducting market research to identify potential sources for the replacement of wrong way sensors. A key component of this research is to determine a realistic period of performance for this project. The Government is requesting information regarding capabilities for Wrong Way Sensor Replacement and Associated Components, in accordance with the Statement of Work. If your company is capable of providing these services, please provide the following to the Points of Contact (POCs) listed below: A copy of your company's capability statement. A projected period of performance, detailing the estimated time it will take to complete this requirement. POCs: SrA Michael Treaster: michael.treaster@us.af.mil SSgt Motaz Irshid: motaz.irshid@us.af.mil DO NOT SUBMIT ANY QUESTIONS OR AN OFFER AT THIS TIME.

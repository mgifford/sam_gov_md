# 7110--Pathology Renovation Phase 2 Casework

- Posted: 2026-03-04 07:21:44.957-05

## Summary

Togus VA Medical Center has a requirement for laboratory casework for the renovation of the Pathology and Laboratory located at the VAMC Togus campus in Augusta ME. The casework must meet or exceed the VHA specifications for Lab Safety, promote environmental stewardship, and integrate with the laboratory design. This requirement includes laboratory mobile casework and professional installation. The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, product, equipment, transportation, and supervision necessary to fulfill the requirement outlined herein. See attached Combined Synopsis Solicitation Products and Commercial Services and Statement of Work (SOW) Mobile Laboratory Casework for Togus VAMC

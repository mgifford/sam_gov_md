# Invitation to Bid for Scrap Mixed Steel located at Naval Base Ventura County (NBVC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 17:27:25.004-04
- Solicitation Number: T002-26-CD

## Summary

The Navy Region Southwest Qualified Recycling Program (QRP) is issuing an invitation to bid on the sale of scrap mixed steel located at Naval Base Ventura County (NBVC) Seal Beach (Ventura, CA). This is a Government Sale. If you are interested, please review the attached announcement form for detailed information and instructions. Bids must be mailed to our office using the bid sheet provided with this email. Please ensure the bid sheet is completed in ink or typed, clearly legible, and signed by the individual submitting the bid. Please submit only the bid sheet. The additional materials in this packet, specifically the terms and conditions, are provided for your review to detail what the job entails. Bids are due no later than 2:00 P.M. (Pacific Time) on May 7, 2026. If you have any questions regarding this bid, please contact our office at 619-556-9475 or email cnrsw-qrp-sales@us.navy.mil.

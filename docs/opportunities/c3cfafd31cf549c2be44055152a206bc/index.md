# Remote Automated Laboratory System Subscription

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-13 08:26:10.662-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0026QA086

## Summary

The Medical Readiness Contracting Office - W, EMSM Contracting Branch is issuing this Sources Sought as a means of conducting market research to identify potential sources having an interest and industry technologies available to support/provide Remote Automated Laboratory System Subscription. The potential contract will be a 12 - month base year plus 4 firm-fixed option year. SALIENT CHARACTERISTICS: Be a Remote Automated Laboratory System capable of performing the following assays: Must provide a centralized, cloud-based platform for organizing, storing, and analyzing POC device data. Must have Bi-directional Device Connectivity (Supports two-way communication with devices, enabling not only data upload but also the ability to send information to devices). Must Integrates features like training, directory management, and certification updates. Must have a wide device compatibility to handle data from a variety of connected devices in point-of-care programs. Must be secure and compliant to offer a trusted, secure environment for managing sensitive patient and operator data in accordance with healthcare standards. Must have easy access and management of data from anywhere. Must streamline the generation of reports and data analysis. Must maintain compliance with stringent Joint Commission (TJC) and the College of American Pathologists (CAP) requirements.

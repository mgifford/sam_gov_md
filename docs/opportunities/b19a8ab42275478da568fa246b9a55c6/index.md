# Intent to Sole Source - Licenses and Services for Virtual Host Servers

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-04 09:34:17.126-05
- Solicitation Number: NOI-OLAO-26-003080

## Summary

This is a Notice of Intent, not a request for proposal. The National Institute of Health, Clinical Center intends to negotiate on an other than full and open competition basis with the following vendor: Richweb. The statutory authority for this sole source requirement is 41 U.S.C. 253(c) (1) as implemented by FAR 6.302-1 only one responsible source and no other supply of service will satisfy agency requirements. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL. All responsible sources that could provide the required may submit a capability statement that will be considered by email (subject line to reference NOI-OLAO-26-003080) by 12:00 PM eastern standard time on 03/14/2026 to: valerie.gregorio@nih.gov. All responses received by the closing date of this synopsis will be considered by the Government. A determination not to compete this requirement, based upon responses to this notice, is solely within the discretion of the Government.

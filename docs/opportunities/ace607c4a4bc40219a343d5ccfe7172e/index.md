# Seattle VAMC Emergency Custodial

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-19 19:15:29.818-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C260-26-AP-2287

## Summary

Contractor shall provide custodial support at the Seattle VA Medical Center. The Seattle VAMC includes approximately 500,000 sq ft of regular routine cleaning space at all buildings within the Seattle VAMC campus. Routine cleaning is defined as the regular cleaning tasks the Contractor shall be responsible for performing at Seattle VAMC buildings. Period of performance is 02/20/2026 - 05/19/2026.

# Kapa Baits for metagenomic sequencing

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 10:20:25.835-04
- Solicitation Number: HFP-2026-133128

## Summary

The purpose of this requirement is to procure myBaits® Custom 480K hybridization capture reagents equivalent to sixty-four (64) enrichment reactions (Rxns) to support targeted metagenomic sequencing for the detection and identification of arthropod contaminants in food products. This procurement is for reagents only; sequencing equipment and associated laboratory instrumentation are already available in the Wiley Building (CPK1).

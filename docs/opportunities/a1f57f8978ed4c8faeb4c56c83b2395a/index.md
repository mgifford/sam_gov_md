## Summary

Amendment 1 has been issued to update offers due date reflected on the 1449 to match that of this announcement and period of performance. The USDA Forest Service, Tonto National Forest, intends to issue a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for commercial services to perform cultural resource survey services in support of Section 106 compliance requirements. Scope of Work The Contractor shall provide all labor, supervision, equipment, materials, and incidentals necessary to perform professional archaeological and cultural resource services, including but not limited to: Intensive pedestrian archaeological survey Cultural resource identification and site recording GIS data collection and spatial documentation National Register eligibility evaluations Technical reporting and documentation compliant with Section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act Preparation of final reports and site forms consistent with Forest Service standards Work will be performed within the boundaries of the Tonto National Forest, Arizona. Set-Aside This requirement is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. Offerors must be registered and active in the System for Award Management (SAM) at the time of submission. Submission Information Please submit Q&A NLT 3/3/26, 1600hrs PST. Responses are due 3/13/26, 1700hrs PST. Send all correspondence to veronica.beck@usda.gov

# Lame Deer Heliport and Concrete Support Structure Remediation

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 13:41:44.931-04
- Solicitation Number: 75H70126R00019

## Summary

SCOPE OF WORK: The existing heliport at the Northern Cheyenne Health Center in Lame Deer, Montana is failing due to concrete cracking and spalling and requires complete replacement. The facility’s structural support systems for the ambulance bay, emergency entrance, and loading dock have also failed and require remediation to restore full operational capacity. The project includes the full demolition and replacement of the existing heliport along with structural concrete remediation work at the ambulance bay, emergency entrance, and loading dock. Major elements of work include: o Demolition, reconstruction, and commissioning of the medical facility’s heliport, including all associated airfield concrete work; o Construction of sidewalks and ramps for patient transport between the heliport and the facility; o Installation of an electrical lighting system suitable for nighttime and low-visibility helicopter operations; o Replacement of the heliport safety fence, access gates, and associated site improvements; o Structural concrete remediation including installation of new beams, columns, and concrete flooring for the ambulance bay and drive, emergency entrance, and loading dock; o Installation of a crawlspace HVAC system to reduce humidity levels. LOCATION OF PROJECT: All work shall be performed at the Northern Cheyenne Health Center at 420 Cheyenne Ave, Lame Deer, MT 59043 TERO: TERO fees do apply. CONSTRUCTION DURATION: Period of Performance is 120 calendar days from issuance of the Notice to Proceed. CONSTRUCTION MAGNITUDE: The magnitude of this construction project is anticipated to be between $1,000,000 and $5,000,000. SITE VISIT: See Section L.5 in the solicitation document. PSC/NAICS: The type of work to be performed will be categorized under PSC code Y1BZ, Construction Of Other Airfield Structures and NAICS code 237310 Highway, Street, and Bridge Construction with a size standard of $45 million. SET ASIDE: This solicitation is being issued as Unrestricted, Full and Open Competition. ***Amendment 01 issued 15 April 2026*** ***Amendment 02 issued 17 April 2026***

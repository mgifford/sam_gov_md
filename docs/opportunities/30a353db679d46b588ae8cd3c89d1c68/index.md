# In accordance with FAR 6.302-1, NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Norfolk, Regional Directorate, intends to award a Sole Source Firm Fixed Price Contract to The Myers-Briggs Company.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-23 09:36:35.433-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926QW074

## Summary

In accordance with FAR 6.302-1, NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Norfolk, Regional Directorate intends to award a Sole Source Firm Fixed Price Contract to The Myers-Briggs Company for MBTI Profile Report From M/Step I and Elevate Platform License. This notice of intent is not a request for proposals or quotations; however, any challenges to this sole source must be received in writing and sent to kelly.l.polson.civ@us.navy.mil to be considered by the Government for the purpose of determining whether or not to compete the requirement. Challenges must include information sufficient to enable the Government to make a determination that an equivalent service exists which would meet the Governments needs as stated herein. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed requirement based on responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government.

# Texas Army National Guard - Structural Collapse Simulator (SCS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 16:59:58.327-04
- Solicitation Number: W912L126QA005

## Summary

The Texas Army National Guard (TXARNG) intends to award a firm-fixed price sole source award to a contractor that can provide a Structural Collapse Simulator (SCS) to simulate the state of buildings due to a natural disaster or a domestic terrorism event for Reset Structural Collapse. However, any interested party that believes itself to be capable of meeting the requirement and is authorized to perform this task as mentioned shall identify themselves and must provide clear and convincing evidence of their capability to fulfill the requirement. A determination by the Government not to compete this requirement based on responses or lack thereof to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Any information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct competitive procurement. See Attachment 1 for more details.

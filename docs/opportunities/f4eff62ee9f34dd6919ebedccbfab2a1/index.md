## Summary

Solicitation SPE300-26-R-0029 was solicited as “Other than Full and Open competition” in accordance with the RFO FAR 6.103-1 Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Servies will Satisfy Agency Requirements. This is a notification that an award has been made IAW 10 U.S.C 3204(a)(1), Justification for Other than Full and Open Competition, as a contract to US Foods INC-Us Foods Oklahoma (CAGE Code 1F9R0), to continue to provide Prime Vendor services to customers in the Northern Texas-Oklahoma Area. This contract, SPE300-26-D-3014, will have a period of performance of 420 days from April 5, 2026 toMay 29, 2027, or until the award of a new contract. This is a Justification and Approval (J & A) for full line distribution and is being posted IAW requirements of FAR Overhaul 6.301(b). A copy of the J&A and the Solicitation/Statement of Work is attached to this notice.

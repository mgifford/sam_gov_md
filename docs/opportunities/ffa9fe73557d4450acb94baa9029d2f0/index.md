# 150-pound Amerex Wheeled Fire Extinguishers Testing and Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 15:52:48.759-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700126Q0004

## Summary

Contracting Department, Marine Corps Installations East, has a requirement for fire extinguisher services aboard Marine Corps Air Station (MCAS) Beaufort, South Carolina. Requirement Description: 1.The Contractor shall provide all labor, equipment, and materials necessary to perform a twelve-year(12)hydrostatic test, a six-year (6) internal examination, and any required maintenance, repairs,testing, and recharging to seventy-six (76), 150-pound Amerex Wheeled Fire Extinguishers (Item: 13J013; Mfr Model: 674) 2.The Contractor shall provide all services on-site at MCAS Beaufort, SC. Off-site maintenance,shipping, or removal of equipment is not permitted. 3.The Contractor must perform services in accordance with the following references: •National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) Standard 10 •Standard for Portable Fire Extinguishers •Amerex Manual for 150-pound Wheeled Fire Extinguisher •OSHA Regulation 1910.157.

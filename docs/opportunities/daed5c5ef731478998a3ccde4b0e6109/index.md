# Temporary Cleaning Services at CMR

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-11 08:35:09.097-04
- Solicitation Number: 19BF5026P0106

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all labor, supervision, and management necessary to perform professional cleaning services twice weekly—every Tuesday and Thursday—at the Chief of Mission Residence (CMR), Liberty Overlook, located on Sandford Drive, Prospect Ridge, The Contractor shall ensure the residence, and all associated outbuildings are maintained in a clean, sanitary, and presentable condition appropriate for representational use. All work shall be performed with discretion, attention to detail, and care for furnishings, finishes, and representational standards.

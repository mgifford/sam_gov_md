PRODUCT DESCRIPTION SCOPE OF WORK: Sterilizing Units: Steam use pressurized steam to generate moist heat to eliminate viable microbes from non-heat-sensitive medical devices, including heat-tolerant products used for surgical and general patient care. Steam Sterilization kills microbes effectively and is often viewed as the gold standard of healthcare Sterilization methods. Typical items to be sterilized include surgical instrument packs, surgical implants, irrigation fluids, devices intended for and used by patients in isolation, and obstetric and dental instruments. The Contractor must demonstrate the ability to meet all requirements for the solicitation.

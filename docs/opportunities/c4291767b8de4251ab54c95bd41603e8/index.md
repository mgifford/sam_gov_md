## Summary

This is a Pre-Solicitation Notice for the Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office (NCO) 4, to provide histology equipment. This equipment requirement is for the James E. Van Zandt Medical Center located in Altoona, PA 16602. The full solicitation will be posted on GSA eBuy’s website at www.ebuy.gsa.gov on or about April 10th, 2026. The full solicitation will be a Small Business set-aside. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) for this procurement is 334516 Analytical Laboratory Instrument Manufacturing; the size standard is 1000 employees.

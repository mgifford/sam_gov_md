# JBOC SVTC Equipment Purchase, Installation, and Integration

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 08:13:26.16-04
- Solicitation Number: FA448426Q0005

## Summary

Notice to Offeror(s)/Supplier(s): Funds are not presently available for this effort. No award will be made under this solicitation until funds are available. The Government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation, either before or after the closing date. In the event the Government cancels this solicitation, the Government has no obligation to reimburse an offeror for any costs. Contractor shall replace Atlona A/V equipment with equipment compliant with Security Technical Implementation Guides (STIGs) and interoperability requirements as prescribed in DOD Instruction 8100.04. The new equipment will integrate with existing government furnished property to support both a Nonsecure Video Teleconferencing System (NVTC) and Secure Video Teleconferencing System (SVTC). All hardware and software must be installed within Bldg. 1758. NVTC, SVTC, and other inputs must be controllable from the same location, via contractor-furnished control panels capable of displaying to the 15-panel wall. All hardware and software that Contractor is required to install must be STIG compliant. All equipment and materials will be new and refurbished equipment is not acceptable. Please see attached SOO for more information. ***Attendance at the site visit is mandatory. Only offerors who attend the site visit and sign the official attendance roster will be eligible for award.*** Site visit will be held at JB MDL on 19 March 2026 at 1000. Please contact Contract Administrator, Noah Whiting, at Noah.whiting.1@us.af.mil to be added to our EAL by 1400 12 March 2026. Please note that all individuals on EAL and planning to enter JB MDL will undergo a background check. Admittance to Government Facilities will depend on the outcome of the same.

# INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE Postage for Meter

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-10 16:03:27.086-04
- Solicitation Number: 75H70926BAO-025

## Summary

Equipment shall be Pitney Bowes SendPro MailCenter 2000 postage meter, Brand Name or Equal. Rental/lease shall include maintenance (including replacement parts), all equipment software and USPS Rates updates, equipment, delivery, installation and training. • Rental shall include maintenance including replacement parts and labor, all equipment software and USPS Rates updates, equipment, delivery, installation and training. • New Equipment ONLY; NO remanufactured or "gray market" items. • New equipment shall be USPS IMI & IMpB-compliant and shall be able to generate IMpB-compliant shipping labels for USPS enhance tracking and postage discounts on both physical and virtual meters. • Automatic feed for mixed envelopes size and mixed thickness. • Requires shape-based pricing scale capable of performing postage cost through use of this scale and must be international compliant. • Dynamic weighing platform to accommodate up to 10 lbs. • Shall have a 15-inch Color touch screen user monitor interface • High speed internet connection. • Must have domestic postage ability. • Shall have the ability to track and generate postage and shipping usage reports from desktop – custom dates range of postage and shipping usage report. Postage and shipping usage/activities can be exported into Excel or PDF if needed • Contractor shall provide training and transfer of current postage from current machine to the new machine and provide training to the FPSU staff.

## Summary

NOS WSR - Data Center Infrastructure 3/23/2026 - Added RFIs listing under Attacmemts This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotations are being requested, and a written solicitation will not be issued. The contractor shall engineer, design, procure, and install a complete and functional data center solution focused on racks/cabinets, an aisle containment solution, Power Distribution Units (PDUs), and an environmental monitoring system according to the PWS Period of Performance will be 180 days from date of award at Building 54, 52010 Jayhawk Drive, McConnell AFB, KS 67221. Note: Required on site installation window is 08 July 2026 to 21 July 2026 “Funds are not presently available for this acquisition. No contract award will be made until appropriated funds are made available." ATTACHMENTS: 1. Combined Synopsis Solicitation 2. Clauses 3. PWS 4. Wage Determination

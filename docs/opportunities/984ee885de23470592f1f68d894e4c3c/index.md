OVERVIEW The Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office 6 is conducting market research to identify potential sources to complete the scope of work below. The place of performance will be the hospital campus of the Central Virginia VA Health Care System in Richmond, Virginia. Forward all questions to the point of contact identified below. SCOPE OF WORK The contractor shall supply all labor, materials, and supervision necessary to install conduit and CAT 6 cabling as listed in the provided drawings for the remaining AP locations at Richmond VAMC and (3) at Ft Harrison to the nearest available IT switches at the specified locations in accordance with the Construction Contract and this statement of work. The Contractor shall complete the AP site preparation in accordance with the SOW, drawings, VA Specifications, and any other documents that have been provided. The Contractor shall manage and perform construction services as further defined in this Contract to include permitting, construction management, submittals, fixtures and equipment, scheduling, inspection, testing, environmental protection, and project closeout. NAICS CODE: 238210 Electrical Contractors and Other Wiring Installation Contractors PRODUCT OR SERVICE CODE (PSC): Y1DA Construction of Hospitals and Infirmaries INSTRUCTIONS FOR RESPONDING: Interested companies can respond, via email, to Donteana.Gibbs@va.gov, with the following information: 1. Firm Point of Contact, including name, title, phone number and email address. 2. Firm name, DUNS/Unique Entity ID number and address of the applicable office. 3. Summary of similar contracts previously performed. Additional information may be requested by the Contracting Officer at a later time. POINT OF CONTACT: Donteana Gibbs Contract Specialist Construction Team 2 Network Contracting Office 6 (NCO 6) Donteana.Gibbs@va.gov DUE DATE FOR RESPONSES: This notice will close on Monday, March 23, 2026, at 12:00PM ET.

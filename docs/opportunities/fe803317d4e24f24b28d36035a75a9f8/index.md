# Fire alarm system & smoke and heat exhaust ventilation system maintenance

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 04:49:10.947-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15897560

## Summary

The American Consulate General in Frankfurt, Germany requires preventive maintenance services for Fire Alarm System (FAS) and the Smoke and Heat Exhaust Ventilation System (SHEVS). The U.S. Government intends to award a contract to a responsible company submitting an acceptable offer with the best overall value. We intend to award a contract based on initial proposals, without holding discussions, although we may hold discussions with companies in the competitive range if there is a need to do so. Qualified companies interested in this RFQ should email Berlin Einkauf with the purchase requisition number in the subject line to request the solicitation documents. Quotations will be accepted until 13 April 2026

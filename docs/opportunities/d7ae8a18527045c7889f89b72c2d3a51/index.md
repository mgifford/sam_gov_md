Barge (CVA-8) FY26 Overhaul: NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Yokosuka (NAVSUP FLCY) has a new requirement in support of Barge (CVA-8) and Commander Fleet Activity Yokosuka (CFAY) Port Operations. The work is composed of one (1) Statement of Work (SOW) of various vessel repair, overhaul, preservation, and replacement work onboard the vessel. Offers will be received until 13 Apr 2026, 10:00 AM (Please submit your proposal to " kazuo.takamura.ln@us.navy.mil " in TO line) PERIOD OF PERFORMANCE: 15 Jun 2026 through 15 Sep 2026 PLACE OF PERFORMANCE: Yokosuka Naval Base, Yokosuka, JAPAN References in the Work Items will be provided to offerors via DoD safe (a web-based tool that provides users the capability to securely send and receive large files, including files that are too large to be transmitted via email.) Please email request for references to: Kazuo Takamura (kazuo.takamura.ln@us.navy.mil) Peter Arrieta (peterjommel.s.arrieta.ln@us.navy.mil) When submitting your proposal, you must specify any hazardous material in accordance with FAR 52.223-3 (Hazardous Material ID) and DFARS 252.223-7001. When submitting your proposal, you must specify if you anticipate sea transportation in accordance with DFARS 252.247-7022. If there is a time limit on your offer, you must specify this at submission of your proposal. If there are any amendments to this RFP, please acknowledge them when submitting your proposal in the proposal comment section.

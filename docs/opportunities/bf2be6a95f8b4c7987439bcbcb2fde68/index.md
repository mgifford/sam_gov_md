# Maintenance and Repair of STERIS Sterilization Processing Equipment at Bassett Army Community Hospital, Fort Wainwright, Alaska

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 12:42:12.892-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0226QA032

## Summary

The Medical Readiness Contracting Office – Pacific intends to award a sole source contract to STERIS Corporation to provide maintenance and repair services for the STERIS Sterilization Processing Equipment for Bassett Army Community Hospital, Fort Wainwright, Alaska. This requirement is for a period of performance from 01 May 2026 through 30 April 2027 (base) and four one-year options. The contractor shall be required to provide maintenance and repair services for Government owned STERIS Sterilization Processing Equipment, to include all labor, parts, material and travel, to repair and/or maintain the Government owned equipment in a fully functional condition in accordance with Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEM) specifications. This notice is neither a request for quotes nor a solicitation of offers; however, all responsible sources may submit a capability statement or quotation which shall be considered by the agency. The NAICS code of this procurement is 811210 and the size standard is $34M.

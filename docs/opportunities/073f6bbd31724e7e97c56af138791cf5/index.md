# ALC Modernization and Sustainment Accelerator Event

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-30 13:55:53.153-04
- Solicitation Number: ALCNCMS01

## Summary

In collaboration with the U.S. Coast Guard, National Center for Manufacturing Sciences (NCMS) will be hosting the ALC Modernization and Sustainment Accelerator from June 16-18, 2026. Aviation Logistics Center (ALC) Elizabeth City, NC, is the hub of Coast Guard aviation logistics support. As the sole industrial complex for Coast Guard aviation, ALC provides depot maintenance, engineering, procurement, supply support and logistics information systems to all Coast Guard operational assets. This event will put industry in direct communication with end users. At the forefront of the event will be a day dedicated to showcasing ALC’s mission and operations with a tour of the base. Exhibitors will then disperse by technology focus area to meet with Subject Matter Experts (SME). These SME panelists will touch on ALC capabilities, problem sets, and immediate needs and then open the floor to Q&A from attending industry. Please see (https://ncms.org/events/aviation-logistics-center-alc-modernization-and-sustainment-accelerator/) for more information and to rigister with NCMS.

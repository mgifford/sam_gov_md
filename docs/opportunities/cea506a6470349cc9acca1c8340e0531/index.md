# J--New Flyer Bus Repairs for Yosemite NP

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-30 12:25:18.518-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P8526Q0035

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice for the purpose of conducting market research and obtaining industry information ONLY. This is NOT a request for quotation. The National Park Service is seeking sources to obtain information from businesses which would be interested and capable of providing services to Yosemite National Park, California. The work consists of diagnostics, repair, and corrective maintenance services for seven New Flyer diesel hybrid electric buses. Services may only be performed by an authorized New Flyer repair facility or New Flyer factory-certified technicians. Interested and capable contractors (i.e. authorized New Flyer repair facilities or New Flyer factory-certified technicians) should complete the attached sources sought survey and return it in accordance with the instructions.

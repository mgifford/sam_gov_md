RFI Title: Cost Per Results Reportable (CPRR) Automated Hematology Analyzer for the Defense Health Agency (DHA) Medical Treatment Facility (MTF). LOCATION: Naval Medical Center Portsmouth, 54 Lewis Minor Street, Bldg 250, Portsmouth, VA 23708. BACKGROUND: The Naval Medical Center Portsmouth (NMCP) Laboratory Department has a current contract for CPRR Automated Hematology Analyzer that is expiring on 31 August 2026 and requires a new contract. The system must provide: reliable and extensive high-throughput (? 200 tests per hour) hematology analysis, supporting timely and accurate diagnostic results for patient care, utilize fluorescent flow cytometry to analyze RNA and DNA content and intracellular cellular structures, include integrated automated QC module capable of performing, evaluating, and documenting quality control without user intervention at defined intervals and maintain traceability of all records in accordance with accreditation guidelines, be capable of connecting with middleware and command center components for integrated total lab automation. DESCRIPTION: NAICS 334516 Analytical Laboratory Instrument Manufacturing, PSC 6632 Analysis Instruments, Medical Laboratory Use. See DRAFT Statement of Work (SOW). RFI SUBMISSION INSTRUCTIONS: Responses to the RFI shall adhere to the following formatting and outline instructions: All submissions shall be made electronically and be in one of the following formats: Microsoft Word or Adobe PDF. Cover Page (1 page) - Title - Organization - Responder’s technical and administrative points of contact (names, addresses, phone numbers and email addresses). - CAGE Code 3. Capabilities statement SHALL address the following: - Contractor’s clear understanding and ability to perform the scope of this requirement as detailed in the DRAFT SOW. - Contractor’s experience performing the same or similar work as detailed in the DRAFT SOW. - Existing contract vehicles (e.g., GSA schedule) under which the responder currently offers these services, if any. - Whether the responder provides the required services to the general public under terms and conditions similar to those offered to the Federal Government.

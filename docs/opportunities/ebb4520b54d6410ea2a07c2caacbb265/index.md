# Sealcoat Trailer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 11:47:33.301-04
- Solicitation Number: FA485526Q0012

## Summary

Cannon AFB, NM requires A sealcoat trailer to allow the hot application of asphalt sealant to asphalt surfaces, extending the life expectancy of the surfaces by 50% or greater and will result in a reduction of maintenance costs. Tank: Sealcoat material trailer with a 700-gallon capacity tank. Chassis: Approximately 20-foot standard length frame, equipped with electric brakes and two 6,000 lb capacity axles. Features: The trailer shall include the following integrated features: Tool rack Work lights Brush storage box Onboard water tank Ramp: A loading/unloading ramp shall be included, with the option for it to be mounted on either the passenger or driver side of the trailer. See attachment 1 for additional characteristics. The award of a contract from this solicitation is still subject to funding.

# 6991PE26Q000003 SBRF PF-611 KESTREL Drydock and Repair

- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 07:35:21.905-04
- Solicitation Number: 6991PE26Q000003

## Summary

The PF-611 KESTREL is a catamaran hull patrol boat of aluminum construction and the purpose of this Combined Synopsis/Solicitation is to provide drydock and repair of core services to haul out the boat, abrasive grit blast and preserve the submerged hull surfaces, and clean and service the jet drives.

# 20--Ocean Gold Seafoods, Inc. MFT System Maintenance

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 08:34:12.736-05
- Solicitation Number: 1305M326Q0081

## Summary

A determination not to compete this proposed contract was made pursuant to the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 13.106-1(b). Ocean Gold Seafoods, Inc. is the only known business that can provide maintenance, testing, training, repair, and modification services for the Multi-Function Trawl (MFT) net system previously developed and supplied under Contract 1305M3240021 awarded in 2024. Due to the specialized design and operational requirements of the MFT system, continued maintenance and support services must be performed by the original developer. The Government intends to award a contract for a one-year base period and four one-year option periods. This procurement is being conducted under Simplified Acquisition Procedures pursuant to FAR 13.5, Simplified Procedures for Certain Commercial Products and Commercial Services, only one source is reasonably available. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 314994. Interested parties may submit their interest and capabilities no later than March 9, 2026 at 9:00 AM Eastern Time. E-mail statements will be accepted but senders are responsible for ensuring complete transmission and timely receipt. All inquiries shall be sent via e-mail to: amanda.rossiter@noaa.gov. The Government contemplates a firm-fixed-price commercial services contract for routine inspection, maintenance, repair, modification, testing, training, and associated shipping and travel support for the Multi-Function Trawl system throughout the life of the contract. The intent of this synopsis is for informational purposes, to improve small business access to acquisition information. A determination by the Government not to compete this action is solely within the discretion of the Government.

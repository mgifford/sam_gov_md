# 173d FW Kingsley Field Multiple Award Task Order Contract (MATOC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 17:58:58.898-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8Z26B0001

## Summary

The 173d Fighter Wing, Kingsley Field, Oregon is seeking information concerning the availability of capable contractors with the ability to perform under a Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Multiple Award Task Order Contract (MATOC) with a period of performance of five years within the state of Oregon. The Government is seeking interested contractors with the ability and experience in a full range of construction to include all phases of building, commercial, pavements, utilities, site preparation, renovation, interior/exterior, HVAC, plumbing, roofing, design build, construction administration, inspection and all other aspects to go with a general understanding and ability to perform on federal construction contracts encompassing a wide range of specialties. This request for information (RFI)s shall not be construed as a formal solicitation or as an obligation of the Government to acquire any products or services. Information provided to the Government as a result of this posting is strictly voluntary and given with no expectation of compensation and clearly provided at no cost to the Government. A formal solicitation will be executed and posted sparately at a future date. The contracting officer will endeavor to post all information regarding this solicitation as soon as it becomes available. The 173d FW is interested at this point only in identifying interested and capable contractors for construction as part of the market research effort. Interested vendors are asked to respond to the contacts listed below via email. Supply a response package including the following: 1. Sources Sought Cover Sheet (see Attachments), 2. Single page overview of recent, within five (5) years, and relevant capabilities and projects. Packages shall not exceed the two (2) page total.

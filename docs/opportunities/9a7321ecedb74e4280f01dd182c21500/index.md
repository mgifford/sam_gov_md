# U.S Government Space Required in Elk Grove, CA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 09:56:28.603-04
- Solicitation Number: DACA055260032300

## Summary

The U.S. Government is seeking a full service lease for retail office space with the following requirements: A minimum of 1,000 rentable square feet (RSF) and a maximum of 1,140 RSF of retail office space for a U.S. Armed Forces Career Center Total initial lease term shall not equal or exceed five (5) years. Located within a defined delineated area in Elk Grove, CA N: Laguna Blvd, S: Laguna Park Dr., E: Laguna Park Dr., W: Laguna Park Dr. Designated on-site parking for up to four (4) government vehicles must be provided on a 24 hours 7 day a week basis Offered space must meet Government requirements per the terms of the Government Lease. Interested respondents may include building owners and representatives with the exclusive right to represent building owners. Submission Requirements Interested respondents must submit the following: Building name and address Location of available space within the building Rentable square feet (RSF) and Usable square feet (USF) offered Date space will be available Proposed rental rate per RSF Evidence of ownership or authorization to represent owner Responses are due no later than 12:00PM PDT on 03/25/26. Responses received after the stated deadline may not be considered. The Government intends to conduct a competitive procurement and issue a Request for Lease Proposals (RLP) to respondents whose properties are determined by the Government to meet the minimum requirements stated herein. Responses shall be made electronically via email to the point of contact identified below. Stacy Mooney Realty Specialist U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Sacramento District 916-557-7875 Stacy.A.Mooney@usace.army.mil This advertisement is not a Request for Lease Proposals (RLP) and does not constitute a solicitation for offers. The Government reserves the right to amend, cancel, or modify this requirement at any time. The Government is not responsible for any costs incured in responding to this notice.

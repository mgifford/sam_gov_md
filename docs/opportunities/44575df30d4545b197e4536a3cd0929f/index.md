# DARPA-SN-26-52: RFI - Low Earth Orbit (LEO) and Medium Earth Orbit (MEO) Satellite Power Capability

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 12:44:41.749-04
- Solicitation Number: DARPA-SN-26-52

## Summary

The Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) is requesting information related to space companies’ ability to produce satellites for Low Earth Orbit (LEO) and Medium Earth Orbit (MEO) which can provide medium to high levels of Orbital Average Power (OAP) to the payload.

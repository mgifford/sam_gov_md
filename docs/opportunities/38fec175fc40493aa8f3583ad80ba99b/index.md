# Essayons General Service Corcoran Saltwater Pumps Purchase

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 17:40:06.604-04
- Solicitation Number: W9127N26QA057

## Summary

USACE, Portland District is hereby issuing this Request for Quote (RFQ), W9127N26QA057, soliciting for the brand name only purchase of four (4) Corcoran saltwater pumps for the Dredge Essayons Vessel. The attached solicitation document, incorporated provisions and clauses are in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular FAC 2025-06. This solicitation is advertised as 100% Small Business Set aside under NAICS code 333914 - Measuring, Dispensing, and Other Pumping Equipment Manufacturing. The Small Business Size standard for this NAICS code is 750 employees. All prospective offerors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) at www.sam.gov to be eligible for award. Lack of SAM registration will make an offeror ineligible for award. See the attached solicitation document for terms and conditions. Please review the Purchase Description (PD) and solicitation in their entirety. Provide pricing for each CLIN listed starting on page 2 of the solicitation document, sign on page 1 in box 30a, and return to be considered for award. Supplies/Services will be inspected/accepted: All will be inspected/accepted at Destination by the Government. Period of Performance Information: The anticipated delivery date is expected no later than 180 days from date of award. If you believe you will need more time please state so and the reasoning within your quote. Point of contact (POC): The point of contact for this opportunity is Jaren Bowman and can be reached by email at Jaren.L.Bowman@usace.army.mil Only electronic (email) quote submission will be accepted for this opportunity and must be sent to both emails below: Jaren.L.Bowman@usace.army.mil: & Brandon.M.Lasich@usace.army.mil

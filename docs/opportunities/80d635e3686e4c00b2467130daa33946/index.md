## Summary

The NNSA requires deep insights into its production enterprise to better understand opportunities to increase efficiency and output. To address the administration’s interest in understanding opportunities for increased production throughput, a more comprehensive assessment of the Office of Defense Programs’ (DP) production process, including trade- and decision-spaces, is required. This project is designed to address this need.

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all plant, labor, material, design and supervision to provide and deliver approximately 8,001 tons of hot mix asphalt (HMA), and 8,072 gallons of Bituminous Tack and Prime Coats. Contractor shall also provide field and laboratory quality control testing, as required by the Government. Please see attached SOW for more information.

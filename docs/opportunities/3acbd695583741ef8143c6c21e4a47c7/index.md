# LSJ: NLM LHNCBC: Software, Database Administration, and Support Services

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-07 15:50:24.694-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N97022F00091-2

## Summary

The Government intends to issue a modification to extend GSA Contract # 47QTCA18D00JA, Task Order (TO) # 75N97022F00091 for seventy-five (75) days to prevent a lapse in service for software, database administration, and support services for NLM LHNCBC from March 25, 2026, to June 07, 2026. This proposed extension is to prevent a break of service while providing the Government with the necessary time to award the competitive recompete acquisition. This acqusition is conducted under the authority of the GSA Multiple-Award Schedule (GSA MAS) program. Consideration of schdule contractors will be restricted in accordance with: FAR 8.405-6(a)(i)(B) Only one source is capable of providing the supplies or services required at the level of quality required because the supplies or services are unique or highly specialized.

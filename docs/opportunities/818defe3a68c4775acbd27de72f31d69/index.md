# J065--Base YR (05/01/2026-04/30/2027) Medtronic Stealthstation S8 Maintenance

## Summary

The Department of Veteran Affairs, VISN 12, awarded a firm-fixed price order to Medtronic USA, Inc. for the preventive and corrective maintenance of the Stealthstation S8 at the Milwaukee VA Medical Center in Milwaukee, WI. Order 36C25226N0281 was awarded for the period of May 1, 2026 through April 30, 2027, with an additional four (4) option years having an ultimate completion date of 04/30/2031. The total contract is $347,650.00.

# Z--Boiler Replacement Project in Lima, MT

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 14:20:25.774-04
- Solicitation Number: 140FGA26Q0015

## Summary

This is a Synopsis/Pre-Solicitation notice. The final solicitation package will follow. This opportunity is only available to Small Businesses. The U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service, has a requirement for the replacement of a boiler at Red Rock Lakes at 27505 S Valley Road, Lima MT 59739. This requirement is being procured in accordance with FAR Part 15, Contracting by Negotiation. This will be a Firm Fixed Priced Contract and Award will be based on a best value lowest price technically acceptable evaluation. The complete Solicitation will be posted to www.sam.gov/ under Contract Opportunities on or about March 26th, 2026. Interested contractors must be registered in SAM (System for Award Management, www.sam.gov) and complete Online Representations and Certifications. A pre-proposal site visit and instructions will be announced with the solicitation. The NAICS code is 238220 Plumbing, Heating and Air-Con and the size standard is $19.5 million. The project magnitude is under $50,000. The work will commence within 10 days of Notice to Proceed and be completed by May 15, 2026. Direct questions in writing to Justine Coleman, Justine_Coleman@ios.doi.gov.

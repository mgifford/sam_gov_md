# PIMC Elevator PM and Repair Services

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 12:46:34.925-04
- Solicitation Number: IHS1519530A

## Summary

This is a federal facility on federal property and the authority having jurisdiction (AHJ) is not the City of Phoenix. The AHJ is the Phoenix Area Indian Health Service Director of the Office of Environmental Health and Engineering. There are four (4) total elevator cars operating in two (2) elevator shafts in the main tower of the hospital. The elevator shaft on the west side houses two (2) cars that serve staff only (PIV access card required to operate – Cars #1 and #2) and the elevator shaft on the and east side houses two (2) elevator cars that serve patients (Car #3 and #4). The elevator mechanical room is accessed on the tower roof in the penthouse above the 5th floor of the hospital tower. SEE ATTACHMENTS FOR MORE INFORMATION AND SUBMITTAL INSTRUCTIONS.

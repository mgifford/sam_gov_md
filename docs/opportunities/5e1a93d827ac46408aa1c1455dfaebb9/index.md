## Summary

The Government requires the supply and delivery of boiler treatment chemicals and associated materials in accordance with the requirements of this solicitation. The contractor shall provide all items listed in the schedule that meet or exceed the specified salient characteristics. Items shall be brand name or equal and must be fully compatible with existing Government equipment and systems. All materials shall comply with applicable safety, environmental, and handling regulations. Delivery shall be coordinated with the designated Government point of contact to ensure timely receipt and proper handling of materials. Offerors shall submit their responses to this RFQ no later than 1 April 2026 at 1630 (JST). All questions shall be submitted via email to ingrid.n.guzmansanchez.mil@usmc.mil no later than 30 March 2026 at 0800 (JST).

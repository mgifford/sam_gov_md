# U.S. Government Space Required in Livermore, California

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 12:25:14.369-05
- Solicitation Number: DACA055260030700

## Summary

The U.S. Government is seeking a full service lease for retail office space with the following requirements: A minimum of 2930 rentable square feet (RSF) and a maximum of 3000 RSF of retail office space for a U.S. Armed Forces Career Center Total initial lease term shall not equal or exceed five (5) years. Located within a defined delineated area in Livermore, California City Limits Undesignated on-site parking for up to Ten (10) government vehicles must be provided on a 24 hours 7 day a week basis Offered space must meet Government requirements per the terms of the Government Lease. Interested respondents may include building owners and representatives with the exclusive right to represent building owners. Submission Requirements Interested respondents must submit the following: Building name and address Location of available space within the building Rentable square feet (RSF) and Usable square feet (USF) offered Date space will be available Proposed rental rate per RSF Evidence of ownership or authorization to represent owner Responses are due no later than 06:00 PM PDT on March 21, 2026. Responses received after the stated deadline may not be considered. The Government intends to conduct a competitive procurement and issue a Request for Lease Proposals (RLP) to respondents whose properties are determined by the Government to meet the minimum requirements stated herein.

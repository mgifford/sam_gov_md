# JetSCAN Oil Analyzer Maintenance and Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-01 15:05:25.462-04
- Solicitation Number: N6833525Q0375
- Awardee: Rolls-Royce Corporation Indianapolis IN 46225 USA
- Award Amount: 100290.00

## Summary

The Naval Warfare Center Aircraft Division, Lakehurst, NJ awarded a Firm Fixed-Price Contract to Rolls Royce Corporation (CAGE: 63005) on 1 April 2026 for the repair of the JetSCAN Oil Analyzer to provide extended annual scheduled maintenance service and repair support coverage for three (3) United States Marine Corps JetSCAN Oil Analysis Systems for the AV-8B Harrier, F-402 engine.

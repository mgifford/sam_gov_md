The Veterans Health Administration, Network Contracting Office 02 intends to solicit from a single source IAW FAR 12.201-1 to obtain a service contract for onsite repairs, repair parts, unlimited phone support, loaner instrument, and scheduled preventative maintenance for NOVA Biomedical OsmoPRO (SN: 24080898A). Anticipated Period of Performance: 5/1/2026 to 4/30/2027 with the provision of four (4) 12-month Options through 4/30/2031. Basic Description of The Requirement: James J. The service contract is needed for onsite repairs, repair parts, unlimited phone support, loaner instrument, and scheduled preventative maintenance. Place of Performance: James J. Peters VA Medical Center 130 West Kingsbridge Road Bronx, NY 10468 Chemistry Laboratory, 2B-15 NAICS code: 811210 (Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance) Small Business Size Standard: $34 Million. Intended Source: Advanced Instruments, LLC D/B/A Nova Biomedical Corporation Two Technology Way Norwood, Massachusetts, 02062 USA THIS IS NOT A NOTICE FOR COMPETITIVE QUOTES; however, any firm that believes it can meet the requirements above, may give written notice prior to the response due date and time.

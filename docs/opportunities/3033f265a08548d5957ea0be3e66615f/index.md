# Wideband Satcom AAU Lifting Adapter

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 10:47:16.647-04
- Solicitation Number: N68335-26-Q-1049

## Summary

The United States Navy Program Office PMA 290, has a new requirement for the manufacturing of Wideband Satcom AAU Lifting Adapter. The Government owns the Build-to-Print Technical Drawing Package and the drawings are considered Export Controlled. All Offerors must be able to provide a certified DD Form 2345. All requesting Offerors must have a current certified DD2345 to obtain the drawings. Drawings will be provided to your designated data custodian on your certified DD2345 via the DoD Safe website. All Offerors, other than the successful Offeror, are required to destroy all export control data, regardless of form or the media on which it may be recorded, including CDs, data stored on other computer storage devices and paper documents. Offerors shall provide proof of such destruction via signed letter on corporate letterhead to the PCO via email at alexis.r.kelly.civ@us.navy.mil

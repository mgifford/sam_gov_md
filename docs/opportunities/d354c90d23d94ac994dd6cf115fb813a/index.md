# Reagents Compatible with the Olink Proximity Extension Assay (PEA)

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 11:02:27.106-04
- Solicitation Number: 1232SA26Q0306

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0306 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for reagents compatible with the Olink Proximity Extension Assay (PEA) to conduct analysis of protein biomarkers to continue ongoing studies. The reagents are required to carry out quantitative analysis of protein levels in plasma and serum samples from clinical studies. The levels of specific biomarkers will be measured using the Target 96 assay. Olink’s Proximity Extension Assay (PEA) technology combines specificity and scalability to enable high-throughput, multiplex protein biomarker analysis. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 334516. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

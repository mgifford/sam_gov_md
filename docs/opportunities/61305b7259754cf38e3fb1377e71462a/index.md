# Special Notice: Common Tactical Truck (CTT) Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) / Area of Interest (AoI) - Anticipated Quantities & Contractor Virtual (MS Teams) Meetings

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-26 07:16:37.968-04
- Solicitation Number: CTT-009

## Summary

To support contractor planning, CTT anticipates the following quantities: · Initial projected quantity: 758 systems under the initial requirement projected over 5 years, pending available funding. · Projected Approved Acquisition Objective (AAO): 36,495 systems. · Multi-vendor consideration: Program quantities may be divided among multiple vendors if multiple contracts are awarded. · Funding contingency: All quantities are subject to change based on funding availability. +++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++ The Program Office and ACC will host 30-minute Teams meetings for interested potential contractors on March 30–31 to address any outstanding questions regarding the CTT CSO and AOI. Please submit your meeting request to mailbox usarmy.detroit.cpe-cl.mbx.jtwv-ctt-rfp@army.mil with your preferred date, and a time will be provided. If you have questions prepared in advance, please include them with your request—responses will be provided during your scheduled session. Please note: This opportunity does not constitute grounds for a deadline extension and is intended solely to clarify questions as you finalize your white papers.

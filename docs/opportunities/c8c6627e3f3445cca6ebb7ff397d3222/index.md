# Notice of Intent to award Janitorial Service at Croatan Ranger District BPA Call

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-16 07:46:10.235-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444126F0035

## Summary

The USDA Forest Service (FS), Procurement & Property Services (PPS), Field Procurement Operations East Zone, Supply & Services CSA #3 is issuing this synopsis as a means of complying with the USDA’s policy, FAR Deviation 12.201-1 Simplified procedures, and FAR deviation 12.201-1(e)(3) Blanket Purchase Agreements (BPAs). The USDA FS intends on awarding a BPA Call Order against BPA for Croatan Ranger District Janitorial Services BPA 12444123A0013 awarded against solicitation number: 12444123Q0030. This is not a solicitation. This synopsis notification of intent to award a BPA Call order does not constitute a request for proposals or submission of any information in response to this notification. This procurement is restricted to the approved company eligible for call orders under BPA 12444123A0013. The scope of the BPA is to provide janitorial services Croatan Ranger District. This requirement will be awarded as a BPA Call Order against the BPA previously solicited on the Government Point of Entry (GPE). The government will not provide feedback or evaluations to companies regarding their submissions in response to this notice.

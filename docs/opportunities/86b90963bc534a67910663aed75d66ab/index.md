## Summary

PROJECT NUMBER: CO NP COLM 10(5) PROJECT NAME: Guardrail Improvement PROJECT DESCRIPTION: Guardrail replacement and new installation – Colorado National Monument, Mesa County, CO. SCOPE OF THE WORK: Work consists of existing guardrail, guardrail post, guardrail block, and end terminal replacement. New guardrail and building up portions of the shoulder is proposed in three areas. One area will have resetting of boulders.

# Procurement of Replacement Air Filters for the U.S. Embassy Hanoi

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 21:33:33.212-04
- Solicitation Number: 19VM3026Q0012

## Summary

Enclosed is the Request for Quotation (RFQ) package #19VM3026Q0012 with proposed technical specifications for the Procurement of Replacement Air filters for the U.S. Embassy Hanoi. The Contractor shall provide a quotation including VAT (if applicable), door-to-door delivery fees to the shipping address listed in the RFQ; and any other anticipated charges needed. The quotation shall notify the delivery schedule, warranty plan, and any other accompanied accessories to the item offered. The U.S. Government intends to award a contract/purchase order for this requirement to the responsible company submitting an acceptable quotation at the lowest price. We intend to award a contract/purchase order based on initial quotations, without holding discussions, although we may hold discussion with companies in the competitive range if there is a need to do so. For offers at or over US $40,000, offerors shall be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) database at https://www.sam.gov prior to submittal of their offer/proposal as prescribed under FAR 4.1102. Failure to be registered at the time of proposal submission may cause the offeror’s proposal to be considered non-responsible and no further consideration will be given. Therefore, offerors are highly encouraged to register immediately if they are interested in submitting a response to this requirement. Quotations are due at 17:00, April 15, 2026 local time. No quotations will be accepted after this time. Quotations must be in English and incomplete proposals will not be accepted.

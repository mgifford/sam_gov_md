## Summary

SYNOPSIS OF AWARD: Stevedoring and Related Terminal Services HTC71126DE126 PORT OF KOPER, SLOVENIA On 02 April 2026, Shipping Consultants Associated, Ltd. was awarded a $30,613,357.50 Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity, fixed-priced contract for Stevedoring and Related Terminal Services at Port of Koper, Slovenia in response to Solicitation HTC71126RE116. Work will be performed at the Port of Koper during the 01 May 2026 through 30 April 2031 performance period with an expected completion date of 30 April 2031. This contract was a competitive acquisition. The U.S. Transportation Command, Directorate of Acquisition, 508 Scott Drive, Scott AFB, IL is the contracting activity.

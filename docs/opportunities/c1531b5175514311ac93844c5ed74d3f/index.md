# Language Translation English to Central Alaskan Yup'ik

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 18:36:21.439-04
- Solicitation Number: 70FBR026Q00000013

## Summary

The Federal Emergency Mangement Agency (FEMA) Presidentially Declared Disaster DR-4893-AK, Typhoon Halong, intends to award a purchase order for in person language interpretation between English and Cental Alaskan Yup'ik. This solicitation uses FAR Subpart

12.6 Streamlined Procedures for Evaluation and Solicitation for Commercial Products and Commercial Services. Interpretation between English and Central Alaskan Yu'pik may be conducted in person and in some cases over the phone or by electronic communication methods. This opportunity is a disaster area set aside for those towns and villages located within the declared disaster area of DR-4893-AK, which can be found at https://www.fema.gov/disaster/4893. Local preference will be given. Review the attached solicitation and performance work statement.

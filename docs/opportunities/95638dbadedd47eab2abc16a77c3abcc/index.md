# Hand Sanitizer Recycling/Disposal

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 11:21:00.686-04
- Solicitation Number: 15F06726Q0000094

## Summary

After COVID-19, the FBI was left with a surplus of expired hand sanitizer. Due to the high alcohol content, this material must be handled and recycled/disposed correctly according to EPA and DOT regulations. The Contractor shall provide all services necessary to recycle or dispose of expired or unused hand sanitizer. Quantity is as follows: 82 full boxes on five pallets. Each box holds 12 bottles, approximately 2184 bottles in total. Each bottle is 750ml. The work includes pickup, transport, recycling/disposal, and provision of documentation in accordance with applicable Federal, State, and local regulations. Questions are due 4/2/26 no later than 4pm EST. Questions must be submitted via email to mgolicz@fbi.gov via the questions Excel template.

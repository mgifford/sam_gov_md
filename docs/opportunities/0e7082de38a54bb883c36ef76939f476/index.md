# Geotechnical Survey AALZ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 16:20:44.92-04
- Solicitation Number: FA446026Q0371

## Summary

Geotechnical survey and report for an Unimproved Landing Zone at All American Landing Zone to understand soils water content, Atterberg limits, and grain size analysis. The contractor will be required to conduct twenty-six borings and these borings will be examined and tested in order to classify the soil samples and provide the Government with a detailed report. A site visit will be conducted 23 April 2026 @ 10:00 AM CST. Questions will be due by 30 April 2026 @ 2:00 PM CST. See attached Combo FA446026Q0371 for more information.

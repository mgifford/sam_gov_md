# Procurement and Installation of One (1) Powerex SET2257 Air Compressor System

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-20 16:58:12.807-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N91026P00152
- Awardee: LBS ENTERPRISES INC White Marsh MD 21162 USA
- Award Amount: 37700.00

## Summary

An air compressor system (coupled with a refrigerated air dryer) is necessary to generate high-pressure air to deliver to a Peak Infinity 5060 nitrogen generator to be purified into 99.95% pure nitrogen gas. A consistent and reliable compressed air source in is required to ensure appropriate operation of our nitrogen generator and our five mass spectrometers within the Clinical Pharmacology Program (CPP) within the Office of the Clinical Director, CCR/NCI/NIH. Our existing Hitachi Air Compressor, M/N: SRL-16.5MB6MH, S/N: QB003096 model requires significant repairs and needs to be replaced by the SET2257HP Air Compressor model.

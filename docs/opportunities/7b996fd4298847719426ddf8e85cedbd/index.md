# Sources Sought for the Establishment of a Center of Excellence for Energetics

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-13 15:47:49.429-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC-26-R-0019

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command-Rock Island (ACC-RI), on behalf of the Office of Project Lead Joint Services (PL JS), is seeking information and feedback from domestic industry sources on the potential for design, development, construction, commissioning, and operation of a comprehensive, modern, and efficient "Center of Excellence" (CoE) for scale-up and production of current and emerging explosives to be located at Blue Grass Army Depot, KY. The Army is interested in rapid acceleration of this capability with completion prior to 2031. Refer to the Sources Sought Attachment 0001 for more details.

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy in Georgetown has a requirement for technician to assist the local facilities staff in Georgetown, Guyana, with repairing and trouble shooting the Multistack Set-B chiller. The technican will help the local team install new controllers on the mechinacal modules and the heat exchange module, including a software upgrade as well as replace the pump motors on the existing pumps.

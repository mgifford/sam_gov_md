# Request for Information (RFI) USINDOPACOM AGT 1500

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-08 06:46:43.383-04
- Solicitation Number: USINDOPACOM_AGT_1500_RFI

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command- Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) is seeking to gain knowledge of interest, capabilities and qualifications of various members of the community for AGT 1500 Engine Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) to RESET Standard in the U.S. Indo-Pacific Command (USINDOPACOM) Region. This is a Request for Information (RFI) only. NOTE: All RFI input is voluntary, and no compensation will be made for your firm’s participation. Since this is an RFI, and not a solicitation or RFP for receipt of proposals or quotations, absolutely no telephone calls will be accepted concerning this notice. The Government is not obligated to issue a solicitation or award a contract as a result of this notice. All questions relating to this RFI shall be submitted in writing only via email to Army Contracting Command point of contact (POC) Christine Marr, christine.m.marr.civ@army.mil.

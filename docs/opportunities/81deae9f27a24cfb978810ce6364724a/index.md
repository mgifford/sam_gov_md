# Zeiss LSM 980 confocal microscope equipped with Airyscan maintenance

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 08:08:18.798-04
- Solicitation Number: 7332015

## Summary

The requirement is for manufacturer-authorized service and maintenance of an existing Zeiss LSM 980 confocal microscope equipped with Airyscan 2. The service shall include diagnostic evaluation, repair, calibration, proprietary software updates, and replacement of OEM components as necessary to restore and maintain full operational capability in accordance with original manufacturer specifications. Carl Zeiss Microscopy, LLC is the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and the only known source capable of providing factory-certified technicians, proprietary diagnostic tools, firmware updates, and genuine replacement parts required to maintain system compatibility and performance integrity.

# Electric Walk-Behind Forklift

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 14:21:40.174-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1022

## Summary

This is an official Request for Quote (RFQ) Notice (FA462526Q1022). Amendment 1: The purpose for this amendment is to add the answers to vendor questions provided by the requirement owner. See Attachment 5. NRH S The U.S. Air Force 509th Contracting Squadron at Whiteman AFB is posting this solicitation, Request for Quote (RFQ), for purchase of an Electric Walk Behind Forklift that meets the specifications of the attached Salient Characteristics dated 05 March 2026. DESCRIPTION OF REQUIREMENT: A functioning Electric Walking Forklift. Characteristics of TOYOTA MODEL 6BWR15 or equal. 3,000 lbs lifting capacity. 24v Electric Battery Powered. DELIVERABLES: The contractor shall deliver 120 days after receipt of the order. Delivery will be to Whiteman AFB, MO 65305. RESPONSES: Questions are due to both the Contracting Officer: Molly Nussbaum, at molly.nussbaum@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 2:00 PM CDT, Wednesday, 12 March 2026. Questions received after that time may not be answered. To ensure all questions and responses are received, questions and responses must include the RFQ Number (FA462526Q1022) and title Electic Walk-Behind Forklift in the subject line of the email submission and are to be sent via email to both the Contracting Officer: Molly Nussbaum, at molly.nussbaum@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 2:00 PM CDT, Wednesday, 25 March 2026.

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs intends to award a firm-fixed-price contract for the procurement of Negative Air Pressure Testing Services. Site Visit Notification: A site visit is scheduled to take place on Thursday April 23, 2026, 10:00 AM Eastern Time. The location of the site visit is the Gainesville VA Medical Center, located at 1601 Southwest Archer Road Gainesville, FL 32608. Check-In Location: Lobby of the Bed Tower 1st Floor. Please plan to arrive promptly, as parking is limited at the Gainesville VAMC facility. The site visit will begin at the scheduled time. Late arrivals may not be permitted to participate. Upon arrival, all attendees must check in with the Contracting Officer Representative (COR) and sign the attendance sheet. This site visit will provide potential offerors an opportunity to inspect the site and gather pertinent information. Attendance is not mandatory but highly encouraged.

# Market Research - Immediate delivery of twenty (20) large, Level 6 Armored SUVs

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-08 16:17:02.637-04
- Solicitation Number: W912R126Q7008

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) and Sources Sought Notice is for market research purposes only. The District of Columbia National Guard (DCNG) requires the immediate delivery of twenty (20) large, Level 6 Armored SUVs, each with a minimum seating capacity for six (6) individuals. This protection level is required to defend against 7.62mm rifle rounds, ensuring robust security for all occupants. CRITICAL REQUIREMENT: Companies should only respond to this notice if they have qualifying vehicles currently in stock and available for immediate shipment. Previous market research indicates that models with the necessary protection levels are available. Please respond with a list of your available, in-stock options that are ready for immediate delivery.

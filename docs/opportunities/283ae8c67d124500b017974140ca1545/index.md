# IONM Intraoperative Neuromonitoring Services - Atlanta VAMC

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 15:22:59.23-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24726Q0246

## Summary

The Contractor shall furnish all key personnel to provide services necessary to perform onsite Interoperative Neurosurgery (IONM) Services to eligible beneficiaries of the Department of Veterans Affairs. The Contractor’s provider(s)’ care shall cover the range of INTEROPERATIVE NEUROSURGERY (IONM) services as would be provided in a state-of-the-art civilian medical treatment facility and the standard of care shall be of a quality, meeting or exceeding currently recognized national standards American Association for Neurological Surgeons. https://www.aans.org/ Place of Performance: Services shall be provided on site, at the Atlanta VA Health Care System, 1670 Clairmont Road, Decatur, GA 30033. The Government contemplates award of a 5-yr Firm-Fixed-Price Indefinite-Delivery / Indefinite-Quantity (FFP ID/IQ) contract resulting from this solicitation. This contract replaces 36C24723C0071.

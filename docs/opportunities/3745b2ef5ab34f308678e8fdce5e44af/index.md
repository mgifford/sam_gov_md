# Pyxis Profile Software Upgrade

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-11 14:37:56.289-04

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office 15 intends to award a sole source contract under the authority of 41 U.S.C 253(c) (1) – FAR 6.302-1 Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements, to Carefusion Solutions, LLC, located at 3750 Torrey View Court, San Diego, CA 92130. Carefusion Solutions, LLC, will provide the software upgrade services, to the existing Pyxis profiling system. The Pyxis system is proprietary and may only be upgraded by Carefusion Solutions, LLC. The place of performance is for the Department of Veterans Affairs – John J. Pershing VA Medical Center, 1500 N. Westwood Blvd., Poplar Bluff, MO 63901-3318. The NAICS code is 541511 – Custom Computer Programming Services, with a size standard of $34 Million, and PSC 7A21. This notice of intent is not a request for quotations; interested parties may express their interest by providing a capabilities statement No Later Than 03/20/2026 at 11:00 am Central Time. The capabilities statement must provide clear and unambiguous evidence to substantiate the capability of the party to provide the required services. A determination not to compete this proposed contract upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Verbal responses are not acceptable and will not be considered. Response shall be submitted via email referencing the “Special Notice - 36C25526Q0253” in the subject line to veronica.flores@va.gov. If after 03/20/2026, no viable responses have been received in response to this announcement, the Department of Veterans Affairs shall negotiate solely with Carefusion Solutions, LLC. This notice of intent to award a sole source contract is not a request for competitive quotes. There will be no solicitation available for competitive quotes. Phone calls will not be accepted. Point of Contact: Betty Flores veronica.flores@va.gov Network Contracting Office 15 3450 South. 4th Street Leavenworth, KS 66048

Ship to Location: USCG Air Station Clearwater 15100 Rescue Way Clearwater, FL 33762 Attn: Jose Calderon Description of Supply: This requirement is for the procurement of a potable water booster pump skid (in-kind) for Great Inagua, a remote island in the Bahamas with limited access to services and contractors. The objective is to replace the existing potable water booster pump skid, which is over 15 years old and has experienced multiple failures. Currently, pump #1 is operating with temporary repairs to maintain water pressure for the hangar and eight housing units, while pump #2's housing and motor have failed and require replacement. The piping system is also severely corroded and must be replaced to ensure a reliable water supply. If this project is not completed, the hangar and eight housing units will not have sufficient water pressure for essential needs such as bathing, cooking, and restroom facilities. Identification of Statutory Exception and Rationale. The request is for a contractor to obtain required potable water booster pump skid from vendor Quantum Flo in Sanford, FL. Due to Great Inagua’s remote location in the Bahamas and limited access to services and contractors, Coast Guard personnel will transport and install the replacement pump skid. Selecting Quantum Flo ensures compatibility with the existing piping and electrical systems, as the entire potable water system was originally designed to operate with this manufacturer’s equipment. 52.212-1 -- Instructions to Offerors -- Commercial Items.

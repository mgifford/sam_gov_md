## Summary

The Department of Veteran s Affairs will solicit for vendors capable of providing Zeiss Forum Software Upgrade for the Salem VA Medical Center, located in Salem, VA. This will be awarded as a Firm-Fixed contract for a One Time Job. A Request for Quotation, RFQ, will be issued on March 12, 2026. To be eligible for the award, the prospective contractor must be registered in SAM database at http://www.sam.gov and comply with the Evaluation for Basis of Award at the time the quote is submitted. The prospective contractor must also have updated and correct information in the VA Financial Service Center s Customer Engagement Portal. If you are an SDVOSB, VOSB or Small Business and plan on subcontracting, please provide a plan to show how your company provides 51% of the requirement. The North American Industry Classification Code, NAICS, is 811210 (Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance), Product Service Code is J065 (Maintenance, Repair, and Rebuilding of Equipment Medical, Dental, and Veterinary Equipment and Supplies) and the Size Standard is $34 Million. Interested and capable contractors should reach out to Satasha Stewart via the information provided below. Point of Contact Satasha Stewart Satasha.Stewart@va.gov *Please direct all questions to the above email address. Phone calls will not be accepted.

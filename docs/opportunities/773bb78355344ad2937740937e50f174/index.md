## Summary

USDA-APHIS-Wildlife Services needs construction of a new kennel building per the final design documents developed by the A&E firm Cushing Terrell. The structure will be a pre-engineered metal building of approximately 3,400SF, containing four sections: kennel areas A & B, mud room and mechanical room. The Predator Research Facility’s mission is to generate and disseminate ecological information related to carnivore management, particularly aimed at reducing conflicts with livestock, humans, and native prey species.

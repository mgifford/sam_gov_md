# Furnishing Movement BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 14:12:01.779-04
- Solicitation Number: FA302226R0003

## Summary

Columbus AFB is in need of a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) to be fulfilled as needed, usually one day (preferably Tuesday), bi-weekly (not to include Mass Move situations). The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items necessary, except for those items specified as governmentfurnished property and services, to perform Furnishings Management Services (FMS) at Columbus Air Force Base (AFB), Mississippi. Site Visit, RSVP, and Base Entry: The Government invites you to a site visit on, 23 April 2026 at 9:00 AM CST. Attendance is highly encouraged but not mandatory. Please note that registration is required by 12:00 PM CST on 16 April 2026. To register, please email the POC requesting a DoD Safe drop-off link to securely upload your completed Entry Authorization Listing (EAL) document (found in the list of SAM.gov attachments). If you do not contact the POC by 12:00 PM CST on 16 April 2026, you will not be able to attend the site visit. Identification: All attendees must bring a valid photo ID. Vehicle Requirements: If driving on base, please ensure the driver has driver's license, vehicle registration, and proof of insurance. IMPORTANT: All contractors should arrive at Columbus AFB’s Visitors Center early and wait for a 14th Contracting Squadron representative to arrive and lead them to the site visit location. The representative will leave the visitors center at 0850. Questions are due by April 30th. Proposals are due by May 13th.

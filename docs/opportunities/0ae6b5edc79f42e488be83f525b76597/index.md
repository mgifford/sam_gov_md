## Summary

PROJECT NUMBER: 548-CSI-139 PROJECT TITLE: Bid Build (BB) BB Construction Renovate IR Cath Lab PROJECT LOCATION: Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center, 7305 N. Military Trail, West Palm Beach, FL, 33410 PROJECT SUMMARY: The contractor shall provide all labor, tools, and materials necessary for the following work: Bid-Build project that includes demolition and construction of a new IR Cath-Lab and associated spaces on the second floor of the VA Medical Center at West Palm Beach, FL. The proposed renovations are located adjacent to the existing Cardiac Lab 2B-238 and modernize approximately 2,110 square feet to the minimum requirements of a Hybrid OR space in accordance with the Surgical and Endovascular Services Design Guide, VA design manuals, design guides and specifications. Project is located at the Thomas H. Corey VA Medical Center, 7305 N. Military Trail, West Palm Beach, FL, 33410.

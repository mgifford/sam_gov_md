# Deschutes National Forest, Bend-Fort Rock Ranger District - Darlene Cultural Heritage Surveys

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 17:07:56.263-05
- Solicitation Number: 1240BE26Q0038

## Summary

The Deschutes National Forest (DNF) seeks a cultural resource contractor to complete pedestrian inventory and cultural resource documentation in compliance with Section 106 of the National Historic Preservation Act for the Darlene 3 and Tepee Fire Site Prep and Reforestation. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1240BE26Q0038 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Darlene Heritage Surveys . This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 541620 The small business size standard is $19.0 mil This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

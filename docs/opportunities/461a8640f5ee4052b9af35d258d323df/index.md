# Air Techniques International (ATI) 2i Ensemble with iProbe Plus Aerosol Photometers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 08:43:09.879-04
- Solicitation Number: HT942626QE009

## Summary

Facility Management Office at USAMRIID has a requirement to procure two (2) Air Techniques International (ATI) 2i Ensemble with iProbe Plus Aerosol Photometers with probes and cases to perform HEPA filter certifications required by Biosafety in Microbiological and Biomedical Laboratories (BMBL). The Aerosol Photometers are required for the Institute to remain compliant per regulatory standards put forth by the Centers for Disease Control (CDC) to certify HEPA filters installed in supply and exhaust filter housings to ensure proper filtration of air translations to and from BSL3 and BSL4 laboratory spaces.

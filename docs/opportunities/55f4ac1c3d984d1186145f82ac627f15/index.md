# Vertical Transportation Equipment (VTE) Maintenance, Repair, & Inspection Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 13:03:41.048-04
- Solicitation Number: 2026-PZI-1699

## Summary

The Civil Engineering Squadron (66 ABG/CE) has a continuing requirement for Vertical Transportation Equipment (VTE) maintenance services. The Contractor shall provide all management, tools, supplies, equipment, parts and labor necessary to maintain, repair, and inspect the real property VTE at Hanscom AFB in a manner that will ensure continuous and safe operations. The VTE and their locations will be listed in Appendix B on the Performance Work Statement (PWS) attachment. *Questions are due no later than 1:00pm ET 27 March 2026. Answers will be provided no later than 1:00 PM ET 31 March 2026. *Offers are due on or before 1:00 PM ET 9 April 2026. *A site visit will NOT be held for this requirement. Amendment 1: Adding PWS

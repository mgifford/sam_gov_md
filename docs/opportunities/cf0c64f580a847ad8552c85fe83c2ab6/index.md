## Summary

Please see attached document JEFO-FY26 Medical All-in-One_(AIO) Computers - DHMSM_Redacted. TAA Compliant All-in-One computer/monitor. The DT Research All-In-One (AIO) is uniquely compatible with our power consumption requirements for both our workstation on wheels (WOWs) and our Operating Room (OR) Anesthesia workflow. Each of the peripheral support hardware items went through extensive testing and software configurations during initial development and has been carefully vetted through the Configuration Control Board (CCB) and tested by the DHMSM Program Management Office (PMO) System Engineering team for connection with the MHS GENESIS system.

# USDA Feasibility Study on Consolidating Wildland Fire Management Operations

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-20 11:01:49.26-04
- Solicitation Number: FY26-004145

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) is conducting market research and requesting industry feedback regarding the feasibility study on consolidating federal wildland fire management operations. Two RFIs are being issued simultaneously (GSA and SAM) as the USDA is considering multiple approaches. This RFI is issued solely for information and planning purposes; it does not constitute a solicitation, and no contract will be awarded from this notice. Please see attached Request for Information - Market Research and Draft Statement Of Objectives

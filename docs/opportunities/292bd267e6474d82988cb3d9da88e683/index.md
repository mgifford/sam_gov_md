# Ramp Clearing Plow

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:37:28.163-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U2776

## Summary

MODEL- Craig WL-RCS (Wheel Loader - Ramp Clearing Snowplow); OVERALL WIDTH - 22ft; MOLDBOARD HEIGHT - 55in ? 65in at center, tall, flared moldboard designed to contain and cast snow at high speeds without spillage; CONSTRUCTION - All-welded steel construction using Grade 50 or higher steel with fusion lugging Installed; CUTTING EDGE - Rigid non trip edge drilled for A.A.S.H.O. blade or equivalent (essential for protecting sensitive tarmac and runway surfaces from damage and reversible for extended life); TRIP MECHANISM - Full-moldboard trip with heavy-duty coil springs. Allows the entire plow blade to tip forward upon impact with a hidden object, protecting the plow, loader, and operator; MOUNTING SYSTEM - OEM Coupler (CAT Fusion) that ensures a secure and correct fit to the 938 loader arms; APPROX WEIGHT - 4,800 - 6,200 lbs and must be matched to the loaders safe operating capacity; STANDARD FEATURES - High-visibility blade guides, adjustable, abrasion-resistant skid shoes, reinforced top and bottom channels for rigidity. Includes all necessary components for safe and effective operation; ADDITIONAL FEATURES REQUIRED - No down pressure float installed. TRAINING - Offer shall include onsite training for up to 15 personnel; Offer shall include two (2) operators manuals; Final price shall include delivery to 76544. BUY AMERICAN FORM - Shall be completed and included with offer.

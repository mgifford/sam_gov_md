# N66001-26-Q- 6073? Notice of Intent to Sole Source to Tel Tech Plus, Inc. (CAGE Code: 3CP25)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-07 15:37:12.183-04
- Solicitation Number: N66001-26-Q-6073

## Summary

Department of the Navy, Naval Information Warfare Center Pacific (NIWC PACIFIC) hereby publicizes its intention to solicit, negotiate and award a commercial, firm-fixed-price purchase order on a non-competitive basis with Tel Tech Plus, Inc. (CAGE 3CP25), San Marcos, California 92078-4374, United States. This is a purchase request for furnish labor and materials to install cable trays and associated wall penetrations for network cable distribution and required separation. Breakdown of materials and labor is noted in the attached document titled NOI_final. A determination by the Government not to compete this requirement based on responses to this notice is solely at the discretion of the Government. The applicable NAICS Code is 238210 and the business size standard is $19 million. The applicable PSC code is N059.This notice is not a request for competitive quotes; however, any responsible source believing that it can fulfill the requirement may submit a quote for consideration by the Government. Comments, questions or concerns regarding this notice may be submitted via e-mail to hannah.m.chichester.civ@us.navy.mil by 10:00 am PST on 13 April 2026. Questions by phone or fax will not be considered. To respond to this announcement, you must be registered to the Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) Solicitation module at https://piee.eb.mil/xhtml/unauth/home/login.xhtml and upload your inquiry under NIWC Pacific/Simplified Acquisition N66001-26-Q-6073. Responding vendors must be registered to the System for Award Management (SAM) website www.sam.gov prior to award of the related order/contract. Registration means a registered UEI and active CAGE Code. Be advised that the aforementioned information is anticipatory in nature and is not binding. A copy of the Request for Quote will be made available upon request. Cognizant Contracting Office: NIWC Pacific, San Diego, CA. Contract Specialist: Hannah Chichester, hannah.m.chichester.civ@us.navy.mil Contracting Officer: Benjamin Ossenfort, benjamin.j.ossenfort.civ@us.navy.mil

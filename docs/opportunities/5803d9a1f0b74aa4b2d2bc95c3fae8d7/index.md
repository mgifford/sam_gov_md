Description of Requirment a. Clear and Concise Description of Services: Service Title: Local Telecommunication Service Brief Description: The objective is to obtain essential local and long-distance telecommunication services for Travis AFB, with support available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. This requirement is for the contractor to furnish all labor, tools, facilities, materials, and services needed to ensure local access and transport to designated circuit demarcation points. The contractor is responsible for providing and installing all necessary equipment and infrastructure, ensuring their solution is compatible with the Government's current systems at no extra cost. These services, which must adhere to all federal, military, and industry standards, include local exchange access and operator assistance functions, and are strictly for government use. All installations must be coordinated with the Government Point of Contact, and aerial cable installations are not authorized. The full and detailed Performance Work Statement (PWS) is included as an attachment. b. Procedures for Response: RESPONSES MUST INCLUDE: 1. Business name and address 2.

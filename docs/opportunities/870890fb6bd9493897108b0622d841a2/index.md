# Notice of Intent to Award a BPA Call Order

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:36:49.158-04

## Summary

THIS IS FOR INFORMATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY. This is a notice of intent to award a competitive BPA Call Order against the Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA). Work includes but is not limited to - perform mowing, trimming, and general grounds maintenance at the Monongahela National Forest Greenbrier Ranger District administrative compound and associated government residences located in Bartow, WV. This is not a solicitation. This synopsis notification of intent to award a BPA Call order does not constitute a request for proposals or submission of any information in response to this notification. The LMIR BPA has a continuous pre-solicitation sharing that contractors may look into joining the BPA at any point in time while the BPA is active by reaching out to the POC on Solicitation 12363N23Q4023.

# Sewage Disposal Tank Cleaning

- Posted: 2026-03-20 14:36:55.853-04

## Summary

SEWAGE DISPOSAL TANK CLEANING This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. Solicitation Number W912EE26QA017 is being issued as a request for quote (RFQ) with the intent to issue one Firm-Fixed Price Contract. This solicitation is being issued under a 100% Small Business Set Aside. The NAICS code for this procurement is 562211, Hazardous Waste Treatment and Disposal, with a small business size determination of $47,000,000.00 in annual revenue. This procurement is being conducted in accordance with regulations at FAR Part 12 – Acquisition of Commercial Items and FAR Part 13 – Simplified Acquisition Procedures. The Revetment branch of The USACE Vicksburg district requires the cleaning of the sewage tanks on Power Barge 5014 and Quarterboats 9401, 9501, and 9901 along with disposal of all sewage currently in the tanks and any sewage water generated in the cleaning process. All Offerors shall submit a quote with content as specified herein. Quotes submitted without the specified content may be determined non-responsive and removed from further consideration. Offers are encouraged to review all clauses that are incorporated into this solicitation. ****SPECIAL ATTENTION SHOULD BE FOCUSED ON FAR 52.212-2 EVALUATION – COMMERCIAL PRODUCTS, AS THIS CLAUSE OUTLINES THE EVALUATION FACTORS FOR THIS REQUIREMENT. **** The government intends to make one award for one firm fixed price contract.

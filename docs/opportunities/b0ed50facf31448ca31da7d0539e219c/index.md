# KRACEN MAC TASK ORDER HMLAT-303 RECOMPETE

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 13:30:17.776-04
- Solicitation Number: N6852025R0046

## Summary

***UPDATE 3/30/2026 Amendment 3- removed A Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) must be attached to offer submissions in accordance with FAR Clause 52.223-3 submission requirement *** The Contractor shall augment uniform Marine Corps personnel by performing aircraft maintenance functions. Aircraft maintenance functions for this Task Order (TO) are identified and defined as Line, Corrosion Control, Tool Room, Non-Destructive Inspection (NDI) and aircraft scheduled and unscheduled Organizational Level (O-Level) Maintenance support. The Government will compete this requirement via the KRACEn MAC and only existing KRACEn MAC awardees are eligible to submit offers in response to this solicitation. Amendment 1 posted to extend due date to 24 March 2026 and to provide answers to questions. Another amendment is forthcoming. 3/19/2026 Amendment 2 posted extending the due date to 31 March 2026 and providing revised attachments.

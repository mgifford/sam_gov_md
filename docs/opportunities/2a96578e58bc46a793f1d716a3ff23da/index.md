# Procurement request for Virtual Acquisition Office (VAO)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 11:46:43.99-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016726Q1062

## Summary

Naval Surface Warfare, Carderock Division (NSWCCD), has a procurement request for a product shall be an online, subscription-based knowledge base specifically designed for federal acquisition professionals. It must provide access to a comprehensive library of federal acquisition regulations, policies, guidance, and related materials. The product required is the Virtual Acquisition Office (VAO). The subscription shall provide a minimum of 15 individual user licenses (passwords) for access to the online service.

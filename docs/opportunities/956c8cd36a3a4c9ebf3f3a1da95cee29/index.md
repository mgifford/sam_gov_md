## Summary

B.3 PRICE/COST SCHEDULE Quantities listed are estimated only and are not guaranteed. This is a 5-year Commercial Firm Fixed Price, shared capacity, Multiple Award Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract, for Wheelchair Transportation Services to support the Baltimore VA Medical Center. The Department of Veteran Affairs intends to award up to three (3) Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) contractors. The task orders will be distributed equitably. Accordingly, funds are not obligated on this contract at award; rather, funds are obligated via task orders issued against the contract. Task orders shall only be issued by a warranted VA Contracting Officer (CO). VA CO will issue task orders to the contractor authorizing services via Standard Form 1449. Each task order shall clearly identify the dollar amount authorized under the individual task order and the period of performance of the individual task order. When a task order period of performance is completed, additional services shall not be provided unless a new task order is issued in accordance with this IDIQ. The minimum dollar amount guaranteed under this contract for the performance period of the five year is $1,000.00. The maximum dollar amount or contract ceiling for all task orders is $50,000,000.00. The maximum amount is not guaranteed. The combined total of all orders placed against this contract cannot exceed the maximum amount/ceiling of this contract.

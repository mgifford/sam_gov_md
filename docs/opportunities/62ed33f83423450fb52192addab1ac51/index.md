# Bandwidth Expansion Software (1 Gb/s)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-17 13:08:43.566-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8N-26-Q-A016

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Synopsis announcement; a market survey for information only, to be used for preliminary planning purposes. THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION FOR PROPOSALS AND NO CONTRACT WILL BE AWARDED FROM THIS SYNOPSIS. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this synopsis or any follow-up information requests. This notice is for planning purposes only. All interested parties should respond. The 174th New York Air National Guard may issue a solicitation and award of a firm fixed price contract for TACLANE-FLEX (KG-175F) Encryptor Bandwidth Expansion Software (1Gb/s), (up from 100 Mb/s) in Syracuse, New York, 13211. Sources Sought Product Description: Bandwidth Expansion Software (1 Gb/s) Software feature that increases throughput of TACLANE-FLEX 100 Mb/s encryptors to 1 Gb/s via a 1000 Base-LX Single Mode Pluggable Optic. All interested concerns will provide this office, in writing, a notice stating their positive intention to submit a quote no later than 2:00PM Eastern on 30 March 2026.

# AGE Jack Trailer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 09:14:46.063-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1015

## Summary

Amendment 1: Due date for questions deadline has been added below. NV The government requires a 4 Degree Trailer Company P/N 12600 (or equivalent) wing jack trailer to support Aerospace Ground Equipment (AGE) operations by providing a safe, efficient, and expedited method for loading, unloading, and transporting up to four 30-ton wing jacks or other related equipment IAW with attached Salient Characteristics. Questions regarding this solicitation will be due no later than 1400 CST 10 April 2026; questions asked after this date will not be answered. Quotes must be received no later than 1400 CST 16 April 2026 to be considered for award; late quotes will not be considered for award.

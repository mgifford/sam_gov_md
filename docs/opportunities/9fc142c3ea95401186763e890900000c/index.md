# Terminal Modernization - Request for Information Refresh

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 08:39:39.15-04
- Solicitation Number: AFNWC_PZT_0415

## Summary

This is a Request for Information (RFI) which is part of a market research refresh related to RFI Notice ID AFNWC_PZT_0327. This RFI is solely for informational purposes and does not constitute a Request for Proposal (RFP) or a commitment to issue an RFP in the future. This RFI does not obligate the United States Air Force (USAF) to contract for any supply or service in any manner. Respondents are advised that the USAF will not pay for any information or administrative costs incurred in response to this RFI; all costs associated with responding to this RFI will be solely at the respondents’ expense. The information received through this RFI will be used solely for market research refresh purposes to identify potential vendors for future contracting actions, which may include, but are not limited to, an Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract. The anticipated North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 334220, Radio and Television Broadcasting and Wireless Communications Equipment Manufacturing. Any response received to this RFI constitutes consent for the submission to be reviewed by military personnel, government civilians, Department of Defense (DoD) contractors, and Federally Funded Research and Development Centers (FFRDCs). The responses may be forwarded to other Government entities in consideration for applicability to other programs. Description of Requirement: Air Force Nuclear Weapons Center (AFNWC) is conducting market research to identify industry sources capable of providing terminal modernization, production restart services and technical studies.

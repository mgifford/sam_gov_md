# Barbershop - DLA HQ MWR

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 10:33:44.259-04
- Solicitation Number: NAFUA1-26-R-0001

## Summary

15 April 2026: Amendment P00001 issued. The Defense Logistics Agency Family & Morale, Welfare, and Recreation (FMWR) invites qualified offerors to submit proposals in response to the Request for Proposal (NAFUA1-26-R-0001) to provide all labor, equipment, materials, and incidentals to operate a barbershop concession at the Andrew T. McNamara Headquarters Complex (DLA Headquarters), Fort Belvoir, Virginia. NOTE: THIS IS A REVENUE-GENERATING OPPORTUNITY FOR A CONTRACTOR TO OPERATE A BARBERSHOP AND PAY A FLAT-FEE OR COMMISSION TO THE NAFI. THE NAFI WILL NOT PAY FOR THESE SERVICES. All questions are due to the Contracting Officer in writing no later than 4:00 PM EDT on April 14, 2026. All proposals are due no later than 4:00 PM EST on April 21, 2026. Site visits are available by appointment only and must be completed no later than 4:00 PM EDT on April 9, 2026. To request a visit, you must send an email to the Contracting Officer at michael.3.thompson@dla.mil with your company information and proposed availability no later than the deadline listed above. Full instructions are detailed in Section L of the solicitation. Should you have any questions regarding the solicitation, please reach out to the Contracting Officer at michael.3.thompson@dla.mil.

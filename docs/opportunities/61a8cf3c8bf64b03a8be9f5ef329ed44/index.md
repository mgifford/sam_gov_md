## Summary

03/12/26 - Amendment 1 released for clarification (changes in red) and to extend response date. ----------- The intent of this Sources Sought notice is to gauge industry interest, capabilities, and relevant experience to support Advanced Research Projects Agency for Health (ARPA-H) Health Science Futures Office (HSF) by providing standardized micro- and nanoplastics to enable technology research and development activities. Specifically, ARPA-H is looking for the capability to provide micro- and nanoplastic (MNP) materials with specific attributes. See attached for sources sought request and response instructions.

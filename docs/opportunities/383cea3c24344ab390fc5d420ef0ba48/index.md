# PARTS KIT,FLUID PRE

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 08:56:38.207-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE7MX26R0038

## Summary

This solicitation is being issued to establish an Indefinite Quantity Contract (IQC) for a 1-year base period and two-year option periods that may be exercised at the discretion of the Government. The total contract period will be three years, if the two options are exercised. Annual price adjustments via an escalation factor will be utilized for a total contract period not to exceed three (3) years. Item will provide stock Replenishment coverage in support of military depots. The solicitation will be unrestricted, issued under FAR Part 15 procedures, and will utilize Other Than Full and Open Competition Procedures as prescribed 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1) and FAR 6.302-1(a)(2). These items will be procured in accordance with the manufacturer’s code and part number and are available from only one or a limited number of sources. Currently Section B will contain one (1) NSN Item managed by DLA Land and Maritime. See table below: CLIN 0001 NSN: 4330-01-125-5432 Nomenclature: PARTS KIT, FLUID PRESSURE FILTER Estimated Annual Demand Quantity: 2265 KT Approved Sources: PALL AEROPOWER CORPORATION 18350 P/N AA-9718E-25KF1 The solicitation will be available on or about April 10, 2026, at http://dibbs.dscc.dla.mil/rfp. While price may be a significant factor in the evaluation of offers, the final award decision will be based upon a combination of Price, Past Performance, Delivery, and other evaluation factors as described in the solicitation. All responsible sources may submit an offer/quote which shall be considered. Drawings/specifications will not be available through BSM DIBBS. This proposed procurement includes a family group of items within the Federal Supply Class 4330. Requires Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) Level 2 Self-Assessment. THIS IS A RESTRICTED SOURCE ITEM AND REQUIRES ENGINEERING SOURCE APPROVAL BY THE GOVERNMENT DESIGN CONTROL ACTIVITY. THIS IS AN AIR FORCE DESIGNATED CRITICAL SAFETY ITEM (CSI).

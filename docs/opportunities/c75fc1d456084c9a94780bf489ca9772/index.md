# Q522--Teleradiology Services

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 08:00:37.853-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24226Q0259

## Summary

The Contractor shall furnish Board Certified Radiology Physician Services in accordance with the specifications contained herein to beneficiaries of the Department of Veterans Affairs and the Albany-Stratton VA Medical Center. The VA Radiology Services are accredited by ACR and follow the standards & guidelines set forth by American College of Radiology. A contractor providing tele-radiology services shall provide services consistent with The Joint Commission (TJC) and that meet or exceed VA and the American College of Radiology Guidelines as defined in this document. Amendment has been added to include answers to vendor questions as well as extend the due date of offers.

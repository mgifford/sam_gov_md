# Notice of Intent to Sole Source- LRS Zone 4 BANAIR Storage Racks, Misawa Airbase

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-21 01:45:13.966-04

## Summary

The 35th Contracting Office at Misawa Air Base, Japan intends to award a sole source contract under the authority of RFO 12.102, simplified Procedures (41 U.S.C. 1901). The associated NAICS code for this is 332999. The sole-source contract will be awarded to: Elements Industrial Storage Limited (hereby the proposed vendor): Peartree Lane, Unit F, Crackley Way Dudley DY2 0UW United Kingdom CAGE Code: U16P0 Description of Requirement: Project is for 393W-Z4 Tactical Storage Racks to the base's Logistic Squadron to store ISU-90s. The contractor shall install, assemble all available components in accordance with the Statement of Work, and repair existing flooring if necessary. Install BANAIR Instantly Adjustable Racking System (IARS), Cargo Cubers, Rolling Stock Crates, WRM crates Provide anchoring and leveling hardware for the storage equipment unless otherwise stated in the manufacturer instruction. A standard shimming solution is included, consisting of levelling hardware to overcome a floor slope of 1/1000 affecting the system to be erected. Assemble new BAN AIR storage equipment: BACC (20 ct.), BACC.S6 (20 ct.), ROLLINGSTACK crate (24 ct.), WRM Crate – 44” (48 ct.), WRM crate – 66" (18 ct.), WRM crate – 83" (10 ct.). Install BANAIR IARS and other purchased storage systems—including Flow-Thru pallets and tire cradles—in accordance with the manufacturers installation manual. Install Metal Guiderails It is the Government's belief that only the proposed candidate possesses the required capabilities to successfully meet this requirement due to the parts’ compatibility with the existing system. It is the Government's intention to solicit and negotiate with only this source. Interested parties may identify their interests to this notice by showing clear and convincing evidence that they can provide all the needed assets, meet the security recquirements and competition would be advantageous to the Government. Send responses to this notice via e-mail to Katie Kershaw, Contracting Specialist at katie.kershaw@us.af.mil and 1st Lt Kade Forrester, Contracting officer at kade.forrester.1@us.af.mil, no later than 10:00 AM (Japan Standard Time) on 24 Apr 26. NO TELEPHONE CALLS OR FACSIMILES WILL BE ACCEPTED. RESPONSES RECEIVED AFTER THE DEADLINE OR WITHOUT THE REQUIRED INFORMATION MAY NOT BE CONSIDERED. THIS IS NOT SOLICITATION. RESPONSES TO THIS NOTICE ARE NOT CONSIDERED AS OFFER AND CANNOT BE ACCEPTED BY THE GOVERNMENT TO FORM A BINDING CONTRACT. THE GOVERNMENT WILL NOT PAY FOR INFORMATION REQUESTED NOR WILL IT COMPENSATE ANY RESPONDENT FOR ANY COST INCURRED IN DEVELOPING INFORMATION PROVIDED TO THE GOVERNMENT.

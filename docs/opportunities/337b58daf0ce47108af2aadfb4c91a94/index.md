# MAF Mattresses - Minot AFB, ND

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 15:24:21.45-04
- Solicitation Number: FA452826QRS01

## Summary

The purpose of this requirement is to purchase 456 mattresses, 912 protective encasements and the pick-up and disposal of all old mattresses from 15 missile alert facilities (MAFs) surrounding Minot Air Force Base, North Dakota. Full performance of the contract, including delivery to all locations and the pickup of the old mattresses, must be completed no later than 90 days After Receipt of Order (ARO). Detailed product specifications and logistical requirements are provided in Attachment 1 – MAF Mattress SOW.

# 187 FW OHWS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 16:29:52.252-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S6N26QA013

## Summary

Up to 85% of Air Force fighter aircrew surveyed reported some type of spine discomfort during their career. The OHWS program is designed to meet the unique physical needs of the rated fighter aircrew. Conditioning is accomplished through a comprehensive, coordinated, multidisciplinary “pre-habilitative” physical training program with focused strength and conditioning, physical therapy, massage therapy, and athletic training. Please see the attached combined synopsis/solicitation and PWS for further details. RFIs are due NLT 7 May 2026 at 2:00 P.M.

# Counter Small Unmanned Aircraft Sunshade System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-26 15:19:20.145-04
- Solicitation Number: FA480326SS001

## Summary

1.0 Description & Purpose The 20th Contracting Squadron is issuing this Sources Sought notice as a means of conducting market research to identify parties having an interest in installing a counter small unmanned aircraft system (c-sUAS) double-net system around an existing aircraft sunshade. The purpose of this notice is to gain knowledge of the capabilities, capacity, and qualifications of various members of industry, including Small Businesses, 8(a) firms, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses (SDVOSB), HUBZone firms, and Women-Owned Small Businesses (WOSB). THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE ONLY. This is not a solicitation for proposals, proposal abstracts, or quotations. The purpose of this notice is to obtain information for market research purposes only. The Government is not obligated to and will not pay for any information received from potential sources as a result of this notice.

2.0 Requested Information Interested parties should provide a capability statement that demonstrates their ability to meet the requirements described in this post. The statement should include: A brief description of your solution and how it meets the attached requirements document. Please include the period of performance required from date of award to work completion. Summaries of past projects of a similar nature, including the installation of anti-drone netting or barriers Confirmation of your ability to install a system based on the provided dimensions and site constraints. Confirmation that you are a US based company able to gain access to military installations.

# Fire Extinguisher Servicing (Halon 1211)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 12:36:10.374-04
- Solicitation Number: FA489726QC044

## Summary

The 366 MXG requires maintenance, inspection, and servicing of the government's fleet of 102x 150lb wheeled Halon 1211 fire extinguishers. Services include annual inspections, 6-year maintenance, 12-year hydrostatic testing, and on-demand emergency servicing. 21 April 2026: Q&A Posted. Please refer to attachments.

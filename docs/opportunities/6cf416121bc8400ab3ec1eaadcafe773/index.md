# Vehicle Rental BPA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 12:50:56.15-04
- Solicitation Number: FA489726QC028

## Summary

The 366th Fighter Wing requires a multiple-award Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) to fulfill its recurring need for commercial vehicle rental services. These rental vehicles will vary between sedans, SUVs, large pack vans, and various trucks. This BPA will provide a simplified method for acquiring various vehicles (sedans, trucks, SUVs, and vans) on a Firm-Fixed-Price basis. It is intended to be a long-term tool to accommodate fluctuating requirements for vehicle support, as the exact quantities and dates of need are not known in advance. The service is commercial in nature, and all awards will be based on a determination of fair and reasonable pricing.

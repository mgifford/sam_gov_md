# Janitorial Services for the Green Mountain & Finger Lakes National Forests Supervisor’s Office

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 07:55:09.411-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444326Q0017

## Summary

The Green Mountain & Finger Lakes National Forests (GMFL) Supervisor’s Office (SO) Site Janitorial Maintenance, located at 4387 U.S. Route 4, Mendon, Vermont 05701. This acquisition is needed to provide a clean and sanitary environment for visitors and employees of the GMFL SO. These services are required throughout the year. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12444326Q0017 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for GMF SO Janitorial services. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 561720 The small business size standard is $22.0. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

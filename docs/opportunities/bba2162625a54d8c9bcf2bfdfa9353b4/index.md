## Summary

This award is for a new contract to manage and operate the Thomas Jefferson National Accelerator Facility (TJNAF) located in Newport News, VA. TJNAF is a Federally Funded Research and Development Center (FFRDC) single purpose laboratory that is expected to evolve into a multi-purpose national laboratory over the next few years with the advancement of the Office of Science’s Advanced Scientific Computing Research (ASCR) High Performance Data Facility (HPDF) project. TJNAF leads the nation in the construction and operation of world-leading accelerator and detector facilities and in developing the underlying technology for nuclear physics research. TJNAF’s primary mission is to deliver breakthrough science and technology in nuclear physics; accelerator science and technology; and large-scale user facilities/advanced instrumentation.

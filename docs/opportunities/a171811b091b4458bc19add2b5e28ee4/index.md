# RFI Intra SWA - 1GB

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-24 07:31:47.965-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102126RFI1003

## Summary

DITCO-Europe is seeking information for a 1GB commercial lease intra-SWA. This publication is a Request for Information (RFI) from the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) regarding services Outside Contiguous United States (OCONUS). The purpose of this RFI is to conduct market research. Information obtained through this RFI will be used to determine appropriate strategies to meet the Agency's requirements. This RFI is being issued solely for information and planning purposes and does not constitute an RFP or a committment for an RFP in the future. This RFI does not commit the Government to contract for any supply or service. Responders are advised that the United States Government will not pay for any information or administrative cost incurred in response to this announcement.

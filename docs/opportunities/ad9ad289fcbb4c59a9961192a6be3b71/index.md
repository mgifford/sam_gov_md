# MDMC Digital Depot

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 16:19:31.558-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700426Q0016

## Summary

The purpose of this amendment is to add revised attachments E1_Attachment 1- Performance Work Statement and E1_Attachment 4- RGS, as well as to include attachment E1_Attachment 5-MDMC Digital Depot Q&A, a questions and answers document from industry. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number M6700426Q0016 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ), for Digital Depot Services Aboard Marine Depot Maintenance Command, Albany GA. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns under the woman owned small business socio-economic category. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at https://www.acquisition.gov/far-overhaul Detailed instructions and requirement details can be found under the five (5) attachments of this combined synopsis/solicitation. All quoters are required to sign and return one copy of the document, E1_Amendment_M6700426Q00160001, in order to be considered responsive.

# B457 Coil Repairs

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 16:54:56.883-04
- Solicitation Number: FA460026Q0033

## Summary

DESCRIPTION OF REQUIREMENT The Contractor shall procure and replace cooling and heating water coils for the AHU serving PMEL spaces in B457. The existing coils are broken due to damage from freezing. The existing AHU is a Daikin model CAH017GDCM (S/N FBOU210100282), with coils of model 5WL1106B-024, part# 619153312. The unit has 2 hot water coils (rated 211 MBH at 6690 cfm and 13.6 gpm) and two chilled water coils (rated 249 MBH at 6690 cfm and 59.4 gpm). APPLICABLE LABOR LAWS This solicitation is subject to the Service Contract Labor Standards RFO 52.222-41 and the current wage determination, Service Contract Act WD # 2015-5005, revision 27, revised 03 Dec 2025. QUESTIONS DUE DATE AND TIME A site visit will be scheduled for 20th April 2026 at 01:00 PM CDT. All prospective offerers that do not have access to Offutt AFB shall submit a filled out EAL request to A1C Hunter Hendrix at hunter.hendrix.1@us.af.mil no later than COB 16th April 2026 . Entry to the installation cannot be guaranteed. OFFER DUE DATE AND TIME Monday, 27 April 2026 at 11:00 AM CDT. CONTRACTING OFFICE CONTACT INFORMATION All offers and questions pertaining to this solicitation shall be sent by email to: A1C Hunter Hendrix at hunter.hendrix.1@us.af.mil Mr. Casey L Hupton at casey.hupton.1@us.af.mil

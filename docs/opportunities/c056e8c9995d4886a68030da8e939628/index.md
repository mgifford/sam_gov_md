## Summary

The purpose of this contract is to provide comprehensive support across multiple task areas to advance the mission of the Haitian National Police (HNP) and the goals of the INL/PAP program. This includes deploying Subject Matter Expert (SME) Advisors, and delivering logistics support to the HNP School and Academy facilities.

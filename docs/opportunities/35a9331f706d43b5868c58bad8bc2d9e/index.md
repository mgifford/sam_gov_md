# JBMHH LMR-EV Maintenance & Repair SVC

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-27 12:21:40.58-04
- Solicitation Number: W91QV126CA017

## Summary

This is a non-personnel service contract to provide maintenance and repairs of the Land Mobile Radio and Emergency Vehicle Equipment (LMR-EV) for the Joint Base Myer Henderson Hall (JBM-HH) Directorate of Emergency Services (DES). The Government shall not exercise any supervision or control over the contract service providers performing the services herein. Such contract service providers shall be accountable solely to the Contractor Officer who, in turn is responsible to the Government. Contractor will be required to utilize E-Verify for all employees. A background check from the local police department from which the employee resides will also be required.

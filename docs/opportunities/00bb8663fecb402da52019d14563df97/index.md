# ESRD StressCheck Software Support Renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 11:25:39.552-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102826R0021

## Summary

Synopsis solicitation HC102826R0021. The Government intends to issue a sole source order to the sole authorized reseller of Engineering Software Research and Development, Inc. (ESRD) products, ESRD. Brand-name, commercial, proprietary ESRD software is required for Fleet Readiness Center East (FRCE) Information Systems Division (ISD) Business Operation. The period of performance is May 1, 2026, through April 30, 2027. The redacted Software Class J&A is posted. Responsible sources may submit a completed and signed solicitation response.

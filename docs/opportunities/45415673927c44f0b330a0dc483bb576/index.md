PLEASE SEE SECTION C OF AMENDED RFQ WITH SOW/DETAILS ABOUT TRAINING Solicitation N0042126P1062 is issued as an intent to sole source. Information received will normally be considered for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. The government will not pay for any information received.

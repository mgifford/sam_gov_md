# Monongahela National Forest FR76 Embankment Stabilization Project

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 07:55:06.993-04
- Solicitation Number: 12445526B0007

## Summary

The Monongahela National Forest requests construction services to construct three (3) solider pile and concrete lagging retaining walls, remove existing drainage culverts, remove and reset existing guardrail, install new drainage culverts, dewater stream, place riprap along streambanks as well as in-stream, regrade stream channel, re-construct and re-grade portions of Forest Road 76 (FR-76), and install rebar and non-shrink, high strength grout along existing bridge abutment foundations as shown in the drawings. Solicitation number 12445526B0007 is issued as an Invitation for Bid (IFB) for Forest Road 76 Embankment Stabilization. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 237310 The small business size standard is 45M This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a bid which will be considered by the agency. The official source of information for this solicitation is the System for Award Management (located at sam.gov). If you are reading this IFB anywhere other than SAM, refer to the solicitation notice on sam.gov to ensure you have the most up-to-date information. The public opening of bids will be held virtually, via Microsoft Teams. Attendence information will be posted at a later date.

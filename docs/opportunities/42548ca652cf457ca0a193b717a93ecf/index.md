# VJETTS Tabletop Exchange

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-19 10:29:46.168-04

## Summary

Join us for the VJETTS Kickoff Event as we introduce the Virtual Joint Environment for Tactical Training Systems (VJETTS)—a new DoD initiative designed to close a critical gap in today’s simulation and training capabilities. VJETTS will establish an interoperable test range backbone for Live, Virtual, and Constructive (LVC) systems, enabling real?time data exchange and synchronized operations across platforms that currently rely on disparate communication protocols including DIS, HLA, TENA, and GRID. This session will explore VJETTS’ core purpose: ensuring secure, real?time interoperability across distributed simulation environments; providing an extensible infrastructure for multi?domain operations, mission rehearsals, and joint testing; and advancing these goals through a forthcoming Prize Challenge that will drive innovation and solution development within the defense training ecosystem. https://www.simpletix.com/e/virtual-joint-environment-for-tactical-tra-tickets-264523

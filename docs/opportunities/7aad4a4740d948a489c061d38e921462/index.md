# 316th CES Aircraft Fire Rescue Training Facility

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-23 14:45:01.857-04
- Solicitation Number: FA2860AFRTF

## Summary

Sources Sought: 316th CES Aircraft Fire Rescue Training Facility Scope: The 316th CES on JB Andrews has a recurring need for Aircraft Fire Rescue Training Facility Inspection, Maintenance and Servicing, on Joint Base Andrews; services desired are contained in the attached Draft PWS NOTE: This Sources Sought is issued solely for market research purposes and does not constitute a Request for Quote (RFQ) or a Request for Proposal (RFP) The Government is conducting market research for Aircraft Fire Rescue Training Facility Inspection, Maintenance and Servicing. The purpose of this RFI is to identify vendors capable of providing Aircraft Fire Rescue Training Facility Inspection, Maintenance, and Servicing that meets the Government’s requirements. Please provide detailed inspection, maintenance, and service information. If you are capable and interested in performing this work, please submit a capabilities statement not to exceed 10 pages and include in your response your business name, UEI, and Small Business type. If you have any questions about the draft PWS, please include those in your emailed response. See attached PWS for more information on the details of this requirement.

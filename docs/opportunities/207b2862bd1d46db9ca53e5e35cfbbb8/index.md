# Provision of services for Supply and Installation of Concertina Razor Wire at US Consulate General Karachi

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-04-10 01:33:41.346-04

## Summary

Enclosed is a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Provision of services for Supply and Installation of Concertina Razor Wire at US Consulate General Karachi. If you would like to submit quotation, follow the instructions in Section 3 & 4 of the solicitation, complete the required portions of the attached document, and submit it to the address shown on the Standard Form 1442 that follows this letter. The U.S. Government intends to award a contract/purchase order to the responsible company submitting an acceptable offer at the lowest price. We intend to award a contract/purchase order based on initial quotations, without holding discussions, although we may hold discussions with companies in the competitive range if there is a need to do so. The Consulate intends to conduct a pre-quotation site visit on April 16th, 2026, at 11:00am (Pakistan standard time). Offerors interested in attending must e-mail: KRCprocurementContracting@state.gov below details on or before April 14th, 2026, 15:00Hrs. Full name/s CNIC/s Vehicle make Vehicle model Vehicle color Vehicle registration # Cell/phone contact# Only two persons from each firm may participate in the pre-proposal site visit. Following the site visit all relevant questions must be submitted no later than COB 20th April 2026 Quotations are due by April 27th, 2026, 15:00Hrs (Pakistan standard time). No quotations will be accepted after this time. Proposals must be in English and incomplete proposals will not be accepted. Bids must be submitted electronically to Krcprocurementcontracting@state.gov. It is important to make sure the submission is made in Adobe Acrobat (pdf) file format not to exceed 30MB. If the file size should exceed 30MB, the submission must be made in separate files and attached to separate emails with less than 30MB each. In order for a quotation to be considered, you must also complete and submit the following: 1. SF-1442 2. Section A, Pricing 3. Section B and Attachment 2: Quotation Breakdown by Divisions. 4. Section L Representations and Certifications 5. Additional information as required in Section J 6. Proof of SAM Registration

# Road 547, 164 and 164Q Road Reconditioning Cibola

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 10:38:09.949-04
- Solicitation Number: 1158555

## Summary

Recondition and repair 18.31 miles of National Forest System Road on Forest Roads 547, 164, and 164Q of the Cibola National Forest and National Grasslands, Mount Taylor Ranger District. This work will include: Grading aggregate and native road surfaces. Cleaning and repairing ditch lines and lead out ditches. Cleaning culvert inlets and outlets. Removing and Replacing damaged culverts. Options include: Placement of aggregate road surface course for road stabilization Placement of aggregate subbase for road shoulder stabilization and culvert top cover.

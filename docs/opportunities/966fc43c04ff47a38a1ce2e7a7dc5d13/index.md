# 2026 Base Laundry Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-27 14:28:51.276-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4626-26-BASE-LAUNDRY-SVCE

## Summary

This procurement is for a non-personal service to provide all personnel, equipment, transportation, materials, supplies, and supervision necessary to provide laundry and dry cleaning services, including pick-up and delivery for multiple locations at Malmstrom AFB as described in the PWS (attachment 1). The contractor shall establish a complete quality control program to ensure the requirements of the contract are provided as specified.

# Nitrile and Latex Laboratory Gloves

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 11:41:35.225-04
- Solicitation Number: 15F06726Q000784

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), Procurement Section, is issuing this Request for Quotation (RFQ) to solicit interested vendors for the purpose of establishing a firm-fixed price, single award, agreement for the acquisition of Nitrile and Latex Laboratory Gloves. If interested in competing for this effort, vendors may participate by submitting a quote in accordance with the procedures set forth in this RFQ. The Government reserves the right to terminate the RFQ at any time during the validity period. All gloves supplied under this procurement must comply with the requirements of ASTM D3578 Standards for the Latex and ASTM D6319 Standards for the Nitrile gloves. Questions shall be submitted via email only to the attention of Lily Calvert at lrcalvert@fbi.gov, no later than April 13th, 2026, at 5:00pm EST. Quotes and any accompanying information shall be submitted via email to the attention of Lily Calvert at lrcalvert@fbi.gov, no later than April 17th, 2026, at 5:00pm EST.

# Marine Corps Base Camp Lejeune, North Carolina Life Support Activities in support of Resolute Strike/FLEET EX 26

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-17 13:27:34.007-04
- Solicitation Number: M67001-26-Q-007

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought announcement to request information only. This is not a Request for Quote or Proposal. This Sources Sought notice is issued for planning purposes only, and does not constitute a solicitation for proposals, offers, bids, and/or quotes. This is not an announcement of a forthcoming solicitation, or a confirmation that the Government will contract for the services contained in the Sources Sought, nor is any commitment on the part of the Government to award a contract implied. Responses to this notice will assist the Government in identifying potential sources in determining if a set aside of the solicitation is appropriate, and in determining whether this approach to securing contracting support is practicable. Respondents are advised that the Government will not pay for any information or administrative cost incurred in response to this Sources Sought. Responses to the Sources Sought will not be returned. Please note that the solicitation number used for this Sources Sought may be different from the one in the actual solicitation, if a solicitation materializes. The 26th MEU Command Element, Marine Corps Base Camp Lejeune, NC, has a requirement for a Basic Life Support Services. The applicable NAICS code is 561210, Facilities Support Services. Interested vendors shall provide the following information: Company name, address, point of contact name, phone number, and email address. CAGE Code. CAGE Code must be registered in SAM.gov (https://www.sam.gov) Business Size under NAICS 561210. If the size standard is small, indicate if your firm is certified under Section 8(a) of the Small Business Act, HUBZone certified, Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business, Woman Owned Small Business, Small Disadvantaged Business, or Small Business. If these services have been acquired by any Government agencies. If so, please provide names of agencies, point(s) of contact, and contract number(s). Include any other supporting documentation. Respondents are encouraged to review the attached draft Pricing Workbook. Please identify any ambiguities or industry-standard gaps found within the draft SOR or Pricing Workbook. Responses to this Sources Sought Notice shall be e-mailed to lucas.a.beltran.mil@usmc.mil and morgan.n.gose.mil@usmc.mil. Responses must be received no later than 24 April 2026 at 11:59 P.M.

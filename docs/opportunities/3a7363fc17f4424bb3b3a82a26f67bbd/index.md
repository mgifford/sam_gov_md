## Summary

The intent of this contract opportunity is to provide renovation/modernization for a new computer laboratory on the third floor of Building 1000 at the Philadelphia Navy Yard for the Naval Surface Warfare Center Philadelphia Division (NSWCPD), in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Building 1000 is utilized by NSWC as a research, development, testing and evaluation facility. The products listed below are required to meet the readiness requirements for the Customer by meeting their APL for ESS. 1. Lenel Access Control System Dual Reader Interface Module – Model LNL-1100- S3 5. Bosch Request to Exit Device – Model DS-160 6. HES Electric Strike – Model HES-1006 7. Magnasphere Balanced Magnetic Switch- Model HSS-L2S-000\ 8. Lenel Access Control Power Supply– Model AL600ULPD8CB

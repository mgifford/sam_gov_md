## Summary

Solicitation for Global Medical Coverage-LE Staff Health Insurance Services This solicitation is for the providing of Health Insurance services to the Locally hired employees of the US Embassy Lome, Togo and their eligible dependents as described in Section C of the attached Solicitation. This insurance shall be provided in accordance with Section C. The estimated numbers of employees and dependents areincluded in the solicitation. This is a fixed price with economic price adjustment requirements type contract. The anticipated performance is for a base period of twelve months and four one-year periods at the option of the Government. All responsible sources may submit an offer, which will be considered The proposal due date is March 19, 2026

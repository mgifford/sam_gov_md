## Summary

This is a combined Pre-Solicitation and Solicitation for a Krane Retrofit Kit produced by Measurement Systems International (MSI). This equipment is used in support of Rigging & Crane Operations Material at Norfolk Naval Shipyard (NNSY), Portsmouth, VA. This requirement will be procured as a brand?name, sole?source acquisition from Measurement Systems International (MSI), a Rice Lake Weighing Systems company. As the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM), MSI is the only source capable of providing the Krane Retrofit Kit that meet the shipyard’s operational and performance requirements. No third?party or substitute items will be considered. This solicitation will result in a Firm?Fixed?Price (FFP) award. Brand?Name, Sole?Source Requirement No substitutes allowed. Please refer to the attachments for the complete description and specifications. Vendors shall submit: - A completed Solicitation (SF 1449) N4215826QE021 - The Continuation Sheets (3) - A Technical Capability Statement (see attachments); and - A vendor's quote Important Note: Quotes must be all?inclusive, including shipping and any other associated costs.

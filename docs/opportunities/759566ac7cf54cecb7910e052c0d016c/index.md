# **Amendment 6** Beluga Whale Explosive Ordnance Demo (EOD) Services

- Posted: 2026-03-30 16:11:45.331-04

## Summary

** Amendment 6** The purpose of this amendment is to publish the official Questions and Answers (Q&A) Revision 1, dated 30 March 2026. Question & Answer to number 7 was added in this revision. As a result, the document added EOD Questions and Answers_Rev_1_20260330, supercedes the previous version. **Amendment 6 END** **Amendment 5** The quotation due date for this solicitation has been extended. All quoters, including those who have already submitted, must resubmit their entire proposal package by the new deadline of 12:00 p.m. AKDT on 31 March 2026. The purpose of this amendment is to reopen this solicitation to incorporate a revised price schedule (Attachment 3). The summary of changes to the price schedule is the unit of issue for Contract Line Item Numbers (CLINs) 0001, 1001, 2001, 3001, and 4001 is changed from "12 months" to "90 each." This change is to ensure small businesses are paid upon performance of services, and to clarify that the government will not be liable for payment if no services are performed during a month. Updated documents: The following documents have been updated and supersede all previous versions: COMBO – Beluga Whale EOD Escort_Rev 3_20260324 Attachment 3 – Price Schedule_20260324 All other terms and conditions of the solicitation remain unchanged. **Amendment 5 End** **Amendment 4** The purpose of this amendment is to incorporate a revised FAR clause 52.219-14 Limitations on Subcontracting (FEB2023) (DEVIATION 2021-O0008) to the COMBO. As a result, the document "COMBO -Beluga Whale EOD Escort_Rev 2_20260306 now supercedes all previous versions. The key change replaces the previous subcontracting clause and clarifies at FAR 52.219-14(f)(1), that quoters must demonstrate how they will meet the required limitations on subcontracting by the end of the base period and again by the end of each subsequent option period. **Amendment 4 End** **Amendment 3** The purpose of this amendment is to incorporate a revised Performance Work Statement (PWS), which is included as Attachment 1 (EOD PWS REV 2 20260302). **Amendment 3 End** **Amendment 2** The purpose of this amendment is to add the clause DAFFARS 5352.201-9101 Ombudsman to the combo in full text. **Amendment 2 End** **Amendment 1** The purpose of this amendment is to publish the official Questions and Answers (Q&A) and incorporate the revised Performance Work Statement (PWS), which is included as Attachment 1 (EOD PWS REV 1 20260226). **Amendment 1 End** Please refer to the attached Combo and accompanying attachments. Any questions shall be directed to the POC’s listed below IN WRITING to the email addresses hannah.tolbert.1@us.af.mil and maureen.badey.1@us.af.mil no later than 12PM AKST on 24 February 2026. All questions will be posted and answered by amendment following this date. For a quote to be considered, it must be emailed to the Contracting Officer, maureen.badey.1@us.af.mil, and Contract Specialist, hannah.tolbert.1@us.af.mil, no later than 12PM AKDT on 13 March 2026.

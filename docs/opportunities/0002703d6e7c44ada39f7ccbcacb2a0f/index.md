# Modernize Wing Joint Mission Planning Center B2155H

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-19 21:14:07.347-04
- Solicitation Number: AF2FY26AandE

## Summary

THIS IS A REQUEST FOR INFORMATION (RFI) ONLY. This RFI is issued solely for information and planning purposes – it does not constitute a Request for Proposal (RFP) or a promise to issue an RFP in the future. This request for information does not commit the Government to contract for any supply or service whatsoever. Further, the Air Force is not seeking proposals at this time and will not accept unsolicited proposals. Respondees are advised that the Government will not pay for any information or administrative costs incurred in response to this RFI; all costs associated with responding to this RFI will be solely at the interested party’s expense. Not responding to this RFI does not preclude participation in any future RFP, if any is issued. REQUIREMENT DESCRIPTION: Provide all necessary professional and technical services to prepare a complete and usable set of 100% construction documents (drawings and specifications) suitable for bidding and construction. This project provides for the complete renovation of approximately 21,672 square feet of interior and exterior space to create a modern, consolidated, and fully functioning Joint Mission Planning Facility on JBPHH, HI. The facility shall include secure spaces, including an approximately 3,000 SF SCIF to support mission planning operations. The SCIF shall be designed to comply with ICD/ICS 705 and applicable DoD/DAF implementing guidance.

# Parachute/ Airborne Boat and Dive Operations Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 10:16:16.992-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700126Q0052

## Summary

SYNOPSIS FOR PARALOFT, BOAT LOCKER, AND DIVE LOCKER SERVICES 2D Reconnaissance Battalion, 2D Marine Division, II Marine Expeditionary Force aboard Camp Lejeune, North Carolina, has a requirement for paraloft, boat locker, and dive locker services to provide full-time support in the areas of parachute/airborne and boating operations. The period of performance will be a one-year base period and four one-year option periods, to begin on June 1, 2026. It is the Government’s intention to issue a firm-fixed-price contract in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 561210 - Facilities Support Services. The Government intends to award it on a Best Value basis. The award will be issued on an all-or-none basis. The Government will solicit and award the contract using simplified acquisition procedures. This synopsis is not a request for quotes. Solicitation #M6700126Q0052 will be posted on or about 5 days after this posting and made available on the Contract Opportunities Portal located at www.sam.gov. It is the responsibility of the offeror to continually view the website for solicitation and any amendments. Contractors interested in doing business with the Government must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at www.sam.gov. Contractors must also ensure annual representations and certifications are current or complete them at the SAM website (www.sam.gov). All prospective offerors interested in submitting an offer must have a Commercial and Government Entity (CAGE) code. Contractors must be registered with the Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) at www.wawf.eb.mil. The point of contact for this solicitation is John Reese, Contract Specialist, at phone number (910) 450-5404 or by email at john.reese@usmc.mil. All responsible sources may submit an offer, which, if timely received, will be considered.

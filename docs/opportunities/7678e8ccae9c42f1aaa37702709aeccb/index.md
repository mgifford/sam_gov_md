# United States Army Alternative Positioning, Navigation and Timing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-20 14:30:34.87-04
- Solicitation Number: FA2541-26-004

## Summary

This task order will provide satellite airtime and engineering & maintenance support for the transition to operation of a custom alternative position, navigation, and timing (ALTPNT), also known as "ALTNAV" solution developed by the United States (U.S.) Army Program Executive Office (PEO) - Intelligence, Electronic Warfare & Sensors (IEW&S) Project Manager (PM) Position, Navigation, and Timing (PNT). This RFI is only open to all current pLEO contractors. Access will only be provided to those who has the appropriate NAICS, 517410 -- Satellite Telecommunications within your SAM Registration assertion.

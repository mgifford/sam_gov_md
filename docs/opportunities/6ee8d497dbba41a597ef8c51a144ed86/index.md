# Electronic Fingerprint Collection and Submissions for DeCA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-31 14:30:57.444-04
- Solicitation Number: HQC00526QE015

## Summary

The Defense Commissary Agency (DeCA) has a new requirement Electronic Fingerprint Collection and Submissions for ALL positions within DeCA. Company will provide walk-in service near all Contiguous United States (CONUS) Commissary locations (to include Alaska, Hawaii, Puerto Rico and Guam) and Outside Continental United States (OCONUS) (Europe, Spain, Italy, Japan and Korea) for electronic fingerprinting. The commercial company would allow applicants to schedule fingerprinting appointments, ensure accurate and complete fingerprint capture and submission to Defense Counterintelligence and Security Agency (DCSA)/Office of Personnel Management (OPM) as required for employment as well as issuance of Common Access Card (CAC) for access to installations and systems. Approximately 3,200 fingerprints will be required per fiscal year.

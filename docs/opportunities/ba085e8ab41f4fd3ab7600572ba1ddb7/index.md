## Summary

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) invites you to submit a quote in response to RFQ# CORHQ-26-Q-0116 for FDIC’s Juniper WAN Tech Refresh Requirement. Please use the attached Price Schedule to provide pricing for all required line items and include your most favorable discount to the Government. The awardee must have an active SAM.gov registration prior to award. Questions are due by 12:00 PM EST on April 23, 2026 (please use attached Q&A template). All quotes are due by 12:00 PM EST on April 30, 2026.

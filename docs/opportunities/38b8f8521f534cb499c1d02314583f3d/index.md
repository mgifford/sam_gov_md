# Justification and Approval (J&A) for the purchase of brand-name commercial products for resale - Coastal Pacific Food Distributors

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-27 12:02:31.399-04
- Solicitation Number: HQC002-26-09

## Summary

This is the Justification and Approval (J&A) for Other Than Full and Open Competition for the award of a sole-source Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to Coastal Pacific Food Distributors. This action is for the supply of a full line of brand-name resale products delivered to the DeCA commissaries listed in the attached justification document. This action is executed under the authority of 10 U.S.C. § 2484(f), as implemented by FAR 6.103-5 under DoW Class Deviation DARS Tracking No. 2026-O0017 (Revolutionary FAR Overhaul Part 6, DFARS Part 206), DeCA may use other than full and open competition for the procurement of brand-name commercial products for authorized resale at DeCA commissaries.

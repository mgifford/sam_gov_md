## Summary

This is an amendment to the Combined Synopsis/Solicitation Notice to: Extend the solicitation due date to March 20, 2026 at 12:00pm EST. Revise Addendum B See revisions highlighted in red font. Include the Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition as an attachment. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. The Offer is being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. This acquisition specifically will procure a software upgrade of the existing National Teleradiology Program (NTP) Picture Archive and Communication System, upgrading IntelePACS version 4.9 to version 5.7 or the most current version available at the time of implementation. The effort includes migration of all NTP specific applications, along with the provision of ongoing system maintenance and technical support for the upgraded platform. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The intended sole source award will be a firm-fixed price contract with Intelerad Medical Systems, Inc. (Intelerad), 800 De Maisonneuve Blvd East, 14th Floor, Montreal, Quebec. See attached Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition. The Statutory Authority for this effort falls under Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Overhaul VA Deviation 6.103-1 entitled, Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements. However, in accordance with FAR Part 5, all responsible sources may submit a capability statement, proposal, or quotation, which shall be considered by the agency. The Point of Contact for this action is: Sean.McAvoy1@va.gov, Contract Specialist; e-mail: Sean.McAvoy1@va.gov. The North American Industry Classification Systems (NAICS) code for this action is 541511. The Product Service Code for this action is DA10.

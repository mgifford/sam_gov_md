# WON 1665555, FY24 AJJY183012 (P-012), PDI: NORTH AIRCRAFT PARKING RAMP, ANDERSEN AIR FORCE BASE, GUAM

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 19:48:07.734-04
- Solicitation Number: N62742-26-R-1300

## Summary

The project is to construct an aircraft parking apron with associated taxiways and shoulders, utility yards, airfield fence, and hydrant fueling pits and piping required to accommodate bombers, tankers, and other aircraft. Work should include all subgrade and subbase work, drainage, airfield lighting, grounding, mooring, markings, fencing, ramp area lighting, and other necessary airfield support.

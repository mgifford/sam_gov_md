# PORTLAND TYPE 3 CEMENT RFQ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 10:24:25.117-04
- Solicitation Number: N6449826Q5024

## Summary

The Naval Surface Warfare Center Philadelphia Division (NSWCPD) has a requirement for The Naval Foundry and Propeller Center (NFPC), to be awarded on a competitive basis, to ensure that cement is being delivered to help foundry remain on top of everyday production needs for its Columbia and Virginia projects. Cement is a vital material used in everyday production in accordance with the statement of work. The official Request for Proposal (RFP) will be published on SAM.gov at https://sam.gov/. Written proposals will be requested. Award is anticipated of a Firm-Fixed-Price contract with estimated period of performance to be a two-year period. This effort is being competed as a small business set-aside.

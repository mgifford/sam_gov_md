7.0 EVALUATION

7.1 The Government intends to award a single small business that provides the best value to the Government which meets all the salient characteristics and provisions listed in this RFQ.

7.2 Best value will be determined by evaluating price, past performance, and product specifications meeting the requirements. Product specifications of the proposal and past performance will be evaluated as acceptable or unacceptable. All factors are rated approximately equal.

7.3 The government may award to other than the lowest priced quote if the factors of product specifications proposed provide the best value and exhibit betterments above the minimum requirements identified in the RFQ.

7.4 Negative or adverse information on FAPIIS or SPRS in the last three years may render your quote unacceptable.

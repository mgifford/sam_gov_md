# Automated Discovery for Design and Control of Turbulent Systems (AutoDIDACTS) Proposers Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-11 13:17:27.976-04
- Solicitation Number: DARPA-SN-26-47

## Summary

The Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) Defense Sciences Office (DSO) is sponsoring a Proposers Day webcast to provide information to potential proposers on the objectives of an anticipated Broad Agency Announcement (BAA) for the Automated Discovery for Design and Control of Turbulent Systems (AutoDIDACTS) program. The Proposers Day will be held via webcast on April 1, 2026, from 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. (Eastern Time). Advance registration is required for viewing the webcast.

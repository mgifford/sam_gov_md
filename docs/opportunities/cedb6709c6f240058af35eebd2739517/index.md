# Red Canyon Visitor Center Janitorial

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 08:35:40.309-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LS26Q0023

## Summary

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), Forest Service (FS), Intermountain Region, Dixie National Forest has a requirement for janitorial services for the Red Canyon Visitor Center. A site visit is scheduled for April 7, 2026 at 1:00 PM MT. Interested vendors will meet the COR at the Red Canyon Visitor’s Center located at 5375 UT-12, Panguitch, UT at the scheduled time. Vendors are not required to submit any information to attend the site visit, but they will be asked to fill out an attendance sheet (Company Name, Employee Name, Phone Number, and Email Address). Questions shall be submitted via email to arlen.fausett@usda.gov and are due no later than April 8, 2026, at 10:00 AM Mountain Time. This will ensure enough time to respond before the solicitation period ends. Please include the solicitation name and number as the subject line of the email.

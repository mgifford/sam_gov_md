# FDA Nationwide Ergonomic Services

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-13 12:13:20.288-04
- Solicitation Number: 75F40126P00020
- Awardee: ES SQUARED, LLC Grand Prairie TX 75051 USA

## Summary

The U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA) has awarded a Time-and-Materials (T&M) type Purchase Order, consisting of one (1) Base Year, four (4) Option Years, and one (1) 6-Month Option to Extend Services per FAR clause 52.217-8, to acquire Nationwide Ergonomic Services to support the FDA's Office of Occupational Safety and Health (OOSH) within the Office of the Chief Scientist (OCS).

# Web Subscription to Federal Employment Law Database

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-18 12:15:16.376-04
- Solicitation Number: J-FY26-0010

## Summary

The On-Demand Web Subscription Service is a Human Resources Information System used for research of full-text government-wide statuses, regulations, and case studies. It contains a wealth of knowledge including OPM, Department, and Agency level laws, rules, and regulations, used by OHR professionals to help make decisions on a day-to-day basis.

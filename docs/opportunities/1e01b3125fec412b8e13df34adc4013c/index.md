# Threat Screening Center AI Enhancement

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-27 09:01:43.29-04
- Solicitation Number: FBI-TSC-AIE

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), Threat Screening Center (TSC) has a critical national security mission to consolidate the United States (U.S.) Government’s approach to terrorism and national security threat screening. To help carry out its mission, the TSC requires enhanced technical solutions that will assist its operational staff by providing automation of time-intensive, repeatable manual tasks entailing large volumes of data across multiple domains. This would be considered a commercially acquired effort. Moreover, automation enhancement is needed for systematic tasks such as gap analysis/enhancement of TSC’s workflow involving querying external systems for data enrichment / enhancement.

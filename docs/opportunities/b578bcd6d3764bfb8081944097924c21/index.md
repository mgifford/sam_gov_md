# Replacement of Dental Dry Vacuum Pump System

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 16:57:46.548-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C26026Q0421

## Summary

This is for the replacement of a dry dental vacuum pump system for the VA So. Oregon Rehab Center & Clinics. Offerors should reference the Salient Characteristics and the Statement of Work included within the attached Request for Quote (RFQ). This is a brand name or equal replacement for the existing Mojave Dual System Pump and Tank. This is for a Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business in an open market competitive bid. Bidder will confirm whether or not the proposed product is a domestic end product. Offerors should reference the Evaluation Factors contained in Section 52.212-2 in the attached RFQ.

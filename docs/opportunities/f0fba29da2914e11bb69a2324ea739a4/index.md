# RFQ: Chiller condensing coils, US Embassy Paramaribo

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 15:03:08.944-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15925484

## Summary

Public notice of Request for quotation PR15925484 The U.S. Embassy in Paramaribo, General Services Office (GSO) Contracting and Procurement, seeks a company; qualified, reliable, responsible and responsive with sufficient resources and work experience to provide a quotation for two (2) COIL731F-CH and two (2) COIL730F-CH for the U.S. Embassy Paramaribo. In order for a company to be considered, the company must be able to meet, satisfy, understand, and comply with all requirements in the Request for Quotations (RFQ), including due date. The price should be DDP (excluding import tax and VAT) to Suriname, Paramaribo. The parts must be delivered no later than 360 days of delivery or acceptance of the Purchase Order. Please submit your bid via e-mail on or before 15:00 pm no later than?April 27th, 2026, Paramaribo time. Late offers will not be considered. Your bid should be comprised of: Completed RFQ; SF-18 Detailed information and specification of the proposed part. For questions, please contact the Procurement Unit via e-mail at prmprocurement@state.gov. Please note that this email address is for contracting and procurement inquiries only. Questions related to visas or other issues will not receive a reply. Businesses entering into a contract or purchase order with the U.S. Government are encouraged to register in SAM (www.sam.gov).

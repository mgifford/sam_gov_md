# Transient Alert Services - Fort Campbell

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 12:50:29.631-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124826RA011

## Summary

This is a non-personal services contract to provide transient alert services. The contractor shall manage and operate the Campbell Army Airfield (CAAF) functions of transient alert services and perform minor airfield maintenance, Common Levels of Support (CLS) 900 services (Airfield Operations). The contractor shall provide all labor, consumable supplies and equipment, except any specified as government furnished, to perform Aircraft Ground Support Services at Fort Campbell, KY.

# GCM A-Kit Production

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-01 12:00:12.433-04

- Awardee: THE BOEING COMPANY Saint Louis MO 63134 USA
- Award Amount: 14446239.48

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) awarded a Firm-Fixed Price order under Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) N0001921G0006 to The Boeing Company, 6200 James S McDonnell Blvd., St. Louis Missouri, 63134-1939, using authority FAR Subpart 6.302-1, only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. NAVAIR procured engineering change proposal (ECP) 6444, 6472, and 6482 A-Kits in order to attach, integrate, and utilize the capabilities of the Next Generation Jammer – Mid Band Pod, AN/ALQ-249(v)1, with the EA-18G platform for fleet use in support of United States Navy requirements. The Boeing Company is the sole vendor qualified to procure A-kits in support of the Next Generation Jammer integration for the E/A-18G ASE program. This synopsis is for informational purposes only.

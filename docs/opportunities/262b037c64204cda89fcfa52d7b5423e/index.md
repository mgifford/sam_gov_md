- Agency: UNITED STATES TRADE AND DEVELOPMENT AGENCY
- Type: Special Notice

## Summary

The U.S. Trade and Development Agency is funding a feasibility study to support the Monte Muambe Rare Earths Mining Project in Mozambique (USTDA Activity No. 2026-11004A). Interested U.S. firms may access the Request for Proposal by visiting USTDA’s website at: https://ustda.gov/work/bid-on-an-overseas-project/.

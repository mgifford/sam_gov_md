# FDC SeaTac - Replace Water Heaters

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:05:58.642-04
- Solicitation Number: 24Z6AQ2

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Prisons (FBOP), Federal Detention Center SeaTac, intends to make a single award to a responsible entity to provide all necessary materials, equipment, and incidental labor for the proper removal and disposal of FOUR (4) existing water heaters, and the complete installation of FOUR (4) new Water Heaters. All specified parts and materials must meet current industry standards. Work to be completed onsite at: Federal Detention Center SeaTac 2425 S. 200th Street SeaTac, WA 98198 ALL qualifying candidates will be provided the opportunity to have an on-site tour of the current water heater system on Wednesday, March 25, 2026, to have a better understanding of the removal and installation requirements. Please send your request to: SET-Facilities@bop.gov by 12:00 p.m. Monday, March 23, 2026 for an on-site tour. You will receive an email confirming date and time. All interested parties will need to complete a NCIC Background check, a requirement to enter the facility (a form will be emailed to you upon request to visit). Awarded primary contractor shall: Acquire all parts, materials, and skilled labor to complete the removal and installation of the water heaters according to the attached Statement of Work.

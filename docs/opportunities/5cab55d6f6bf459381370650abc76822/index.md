## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center, Network Contracting Office (NCO) 6, awarded an Emergency sole source contract with BLUE CORD DESIGN AND CONSTRUCTION, LLC for Emergency Piping Liner Installation, at the Asheville VA Medical Center (VAMC). 819.7008 Sole source awards to a certified service-disabled veteran-owned small business. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quotes.

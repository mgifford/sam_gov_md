## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial supplies, issued unrestricted and full/open among Federal Supply Schedule (FSS) contract holders, and prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 8.4, as supplemented with the additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. This is a request for quotes under solicitation number: 36C26226Q0580 The Department of Veterans Affairs Network Contracting Office (NCO) 22 is looking for a contractor who can supply the Gallium-68 (GA-68) Prostate Specific Membrane Antigen (PSMA) radiopharmaceutical for VA San Diego Healthcare System located in San Diego, California. The Contractor shall furnish all supplies/services at participating VA medical centers. Shipping term is FOB Destination. Shipping must be included in the unit cost.

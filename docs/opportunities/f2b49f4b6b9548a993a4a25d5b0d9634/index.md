THIS IS A PRESOLICITATION NOTICE OF A SET-ASIDE FOR SDVOSB BB RENOVATE GROUND FLOOR HALLS AND WALLS. This procurement is set-aside based on an order of priority as established in 38 U.S.C. 8127. Project # 673-21-153 BB RENOVATE GROUND FLOOR HALLS AND WALLS, at the James A Haley Veterans Affairs Medical Center, located at 13000 Bruce B Downs Blvd, Tampa, FL 33612, RFQ # 36C24826Q0451. THE BID/BUILD CONTRACT SHALL INCLUDE include but not be limited to the renovation of the halls and walls on the ground floor of building 1 at the James A. Haley VA hospital, located at 13000 Bruce B. Downs boulevard, Tampa, Florida as required by the drawings and specifications included in this acquisition package. The scope of work includes asbestos abatement and the replacement of flooring, ceilings, doors, hardware, light fixtures, crash rails, handrails, drinking fountains and miscellaneous elements that are directly related to the hallways. This work shall include but not be limited to architectural, mechanical, plumbing, electrical, and abatement. AS REQUIRED BY DRAWINGS AND SPECIFICATIONS (See attached SOW) The NAICS code for this procurement is 236220, Commercial and Institutional Building Construction with a small business size standard of $45M. The VAAR 836.204 magnitude of this project is between $5,000,000.00 and $10,000,000.00. The project will be 100% set-aside for Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses. All businesses desiring to be considered must be registered with SAM.gov prior to contract award, during contract period of performance, and must continue to remain registered through the final invoice payment. Business size and NAICS will be verified at Small Business Search (https://search.certifications.sba.gov/ ).

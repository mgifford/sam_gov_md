# Sources Sought Announcement for Joint Battle Command Platform (JBC-P) Mounted Family of Computer Systems (MFoCS) Block II Hardware

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 15:19:31.166-04
- Solicitation Number: DLA-Aberdeen-26-035

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency-Aberdeen, on behalf of the Army Integrated Logistics Supply Center (ILSC) – Supply Chain Management Directorate (SCMD) – Strategic Sourcing Directorate (SSD), intends to acquire Parts to support the Joint Battle Command Platform (JBC-P). Requirements Description: Joint Battle Command-Platform is the Army’s friendly force tracking system, equipping soldiers with a faster satellite network, secure data encryption and advanced logistics. The subject MFoCS computer hardware and peripheral equipment are essential elements of the JBC-P system. The Government does not possess the technical data package (TDP). The TDP is not available for release. The TDP includes level 3. production information.

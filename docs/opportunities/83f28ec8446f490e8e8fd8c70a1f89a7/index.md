# Threat Coordination Group on Adversarial Threats to Armor (Main Battle Tanks and Armored Vehicles)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 14:01:43.725-04

## Summary

Scope of Engagement. The TCG will address global technology trends relevant to acquisition decision-making, including but not limited to: Peer and near-peer technology trends Threat armor and anti-armor capabilities Survivability and Lethality challenges in contested environments Counter-UAS and emerging aerial threat considerations Threat static-displays Classification and Security TCG discussions at minimum will be conducted at the SECRET//NOFORN level. No classified information will be posted to SAM.gov. Participation is limited to U.S. citizens with a current SECRET clearance (or higher) affiliated with a cleared U.S. Facility. Eligibility U.S.-based companies with an active Facility Clearance (FCL). Experience supporting armor systems, sensors, sUAS and counter-sUAS, electronic warfare, or threat modeling. Requested Information from Industry Interested parties are requested to submit the following unclassified information to the POCs: Company name, CAGE code, and primary point of contact Confirmation of facility clearance and clearance level Brief description of relevant capabilities and portfolio alignment (no more than 1 paragraph in an email to the POC) General areas of threat or intelligence interested related to armor and/or anti-armor Anticipated number of proposed attendees and clearance levels (Limit of 5, ideally system engineers or personnel with security clearances that influence capability development) Anticipated logistics Timeframe: 19-21 May 2026 Location: Cleared facility in Charlottesville, VA. Format: In-person, classified environment. Specific location and participation details will be provided to selected participants. Important Disclaimers Participation is voluntary and at no cost to the Government The Government will not reimburse travel or participation expenses The Government reserves the right to limit participation to ensure balanced and effective discussions given space constraints This notice does not obligate the Government to take any future acquisition action

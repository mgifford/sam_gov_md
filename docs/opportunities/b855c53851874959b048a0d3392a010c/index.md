- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:03:02.399-04

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with FAR Part 12, for twenty (20) Hand-Held Rotary Heat Sealers for DLA Distribution Red River, Texas. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number SP330026Q0060 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ), to provide twenty (20) hand-held rotary heat sealers for DLA Distribution Red River, Tx. This acquisition is being solicited as a 100% small business set-aside. The NAICS code is 333993 and the size standard is 600 employees. The Product or Service Code for this acquisition is 3540. The closing date and time for the submission of questions is: 1:00 PM ET on March 16, 2026. The closing date and time for submission of quotes is: 1:00 PM ET on March 19, 2026. As a result of this solicitation, one (1) firm-fixed-price purchase order will be issued.

## Summary

This requirement is for to perform necessary repairs on a 2019 FORD F550 4X4 STAKE BED TRUCK EXT CAB. G71-1361Y, VIN: 1FD0X5HT8KEG57072. All equipment must be covered by the manufacturer’s warranty.

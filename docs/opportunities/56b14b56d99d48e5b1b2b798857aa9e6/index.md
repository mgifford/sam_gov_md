# Request for Information: Detroit MI Family Member Update

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-02 11:24:14.8-04
- Solicitation Number: WPR04022026

## Summary

The Contractor shall furnish facilities, furniture, equipment and supplies, management, supervision, and labor to provide meeting space, audio video support, and light refreshments for the Detroit, Michigan Family Member Update (FMU) in support of the Defense POW/MIA Accounting Agency (DPAA). The purpose for the FMU is to meet with family members of missing servicemen from WWII, the Korean War, the Cold War, and the Vietnam War and brief them on the U.S. government’s efforts to recover their loved one’s remains and return them to their families for burial with full military honors. Date: Saturday, 2 May 2026 Location: Detroit, Michigan Meeting: Defense POW/MIA Accounting Agency Family Member Update (FMU) Estimated Attendance: 450

# Dam Valve Replacement/Repair for the Hoosier National Forest

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 13:47:33.804-05
- Solicitation Number: 12444526Q0013

## Summary

The Hoosier National Forest has three earthen dams that have maintenance needs with their valve operations. These dams are located on the Tell City Ranger District (See attached maps for locations and directions). The existing valve systems for all three dams are different. Known equipment and diagrams for each dam have been included in attachments to this solicitation. A copy of the as-builts can be acquired upon request. The scope of work includes the replacement of the dam valve assemblies at Celina Lake and Saddle Lake dams, and the repair of the dam valve assembly at the U-38 dam located on the Hoosier National Forest

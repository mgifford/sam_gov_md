# Wellness Application for the USCP

- Agency: THE LEGISLATIVE BRANCH
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 16:13:29.581-04
- Solicitation Number: 202600007

## Summary

The purpose of this requirement is to acquire a comprehensive digital wellness application that supports the physical, mental, emotional, spiritual, and occupational well-being of the United States Capitol Police workforce, including officers, civilian employees, supervisors, retirees, and family members. The Contractor shall provide a secure, confidential, user-friendly mobile and web platform that delivers 24/7 access to wellness resources, professional support connections, peer support tools, educational materials, analytics, and crisis response pathways that are tailored to the needs of law enforcement personnel.

## Summary

The Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, and equipment to provide and install normal and emergency power outlets, data drops, and required equipment bracing to support Electronic Health Record (EHR) equipment deployment on the St. Cloud VA campus as shown and described in the project drawings and specifications.

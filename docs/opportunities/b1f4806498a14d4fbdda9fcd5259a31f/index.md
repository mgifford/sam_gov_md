# DDSP Lease of Portable Latrines & Trailer Pumping

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:35:21.906-04
- Solicitation Number: SP3300-26-Q-0081

## Summary

This Request for Quotation (RFQ) is being issued to establish a firm-fixed-price (FFP) purchase to lease 6 (six) portable latrines & trailer pumping IAW Statement 1 – Statement of Work for DLA Distribution Susquehanna, PA (DDSP). The Government intends to award one (1) purchase order as a result of this RFQ and will be issued on a Standard Form (SF) 1449. This acquisition is not a Defense Priorities and Allocations System (DPAS) rated procurement. The period of performance is for a base year and two one-year options. Vendors must provide unit prices rounded to two decimal points in their quotes.

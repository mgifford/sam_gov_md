# National Law Enforcement Terminal System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-17 15:36:14.182-04
- Solicitation Number: NLETS2026

## Summary

The 42nd Contracting Squadron at Maxwell AFB, AL intends to award a sole source contract to Alabama Law Enforcement Agency (ALEA), 301 S Ripley St, Montgomery, AL 36104 for the purchase of continuous service access to law enforcement databases through the National Law Enforcement Terminal System (NLETS) to support the 42d Security Forces Squadron at both Maxwell AFB and Gunter Annex. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 561611.

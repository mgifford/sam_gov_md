## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR), Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Items, as a supplemented additional information in the notice. Solicitation number W9127Q26QA003 is being issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) with Open Market Pricing.

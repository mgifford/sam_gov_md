# NOI - IC AVVIGO Guidance System Service Contract Plan for NHLBI Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 15:50:38.136-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N98026Q00205

## Summary

Notice of Intent to Sole Source: The National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHLBI) intends to award a sole source contract to Boston Scientific Corporation for a service agreement for the IC AVVIGO Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Guidance System located in the NIH Clinical Center Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory. This acquisition is conducted under FAR Part 13 and FAR Part 12. This notice is not a request for competitive quotes; however, interested parties may submit a capability statement.

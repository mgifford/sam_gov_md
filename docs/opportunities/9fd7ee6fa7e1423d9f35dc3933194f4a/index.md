## Summary

SERVICES REQUIRED: The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Healthcare Network Downstate New York (VISN 2) has a requirement for Home Oxygen Services to be furnished by a Contractor to our VA Beneficiaries on the Home Oxygen Program. If not accredited by Joint Commission, Contractor must have current accreditation with a nationally recognized accreditation organization that meets or exceeds Joint Commission standards and must maintain accreditation throughout term of contract

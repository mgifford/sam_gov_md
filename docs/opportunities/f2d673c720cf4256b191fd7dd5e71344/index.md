The Salient Characteristics must include: Must have Integrated Light Carrier that is fully incorporated into the textured handle with built-in protection Esophagoscope must have a barrel and tip that follow ergonomic features of smooth insertion technology that facilitates gentle insertion while maintaining standard technique. Must provide nearly full lumen view with constant cool illumination. Will have a Large Fiber Bundle: for Enhanced distal illumination. Must have Aspiration Channel on the exterior for maximum lumen clearance. Must have Oval Fiber Optic Light Carrier for more viewing and instrumentation space in the main tube. Must also provide bright illumination. Must have a Smooth Distal Tip for ease of insertion. Must provide a 15 mm Proximal End designed for easy attachment of accessories. Esophagoscopy instruments in the set must be capable of extracting sharp or large objects (e.g., bones, cones, dentures) and manage food impactions.

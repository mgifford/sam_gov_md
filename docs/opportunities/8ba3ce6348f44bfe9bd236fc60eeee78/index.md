## Summary

KFFO LVD AERIAL SEEDING BOWMAN WELLS FIRE The Oregon State Office of the Bureau of Land Management - Portland, OR has a requirement for the Lakeview District Office, located at 1301 S. G Street - Lakeview, OR 97630 USA and 2795 Anderson Ave, Bldg. 25 Klamath Falls, OR 97603 USA. The Lakeview District Office has a requirement to have a vendor provide "Broadcast Seeding Services" for the Klamath Falls Field Office and Lakeview Field Office area of responsibility. This procurement is anticipated to be a Small Business set-aside. The NAICS code is 115310 "Support Activities for Forestry". The size standard is $11.5 Million. The anticipated contract type will be a firm fixed-price. The Request for Quotation No. 140L4326Q0028, using the principles of FAR 12, Acquisition of Commercial Items will be posted to SAM.gov on or about March 25th, 2026. These dates are subject to change. All contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management database (www.SAM.gov) to be eligible for award of a contract. The point of contacts (POCs) for this pre-solicitation notice is Kevin J. Cochran, Contracting Specialist, at kcochran@ios.doi.gov. Comments should be forwarded in writing to Kevin J. Cochran at the above referenced email address.

# GRCA South Rim Generator Maintenance BPA

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-03 13:32:01.521-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P1526Q0064

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) is to obtain information from industry for potential sources capable of performing services for the U.S. Department of the Interior, National Park Service (NPS) on behalf of Grand Canyon National Park (GRCA) located in Arizona. Grand Canyon National Park (GRCA) has twelve (12) generators located at various sites throughout the South Rim that support critical infrastructure and visitor services. Due to the essential nature of these generators, they must remain operational at all times to ensure continuity of park operations, safety systems, and emergency response capabilities. Routine inspections and maintenance, preventative maintenance, corrective repairs, and the potential for unforeseen emergency repairs are required on a recurring and sometimes urgent basis.

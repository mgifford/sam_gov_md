# Inyo National Forest (NF) Wastewater Treatment Plant (WWTP) Operations (Ops) & Maintenance (Maint)

- Posted: 2026-03-12 18:47:54.872-04

## Summary

------------------------------------------------------------------------------- Q&A Memorandum: Uploaded 12 March 2026 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- USFS PPS FPO SWZ CSA #2 is pursuing a contract for services to provide Wastewater Treatment Plant (WWTP) Operations (Ops) and Maintenance (Maint) services at various facilities on the Inyo National Forest. The facilities included are the Bishop Creek WWTP, Rock Creek WWTP, Convict Lake WWTP, and the lift station at the Eastern Sierra Visitor Center. Services shall be performed by the awardee in accordance with the specifications, terms, and conditions of this solicitation, including those found in the solicitation attachments (see Solicitation Section D). SET-ASIDE: This solicitation is set aside 100% for Small Business entities under NAICS 221320 - Sewage Treatment Facilities. PROJECT LOCATION: Inyo National Forest, various locations. See solicitation and its attachments for location details. SOLICITATION POINT OF CONTACT: Matthew (Matt) Killian, Contracting Officer matthew.killian@usda.gov SITE VISIT INFORMATION: A site visit will be held at 9:00 AM PST on 4 March 2026 at the following location: Interagency Visitor Center Lift Station US-395 & CA-136 Lone Pine, CA 93545 NOTE: the Site Visit will be conducted at multiple locations, beginning with the site identified above. Information about the additional locations will be provided to each vendor upon Site Visit attendance registration. To attend the site visit, contact the Contracting Officer, Matthew (Matt) Killian, via email at matthew.killian@usda.gov.

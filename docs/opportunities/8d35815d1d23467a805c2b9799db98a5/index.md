# MAINTENANCE SERVICES FOR Biocare intelliPATH FLX

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-19 10:02:43.218-04
- Solicitation Number: BCPAT01-0001

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all labor, material and equipment necessary to maintain and provide preventive maintenance for the Biocare intelliPATH FLX Automated Slide Staining System. This instrument is used for automated, high throughput IHC staining of wide format slides of whole mount FFPE prostate tissue sections to characterize tumor foci and protein expression within each sample and support the research projects studying prostate cancer development, progression, and drug resistance. All maintenance services shall be performed on-site in accordance with the manufacturer’s standard commercial maintenance practices.

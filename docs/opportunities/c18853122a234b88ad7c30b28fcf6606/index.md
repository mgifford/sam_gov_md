# Envitia Software Renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 08:56:39.98-04
- Solicitation Number: FD2399-2600117

## Summary

Envitia's MapLink Pro is a suite of powerful SDKs that enables developers to quickly build and deploy high-performance mapping and terrain visualization systems. Leveraged by the majority of the world's largest systems integrators where high performance map visualization within situational awareness is mission-critical, MapLink Pro is the “visualization toolkit of choice” for the management and display of geospatial and live track data. MapLink Pro is the dynamic mapping visualization engine underpinning some the world's most well-known C2 and ISR applications. These deployments range from strategic SIGINT platforms and operational surveillance assets, to tactical mission planning and air defense systems.

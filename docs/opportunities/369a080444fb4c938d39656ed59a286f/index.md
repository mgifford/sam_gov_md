The Great Lakes Acquisition Center (NCO 12), Department of Veterans Affairs, is seeking information from sources capable of performing industrial level mechanical services on critical healthcare utility and support equipment. The Hines Boiler Plant houses (4) four, 35,000 lb/hr water tube steam boilers. The Joliet CBOC has (3) three Williams & Davis model 3050W-2004-01 Boilers with Limpsfield-LCN 9 burners. Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, equipment, and tools necessary to complete Calibrations, Safety Device Testing, inspections (internal & External), preventative maintenance, and repair. Services shall be performed on-site at Edward Hines Jr. VA Hospital, Hines, IL. and the Joliet CBOC located in Joliet IL Equipment is located within the boiler plants at these locations. The plants shall remain operational at all times. Services shall be performed in accordance with the SOW, the original equipment manufacturers' (OEM) recommendations; any local, state, and federal standards; and VHA DIRECTIVE 1810, 578-12-138A-078 Boiler Plant Operations (Hines Directive) VA Policies & Procedures; Boiler Device Safety testing manual latest revision (currently v8).

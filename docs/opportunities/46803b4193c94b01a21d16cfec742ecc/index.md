## Summary

Page 3 of 3 Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Network Contracting Office 2 (NCO 2) intends to award a sole source contract to Siemens Medical Solutions USA, Inc. for preventative maintenance and repair services for Siemens imaging equipment. Period of performance: 07/01/2026 - 06/30/2027 with a provision of four (4) option years. Basic description of the requirement: Siemens Medical Solutions USA, Inc (Siemens) is the original equipment manufacturer (OEM) and is the sole authorized licenser of equipment/software and provider of maintenance and support. Siemens will provide a full-service agreement to include: authorized/trained technician, travel, labor, equipment, tools and materials, OEM parts, repair services, telephone and remote diagnostic support, and software updates to the hardware and software in place as required, and preventative maintenance.

## Summary

This award notice announces the contract award to Brasfield Gorrie, LLC, under solicitation number 70Z04725PCNI0007 for Design-Build Utility Infrastructure, Roads & Clinic, USCG Base Charleston. The contract was awarded on April 27, 2026, in the amount of $95,538,222.00 for Base Items 0001-0004 and Option Items 0001 and 0002. The purpose of this contract is to construct buildings, utilities and infrastructure at Base Charleston, South Carolina, to include a clinic, entry control point, perimeter fencing, and roadways necessary to support the existing and future facilities and waterfront activities.

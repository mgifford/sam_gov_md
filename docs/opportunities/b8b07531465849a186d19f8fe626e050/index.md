## Summary

The contractor shall prepare for and accomplish the voyage repair availability (VRA) of the MSC Ship as described in the work package. The work items- Category 'A' are provided as "Attachment J-2 Work Items Description." Category A includes definitized work items for which a firm fixed price has been established and will be executed during the period of performance and included in the base contract. Only Category A work items are to be priced.

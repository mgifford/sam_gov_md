# Commercial Weather Data Pilot (CWDP) Program – Wildfire Imagery Pilot Study

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 09:18:15.945-04
- Solicitation Number: 1332KP26R0016

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1332KP26R0016 is issued as a request for proposal (RFP) to acquire available commercial multi-spectral imagery observations and investigate the utility of radiometric data developed by commercial vendors to aid in wildfire detection and characterization, monitoring, and mapping. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. Proposals are required to be received in the contracting office no later than 2:00 P.M., Eastern Time on April 30, 2026. Questions related to this RFP are due no later than 2:00 P.M. Eastern Time on April 20, 2026. Questions may be submitted in the attached question form.

# Summer Camp

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-19 12:05:53.677-04
- Solicitation Number: W912TF26QA010

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6 as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; proposals are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. The solicitation number is W912TF26QA010 and is issued as a Request for Quote (RFQ), unless otherwise indicated herein. The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2026-01. The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 721214 - Recreational and Vacation Camps (Except Campgrounds), a small business size standard of $9M. The solicitation will start on the date this solicitation is posted and will end on 19 May 2026, 4:00PM. Eastern Time. Free On Board (FOB) Destination shall be required to 1 Minuteman Way, Concord, NH 03301. The Government intends to award a Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) contract. The NH Army National Guard requires the following items: CLIN 0001: 2026 Youth Summer Camp (QTY= 1 Job) Must meet the following salient characteristics: The contractor shall provide a summer camp for youths as described in the performance work statement. Please see the performance work statement for a complete description of the Government’s need. All facilities must be located within the geographical confines of the state of New Hampshire. Period of performance is from 26 July 2026 to 29 July 2026. CLIN 0002: Breakfast Meals (QTY= 1 Job) Provide on-site breakfast for 50 youth (150 Meals) for the duration of the period of performance. CLIN 0003: Lunch Meals (QTY= 1 Job) Provide on-site lunch for 50 youth (200 Meals) for the duration of the period of performance. CLIN 0004: Dinner Meals (QTY= 1 Job) Provide on-site dinner for 50 youth (150 Meals) for the duration of the period of performance. Interested parties should see “Instructions to Offers” in the Provisions and Clauses document for details on how to submit your quotes (IAW 52.212-1) Interested parties should also see “Evaluation factors” in the Provisions and Clauses document for a description on how the Government intends to evaluate your quotes (IAW FAR 52.212-2) Quotes must be valid for sixty (60) days. All technical or contractual questions shall be submitted in writing via e-mail no later than 12:00 noon EST on 12 May 2026 to Grace Jordan at grace.jordan.1@us.af.mil and Richard Trafton at Richard.C.Trafton.civ@army.mil. Telephone inquiries will not be accepted. If the Government responses to technical questions affect any portion of the solicitation then an Amendment of Solicitation will be issued. The subject of your email must include your company's legal name, state the nature of your email and include the Solicitation Number W912TF26QA010. For example, the subject of your email should be similar to the following: Quote Submission by Company XXXXX, Solicitation Number W912TF26QA010 Company XXXXX Question Regarding Solicitation Number W912TF26QA010. This solicitation requires registration with the System for Award Management (SAM) in order to be considered for award, pursuant to applicable regulations and guidelines. Registration information can be found at www.sam.gov. Registration must be "ACTIVE" at the time of award. See the “Provisions & Clauses” attachment for a complete list of provisions & clauses applicable to this solicitation

# CANCELED - Pharmaceutical Reverse Distribution Services for Expired, Short-Dated, and Hazardous Medications at VA Northeast Ohio Healthcare System (VANEOHS) Pharmacy Locations

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 12:43:05.855-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25026Q0321

## Summary

This solicitation is canceled. No award will be made. See attached cancellation notice for details. Further acquisition activity related to this requirement may be announced at a future date. Pharmaceutical reverse distribution services for the VA Northeast Ohio Healthcare System (VANEOHS), to provide on?site identification, preparation, and shipment of expired and short?dated pharmaceuticals; processing of controlled and non?controlled items for manufacturer credit through the VA Pharmaceutical Prime Vendor (PPV); detailed credit and disposal reporting; and compliant destruction of non?returnable and hazardous pharmaceuticals. The requirement includes recurring on?site service visits to seven VANEOHS pharmacy locations under a firm?fixed, flat?fee, per?site?visit pricing model that is not commission?based and is independent of credit value.

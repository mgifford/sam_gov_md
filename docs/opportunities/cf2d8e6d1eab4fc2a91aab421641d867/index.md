# Q505--Locum Gastroenterology (GI) Physician Services MSO-2026-COM-0017

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-03-18 16:49:21.603-04

- Awardee: null

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), intends to enter into an indefinite-delivery indefinite-quantity (IDIQ) contract for Board Certified/Board Eligible in Gastroenterology and provide Gastroenterology Physician Services on site in accordance with the specifications contained herein to beneficiaries of the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) and the South Texas Veterans Health Care System (STVHCS), Audie L. Murphy VA Medical Center. The intended term of contract is for one (1) base year plus four (4) one-year opts if the requirement continues to exist. Solicitation documents are anticipated to be available February 27, 2026, or shortly thereafter and will be posted via the System for Award Management (SAM) website. Prospective Offerors should not expect notification of issuance of amendments other than via the SAM website and are responsible for obtaining all amendments as posted. Prior to submission of a quote, Offerors are expected to read the solicitation documentation in its entirety to fully understand the scope of work and terms and conditions of the requirement. The VA anticipates issuing a 100% Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business set-aside for this requirement. The solicitation will be available for download from SAM.gov. Interested parties will be responsible for downloading copies of the solicitation (and any subsequent amendments). The solicitation will provide instructions to offerors about the quote submission process. Prospective offerors are encouraged to register their interest in the solicitation on the SAM website so that they are notified of the issuance of the solicitation and any amendments. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 621111, Offices of Physicians, except Mental Health Specialists and the size standard is $16 Million. The classification code is Q505. Offers from all capable and responsible SDVOSB sources will be considered. Firms should ensure their registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) database, https://www.sam.gov/, is current and should ensure the applicable NAICS code (621111) is included in the SAM profile prior to submission of proposals. Note 1: This is not a solicitation. Questions will only be answered after a solicitation is posted and within the timeframe specified within the Request for Quote. During that timeframe, telephone requests for information will NOT be accepted. Note 2: This is a new procurement there is no incumbent.

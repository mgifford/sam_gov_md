# PAE Maritime Spring 2026 Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-21 09:55:08.847-04
- Solicitation Number: N61177-26-ID-0001

## Summary

This event is closed to media attendance. Registration is open for the Portfolio Acquisition Executive Maritime (PAE Maritime) Spring 2026 Industry Day! Register now at https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/L0MCbwi7y3 PAE Maritime invites industry partners to an in-person industry day on May 13, 2026, to hear from senior leaders representing the requirements, program management, and contracting disciplines about the establishment of the PAE and its priorities across the shipbuilding, modernization and sustainment domains. With responsibility for aircraft carrier, surface, expeditionary, mine warfare, and auxiliary fleet ship construction and sustainment, PAE Maritime officials will outline the scope of the portfolio, introduce key leaders and points of contact, and highlight upcoming contracting opportunities associated with the fiscal year 2027 President’s Budget. WHAT: PAE Maritime Spring 2026 Industry Day WHEN: Wednesday, May 13, 10:00 a.m.* – 5:00 p.m. (*tentative—start time may shift slightly) WHERE: Washington, D.C. (Venue to be provided to registered attendees) WHO: Those interested in potential collaborations with PAE Maritime are encouraged to attend. Attendance is open to representatives of large and small companies; commercial; academia; consortiums; and other government organizations. IMPORTANT NOTE: Due to space limitations, attendance from each organization will be limited to a maximum of two representatives. Organizations should deconflict attendees internally before submission. Additional registrations may be declined. We look forward to seeing you at Industry Day!

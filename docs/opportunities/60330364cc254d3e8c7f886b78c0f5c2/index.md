# Department of Defense Cyber Defense Command (DCDC) Signage

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 10:53:12.375-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102826R0029

## Summary

Department of Defense Cyber Defense Command (DCDC) high-quality branding and signage in office spaces and outdoor spaces at the Defense Information System Agency Headquarters(DISA HQ). The Contractor shall design and manufacture or otherwise acquire signage as per the current designs photos and replacement details are provided in Technical Exhibit 2. The Contractor shall submit digital design proofs and material samples for all signage to the COR for written approval prior to fabrication. Proofs shall include dimensions, colors, font styles, and mounting hardware specifications.

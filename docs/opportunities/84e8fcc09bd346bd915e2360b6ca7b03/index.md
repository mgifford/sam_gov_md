# Notice of Intent to Sole Source - SIUS Target System Repair Parts

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 07:10:01.766-04
- Solicitation Number: PANMCC26P0000040239

## Summary

The United States Army Marksmanship Unit (USAMU) intends to award a firm fixed price requirements contract for SIUS Target System Repair Parts. This contract will be for the of repair parts and expendables for the SUIS Automated Electronic Precision Target Scoring System on an “as required basis” for a one year base period and four, one-year option periods. The NAICS code is 332721 with a size standard of 500 employees. This requirement is for USAMU located at Fort Benning, Georgia, it is currently the recognized targetry system for the International Shooting Sports Federation (ISSF). The performance period for this contract will be from 1 SEP 2026 to 31 AUG 2031. Contract award will be made to the only contractor available, who is the sole manufacturer of parts for the SUIS target scoring system. The requirements contract consists of the procurement of replacement parts and expendables to keep the USAMU’s Suis Ascor System operational for training and competition. Comments shall be received, no later than 10:00 AM, ET, Fort Knox local time, on 17 April 2026 via e-mail to both Mr. Kelly Tipple at kelly.m.tipple.civ@army.mil and Ms. Brabara J. Jeffries at barbara.j.jeffries5.civ@army.mil. Any firm/individual sumitting comments shall include PANMCC-25-P-0000040239 in the subject line of the communication and identify their name, point of contact, title, address and telephone number.

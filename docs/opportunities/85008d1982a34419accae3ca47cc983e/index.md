## Summary

THE INTENT OF THIS NOTICE IS TO IDENTIFY POTENTIAL OFFERORS ONLY. This is a sources sought synopsis in support of a market survey being conducted by the 20th Contracting Squadron, Shaw AFB, SC 29152 in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation Part 10, Market Research, to determine interest and capabilities of potential sources to provide: Dronebuster 4 This synopsis is for informational and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation, a synopsis of a solicitation, a commitment on the part of the Government to later publish a solicitation, or ultimately award a contract. The Government will not reimburse the costs of preparing or submitting a response to this synopsis. Interested sources shall indicate whether they are capable of providing the subject requirement, how long after issuance of a purchase order would it take to complete the requirement, and furthermore indicate their size and status (i.e., small business, 8(a), HUBZone, WOSB, SDVOSB, etc.). No other information is required at this time. Based upon the responses received, the Government will determine the set-aside method for the procurement. An award will not be made on offers received in response to this notice. This source sought synopsis is not a Request for Quote (RFQ); it is a market research tool being utilized to determine the availability of sources. Although this is a Small Business set-aside RFI, all submissions will be accepted and reviewed and can be submitted directly at the email location below. This is intended to be a Firm Fixed Price. Interested sources capable of providing this requirement must respond via e-mail to motaz.irshid@us.af.mil, referencing Dronebuster 4 no later than 26 March 2026, at 8:00 AM EST.

# Navy Medical Readiness Logistics Command (NMRLC) - Warehouse Support Services (WSS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 14:28:54.994-05
- Solicitation Number: A027723

## Summary

This contract will provide warehouse support, supporting all aspects of warehousing operations. The required support includes maintaining all Civil Engineering Equipment (CEE) in support of NMRLC EXMEDS programs. CEE consists of Civil Engineer Support Equipment (CESE), Civil Engineer End Items (CEEI) Configurable Shelters, Containers, and Expeditionary Tool Kits. This scope of work includes all collateral and installed equipment associated with the CEE. 95% of all monthly scheduled CEE workorders shall be in a ready for issue status, this percentage will be used to measure the contractor�s monthly performance. Contractors in coordination with NMRLC staff will pre-assign CEE material to EXMEDS. The contractor shall provide labor required for warehouse support services, supporting all aspects of warehousing/production operations. As these are non-commercial support services, they will be competed among Seaport MAC holders who qualify as SDVOSB.

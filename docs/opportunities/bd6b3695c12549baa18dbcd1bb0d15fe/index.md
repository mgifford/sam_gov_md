# 561A4-26-509 | Repair Steam line and Condensate System building 53

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 07:13:31.174-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24226R0083

## Summary

This firm fixed-price contract is for steam line and condensate system repairs at Building 53 of the VA Health Care System in Lyons New Jersey, Including replacement of the steam condensate tank, pump, controls, and associated steam distribution components to restore reliable system operation in support of medical center functions.

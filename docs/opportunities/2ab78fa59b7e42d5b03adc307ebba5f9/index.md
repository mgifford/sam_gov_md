## Summary

This is a market survey for a Tactical Intelligence Platform for International Travel. This is not a screening information request or request for a proposal of any kind. The FAA is not seeking or accepting unsolicited proposals. Vendors will not be notified of the results of this market survey or the results of the information submitted. The purpose of this market survey is to solicit statements of interest and capabilities from all businesses capable of providing ASH with a Tactical Intelligence Platform for International Travel as described in this market survey. The Office of Investigations & Professional Responsibility (AXI-1) International Travel Security Program (ITSP)’s mission is to protect the Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) and the National Airspace System by preparing FAA employees who travel internationally with timely and pertinent information concerning the dangers and risks they may encounter while abroad, and countermeasures to protect themselves from becoming victims of terrorism and crime or targets of foreign intelligence entities. Furthermore, the ITSP ensures the FAA’s Duty of Care by monitoring safety and security concerns that could impact employees traveling internationally on official FAA business and advises them appropriately before departure and while on travel. The FAA requires a submittal demonstrating your organization’s strategy, capability, and methodology of performance by responding to the inquiries below and completing them as they would relate to the FAA. Your response must not exceed a maximum of ten (10) pages. Use font size Times New Roman 11.

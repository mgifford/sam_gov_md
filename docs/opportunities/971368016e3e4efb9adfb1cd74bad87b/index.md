- Posted: 2026-04-22 09:31:51.435-04

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Baltimore District is considering a requirement for Janitorial Services. Jennings Randolph Lake is located in the Robert W. Craig Campground off West Virginia State Highway Route 46, five miles from Elk Garden, West Virginia. Although minor janitorial services are provided throughout the year some of the services are pro-vided seasonal from 01 January to 31 December each year.

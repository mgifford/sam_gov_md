The Naval Surface Warfare Center, Dahlgren Division (NSWCDD), intends to procure on a Brand Name or Equal basis for Avalue Technology Inc., manufactured products. It was determined that these touch panel personal computers (PC) are the only solution compatible with current systems. Avalue Technology Inc. is located in Marlboro, New Jersey. All costs associated with this requirement must be included in the quoted price.

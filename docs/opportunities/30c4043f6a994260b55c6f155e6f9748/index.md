# Site G Managed Services Support for Government-Owned Pharmaceutical, Medical Supplies, and Medical Equipment (Site G)

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 12:16:08.197-04
- Solicitation Number: 47QFCA26R0010

## Summary

General Services Administration (GSA) Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS) is issuing this Request for Proposal (RFP) for Managed Services Support for Government-Owned Pharmaceutical, Medical Supplies, and Medical Equipment (Site G) on behalf of a United States Government Agency. The contract is to provide managed services for storing, managing, and maintaining medical products as well as monitoring, reporting, and directing logistics of Government provided pharmaceuticals, medical and non-medical supplies, and equipment. See attached documents for more information.

# LIBI - Traffic Control Barricades for event

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 12:11:51.427-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P1526Q0072

## Summary

Little Bighorn Battlefield National Monument (LIBI) requires the rental, delivery, set up and maintaining of traffic control barriers for the upcoming 150th Anniversary of the Battle of Little Bighorn. Additional specifications are attached. The Period of Performance will run from June 24 through June 27, 2026, while the event itself will occur from June 25 through June 27, 2026. This is being conducted as a service contract. This is 100% a Total Small Business Set-Aside. Questions are due April 30, 2026 by 12pm MT and must be submitted electronically to lindsay_mclaughlin@ios.doi.gov. A consolidated list of questions with Government responses will be posted as an amendment to this solicitation. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation. Follow all terms set forth to submit a quote.

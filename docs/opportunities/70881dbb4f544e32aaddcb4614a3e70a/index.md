# SOLE SOURCE - AN/SPG-62 Antenna Reflector Manufacturing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 12:07:42.043-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016419DWP49_Modification

## Summary

Naval Surface Warfare Center, Crane (NSWC Crane) intends to negotiate and award an additional 12-months of Firm-Fixed-Prices (FFP) for up to 35 units with General Dynamics, Marion, Virginia, under current Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) N0016419DWP49, for new manufacture of AN/SPG-62 Reflector (P/N 6778045-50), for the MK-99 Fire Control System (FCS) for US Navy and Foreign Military Sales (FMS) customers, with an estimated modification increase to N0016419DWP49 IDIQ total of $15M resulting in a cumulative IDIQ ceiling of $31.4M. Current IDIQ N0016419DWP49 was awarded on a sole source basis in accordance with FAR 6.302-1(a)(2) only one responsible source without unacceptable delays. This modification will be awarded in accordance with Revolutionary FAR Overhaul 6. 103-1 only one responsible source without unacceptable delays. The requirement is being solicited on a sole source basis due to time sensitive requirements. NSWC Crane does anticipate to solicit a competitive follow-on procurement via SAM.gov for the AN/SPG-62 reflectors in Fiscal Year 2026. Responsible sources may submit a quotation, bid, or proposal, as appropriate, which will be considered by the agency. Information received in response to this synopsis will be reviewed and considered. Questions or inquiries should be directed to Shayden Flynn, Code 0234, at email shayden.m.flynn.civ@us.navy.mil. Please reference the above solicitation number when responding to this notice.

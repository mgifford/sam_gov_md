# Central Texas C-MAC Laryngoscope

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-10 11:02:56.037-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25726Q0452

## Summary

This is a source(s) sought notice for information purposes only and it is not a solicitation announcement or an obligation or commitment by the Government. This notice is intended strictly for market research. The Department of Veterans Affairs - Network Contracting Office 17 is seeking information in response to this Sources Sought Notice for market research purposes only. Interested Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Businesses, Veteran Owned Small Businesses, Small Business Administration Section 8a, Women-Owned, and other socio-economically disadvantaged small businesses who believe they possess the experience and qualifications to successfully perform the scope of work described below are encouraged to respond in order for us to make a set-aside determination and to determine if any domestic products are available in the market. Any business, regardless of business size, who believe they possess the experience and qualifications to successfully perform the scope of work described below, can respond to this source sought notice. Responses are being sought from qualified businesses capable of providing the required equipment and services outlined below. The anticipated North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for the work 339112 Surgical and Medical Instrument Manufacturing, with a small business size standard of 1,000 Employees. See below for summary of the project scope. The Department of Veterans Affairs - Network Contracting Office 17 is seeking to procure Video Laryngoscopy Equipment, including pocket monitors, C-MAC monitors, S-Imagers, single use laryngoscope blades, connection cables, portable power components, and the video intubation platform cart. This equipment is intended for use by the Olin E. Teague Veterans’ Medical Center, Station 674 (CTVHCS), Medical Services Department, located in Temple, Texas. Please refer to the attached document for additional details.

# Avantus Security Escort Sole Source

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-31 15:20:33.571-04
- Solicitation Number: FA240126C0012
- Awardee: AVANTUS FEDERAL LLC Mc Lean VA 22102 USA
- Award Amount: 7020945.57

## Summary

SDA awarded a sole source labor hour contract to Avantus Federal, LLC for Security Escort Services, to urgently accomodate an expansion of projects. These Security Escorts will provide cleared security escort services for SDA operations facilities and construction projects at SDA's locations including, but not limited to, the Pentagon, Grand Forks Air Force Base, and Redstone Arsenal. Avantus is the incumbent for SETA Enterprise Services and is currently providing these services to SDA and will provide necessary continuity of operations and experience, as their personnel hold key positions that are intimately involved in maintaining construction projects and operating centers across CONUS.

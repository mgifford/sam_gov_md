# K9 Detection (Role Player) Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 08:10:44.387-04
- Solicitation Number: HQ003426QE077

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide 4–6 role players per training session, 4 sessions per week, to support K-9 training scenarios in both indoor and outdoor settings. Role players will simulate bystanders, individuals carrying scent aids, and passersby in high-foot-traffic simulations.

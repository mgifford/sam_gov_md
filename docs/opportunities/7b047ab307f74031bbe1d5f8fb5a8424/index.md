# Monongahela NF Potomac Pavement Preservation Project

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 15:10:00.318-04

## Summary

Description This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12445526Q0033 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the Monongahela National Forest Potomac Pavement Preservation Project. The Monongahela National Forest has a need for road repairs in the Potomac Ranger District. Different levels of asphalt repair are required and are described in the project plans. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 237310 Highway, Street, and Bridge Construction. The small business size standard is $45M. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. There is no planned site visit for this solicitation. Potential bidders may visit the site at their convenience. Questions regarding this solicitation must be submitted no later than March 9, 2026 by 2 pm Eastern Time. This will ensure enough time to respond before the solicitation period ends. Please include the solicitation name and number as the subject line of the email.

# Vertical Roll Out Sheet Storage Racks

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 15:31:45.035-04
- Solicitation Number: 1333ND26QNB130175

## Summary

The Government hereby posts all the questions received and responses to questions dated April 21, 2026. The RFQ due date is being extend until 11:00am, April 24, 2026. The reason for the extension is additional question have been received about the specfications and responses, will be posted on Tuesday, April 21, 2026. The Government hereby adds a response to questions recevied. The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Fabrication Technology Group requires a roll out sheet rack to store and manage sheet metal materials in a laboratory environment. The rack will support fabrication processes by providing efficient, safe, and accessible storage for sheet materials, particularly those with dimensions up to 52" x 48". The system must optimize space, ensure ease of access. The system shall be installed by the Contractor. To acquire two roll out vertical sheet racks to store sheet metal materials efficiently and improve accessibility in the NIST Fabrication Technology Group’s Metal Storeroom.

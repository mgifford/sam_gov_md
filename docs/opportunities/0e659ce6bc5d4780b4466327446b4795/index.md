# Master Musician Coordinator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 10:03:29.379-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV-26-Q-A0023

## Summary

The 409th CSB, Theater Contracting Center is publishing this solicitation for a Master Musician Coordinator. Please see attched Performance Work Statement (PWS). The Contractor shall provide Master Musician Coordination services to the USAG Rheinland-Pfalz Religious Support Office (RSO), serving both the Baumholder Military Community (BMC) and the Kaiserslautern Military Community (KMC). This is a non-personal services contract between the USAG Rheinland-Pfalz RSO and the Contractor to perform tasks and provide deliverables identified in the PWS. The Contractor shall provide musicians to play appropriate preludes, interludes, offertories and postludes as well as accompany congregational singing in support of worship services and other special events. The Contractor shall also provide musicians to rehearse, in advance of the service, planned selections with choirs, soloists, groups or ensembles that require accompaniment and will be performing at chapel services or special events. All vendors who have questions, comments or are interested in providing the equipment specified in the attachments should submit quotes to: Mrs. Kelsey Males Email: kelsey.j.males.civ@army.mil Mr. Brandon Donalson Email: brandon.m.donalson.civ@army.mil Please include the title "Master Musician Coordinator" in the subject line of the request, and include the following information: Company name and address Cage Code Company business size by NAICS code Web Page URL Point of contact for questions/clarifications Name Telephone number email address The Government will not return any information submitted in response to this notice. All questions must be in writing. In all responses, please reference Solicitation W564KV26QA023. Questions for this solictation shall be submitted no later than 7 days prior to solicitation clost date.

# Mobile and Transportable Microreactor System

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 16:18:39.013-04
- Solicitation Number: 70RSAT26R00000003

## Summary

AMENDMENT 0002: Effective the date of this notice, this solicitation is cancelled. No awards will be made for the design and development of transportable and mobile microreactor systems under this solicitation. The Department would like to express its sincere appreciation to the offerors who participated in this solicitation. Thank you. AMENDMENT 0001: The purpose of this amendment is to provide responses to offeror-submitted questions and comments. Questions, comments, and government responses are included in the file named "70RSAT26R00000003 - Q and C Responses - 20251231.pdf" in the Attachments/Link section. Offerors are reminded of this solicitation's closing date of 4:00 PM Eastern Standard Time on Friday, January 9, 2026. BASE NOTICE: This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 70RSAT26R00000003 is issued as a request for proposal (RFP) for mobile and transportable microreactor system development, testing, and deployment. This acquisition is a full and open competition. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. A Federal Priorities and Allocations System (FPAS) assigned rating may be included at a future date. Questions related to this solicitation must be received by the Government by or before 4:00 PM Eastern Standard Time on Tuesday, December 30, 2025. Please refer to the attachments to this notice for full details regarding this opportunity.

# Emulsion and Sealcoat Products IDIQ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:27:42.401-04
- Solicitation Number: FA500026Q0015

## Summary

AMENDMENT 0001: Please see attached amendment for updates to the RFQ close date, SOW and Solicitation document. This solicitation is for commercial products prepared IAW RFO Part 12, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. The 773rd CES requires a commodities indefinite-delivery, indefinite-quantity (IDIQ) contract for streamlined procurement of asphalt emulsion and seal coat products to complete facility and roadway projects on JBER, AK. The anticipated ordering period is 1 May 2026 through 30 April 2031. This requirement includes a brand name purchase for both GSB-78 and GSB-88 (ref: Brand Name Justification). These products can not be ordered and shipped, as they are mixed, kept in specialized containers and used immediately when needed. Furthermore, the CSS-1 product has to be a minimum temperature when acquired by 773 CES so the product flows and sprays properly. JBER does not currently have the means or capacity to store and heat any products on a continuous bases, apart from the small volume contained in the HAZMAT Gov tanker. Please reference page 18 of the Solicitation for further instructions to offerors and evaluation criteria. All questions can be addressed to the CO, Christina Shorkey at Christina.Shorkey.2@us.af.mil

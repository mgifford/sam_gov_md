## Summary

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Region 5 is conducting market research to identify qualified businesses capable of providing environmental laboratory analytical services (Tier III) to support the Superfund Program in the states of Wisconsin, Illinois, Ohio, Michigan, Indiana and Minnesota. This notice is not a solicitation and no contract will be awarded from this announcement.

# V225--Ground Ambulance Services Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ); Base Year Plus Four One Year Ordering Periods

## Summary

SYNOPSIS DESCRIPTION: The Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office (NCO) 19 Rocky Mountain Acquisition Center, 6162 S. Willow Drive, Suite 300, Greenwood Village CO 80111 intends to solicit sources to enter into a firm-fixed price contract to provide Ground Ambulance Services Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) for the VA Eastern Colorado Health Care System on a base year plus four one (1) year ordering periods . The solicitation package will be available for download on or about May 19, 2025. The applicable NAICS code is 621910 Ambulance Services with a small business size standard of $22.5 Million. This solicitation is a 100% SDVOSB Set-Aside. The solicitation reference is 36C25925R0068 Ground Ambulance Services VA Eastern Colorado Health Care System and should be referenced in all correspondence/ inquiries. All information for this solicitation will be posted on Contract Opportunities at https://www.sam.gov/. No hard copies of the solicitation will be provided. Any amendments to this solicitation will be available at this website and offerors are advised that they are responsible for obtaining amendments to this solicitation at this website prior to the time of responding to the solicitation. This is the pre-solicitation notice only.

# EDAT Technology Roadshow Tampa (on behalf of Kentro)

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-01 09:50:07.81-04

## Summary

Kentro, on behalf of USSOCOM, is interested in soliciting presentations from Industry Partners (IPs) to showcase their information technology (IT) capabilities they feel will bring value to the Government. See attachment for more detail. Presenters strongly encourages the use of product/solution demonstrations over static capability PowerPoint presentations. Show us how your product will be used in the unique SOF environment but be prepared to answer detailed questions about how it works and to present back-up information after demonstrating your product/solution.

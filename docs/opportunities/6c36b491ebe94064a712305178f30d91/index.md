## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Veterans Health Administration, Network Contracting Office (NCO 2), Buffalo, New York intends to solicit a request for quotes (RFQ) from contractors to provide Laser Marking, Commissioning, and Data Optimization at the NY Harbor Healthcare System, Manhattan campus as defined in the Statement of Work (SOW) of the solicitation under a firm-fixed priced contract. The VA anticipates issuing an RFQ for this requirement on or about March 10, 2026 per the requirement in RFO 5.101(c) directly to sources identified during market research IAW RFO 12-201-1(c). NAICS Code is 811210 Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance with a Small Business Size Standard of $34.0 Million. The classification code is DA10 IT and Telecom Business Application/Application Development Software As A Service. Period of Performance: Anticipated April 1, 2026 September 30, 2026. Questions concerning this announcement should be directed to the Contract Specialist using the email link provided below. Telephone requests for information or questions will NOT be accepted.

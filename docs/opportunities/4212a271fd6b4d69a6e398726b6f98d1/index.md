# Contract Modification for Acoustic Device Countermeasure (ADC) MK 5

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 10:48:58.807-04
- Solicitation Number: N0002418C6209MOD

## Summary

The Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA) intends to award a contract modification to Leidos Holdings, Inc. under Contract No. N00024-18-C-6209. The modification will be issued using other than full and open competition pursuant to 10 U.S.C. § 3204(a)(1), as implemented by Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.302-1. The Acoustic Device Countermeasure (ADC) MK 5 is an expendable, mobile acoustic countermeasure designed to increase a submarine’s survivability against torpedo threats. Leidos is the original developer of the ADC MK 5 system, and the Government does not currently possess a complete Technical Data Package (TDP) with build-to-print specifications. This action is required to procure additional Low-Rate Initial Production (LRIP) units and associated engineering support to meet critical fleet deployment and testing schedules. Leidos is determined to be the only available source capable of fulfilling these requirements without causing unacceptable schedule delays while the TDP is finalized for future full and open competition. Interested parties may express their interest and capability in response to this notice. Parties may submit a capability statement detailing their technical expertise and ability to fulfill the requirements, which will be considered by NAVSEA. Responses shall be submitted via email to christopher.t.venturino.civ@us.navy.mil and cc jennifer.l.kramer26civ@us.navy.mil and reference the solicitation number. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this synopsis or any follow-up information requests.

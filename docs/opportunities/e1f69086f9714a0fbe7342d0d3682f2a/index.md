# Leasing of Personal Protection Equipment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 08:32:52.153-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV26QA024

## Summary

The 409th CSB, Theater Contracting Center is publishing this solicitation for a Leasing of Personal Protection Equipment. Please see attached Performance Work Statement (PWS). The Contractor shall provide Leasing of Personal Protection Equipment. This is a non-personal services contract to perform tasks and provide deliverables identified in the PWS. The Contractor shall provide leasing of personal protective equipment in the form of personal protective clothing with cabinet compartment. All vendors who have questions, comments or are interested in providing the equipment specified in the attachments should submit quotes to: Mr. Dion Benton Email: dion.d.benton.civ@army.mil Mrs. Judy Biboum Email: brandon.m.donalson.civ@army.mil Please include the title "Leasing of Personal Protection Equipment" in the subject line of the request, and include the following information: Company name and address Cage Code Company business size by NAICS code Web Page URL Point of contact for questions/clarifications Name Telephone number email address The Government will not return any information submitted in response to this notice. All questions must be in writing. In all responses, please reference Solicitation W564KV26QA024. Questions for this solicitation shall be submitted no later than 5 days prior to solicitation close date.

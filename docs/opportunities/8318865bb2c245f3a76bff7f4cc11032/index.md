# Various Construction Materials for Shower, Shave, Latrine (SSL) Facility

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 15:48:12.283-04
- Solicitation Number: FA489726QC026

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial supplies prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will NOT be issued. The 366th Contracting Squadron, Mountain Home AFB, ID is soliciting a requirement for various materials to construct a shower, shave, latrine facility in accordance with the attachments. This procurement will be conducted as a 100% Small Business set-aside. Award will be made to the responsible Offeror whose offer conforms to all solicitation requirements, such as terms and conditions, representations and certifications, and technical requirements, Please refer to ALL the attached documents for complete specifications and details. Questions must be submitted via email by or before 11:00 AM MDT, 11 March 2026. Quotes are due by 11:00 AM MDT, 20 March 2026.

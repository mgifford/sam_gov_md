# Solicitation - U.S. Mission Accra, Ghana

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-04-22 14:30:07.038-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AQMM26R0141

## Summary

*** DUE TO THE FISCAL YEAR ROLLOVER, THE PREVIOUS PRESOLICITATION #19AQMM25R0295 HAS CHANGED TO THE CURRENT #19AQMM26R0141 **** The U.S. Department of State (DOS) has a requirement for a qualified Contractor to provide Local Guard Services to support the U.S. Mission in Accra, Ghana. The Government requires a secure environment to conduct their mission. The Contractor shall provide the organizational structure, management, and qualified staff at levels adequate to meet or exceed the requirement contained in the Performance Work Statement. The Contractor shall be required to provide services in a manner that prevent loss or injury to U.S. personnel, dependents, property; destruction of assets; to prevent unauthorized access; and deter potential terrorist attacks. Anticipated period of performance of the contract is one base year and four one-year options (to be exercised at the sole discretion of the Government). An in-person Site Visit will be conducted on 16 March 2026 (see Section L.1 of the solicitation). Please fill out the Restricted Access Request form (if you have not done so already). Once offerors have emailed their Restricted Access Request forms to the Contracting Officer, offerors are required to hit the "request access" button in SAM.gov in order to view the solicitation and all related exhibits. --------------------------------------------------------------------------- Amendment A001 - The purpose of this amendment is to: - Respond to the Site Visit questions posted by potential offerors. - Update the VAT to 20% in Section B.7.5 - Incorporate the updated Exhibits D, E, F, M, P, Q and S. - Incorporate Section L.11.1.8, US Person Certification.

# Household Cleaning Services - Hamilton, Bermuda

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 12:05:34.412-04
- Solicitation Number: 19UK5626Q0021

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy – London requires household cleaning services for the US Consulate General in Hamilton, Bermuda. The anticipated period of performance is one year from the date of award. Only companies specializing in this field and located in Bermuda should express interest in this opportunity. The Government may award the contract based on the initial offer without discussion. The resultant award will be on a firm fixed price purchase order. Please see the attached Statement of Work for additional details, information regarding a site visit and instructions for submitting an offer. Electronic Submissions will be accepted. Please submit your offer prior to the response date and time.

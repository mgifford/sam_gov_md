# Preventive Maintenance and unlimited repair agreement for a Thermo Fisher Q Exactive mass spectrometer, Base + 2 Option years

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-13 14:52:55.93-04
- Solicitation Number: 75F40126SSN133878

## Summary

See attached Sources Sought Notice for a Preventive Maintenance and unlimited repair agreement for a Thermo Fisher Q Exactive mass spectrometer, plus the instrument-controlling computer and software programs (Xcalibur and DCMSlink for Xcalibur) for a base year plus two (2) possible one-year option years. The instrument was under a maintenance agreement that expired on December 8, 2025; and since then, the Instrument’s turbo-molecular pump went out; therefore, the instrument will require a pre-inspection and repairs to bring the instrument to operational condition prior to the new service contract becoming active. Once the mass spec system has been repaired, the vendor/service provider shall maintain the mass spectrometer system (including vacuum pumps, computer, and other accessories) in a functional state for the periods of performance outlined in the SSN.

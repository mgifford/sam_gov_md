- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 12:39:38.708-04

## Summary

The Department of the Air Force, Joint Base Charleston, is issuing this Request for Quote (RFQ) to procure one (1) new Industrial Can Densifier in support of the 437th Aircraft Maintenance Squadron (437 AMXS). Offerors must submit technical literature (e.g., product brochures, specification sheets, or detailed product descriptions) that clearly demonstrates the quoted product meets or exceeds all minimum requirements outlined in Attachment 1 - Salient Characteristics. Please refer to FAR 52.212-1 and FAR 52.212-2 within the attached SF 1449 for complete submission instructions and the specific evaluation criteria that will be used to determine the best value to the Government.

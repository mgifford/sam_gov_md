# Repair and Renovate Dorm 742

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 13:44:06.663-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4626-26-R-0009

## Summary

The contractor shall perform all services and provide all labor, materials, and equipment necessary to complete work. The scope of work is primarily interior focused and includes demolition; replacement of furnishings, architectural finishes, and reconfiguration of interior spaces including widening doorways. Exterior work includes window replacement and repair or replacement of damaged sidewalks. The project also includes structural modifications, as well as mechanical, electrical plumbing, and fire protection work. The Government will conduct a site visit as follows: Date/Time: 28 April 2026 at 10:00 AM MDT Location: 341 Contracting Squadron 7015 Goddard Drive, Great Falls, MT 59402, Bldg 145, Rm 305 Proposals must be recieved no later than 18 May 2026 at 2:00 PM MDT.

# Hood Certification and Testing

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 12:53:13.866-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C26026Q0418

## Summary

The Portland VA Medical Center requires testing, balancing and certification of biological safety cabinets, chemical fume hoods, laminar flow clean benches, and compounding rooms. The Work is located at the Portland VA Medical Center, 3710 S.W. Veterans Hospital Road, Portland Oregon 97239 and Vancouver VA Medical Center, 1601 East 4th Plan Boulevard, Vancouver, WA 98661 The Contractor shall be NSF-49 accredited with a minimum of 18 months BSC testing experience. The Contractor must have a maximum response time of 24 hours for repairs. The Contractor shall follow Federal and State OSHA regulations and take all reasonable safety precautions necessary (i.e., Lockout/Tag-out) to protect persons and property from injury or damage to personal property.

## Summary

This Sources Sought Notice (SS) is solely for information and planning purposes only regarding vendors who are capable of providing Western Shelter Systems LLC, Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) manufactured items listed in the “Deliverables and Services” section below and shall not be construed as a solicitation or as an obligation on the part of the USDA Forest Service. The purpose of this SS is to identify qualified sources capable of providing items described herein. The Government will not pay for, nor reimburse, any expenses incurred in responding to this SS. The NIFC does not intend to notify respondents of the evaluation and will not share information made available; please ensure any sensitive or protected information submitted is marked as such.

# INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE - FTTS-F16483-FK-USAF Force Tec Kits

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-19 03:35:22.015-04

## Summary

The Government intends to award this requirement for two (2) Force Tec Kits (FTTS-F16483-FK-USAF) on a sole source basis. This kit is comprehensive, military-grade tool kit, purpose-engineered for use by the United States Air Force, to support repair, maintenance, assembly, and repair operations in the field, hanger and depot environments.

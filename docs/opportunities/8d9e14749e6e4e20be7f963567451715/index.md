# Readiness Support Services for Commander, Naval Surface Group Central, Bahrain

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 11:11:24.371-04
- Solicitation Number: N4033926RS001

## Summary

THIS REQUIREMENT IS ONLY FOR SEAPORT MAC HOLDERS TO SUBMIT A PROPOSAL. Naval Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) Fleet Logistics Center (FLC) Bahrain intends to award a firm fixed price with cost-reimbursable CLINs for travel service task order from SeaPort NxG for engineering, technical support, readiness support, training support and financial services in support of Commander, Naval Surface Force, Atlantic (COMNAVSURFLANT). COMNAVSURFLANT directly supports the material readiness of 4 surface ships and up to 10 deployed ships by providing training, management and engineering support services. This update is to inform all concerned parties of the clause being updated and reflected in the solicitation. Please be advised that due to current events the revised deadline for questions is 20 March 206, and the proposal submission deadline is 27 April 2026 (EST).

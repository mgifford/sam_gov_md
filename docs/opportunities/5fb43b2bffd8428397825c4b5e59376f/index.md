## Summary

Notice of Intent to Award a BPA Call Order against The Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) BPA Issue with link to LMIR BPA. Active link below: Original documents pertaining to the LMIR Blanket Purchase Agreement can be found in the attachments located at https://sam.gov/workspace/contract/opp/8f26188b0e7445b3b4f9bb71484448f4/view "Attachments/Links." BPA Call Order Exceeding $20,000 Synopsis Requisition 1164750. The Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) BPA, 12363N23Q4023, is a National BPA that consists of a large variety of work including Professional Services, Natural Resources Restoration, Engineering, Project Management, NEPA, Communications, and more. The BPA can be utilized on all Forests and Grasslands and is available for all federal agencies to utilize. The USDA Forest Service (USFS) intends on awarding a competitive BPA Call Order against the LMIR BPA for 127EAV26Q0035 – Spence Basin Heritage Survey Prescott National Forest. This is not a solicitation. This synopsis notification of intent to award a BPA Call order does not constitute a request for proposals or submission of any information in response to this notification. This procurement is restricted to those approved companies eligible for call orders under the Land Management Integrated Resources BPA (LMIR) for USFS. The LMIR BPA has continuous pre-solicitation sharing so that contractors may submit a proposal to join the BPA at any point in time while the BPA is active. If your firm would like to join, please contact the LMIR BPA Team at SM.FS.lmirbpa@usda.gov. Original documents pertaining to the LMIR Blanket Purchase Agreement can be found in the attachments located at https://www.fs.usda.gov/business/lmir/?tab=business/lmir/ under "Attachments/Links." Please send any questions to shavera.leveille@usda.gov no later than 1700 MST, April 7, 2026.

# Fully Integrated ScriptPro Medication Delivery System

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 14:00:18.709-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25726Q0290

## Summary

The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, quality control and other items and non-personal services as required for installation and support of Brand Name Only Scriptpro fully integrated medication delivery system, as indicated in the attached Statement of Work. The Contractor must provide all labor, material, tools, management, travel, software, and equipment necessary to supply, install and provide support services for complete installation of fully integrated medication delivery system which includes a Robotic Prescription Dispensing System, Workstations, Enterprise Workflow Management Platform and Software, Custom Fixtures, and Support Services to support the Outpatient Satellite Pharmacy in the Emergency Department at Dallas VAMC 4500 S Lancaster Rd Dallas, TX 75216. The government makes no representation or guarantees as to the condition of equipment on the start date of the contract.

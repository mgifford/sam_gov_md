# Ultra Purified Water Service AMLK/SEA

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 15:22:07.977-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C26026Q0467

## Summary

Multiple purified water systems at Seattle and American Lake medical campuses must be maintained properly and on time for ST-108 compliance. This contract ensures the operational integrity of critical water treatment and humidification systems at both Seattle and American Lake medical campuses through scheduled maintenance visits (monthly or quarterly, as specified). All tasks aim to maintain system reliability, efficiency, and compliance with operational standards.

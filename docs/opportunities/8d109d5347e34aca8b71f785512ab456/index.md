# Request For Information - Basic Expeditionary Airfield Resources (BEAR) Water System Freeze Protection

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-08 10:03:23.623-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8534-BEARWATER

## Summary

This Request For Information (RFI) is being utilized to identify sources capable of providing expertise, capabilities, and exprience to meet the requirement of a Water Freeze Protection System as outlined within section 3.3 of the draft Purchase Description attached. The Government seeks to provide the attached Purchase Description and prospective contractor's capability survey to identify and understand commercial and commercial-off-the-shelf capabilites, concepts, or methodologies to sustain water and wastewater operational flow in frigid temperatures (-25 degrees Fahrenheit and extended protection down to -65 degrees Fahrenheit). The contract type and acquisition strategy, to include pricing arrangement, quantities, timelines, et al., are considered to be determined and will be shaped in part by the findings found within responses to this notice as well as other sources of potential market solutions. PLEASE NOTE: This notice is NOT a solicitation. Contractors/Institutions responding to this market research are placed on notice that participation in this survey may not ensure participation in future solicitations or contract awards. This request does not constitute a request for proposal and is for market research purposes only. The Government will not reimburse participants for any expenses associated with their participation in this survey.

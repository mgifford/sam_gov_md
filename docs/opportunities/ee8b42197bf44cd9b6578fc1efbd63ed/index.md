Statement of Need CT Scanner Contrast Power Injection System Radiology Department Altoona VA Medical Center Summary The Stellant Flex system is the current system being used in the Radiology CT scanner room at Altoona VA Medical Center (VAMC). The current system has reached end of life and Altoona VAMC requires a new system with the newest technology. Project Scope The contractor shall provide delivery of a complete Power Injection System and all related Installation to make system fully ready for patient use in accordance with Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) specifications. The CT Scanner Contrast Power Injection System shall at a minimum meet all the following salient characteristics. The Stellant FLEX- Medrad Stellant FLEX Injection System is a Name Brand Product provided for reference of salient characteristics only. Salient Characteristics Power injectors Designed for complex contrast enhanced CT protocols Creates a tight contrast bolus Enables precisely timed contrast delivery Provides more uniform attenuation of the right and left ventricles Minimizes artifacts by achieving proper attenuation levels Visualize the right coronary arteries and right ventricles in a single study by achieving more uniform attenuation Informatics ready platform enables interconnectivity between your CT scanner, power injector, PACS, RIS, & speech recognition systems Storage and recall of up to 250 protocols Enhanced protocol manager Snap-on/twist-off syringe design Automatic plunger advance and retract when attaching and detaching syringes Automatic filling and priming Multi-phase programming (with hold/pause feature) Integrated saline test inject Programmable pressure limit On-board eGFR and weight-based dosing Injection enabled when head is tilted down Automated data capture Interface with PACS/RIS/SR Export of a DICOM secondary capture to PACS Delivery/Installation Shall be coordinated upon award and at least 5 business days prior to shipment

# High-Performance Compute System Administration Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-21 13:09:50.269-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926HPE

## Summary

The Fleet Logistics Center Norfolk (FLCN), Norfolk, Virginia is seeking information on qualified sources for planning purposes in an effort to derive one contract to provide on-site system administration support services in support of NAVOCEANO’s high performance computing infrastructure in accordance with the draft Performance Work Statement (PWS). The anticipated Period of Performance for an eventual contract, if awarded, is twelve (12) months after the date of award. THIS SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE IS FOR MARKET RESEARCH AND PLANNING PURPOSES ONLY. IT DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A SOLICITATION AND SHALL NOT BE CONSTRUED AS A COMMITMENT BY THE GOVERNMENT TO AWARD A CONTRACT FROM RESPONSES TO THIS ANNOUNCEMENT. ANY INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY INTERESTED PARTIES IS STRICTLY VOLUNTARY AND NO MONETARY COMPENSATION WILL BE PROVIDED FOR RESPONSE PREPARATION.

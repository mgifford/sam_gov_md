# Solicitation_Firing Mechanism

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 09:35:11.96-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CH-25-R-0139

## Summary

***AMD 0003 released on 17 APR 2026 and updates administrative changes.*** ***AMD 0002 released on 17 APR 2026 and extends proposal due date to 12 MAY 2026.*** ***AMD 0001 released on 2 APR 2026 and extends proposal due date to 21 APR 2026.*** The United States Army Contracting Command - Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) intends to award a One-time Buy with 100% Option, Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) contract for Firing Mechanism, National Stock Number (NSN): 1010-01-679-5089, Part Number (PN): 12901960. This solicitation is intended to result in a Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) Contract for a total quantity of 498 EA with 100% option for NSN: 1010-01-679-5089. This solicitation is 100% set aside for Small Businesses. There is a Technical Data Package (TDP) associated with this procurement. The TDP is EXPORT CONTROLLED, Distribution D. You must request access to the TDP via SAM.gov and have JCP Certification. You must be certified under the United States/Canada Joint Certification Program (JCP) (https://www.dla.mil/HQ/LogisticsOperations/Services/JCP/) to be granted access to the TDP for this solicitation. CAUTION: This solicitation will be issued electronically as will any amendments thereto. Because of this, the Government is under no obligation and is, in fact, unable to maintain a mailing list. The burden is therefore on any interested parties to periodically access the above internet address in order to obtain any amendments which may be issued. Failure to obtain any amendments and to respond to them prior to the date and time set for receipt of proposals may render your proposal nonresponsive and result in the rejection of the proposal. All questions pertaining to this solicitation shall be directed via email to Contract Specialist, Kaley Weeden, at kaley.h.weeden.civ@army.mil.

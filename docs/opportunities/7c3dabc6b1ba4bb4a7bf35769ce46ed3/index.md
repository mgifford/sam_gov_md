# INL/El Salvador Single Purpose Green Canines

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 10:29:07.789-04
- Solicitation Number: 191NLE26R0066

## Summary

The U.S. Department of State, Bureau of International Narcotics and Law Enforcement Affairs (INL) San Salvador, is soliciting proposals for the procurement and delivery of ten (10) green dogs. The selected canines will be donated to the Government of El Salvador Canine Program for law enforcement purposes. Evaluation Method: Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) Important Notice: Vendors must read the solicitation and Statement of Work (SOW) in their entirety and ensure all submission requirements are met. Vendors will not have the opportunity to revise submissions that fail to meet submission requirements. For complete submission guidance, technical specifications, and CLIN structure details, refer to solicitation document 191NLE26R0066 and the SOW, specifically: Section 1.6.2: Format for Technical and Cost/Price Quotation Section 1.6.3: Detailed Submission Requirements

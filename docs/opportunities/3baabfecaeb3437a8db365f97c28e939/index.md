# Golf Cart Rental

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-31 11:40:43.145-04
- Solicitation Number: FA302226Q0312

## Summary

This Statement of Work (SOW) defines the scope of work to provide rental golf carts for the 2026 Columbus AFB America 250 celebration for the 4th July. The contractor shall provide all materials, equipment, management, coordination, and transportation required to accomplish this work. 2. Requirements a. All carts will be gas powered. Carts will be delivered on 3 July 2026. b. Contractor will provide 18 two passenger golf carts and 2 utility bed carts by 1000L on 3 July 2026. c. Carts will be delivered to the parking lot northwest of the bowling center (building 736) on Columbus Air Force Base, Mississippi. (Columbus Bowling Center, Columbus MS 39710) See attachment 1 for map. d. All provided carts will be fully fueled or as close to fully fueled as possible when delivered to Columbus Air Force Base. e. Contractor will pick up golf carts from the hours of 1000 to1400 PM CST on 6 July 2026. f. Total number of carts needed is 20. g. Golf carts will be driven by Freedom Fest staff to allow for rapid transportation to areas of need, transportation of handicapped individuals, and medical staff transportation.

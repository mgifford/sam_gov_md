## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE ONLY. This notice does NOT constitute a request for proposals, request for quote, or invitation for bid. No formal solicitation document exists at this time. We are seeking information for market research purposes only. The Government will not pay for any information solicited. INTRODUCTION: The Salisbury VA Medical Center/Health Care System (SVAHCS) has a new requirement for Large Bone Power Drill Set. This requirement is needed for Salisbury VA Medical Center (VAMC), Kernersville Health Care Center (HCC), and Charlotte Health Care Center (HCC). The VHA Network Contracting Office 6 (NCO 6) is conducting a market survey to find qualified vendors who can provide the following item: SALIENT CHARACTERISTICS: Large Bone Drills Must be Orthopedic rotary handpiece with dual trigger. The dual trigger rotary drill must be able to drill, ream, saw, and burr while delivering the torque necessary for heavy-duty reaming applications. Must have collet mechanism twist-lock collet ring that allows user to rotate left or right to quickly unlock, remove and snap in new attachment. Handpiece must have hermetically sealed components Must have as attachments: Dual trigger wire collet, small, 0.6-1.8mm Dual trigger pin collet, medium 2.0-3.2mm Small AO drill attachment ¼ drill chuck with key Hudson modified trinkle drill attachment Hudson modified trinkle reamer attachment ¼ reamer chuck with key Micro sagittal saw attachment Large lithium-ion battery Small lithium-ion battery System must use lithium-ion battery packs

13.2 V that can be sterilized before surgery. Must include lithium-ion battery charger with small footprint and rapid battery processing. Battery charger must meet dimensions. Width 16 Height 4 Depth

6.5 Weight 4 lbs. Must be able to charge 4 batteries at a time. Must have clear confirmation of charging level Dimensions height 15.0 cm, width 2.75 cm, depth 11.8 cm Mass 710 gm as single drill handpiece without battery Must have reaming torque of 153 in-lbs Must have drill speed of 1500 RPM Must have protection ratings of: IPX6, IPX8, IPX9 for cleaning and sterilization purposes Must have all characteristics in single drill handpiece Must include container sterilization. OBJECTIVE: Enable fast, secure attachment changes using the drill s proprietary twist collet ring mechanism, which allows left/right rotation to lock/unlock attachments for quick removal and re connection. Standardize powered instrumentation with

13.2 V sterilizable lithium ion battery packs for consistent power delivery and workflow flexibility (small and large battery options).

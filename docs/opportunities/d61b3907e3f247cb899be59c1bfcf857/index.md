# Air Advisor Academic Support Services at JB-MDL NJ Solicitation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 09:50:30.421-04
- Solicitation Number: FA448426R0010

## Summary

The 87th Contracting Squadron has a requirement for Air Advisory Academic Services on behalf of the Expeditionary Center (EC). The contractor shall furnish on-site academic instructors, registrar management, and training support services to enhance the EC’s education and training mission across multiple AAA courses, including the Air Advisor Initial Qualification Course, Air Advisor Senior Leader Course, and the Air Advisor Mission Commander/Team Sergeant Qualification Course. These courses vary in length and intensity depending on student rank and prior experience, and are designed to prepare personnel to engage effectively with partner nation counterparts in support of Combatant Commands (CCMDs) and other mission areas focused on security cooperation, assistance, and force enhancement. Instruction will cover topics such as foreign internal defense (FID), counterinsurgency (COIN), irregular warfare (IW), and building partnership capacity. Delivery periods for individual task orders will be responsive to evolving real-world demands and operational schedules. The acquisition is being solicited as an SDVOSB set-aside utilizing the SAM.gov platform. Offerors should submit all questions or comments relative to the solciitation electronically only. Questions are due 9 Apr 2026 3PM EST Proposals are due 30 Apr 2026 3PM EST

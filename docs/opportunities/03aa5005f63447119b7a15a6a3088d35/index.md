## Summary

Please note that Two Questions were recieved. Question: 1. What the biggest challenge with the Autopacker machines? Answer: Consistency with the bags. Consistent quality/manufacturing is important to avoid technical difficulties such as bags jamming and/or tearing at the side or bottom seams. These issues create downtime with the machine and directly affect prescription production. Question: 2. Can samples of the current bags be given out? Answer: Unfortunately, we are unable to provide samples of the current bag, as it contains proprietary information specific to the manufacturer. This is why we hosted the site visit-to allow you all to view the machine and ask questions. Samples and Quotes are Due 4/15/2026 at 4pm as noted in the RFQ Thank you!

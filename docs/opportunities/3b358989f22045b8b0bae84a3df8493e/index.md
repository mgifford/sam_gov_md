# J2 Physical Security Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 00:19:57.838-04
- Solicitation Number: W91QEX-26-R-A-0001

## Summary

This contractor shall provide security service support to USSOUTHCOM’s collateral and SCI security programs, including but not limited to administrative, personnel, physical, information, and industrial security functions within the headquarters (HQ) and throughout USSOUTHCOM’s area of responsibility (AOR) which includes Central and South America, the Caribbean and the adjacent waters of the Pacific and Atlantic Oceans and the Gulf of Mexico. These programs are designed to protect the command’s people, information, property and operations from acts of espionage, sabotage, terrorism, workplace violence, and other criminal acts; as well as, acts of negligence affecting the command’s security posture. Please see Performance Work Statement for specific details. The Contractor shall submit all questions and concerns in writing via email to Contract Specialist, Cristina Tellechea at cristina.a.tellechea.civ@mail.mil and Contracting Officer, Sherwin Riley, Sherwin.d.riley.civ@mail.mil no later than Friday, 24 April 2026 at 6:00 p.m. (EST). The email shall be entitled "Questions for Solicitation W91QEX-26-R-A001". The body of the email shall be numbered per question and contain questions in a clear and concise manner with specific reference to the page number and section of the solicitation, if applicable. No questions will be answered if received after 6:00 p.m. (EST) on 24 April 2026. Answers shall be provided as an amendment to the solicitation notice as issued via SAM website no later than Friday, 1 May 2026 by 5:00 p.m. (EST) and shall be structured as an all-inclusive question/answer document encompassing all of the Contractor's questions and the Government's responses to those questions.

## Summary

This is a notice of intent to award a sole-source contract pursuant to 10 U.S.C 2304 (C)(1), as implemented by FAR 6.103-1(b) and 6.103-1(d), only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements and brand-name descriptions. The Department of Veterans Affairs Providence Medical Center, 830 Chalkstone Avenue, Providence, RI requires a Transonic HD03 Hemodialysis Flow System. The Government intends to award a firm-fixed price sole source contract to Transonic Systems, Inc. for the following: Item No. Description Quantity HD03 HD03 Monitor 2 H4FX H4FX Sensor for the HD03 2 PMM Patient MEAS MOD w/o PAT ID IN 2 A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed contract based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quotes. This is a written notice to inform the public of the Government's intent to award on a sole source basis. Interested parties may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement. Any response to this notice must show clear and convincing evidence that competition will benefit the Government. Please contact Kim McCarthy, Contracting Specialist, with the responses or questions regarding this requirement at kim.mccarthy@va.gov. Please respond before April 7, 2026 11:00AM Eastern Standard Time (EST)

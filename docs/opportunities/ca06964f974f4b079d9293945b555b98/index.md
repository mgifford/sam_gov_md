# Inspection, Maintenance, and Repair of AC-9 Life Rafts

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-31 10:43:25.228-04
- Solicitation Number: FA282326Q3003

## Summary

The Air Force Test Center Operational Contracting Division (AFTC/PZIOA) Eglin AFB, FL intends to award a sole source contract to AIR CRUISERS COMPANY LLC, 269 E Main ST Liberty, Mississippi 39645-7250, United States, CAGE: 46RK2 to provide Inspection, Maintenance, and Repair of ten (10) AC-9 Life Rafts. The anticipated contract will be a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Commercial Purchase Order. The statutory authority which permits sole source for this acquisition is 41 United States Code (USC) 1901, as implemented by Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 12.102(a), revised by the Office of the Under Secretary of Defense for Acquisition and Sustainment Memorandum, 'Class Deviation – Revolutionary FAR Overhaul,' effective February 1, 2026. The applicable text for FAR Part 12 is located on the Revolutionary FAR Overhaul web page at https://www.acquisition.gov/far-overhaul/far-part-deviation-guide/far-overhaul-part-12. This authority is supported by an approved FAR Part 12 Single Source Justification. Competition is being limited due to: It is the Government's intent to proceed with this sole-source action. The applicable NAICS code is 326299. The size standard as defined by the U.S. Small Business Administration is 650 employees. The anticipated award date is 08 April 2026. CLIN 0001 Standard CMM recommended overhaul/inspection of AC-9 life rafts; Qty 10 CLIN 0002 Parts and Repair of AC-9 Rafts CLIN 0003 Shipping via FedEx Express THIS NOTICE OF INTENT IS NOT A REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS OR QUOTATIONS. No solicitation package will be issued on the Government Point of Entry. A copy of the solicitation can be obtained by contacting the Point of Contact listed below. Verbal responses are not acceptable and will not be considered. Questions shall be submitted electronically to Liudmila Stidam at liudmila.stidam@us.af.mil.

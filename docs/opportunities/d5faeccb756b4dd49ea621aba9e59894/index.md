# Modoc NF Veterinary Services - Sole Source Award

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-25 18:19:47.864-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAY26P0027

## Summary

Contract 127EAY26P0027 is a Sole Source award for Veterinary Services for the United States Forest Service, Double Devil Wild Horse and Burro Corrals, for animals brought in from Federal lands to the facility for short-term holding. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 541940. The purchase order was awarded to Modoc Veterinary Center in the amount of $165,000.00 for 12 months of on call Veterinary Services.

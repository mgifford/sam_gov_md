## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, Streamlined Procedures for Evaluation and Solicitation for Commercial Items as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement and the attached Request for Quotation FA527026Q0015 constitute the only solicitation being issued; the solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Regulation.

# Office Manager (OM) and Executive Assistance (EA) Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-13 08:31:23.477-04
- Solicitation Number: SPJA26-33

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide comprehensive support services in office management and services in executive assistance to SSP Senior Executive Service Leadership at SSP Headquarters. The Contractor shall provide qualified personnel, processes, and tools that meet SSP requirements to deliver timely and effective office management support and effective executive assistance support. This includes managing complex schedules, coordinating high-level meetings, executive conferences, and preparing detailed correspondence.

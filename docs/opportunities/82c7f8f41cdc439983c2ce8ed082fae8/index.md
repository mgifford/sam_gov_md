## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) seeks a qualified contractor to provide preventive maintenance, corrective maintenance, training, and related support services to maintain operational readiness of HRT mobility platforms and logistical support equipment in accordance with the Statement of Work (SOW). This is a small business set-aside with the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code anticipated for this requirement is: 336612 - Boat Building and small business size standard in millions of dollars for the selected NAICS is 1,000 employees.

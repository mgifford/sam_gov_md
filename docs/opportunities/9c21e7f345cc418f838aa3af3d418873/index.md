## Summary

This Source Sought Notice (SSN) is issued for market information and planning for future acquisition in accordance with (IAW) Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 15.201(e). Responses to notice 36C26226Q0630 will improve the understanding of the Governments requirement and industry capabilities. It will enhance the Government s ability to obtain quality supplies and allow for potential offerors to judge whether or how they can satisfy the Government s requirement. This is not a solicitation announcement. Responses to this notice cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. Information received shall be safeguarded adequately from unauthorized disclosure. The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Network Contracting Office 22 (NCO 22) is seeking sources that can provide Brand name or Equal to Motorola radios for VA Greater Los Angeles (GLA) Healthcare System. Reference attached SSN 36C26226Q0630, Statement of Work (SOW) for details. VA GLA will have a site visit on April 20, 2026, from 10:00 am to 11:00 am. Site visit point of contact is Robert.Dalley@va.gov, Mobile: (310)709-8338. Meeting place is Sepulveda Ambulatory Care Center, Police Station, 16111 Plummer Street, Sepulveda, CA 91343-2036. All interested parties are encouraged to attend. Inquiries shall be submitted via email to Anthony.delacruz@va.gov. Please Reference SSN 36C26226Q0630 in subject email. Thank you for your interest in fulfilling the Governments requirement.

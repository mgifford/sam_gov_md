# High Pressure Air Compressor, 18,000 hour overhaul

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 16:16:11.875-04

## Summary

OEM (Curtiss Wright) 18,000 Hour HPAC Overhaul NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound, in support of Military Sealift Command, intends to negotiate a SOLE SOURCE REQUIREMENT FOR THE OVERHAUL OF TWO OEM CURTISS WRIGHT N20NL-11C HIGH PRESSURE AIR COMPRESSORS. The anticipated posting date of the solicitation is on or about 15Apr2026 with a closing date on or about 22Apr2026. This requirement is being procured using RFO Part 12 Procedures. This solicitation and any subsequent amendments will be posted on the SAM.Gov Website: https://sam.gov/content/home/ Interested persons may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement or submit proposals prior to offer due date, which will normally be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. A determination not to compete this action based upon responses received to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Prospective offerors are responsible for downloading their own copy of the solicitation from this website and for frequently monitoring that site for any amendments. The Government is not responsible for any inability of the offeror to access the posted documents. Prospective offerors should register themselves on the SAM.Gov website. No telephone or FAX requests will be accepted, and no hard-copy solicitation/amendment will be mailed or FAXed. Offerors must also be registered in the System for Awards Management (SAM) in order to receive a Government Award in this procurement at https://sam.gov/content/home All questions regarding this solicitation should be submitted to J.

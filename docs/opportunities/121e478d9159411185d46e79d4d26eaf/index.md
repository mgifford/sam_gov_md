# Dry Ice for WBAMC

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 11:24:13.456-04

## Summary

This requirement is for dry ice for William Beaumont Army Medical Center (WBAMC), Fort Bliss, TX. The resultant contract (purchase order) will be a one-year base period plus four (4) one-year options, the periods of performance of which are: Base: 17 May 2026 through 16 May 2027 Option 1: 17 May 2027 through 16 April 2028 Option 2: 17 May 2028 through 16 April 2029 Option 3: 17 May 2029 through 16 April 2030 Option 4: 17 May 2030 through 16 April 2031

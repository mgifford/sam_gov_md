- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 15:26:25.315-04

## Summary

The U.S. Geological Survey Texas Water Science Center requires a modification to be completed on government owned Mercedes-Benz 2024 Sprinter Cargo van into a mobile water sampling laboratory. Delivery to Fort Worth TX is required by 5/29/2026. There are no option periods. Competitive Request for Quotations (RFQ) 140G0126Q0061 is a small business set aside under NAICS code is 811121 Automotive Body, Paint and Interior Repair and Maintenance. The size standard is $9 Million. The anticipated award date of the contract is 4/1/2026. A vendor must be registered at https://www.sam.gov to be awarded the purchase order. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which shall be considered by the agency. Quotations shall be submitted to Mindie Dixon at mindie_dixon@ios.doi.gov

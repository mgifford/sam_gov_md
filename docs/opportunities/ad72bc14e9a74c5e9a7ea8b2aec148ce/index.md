# JOFOC - Provision of Mobile Voice, Data & other Telecommunications Services

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-07 05:58:34.396-04
- Solicitation Number: 19WZ6026Q00002

## Summary

The purpose of this acquisition is to award on a sole-source basis a firm-fixed-price contract to MTN Eswatini to provide mobile voice, data, and related telecommunications services supporting the U.S. Embassy Mbabane at a total cost of $72,220.00.

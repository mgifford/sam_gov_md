# Dehumidifier Purchase

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 10:17:28.369-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124M26QA009

## Summary

This solicitation is for the purchase of 200 personal dehumidifier. Dehumidifiers shall meet or exceed the following specifications and salient characteristics which are similar or equal to a TABYIK model CS50-SR dehumidifier: 1. Usage: Units will be utilized in a standard military barracks room. 2. Portable: Units shall be compact in design with a minimal over-all size and footprint that meet the requirements as spelled out in this document. They must be easily relocatable with little or no effort. Casters are preferred; however, wheels are acceptable. 3. Physical Features: The height of the unit should not exceed 21”. The units should be white or black in color. 4. Power Requirements: Units must be 110/120 VAC and work in a standard wall outlet with an American standard three prong outlet with a ground. 5. Required Operational Features: Extracts at least 35 pints of water per 24 hours Must have an external drainage connection and included drainage hose of at least 10 ft Must have an internal pump and capability to automatically drain the collected water into an elevated sink Auto shutoff that prevents overflow Handle(s) and wheels for easy portability ENERGY-STAR certified

# weather radar system for the United States Naval Academy

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 06:54:11.827-05
- Solicitation Number: N0018926Q0037

## Summary

The proposed simplified acquisition is being solicited on a full and open competition for a weather radar system for the United States Naval Academy. This combined synopsis-solicitation was updated 2/17 to revised the attached solicitation and add questions and answers. On 3/6, this combined synopsis-solicitation was revised to add additional industry questions and answers as an attachment. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial supplies/services in accordance with the information in FAR Subpart 12.6 and FAR Subpart 13.106, using Simplified Acquisition Procedures for commercial items. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; and a written solicitation will not be issued. This combined synopsis/solicitation will be available for download on or about 4 February 2026 under Request for Quotation (RFQ) number N0018926Q0037 at the following website: https://beta.sam.gov/. This solicitation documents and incorporates provisions and clauses in effect through FAC 2025-06 and DFARS Change Notice 20251110. It is the responsibility of the contractor to be familiar with the applicable clauses and provisions. The clauses may be accessed in full text at these addresses: www.acqnet.gov/far and http://www.acq.osd.mil/dpap/dfars/index.htm. This acquisition will result in a Firm-Fixed-Price contract under NAICS 334511. The Small Business Size Standard is 1,350. See attached combined synopsis-solicitation for full details.

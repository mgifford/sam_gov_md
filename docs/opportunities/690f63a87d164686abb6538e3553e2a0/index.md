# INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE - SIMULIA ABAQUS SOFTWARE USMA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 16:59:07.047-04
- Solicitation Number: W911SD26QA059

## Summary

Synopsis: MICC-West Point intends to solicit and negotiate with only one source under the authority of FAR 12.102(a) “Only one source reasonably available” based on the company being the sole provider for the services and is therefore the only source capable of satisfying the Government requirements. The Government intends to solicit and negotiate with: DASSAULT SYSTEMS AMERICAS CORP. 175 Wyman ST Waltham, Massachusetts 02451-1223 United States This is a new requirement for a subscription for Cabell’s Scholarly Analytics database of online journals for the United States Military Academy Library. The NAICS Code is 513210 – Software Publishers with a size standard of $47.0 million dollars. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive proposals or quotes. However, any responsible source who believes it is capable of meeting the requirement may submit a capability statement, proposal, or quotation, which shall be considered by the agency, only if received by the closing date and time of this notice. A determination not to compete the proposed requirement based upon the responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Responses to this notice are due on or before 03/27/2026 by 4:00 pm Eastern Standard Time (EST) by email to Leslie Duron at leslie.a.duron.civ@army.mil.

# Pathology Services

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-17 10:10:56.068-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24526Q0457

## Summary

The purpose of this Sources Sought Notice is to obtain market information and identify potential contractors capable of providing pathology services for renal patients at the Washington DC VA Medical Center. The Washington DC VAMC is seeking renal biopsy services for patients with chronic kidney conditions. The Department of Pathology and Laboratory Medicine Services requires expert consultation and advanced laboratory testing on human kidney specimens, as well as supplementary analyses, with the capability to deliver a 24-hour, same-day turnaround. The contractor will serve as a reference laboratory for professional pathology services, providing laboratory testing, consultation, and reference testing specifically for kidney biopsies.

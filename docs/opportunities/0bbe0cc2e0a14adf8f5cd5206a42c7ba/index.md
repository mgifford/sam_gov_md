# Market Research, Capability Call, and Virtual Industry Day Announcement: 2030 Census Questionnaire Assistance (CQA)

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-21 12:01:54.605-04
- Solicitation Number: YA1323-26-CQASN-0002

## Summary

The purpose of this notice is to: -Update industry on the United States Census Bureau (USCB) 2030 Census Questionnaire Assistance (CQA) requirement. -Identify parties having an interest in and the resources to support this requirement for 2030 CQA. -Request vendor feedback that can contribute to determining the final method of acquisition. -Provide notice/instruction for the 2030 CQA Virtual Industry Day. Please see the attached documents: -Market Research, Capability Call, and Virtual Industry Day Announcement: 2030 Census Questionnaire Assistance (CQA) -Draft 2030 CQA Requirements Document (PDF File) -2030 CQA Questions & Comments Template (Excel File). Final Responses to this Notice and the Requirements Document are due no later than 4:00 pm EST, May 15, 2026. The CQA virtual Industry Day will be held Thursday, May 7, 2026, from 1:00 pm EST to 2:00 pm EST. Questions to be considered for Industry Day response must be submitted no later than 4:00 pm EST on April 27, 2026. Please read this notice and associated instructions carefully as you must register to attend this industry day.

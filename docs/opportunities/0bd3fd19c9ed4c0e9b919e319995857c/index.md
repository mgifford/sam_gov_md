# Graph Prism Software

## Summary

This solicitation is being issued as a small business set-aside. The Naval Medical Research Command (NMRC) Management Information Department needs graph prism software to take raw data to a graph ready for presentations, posters and publications. This requirement is for authorized approved vendors to perform services for brand name equipment. The Product Service Code is R699. The NAICS code for this action is 423430. This requirement will be procured under the guidelines of FAR Part 12 Commercial Items. NMRC requires that all contractors doing business with the Acquisition Office must be registered with the Systems for Award Management (SAM). No award can be made unless the vendor is registered in Systems for Award Management. For additional information and to register in SAM, please access the following website: https://www.sam.gov/. In order to register with the SAM and to be eligible to receive an award from this Acquisition Office, all offers must have a Unique Entity Identification Number (UEI). A Sam.gov number may be acquired free of charge by contacting Sam.gov online at www.sam.gov or by phone at (866) 606-8220.

# Sources Sought - RT-4110 Blade Radio Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-20 10:15:18.549-04
- Solicitation Number: A059955

## Summary

This is not a solicitation, but rather a SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT to acquire a company able to tear down, evaluate, and repair the RT-4110 Blade Radio, part number 270-3186-070. The Statement of Work (SOW) is found in Attachment 1: “Blade Radio Repair, Statement of Work, 28 January 2026”. In accordance with FAR Part 10, Market Research, Headquarters at the Utah Test and Training Range/A4L is seeking information for potential sources for the tear down, evaluate, and repair of the RT-4110 Blade Radio, part number 270-3186-070.

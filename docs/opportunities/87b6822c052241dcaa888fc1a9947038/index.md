# CGC SPENCER FY26 DS1

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 14:02:11.548-04
- Solicitation Number: 70Z08026QMECP0018

## Summary

This requirement is for a contractor to provide all necessary facilities, materials, equipment and personnel to perform dockside repairs and maintenance to the US Coast Guard Cutter (USCGC) SPENCER (WMEC-905), 270-foot Medium Endurance Cutter. The required period of performance is a start date on or about 31 August 2026 and an end date on or about 25 October 2026.

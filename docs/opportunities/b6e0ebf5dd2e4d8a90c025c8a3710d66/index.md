POC: Lateisha Robinson/ Lateisha.Robinson@va.gov DESCRIPTION: NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD A SOLE SOURCE CONTRACT TO: Encore Fire Protection The project encompasses the replacement of all fire alarm system components associated with 18 control panels and 23 remote annunciators. Scope includes reworking and reconnecting fiber optic cables across 18 fiber cabinets, performing all necessary programming, testing, and commissioning of new devices, and ensuring system functionality with code print-out capabilities. The upgraded system must fully comply with NFPA 72, 2025 VA edition, and eliminate all existing ground faults. Encore Fire Protection is an Edwards Systems Technology Strategic Partner and is currently the only business able to work on the system.

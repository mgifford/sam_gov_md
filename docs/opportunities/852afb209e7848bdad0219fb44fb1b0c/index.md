# Meeks Mesa Campground Construction

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 10:18:39.112-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LT26Q0033

## Summary

This project includes the construction of the Meeks Mesa Campground and Trailhead. The base bid work includes: Removal and disposal of existing vegetation that is affected by new construction. Constructing new asphalt roads and spurs with line striping and crosswalk hatch. Widening state route 24 to include pavement, lane striping and culvert extensions. Constructing new concrete group site, concrete family units, aggregate family units, tent pads and installing new campground site furniture. Installing new family unit shelters and group site shelter. Installing new single and double precast concrete vault restrooms. Installing directional and regulatory signs, road closure gates, dumpster pad with enclosure wall, unit markers, bulletin board with fee tube, barrier rocks, concrete and aggregate surface paths. Constructing rockery retaining walls. Constructing drainage swales. Constructing post and pole fencing. Constructing a major site I.D. sign with stone base and minor site I.D. sign. Bid Options work includes: Constructing native surface trails. Constructing berm and boulder cluster pods. Coloring asphalt with mix additive. Quantities associated with these options are identified in the Schedule of Items.

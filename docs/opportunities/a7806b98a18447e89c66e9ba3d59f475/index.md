# AZ & NM Mine Site Maintenance and Minor Construction

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 13:39:50.733-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAX26Q0018

## Summary

The Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision for site maintenance and minor construction of project areas within the Coronado National Forest. The project areas are mine waste repository areas and former mine sites remediated under CERCLA Activities onsite will include soil decompaction, dry seeding, installing erosion control fabric, removal of existing fencing, and a mine feature closure.

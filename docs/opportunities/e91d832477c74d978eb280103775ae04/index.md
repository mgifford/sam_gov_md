# DLA MedSurg Prime Vendor Gen VI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 10:34:11.045-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE2DV25R0001

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) provides supply support and logistics services to the Army, Air Force, Navy, Marine Corps, and several federal agencies. The MSPV Program is comprised of three Global Regions (North, South, and West); each region may be supported by a Primary and Backup PV. These levels of support combine to provide routine ordering capability throughout the world. The MSPV program is the primary distribution channel for procurement and delivery of a full range of Commercially Available Off the Shelf medical/surgical supplies to Medical Treatment Facilities (MTFs), clinics, and operational customers throughout each of the three Global Regions. This acquisition is UNRESTRICTED for all Global Regions. Each PV provides medical surgical products for which it has been authorized as a distributor, under a designated pricing instrument authorized by DLA Troop Support Medical. Customers order directly through the Prime Vendor or through DLAs internal web order interface. Items supported under the contract include medical and surgical supplies and some small medical equipment. All items are brand name specific or generic commercial products that are identified by the manufacturer's item descriptions and part number and conform to the manufacturer's commercial specifications. The following major medical supply classes may be included (but not necessarily limited to): 6505 (Drugs, biologicals); 6510 (Surgical dressing materials); 6515 (Medical & Surgical instruments, equipment); 6545 (Medical sets, kits, and equipment - not including military configured kits); 6670 (Scales and Balances); 6532 (Hospital and Surgical Clothing and Related Special Purpose Items); 8520 (Toilet Soap, Shaving Preparations, and Dentifrices); 4240 (Safety and Rescue Equipment). A Medical Air Bridge and Prime Vendor War Readiness support is also provided.

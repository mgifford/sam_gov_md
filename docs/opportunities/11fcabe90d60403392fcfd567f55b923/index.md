## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice ONLY. The U.S. Government currently intends to award a contract for the removal and reinstallation of solar panels on the roof of Building 69 at Fort McNair on a SOLE SOURCE basis, but is seeking vendors that may be able to provide this requirement in order to support a competitive procurement.

# Catalog of U.S. Publications System Migration

- Agency: UNITED STATES GOVERNMENT PUBLISHING OFFICE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 12:57:03.286-04
- Solicitation Number: 040ADV-26-R-0025

## Summary

The Library Services & Content Management (LSCM) of the U.S. Government Publishing Office plans to replace its current Integrated Library System (ILS), Aleph, with a new ILS or Library Services Platform (LSP). This process will migrate the current Catalog of U.S. Publications (CGP) data, existing, custom indexes, services, and replace federated search funcionality for federal databases (MetaLib).

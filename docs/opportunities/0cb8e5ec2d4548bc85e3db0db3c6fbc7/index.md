# Provision of Serviced Apartments, London, United Kingdom

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 11:35:35.333-04
- Solicitation Number: 19UK5626Q0024

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy – London requires fully furnished, serviced apartments with up to three bedrooms suitable for short-term occupancy in the Wimbledon, Putney, Nine Elms/Battersea, St. John’s Wood, and Marylebone areas of London, United Kingdom. Contract type will be indefinite delivery indefinite quantity. The anticipated performance period will be one year from the date of contract award. Only companies specializing in this field should express interest in this opportunity. The Government may award contract(s) based on initial offer(s) without discussion. Resultant contract(s) will be firm fixed price type contract(s) under which will be issued firm, fixed-price task orders. This opportunity is subject to FAR 52.229-12 – Tax on Certain Foreign Procurements. Electronic Submissions will be accepted. Please submit your offer prior to the response date and time.

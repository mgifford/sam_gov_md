# Well-Septic Repairs and installation at the Florida National Cemetery

- Posted: 2026-04-10 11:21:56.512-04

## Summary

Florida National Well-Septic Repairs The Department of Veterans Affairs, National Cemetery Administration has a requirement for a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract for a Contractor to provide all Management and Supervision, Labor, Equipment, Material, Supplies, etc., necessary and required repair and install new well system at the Florida National Cemetery. All work will be done as defined and described in accordance with applicable Local, State and Federal laws, regulations and in accordance with the Terms and Conditions, of the Scope of Work, Specifications and Drawings herein. This Invitations for Bids (IFB) procurement opportunity is 100% Set-Aside for Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small-Business, (SDVOSB) firms; Authority 38 U.S.C. 8127 (Public Law 109-461). Bidders must be SBA VetCert verified prior to submitting a bid in accordance with VAAR 819-7003(B) AND FAR PROVISION 52.204-7(a). Bidder must represent that it is a SDVOSB concern under NAICS code assigned to this acquisition and is SBA VetCert verified for eligibility in the https://veterans.certify.sba.gov/ - database. In accordance with VAAR 819.7003 (b), SDVOSB firms must be verified in the SAB VetCert database, https://veterans.certify.sba.gov. Prospective bidders must also be registered in Vendor Information Pageshttps://veterans.certify.sba.gov/ Contractor must be certified Vets 4212 athttps://www.dol.gov/agencies/vets/programs/vets 4212 at the time of Bid submission and at time of award. The Product / Service Code is Y1PZ- Construction of Other Non-Building Facilities The North American Industry Classification System code is 237110 (Water and Sewer Line and Related Structures Construction. The Size Standard for this procurement is $45 million dollars. The Magnitude of Construction range for this procurement isestimated to be between $250,000 and $500,000. Please See FAR 52.236-27 Site Visit for detailed site visitinformation. Interested Bidders are strongly encouraged to Participate in a organized Pre-Bid Site Visit at the Florida NC On April 16th at 9 am EST at 6502 SW 102nd Ave, Bushnell Florida. Interested bidders will be required to furnish a Bid Bond; and the awarded Contractor will be required to provide Performance and Payment Bonds. Requests for Information (RFI) must be sent via e-mail to the Contracting Officer IAW page 8 Submittal of bid Instructions and page 87 for RFI instruction. Bids will be accepted on total price of all line items.

# ENERSYS-UPS BATTERY REPLACEMENT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-07 08:34:05.099-04

## Summary

NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound is posting a sources sought synopsis as a means of conducting market research to identify parties having interest and recourse to support the ships UPS Battery Replacement for the USNS Mount Whitney- Military Sealift Command. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code assignment to this procurement is 335910 with a small business size standard of 1250 employees. The results of the market research may contribute to future procurement process decisions. No feedback or evaluations will be provided regarding any submissions on the sources sought. Please provide responses NLT 10 April 2026 at 10:00 AM Eastern Daylight Time. The results of the market research may contribute to the determination that only one or more multiple responsible sources exists that have the required capabilities and interest.

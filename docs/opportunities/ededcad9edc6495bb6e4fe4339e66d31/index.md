# FPAC Business Operations Branch (BOB) IT CPIC, Portfolio Management, and Financial Management Support Services

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 13:50:01.334-04

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Farm Production and Conservation (FPAC) Mission Area, intends to consolidate multiple related support requirements into a single acquisition for the FPAC Business Operations Branch (BOB). This consolidated requirement will provide integrated support for IT Capital Planning and Investment Control (CPIC), IT Portfolio Management, IT Financial Management, and Program/Project Management activities across the FPAC Information Solutions Division (ISD). BOB oversees strategic IT governance, financial oversight, and enterprise investment management for a multi?agency IT portfolio valued at approximately $1B annually. The consolidated scope includes support for CPIC processes, business case development, governance board (IRB) operations, Earned Value Management, Technology Business Management (TBM), FITARA compliance, spend plan management, budget formulation, Inter?Agency Agreement tracking, financial reporting, and maintenance/support of the BOB Financial Management Tool (FMT). Requirements also include full lifecycle program and project management and support for monthly planning, performance reporting, and transition efforts.

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command-Redstone, Operations, Services, & Training Division, plans to obtain information to serve as market research to help identify potential sources that can provide a brand name purchase of one (1) Optical Spectrum Analyzer High Performance model or equivalent that will be a suitable replacement for an aging equipment. This item shall be delivered to the Army Primary Standards Laboratory (APSL), Building 5435 Fowler Road, Redstone Arsenal, AL. The Optical Spectrum Analyzer shall include the following specifications or equivalent (if not brand name): Yokogawa AQ6370E-20 Optical Spectrum Analyzer High Qty:1 Performance Model Wavelength range?600 to 1700 nm Wavelength accuracy?±0.01 nm Wavelength resolution?0.02 nm Close-in Dynamic range?78 dB typ.

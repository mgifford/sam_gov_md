## Summary

The purpose of this post award synopsis is to notify industry that Northrop Grumman Systems Corporation with a CAGE Code of 06481 has been awarded a sole source contract modification to Contract FA8128-21-D-0001 on 2 April 2026, at an estimated value of $18,930,001. The Total Contract Estimated Value is now $33,930,000. This effort is in support of the Air-Launched Cruise Missile. An Indefinite-Delivery-Indefinite-Quantity (IDIQ) contract was awarded on 5 March 2021 to support recurring and non-recurring engineering services for the Inertial Navigation Element. Each task order under the IDIQ is negotiated separately and is administered by the Air Force Nuclear Weapons Center (AFNWC), Air-Delivered Capabilities Directorate (ND), Missile Sustainment Division (AFNWC/NDM).

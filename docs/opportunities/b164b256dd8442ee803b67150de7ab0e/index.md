## Summary

This procurement is Fixed-price with Economic Price Adjustment and was awarded sole source. This contract is sole source per 10 U.S.C. 2304(c)(1) Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements , as implemented by FAR 6.302-1. It is sole source as there was only one respinsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. In the current orthopedic marketplace, Red One Medical is the sole distributor for ZImmer Biomet products and product selection is not based solely on price and is overshadowed by physician prference/experience and highly specialized training on use of specific surgical items.

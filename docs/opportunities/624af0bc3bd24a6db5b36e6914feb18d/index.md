This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for non-personal services to the Government with two (2) 20-foot and two (2) 10-foot refrigerated containers capable of performing services at the required temperatures listed in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). Units shall be equipped with standard safety equipment as required by local laws. Containers must be in compliance with all refrigeration requirements stipulated under this contract during the entire period of performance at the location and dates specified in table 1 in PWS, with the format in subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes only solicitation; proposals are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued.

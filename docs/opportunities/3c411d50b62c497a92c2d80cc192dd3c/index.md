## Summary

7 quotes were initially received. In accordance with the soliciation quotes were evaluated based on Lowest Price Technically Acceptable. After noticing a discrepancy in the solicitation, Final Revisions were requested with two responses received. The Government will evaluate quotations based on the lowest price technically acceptable criteria. Only the lowest priced offer will be evaluated for Technical Acceptability. Should the lowest priced offer not receive an acceptable technical or past performance rating, the process will continue in order of lowest priced offer until the lowest price, technically acceptable offer with acceptable or neutral past performance is identified.

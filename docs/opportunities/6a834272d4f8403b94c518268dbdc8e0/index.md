# AT Coach Simulators and Licensing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-22 14:07:04.093-04

## Summary

This Is a Notice of Intent to Award a Sole Source Contract and is Not a Request for Competitive Quotes. This non-competitive award is implemented by FAR 6.103-1(b), as it has been deemed that only one source is available to meet this specific requirement. The Government intends to award a sole source contract to UFA Inc. for ATCoach Radar Simulator systems and licensing. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quotes. However, all quotes received by the date specified herein will be considered by the Government. Interested parties may identify their interest and may submit a quote and/or capability statement by 1:00 PM CST, 06 May 2026. Capability statements and quotes shall be sent via email to ian.smith.33@us.af.mil and kimberley.alvarez.1@us.af.mil. A determination by the Government not to compete the proposed contract, based upon responses to this notice, is solely within the discretion of the Government.

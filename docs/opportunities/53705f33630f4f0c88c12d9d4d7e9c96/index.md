## Summary

SUBJECT: U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Baltimore District Industry Day Notification for Kimbrough Ambulatory Care Center Fort Meade, MD The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Baltimore District invites industry representatives to an Industry Day for the construction of the new Kimbrough Ambulatory Care Center at Fort Meade, Maryland. This event will offer a comprehensive overview of this vital project and a forum for engaging with industry experts to gather and share information. *Participation in this Industry Day does not constitute a commitment by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers to award a contract, nor does it provide any advantage in the competitive procurement process. *Registration surveys must be submitted NLT 1600 on 13 April 2026.* Date: 27-28 April 2026 Time: 0900 (Check-in begins at 0815) Location: George H. Fallon Federal Building 31 Hopkins Plaza, Baltimore, MD 21201 Ground Floor, G121 (General Session) and G111 (1-on-1 Interview Sessions) Security: All visitors are required to enter the George Fallon Building through the General Entrance on the side of the building facing the Plaza. Upon entry, visitors will be screened and issued a VISITOR badge which must be worn at all times while in the building. The event will be held on the ground floor in Suite G100 (Department of Veterans Affairs - Professional Development Academy). Please take the elevators to level "G" to access the suite, where you will be directed to rooms G121 and G111. Parking: Parking options in the vicinity of the Fallon Building include: Downunder Garage (beneath Fallon Federal Building) -110 W. Lombard St. CFG Bank Arena Garage - 99 S Howard St. Landmark Parking – 20 S Charles St. Lombard Garage - 221 W. Lombard St. 210 W. Baltimore St. Garage – 210 W. Baltimore St. *Due to the capacity of the location, we will be limiting attendees to two (2) persons per firm. Firms that are planning to attend should register as early as possible. The event doors will be closed once maximum capacity has been reached. Registration Form To register for the event, please complete the registration survey using the Microsoft Forms link below. All interested parties are encouraged to respond, even if not attending. A limited number of individual breakout sessions with Government representatives will be available to discuss project-specific questions. Due to time constraints, priority for these sessions will be given to firms interested in serving as the prime contractor. The one-on-one sessions are assigned on a "first come, first serve" basis. Registration surveys must be submitted NLT 1600 on 13 April 2026.* https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/3qVSCgLwXV Schedule Day 1: Registration, General Session and One-on-One Interview Sessions 08:15: Registration 09:00: General Session (Introductions, Senior Leader Remarks, Project Overview, Q&A) 11:30: One-on-One Interview Session 1 (Room G111) 12:15: Lunch Break 13:00: One-on-One Interview Session 2 (Room G111) 14:00: One-on-One Interview Session 3 (Room G111) 15:00: Day 1 Concludes Day 2: One-on-One Interviews 09:00: One-on-One Interview Session 4 (Room G111) 10:00: One-on-One Interview Session 5 (Room G111) 11:00: One-on-One Interview Session 6 (Room G111) 12:00: Lunch Break 13:00: One-on-One Interview Session 7 (Room G111) 14:00: One-on-One Interview Session 8 (Room G111) 15:00: Event Concludes If you have questions concerning this opportunity, please contact the Contract Specialist Robert Ferebee at Robert.L.Ferebee@usace.army.mil, as well as Contracting Officer, Tamara Bonomolo at Tamara.C.Bonomolo@usace.army.mil.

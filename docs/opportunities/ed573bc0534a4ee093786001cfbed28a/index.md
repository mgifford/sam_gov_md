# Contractor-Owned Contractor-Operated (COCO) Fuel Storage Services in Port of Salalah, Oman

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 14:41:29.936-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE603-26-R-0503

## Summary

The service is for Contractor-Owned Contractor-Operated (COCO) for fuel storage services with the capability to receive via tanker/barge, store, protect, and additize as required. In addition to ship commercial JP-5 and F-76 marine tanker/barge, Naval vessells, and tank truck. Storage (fill capacity) of 670,000 barrels (BBLS) of JP-5, 790,000 BBLS of F-76 must be availabe upon award at a single facility. Storage (fill capacity) of 1,460,000 BBLS is required. A minimum of two tanks per fuel grade interconnected and isolated from other facilities and products handled withing the storage facility as required.

# Starshield

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) PMA-207 Executive Lift awarded a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) effort to L3 Technologies Integrated Systems (L3) Greenville, Texas, for the installation and integration of a Starshield Modification Kit for the C-37 Aircraft Platform. The period of performance was 15 January 2026 through 31 March 2026. This action was awarded on a sole source basis to L3 under the authority permitting other than full and open competition, Title 10, U.S.C. 3204(a)(2), as implemented by FAR 6.302-2(a)(2), Unusual and compelling urgency in which delay in award of contract would result in serious injury, financial, or other, to the Government.

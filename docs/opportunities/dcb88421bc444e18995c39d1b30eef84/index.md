# Amendment 01 Textile Warehouse Office Addition and Renovation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 16:11:10.491-04
- Solicitation Number: W91QF426BA001Amendment01

## Summary

This Agreement is for the sole award for specific Sustainment, Restoration, and Modernization (SRM) of the USDB Textiles Warehouse (Building B1146 – 1251 USDB Road, Fort Leavenworth, Kansas 66048) for the Directorate of Public Works (DPW) on Fort Leavenworth, KS. The contractor shall provide all supervision, management, personnel, labor, equipment, transportation, supplies, and any other items necessary to complete various construction projects. This work may include but is not limited to the following: addition of an approximate 1,280 SF metal building office addition and new recessed truck dock; repair or replacement of mechanical, electrical and fire protection and detection systems; repairs related to building renovation or demolition; interior remodeling work including carpentry, painting, floor and wall covering or finishes. This acquisition will require a general contractor with experience and expertise capable of managing numerous task orders at the same time, which will require construction scheduling, cost estimating, quality control, and coordination of subcontractors in a wide variety of construction trades.

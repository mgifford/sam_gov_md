## Summary

The Army Contracting Command – Aberdeen Proving Ground- Fort Belvoir, 10205 Burbeck Road Fort Belvoir, VA 22060, awarded a sole source, firm-fixed-price (FFP) purchase order for the procurement of HID Global Corporation NCITE Vetting Service and NCITE Kiosks Software Subscriptions, to the sole proprietor of the product. The requirement was awarded under 10 United States Code (U.S.C.) §3204(a)(1) as implemented in Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.302-1(a)(2)(iii)(A), substantial duplication of cost to the Government that is not expected to be recovered through competition. Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. This contract was awarded with a face value of $264,470.00 and a total contract value of $17,176,770.00 inclusive of all unexercised options for increased quantities.

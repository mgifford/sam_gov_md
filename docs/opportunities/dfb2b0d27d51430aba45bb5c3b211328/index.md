# Coolant Machine Maintenance Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 16:30:04.769-04
- Solicitation Number: N4215826900F0023

## Summary

The Norfolk Naval Shipyard (NNSY) Service Contracting Department, Portsmouth, VA 23709-5000, intends to award a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract that provide Service for Continual Maintenance of Machine Coolant for Industrial Plant Equipment (IPE) in accordance with the enclosed Combined Synopsis Solicitation. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set Aside utilizing Product Service Code (PSC) J068, Maint/Repair/Rebuild Of Equipment-Chemicals and Chemical Products. The North American Industry Classification Standard (NAICS) code applicable to this procurement is 811310, Commercial and Industrial Machinery and Equipment (except Automotive and Electronic) Repair and Maintenance. The small business size standard is 12,500,000.

# Internet Services USGS New England WSC ME and NH

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 10:55:36.924-04
- Solicitation Number: 140G0126Q0092

## Summary

The USGS New England Water Science Center (WSC) two offices, one each located in Augusta, ME and Pembroke, NH, have a requirement for internet services. Installation of a fiber optic service providing internet access for the New England WSC, Augusta, ME office located at 196 Whitten Rd Augusta, ME 04330 and the New England WSC, NH office located at 331 Commerce Way, Suite 2, Pembroke, NH 03275. Base Year plus four options with a Period of Performance starting 5/1/2026. Request for Quote (RFQ) 140G0126Q0092 will be issued on or about 4/6/2026 with written quotes due on or about 4/13/2026. This acquisition is full and open. The NAICS code is 517111. The size standard is 1,500 employees. The anticipated award date of the contract is 4/17/2026. A vendor must be registered at https://www.sam.gov/index.html to be awarded the contract. All responsible sources may submit a bid or quotation which shall be considered by the agency. Responses may be emailed to krschneider@usgs.gov.

## Summary

Combined Synopsis/Solicitation – JOHN H. KERR DAM RIGHT WING DIKE SOIL SAMPLING Solicitation No. W912PM26QA026. This solicitation is issued as an RFQ. Date of Issue: 30 March 2026 This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. This combined synopsis/solicitation is being issued as unrestricted. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. This solicitation is a Request for Quote (RFQ). The associated North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 541380 with a size standard of $19M. The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Wilmington District needs a Contractor to furnish all services, labor, equipment, supplies, tools and incidentals necessary to perform geotechnical soils laboratory testing. Testing shall be conducted by a USACE-validated lab in accordance with ASTM methods and quantities described herein. The resulting award issued from this request will result in one award. The solicitation can only be changed by formal written amendment issued by the Contracting Officer.

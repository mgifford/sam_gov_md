The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), VISN 22 Network Contracting Office, is seeking sources that can provide the below requested items for MiPacs Server. VA San Diego Dental currently uses and operates under Planmeca s Romexis dental imaging and practice management software suite, widely used in dental practices and healthcare settings to manage dental imaging and patient data. Romexis Server provides a centralized platform for storing, managing, and accessing dental images and veteran patient information. It integrates with various dental imaging devices and software solutions, facilitating a comprehensive and efficient workflow within dental practices. The current operating system is no longer supported by VA OI&T and require update to the newest available OS (Windows 10 IoT Enterprise LTSC 2021). This procurement is intended to upgrade its existing dental imaging servers from the current Planmeca Romexis platform to the MiPACS Dental Enterprise Solution. This procurement aims to enhance the management, storage, and retrieval of dental images across VA dental facilities. The upgrade will include the migration of dental imaging data, implementation of secure and scalable MiPACS Storage Servers, integration with existing VA systems and imaging devices, and configuration to ensure compliance with VA data security policies and federal regulations, including HIPAA.Â Veteran dental x-ray images will be stored and maintained on premises using VA-managed hard storage infrastructure.

## Summary

SYNOPSIS OF AWARD: Stevedoring and Related Terminal Services HTC71126DE133 PORT OF WILHELMSHAVEN, GERMANY On 23 April 2026, BLG Logistics was awarded a $18,555,040.63 Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Quantity, fixed-priced contract for Stevedoring and Related Terminal Services at Wilhelmshaven, Germany in response to Solicitation HTC71125RE142. Work will be performed at the Port of Wilhelmshaven, Germany during the 01 May 2026 through 30 April 2031 performance period with an expected completion date of 30 April 2031. This contract was a competitive acquisition. The U.S. Transportation Command, Directorate of Acquisition, 508 Scott Drive, Scott AFB, IL is the contracting activity.

# MD021 Rockville - Snow and Ice Removal Services FY26

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 11:48:11.895-04
- Solicitation Number: W15qkn26Qa043

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command - New Jersey (ACC-NJ) on behalf of the 99th Readiness Division DPW has a requirement to procure new Snow and Ice Removal Services. The scope of this requirement is provide a clean workplace free of snow and ice by providing all personnel, supplies, supervision, tools, materials, equipment, transportation, fuel and other items and nonpersonal services necessary to provide Snow Removal Services in accordance with the Performance Work Statement (PWS) at the **Maus-Warfield USARC (MD021), 1850 Baltimore Road, Rockville, MD 20851.

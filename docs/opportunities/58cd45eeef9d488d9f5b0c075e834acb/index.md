# Data Integration Platform

- Agency: COURT SERVICES AND OFFENDER SUPERVISION AGENCY
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-19 12:32:12.085-04
- Solicitation Number: 9594CS26R0004

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice only.This is not a request for proposal and no contract action will be awarded from this notice. No solicitation, specifications, drawings are available at this time. This notice is a market research tool used for planning purposes only to determine the availability and capability of any sources (small or large) who can perform the work described herein. All information contained in this Sources Sought Notice is preliminary as well as subject to modification and is in no way binding on the Government. The Court Services and Offender Supervision Agency (CSOSA), Office of Information Technology, is seeking a secure, FedRAMP Moderate, cloud-based SaaS data integration platform designed for law enforcement that can quickly convert siloed, surface unstructured, and varied data sources into data intelligence, producing support formats such as maps, charts, tables, link analysis, network visualizations, and other analytics. The resultant data can then be displayed on various devices such as laptops and mobile devices. Responses to this notice must include all information requested in the attached notice.

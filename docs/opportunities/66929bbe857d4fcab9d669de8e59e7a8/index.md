# Sources Sought Oil Water Separator Maintenance Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-24 12:29:07.558-04
- Solicitation Number: FA254326SS001

## Summary

This is an official Sources Sought. The government has a need for a potential Service of management and maintenance for Oil Water Separators, Grease Traps, Septic Tanks and Sand interceptors; and therefore the U.S. Air Force 460th Contracting Squadron at Buckley SFB, CO is posting this Sources Sought to find (potential) interested and capable companies. SUBMISSION DETAILS: Capabilities statement Any questions or concerns are encouraged as it will help identify problems or mistakes. Please include the following information in your response/narrative: - Company name, address, and point of contact, with email address and phone number - CAGE Code and/or Unique Entity Identification (UEI) number Responses are to be sent via email to SrA Guillermo Torres at guillermo.torres_barzallo.1@spaceforce.mil and TSgt Michael Roybal at michael.roybal.1@spaceforce.mil NLT 4 PM MST, 03 April 2026. Please include the Sources Sought Identification Number or title in the subject line of the email submission. Attached will be a draft PWS (Performance Work Statement) that will give the proper information of the requirement. Please note this is in no way an obligation for the government, no award or contract will come out directly from this request and that the requirement is simply a concept at this moment and could not move forward in development.

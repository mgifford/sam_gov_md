# 2026 Kaniksu Fuels Treatments SSC-Region 6 Pacific Northwest Stewardship BPA- Colville National Forest

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 13:21:01.96-04
- Solicitation Number: 12363N26Q4089

## Summary

This procurement is restricted to the approved companies eligible for call orders under the R6 Pacific Northwest Stewardship BPA for the Colville National Forest in the state of Washington. This requirement may be awarded to one, or multiple, vendor as a BPA Call Order against BPA's previously solicited on the Government Point of Entry (GPE). The government will not provide feedback or evaluations to companies regarding their submissions in response to this notice that are not eligible for awards under the R6 Pacific Northwest Stewardship BPA.

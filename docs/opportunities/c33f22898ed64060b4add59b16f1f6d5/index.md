# Shenango River Lake Ranger Station Building Siding Replacement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 09:51:41.717-04
- Solicitation Number: W911WN26QA022

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Pittsburgh District, has a need for a Commercial Construction Contract for Siding Replacement at the Shenango River Lake Ranger Station Building in Transfer, Mercer County, Pennsylvania. The scope of work requires the contractor to furnish all labor, materials, and equipment for the complete replacement of the exterior siding system on the Shenango Ranger Station. This includes the demolition and disposal of the existing cedar siding, insulation, and furring boards. The contractor will be responsible for the temporary relocation and reinstallation of all exterior utilities and fixtures, such as electrical services, lighting, and signage, ensuring essential services are maintained throughout the project. The work also involves installing new prefinished aluminum coverings for all window and door openings, which may require the removal and resetting of existing windows. A new siding system will be installed, consisting of pressure-treated lumber furring strips, rigid insulation, and approximately 2,500 square feet of lap cement fiberboard siding with all associated components. Additionally, the project includes replacing the existing cedar soffit on the porch ceiling with cement board soffit and installing new aluminum gravel stops, flashing, and a new gutter at the porch roof to integrate with the existing EPDM roof. The work shall be completed within 120 Calendar Days after written Notice to Proceed (NTP). This acquisition is 100% Small Business Set Aside. The resulting award will be for one (1) Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) Purchase Order. This is a Request for Quote (RFQ) and will be submitted through Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) which posts the opportunity to SAM.gov. Therefore, requests for hardcopies will not be honored. The North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) for this procurement is 238170 - Siding Contractors. The Small Business Size Standard is $19,000,000.00. Information regarding the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers process for settling requests for equitable adjustments and duration goals to achieve definitization of equitable adjustments for change orders under construction contracts can be found by going to https://www.usace.army.mil/Business-With-Us/Partnering/ then clicking on View FFP Contract Changes Playbook Here. Amendment 0001 is to add or remove RFO and DFARS Class Deviation Provisions and Clauses.

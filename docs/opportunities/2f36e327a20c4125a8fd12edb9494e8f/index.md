# K--Intent to Sole Source Telonics Radio Collar Repair

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-30 13:15:12.542-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P1426Q0052

## Summary

This is a notice of intent to award a sole source contract to Telonics, Inc., 932 E. Impala Avenue, Mesa, Arizona 85204. This is not a competitive Request for Quote. The Contracting Operations Central, Northern Rockies Major Acquisition Buying Office (MABO), intends to issue a Sole Source award for refurbishing nineteen (19) Telonics TGW-4677 and five (5) TGW-4577 GPS collars with CR-5 mechanisms for the National Park Service (NPS), Yellowstone National Park. Please see attached for additional information.

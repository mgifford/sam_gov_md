# MCAS Beaufort Catholic Priest Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 10:56:50.074-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700126Q0053

## Summary

PRE-SOLICITATION NOTICE This notice is not a request for quotes. The Marine Corps Installations East (MCIEAST)/Marine Corps Air Station (MCAS) Beaufort, South Carolina has a requirement for a non-personal services contract to provide Roman Catholic (RC) religious ministries for members of the military, their dependents, and other authorized persons. The requirement is for a base period of twelve (12) months and two (2) twelve-month option periods to be exercised at the discretion of the Government. It is the Government’s intention to issue a single firm fixed price contract. The proposed acquisition is set aside 100% for Small Businesses. The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 813110. The NAICS Industry Description is Religious Organizations. The Government will solicit and award this contract in accordance with FAR Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. The Solicitation will be posted on or about fifteen (15) days after this posting and made available on the Contract Opportunities portal located at https://www.sam.gov/ . If you are an interested party, please inform the procuring agency of your interest in the upcoming solicitation. It is the responsibility of the offeror to monitor the website for solicitation and any associated amendments. Contractors interested in doing business with the Government must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at https://www.sam.gov/ . Contractors must also ensure annual representations and certifications are current within the SAM website and possess a Commercial and Government Entity Code (CAGE Code). Interested parties must agree to implement the Department of Defense web-based software application Wide Area Work Flow-Receipt and Acceptance (WAWF-RA). Additionally, contractors must also be registered with Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) at https://piee.eb.mil . The point of contact for this notice is Mrs. Tiffany Morris, Contract Specialist, at phone number (910) 451-3017 or email tiffany.morris@usmc.mil.

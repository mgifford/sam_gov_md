# Fitting, Connectors and Tubing

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 14:08:55.04-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U2749

## Summary

Fitting, Connectors and Tubing REPOST with additional items. Brand Name or Equal. Swagelok Products Proposals must offer Swagelok brand products or an equal product. If offering Swagelok, the offeror must be an authorized Swagelok dealer or provide proof of authorization. If offering an equal product, the offeror must submit technical data sheets and material certifications demonstrating that the product is fully interchangeable, intermixable, and equivalent in form, fit, function, and performance (including material specifications, pressure ratings, and temperature tolerances) to the specified Swagelok parts. Failure to provide sufficient evidence of equality with the proposal may result in the proposal being deemed non-responsive.

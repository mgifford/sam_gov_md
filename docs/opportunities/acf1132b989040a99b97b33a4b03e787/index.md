# Loading Dock Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-16 15:59:59.645-04

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice, FA441726Q0082, seeking responses to determine interested parties for participation in the acquisition. The 1st Special Operations Contracting Squadron at Hurlburt Field Air Force Base, Florida, plans to establish a firm-fixed price contract to provide Aircraft Restoration. For additional information, see the attached draft Statement of Work (See Attachment 1).

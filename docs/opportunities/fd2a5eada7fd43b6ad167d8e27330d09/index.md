## Summary

The requirement is for a comprehensive maintenance and repair services contract to support critical audiovisual infrastructure. The prospective contractor shall provide all necessary labor, parts, materials, and supervision to maintain Video Teleconferencing (VTC) systems, large-scale video walls, and all associated infrastructure.

# remove and replace conveyor system

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-21 17:31:08.47-04
- Solicitation Number: 12639526Q0113

## Summary

This is a sources sought notice. The USDA AMS C&T is looking for any small business interests that are capable of designing, manufacturing, and replacing the existing conveyor system at two (2) USDA classifying facilities, one (1) in Abilene, and one (1) in Corpus Christi. The contractor must also be able to service/maintenance the newly installed conveyor system for 10 years after install. While each requirement will be done through seperate contracts for each facility, this sources sought is combined to seek any small business interest capable of performing the work as part of the market research for both requirements. USDA is not looking for quotes at this time and will not except any quotes in response to this sources sought notice. If you are a small business capable of satifying the requirement, please email your company's capability statement, previous work in similar scope, and interest in being considered for the future solicitation. USDA anticipates posting the full solicitation RFQ after May 5th based on the results of this sources sought notice and other market research. Please see the attached statement of work and supporting docs for scope of work required before responding to understand the scope of the work required.

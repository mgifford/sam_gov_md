# Chapel Stained Glass Restoration

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 10:42:32.943-04
- Solicitation Number: FA330026Q0012

## Summary

The Maxwell Chapel facilities require cleaning and restoration of the stained glass for Faith and Grace chapel in an effort to maintain and uphold the central points of spiritual care to base personnel. This contract covers the cleaning of the stained glass and removal, cleaning, and re-installation of stained glass protective layer for Grace Chapel. This contract covers the removal and cleaning of the stained glass as well as the installation of a glass protective layer for Faith Chapel. Contractor shall provide all labor, equipment and materials to perform the following tasks to complete this Statement of Work.

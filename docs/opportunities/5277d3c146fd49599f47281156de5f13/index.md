## Summary

Solicitation Posting for W912CH-25-R-0099 ? Stryker Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) Sustainment Services SupportUnder the authority of FAR 6.302-1, Only One Responsible Source, the United States Army Contracting Command - Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) intends to award a sole source, five-year Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF) and Cost Only Requirements contract for Stryker Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) Sustainment Services Support. This action will be sole sourced to General Dynamics Land Systems (GDLS). This action will be noncompetitive, sole source for the following services: Field Level Maintenance, Field Service Representative support, New Equipment Training, Fielding, and Deprocessing, for any work outside of the Interactive Electronic Technical Manual (IETM).The solicitation, W912CH-25-R-0099, is attached and will contain the required information and a specific due date and time.CAUTION: This solicitation will be issued electronically as will any amendments thereto. Because of this, the Government is under no obligation and is, in fact, unable to maintain a mailing list. The burden is therefore on any interested parties to periodically access the above internet address in order to obtain any amendments which maybe issued. Failure to obtain any amendments and to respond to them prior to the date and time set for receipt of proposals may render your proposal nonresponsive and result in the rejection of the proposal.NOTICE TO ALL CONTRACTORS: All contractors who provide goods/services to the Department of Defense (DoD) must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM). You may register via the Internet at https://www.sam.gov/SAM/pages/public/loginFAQ.jsf. Further information may be obtained through the internet at: https://www.sam.gov/SAM/pages/public/loginFAQ.jsf.

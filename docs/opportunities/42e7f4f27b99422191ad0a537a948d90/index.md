## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs intends to issue solicitation 36C25226Q0136 on 4/13/2026 for Sterile Processing System, Legionella and Dental Water Testing at the Oscar G. Johnson VA Medical Center, Iron Mountain, MI location. The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, supplies, equipment, and management/supervision needed to provide water testing services at the Oscar G. Johnson VA Medical Center, Iron Mountain, MI location in accordance with the Statement of Work. This requirement is being solicited as a 100% SDVOSB set-aside.

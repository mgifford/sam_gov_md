# Residence Remodeling

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 08:02:35.559-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AU9026Q0005

## Summary

This solicitation specification includes the construction of 2 drywall partitions including electrical installations, AC installations, sanitary installations, interior doors, in two USG-owned residences in Vienna, Austria. The work consists of, but is not limited to the following: • New Bedroom 3 area = 13,90 m² • Modified Living/dining/kitchen area = 33,00 m² • Modified kitchen area = 14,00 m² • Modified Bathroom 2 area = 4,74 m² Estimated size of the project according to FAR 36.204 is between $25,000 and $100,000.

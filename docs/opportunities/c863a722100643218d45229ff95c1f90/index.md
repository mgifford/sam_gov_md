# FY26 State Family Programs Workshop and Youth Symposium

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 17:24:17.055-04
- Solicitation Number: W912NR26QA009

## Summary

The Louisiana National Guard (LANG) Office of Family Programs (OFP), is seeking a facility to provide 70 sleeping rooms, conference space, parking, catering, and dining accommodations for approximately 125 participants (i.e. Service Members, Family Members, youth, family readiness volunteers, and event staff) on Friday, 24 April 2026 – Sunday, 26 April 2026 in Baton Rouge, LA. The contractor shall provide services between the hours of 0900-2000 CST on Friday, 24 April 2026 through 0630-1700 CST on Sunday, 26 April 2026 except on recognized US holidays or when the Government facility/installation is closed due to local or national emergencies, administrative closings, or similar Government-directed facility/installation closings. Performance will be in Baton Rouge, LA. The contractor shall at all times maintain an adequate workforce for the uninterrupted performance of all tasks defined within this PWS.

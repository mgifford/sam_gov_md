# Detention Center Services

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 12:08:13.209-04
- Solicitation Number: 70B03C25Q00000375

## Summary

The U.S. Customs and Border Protection intends to award a Multiple-Award Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) with eight (8) different jails that will cover Vermont and Northern New York. Detainees will be transferred by United States Border Patrol to any of the following counties: Albany County, NY; Clinton County, NY; Franklin County, NY; Montgomery County, NY; Rensselaer County, NY; Schenectady County, NY; Coos County, NH and Vermont Department of Corrections, VT. See attached SF-18 solicitation for submission instructions. Please only submit a quote for the county where you are located.

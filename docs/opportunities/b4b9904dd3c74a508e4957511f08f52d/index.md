## Summary

Request for Quotation 191N6526Q0022 HVAC & Electrical Power Generation and Transmission Training at U S Embassy New Delhi, India Enclosed is a Request for Quotes (RFQ) for HVAC & Electrical Power Generation and Transmission Training. If you would like to submit a quotation, follow the instructions in Section–J of the solicitation, complete the required portions of the attached document, and submit it to the address provided in para 4 below. Quotations are due by 1400 hrs. (India Time) of April 3, 2026. Your quotation must be submitted electronically to Newdelhibids@state.gov with the subject line “Solicitation No. 191N6526Q0022 for HVAC & Electrical Power Generation and Transmission Training”. The file size must not exceed 10MB. If the file size exceeds 10MB, the submission must be made in separate files and attached to separate emails with less than 10MB each. No quotations shall be accepted after 1700 hrs. (India Time) of April 3, 2026. Quotations must be in English. Incomplete quotations will not be accepted. Quotation submitted to any other e-mail ID other than Newdelhibids@state.gov shall be rejected and dealt in accordance with FAR provision 52.215-1. The U.S. Government intends to award a contract/purchase order to the responsible company submitting an acceptable offer at the lowest price. We intend to award a contract/purchase order based on initial proposals, without holding discussions, although we may hold discussions with companies in the competitive range if there is a need to do so.

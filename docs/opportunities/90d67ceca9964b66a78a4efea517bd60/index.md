## Summary

Network 21 Contracting Office anticipates awarding a sole source procurement to Fidelis Sustainability Distribution, INC. NAICS Code 339114 - Dental Instrument, Equipment, and Supplies, with a size standard of 750 employees will be utilized. The Government intends to solicit and negotiate with only one source under the authority of VAAR 819.7008 due to the sole distributorship and continued need for this procurement. No solicitation will be issued. A determination not to compete this proposed contract is based solely within the discretion of the Government. This notice of intent is not a request for quotes or information. Please send all questions regarding this procurement to Richard Leistiko, Contracting Officer at the North Las Vegas, VA NCO21 Contracting Office. richard.leistiko@va.gov

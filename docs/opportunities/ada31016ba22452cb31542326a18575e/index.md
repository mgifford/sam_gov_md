# Upfit LU87 F250 Patrol Vehicle

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 09:58:15.175-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444526Q0023

## Summary

The U.S. Forest Service Law Enforcement and Investigations for Region 13 Subregion 1 have a need to final stage Upfit (6) LU87 Ford F250 Patrol Vehicles. The vendor will be responsible for pick-up and delivery of vehicles, acquisition of supplies and services of upfitting. Each vehicle must function as an emergency vehicle to provide for the security of prisoners and provide law enforcement operational capabilities to the officer.

# (Flight Valves) 2 Way, 540 g Solenoid Valves

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 15:08:11.657-04
- Solicitation Number: 80KSC026Q0915

## Summary

NASA/KSC has a requirement for (28 - Flight Valves) 2 Way, 540 g Solenoid Valves. NASA/KSC intends to issue a sole source purchse order to acquire 2 Way, 540 g Solenoid Valves from Marotta Controls under the authority of FAR 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. The Government intends to acquire a commercial product or service using FAR Part 12. Interested organizations may submit their capabilities and qualifications to perform the effort electronically via email to kodi.d.coles@nasa.gov not later than Eastern Standard Time on May 6, 2026. Such capabilities/qualifications will be evaluated solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct this acquisition on a competitive basis. A determination by the Government not to compete this acquisition on a full and open competition basis, based upon responses to this notice, is solely within the discretion of the Government. NASA Clause 1852.215-84, Ombudsman, is applicable. The Center Ombudsman for this acquisition can be found at : https://www.hq.nasa.gov/office/procurement/regs/Procurement-Ombuds-Comp-Advocate-Listing.pdf

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart

12.6 Description The Mt. Baker-Snoqualmie National Forest has a requirement for the design, modification, delivery, and placement of one (1) 40' x 8' x 9'6" modified high cube shipping container. Destination: Skykomish Ranger Station, 74920 NE Stevens Pass Hwy, Skykomish, WA 98288 Delivery: Mandatory 90-day delivery from date of award (July). Earlier Delivery preferred. Key Technical Requirements: Condition: New (one-trip). Modifications: Interior spray foam (R-10.5+), 100Amp electrical panel, 6 GFCI receptacles, LED interior lighting, exterior motion-detected floodlight, and 18,500 BTU HVAC unit with heat. Site Constraints: Delivery must be via tilt-deck or roll-off truck. Cranes and standard flatbeds are prohibited [RFQ provided]. Evaluation Criteria (FAR 12.602): Award will be made on a "Most Advantageous to the Government" basis (Comparative Evaluation) considering: Technical/Capability: Meeting salient characteristics, conceptual drawings, and delivery confirmation. Past Performance: Recent (last 3 years) relevance and quality. Price: Evaluated for fairness and reasonableness. See attached Statement of Work, modification drawings and pictures of drop off site.

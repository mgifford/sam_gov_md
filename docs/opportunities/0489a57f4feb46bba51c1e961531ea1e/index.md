# Universal Weapons Racks

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:46:51.12-04
- Solicitation Number: FA665626R0002

## Summary

Procure and install Universal Weapons Racks. The designated contractor shall furnish all necessary labor, equipment, tools, appliances, transportation, and materials required to install racks sufficient to house one thousand and eighty (1,080) M4 rifles and four hundred and twenty (420) M18 pistols. In accordance with federal guidelines, sustainable principles, including life-cycle cost-effectiveness practices, shall be integrated into the design, development, and installation of the project.

# Logistics Modernization Integration Support (LMIS) for Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Distribution, Pool 1, Enterprise Facility Modernization, EDC Equipment Modernization

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-30 15:53:25.72-04
- Solicitation Number: SP3300-20-F-1300-P00038

## Summary

In accordance with FAR 6.302-1, the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) anticipates issuing modifications to existing EDC Modernization Delivery Order SP3300-20-F-1300 on a sole source basis to Advantaged Solutions, Inc. for relocation of estops, reapris to catwalk frame, revised testing approach, go live shift, additional software development due to design gaps, and an ATO to meet cybersecurity requirements. Advantage Solutions Inc. has a general contractor on site to make modifications to the facility, to include the requirements listed above. Awarding to another vendor would add unwarranted complexity to the integration of systems. Additional vendors would require them to mobilize multiple times at an increased cost. ASI is already contracted to perform the installation and integration requirements listed above, which need to be appropriately coordinated for the work to be performed. In accordance with FAR 6.302-1, Advantage Solutions, Inc. is the only responsible source capable of providing these requirements at this time. The J&A for the modifications are being posted as part of this notice. This is not a solicitation for proposals or an invitation for bids. The Government does not anticipate receiving responses to this notice of a non-competitive action.

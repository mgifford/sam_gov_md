# MK6 Ammunition Hoist Components and Spares

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-15 12:33:48.883-04

## Summary

McNally Industries, LLC, Grantsburg, Wisconsin, was awarded a $29,600.00 firm-fixed-price modification to Order N00024-26-F-5317 under previously awarded Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) N00024-23-G-5320 for the procurement of end of life components for the MK6 Ammunition Hoist Assembly. This order was issued on a sole source basis pursuant to 10 USC 3204 (a) (1), the property needed by the agency are available from only one responsible source and no other type of property or services will satisfy the needs of the agency. McNally Industries is the only source with the technical knowledge and facilities that is capable of meeting the Governmet's needs without an unacceptable delay.

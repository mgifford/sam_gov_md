- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 07:32:52.787-04

## Summary

******29 January 2026 - Amendment 0001 - This amendment is to provide responses to questions asked by contractors to the Government. Included in this Admenment as a pdf is a question and response document. The Governement intends to award a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Contract. The Contractor shall provide Catholic Administrative Pastoral Life Coordination services. All services shall be conducted in alignment with the Archdiocese for Military Service (AMS) requirements. These services include, but are not limited to, the tasks outlined in Part 5 of the PWS. The Contractor shall perform all required task under this requirement in English. There is no current contractor performing this service in USAG Wiesbaden. Attached is the Performance Work Statement (PWS) for detailed description of this service requirement. RFQ Closing time is 11:00 AM German time on 02 February 2026. ****It would be of great help and support if you inform us on your interest in submitting a quote****

# Texas Army National Guard: Mobile Medical and Dental Readiness Services- IDIQ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 13:00:35.756-04
- Solicitation Number: W912L126RA001

## Summary

This is a pre-solicitation notice. The Texas Army National Guard intends to issue an Request for Proposal (RFP) to award a single firm fixed-price Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity contract for services, non-personal, to provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and quality control necessary, except as specified as Government Furnished, to perform Mobile Medical and Dental Services as defined in the PWS. The ordering period will be for 60 months. This requirement will be full and open competition. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 621498. The tentative date for issuing the solicitation is on-or-about 1 May 2026. Interested offerors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM). To register go to www.SAM.gov. Instructions for registering are on the web page (there is no fee for registration). The solicitation and associated information will be available only from the Contract Opportunities page at SAM.gov.

# M500 Water Main Replacement, Maxwell AFB

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 16:48:35.121-04
- Solicitation Number: PNQS2603400002

## Summary

Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials and equipment necessary to perform all work in connection with Project number PNQS260340 M500 Water Main Replacement Bldg 500 at Maxwell AFB, Alabama . The Magnitude of Construction is between $200,000 and $250,000. Proposals will be evaluated using Lowest Price Technically Acceptable or Trade-off as determined by the Contracting Officer. The applicable wage rate for this project is Davis Bacon Act WD#: AL260093. This is a Total Small Business Set Aside and contractor must be located within 250 mile radius of Maxwell AFB. This is a Solicitation. Please submit your proposals to the POC and alternate POC via email.

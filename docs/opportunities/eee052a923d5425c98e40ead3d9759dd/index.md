## Summary

For Building 852, Unaccompanied Housing #7, at NAS Lemoore, CA The project requires repaving the Parking Lot using the Full Depth Recycling method. This includes new non-reflective thermoplastic striping, new handicapped signage and poles, new concrete parking bumpers, and new xeriscape cobbles—all in accordance with the project drawings and specifications. The Contractor must conduct a pavement survey to ensure proper drainage. The Contractor is responsible for utilizing all recycled materials, including grinding, milled, and pulverized materials, to correct low spots and ensure proper drainage. Additionally, the Contractor must dispose of any remaining recycled materials, waste materials, and debris from the project site to a disposal facility located outside the Naval Air Station Lemoore Base.

# T56 Series 3.0/-15 and 3.5/-15A Joint Follow on Contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-16 11:27:41.176-04
- Solicitation Number: FA812126R0010

## Summary

The Air Force Sustainment center (AFSC) at Tinker AFB, Oklahoma City, OK, is soliciting a repair/overhaul contract for the T56 Engine. The period of performance is 1, 5 year basic with 1, 5 year option, for a total of 10 years. This is a competitive Multiple Award Contract (MAC), non-commercial, acquisition. The contemplated contract will be a firm-fixed price, Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Multiple Award Contract. This requirement supports United States Air Force (USAF), United States Navy (USN) and Forgein Militatry Sales (FMS) customers. Qualification Requirements: Sources must be qualified prior to being considered for award. To be eligible for contract award, the offeror must be an approved source in accordance with (IAW) Revolutionary Federal Acquition Regulation Overhaul RFO 52.209-1, Qualification Requirements. The Government is not required to delay contract award to review pending Source Approval Requests (SARs). Therefore, offerors are encouraged to submit SARs as soon as possible. If the Government has not completed review of a SAR when the contract is awarded, the SAR will be retained, and the source will be reviewed as a possible source for future awards. Moreover, awardees must be Rolls Royce certified. This solicitation is subject to the “Availability of Funds” clause RFO 52.232-18: Funds are not presently available for this contract. The Government’s obligation under this contract is contingent upon the availability of appropriated funds from which payment for contract purposes can be made. No legal liability on the part of the Government for any payment may arise until funds are made available to the Contracting Officer for this contract and until the Contractor receives notice of such availability, to be confirmed in writing by the Contracting Officer. Due to RFO changes, the attachment labeled, SOL_Attach_10_FA8121-26-R-0010 Clauses DAF will have the list of applicable RFO and R-DFARS clauses. Please view all attached documents, for example Instructions to Offeror's, Evaluation Criteria, and Ordering Procedures. The solicitation closing date and time is: May 22, 2026, 3:00pm.

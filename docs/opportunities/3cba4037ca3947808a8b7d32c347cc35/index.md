# CAT AA AP600 Paver Front Forward Assist Installation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 11:39:27.764-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462626CATPaver

## Summary

This is a presolicitation notice issued in accordance with RFO 12.201?1(c)(2). Upon closure of this notice, the solicitation will be issued directly to vendors. The requirement is for the installation of a front forward assist for the CAT AA AP600 Paver currently in operation at Malmstrom Air Force Base, as outlined in the Statement of Work. This requirement is for a commercial product; therefore, in accordance with RFO 16.201(a), a fixed?price contract type will be utilized. Specifically, a firm?fixed?price contract will be employed in accordance with RFO 16.202?2, as the appropriate fixed?price contract vehicle for this acquisition. Award will be based on a determination of fair and reasonable pricing in accordance with RFO 12.204(a). All responsible sources may submit a quotation, bid, or proposal, as appropriate, and all submissions will be considered by 341 CONS.

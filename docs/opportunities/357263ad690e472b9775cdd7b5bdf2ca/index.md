# P26-075 Zeiss Scanbox Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 17:23:03.656-04
- Solicitation Number: FA813226Q0028

## Summary

A replacement sensor in PMXG's current Zeiss Scanbox Series 5. A repair must be done to the existing sensor for the Scanbox to calibrate to its environment. The Scanbox is extremely precise and allows for accurate measurement of parts without an operator present inside of the measurement room.

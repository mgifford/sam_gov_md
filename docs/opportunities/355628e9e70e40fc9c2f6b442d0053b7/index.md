## Summary

Joint Communications Support Element (JCSE), located on MacDill AFB, FL is seeking purchase and installation of four dedicated 20-amp quad receptacles, and two fiber optic conduit runs to the third and uppermost (5th) deck of a 30-foot-high elevated work platform. This requirement is for all labor, tools, materials, and equipment necessary to install new electrical and fiber optic lines to an elevated platform at MacDill AFB.

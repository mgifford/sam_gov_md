## Summary

Roman Catholic (RC) Priest services are essential for the delivery of Religious Ministry to meet the religious requirements for assigned personnel for Naval Station Newport, RI. The contractor will perform services at Naval Station Newport, RI Religious Ministry facilities, authorized locations as required by contract services (e.g., hospitals, funeral homes, cemeteries), or locations determined by the Religious Ministry Team (RMT).

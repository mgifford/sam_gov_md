# B-52H Bomber Outdoor Scale Model - Minot AFB, ND

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 10:40:22.624-04
- Solicitation Number: FA452826QMH01

## Summary

The 5th Maintenance Squadron at Minot AFB (MAFB) is requesting a 1:8 scale model of a B-52 Stratofortress Bomber for outdoor use. The model is for display, and as such, only needs to include exterior details. The model must be built to withstand extreme UV exposure and Minot's extreme winter weather. The Contractor will be required to deliver the model to Minot AFB and provide detailed instructions on the proper assembly of the model and mount. The Contractor is not required to install the model or to be present during its installation. Key Dates: Question Deadline: 7 Apr 26, 5:00pm CDT Quote Deadline: 14 Apr 26, 1:00pm CDT

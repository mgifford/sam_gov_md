# ZERO TURN LAWN MOWER 52 INCH

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 08:27:24.325-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U3039

## Summary

This machine is equipped with a minimum 27-horsepower, 2-cylinder gasoline engine that features electronic fuel injection, eliminating the need for a choke, and includes a replaceable engine oil filter. It comes with a 52-inch fabricated mowing deck, ROPS bar protection, and greaseable spindles. The unit has a hydrostatic transmission with a cooling fan and a hand-operated parking brake.The mower is designed with front and rear frame-mounted suspension with shock absorbers and has a fuel capacity greater than five gallons. For operator comfort and convenience, it includes an adjustable suspension seat with a seat belt, an easy-access floor plate for mower deck spindle access, a fuel level gauge, a USB port, an hour meter, and a cup holder.The machine will have IUID labels and be delivered to Fort Drum, NY, fully assembled and operational. All operator, service, and parts manuals are to be included. The preferred delivery date is 120 days after the order is placed

# 2026 Charleston Airshow P-51 Mustang Warbird Demonstration

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 17:00:46.945-04
- Solicitation Number: FA441826Q0040

## Summary

The contractor is responsible for providing labor, materials, and travel to and from Joint Base Charleston (JB CHS), SC in order to provide two 15-minute P-51 warbird aerobatic flight demonstrations for the 2026 Charleston Airshow. Demonstrations are to occur on May 2, 2026, and May 3, 2026, respectively. All work shall be performed in accordance with Atch-01, P-51 Mustang Warbird Fighter Demonstration SOW_05 Mar 2026. Please be advised that the full requirements, including Instructions to Offerors (FAR 52.212-1) and the basis for evaluation (FAR 52.212-2), are detailed in the solicitation attachments. A thorough review of all attached documents is required for a compliant submission.

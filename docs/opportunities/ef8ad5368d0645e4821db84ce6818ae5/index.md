## Summary

The Orthopedics, Neurosurgery and Podiatry Department require surgical helmets to perform a full range of surgical related activity including intraoperative decortication of bone, drilling and placement of implants. The consolidated operating room equipment (CORE) to console provides a convenient single control center for a multitude of powered instruments. It works across multiple specialties and with advanced drills and instrument portfolios.

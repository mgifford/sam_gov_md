# NATO Request for Information: Provision of Commercial IP Transport

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-19 12:36:23.336-04
- Solicitation Number: RFI-42603961

## Summary

The NATO Communications and Information Agency (NCIA) has issued a Request for Information (RFI) to identify qualified vendors and gather input on potential solutions to support the upcoming provision of a commercial IP Transport Service between NATO and the NATO nations. This RFI is issued for planning purposes only and is not a request for bids. The RFI is part of NCIA’s effort to ensure it has a clear understanding of the marketplace, available capabilities, and potential acquisition strategies. Submission instructions and additional details can be found in the RFI (attached). Do NOT submit your RFI response to the Department of Commerce. Submission instructions and additional details can be found in the RFI. The NCIA reference for this RFI is RFI-42603961, and all correspondence concerning this matter should reference this number. The response deadline is 12:00 hours Central European Time (CET) on 16 May 2026. Your participation in this RFI is greatly appreciated.

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Health & Human Services (HHS) awarded a sole-source order to Drodex, Inc. on April 1, 2026, in the amount of $159,868.24 for staffing support services to the Office of Facilities Management (OFM)/Office of Administration (OA) under the Administration for Strategic Preparedness and Response (ASPR). This order was issued against the contractor’s GSA OASIS+ Contract (No. 47QRCA25DS781) pursuant to the authority of RFO 16.507-6(b)(1), based on the agency’s determination that the need for these services is of such urgency that providing a fair opportunity would result in unacceptable delays. This notice is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a request for competitive proposals or quotations.

# Maintenance of Optic System Sensors

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-16 13:27:58.141-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926OSM

## Summary

The Fleet Logistics Center Norfolk (FLCN), Norfolk, Virginia is seeking information on qualified and experienced sources for planning purposes in an effort to derive one contract to provide maintenance to optics systems in support of meteorological and oceanographic operations globally in accordance with the Draft Performance Work Statement (PWS). THIS SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE IS FOR MARKET RESEARCH AND PLANNING PURPOSES ONLY. IT DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A SOLICITATION AND SHALL NOT BE CONSTRUED AS A COMMITMENT BY THE GOVERNMENT TO AWARD A CONTRACT FORM RESPONSES TO THIS ANNOUNCEMENT. ANY INFORMATION SUBMITTED BY INTERESTED PARTIES IS STRICTLY VOLUNTARY AND NO MONETARY COMPENSATION WILL BE PROVIDED FOR RESPONDSE PREPARATION.

## Summary

On behalf of the Department of Health and Human Services, GSA is conducting market research to understand capabilities related to providing the below requirement: A Request For Information (RFI) has been issued through the GSA Market Research As a Service (MRAS) survey tool www.gsa.gov/mras on behalf of the Department of Health and Human Services, Office of Mission Acquisition Solutions (OMAS), GSA is conducting market research to understand capabilities related to providing the below requirement: HHS/OMAS on behalf of Indian Health Services is conducting market research to identify qualified Indian Small Business Economic Enterprise (ISBEE) and Indian Economic Enterprise (IEE) sources for set-aside. Buy Indian Act (25 U.S.C. 47). BACKGROUND: Requiremnt is for leasing (64) multifunctional printers with 60-months (base plus options) Full Value Maintenance (FVM) located throughout the Gallup Indian Medical Center (GIMC), Tohatchi Healthcare Center (THC) and the Administrative Services Building (ASB) located in New Mexico. These multifunctional printers support daily staff needs as well as provide patients with pertinent information about care, medication, support services and health related documents. The purpose of the RFI is to gather input from industry on the solutions and services available in the marketplace & to seek ISBEE/IEE interest and capabilities.

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information in this notice. This announcement constitutes only solicitation; proposals are being requested, and a written solicitation will be issued. Solicitation number W15QKN-26-Q-A048 is being issued as a request for quotation (RFQ) and incorporates provisions and clauses in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular 202506. This requirement is being completed on a 100% Small Business set-aside basis under NAICS code 333248 with a small business size standard of 750 employees. The United States Army Contracting Command - New Jersey (ACC-NJ), Picatinny Arsenal, NJ 07806-5000 intends to award one (1) firm-fixed-price (FFP) purchase order, on a competitive basis, for the purchase and delivery of an Vacuum System (and Ancillary Pumps) in accordance with the following solicitation requirements, Statement of Work (SoW), Contract Data Requirements List (CDRLs)/DD Form 1423, and Contract Addendum Quality Assurance Requirements.

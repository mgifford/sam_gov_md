# Tionesta Dam Security Fence Installation

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 07:50:42.017-05
- Solicitation Number: W911WN26QA027

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers, Pittsburgh District, has a need for a Commercial Construction Contract for installation of Security Fences at Tionesta Dam, Tionesta, Forest County, Pennsylvania. The scope of work includes removing and replacing existing security fencing at two locations, as well as installing new fencing at one additional location within Tionesta Dam property. At the Control Tower Bridge, the new security fence will be 12 feet high and will include a barbed-wire apron mounted on extension arms, along with gates matching the height and configuration of the fence. New fencing at the Control Tower Tunnel and the Visitor Information Center (VIC) – Fuel Tank Area will consist of 8-foot-high security fencing. In general, the work includes demolition, disposal, and installation of complete security fencing systems in accordance with the drawings, specifications, and site conditions. The NAICS Code is 238990 – All Other Specialty Trade Contractors, and the Small Business Size Standard is $19,000,000.00. This procurement is 100% Set-Aside for Small Business. In accordance with DFARS 252.204-7020, all contractors are required to have a current (i.e., not more than three years old) NIST SP 800-171 DoD Assessment on record in the Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS) to be considered for award.

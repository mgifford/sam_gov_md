# Market Survey for the procurement of NSN 5998-01-660-0959 (CIRCUIT CARD ASSEMB) on the AN/MSQ-135 Mobile Tower System (MOTS) system.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-09 12:53:23.449-04
- Solicitation Number: DLA-Aberdeen-26-032

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency-Aberdeen, on behalf of the Army Integrated Logistics Supply Center (ILSC) – Supply Chain Management Directorate (SCMD) – Strategic Sourcing Directorate (SSD), intends to acquire Parts to support the AN/MSQ-135 Mobile Tower System (MOTS). Requirements Description: The items to be procured are for the AN/MSQ-135 Mobile Tower System (MOTS). The MOTS is a rapidly deployable, air traffic control (ATC) tower and Airfield Lighting System (ALS) that quickly establishes Air Traffic Services (ATS). The system provides digital air/ground communication and digital linkage into the Army Airspace Command and Control (A2C2) and Air Traffic Services (ATS) networks. Please note that this is not a Request for Proposal. This notice is provided for planning purposes and is not to be construed as a commitment by the U.S. Government. The following are the Army’s estimated forecasts* for initial production: NIIN: 01-660-0959 Nomenclature: Circuit Card Assembly QTY: 21 *Quantities listed are estimations and not a guarantee. Final quantities will be determined at time of solicitation. The Government does possess the technical data package (TDP) for the parts. The TDP is available for release. The TDP includes level 2 developmental information. The circuit card assembly was last purchased on 07/10/2024 through delivery order SPRBL124F0122 for a quantity of 14ea. Items and/or Services to Be Procured: NSN: 5998-01-660-0959 NOMENCLATURE: CIRCUIT CARD ASSEMB PART NUMBER: 5402650-001 CAGE: 2J622 Requested Response Information: We request that sources who can supply the listed parts or services to submit information to show their capability to manufacture or supply the same or similar parts/services past repair performance or purchases. At a minimum the support information you submit should include, but is not limited to, the following types of documentation: Questionnaire, drawings, test results, information on previous Government and commercial contracts, data rights, etc. In your response, please include your company name, CAGE code, and a technical point of contract with email address and telephone number. Notice, Response Due Date, and Points of Contact: This is NOT a Request for Quote or Proposal.

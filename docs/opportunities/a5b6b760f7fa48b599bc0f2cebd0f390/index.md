# Residential Generator Managing System (RGMS)

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-19 09:54:20.377-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AQMM26RResidentialGeneratorManagingSystem

## Summary

The anticipated IDIQ contract will require contractors to: • Procure residential standby generators in accordance with Government technical specifications. • Procure compatible Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS). • Procure required accessories and ancillary components. • Prepare equipment for export and international shipment. • Coordinate freight forwarding services. • Provide export documentation and commercial shipping documentation. • Deliver equipment to designated domestic or overseas U.S. Government locations. This requirement is strictly limited to procurement and shipment. Installation, commissioning, inspection, and testing services are NOT included under this anticipated requirement.

## Summary

Amendment 0002 is issued to incorporate the revised Attachment 3, titled Atch 3_SABER_Bid_Schedule_10 Apr 26, which replaces the previous version dated 19 Mar 26. This revision corrects the estimated annual usage to $25 million, based on historical data, from the previously stated $40 million, which is the IDIQ Annual Ceiling. Consequently, Column E of the Bid Schedule has been updated to reflect the Adjusted Annual Amount based on Percent of Estimated Volume. The date and time for receipt of bids remains 27 April 2026, at 1:00 PM Eastern Time. The bid opening will be held on 27 April 2026, at the 633 Contracting Squadron, 14 Burrell Street, Building 67, Langley AFB, VA 23665.

# UTVs for Field Training Operations

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 09:03:04.508-04
- Solicitation Number: F2XJA56097AW01

## Summary

The Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps (AFROTC) at Maxwell AFB, AL 36112-6334, requires four Utility Task Vehicles (UTVs) to support field training operations at Maxwell AFB. These vehicles are essential for the transportation of personnel, equipment, and supplies across varied and rugged terrain within designated training areas. The UTVs are required for field training operations in accordance with (IAW) the attached Statement of Requirement (SOR).

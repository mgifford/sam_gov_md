# Defense Contract Management Agency Cyber Security Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-17 09:03:55.591-04
- Solicitation Number: 642674444

## Summary

DCMA’s Cyber Security Directorate is seeking information for potential sources for skilled contract labor support to achieve the tactical and strategic cybersecurity goals of the DoD and DCMA. Areas of focus include cybersecurity management, compliance, and engineering support and includes services and support related to cybersecurity, including program management, enterprise risk management, compliance auditing, plan of action and milestone (POA&M) management, privacy, cyberspace operations management, systems access controls and management, training, policy, cyber incident analysis & investigations, forensic analysis & investigations, engineering, vulnerability assessment management, and insider threat support.

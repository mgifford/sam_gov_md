# Compressed Gases

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:14:58.32-04
- Solicitation Number: 1305M326Q0071

## Summary

(i) This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1305M26Q0071 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ) for a time-and-materials purchase order providing compressed gases to the Global Monitoring Laboratory (GML) in Boulder, CO. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. (ii) A list of line-item numbers can be found in the SF1449 within the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071, and items, quantities, and units of measure can be found in the attachment labeled Attachment 2 - Pricing Schedule. (iii) Description of requirements for the items to be acquired can be found in the attachment labeled Attachment 1 - Statement of Need (SON). (iv) Dates and place of delivery and acceptance and f.o.b. point can be found in clauses CAR 1352.246-70 and CAR 1352.270-70 within Section 3 - Contract Terms and Conditions of the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071. (v) A list of solicitation provisions that apply to the acquisition can be found in Section 5 - Provisions within the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071. (vi) A list of contract clauses that apply to the acquisition can be found in Section 3 - Contract Terms and Conditions within the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071. (vii) The date, time, and place for receipt of quote and point of contact can be found in the solicitation details within the SAM.gov post.

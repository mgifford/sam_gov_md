# Targeting Drones

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 10:54:15.74-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U2895

## Summary

The objective of this procurement is to acquire low-cost, expendable NDAA-compliant targeting drones, along with their associated controllers and goggles, to support training and evaluation activities. While the Government requires complete systems including aircraft, controllers, and goggles the emphasis on expendability applies only to the drone airframes themselves, which must be affordable, easily replaceable, and suitable for repeated loss during training scenarios, while all control equipment remains non-expendable and reusable.

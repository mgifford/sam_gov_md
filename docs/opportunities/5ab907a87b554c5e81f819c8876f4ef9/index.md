- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Install two (2) additional Vindicator IDS Passive Infrared (PIR) motion detection devices. The contractor shall provide all labor, supervision, and materials required to complete this project. All employees must be qualified to perform installation, electrical and electronic troubleshooting, repair, testing, diagnostics and proprietary programming of Vindicator based annunciators and devices.

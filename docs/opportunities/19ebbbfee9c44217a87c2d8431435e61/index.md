Requirement Description The 99th Readiness Division has a requirement for a non-personal services contract to provide all personnel, supplies, supervision, tools, materials, equipment, and transportation necessary for refuse collection and recycling services at the Bristol Memorial Armed Forces Reserve Center (AFRC) in Bristol, PA. The contractor shall perform to the standards outlined in the attached Performance Work Statement (PWS) and in accordance with all local, state, and federal regulations. 3.

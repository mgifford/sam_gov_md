# Federal Services System Support and Oversight (FSSSO)

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-06 10:41:51.536-05
- Solicitation Number: 270002

## Summary

This program supports CMS in implementing the Affordable Care Act and the No Surprises Act by strengthening Marketplace enrollment assistance, training and supporting in-person Assisters, maintaining critical Salesforce and CMS IT systems, and designing and operating federal dispute resolution and consumer complaint processes to protect Americans from surprise medical bills and improve healthcare transparency.

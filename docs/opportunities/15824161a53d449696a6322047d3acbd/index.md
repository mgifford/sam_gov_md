## Summary

Title: Notice of Intent to Sole Source – Blood Bank Computer System Software Maintenance NAICS Code: 541511 – Custom Computer Programming Services Product Service Code (PSC): 7A20 – IT and Telecom – Application Development Software (Perpetual License Software) Place of Performance: National Institutes of Health Clinical Center 10 Center Drive Bethesda, MD 20892 POTS: 26-002842 Description This notice is issued for informational purposes only and does not constitute a request for quotations. A solicitation document will not be issued, and quotations will not be requested. The National Institutes of Health (NIH), Clinical Center, Office of Purchasing and Contracts (OPC), on behalf of the Department of Transfusion Medicine, intends to award a firm-fixed-price contract on a sole-source basis to Blood Bank Computer System Corporation (BBCS) for a one-year Express Maintenance Software Maintenance Service Agreement. The anticipated period of performance is March 18, 2026, through March 17, 2027. The Department of Transfusion Medicine requires continued support, software services, and licenses for the BBCS proprietary Blood Donor Software Application. This software supports the collection, testing, manufacturing, shipping, and inventory management of blood products at the NIH Clinical Center. The system is essential to the daily operations of the Transfusion Services Section and integrates with the Clinical Center’s Hospital Information System (HIS). The BBCS software also generates operational and inventory reports used to monitor blood supply levels within the hospital and maintains electronic records for both patients and blood donors, which are critical to transfusion operations and regulatory compliance.

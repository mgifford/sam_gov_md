# safeTALK Training for the Trainer (T4T) and safeTALK Materials for Keesler Air Force Base, MS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-20 14:05:56.07-04
- Solicitation Number: FA301026safeTALK

## Summary

This notice serves to announce the intent of the 81st Contracting Squadron to award a sole source personal service contract for safeTALK T4T and safeTALK materials for the 81 Training Wing at Keesler Air Force Base, MS. The proposed contract action is for a service for which the Government intends to solicit and negotiate with only one source under the authority of FAR 6.104-2 and FAR 12.102(a), as it has been deemed that only one source is available to meet this specific requirement. The Government intends to award a sole source contract to LivingWorks Education USA Inc. The applicable NAICS code for this acquisition is 611710 (Educational Support Services) and the size standard is $24M. The acquisition will be awarded as a commercial service using Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services under the authority of FAR part 12. The Government anticipates award of a firm-fixed price contract as a result of soliciting with only one source. The period of performance for this requirement is estimated to be 01 April 2026 – 31 August 2026, with no option periods, for a total performance not to exceed 5 months. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quotes. However, all quotes received by the date specified herein will be considered by the Government. Interested parties may identify their interests and may submit a quote and/or capability statement by 3:00 PM CST, 25 March 2026. Capability statements and quotes shall be sent via email to stacey.zawalski.1@us.af.mil and priscilla.brown.5@us.af.mil. A determination by the Government not to compete the proposed contract, based upon responses to this notice, is solely within the discretion of the Government. Questions concerning this notice can be addressed to the attention of Stacey Zawalski at (228) 377-1829 or by email at stacey.zawalski.1@us.af.mil.

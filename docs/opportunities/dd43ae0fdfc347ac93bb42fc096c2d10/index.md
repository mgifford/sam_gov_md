# Gas Ventilation Emergency Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 14:10:27.548-04

## Summary

Emergency repair for Gas and Ventilation Equipment that utilize Natural Gas ignition for heating during operation, associated ventilation and exhaust systems. This Performance Work Statement (PWS) outlines the requirements for equipment emergency maintenance services and repair parts to be performed by the Contractor. Serviced at the Anniston Army Depot (ANAD) Anniston Alabama.

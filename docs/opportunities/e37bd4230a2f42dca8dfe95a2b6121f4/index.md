## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Veterans Health Administration, Network Contracting Office (NCO 2), Buffalo, New York intends to solicit quotes from small business contractors for service of a Canon Imageress printer in support of the Lyons VA Medical Center 151 Knollcroft Rd., Building 1, Rm 218, Lyons, NJ 07939 The VA anticipates issuing an RFQ for this requirement on or about February 6, 2026, to be posted to https://www.sam.gov. This procurement will be solicited in accordance with FAR Part 12 Commercial Items and FAR Part 19 Small Business. Interested contractors will be responsible for downloading copies of the solicitation (and any subsequent amendments) from that website, and no hard copies will be made available. The solicitation will provide instructions to contractors about the quote submission process. Perspective offerors must be registered in System for Award Management (SAM) at the time of submission of quotes. Contractors may obtain SAM information at https://www.sam.gov. NAICS Code is 811210 Computer and Office Machine Repair and Maintenance with a Small Business Size Standard of $34M. The Product/Service Code is J074. The solicitation will be a small business set aside. All responsible small businesses under the identified NAICS may submit quotes to be considered by the Agency. Questions concerning this announcement should be directed to the Contract Specialist. Telephone requests for information or questions will NOT be accepted. Please email requests or questions to Stephanie Decker at the following e-mail address: Stephanie.Decker2@va.gov and ensure that the subject line includes: RFQ no. 36C24226Q0323. Contracting Office Address: Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office (NCO2), 3495 Bailey Avenue - Building 3, 2nd floor Buffalo, NY 14215.

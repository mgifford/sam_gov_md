## Summary

N00019-25-R-0018-0001 | 24 March 2026 Through this FOSR amendment, the Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR) Patuxent River, MD hereby announces the cancellation of solicitation notice N00001925R0018 for the MH-60R/S PMI Maintenance and Squadron Support in Rota, Spain as of March 24, 2026.

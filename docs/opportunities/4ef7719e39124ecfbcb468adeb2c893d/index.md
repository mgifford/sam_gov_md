# NATO Business Opportunity: Finance Governance Contractor Services

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-21 16:53:20.827-04
- Solicitation Number: RFP-ACT-JATEC-01-26

## Summary

NATO Joint Force Training Center (JFTC) has issued a Request for Proposal (RFP) on behalf of the Joint Analysis, Training and Education Centre (JATEC) in Bydgoszcz, Poland. The JATEC is seeking the following Finance Governance Contractors: 1) a Travel Operations and NATO Financial System Support Specialist and 2) a Financial Operations Contractor for Non-NATO Personnel. The reference for the RFP is RFP-ACT-JATEC-01-26 and all correspondence regarding the RFP should include that reference. Type of Contract: Level of Effort, Fixed Price Partial Bidding is allowed. The intent is for the competitive selection of the lowest priced compliant bid(s). Bids shall be received at the JFTC Contracting Office NLT 27 May 2026, 13:00 hours, CET. REQUIREMENT Please see attached. BECOMING ELIGIBLE TO BID A Declaration of Eligibility (DOE) is NOT required for participation. All of the information needed for bidding is included in the RFP.

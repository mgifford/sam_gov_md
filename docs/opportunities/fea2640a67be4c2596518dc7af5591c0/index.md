# Generator Repair

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 17:40:12.846-04
- Solicitation Number: 127EAT26Q0026

## Summary

This solicitation is for the repair of a Kohler Diesel Generator, Model Number: 230RE0ZJE. The scope includes the removal and replacement of the blown circuit board and fuse, necessary to programming and testing to ensure proper functionality of the unit. The project is located at: Acton Work Center 33708 Crown Valley Rd. Acton, CA 93510 A pre-bid tour for this project is not scheduled, however the site is open for to visit and is encouraged. Questions should be submitted by 3/27/26 to christopher.malley@usda.gov Amendment 0001 is posted to provide response to a question received regarding which circuit board is to be replaced; Attachments 5 & 6 are incorporated as part of the response and the quote due date is extended to 4/8/26 @ 5:00 PM PT. Amendment 0002 is posted to further clarify that the term "Circuit Board" to be replaced is referencing the entire Kohler Controller Assembly GM10193-6. Additionally, the fuse to be replaced is 5 AMP, 250VAC. Quotes are now due by 10 April 2026 @ 1:00 PM PT and may be e-mailed to: Government Point of Contact: Christopher Malley, CS at christopher.malley@usda.gov

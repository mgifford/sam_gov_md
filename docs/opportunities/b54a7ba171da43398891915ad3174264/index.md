# School Information Center Materials Jobber

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 17:45:44.162-04
- Solicitation Number: HE125426RE021

## Summary

In accordance with the PWS, DoDEA requires customizable information center materials selection and acquisition services to ensure the collection needs of each school are met. The selection and acquisition services include a customizable web-based selection and acquisition tool, pre-processing, Machine-Readable- Cataloguing (MARC) records, shipping, invoicing, data/reports management, technical support, and related product training. DoDEA also requires a Contractor hosted eBook platform that can interface with MackinVision and can authenticate through Google login. DoDEA expects to make one award. Please see Solicitation 26RE021_SOL_041426 and accompanying attachments for additional information.

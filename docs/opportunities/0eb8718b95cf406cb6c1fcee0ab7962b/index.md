## Summary

Notice to Offeror(s)/Supplier(s): Funds are not presently available for this contract. No award will be made under this solicitation until funds are available. The Government?s obligation under this contract is contingent upon the availability of appropriated funds from which payment for contract purposes can be made. No legal liability on the part of the Government for any payment may arise until funds are made available to the Contracting Officer for this contract and until the Contractor receives notice of such availability, to be confirmed inwriting by the Contracting Officer. The Government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation, either before or after the closing date. In the event the Government cancels this solicitation, the Government has no obligation to reimburse an offeror for any costs.This is a Pre-solicitation notice for a construction requirement to build a new 120ft tower at the Poinsett Range and move the existing equipment to the new tower in accordance with the Statement of Objectives. This requirement is for a construction contract to replace an existing structure and is expected to increase coverage to the range by reducing dead spots. This project will include a new tower, antennas, antenna cables, ice bridge between the shelter and antenna, and ground ring tied into the shelter ground. The Contractor shall be responsible for planning, designing, constructing, and managing all activities necessary to achieve the objectives of the attached Statement of Objectives (SOO). The Government will not direct or control the Contractor?s construction means and methods but will evaluate performance based on compliance with the objectives and acceptance criteria defined herein. The purpose of the attached SOO is to define the objectives, performance outcomes, and constraints for the construction effort while allowing Offerors the flexibility to propose effective and innovative design and construction solutions. The Contractor shall develop the detailed technical approach and supporting documentation necessary to meet the objectives of the SOO. Please refer to attached SOO and all attachments for details.Solicitation Date: To be determined.

# Combined Synopsis/Solicitation for Laundry and Dry-Cleaning Services Fort Hood, Texas

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 12:34:47.608-05
- Solicitation Number: W9115126QA0018

## Summary

This is a non-personnel services contract to provide Laundry and Dry-Cleaning services. The Contractor shall operate and manage the Laundry Distribution and Collection Point(s) for the issue, receipt, requisition, exchange, warehousing, and turn-in of linens IAW AR 210-130, Laundry and Dry-Cleaning Operations; AR 710-4, Property Accountability; and other regulatory requirements. Timely and accurate submission of deliverables is essential, and all linens must be thoroughly cleaned, properly folded and bundled, and free of damage. The contractor is also responsible for cleaning specialty items like sleep systems, ensuring they meet specific standards for cleanliness and condition. All questions must be submitted via email no later than 11:00 AM (CST) on March 3, 2026 to both the Contract Specialist ruth.adewole.civ@army.mil and the Contract Officer justin.j.mansfield.mil@army.mil.

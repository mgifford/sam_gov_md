## Summary

Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ), Single Award Task Order Contract (SATOC) for Assistant Secretary of the Army for Installations, Energy, and Environment (ASA(IEE)) Environmental Program and Training Support. The purpose of this contract is to provide services supporting the Installation, Environmental, and Energy (IEE) programs. The support services include but not limited to environmental program support, training support and systems support necessary to achieve the mission of the Assistant Secretary of the Army for Installations, Energy, and Environment (ASA(IEE)). In support of program realignment efforts within DoD, these services may also include support for migration, calibration, and sustainment of DoD Department, Component, and/or Agency level data in WEBCASS-E which is the Army?s Systems of Record (SoR) for all Army Environmental Programs. Refer to Section C and Appendix A(Seed Task Order).

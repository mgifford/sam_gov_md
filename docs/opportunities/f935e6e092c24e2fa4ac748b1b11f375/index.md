# Government Responses to DRAFT Request for Proposal Industry Feedback - Project Management Mounted Armored Vehicles

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 08:03:53.567-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CH25R0182-USG_Responses_QRM

## Summary

DISTRIBUTION STATEMENT A. Approved for public release; distribution is unlimited. This announcement is a follow-up to the pre-solicitation Notice W912CH-25-R-0182, originally posted on March 6, 2026. The purpose of this announcement is to provide the Government's official responses to questions received from industry regarding the Draft Request for Proposal (RFP) for the Project Management Mounted Armored Vehicle Logistic Fielding Support Services requirement. The Government thanks all parties for their timely submission of questions. All questions submitted by the response deadline of March 13, 2026, have been reviewed. A consolidated list of questions and the corresponding USG answers is provided as an attachment to this notice, titled " Question_Response_Matrix_PM_MAV_LFSS_RFP." This announcement is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a formal RFP. The Government is not seeking or accepting proposals at this time, or any additional feedback. Any information provided in the attached Q&A document is subject to change. It remains the responsibility of all interested parties to continue to monitor SAM.gov for any future notices, amendments, or the release of the official RFP for this requirement.

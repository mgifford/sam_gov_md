# Maintenance and Repair of Washer and Dryers, Fort Bragg NC

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 11:39:35.076-04
- Solicitation Number: W91247-26-Q-A009

## Summary

AMENDMENT 0001: The purpose of this amendment is to extend the closing date to 19 March 2026 and incorporate the Government responses to Contractor questions. Please see attached. Note all PWS changes are marked in red. The Mission and Installation Contracting Command – Fort Bragg (MICC-FB) has a requirement from the Directorate of Public Works (DPW) to provide maintenance and repair of Government owned washers and dryers, to include but not limited to, Maytag, Speed Queen, and Whirlpool washer and dryers for Pope Army Airfield, Camp Mackall, Fort Bragg, and Aberdeen Training Facility (ATF). See attached documents for further details. Please note Technical Exhibits 1 through 6 are incorporated into the PWS.

# Nellis AFB Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-05 23:09:19.907-04
- Solicitation Number: FY26SPLNellisAFBIndustryDay

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Los Angeles District (SPL) will hold an industry day on Thurs, March 5th, 2026 from 9:30A to 5P to discuss upcoming business opportunities at Nellis AFB, NV. Location: Home2 Suites by Hilton, Las Vegas Convention Center, 755 Sierra Vista Dr, LV, NV 89169 Tentative Agenda (subject to change): -9:00AM – 9:30AM: Check-in/Sign-in/Settled -9:30AM-10:30AM: Aircraft Bed Down Program at Nellis AFB (See W912PL26S0004 for Reference) -10:30A – 11:00AM: Break/Transition -11:00AM-12:00PM: Other Business Opportunities at Nellis AFB -Lunch On Your Own: 12:00PM – 1:30PM -1:30PM – 2:00PM – Check-in/Sign-in/Settled -2:00PM-3:00PM: Aircraft Bed Down at Nellis AFB (See W912PL26S0004 for Reference)(Repeat of Morning Session) -3:00PM – 3:30PM: Break/Transition -3:30PM-4:30PM: Other Business Opportunities at Nellis AFB (Repeat of Morning Session) -4:30PM – 5:00PM: Breakdown/Close Note: -No refreshments will be provided before the meetings or throughout the day. -Industry Day slides will be available on this posting starting Mar 9 for 30 days -The current SPL forecast can be found at: https://www.spl.usace.army.mil/Business-With-Us/Small-Business-Program/ -RSVP for time slots for AM and PM times are on a first come first served basis. -USACE reserves the right to modify afternoon sessions based on level of interest/participation. -No industry day MS Teams simulcast Please RSVP to Roger Minami at: kinya.r.minami@usace.army.mil. Please RSVP early as seating is limited. Multiple individuals representing a single firm are permitted. Due to room size, SPL reserves the right to limit the size of your party When sending in your RSVP, please include the following info: 1.) Names of those within your party 2.) Business titles 3.) Email addresses and phone numbers 4.) Is your firm interested in attending the AM or PM session? 5.) Your firm’s interest in: a.) Aircraft Bed down program b.) other opportunities at Nellis AFB c.) Both 6.) Your firm’s interest in responding to potential solicitations as: a.) Prime contractor for the aircraft bed down program – primarily work requiring large single/aggregate bonding capacity (see magnitude ranges in W912PL26S0004); b.) Prime contractor for other Nellis AFB opportunities. Indicate the following interest: i. primarily targeting small $100K - $1M; ii. medium $1M - $10M; or iii. large requirements $10M - $25M+ c.) Prime contractor for both the aircraft bed down program and other Nellis AFB opportunities d.) Subcontractor opportunities for the Aircraft Bed down Program e.) Subcontractor opportunities for other Nellis AFB opportunities This is not a solicitation announcement for proposals and no contract will be awarded from this announcement. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this announcement or any follow-up information requests. No telephone calls will be accepted requesting a bid package or solicitation because there is no bid package or solicitation available. In order to protect the procurement integrity of any future procurement, if any, that may arise from this announcement, information regarding the technical points of contact will not be given at this time and no appointments for presentations will be made at this time. No reimbursements will be made for participation in any industry days. Items above subject to change

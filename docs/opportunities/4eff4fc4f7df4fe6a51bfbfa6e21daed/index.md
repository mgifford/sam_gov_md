# Eighteen Mile Creek SATOC

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 15:56:54.869-04
- Solicitation Number: W912DQ26RA031

## Summary

This solicitation is for a SATOC to support recurring remedial action efforts at the Eighteen Mile Creek Superfund Site, Operable Unit 1 (OU1), OU2, and OU4. The overall objective is to perform excavation and transportation and disposal of lead-contaminated soil that exceed the Record of Decision (ROD) specified cleanup levels.

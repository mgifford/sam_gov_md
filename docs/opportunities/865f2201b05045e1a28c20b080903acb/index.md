# RFI: Design-Build Tier 2 Mobile Command Post (MCPV) Gooseneck Trailer Systems with Integrated C4 Capabilities

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 08:27:44.065-04
- Solicitation Number: 26-R-0000194

## Summary

Change 4/15/26 - Due date discrepancy in RFI attachment has been updated to match due date on SAM.gov of 5/4/26. The Government is conducting market research to identify qualified vendors capable of providing design-build solutions for Tier 2 Gooseneck Mobile Command Post Vehicle (MCPV) Trailer systems. The anticipated requirement will support a wide range of mission-critical operations requiring rapidly deployable mobile command, control, communications, and computing (C4) capabilities. The Government is considering a performance-based acquisition approach under a potential Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract. The MCPV systems are expected to be self-contained, highly mobile platforms integrating advanced communications technologies (including satellite, RF, and cellular), onboard power generation, and configurable workspace to support approximately 15–20 workstations. This RFI seeks industry input on: Technical capabilities and innovative design approaches Integration of communications, power, and vehicle systems Production capacity and delivery timelines Lifecycle sustainment and support strategies Recommended contract structure and performance metrics The Government is particularly interested in solutions that emphasize modularity, scalability, interoperability, and long-term maintainability. This notice is issued for information and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation. The Government does not intend to award a contract based on this RFI and will not reimburse respondents for any costs incurred in preparing a response.

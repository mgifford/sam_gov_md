## Summary

The 60th Maintenance Squadron's Crash Damaged/Disabled Aircraft Recovery (CDDAR) team at Travis Air Force Base, California, has a requirement for 4 (four) Storage/Travel Cases. The required model is an ATA-300 Part # 84-7465-15T Lifting Bag Case - 15 Ton. These cases allow secure storage and transportation of critical crash recovery items used for stabilizing and lifting capabilities for multiple types of aircraft, including C5, C-17, and KC-46.

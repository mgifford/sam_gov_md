# Aviation Contracting Industry Day

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-13 13:29:21.518-04
- Solicitation Number: SN-AV-2026-02

## Summary

The US Forest Service will be hosting an Industry Day event in Boise on May 12, 2026 (9AM to 4PM) to discuss Aviation Contracting issues in the wildland fire support realm. We plan to have participation from our partner Agencies (DOI) and have a variety of US Forest Service personnel from the program and contracting side. An agenda and more details on the event and how to RSVP will be posted as soon as they've been finalized. The event will be hosted at: Jack's Urban Meeting Place 1000 W Myrtle St Boise, ID 83702

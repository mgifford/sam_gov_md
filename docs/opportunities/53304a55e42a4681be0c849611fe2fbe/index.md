# Fuels Preventative and Corrective Maintenance - Pacific Ocean Division - Far East District (POF)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 16:23:16.856-04
- Solicitation Number: W912DY26RA005

## Summary

The United States Army Corps of Engineers, Engineering and Support Center Huntsville, Alabama (CEHNC) Fuels program requires a combination acquisition strategy/acquisition plan, hereby referred to as AS/AP, for the timely and efficient procurement of preventative and corrective maintenance services in support of the Defense Logistics Agency - Energy (DLA-E) (hereby referred to as DLA) in the Pacific Ocean Division- Far East District (POF). Specifically, work will be performed in South Korea. The location is based on the current USACE military programs boundaries and the customer (i.e. Army, Air Force, Navy, etc.).

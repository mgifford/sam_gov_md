# RFP - Medical Supplies

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 14:13:40.324-04
- Solicitation Number: PR-000648

## Summary

The FBI intends to award a single award, Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract for medical supplies and equipment to maintain and stock items included in the medical deployment kits, as well as other medical supplies required to support the Occupational Health and Operational Medicine program. Please see the attached RFP for complete details and instructions. Questions pertaining to the solicitation are due by 5:00 PM/EST on Monday, April 13th, 2026. All questions shall be submitted using the enclosed Offeror Question Submission Attachment. Proposals must be received by 5:00 PM/EST on Monday, April 27th, 2026. Amendment 1 - 4/16/26 - Question and Answers document attached. RFP_Medical Supplies.V1 attached.

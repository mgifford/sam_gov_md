## Summary

24 April 2026 update: AM0003 added ACM survey report to specifications. FY26 REPAIR WHOLE HOUSE, BUILDING 1255, MCAS IWAKUNI, This project revitalizes six officer (O1-O3 and W1-W3) family housing units located in the North Side Housing Area at Marine Corps Air Station Iwakuni (MCASI), Japan. Sustainment work includes, but not limited to, exterior painting; repairing and painting/tiling all interior walls and ceilings; repairing cracked/ broken concrete sub-base. Work also includes the following replacement: deteriorated roofing and flashing; the metal roof, drains and gutters or the enclosed service court; disfigured window and door screens; all interior doors and hardware; closet shelving; kitchen and bathroom cabinets, fixtures and hardware, range hoods, garbage disposals; all vinyl composition tile and sheet vinyl materials; all sewage/domestic drain piping, cold and hot water piping, heating and cooling piping, sewage/domestic vent piping and associated hardware such as gauges, valves and monitoring equipment; all lighting fixtures, switches, electrical outlets and wiring to meet the National Electrical Code (NEC); as well as TV, telephone and internet access receptacles and wiring; exterior lighting fixtures and switches. Modernization work includes, but not limited to, installation of additional lighting fixtures, electrical outlets and TV, telephone and internet access receptacles and wiring.

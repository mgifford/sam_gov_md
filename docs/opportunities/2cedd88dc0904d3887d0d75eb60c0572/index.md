# Raw Materials

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 15:35:55.534-04
- Solicitation Number: FA480126Q0005

## Summary

Base Course: 823 RHS requires 2500 tons of Base Course material (3/4",

143.6 PCF, +80 CBR) for training operations on Holloman AFB. Deliveries of material must be accompanied by copies of certified waybills and delivery tickets. Interested parties who submit a bid must also submit laboratory test reports proving that their proposed materials meet the American Society of Testing and Materials standards (ASTM). Proposed samples must conform with ASTM D75/D75M. Coarse aggregate that is supplied from more than one source must be stockpiled separately. The Government will perform initial tests upon receipt of the first delivery to ensure the proposed material meets all requirements specified in the Statement of Work. This testing will be completed for each source (or batch) if materials from more than one source are proposed. Desired delivery date is 11 May 2026. Concrete: 823 RHS requires approximately 2100 Cubic Yards of wet concrete mix/Type V Cement/5,000-PSI rated. Desired delivery start date is 15 June 2026. Asphalt & Prime Coat: 823 requires approximately 350 Tons of P401 Hot Mix Asphalt and approximately 380 Gallons of Asphalt Prime Coat Oil. Desired delivery start date is 11 August 2026. PLEASE see attached Solicitation and SOW.

# Gardening Services for the U.S. Consulate General in Matamoros, Mexico

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 15:43:43.986-04
- Solicitation Number: 19MX5026R0139

## Summary

The U.S. Consulate General Matamoros seeks a contractor to provide comprehensive gardening services, including all necessary labor, materials, equipment, and insurance. The contract is fixed-price per month for routine services, with an additional fixed rate per square meter for temporary or extra services. Work will begin approximately ten days after contract award and submission of required insurance certificates, unless an earlier start is agreed upon. For more information, offerors should carefully review all solicitation attachments. All questions should be submitted no later than April 23rd, 2026.

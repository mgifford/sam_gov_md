## Summary

Fort Drum, New York has a requirement for Fitness Equipment Service and Repair. A site visit will be conducted on Friday March 27, 2026 at 10:00 AM EST. The meeting place will be held at FALCON HOLISTIC HEALTH AND FITNESS (FALCON H2F), located at 20460 Anaconda Road, Fort Drum, NY 13602. To access Fort Drum, contractors shall be restricted to physically sign in at Paul Cerjan Gate for entry, unless they have a Department of Defense (DoD) issued ID card, a local temporary pass, or a local access badge (LAB). For contractors who do not have access will need to visit the welcome center Bldg. 11008 outside of Paul Cerjan (Main) gate with a picture identification (must be Real ID compliant), registration, and proof of insurance to gain a one-day pass. The visitor center is open 5:30 a.m. to 10:00 p.m. seven days a week. If you have any issues, please call Kayla Rogers at 520-852-2764. Questions are due no later than March 30, 2026, at 10:00 AM EDT. Quotes are due on April 1, 2026 at 10:00 AM EST. Please see Solicitation_W911S226QA035, attachments and exhibits.

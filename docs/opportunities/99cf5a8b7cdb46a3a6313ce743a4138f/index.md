## Summary

Hanford Tank Waste Operations & Closure (H2C) is the Integrated Tank Disposition Contractor (ITDC) and requires civil surveying and dome deflection surveying services to support the scheduled work within mission requirements. These services are needed in support of H2C’s prime contract with the U.S Department of Energy.

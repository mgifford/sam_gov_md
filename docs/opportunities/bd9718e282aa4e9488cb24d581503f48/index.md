# J--NORTH DAKOTA PCB REFURBISHMENT

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-25 14:17:53.76-04

## Summary

NORTH DAKOTA PCB REFURBISHMENT. Special Notice: It is the Western Area Power Administration's (WAPA's) intent to award a purchase order on a sole source basis to Bennett-Alvarado, Inc. for PCB refurbishment. Refurbishment work/modifications will be performed at the MEPPI factory in Warrendale, PA No solicitation will be posted. THIS IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITIVE PROPOSALS/QUOTES. Responsible sources may submit a capability statement in response to this requirement, which shall be considered by the Agency. Capability statements must be received no later than the due date and time stated on this notice and shall include documentation on the ability to satisfy the Government’s requirements. A determination by the Government not to compete based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Questions concerning this notice may directed to M Kris Pfeifle at pfeifle@wapa.gov and must be in electronic format.

# DLA FAMILY DAY EVENT PLANNING & OPERATIONS - FT BELVOIR MWR

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 16:00:33.023-04
- Solicitation Number: NAFUA1-26-Q-0008

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency Family & Morale, Welfare, and Recreation (FMWR) invites qualified offerors to submit quotes in response to the Request for Quote (NAFUA1-26-Q-0008) to provide all labor, equipment, materials, and incidentals for the Andrew T. McNamara Headquarters Complex Family Day event operations at the Headquarters Complex, Fort Belvoir, Virginia. All questions are due to the Contracting Officer in writing no later than 3:00 PM EDT on April 20, 2026. All quotes are due no later than 4:00 PM EDT on April 24, 2026. Site visits and virtual discussions may be requested. Please reference Section L of the solicitation for further details. Should you have any questions regarding the solicitation, please reach out to the Contracting Officer at michael.3.thompson@dla.mil or by phone at 571-643-3754.

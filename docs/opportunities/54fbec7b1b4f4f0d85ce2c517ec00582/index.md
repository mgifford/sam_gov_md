## Summary

This is a Special Notice of intent to award on a sole source basis. This is not a solicitation. The Department of Veterans Affairs intends to negotiate a requirement of Emergency Interim Medical Waste Disposal Services for the VA White River Junction Healthcare System, VT, VA Maine Healthcare System, ME, and VA Providence Healthcare System, RI. The applicable NAICS code is 562112. The PSC/FSC is S222.

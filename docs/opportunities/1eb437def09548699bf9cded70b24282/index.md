# Question to the Contractors on MIOSS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 13:22:58.101-04
- Solicitation Number: MIOSS-ID-2026-Suggestions-for-Sections-L-M

## Summary

The Government is requesting the input from our Contracting Community on the Medical Simulation Training Center (MSTC) Instructor Operator Support Services (MIOSS) contract. The Government is considering utilizing a Trade Off – Best Value contracting approach for the MIOSS effort. Interested parties are requested to submit recommendations for formulation of Section L (Instructions, Conditions, and Notices to Offerors) and Section M (Evaluation Factors for Award) and its criteria by using the Draft PWS attached. Submissions should identify and justify evaluation factors that will achieve the following objectives: Deliver tangible or intangible benefits to the Government. Drive operational efficiencies in fulfilling the MIOSS requirements. Ensure contractual and operational flexibility to accommodate evolving medical support requirements over the period of performance. Since the Governement is looking to release the Draft RFI in the next week or two, all recommendations and related correspondence shall be submitted in writing, prior to 10:00 am EST, on 24 April 2026 to: usarmy.orlando.cpe-st3.mbx.mioss@army.mil In subject field please "Suggestions for Section L&M" and your company name. - - DISCLAIMER: THIS MESSAGE IS ISSUED SOLELY FOR INFORMATION AND PLANNING PURPOSES ONLY.

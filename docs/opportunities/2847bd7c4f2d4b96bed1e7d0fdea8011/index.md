## Summary

Army Contracting Command – Rock Island (ACC-RI) issued the Tradewinds Solutions Marketplace (TSM) long-term, open call on 31 October 2022 for new and novel technology solutions to support the DoD in advancing research, development, prototyping, and adoption of Artificial Intelligence (AI)/Machine Learning (ML), digital and data analytics capabilities. This initiative is governed by the Chief Digital and Artificial Intelligence Office (CDAO). The TSM solicits, collects, assesses, and curates AI/ML, digital and data analytics solutions, and makes those solutions available through rapid acquisition methods DoD-wide. The TSM serves Government laboratories, industry and academic organizations, and individual developers by providing a forum to showcase their relevant research, products, and services to prospective Government customers, and serves Department of Defense (DoD) by providing a forum to access AI/ML, digital and data analytics solutions and rapidly ingest game-changing technology solutions. The TSM is a digital environment of competed video pitches. The video solutions housed within the marketplace have been assessed and vetted through competitive procedures which conform to the competition requirements of 10 U.S.C. 3026, 3458, 4021 and 4022, Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR)/Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS) Part 35, and Class Deviation 2022-O0007, implementing Section 803 of the Fiscal Year (FY) 2022 National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA), and thus are readily available to be viewed, selected, and awarded by DoD activities.

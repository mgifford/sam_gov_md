# Medical Gas Equipment PM Services

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 17:24:09.162-04
- Solicitation Number: ACQ-IHS-2026-U-0009

## Summary

The objective of this Scope of Work is to comply with all preventative maintenance, corrective maintenance, and emergency repairs, of items on the medical gas equipment inventory, in accordance with and as required by NFPA 99 (2012). All testing, inspection, and maintenance activities are to be documented in such a way as to comply with TJC Standard PE.04.01.01 EP #3, Medical Gas and Vacuum Systems are Inspected and Tested. See attached SOW.

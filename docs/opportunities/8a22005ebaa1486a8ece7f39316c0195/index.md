# Bowling Center Lane Walker - Notice of Intent to Single Source

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-29 22:29:48.439-04
- Solicitation Number: FA520526QB045

## Summary

The 35th Contracting Squadron, Misawa Air Base, Japan intends to issue a single-source procurement action to American Bowling Service, Inc. to provide a Bowling Center Lane Walker to the Walmsley Bowling Center, 35th Force Support Squadron. The proposed contract is for items for which the Government intends to solicit from only one source in accordance with (IAW) RFO 13.101(b) and RFO 6.103-1(d). This is a notice of intent to award a single source procurement action and is not a request for competitive proposals. However, the Government will consider all proposals received within 7 days after the date of publication of this notice. Firms who want to challenge the single source must submit an interest letter that demonstrates your firm’s ability to provide an equivalent supply/service as described above. Interested parties must provide a rationale as to why they should be considered.

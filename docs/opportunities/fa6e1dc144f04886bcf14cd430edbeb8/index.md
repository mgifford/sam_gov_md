# Steel Castings Horse Box, Quoin Block, and Quoin Stay

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 11:18:04.186-04
- Solicitation Number: W912EK26RCAST

## Summary

The United States Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) - Rock Island District has a requirement for steel castings finished to specifications and drawings for Horse Box, Quoin Block, and Quoin Stay for Illinois Waterway Project Office. Additional information on the requirement will be provided when the solicitation is issued. USACE- Rock Island District intends to advertise this requirement as 100% Small Business Set-Aside. A single Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Contract is anticipated to be awarded to the lowest price offeror who conforms to the specifications and drawings of the requirement. Any companies interested in this requirement please provide capability statements via email to contract specialist Carrie Holub at carrie.l.holub@usace.army.mil. The presolicitation notice is for information purposes only and is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government to procure any supplies or services, or for the Government to pay for the information received. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this presolicitation notice or any follow up information requested. Consequently, no solicitation is being issued at this time. Therefore, no award will be made as a result of providing this information.

## Summary

The purpose of this solicitation is for 6th Medical Group courier service requirement, the contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform to pick up and deliver medical supplies, medical records, mail, laboratory specimens, diagnostic imaging films, to and from their locations listed in the Performance Work Statement (PWS).

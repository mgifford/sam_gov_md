# Request for Quote (RFQ) - EDCO 30 in Self-Propelled Gasoline Concrete Saw (ED31400)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 13:09:46.516-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U2926

## Summary

The purpose of this amendment is to update the Schedule of Supplies/Services to add a new Contract Line Item Number CLIN 002 for one 1 30 inch concrete saw blade. Clarify the Salient Characteristics for CLIN 001 EDCO SS30 30 Inch Gasoline Self-Propelled Concrete Saw or Equal to explicitly note that the blade is not included with the base unit and must be quoted separately under CLIN 002.Establish the minimum firm requirements and salient characteristics for both CLIN 001 and CLIN 002 to facilitate Brand Name or Equal evaluations. All other terms and conditions of the solicitation remain unchanged.

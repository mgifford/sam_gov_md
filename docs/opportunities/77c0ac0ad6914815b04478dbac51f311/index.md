## Summary

Notice of Intent to Sole Source NAICS: 541620 Environmental Consulting Services PSC: F113 Environmental Systems Protection Invasive Species Subject: Notice of Intent to Award a Sole Source Purchase Order to Systech Water Resources Inc. The Bureau of Reclamation, Mid-Pacific Region, intends to issue a sole source award to Systech Water Resources Inc. for specialized environmental technical support services related to the Watershed Analysis Risk Management Framework (WARMF) model. This action is being taken in accordance with FAR 13.106 1(b)(1), which permits soliciting from a single source when only one responsible source is reasonably available and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. Requirement Description Reclamation requires 80 hours of technical support services to provide real time analytical and modeling assistance for managers and stakeholders using the WARMF watershed model. These services support operational decision making related to flow and salinity monitoring within the watershed. Work will include, but is not limited to: Updating model inputs based on real time data Preparing and releasing model updates Assisting Reclamation staff with interpretation of model outputs Providing training and technical guidance on use of WARMF tools and results Systech Water Resources Inc. is the original developer and long standing technical authority for the WARMF model. Their proprietary expertise, model specific knowledge, and access to the underlying framework make them the only known vendor capable of supporting the required forecasting, calibration, and real time operational modeling functions needed for Reclamation's program mission. This is NOT a Request for Competitive Quotes This notice is for informational purposes only. It does not constitute a solicitation or request for proposals. However, responsible sources may submit a capability statement demonstrating their ability to meet this requirement. All submissions must be received by the date/time listed in this posting to be considered. Please send responses to Sarah Anaya sarah_anaya@ios.doi.gov .The Government will review any responses received; however, a determination by the Government to compete this requirement rests solely with the Contracting Officer.

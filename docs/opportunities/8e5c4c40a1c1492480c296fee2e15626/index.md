# Intent to Sole Source Robotic Prescription Dispensing System Lease and Support for Portland Area Indian Health Services

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-17 19:06:14.861-04
- Solicitation Number: 75H71326Q00020

## Summary

The Indian Health Service (IHS), Portland Area Office, intends to award one (1) firm-fixed-price commercial services contract as a sole source basis to ScriptPro USA, Inc., pursuant to 41 U.S.C. 3304(a)(1), as implemented by RFO 6.302-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. The Government requires continuation/renewal of the operating lease for the currently installed robotic prescription dispensing system at Portland Area Indian Health Services clinics at Western Oregon Service Unit (Chemawa Indian Health Center), including maintenance, support, and an upgrade of the workflow platform server operating system to an actively supported operating system. The requirement supports outpatient pharmacy operations, medication safety, and continuity of direct patient care. See the attached statement of work for more detail. Additionally, ScriptPro USA, Inc. has demonstrated the capability to provide the equipment, maintenance, support, OEM updates, and the required upgrade of the workflow platform server operating system to an actively supported operating system within the timeframe necessary to avoid a lapse in pharmacy operations and patient-care support. Place of Performance: Chemawa Indian Health Center 3750 Chemawa Rd NE Salem, OR 97305 Period of Performance: Base Period: 04/01/2026 – 03/31/2027 Option Period 1: 04/01/2027 – 03/31/2028 Option Period 2: 04/01/2028 – 03/31/2029 Option Period 3: 04/01/2029 – 03/31/2030 Option Period 4: 04/01/2030 – 03/31/2031 Product or Service Code: W065 - Lease or Rental of Equipment - Medical, Dental, and Veterinary Equipment and Supplies NAICS Code: 532490 - Other Commercial and Industrial Machinery and Equipment Rental and Leasing Set-Aside: No small business set-aside is anticipated for this action. This notice is posted in accordance with 48 CFR Part 326, Subpart 326.603-3 and IHM 5.5-6, Indian Health Service must use the negotiation authority of the Buy Indian Act, 25 U.S.C. 47, to give preference to Indian Economic Enterprises (IEE) whenever the use of that authority is authorized and practicable. All responsible sources may submit a capability statement or quotation for consideration. Responses are due no later than 3/23/2026 at 5:00 PM Pacific. Responses shall be submitted via email to jacob.blalock@ihs.gov and pao.doa@ihs.gov. The subject line of each response shall read: “75H71326Q00020 [VENDOR NAME]”

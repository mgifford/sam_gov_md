# San Joaquin/Stockton DWSC FY26 Maintenance Dredging Project

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 17:35:26.998-04
- Solicitation Number: W912P726BLI01

## Summary

This is a pre-solicitation notice for San Joaquin/Stockton DWSC FY256 Maintenance Dredging. JOB DESCRIPTION: The work includes annual maintenance dredging of the San Joaquin/Port of Stockton River DWSC to -35-ft (MLLW) plus 1-ft of paid over-depth, except the Sediment Trap, which shall be dredged to -38.5-ft (MLLW) plus 1-ft of paid overdepth. All dredge material is suitable for the upland sites shown on plan sheets. Dredge material may be suitable for beneficial reuses at Contractor furnished sites in accordance with the permit requirements of the specific reuse sites and per the requirements in specification. This requirement is for Full and Open Competition. It will be issued as an Invitation for Bid (IFB) which will result in the award of a single Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract. This Procurement will be conducted under PSC CODE: Y1KF, NAICS Code: 237990. The size standard for this code is $37,000,000. All questions should be directed to the Contract Specialist, Lisa Ip: Lisa.I.Ip@usace.army.mil. Estimated Cost Range is between $5,000,000 - $10,000,000.The solicitation will be available on/about April 10, 2026 for viewing/downloading the solicitation and documents. ALL BIDS will be due on/about May 11, 2026 at 11:30 AM PDT. The specific date and time will be identified in the solicitation. BIDDERS ARE ADVISED THAT THIS PROJECT MAY BE DELAYED, CANCELLED, OR REVISED AT ANY TIME DURING THE SOLICITATION, SELECTION, EVALUATION, AND/OR FINAL AWARD PROCESS. NOTE :a) All prospective Offerors (including their subcontractors) must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM - www.SAM.gov) and the Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment (PIEE) website in order to be able to view and download solicitation information, and to submit offers. b) Solicitation documents, plans, and specifications are only available to be download from PIEE, which may be accessed at https://wawf.eb.mil OR https://piee.eb.mil OR https://piee.eb.mil/xhtml/unauth/home/login.xhtml.c) Offers MUST BE submitted electronically through the PIEE website. d) Registration should be completed one (1) week prior to the anticipated solicitation release date. USACE will not notify Offerors of any changes to the solicitation; Offerors must monitor PIEE and SAM for the solicitation to be posted, and for any posted changes or amendments. e) See attachments PIEE Solicitation Module Vendor Access Instructions, SOP PIEE Proposal Manager Offeror, and SOP PIEE Posting Offer for instructions on getting access to the PIEE Solicitation Module and submitting Offeror proposals.

# Exception to Fair Opportunity for the MACRO Bridge Task Order

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-15 16:03:03.276-04
- Solicitation Number: 47QFCA26R0005

## Summary

General Services Administration (GSA) Assisted Acquisition Services (AAS), in support of the Department of Defense (DoD), proposed the award of a sole source Cost-Plus-Fixed-Fee (CPFF) Task Order (TO) for the Mission Awareness Capabilities Ramp-up and Optimization (MACRO) Task Order (TO) Bridge under the GSA ASTRO Multiple Award Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity Development/Systems 47QFCA26F0015 Pool contract vehicle. This effort will provide continued support to the DoD for comprehensive system engineering services support including Program Management, Analytical and Execution Planning (AEP) Services, Engineering, Development, Integration, and Sustainment (EDIS) Services, Test and Evaluation (T&E) Services, and Training Support. The TO was awarded on March 25, 2026. This sole source effort will allow for mission essential activities to continue until award of the competitive Integrated, Next-Generation, Versatile, Intelligence, Capabilities, Technologies, and Applications (INVICTA) TO.

## Summary

UPDATE #1 The purpose of this amendment is to extend the quotations date and time. Quotations are due no later than 1:00 P.M. PST on Thursday, May 14, 2026. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation Number HT941026Q2004 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ). The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code for this acquisition is 334516 – Analytical Laboratory Instrument Manufacturing; the size standard is 1000 employees. The Product and Service Code (PSC) for this requirement is 6550 – In-Vitro Diagnostic Substances, Reagents, Test Kits and Sets. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This is an unrestricted procurement, inviting full and open competition. It is the responsibility of the contractor to be familiar with the applicable clauses and provisions. The requirement is for a cost per reportable result (CPRR) contract for coagulation analyzers to support the following military treatment facilities (MTFs) and their workloads. See attachments.

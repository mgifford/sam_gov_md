- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation and Notice of Intent for a sole source award for services, prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information in this notice. This solicitation is issued as a Request for Quote (RFQ) number FA857926Q0014. This number is for tracking purposes only. The contractor shall furnish labor, travel, parts, and material necessary to provide scheduled and non-scheduled maintenance of the DataScribe Duplication System to the 402d Software Engineering Group (SWEG), Robins Air Force Base, GA. Services required include equipment maintenance, software and any needed upgrades. This contract is a base year with four option years.

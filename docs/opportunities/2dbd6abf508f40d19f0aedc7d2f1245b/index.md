# Request for Information (RFI) - Cold-Water Regulator Kits

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-06 12:31:46.002-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8629-26-R-5011

## Summary

This Sources Sought Synopsis (SSS) / Request for Information (RFI) is issued for informational purposes and market research only; it does not constitute a solicitation. The Government will not reimburse any company or individual for any expenses associated with preparing/submitting information in response to this posting. The information provided may be used by the Government in developing its acquisition strategy, statement of work/statement of objectives, and/or performance specifications. Interested parties are responsible for adequately marking proprietary or competition sensitive information contained within their response as instructed later in this posting. The United States (US) Air Force Life Cycle Management Center (AFLCMC), Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance & Special Operations Forces (ISR & SOF) Directorate, Special Warfare (SW) Program Office (AFLCMC/WISN) is conducting market research to identify possible sources capable of providing a Cold-Water Regulator Kit. Modernizing the SOF’s CWR inventory will directly enhance diver safety and increase operational readiness in cold water environments. Providing the units with associated tool kits will allow us to streamline maintenance operations and lower maintenance turnaround times by keeping our fleet mission ready. See attachment for further details and instructions on how to respond to this RFI.

# 49 NSN Gentex SPE4AX17D9402 Follow-On, Solicitation SPE4A726R0491

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 14:04:37.568-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE4A726R0491

## Summary

This agency proposes to issue a solicitation for a long-term strategic / sole source contract which will include multiple NSNs to support several weapon systems including 06F, 19F, VVN, and MBN, or Hercules C130 Aircraft, F15 Eagle, Aviation Life Support Systems, and Consolidated Ground Support Equipment, respectively. A total of 49 NSNs are targeted for this contract effort. It is anticipated that all 49 will be priced for the initial contract award. The proposed action is intended to be awarded on a sole source basis to Gentex CAGEs 60420, 97427, and 7L772 as supported by a J&A IAW FAR 6.302-1. The proposed sole source contract will be issued to Gentex Corporation CAGE: 60420. The proposed contract will be for a total 10-year period of performance. Letters expressing interest in subcontracting provided to the contracting officer will be forwarded to the identified OEM. This is proposed to be a FAR 15 effort. The proposed contract will be a fixed price, Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity contract for 2-year base with (4) four 2-year option periods with prospective price redetermination for DLA Direct support. The attached list of NSNs are the items that have been identified for this current action. If other NSNs are subsequently added, as defined by the terms of the contract, they will be synopsized separately. This will be a competitively awarded contract and best value selection methods will be used. The Government will award a single contract for this action. The solicitation will be posted on DIBBS on or around 4/13/2026. For the full list of items to be solicited, see Attached list of NSNs, Item Name, AMC/AMSC, Approved CAGE & P/N, Estimated Annual Demand, and required PLT/delivery.

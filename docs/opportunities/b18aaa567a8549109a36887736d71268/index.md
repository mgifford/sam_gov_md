# Tower Simulation Systems E2 - Challenge Based Acquisition

- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-18 14:27:12.796-04
- Solicitation Number: FAA_TSS_E2_ChBA

## Summary

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) has a requirement for air traffic controller training simulator operation and related services under the Tower Simulation System (TSS) program. The FAA intends to deploy additional TSSs to training sites around the National Airspace Systems (NAS) to improve controllers’ access to high-fidelity simulators and facilitate faster, more efficient controller training and certification. The FAA is also seeking to improve the capabilities of the TSS and the delivery of TSS services through technological innovation. The FAA has designated this effort “TSS E2.” For TSS E2, the FAA is issuing a Challenge Based Acquisition (ChBA). An overview describing the ChBA process is provided in the “TSS E2 Request for Concept Papers Feb 2026” document. As part of Phase I of the ChBA, the FAA is issuing a Request for Concept Papers (RCP). The details of the RCP requirements, including the instructions and evaluation approach of the RCP is provided in the “TSS E2 Request for Concept Papers Feb 2026” document. The Statement of Objectives (SOO) referenced in this document are the finalized objectives resulting from the “FAA Tower Simulation E2 – Statement of Objectives” Special Notice that was issued on 1/29/2026. The SOO is posted with this announcement. In addition, responses to questions received on the SOO are provided in the document titled “Responses to SOO Questions”. Additional supporting documents are also provided as Government Furnished Information (GFI) at the following link: https://faa-aoc.box.com/s/zlev2xhzssgdigj0okp5ny95d89n1dm0 If clicking the link does not take you to the site, please copy/paste the link into your browser.

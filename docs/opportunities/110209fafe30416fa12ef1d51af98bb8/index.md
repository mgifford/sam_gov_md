## Summary

Amendment 0001 - Please note Amendment 0001. The purpose of this amendment is to correct the solicitation due date to March 16th. The 555th Software Engineering Squadron (SWES) is requiring the Remote Monitoring and Maintenance (RMM) system that is designed to modernize the Air Force's monitoring and maintenance of Navigational Aids (NAVAIDs) on airfields. The Airfield Data Management Server will be a 5 year Blanket Purchase Agreement.

# INDUSTRY DAY - Fort Devens Collective Training Barracks

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-15 13:19:33.844-04

## Summary

*PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS INDUSTRY DAY IS VIRTUAL* Please join us 15 April 2026 @ 1000 Eastern for an Industry Day hosted by the US Army Corps of Engineers, Louisville District for the Design-Bid-Build construction of the Collective Training Barracks located at Fort Devens Remote Forces Training Area, MA. This project is a Design-Bid-Build Construct a 300-person Collective Training (CT) Enlisted Barracks for Major Exercises, Annual Training, Battle Assembly, and Mobilization training at Devens RFTA, MA. This Collective Training Enlisted Barracks floorplan is approximately 46,000 SF will be based on the standard design for an Operational Readiness Training Complex (ORTC). The project will include information systems, fire protection and alarm systems, Intrusion Detection System (IDS), and an Energy Monitoring Control Systems (EMCS) connection. Supporting facilities include site development, utilities and connections, lighting, paving, parking, walks, curbs and gutters, storm draining landscaping and signage. Sustainability/Energy and cybersecurity measures are included. All fixtures, furnishings, and equipment will be provided. Antiterrorism force protection and physical security measures will be incorporated into the design including minimum standoff distances from roads, parking areas and vehicle unloading areas. Access for individuals with disabilities will be provided. Facilities will be designed to a minimum life of 40 years in accordance with DoD's Unified Facilities Criteria 1-200-02 including energy efficiencies, building envelope and integrated building systems performance. Demolition of two (2) adjacent buildings is required for this project. This is not a Request for Proposal, an Invitation for Bid, or a Request for Quotation. Therefore, this event is not to be construed as a committment by the Government to enter into a contract nor will the Government pay for information provided to the Government in a response nor pay for administrative costs incurred attending the Industry Day event. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with travel or providing information in response to this announcement.

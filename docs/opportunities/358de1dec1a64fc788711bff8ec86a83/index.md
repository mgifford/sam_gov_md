# Maintenance for Quadient DS-1200 Serial # RAV7298 Folder Inserter in support of USDA-NASS

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. The United States Department of Agricultural (USDA) - National Agricultural Statistics Service (NASS) intends to single firm-fixed-price purchase order award The Contractor shall provide maintenance service on the following equipment: Quadient DS-1200 Serial # RAV7298 Folder Inserter. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0465 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Annual Maintenance on Quadient DS-1200 Folder Inserter. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 561499 - All Other Business Support Services The small business size standard is $21.5M This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. **See Attachments included with this announcement. **

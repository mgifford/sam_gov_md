# 6640 Reagents, Consumables, and Service Contract for iSED Analyzer Portland VAMC

- Posted: 2026-04-23 10:31:33.658-04

## Summary

This contract is needed to provide reagents, consumables, and a service maintenance contract for two automated ALCOR iSED Erythrocyte Sedimentation Rate testing analyzers at VA Portland Health Care System Hematology section in Pathology & Laboratory Medicine Service. A new five-year reagent and service contract, effective 7/15/2026, is needed to ensure continuity of service with current equipment. The iSED ESR (erythrocyte sedimentation rate) is a fully automated ESR testing system. There are no disposables and uses a primary barcoded lavender EDTA tube which are currently being used for other testing in our laboratory. The supplies required to operate the analyzers are quality control material, a wash solution, thermal printer paper rolls, and test cards. GENERAL REQUIREMENTS: All related consumables and supplies All necessary services to keep equipment in manufacturer's operating conditions, both preventive maintenance and emergency repairs.

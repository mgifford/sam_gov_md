# HEAVY DUTY INDUSTRIAL TRAILERS TLSC-E

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 07:43:11.02-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV26QA036

## Summary

The 409th CSB, Theater Contracting Center is publishing this presolicitation for the purchase of four (04) each heavy duty industrial trailers, to support TLSC-E/ A and Supply Support Center Europe/ A (SAE &A) operations at Kaiserslautern Army Depot, KAISERSLAUTERN (Germany). The heavy duty industrial trailers will be used for mission-essential operations and for the safe and secure transport of materials both indoors and outdoors within the installation. All vendors who have questions, comments and are interested in providing the equipment specified in the Attachment- SALIENT CHARACTERISTICS HEAVY DUTY INDUSTRIAL TRAILERS- DRAFT, should submit their interest via email to: Ms. Coralia Kelly Email: coralia.a.kelly.ln@army.mil Ms. Judy Biboum Email: judy.i.biboum.civ@army.mil Please include the title " W564KV26QA036- Purchase of four (04) each heavy duty industrial trailers " in the subject line of the request, and include the following information: Company name and address Cage Code UEI Point of contact for questions/clarifications Name Telephone number email address The Government will not return any information submitted in response to this notice. All questions must be in writing.

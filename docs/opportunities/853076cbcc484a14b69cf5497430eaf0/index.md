# USAFA Farish Recreation Area - Summer Cleaning Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-03 21:14:48.235-04
- Solicitation Number: FA700026RFI403

## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT: This is a Sources Sought Notice for market research purposes only. This is NOT a solicitation for proposals, proposal abstracts, or quotations. The purpose of this notice is to obtain information regarding the availability and capability of qualified businesses to provide seasonal cleaning services at the Farish Recreation Area, located at 12005 Rampart Range Road, Woodland Park, CO. This notice does not constitute a commitment by the Government. All information submitted in response to this announcement is voluntary and the Government will not pay for information requested nor will it compensate any respondent for any cost incurred in developing information provided to the Government. The 10th Force Support Squadron (10 FSS) requires non-personal services for seasonal cleaning at the Farish Recreation Area. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, transportation, and supervision necessary to perform deep cleaning and quick cleaning services as defined in the attached Performance Work Statement (PWS). The services are required seasonally from approximately May 1 through October 31 each year, on an as-ordered basis. You may view the Farish Recreational Area website here: https://usafasupport.com/farish-recreation-area-2/ Interested parties are invited to submit a Capabilities Statement that includes the following information: Company Information: Company name, address, point of contact (name, email, and phone number), UEI number, and CAGE code. Capability Narrative: A brief narrative describing your company's ability to meet the requirements outlined in the PWS. Please address your capability to provide all necessary personnel, equipment, and supplies. Please submit your Capabilities Statement and any questions via email to tammy.martin.6@us.af.mil no later than April 8, 2026 at 4:00 PM MDT.

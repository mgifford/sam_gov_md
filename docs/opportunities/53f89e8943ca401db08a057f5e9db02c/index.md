# Guam Defense System (GDS) Battle Manager Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-14 10:56:10.335-04
- Solicitation Number: W50RAJ_GDSIndustryDay

## Summary

INDUSTRY DAY ANNOUCEMENT Event: Guam Defense System (GDS) Battle Manager Industry Day PSC: AC33 NAICS: 541715 Location: Redstone Arsenal, Alabama GDS BM Description: The Guam Defense System Battle Manager (GDS BM) is a critical component of the GDS, serving as a joint Command and Control (C2) layer that integrates from existing Department of War (DoW) C2 systems to provide a common operating picture (COP)/common tactical picture (CTP) and fire direction to defend against both ballistic and non-ballistic threats. Portfolio Acquisition Executive Fires (PAE Fires) will host a Guam Defense System (GDS) Battle Manager Industry Day Event on May 8, 2026, at the Bob Jones Auditorium on Redstone Arsenal in Huntsville, Alabama. The purpose of the Industry Day is to provide an overview of the Guam Defense System Battle Manager requirement. All costs associated with attending the Industry Day will be solely at the respondent/attendee’s expense. Participation in the GDS Battle Manager Request for White Paper (RFWP) or any other future PAE Fires transaction or procurement opportunity is not contingent upon attending this Industry Day. Target Audience: Prime Contractor, Subcontractor or Joint Venture Offerors with the technical capabilities, facilities, and personnel to develop and deliver the GDS Battle Manager that are interested in submitting a White Paper. When: May 08, 2026, 0930-1230 Central Standard Time (CST) Below is a tentative schedule for planning purposes: 0930 – 1000 Registration 1000 – 1010 Welcome / Opening Comments 1010 – 1030 Strategic and Operational Need of GDS Battle Manager 1030 – 1100 High Level Requirement Overview 1100 – 1110 Break 1110 – 1150 Government Reference Architecture 1150 – 1200 Break 1200 – 1230 Acquisition Contract Strategy / Way Ahead / Questions Where: 5304 Martin Road, Bob Jones Auditorium, Sparkman Complex Registration Details: In order to attend the Industry Day Event, interested participants must provide their name, email address, company name and CAGE Code via email to Barbara Cantrell (barbara.a.cantrell2.civ@army.mil) and Andrea Russell (andrea.a.russell5.ctr@army.mil) no later than 1700 CST, 29 April 2026. The email subject line shall state “RSVP GDS Battle Manager Industry Day Event”. An email confirmation will be sent by the PAE Fires Acquisition Team verifying successful receipt of registration along with a detailed Industry Day schedule, maps, and directions. Please advise anyone who does not have a CAC that they will need to pre-register with the Visitor Center for base access at https://pass.aie.army.mil/?b=usa&i=redstone&e=TXBVRLKL. Individuals will receive a text message confirming successful registration. They can use their Driver’s License to pass through the gate. A gate pass will not be needed unless registration is not successful. Visitors can obtain a Visitor’s Day Pass on the day of the visit. You must bring your driver’s license or passport to the Visitor Center at Gate 9. Visitor Center Information: Gate 9, Rideout Road, MAIN GATE Open 24/7 Visitor Control Center located enroute to Gate 9, Open 7 a.m.to 3 p.m., Monday – Friday (closed on federal holidays) Each person attending industry day must provide a CAC, Driver's License or Passport to attend the meeting.

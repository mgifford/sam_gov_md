# USACE Chicago Industry Open House

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-23 15:43:51.877-04
- Solicitation Number: OSBP2601

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Chicago District will host its annual Industry Open House on Wednesday, April 1, 2026, from 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM (CST) at: USACE Chicago District Headquarters 231 South LaSalle Street (Basement Level) Chicago, IL 60604 This event is open to businesses of all sizes interested in partnering with the Chicago District. Attendees will receive strategic updates from District leadership and have the opportunity to engage directly with program and acquisition personnel, as well as industry partners. The Open House provides an ideal forum for organizations to present their capabilities and gain insight into upcoming projects and contracting opportunities. This is an in-person event, and advance registration is required. To register, please complete the attached invitation. Firms are also encouraged to submit their capability statement in advance to support meaningful engagement discussions. Registration materials and capability statements should be submitted to LRC.SBO@usace.army.mil with the subject line: “Industry Open House Registration.” ? Registration Deadline: March 31, 2026 We look forward to your participation and to strengthening partnerships that support the delivery of vital infrastructure and mission success across the region.

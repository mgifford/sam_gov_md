The Department of Veteran Affairs, Network Contracting Office 21 (NCO 21), intends to negotiate a sole source Firm-Fixed Price contract for the operation and maintenance (O&M) of the water filtration systems that supplies water to the Sterile Processing Service (SPS) departments and research department in the Veterans Affairs Northern California Healthcare System.

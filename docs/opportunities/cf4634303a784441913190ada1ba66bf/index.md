# Ground Maintenance Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Saturn Landscaping Plus Inc, Perris, California, has been selected for an indefinite-delivery, indefinite- quantity contract award expected on 16 April 2026 with a maximum estimated aggregate value of $10,650,356.20 under a single award IDIQ contract for grounds maintenance services .The contract will encompass all grounds maintenance services to include but not limited to the mowing of BASH and Improved grounds. Work will be performed on Eielson Air Force Base, Alaska with work is expected to be completed 31 March 2031. The award is the result of a competitive acquisition, and six (6) offers were received. The 354th Contracting Squadron Eielson AFB, Alaska, is the contracting activity (FA500426D0001).

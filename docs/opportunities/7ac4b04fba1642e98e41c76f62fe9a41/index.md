# ATLANTIC UNDERSEA TEST AND EVALUATION CENTER (AUTEC) WATER TANKS REPLACEMENT, ALANTIC UNDERSEA TEST AND EVALUATION CENTER (AUTEC), ANDROS ISLAND, BAHAMAS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:00:26.201-04
- Solicitation Number: N6945026R0006

## Summary

This project entails repairs, by replacing, a portion of the potable and fire supply water system at AUTEC, returning the system to the original design intent while updating the system to account for current use characteristics. The items to be updated include two (2) finished water holding/contact tanks, an elevated storage tank, and interconnecting piping. Two existing finished water tanks that have reached the end of their useful life will be demolished, along with abandoned piping. Amendments 3/12/2026 - See Amendment 0001

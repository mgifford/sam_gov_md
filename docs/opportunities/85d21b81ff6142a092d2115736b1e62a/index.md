# LAK/RND Fire Suppression Maintenance & UV Systems Maintenance Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-04 13:38:07.919-05

## Summary

The 502d Contracting Squadron at Joint Base San Antonio Lackland awarded a contract for LAK/RND Fire Suppression & UV System Maintenance Services, including inspection, maintenance, and repair of fire suppression equipment & UV across JBSA facilities. The contractor is required to provide all labor, tools, materials, and equipment necessary to perform the services in accordance with the terms and conditions of the solicitation and resulting contract.

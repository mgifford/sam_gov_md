# Sources Sought Announcement (SSA) for SICPS Weapon System Installation Kit

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-23 08:40:41.014-04
- Solicitation Number: DLA-Aberdeen-26-045

## Summary

DESCRIPTION The Defense Logistics Agency-Aberdeen, on behalf of the Army Integrated Logistics Supply Center (ILSC) – Supply Chain Management Directorate (SCMD) – Strategic Sourcing Directorate (SSD), intends to acquire Parts to support the Standardized Integrated Command Post System (SICPS). Requirements Description: The subject Installation Kit (MK-3035/VRC) is utilized for installation of Near Term Digital Radio (NTDR) into Standardized Integrated Command Post System (SICPS), Rigid Wall System (RWS), Tactical Command System AN/TSQ-232 (V)2 and (V)4. The kit includes items such as mounting brackets, cables, and miscellaneous hardware utilized for installing NTDR equipment into a shelter that is mounted onto a US Army High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle (HMMWV). The kit includes manufactured and commercial off-the-shelf parts. Note: The Government will impose a Government Acceptance of Delivery process requirement onto any source of supply whom has not provided the subject kit to the Government within the last five years. For a source whom has provided the kit within the last five years, a waiver will be granted. A waiver may also be granted for a source whom has provided a similar form/fit/function kit within the last five years, subject to the government’s discretion. For the case of a source whom has not been granted a waiver, the source shall be required to perform the Government Acceptance of Delivery process. The process is defined within a document that will be provided to a source of supply. The document can be provided to responders to this SSA as reference purpose only, upon request. The Government does possess the technical data package (TDP) for the parts. The TDP is available for conditional release upon request. The TDP includes level 3. production information. The kit has been previously procured by the Government. Items and/or Services to Be Procured: NSN: 5895015428256 NOMENCLATURE: INSTALLATION KIT, ELECTORNIC EQUIPMENT, MK-3035/VRC PART NUMBER: ALH-100226-001 CAGE: 318U8 Requested Response Information: We request that sources who can manufacture the installation kit submit information to indicate their capability. At a minimum, the information you submit should include, but is not limited to, the following types of documentation: response to “Contractor Questionnaire” appearing at end of SSA, and information on responder’s previous Government and commercial contracts and data rights. In your response, please include your company name, CAGE code, and a technical point of contract with email address and telephone number. Notice, Response Due Date, and Points of Contact: This is NOT a Request for Quote or Proposal. The Government does not intend to award a contract on the basis of this SSA or reimburse any costs associated with the preparation of responses to this SSA. This SSA is for planning and market research purposes only and shall not be construed as a commitment by the Government. The information gathered from this announcement will be used to determine if responsible sources exist, and to assist in determining if this effort can be competitive and/or set aside for Small Business. Furthermore, the Government will use the information, in part, to determine the best acquisition strategy for a potential procurement action and/or to assist in making acquisition strategy decisions in the future. All SSA responses should be emailed to the Points of Contact at the email addresses listed below. No phone inquiries will be accepted, either as a response to this action or for information requests. All requests for information must be submitted via email to the Technical Point of Contact and the DLA Aberdeen Small Business Office. Please submit responses by 5/22/2026 Technical Point of Contact: michael.r.toscan.ctr@army.mil Secondary Point of Contact: darrick.l.kibble.ctr@army.mil DLA Aberdeen Small Business: DLA.Land.and.Maritime.Small.Business.Office@dla.mil

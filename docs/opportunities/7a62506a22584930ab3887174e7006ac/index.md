# FY26 AFLCMC Audio-Visual System B892 Conference Room

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 16:58:27.866-04
- Solicitation Number: F2XTRA6075A001

## Summary

The 42nd Contracting Squadron, 50 S. LeMay Plaza, Bldg. 804, Maxwell AFB, AL requires the procurement of Audio/Visual (A/V) Equipment in accordance with (IAW) the Statement of Requirements paragraphs 4.1.1- 4.5.1 (SOR) (see Attachment 1) for the Air Force Life Cycle Management Center (AFLCMC) at Maxwell-Gunter Annex AFB, AL. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in part 12. as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested and a written solicitation will not be issued. THE GOVERNMENT IS SOLICITING FOR “OPEN MARKET” QUOTES ONLY. GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION (GSA) QUOTES OR OTHER GOVERNMENT-WIDE ACQUISITION CONTRACTS (GWAC) QUOTES WILL NOT BE CONSIDERED. ALL PRODUCTS MUST BE NEW: NO REFURBISHED OR USED ITEMS WILL BE ACCEPTED. CONTRACTOR MUST BE CLEARED ON THE COMMERCIAL AND GOVERNMENT ENTITY PROGRAM. THIS REQUIREMENT WILL BE SOLICITED AS 100% SMALL BUSINESS SET -ASIDE. THIS REQUIREMENT IS RESTRICTED TO SMALL BUSINESSES. THE CONTRACTOR MUST POSSESS AT A MINIMUM A FAVOURABLY ADJUDICATED TIER 3 (SECRET) CLEARANCE AND AN APPROPRIATE FACILITY SECURITY CLEARANCE LETTER AT A MINIMUM OF SECRET. THIS SOLICITATION CONTAINS A DD FORM 254, DOD CONTRACT SECURITY CLASSIFCATION SPECIFICAITON.

# USNS WALLY SCHIRRA - FY 26 MTA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 13:36:26.158-04
- Solicitation Number: N3220526R0028

## Summary

Prepare for and accomplish the Mid Term Availabilty of the USNS WALLY SCHIRRA (T-AKE 8) as specified[ER1] in the solicitation and in accordance with the work item package. The anticipated period of performance is 26 OCTOBER 2026 to 14 DECEMBER 2026. The place of performance for this contract shall be the contractor’s facility. Due to operational and national security availability considerations, the location shall be West Coast. All responsible sources may submit a proposal, which shall be considered by the agency. The solicitation will be made available on or about 7 APRIL 2026[ER2] through the Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment Solicitation Module. https://piee.eb.mil/sol/xhtml/unauth/index.xhtml To obtain access to the work item package and government furnished information technical data, complete and return the attached non-disclosure agreement to the point of contact of this announcement. The decision to solicit under full and open competition was made from market research. The MSC Office of Small Business and Small Business Administration Procurement Center Representative concurs. Questions or comments regarding this notice may be addressed to the point of contact of this announcement.

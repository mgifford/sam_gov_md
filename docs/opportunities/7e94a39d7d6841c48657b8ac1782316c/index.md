# F--GRCA Hazardous Waste Pick up Blanket Purchase Agreement

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-07 09:24:39.374-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P1526Q0065

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) is to obtain information from industry for potential sources capable of performing services for the U.S. Department of the Interior, National Park Service (NPS) on behalf of Grand Canyon National Park (GRCA) located in Arizona. Grand Canyon National Park (GRCA) has an ongoing requirement for hazardous materials and universal waste collection, transportation, and disposal services. These services include, but are not limited to, the compliant handling of waste oils, solvents, paints, aerosols, batteries, fluorescent lamps, mercury-containing devices, and electronic waste. See attached Sources Sought notice for more information.

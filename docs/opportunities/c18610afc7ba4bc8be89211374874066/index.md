## Summary

ON OR BEFORE APRIL 24, 2026 I WILL POST A SOLICITATION AS A SDVOSB SET ASIDE FOR EMPLOYEES ASSISTANCE PROGRAM. EAP services are extended to family members of employees with alcohol and/or drug problems, and to employees who have family members with alcohol and/or drug problems. For the purpose of the extended services, "family member" means the following relatives of the employee: (1) spouse, and spouse's parents; (2) children, including adopted/foster children, and their spouses; (3) parents; (4) brothers and sisters, and their spouses; and (5) any individual related by blood or affinity whose close association with employee is the equivalent of a family relationship. TO BE CONSIDERED FOR AWARD VENDOR MUST BE A VIP VERIFIED SDVOSB ON OR BEFORE THE CLOSING OF THIS SOLICITATION APRIL 28, 2026 12PM EST. PLEASE REVIEW THE SOW INSERTED IN THIS SOLICITATION TO GET A DETAILED DESCRIPTION OF WHAT S REQUIRED. ALL OFFERS ARE TO BE SBMITTED TO QUINCY.ALEXANDER@VA.GOV ON OR BEFORE TUESDAY, APRIL 28, 2026 12PM EST.

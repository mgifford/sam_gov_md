# KARDEX REMSTAR LEKTRIEVER RELOCATION

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:07:42.449-04
- Solicitation Number: FA812526Q0039

## Summary

The Air Force intends to solicit and award a firm fixed price, single sole source purchase order contract to Southwest Solutions Group, Inc; Lewisville, TX; CAGE Code (0HDL3), under the authority of FAR 12.102(a) 41 U.S.C. 1901 as implemented by FAR 12.201-1, for the Kardex Remstar Lektriever Carousel Relocation for the Oklahoma City Air Logistics Complex (OC-ALC). The NAICS code for this requirement is 333248 with a size standard of 750 employees. Contractor registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) – which results in receiving a unique, active CAGE Code – is required prior to receiving any contracts or orders from the Government in accordance with FAR 4.11. Contractors can register electronically at https://sam.gov/ . Interested persons may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement by submitting a quote and information. Electronic procedures will be used for this solicitation through SAM at https://sam.gov/. No telephone requests.

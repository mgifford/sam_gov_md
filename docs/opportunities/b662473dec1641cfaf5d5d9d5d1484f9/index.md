## Summary

Amendment 2: This amendment includes additional Q&A, revised PWS, site maps, attendance roster from site visit, extends submission due date to April 6, 2026, and establishes final Q&A due date as March 24, 2026. Amendment 1: This amendment provides details for a site visit for offerors, updates the PPQ submission date in Addendum to 52.212-1 Paragraph 6.7, adds PPQ template, and adds Q&A sheet to respond to offeror questions. The contractor shall provide a self-sustaining base located at Combat Trains Command Post (CTCP) Victory (south region of Summit Bechtel Reserve 37°53'25.2"N 81°06'40.2"W), in Fayette County, WV, and additional support tentage at the Raleigh County Memorial Airport (RCMA) located in the surrounding area. Services will include: site design, preparation and maintenance (pre-, during, and post- award); food: delivery, prepared and served; potable water delivery and distribution; lodging for up to 375 personnel at the CTCP Victory; HVAC; generated power; lighting; cots; several support tents (dining, operational command, etc.); ice; latrines; black and grey water removal; showers; fuel for all contracted support equipment to maintain base; sinks; dumpsters; trash cans; trash removal; interior lighting for tents and exterior generated tower sets.

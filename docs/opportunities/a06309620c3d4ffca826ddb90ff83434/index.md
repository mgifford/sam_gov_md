# Kaiserslautern Military Community (KMC) Portable Light Towers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 07:04:48.779-04
- Solicitation Number: F3N0426068A001

## Summary

4 Portable Light Towers The requirement consists of a mobile, diesel-powered, trailer-mounted lighting system designed for durability and ease of transport. The system must be equipped with four or more high efficiency 320W LED floodlights, capable of producing a minimum total luminous flux of over 211,000 lumens and illuminating an area of at least 9,200 square meters. The system must feature a hydraulic vertical mast with a rotation capability of approximately 340 degrees to allow for precise light positioning. For operational flexibility, the unit must provide auxiliary power through an integrated alternator delivering at least 4.2kVA at 240V, complete with an external 16A outlet socket to power ancillary equipment. The entire system shall be mounted on a road-ready trailer that includes a foldable drawbar to minimize its footprint for storage and transport.

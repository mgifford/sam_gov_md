# Procurement of Multitron HT Incubator

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-14 11:32:00.304-04
- Solicitation Number: CDER-2026-133237

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide a commercially available incubator shaker system that supports mammalian cell culture at production-relevant volumes under controlled environmental and agitation conditions. The system shall: Be compatible with existing CHO suspension culture processes Support simultaneous processing of multiple culture conditions Provide sufficient capacity and operational flexibility Operate reliably within a laboratory environment This requirement is limited to the provision of equipment and associated services and does not include process development or modification of existing protocols. Vendors shall clearly demonstrate how their proposed solution meets each salient characteristic.

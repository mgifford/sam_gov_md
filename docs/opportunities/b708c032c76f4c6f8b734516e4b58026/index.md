# U.S. Embassy Antananarivo - NEC Building Exterior Wall Painting

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 06:27:38.461-04
- Solicitation Number: 19MA1026Q0007

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy in Antananarivo requires painting maintenance and repair services for the existing exterior wall façade of the Embassy Compound. The façade walls are constructed of reinforced concrete and are finished with a textured coating system. The U.S. Government intends to award a purchase order to the responsible company submitting an acceptable offer at the lowest price.

## Summary

The subject requirement is for the acquisition of NSN 3040-01-483-9133 gear mechanism sub-assembly, right. An Indefinite Quantity Long-Term Contract with a three year base period and 2 option years is anticipated. FOB origin is required; inspection/acceptance is origin. This solicitation will be available on the Internet at https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil/rfp after the issue date on or about 3/25/2026. Hard copies of this solicitation are not available. This solicitation is a 100% Small Business set-aside. The small business size standard is 750 employees for NAICS code 333613. While price may be a significant factor in the evaluation of offers, the final award decision will be based upon a combination of price, past performance, and delivery. The proposed procurement contains an option to extend the contract period one or more times at the discretion of the Government. Total duration shall not exceed five years. Based on market research the item is manufactured for military use only and is not commercial; therefore, the Government is not using the policies contained in FAR Part 12 in its solicitation for this item. However, interested persons may identify to the contracting officer their interest and capability to satisfy the Government’s requirement with a commercial item within 15 days of this notice.

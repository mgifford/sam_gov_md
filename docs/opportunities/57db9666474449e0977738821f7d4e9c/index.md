# WBAMC Washer Disinfector Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 12:28:00.991-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0026QA102

## Summary

This requirement is for maintenance of six (6) government-owned Steris washer/disinfector units for William Beaumont Army Medical Center (WBAMC), Fort Bliss, TX. The resultant contract (purchase order) will be a base plus one (1) option, the periods of performance of which are: Base: 1 May 2026 through 30 April 2027 Option 1: 1 May 2027 through 30 April 2028

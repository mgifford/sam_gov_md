Questions regarding this pre-solicitation notice must be sent in writing to Ms. Yuliya Kochkina, Contract Specialist, NCO 21 at email: Yuliya.Kochkina@va.gov The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Network Contracting Office (NCO) 21 will be issuing a Request for Proposal (RFP) for a general construction contractor for the project titled: Replace Dietetics and Cath Lab Flooring, Buildings 1 and 1D, Project: 654-22-003 at VA Sierra Nevada Health Care System, Reno, NV identified in the Statement of Work (SOW) located at: 975 Kirman Avenue, Reno, NV 89502 to be attached at a future date. Work includes but is not limited to the following: Vendors must be able to provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision required to perform replacement of flooring in the Cath Lab & Dietetics Services (kitchen) spaces located in Buildings 1 and 1D. Work includes demolition, recycling and disposal of existing flooring, and installation (new construction) of new flooring. The work should also account for temporary removal, storage, and reinstallation of furnishing and equipment as required for the successful replacement of flooring products as well as infection control and life safety requirement. Additional work includes completely preparing site for building operations, including demolition and removal of existing flooring, and furnish all services, materials, supplies, temporary equipment and items, specifically services, and project supervision in coordination with the VA COR. Vendors must demonstrate the ability to meet all requirements. The full SOW, Specifications, Drawings, and any attachments, including all details, will be published when the Solicitation is posted online to SAM.gov.

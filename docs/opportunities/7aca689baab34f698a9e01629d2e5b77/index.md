- Posted: 2026-03-19 13:11:32.751-04

## Summary

The Prescott National Forest requires 1) the safety inspection of three bridges in accordance with the March 2022 Specifications for the National Bridge Inventory (SNBI) and all its references and 2) load ratings in accordance with AASHTOs Manual for Bridge Evaluation (MBE), 3rd ed., 2018. Questions are required to be submitted through email to the Contracting Officer at blaine.greenwalt@usda.gov and are due no later than March 20th, 2026, 3:00 PM Pacific Time. No questions will be accepted after this date and time. Offers or responses to the solicitation (include the solicitation number in the name of the document) should be sent to the contracting officer by email; blaine.greenwalt@usda.gov before the date and time listed in Block 8.

The U.S. Department of Health & Human Services (HHS) awarded a sole-source order to Drodex, Inc. on March 27, 2026, in the amount of $2,753,124.70 for staffing support services to the Immediate Office under the Administration for Strategic Preparedness and Response (ASPR).

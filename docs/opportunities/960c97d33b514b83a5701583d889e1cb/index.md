# N--HVAC REPAIR AND REPLACEMENT - Death Valley National Park, CA

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 18:17:27.257-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P8126B0002

## Summary

**The National Park Service anticipates Posting the Invitation For Bid (Solicitation) for this project approximately 4/30/26)** This project will replace failed or failing heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC), including evaporative coolers, in Death Valley National Park with consistent equipment that will provide heating and cooling for the comfort and safety of employees, residential tenants, and visitors. The work location is Death Valley National Park, including Furnace Creek, Cow Creek, and Stovepipe Wells.

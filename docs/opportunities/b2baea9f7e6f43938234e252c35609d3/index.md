# 2026 HAFB AIR SHOW - PORTABLE TOILETS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 17:58:54.652-04
- Solicitation Number: FA820126Q0014

## Summary

The 75th Air Base Wing is seeking comprehensive portable sanitation services for the Warriors Over the Wasatch Airshow, scheduled to be held at Hill Air Force Base, Utah, from June 26-28, 2026. The contractor will be responsible for furnishing all labor, equipment, materials, transportation, and supervision required to support the event. This includes supplying, delivering, and installing standard and ADA-compliant portable toilets, hand-wash stations, 250-gallon holding tanks, and potentially babychanging stations. The scope of work requires the contractor to maintain all units in a clean, sanitary, fully stocked, and operational condition throughout the airshow. This involves initial placement according to the government's site plan, daily servicing such as pumping, waste removal, cleaning, and restocking, as well as on-call emergency services. The performance period will begin with delivery and staging 1-2 days prior to the event, continue with servicing throughout the two-day airshow, and conclude with the complete removal of all units and waste within 24 hours of the event's end. The contractor must ensure that all waste is disposed of in compliance with federal, state, and local regulations and that all personnel meet base access and security requirements.

# Anderometer Hydraulics Replacement/Repair Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 10:21:11.892-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016726Q1060

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. Naval Surface Warfare Center Carderock Division (NSWCCD) intends to award a firm fixed price (FFP) purchase order using Simplified Acquisition Procedures. The Government intends to solicit for a Anderometer Hydraulics Replacement/Repair Services as detailed in the attached solicitation. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation and a written solicitation will not be issued. SITE VISIT: All contractors are highly encouraged to complete an on-site visit to perform an initial inspection of the defunct anderometer PRIOR to submitting a firm fixed price quote detailing the designing specifications for system repair. Contractor shall submit all site visit requests to Tracy McDonough at tracy.l.mcdonough2.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 10:00 AM EDT on Monday 30 March 2026. This is procurement IS set aside for small business. The NAICS code is 811310 and the Small Business Standard is $12.5 Million. Questions/clarification regarding this solicitation must be submitted via email to tracy.l.mcdonough2.civ@us.navy.mil by 10:00 AM EDT on Monday, 30 March 2026. All questions received before this deadline will be answered in an amendment to the solicitation. Quote packages are due on or before 10:00 AM EDT on Thursday, 2 April 2026. Responses shall include Official Company Name, Cage Code, and Point of Contact name & phone number. Each response must clearly indicate the capability of the quoter to meet all specifications and requirements. The Point of Contact for this acquisition is Tracy McDonough tracy.l.mcdonough2.civ@us.navy.mil

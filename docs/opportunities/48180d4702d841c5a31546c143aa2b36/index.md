# Repair of Hoop Buildings

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 13:52:55.184-04

## Summary

The USDA National Centers for Animal Health (NCAH) in Ames, IA, needs repairs to Hoop Buildings H1, H2, H3, H4, H5 & H6. The hoop buildings were damaged by windstorms and need a variety of repairs, which are specified in the SOW. The scope also includes providing reports on other additional repairs identified, if any. Solicitation documents and photos are posted below. A document is posted below outlining required documentation for entrance to the USDA NCAH campus in Ames. Contractors wishing to attend the site visit must send names to james.g.roloff@usda.gov no later than May 1.

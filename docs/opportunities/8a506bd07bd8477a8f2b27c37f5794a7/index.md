# Pre-solicitation Notice ? Procurement of Projection System Upgrade in support of P-8A Aircrew Training Device (ATD) - Operational Flight Trainer (OFT)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 18:10:07.796-04
- Solicitation Number: N6134026R1010-1

## Summary

The Naval Air Warfare Center Training Systems Division (NAWCTSD) Orlando has a requirement to procure new projection systems in support of training systems for the USN and RAAF P-8 aircraft platforms, providing realistic training events and qualification exercises for pilots, aircrew and maintainers. This procurement will be solicited competitively as a small business set aside in accordance with FAR Parts 6, 12, and 19 and will result in a new Firm Fixed Price contract. The anticipated Solicitation number will be N61340-26-R-1010. The NAICS code for this requirement is 333310 Commercial and Service Industry Machinery Manufacturing and the Product Service Code (PSC) is 6910 (Training Aids). This notice does not constitute a formal solicitation.

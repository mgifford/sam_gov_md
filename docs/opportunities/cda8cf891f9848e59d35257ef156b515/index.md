# Kohler Generators and Automatic Transfer Switches

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:19:00.275-04
- Solicitation Number: N6600126Q6041

## Summary

The Government is seeking to acquire commercial-off-the-shelf (COTS) Brand Name Kohler generators and automatic transfer switches. To be considered acceptable and eligible for award, quotes must provide all of the items and quantities listed below. The Government will not consider quotes or offers for partial items or quantities. See RFQ Attachment C1.5 ATTACHMENT (01) ITEM LIST - REVISED for the full list of required Brand Name items. Contractors will be required to complete a Generator Acceptance Test Report prior to shipment of items. (See Attachment (03)).The expected delivery date is 15 June 2026. The Government is seeking Free on Board (FOB) Destination pricing to the following shipping address: San Diego, CA 92110 (full address will be disclosed at award).

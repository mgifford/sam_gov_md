PROCUREMENT TITLE: REPLACE OBSOLETE/DISCONTINUED STRYKER 8™ SYSTEM INTENT TO AWARD: The Department of Veterans Affairs, VISN 01, VAHCS West Haven, 950 Campbell Avenue, West Haven, CT 06516-2770, intends to award one (1) Order under FSS 36F79720D0041, to the SDVOSB TrillaMed LLC, to replace the Obsolete/Discontinued items described below.

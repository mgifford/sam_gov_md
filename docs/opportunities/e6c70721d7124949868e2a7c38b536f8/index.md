Solicitation number HC108426R0005 is issued as a request for proposal (RFP). This requirement is for proprietary, commercial off-the-shelf, brand-name Menlo Security, Inc.’s Cloud Browser product, as a Menlo Security, Inc. reseller-provided managed service, providing the Department of War’s remote browsing isolation solution for Cloud Based Internet Isolation (CBII) service. The action includes the renewal of existing subscription licenses, the procurement of new subscription licenses, and the incorporation of Menlo Security, Inc.’s Highly Evasive and Adaptive Threats Shield with Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning capabilities to enhance cybersecurity measures.

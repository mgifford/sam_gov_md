On behalf of the NIH Small Business Program Office (SBPO), you are cordially invited to participate in our upcoming Vendor Outreach Session (VOS) designed to strengthen engagement between small businesses and the NIH acquisition community. Event Details: Date: Wednesday, April 29, 2026 Time: 10:00 AM – 12:30 PM Location: 6700 Rockledge Drive Rm 6700B Bethesda, MD 20817 Parking: Paid parking is available on-site This session will provide an opportunity for small businesses to: Overview provided by senior acquisition leadership Gain insight into the NIH centralization Better understand NIH procurement priorities and processes We encourage participation from small business representatives to foster meaningful dialogue and collaboration.

# The ACC-RSA has a requirement to support a requirement for care of supplies in storage services necessary in support of the Multiple Launch Rocket Systems and High Mobility Artillery Rocket Systems launchers repair cycle float and equipment force pool.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-09 11:33:13.366-04
- Solicitation Number: W31P4Q-26-R-XXXX

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command-Redstone Arsenal hereby issue the following Sources Sought notice as a means of conducting market research to identify parties having an interest in and the resources to support a requirment for care of supplies in storage (COSIS) services necessary in support of the Multiple Launche Rocket Systems (MLRS) and High Mobility Artillery Rocket Systems (HIMARS) launchers repair cycle float (RFC) and equipment force pool (EFP) at Red River Army Depot (RRAD), Texas (TX) for the Government and foreign military sales (FMS) customers.

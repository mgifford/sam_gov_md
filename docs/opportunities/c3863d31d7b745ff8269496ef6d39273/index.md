## Summary

This award is for a five-year Requirements production contract that allows for Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) and Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF) Delivery Orders (DOs) for the 120mm, Advanced Multi-Purpose (AMP) M1147 High Explosive Multi-Purpose with Tracer (HEMP-T) Cartridges for US Army and Foreign Military Sales (FMS) customers, both known and unknown.

## Summary

The Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) invites you to submit a quote in response to RFQ# CORHQ-26-Q-0128 for FDIC’s Digicert Subscription Maintenance. Please use the attached Price Schedule to provide pricing for all required line items and include your most favorable discount to the Government. Any Terms and Conditions, EULAs, or MSAs must be submitted with your quote for incorporation into the resulting contract. The FDIC prefers Government-approved terms. Each offer shall be divided into three (3) separate parts: Volume I – Pricing Volume II – Additional Information Volume III – SCRM Information Please note a submission without the components listed in Provision 7.3.2-09 – General Proposal Instructions will be omitted from consideration of award. The awardee must have an active SAM.gov registration prior to award. Questions regarding this RFQ are due no later than 12:00 PM EST on April 16, 2026. All quotes must be submitted by 12:00 PM EST on April 23, 2026.

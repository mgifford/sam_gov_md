# J--FY26 LE VEHICLE MEDFORD

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-11 16:12:55.446-04
- Solicitation Number: 140L4326Q0029

## Summary

This procurement is unrestricted. The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Medford District Office, intends to issue a Request For Quote for a one (1) firm fixed price commercial items services purchase order for FY26 Law Enforcement (LE) Vehicle Outfitting conducted under FAR Part 13. Contractor shall remove LE equipment from an older 2018 F150 LE vehicle and install the removed and new contractor provided equipment into a new 2025 F150 LE vehicle. The performance period is from date of award through 12/30/2026. The NAICS code is 811198 and the small business size standard $10M. Solicitation is anticipated to be issued on or about 03/11/2026. The basis of award will utilize the lowest price technically acceptable and relevant experience. All responsible sources may submit a proposal which shall be considered by the agency.

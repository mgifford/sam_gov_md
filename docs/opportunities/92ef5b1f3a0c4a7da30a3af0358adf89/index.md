# 124th FSS Dining Facility Attendants and Contract Cook Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-11 13:36:30.687-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S73-26-R-A001

## Summary

The Idaho Air National Guard (IDANG), 124 Force Support Squadron (FSS) has a requirement for Food Service Contractors to provide in garrison support for the Dining Facility, on Gowen Field. The procurement is 100% Small Business (SB) Set-Aside. The NAICS code for this requirement will be 722310 with a size standard of $47 million. Any resultant contract will be effective for one (1) twelve-month Base Period, and may include up to four (4) twelve-month Option Periods which may be exercised at the Government’s discretion. The Government intends to award a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract in accordance with all terms, conditions, clauses, and provisions, as will be stated in the solicitation. Additionally, the tentative start date for the contract is May 1, 2026.

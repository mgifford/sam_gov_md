# Hampton Roads Small Business Expo

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-31 13:00:35.196-04

## Summary

The Hampton Roads Small Business Navy Expo will be held on May 12th - 13th, 2026 at the Slover Library in Norfolk, VA. The event will take place during the AFCEA Maritime Operations & Industrial Warfare Summit. Please come out and hear from Department of the Navy, Office of Small Business Programs Director, DCAA, CMMC, BAFO Proposal Lab, Navy Contracting Officers panel, SBP panel, Mentor Protege Program and other topics. Small business sponsorship tables are available and will be located in the ballroom at the Hilton on the Main. This premiere event will once again be one of the best of the year! Thanks for the support :-)

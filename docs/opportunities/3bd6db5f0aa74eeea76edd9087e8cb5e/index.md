## Summary

PART: U.S. GOVERNMENT PROCUREMENTS SUBPART: 70 GENERAL PURPOSE COMMERCIAL INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY EQUIPMENT, SOFTWARE, AND SERVICES AFTC/PZIOA Directorate of Contracting, 5 South Wolfe Avenue, Edwards AFB, CA 93524 SUBJECT: Comprehensive Cost and Requirements (CCaR) System Support/Project Management Reporting Tool (PMRT) REF NO.: F1SEAJ6065A001 DESC: THIS IS A NOTICE OF CONTRACT ACTION (NOCA); THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION FOR PROPOSALS. NO REIMBURSEMENT WILL BE MADE FOR ANY COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH PROVIDING INFORMATION IN RESPONSE TO THIS NOCA OR ANY FOLLOW-UP INFORMATION REQUESTS. THE PURPOSE OF THIS NOCA IS TO NOTIFY INDUSTRY OF THE USAF'S INTENT OF CONTRACT ACTION AND POSSIBLY GAIN KNOWLEDGE OF OTHER POTENTIALLY QUALIFIED SOURCES AND THEIR SIZE CLASSIFICATIONS (SMALL BUSINESS, LARGE BUSINESS, ETC.) PLEASE DO NOT REQUEST A COPY OF A SOLICITATION, AS ONE DOES NOT EXIST. The Air Force Test Center (AFTC), Directorate of Contracting, Edwards Air Force Base, California, intends to award a sole source contract to Integrated Data Services, Inc., 2831 Saint Rose PKWY STE 324 Henderson, Nevada 89052-4840 for the computer based Comprehensive Cost and Requirements (CCaR)/Project Management Reporting Tool (PMRT) System Support. This will be in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 12-Acquisition of Commercial Items. Integrated Data Services, Inc. is the only known company that is capable of providing CCaR software. Acquisition of services will be to provide Administrative and Training support to the use of the Air Force mandated Comprehensive Cost and Requirement (CCaR)/(PMRT) accounting system. The North American Industry Classification System Code (NAICS) is 541511 and the size standard is $34M. The Government intends to solicit and negotiate with only one source under the authority of 10 U.S.C. 3572. This notice is not a request for competitive proposals. Any interested parties believing they can meet all the requirements listed above, may submit a capability statement detailing how all requirements can be met. All information furnished shall be in writing and must contain sufficient detail to allow the government representative to evaluate and determine if it can meet the requirement. Responses must be received no later than (NLT) Monday 9:00 A.M., Pacific Standard Time, 17 March 2026. Responses should be submitted to: Brian Wu (AFTC/PZIOA) E-mail: brian.wu@us.af.mil. Alternate contractual point of contact Norman Mackie, E-mail: norman.mackie.1@us.af.mil. Requests to be placed on a mailing list will not be honored.

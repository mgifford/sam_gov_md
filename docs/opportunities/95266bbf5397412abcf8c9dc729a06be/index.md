## Summary

IA-Port Louisa NWR Boat Ramp Project. Remove existing concrete boat ramp,and replace with new concrete boat ramp. Seeking contractors to repair a damaged and degraded public boat ramp to the Mississippi River from the Louisa Division of the refuge. With river levels low, refuge staff have determined that previously-identified concrete degradation of a section of the ramp is accelerating and presents a property hazard to users of the river that must be monitored by refuge staff on a weekly basis to ensure its continued use. Site Visit- March 11, 2026, 9:00 AM CST, email Nathan Barnett, Nathan_barnett@fws.gov, or call, (636)474-9749, no later than 24 hours prior to the site visit. Questions- March 12, 2026, emailed to Bill Koski, william_koski@fws.gov Quotes Due- March 27, 2026, at 3:00 PM EST, to emailed to Bill Koski, william_koski@fws.gov

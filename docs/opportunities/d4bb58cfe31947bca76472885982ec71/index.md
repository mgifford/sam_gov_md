# PA154/PA157/PA191 Metal Containers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 10:17:38.495-04
- Solicitation Number: W15QKN26D0005
- Awardee: MOUNTAIN HORSE LLC Colorado Springs CO 80903 USA
- Award Amount: 31710221.30

## Summary

This effort is for the award of Manufacture and Delivery of PA154/PA157/PA191 Mortar Ammunition Metal Containers, which are gasket sealed rectangular metal containers that serve as the outer pack for 60mm/81mm/120mm Mortar Cartridges. The PA154 Metal Container supports the 120mm Smoke and Illumination (Illum) Mortar Cartridges, M929/M930/M983. The PA 157 Metal Container supports the 81mm Smoke and Illum. Mortar Cartridges M819/M853A1/M816. The PA191 Metal Container supports the 60mm Smoke and Illum. Mortar Cartridges M722A1/M721/M767. Additionally, the PA154 holds two 120mm Mortar Cartridges, the PA157 holds three 81mm Mortar Cartridges, and the PA191 holds eight 60mm Mortar Cartridges.

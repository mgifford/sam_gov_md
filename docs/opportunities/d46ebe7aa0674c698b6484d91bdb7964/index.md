# Fitness Equipment Maintenance & Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 16:29:58.647-04
- Solicitation Number: H9224026QE023

## Summary

Naval Special Warfare Command (NSWC) has a requirement for maintenance and repair of fitness equipment located in San Diego, CA. The acquisition will be conducted in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 12. This acquisition will utilize a 100% small business set-aside. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this effort is 811310, and the small business size standard is $12.5M. The Government intends to award a five-year Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) type contract, issuing Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Task Orders. The estimated minimum contract value is $1,000.00 and the estimated maximum contract value is $100,000.00. This synopsis shall not be construed as a RFP or an obligation on the part of the Government.

This is an amendment to post the answers to the questions that were submitted to this RFI. =============================================================================================== The Executive Office of the President (EOP) Office of Administration (OA) is conducting this Request for Information (RFI) to identify Contractors who possess the capabilities to provide services as specified herein and to determine their availability and capability. Questions regarding this RFI shall be emailed, utilizing the attached spreadsheet titled "RFI 11316026N0001OAS_Questions", to the Contracting Officer (CO), Heather Poindexter (e-mail: Heather.L.Poindexter@oa.eop.gov), and Contract Specialist (CS), Jeanniene Warner (e-mail: Jeanniene.C.Warner@oa.eop.gov) no later than March 16, 2026 at 12:00 PM Eastern Daylight Time (EDT).

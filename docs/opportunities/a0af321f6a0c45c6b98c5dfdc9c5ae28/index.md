# G&L Horizontal Boring Mills Preventive Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 12:22:51.702-04
- Solicitation Number: N32253-26-Q-0015

## Summary

PHNSY & IMF intends to award a sole source, firm fixed price contract to Fives Machining Systems to provide a qualified and authorized original equipment manufacturer (OEM) to provide on-site preventive maintenance on G & L Horizontal Boring mills. This procurement is conducted under the authority of FAR 6.302-1, "Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements." This requirement consists of one-year base period, four (4) one-year option periods and a 6 month extension. The planned period of performance is 4/27/2026-4/26/2031. Please note the following: This notice is NOT a request for competitive proposals, and a formal solicitation will not be issued. This announcement is for informational purposes only. However, responsible sources may submit a capability statement for consideration. Submissions must be sent via email to both the primary and secondary points of contact by the closing date of this notice.

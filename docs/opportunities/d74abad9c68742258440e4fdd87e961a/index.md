## Summary

Amendment 1 - FA520526R00040001 - Misawa Information Monitoring and Protection Services (IMPS) The purpose of this amendment is to: Provide updated PWS. PWS updated to reflect: - Clarified work hours for SSM, CST and CAGs - Deliverable/requirement for mission essential work Add mission essential provision and clause. All other terms and conditions remain unchanged and in effect. These changes comes as a result of a request from the customer to ensure Intelligence Community Directive (ICD) 705 compliance. **************************************************************************************************************************************************** This is an official solicitation, FA520526R0004, for Information Monitoring and Protection Services (IMPS) at Misawa Air Base. See attachments for additional information. "The Contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment and its maintenance, supplies, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items to perform construction security surveillance services to support secure construction projects IAW ICS 705 and DoD policies. Information Monitoring and Protection Services (IMPS) will be used for the successful design, build and accreditation of SCIFs. Certain personnel are required to have secret or top secret clearances, depending on the role specified in the attached PWS." Interested vendors are required to possess a TS Facility Clearance. the propossed pricing shall be fully burdened. This is will be a firm-fixed-price IDIQ, base plus 4 option year contract. This is a solicitation and proposals are currently being requested. When submitting your offer, include both points of contract in your email. Deadline for feedback, clarifications, or questions is Monday, 16 March 2026 at 0900 JST (UTC+9). See Solicitation for submission instructions. Deadline for offers is Wednesday, 01 April 2026 at 1000 JST (UTC+9).

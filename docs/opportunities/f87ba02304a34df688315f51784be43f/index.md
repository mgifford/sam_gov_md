# Electric Sit-Down Tow Tractor

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 13:59:37.091-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462526Q1049

## Summary

This is an official Request for Quote (RFQ) Notice (FA462526Q1049). The U.S. Air Force 509th Contracting Squadron at Whiteman AFB is posting this solicitation, Request for Quote (RFQ), for purchase of an Electric Sit-Down Tow Tractor that meets the specifications of the attached Salient Characteristics dated 28 July 2025. DESCRIPTION OF REQUIREMENT: A functioning Electric Sit-Down Tow Tractor. Characteristics of TOYOTA MODEL CBT4 or equal. 8,800 lbs tow capacity. 48V Electric Battery Powered. DELIVERABLES: The contractor shall deliver 120 days after receipt of the order. Delivery will be to Whiteman AFB, MO 65305. RESPONSES: Questions are due to both the Contracting Officer: Molly Nussbaum, at molly.nussbaum@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 2:00 PM CDT, Tuesday, 24 March 2026. Questions received after that time may not be answered. To ensure all questions and responses are received, questions and responses must include the RFQ Number (FA462526Q1049) and title Electic Sit-Down Tow Tractor in the subject line of the email submission and are to be sent via email to both the Contracting Officer: Molly Nussbaum, at molly.nussbaum@us.af.mil and the Contract Specialist: Najee Holland, at najee.holland@us.af.mil NLT 2:00 PM CDT, Thursday, 26 March 2026.

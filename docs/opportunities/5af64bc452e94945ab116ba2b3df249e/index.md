## Summary

The International Narcotics and Law Enforcement Affairs Office (INL) in Bogota has a requirement for the supply of veterinary tools for K9. All interested sellers should submit their quotations according to the attached PR15696251_RFQ by May 8, 2026 at 4:00 pm (local Bogota time) via e-mail to Arizak@state.gov and BogotaINLSolicitation@state.gov. No quotations will be accepted after this time. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotations are being requested by this notice alone, and a written solicitation will not be issued. To be considered for award, please follow the instructions given in the document INL-RFQ Section 3.

# 316 CES Washer Dryer and Cooktops Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 14:38:01.212-04
- Solicitation Number: FA286026Q0011

## Summary

316 CES/CEOES require leasing, installation and maintenance of 387 combined washers, dryers, and electric cooktops on Joint Base Andrews (JBA). This requirement will require the contractor to provide furnishing of all appliances, personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, transportation and all other items and services necessary to provide and install contractor owned washers, dryers, and built-in electric cook tops on (JBA). The contractor is responsible for providing and maintaining 70 Standard Washers, 2 Standard Dryers, 35 Vent-less Dryers, 11 Front-load washers, 3 ADA-Compliant Washers, 54 Washer/Dryer Combo Units for laundry center, 52 Stackable Dryer/Dryer sets, 100 Built-in Electric 2-Cook tops, and 60 Built-in 4 Electric Cook-tops. All work shall be performed IAW the Performance Work Statement (PWS) and all local, state, and federal rules and procedures as outlined in the PWS. The period of performance is three hundred and sixty-five (365) days with four (4) options years, plus the inclusion of 52.217-8 clause to extend the services of the contract up to six months. A site visit for this solicitation will be held on 23 April 2026 at 0900. Interested parties shall complete a JBA base access request via Attachment #4 and submit to kala.kim@us.af.mil and ryan.lockard@us.af.mil no later than 17 April 2026 at 4:30pm EST. Failure to correctly complete a base access request, or to submit by 17 April 2026, may result in disqualified access to the base. Please see the attached documents: Washer Dryer Cooktop Combo Attachemnt 1- performance work statement Attachment 2- SCA Wage Determination Attachment 3- Contractor Price Estimate Attachment 4- Base Access Form Attachment 5- Washer Dryer Cooktop

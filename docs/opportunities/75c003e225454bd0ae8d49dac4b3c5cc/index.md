## Summary

Fire Ecology Research REQUEST FOR QUOTATION SUBJECT TO AVAILABILITY OF FUNDS A firm fixed price purchase order subject to the Service Contract act is planned. This is a solicitation for commercial services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotations/proposals are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. This solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC) 2026-01, effective 03/13/2026. NAICS Code: 541620 Environmental Consulting Services. Size standard: $19.0 Requirement: The USGS Western Ecological Research Center requires research and data synthesization in support of a fire ecological project. The contractor shall: use statistical analyses to diagnose how fire response strategies vary statewide in California and provide research in digital formats. Contractor requirements: Contractor will assume all tasks related to statistical analyses of ecological data. Contractor shall provide the necessary staff and equipment to perform the task outlined in the statement of work. Submission instructions: Signed SF18 and any amendments if applicable. Firm fixed price quote with full breakdown. Email quotes and documents to: Anthony Berry at amberry@ios.doi.gov no later than 04/13/2026 at 5:00am Pacific time. Physical submissions and late submissions shall not be accepted. It is the vendors responsibility to provide adequate information regarding technical expertise and past performance to facilitate evaluation of their submissions. Technical Submission Requirements: Vendors shall submit sufficient information to allow for evaluation of their quote, including: Technical approach describing how the work will be performed Relevant experience performing similar work Key personnel qualifications At least one (1) prior project of comparable scope and complexity

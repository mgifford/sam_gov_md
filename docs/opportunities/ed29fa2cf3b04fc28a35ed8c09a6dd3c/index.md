# Full Food Services, Naval Support Activity, Panama City

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-27 17:12:29.217-04
- Solicitation Number: N6883626C0003
- Awardee: FLORIDA DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION Tallahassee FL 32399 USA

## Summary

The Firm-Fixed Price Contract N6883626C0003 was awarded to Florida Department of Education (FLDOE) for Full Food and Mess Attendant Services in support of Naval Support Activity (NSA) Panama City, FL for a total contract value of $12,099,799.86. This requirement was solicited as a 100% Small Business Set-Aside pursuant to the Randolph-Sheppard Act in which seven (7) firms submitted offers in response to the solicitation. Contract N6883626C0003 includes a base 12-month period, four 12-month option periods, and a six-month extension in accordance with FAR 52.217-8 (if necessary). The base period of performance is from 1 April 2026 through 31 March 2027.

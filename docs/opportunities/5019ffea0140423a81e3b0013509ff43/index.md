# Defense Procurement Network

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:26:11.557-04
- Solicitation Number: H9821026S007

## Summary

The contractor shall provide a commercial off-the-shelf software application to support the Defense Procurement Network (DPN), a business-matching and event-management platform for the Department of Human Resources Activity (DHRA). The DPN will enable DHRA program offices to engage with small businesses nationwide while providing another avenue of market research initiatives. This will help to reduce redundancies while providing direct access to on their processes, requirements, contracting vehicles utilized and points of engagement.

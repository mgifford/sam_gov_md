# NASA Space to Soil Challenge: Call for Submissions and Self-Nominations for Judges

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-18 16:18:49.939-04
- Solicitation Number: 80JSC026BR0002

## Summary

The purpose of this SAM.gov notice is to announce that the National Aeronautics and Space Administration’s Earth Science Technology Office (ESTO) has contracted with Blue Clarity LLC to implement the NASA Space to Soil Challenge to advance the Agency's science and exploration goals, particularly in small satellite computational technologies to support regenerative agriculture and forestry innovation through adaptive sensing and onboard processing. Open and carefully read the attached PDF file entitled “NASA Space to Soil Challenge Summary.” Direct links have been provided for more information on registration for the Challenge and for completing the application for the judging process. After reading the PDF attachment, by email only, address questions or other comments with a subject line to read “NASA Space to Soil Challenge Question/Clarification” to the listed primary point of contact. Technical questions regarding the challenge website and how to submit should be emailed directly to info@NASA-Space-to-Soil.org.

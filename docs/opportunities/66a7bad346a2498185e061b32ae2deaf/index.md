# Painting Frame Fabrication Services

- Agency: LIBRARY OF CONGRESS
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 17:17:35.943-04
- Solicitation Number: 030ADV26Q0046

## Summary

The Library of Congress (LOC) Exhibits Office (EO) seeks a highly experienced museum exhibition Framer to create custom, conservation grade frames, acrylic glazing, and support components as needed, for paintings on canvas. Contractor must also fit the paintings in the frames on-site at LOC prior to display and provide and attach hanging and security hardware to the frames, appropriate to secure the finished objects to the exhibit walls. The installation of the frames at the exhibit will be completed by LOC.

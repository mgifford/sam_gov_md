# Aircraft Fire Extinguisher Cartridges MT20 & TY60

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 12:05:00.886-04
- Solicitation Number: PANROC26P0000029998

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command, Rock Island intends to procure Aircraft Fire Extinguisher Cartridges, Department of Defense Identification Code (DODIC) MT20 and TY60 via a limited competition utilizing tradeoffs between Ametek Ameron and KIDDE Technologies, in accordance with (IAW) Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. This notice is intended for subcontracting opportunities only; it is not a request for competitive proposals. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code applicable to this announcement is 325920 with a size standard of 750 employees. This synopsis does not constitute an RFP and shall not be construed as a commitment by the Government for any purpose. All questions and comments regarding this notice shall be directed to Emily Kundel at emily.a.kundel.civ@army.mil, Meghan Newman at meghan.p.newman.civ@army.mil, and Caitlyn Smith at caitlyn.l.smith3.civ@army.mil.

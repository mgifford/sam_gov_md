# W. Kerr Scott Lake and Dam Bridge Repainting

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:14:20.487-04
- Solicitation Number: W912PM26BA005

## Summary

The US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Wilmington District (SAW) has a requirement for repainting service of the W. Kerr Scott Dam outlet tower access bridge located in Wilkesboro, NC. Included in this requirement is the removal of the original lead-based coating system which has reached end of life, and replacing it with a vinyl coating system at W. Kerr Scott Reservoir.

# NOI - Preventative Maintenance and Support for Sony ID7000 Cell Analyzer

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-24 07:14:02.899-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N98026Q00253

## Summary

The National Institute on Aging (NIA), Intramural Research Program (IRP), intends to award a sole source purchase order to Sony Biotechnology Inc. for preventative maintenance and service support for a Sony ID7000 Cell Analyzer. This requirement includes OEM-authorized service, repair, and annual preventative maintenance to ensure the instrument remains fully operational and calibrated in accordance with manufacturer specifications. The period of performance is May 11, 2026 through May 10, 2027. This notice is not a request for competitive quotes; however, interested parties may submit capability statements demonstrating their ability to meet the Government’s requirement. Please refer to the attached Notice of Intent for complete details.

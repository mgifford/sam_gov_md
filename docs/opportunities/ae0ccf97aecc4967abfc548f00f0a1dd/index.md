# DEPARTMENT OF STATE WORLDWIDE AVIATION SUPPORT SERVICES

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 09:58:11.071-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AQMM26N0198

## Summary

The U.S. Department of State (DoS), Bureau of International Narcotics and Law Enforcement Affairs (INL), Office of Aviation and Cross Border Programs, Aviation Division (INL/ACB/A), also referred to as the Air Wing, is conducting market research to identify qualified sources capable of providing comprehensive worldwide aviation support services. This RFI is issued solely for information and planning purposes; it does not constitute a solicitation and does not commit the Government to contract for any supply or service. Responses to this RFI will inform the Department’s acquisition strategy for a forthcoming Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract. Capable offerors are requested to respond to the Department's Request for Information which may be found at https://forms.cloud.microsoft/g/n6tZ5LR0bu?origin=lprLink.

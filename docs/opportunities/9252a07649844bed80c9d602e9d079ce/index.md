## Summary

Update Number 0001: The purpose of this amendment is to assist interested offerors in preparing their responses offline, a PDF copy of the full market survey questionnaire has been added to the attachments/links section of this notice. The Contractor shall provide program engineering and IPT management services directly to the NIWC Pacific NEN program office and its procurement and installation efforts. The Contractor shall perform program planning, engineering and integration, project management (PM), and technical analysis. Attached to this notice are the official Market Survey document and a consolidated Question and Answer (QA) document. The QA provides responses to all timely questions received from interested parties regarding this requirement.

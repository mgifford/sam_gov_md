# Vegetation Management Services for Chattahoochee-Oconee & Conasauga Ranger Districts

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 12:54:59.327-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444726Q0013

## Summary

The USDA Forest Service, Chattahoochee?Oconee National Forests, requires vegetation management services on the Oconee Ranger District (ORD) and Conasauga Ranger District (CRD). Work includes: Wildlife midstory vegetation control Site preparation (foliar and mechanical) Seedling release and weeding treatments All labor, equipment, supervision, tools, herbicides, and materials shall be provided by the contractor. No Government-furnished materials or equipment will be provided. Period of Performance August 1, 2026 – October 31, 2026 Project Locations Oconee Ranger District (ORD): Putnam, Jones, Jasper, Oglethorpe & Greene Counties, GA Conasauga Ranger District (CRD): Murray & Chattooga Counties, GA Scope of Work The contractor shall perform: Oconee Ranger District (ORD) 457 acres – Wildlife midstory treatment (cut?surface & foliar) 256 acres – Site preparation (foliar) 26 acres – Site preparation (mechanical) Conasauga Ranger District (CRD) 174 acres – Seedling release and weeding (basal bark, foliar, manual slashdown) Total acreage: 913 acres A detailed Statement of Work is included as an attachment.

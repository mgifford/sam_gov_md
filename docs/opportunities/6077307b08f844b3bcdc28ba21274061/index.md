## Summary

THIS IS A PRESOLICITATION NOTICE FOR A COMMERCIAL ACQUISITION USING FAR PART 12 UNDER SIMPLIFIED ACQUISITION PROCEDURES. THIS PRESOLICITATION NOTICE IS NOT A REQUEST FOR QUOTES (RFQ). The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Veterans Health Administration (VHA), Network Contracting Office (NCO) 15, intends to issue a Request for Quote (RFQ) for radiopharmaceuticals for the Robert J. Dole VA Medical Center, 5500 E. Kellog Avenue, Wichita, KS 67218-1607. The anticipated RFQ number is 36C25526Q0306, associated with Action ID# 36C255-26-AP-1665. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code is 325412, Pharmaceutical Preparation Manufacturing, and the Product Service Code (PSC) is 6505, Drugs and Biologicals. This requirement will be issued as an unrestricted solicitation. The Government anticipates awarding a single-award, firm-fixed-price, five-year Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract with five (5) twelve-month ordering periods for the provision and delivery of radiopharmaceuticals to support Nuclear Medicine Services at the Robert J. Dole VA Medical Center in Wichita, Kansas. Individual task/delivery orders will be issued by the NCO 15 Contracting Officer under the resultant IDIQ contract. The contractor shall furnish all labor, supervision, supplies, equipment, licenses, and transportation necessary to provide radiopharmaceuticals in accordance with the forthcoming solicitation and Statement of Work. The RFQ is expected to be posted on or about April 24, 2026, with quotes due on or about May 15, 2026; the official closing date and time for receipt of quotes will be stated in the RFQ. NCO 15 Contracting Office Address: Network Contracting Office 15 3450 S. 4th Street Leavenworth, KS 66048 Place of Performance: Robert J. Dole VA Medical Center 5500 E. Kellog Avenue Wichita, KS 67218-1607 Solicitation Number: 36C25526Q0306 Action ID#: 36C255-26-AP-1665 NAICS: 325412 PSC: 6505 The RFQ and any amendments will be issued electronically and will be available only through the Governmentwide Point of Entry (GPE) at SAM.gov (Contract Opportunities). Interested parties are responsible for monitoring the GPE for the posting of the RFQ and any amendments. No paper copies of the solicitation will be issued. This presolicitation notice is not a request for quotes, and no telephone requests for the RFQ will be accepted. No contract award will be made from this announcement. All interested vendors must be registered and active in the System for Award Management (SAM) at https://sam.gov under the applicable NAICS code at the time of quote submission and at the time of award. Questions concerning this announcement may be directed to: Point of Contact: Erika Kobulnicky, Contracting Officer Network Contracting Office 15 Email: Erika.Kobunicky@va.gov

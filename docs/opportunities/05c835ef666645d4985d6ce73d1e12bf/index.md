# SHIPPINGPORT (ARDM)-4 - Government Owned Contractor Operated (GOCO) Operation and Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-14 10:20:27.757-04
- Solicitation Number: N00024-26-C-4311
- Awardee: ELECTRIC BOAT CORPORATION Groton CT 06340 USA
- Award Amount: 57087819.00

## Summary

General Dynamics Electric Boat, Groton, CT was awarded a $10,610,029 Cost Plus Fixed Fee contract, inclusive of one base year and four option years, for the operation, maintenance and protection of the Navy's floating dry dock, SHIPPINGPORT (ARDM-4). This contract contains options, which if exercised, will make the cumulative value of the contract $57,087,819. This contract was not awarded competitively.

## Summary

Plant maintenance and holiday decor services for multiple VISN 5 locations directed under the Washington DC VA Medical Center. The Department of Veterans Affairs, Veterans Health Administration, Maryland Healthcare System has awarded an Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity Contract (IDIQ) to Omniscape Solutions LLC for a period of 5 years with 5 individual pricing periods. The contract award date is 3/10/2026 and the ordering period is 1 April 2026 through 31 March 2031.

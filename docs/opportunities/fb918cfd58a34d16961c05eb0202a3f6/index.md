## Summary

The primary objective of the project is to apply a protective coating to the offshore structure, which will prevent corrosion and ensure the integrity of the structure is maintained. The recoating project will include surface preparation, blasting, and painting, utilizing high-quality coatings and techniques that meet the applicable standards and regulations. The coating will be applied to the entire structure, including the underwater portion, to ensure complete protection against corrosion.

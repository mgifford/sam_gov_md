Requirement State: Sanford City: North Carolina Delineated Area: North: Intersection of Jefferson Highway and Highway 421 South: Intersection of Tramway Road and Jefferson Davis Highway East: Intersection of Tramway Road and Highway 421 West: Intersection of Tramway Road and Jefferson Davis Highway Minimum RSF: 11,285 Maximum RSF: 11,285 Space Type: Office Space All services, supplies, utilities, and tenant alterations to be provided as part of the rental consideration. The Department of Veterans Affairs will consider the cost of moving, tenant improvements, etc., when deciding whether the Sanford CBOC should relocate. Any expressions of interest should be directed to Jackie Bray, Lease Contracting Officer. Email (Jackie.Bray@va.gov) is the preferred method of communication.

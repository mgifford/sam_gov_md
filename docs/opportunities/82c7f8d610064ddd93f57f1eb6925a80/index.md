# REQUEST FOR INFORMATION (RFI)/SOURCES SOUGHT ? NON-PERSONAL ACQUISITION ENGINEERING, PRODUCT SUPPORT AND LOGISTICS (PSL) SUPPORT TO THE MARITIME ELECTROMAGNETIC WARFARE DIVISION- PSC R706 - NAICS 541330

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-13 16:21:45.18-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016426SNB39

## Summary

This Request for Information/Sources Sought Notice is issued by Naval Surface Warfare Center (NSWC), Crane Division Contracting Department, Crane, IN. The NSWC Crane Contracting Department is seeking potential sources to provide non-personal Acquisition Engineering, Product Support and Logistics (PSL) support for current and future Maritime Electromagnetic Warfare (MEW) projects in the areas of programmatic services, supply support, technical manual development, training development, data integrity validation, reliability, maintainability, data analytics, and technical writing. These services are in support of full life-cycle support for military and national security Electromagnetic Warfare and Decoy systems, including Special Projects, Operations, Sustainment Software, Sustaining Engineering, Modernization and Fleet Services.

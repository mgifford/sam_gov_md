# Repair Deep Well 051 for Closure - Site Visit

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 10:42:20-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC26R0001

## Summary

This is a notice that a solicitation is anticipated to be forthcoming and will have all the necessary documents. Please see the attached Scope of Work (SOW) PR-2026-0214 for detailed project requirements. IAW FAR 36.204(d), the magnitude of this project is between $100,000.00 and $500,000.00. Performance and Payment Bonds are required. The scope of this project is to seal an approximately 380ft deep, disused water well. The Contractor is required to employ the necessary equipment, instruments, and materials to accomplish eh task of sealing this old well in accordance with Illinois Administrative Code Title 77 Chapter I Section 920.120. The Contractor shall complete the necessary reporting to the County of Rock Island Public Health Department including paying fees. There will be a Pre-Solicitation Site Visit held on Tuesday, April 28, 2026 at 9:00am Central Time to ensure all potential contractors have a clear understanding of the site and scope of work. All site visit attendees will meet at the location identified on the Site Visit Map attachment. ******PLEASE READ CAREFULLY FOR IMPORTANT SITE VISIT INFORMATION****** ALL ATENDEES MUST ACCESS THE ISLAND THROUGH THE MOLINE GATE AND STOP AT BUILDING 23, VISITOR CONTROL CENTER, TO GAIN ACCESS TO THE ISLAND UNLESS YOU HAVE AN EXISTING BADGE TO GAIN ACCESS. YOU WILL NEED TO SHOW A VALID DRIVERS LICENSE. PLEASE ALLOW 60 MINUTES PRIOR TO THE SITE VISIT FOR PROCESSING. NOTE: Effective May 7, 2025, non-Real ID compliant identification will no longer be accepted for accessing Government Facilities. PLESE EMAIL THE NAMES OF THE ATTENDEES FROM YOUR COMPANY TO hila.r.anderson.civ@army.mil and kathleen.m.ward20.civ@army.mil.

PROJECT DESCRIPTION: The Norfolk District Corps of Engineers is contemplating a firm-fixed-price contract for the Design-Bid-Build construction of Building 32 (B32) at Defense Supply Center Richmond (DSCR), VA. The scope of the project in B32 consists of 5 bays, named H, I, K, L, and M. The boiler central plant is located in Bay G, which will require work as part of this project. The interior demolition of bays H through M will include removal of the existing drop ceiling, removal of existing flooring, existing toilet rooms, and MEP systems. The new work in Bays H and I will provide spaces for auditoriums, classrooms, conference rooms, and workstations. The new work in Bays K, L, and M will provide an open-floor concept similar to the style in WH 15 H-bay and installation of new MEP systems to accommodate the new floor plan. Chilled water and heating hot water plants will be upgraded to include new pumps, chillers, and steam to hot water heat exchangers to serve the building’s heating and cooling needs.

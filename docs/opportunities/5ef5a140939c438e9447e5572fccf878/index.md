# Remanufacture F108 Turbine Clearance Control Valve

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 14:33:07.978-05
- Solicitation Number: FA8122-25-R-0003

## Summary

This acquisition is to acquire the remanufacture of the F108 Turbine Clearance Control Valve, NSN: 4810-01-460-3279PN. This is a critical safety item and qualification requirements apply. The selected awardee must be an approved qualified source at time of award. For qualification information and Source Approval Requests (SAR), contact the Source Development, AFSC/Small Business Office at afsc.sb.workflow@us.af.mil. This solicitation is being issued as a sole source requirment, IAW DAFFARS 5313.501, to AAR Corp. All responsible sources may submit a capability statement, proposal, or quotation, which shall be considered by the agency. However, Vendors are required to have an approved SAR prior to award. Award will not be delayed pending SAR approval. The effective date of the resultant contract will be on or after 18 September 2025.

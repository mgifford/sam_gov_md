# Full Food and Mess Attendant Services, Meridian, MS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-17 08:26:05.434-04
- Solicitation Number: N6883626C0001

## Summary

The Firm-Fixed Price Contract N6883626C0001 was awarded to Mississippi Department of Rehabilitation Services for Full Food and Mess Attendant Services in support of Naval Air Station (NAS) Meridian, MS for a total contract value of $11,878,485.30. This requirement was solicited as a 100% Small Business Set-Aside pursuant to the Randolph-Sheppard Act in which eight (8) firms submitted offers in response to the solicitation. Contract N6883626C0001 includes a base 12-month period, four 12-month option periods, and a six-month extension in accordance with FAR 52.217-8 (if necessary). The base period of performance is from 1 April 2026 through 31 March 2026.

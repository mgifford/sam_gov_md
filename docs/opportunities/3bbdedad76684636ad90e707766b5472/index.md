# USDA Forest Service Wolf Creek Job Corps Center Water Tank Cleaning and Inspection

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 08:30:00.009-04
- Solicitation Number: 12318726Q0046

## Summary

The Washington Office PPS Job Corps Support Branch has a requirement for a contractor to provide cleaning services for a cast-in-place concrete potable water storage tanks located at the Wolf Creek Job Corps Center. The Government is looking to award a Firm Fixed Price type contract to a single contractor who offers the best value to the Government, price and other factors considered. The tank is a cast-in-place concrete with an approximate capacity of 165,000 gallons.

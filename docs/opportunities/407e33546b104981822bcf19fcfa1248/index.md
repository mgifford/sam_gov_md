THIS IS A FIRM FIXED-PRICE SUPPLY TYPE CONTRACT NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound is negotiating a TOTAL SMALL BUSINESS SET ASIDE requirement for the procurement of Ready Service Lockers.

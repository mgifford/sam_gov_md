## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), Network Contracting Office (NCO) 21, plans to issue a Request for Quote (RFQ) for Health Care for Homeless Veterans (HCHV) Contracted Emergency Residential Services (CERS) for Homeless Geriatric Veterans in San Mateo, Santa Clara, or Southern Alameda County for VA Palo Alto Health Care System (VAPAHCS) in accordance with the performance work statement contained herein. The solicitation number for this RFQ is 36C26126Q0539. The solicitation will be issued on or about April 22nd, 2026, with an anticipated due date of May 29th, 2026. The North American Industry Classification Systems (NAICS) is 624221, size standard $13.5M. Contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) at www.sam.gov. Please send any questions or inquires to Lacy.Morgan@va.gov.

# Carbon Crunch

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 15:44:30.853-04
- Solicitation Number: DARPA-PS-26-15

## Summary

Please contact the Carbon Crunch email for Amendment 0002. Carbon Crunch aims to enable scalable, fast, and agile production of hypersonic aeroshells. The program will focus on accelerating carbon-carbon (C-C) hypersonic aeroshell production by developing and applying cutting-edge composite manufacturing methods that are inherently faster and more scalable. Performers will be asked to demonstrate an end-to-end manufacturing process (from raw materials to near-net-shape densification) that demonstrates high quality, rapid production, and quick design-cycle turnaround. Once the process demonstrates reasonable cost and orders-of-magnitude improvement in throughput, the technology can be transitioned to hypersonic programs of record.

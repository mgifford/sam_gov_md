## Summary

Request for Proposal (RFP) N0017426R1012 is hereby issued as of 24 March 2026. The proposed contract action will be a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) supply type contract for the fabrication, inspection, and delivery of MK 70 Booster Ballast Hardware Kits, in accordance with the Statement of Work (SOW) and applicable drawing package. The contractor shall fabricate, inspect, test, and deliver MK70 Booster Ballast Hardware Kits in accordance with Statement of Work (SOW), packaged as directed, and ready for use for the Base and four (4) consecutive 12-month Option Years. The anticipated Period of Performance (PoP) is 6/30/2026- 6/30/2031. This is a follow on requirement to N0017423D0001. This is a total small business set-aside with NAICS Code 332999 and PSC 1340. Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) Level 2 Self is required. There are no known security requirements associated with this requirement. Please refer to attachments for more information.

# Big Top Shelter Sustainment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 09:46:05.647-04
- Solicitation Number: FA481926Q0007

## Summary

Amendment to the solicitation 1. Attached Questions and Answers (Q and A FA481926Q0007) 2. Attached updated PWS - Paragraph 2.1 change semi annually to annually. (PWS dated 9 March 2026) Annual Preventative Maintenance existing aircraft sunshades on Tyndall AFB flight line. Option years one through four will add 6 additional Sunshades for a total of 12. All Five (5) years will include one (1) larger sunshade. The Contractor shall inspect, maintain and repair aircraft sunshades, to includes structure, structural connections, fabric, lights, electric, lightning protection, obstruction lighting, safety reflective devices, and any other base-unique attached features, as applicable. Attachments: 1. Solicitation - FA481926Q0007 2. Big Top Sustainment PWS 25 Feb 26

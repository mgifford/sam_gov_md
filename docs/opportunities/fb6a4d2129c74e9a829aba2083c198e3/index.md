# Justification for Award of Single Source Task Order to Carahsoft Technology Corp.

- Agency: SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-31 14:09:51.375-04
- Solicitation Number: 67000-26-0002

## Summary

The requirement is for access to Wolters Kluwer Financial Services’ TeamMate+ Software License Maintenance on-premise, migration to the cloud, and hosted maintenance for previously purchased licenses, to provide project management software specifically tailored to meet the needs of the Office of Inspector General (OIG) auditors and managers to effectively plan, manage, and maintain ongoing and future projects in one central area.

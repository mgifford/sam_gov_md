# M82 Energetics

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-27 07:50:26.497-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC26RA019

## Summary

The United States Army Contracting Command-Rock Island requests a proposal for the energetic subcomponents of the M82 Simulant Screening Grenade in support of Pine Bluff Arsenal, in accordance with (IAW) Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR 15)-Contracting by Negotiation. This solicitation is expected to result in a five (5) year, Firm Fixed price (FFP) Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to the offeror whose propose is responsible and adheres to all the terms and conditions of the solicitation with the lowest priced technically acceptable proposal. This requirement is issued under NACIS 332993 and will be a Small Business Set Aside.

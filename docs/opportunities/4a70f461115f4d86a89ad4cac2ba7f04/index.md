# Dover AFB - AFMAO Cadaver Racks

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-03 15:34:43.438-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4497-26-Racks

## Summary

This is a Request for Information for Market Research purposes ONLY. We are looking for vendors capable of supplying the items with the following specifications: A 3-tier, free-standing cadaver rack system designed for a refrigerated vehicle. Capacity: Each of the three tiers must support at least 500 lbs. The system must be able to hold either a standard transfer case (approx. 88"L x 28"W x 18"H) or a 23" wide stainless steel body tray. Dimensions & Clearance: The total height cannot exceed 74". There must be at least 2-4 inches of clearance above a stored transfer case, and the bottom rack must be approximately 2 inches off the floor. Materials: All racks and support columns must be made of rust-resistant, .120" thick stainless steel tubing. Accessories: The inquiry includes a requirement for 96 compatible stainless steel body trays (23" width) and 96 nylon securing straps with buckles. Features & Delivery: The system must include leveling pads and be delivered disassembled and palletized.

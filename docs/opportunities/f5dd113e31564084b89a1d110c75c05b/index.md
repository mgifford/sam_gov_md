# Immersive Mixed Reality Medical Education for Rapid Simulation and Emergency Care - (AFMEDCOM)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-23 09:15:30.565-04
- Solicitation Number: FA800326SS003

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) seeks to identify commercial products and capabilities for a fully immersive mixed reality (MR) simulation platform. The information gathered will be used to analyze the marketplace and identify available solutions that meet government requirements. All businesses, both large and small, are encouraged to respond. The anticipated North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 513210: Software Publishers. Please note, responses to this notice are not offers and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract.

# MCIA Geospatial Support Services (GSS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 18:11:18.84-04
- Solicitation Number: M0026426R0010

## Summary

The scope of this effort is to provide support the Marine Corps Intelligence Activity (MCIA) with geospatial technical services to support integration and dissemination of geospatial information and intelligence products. This requirement is to provide the enhancements and technical support needed to sustain and improve the MCIA Geospatial Support Services (GSS) technology, the command centralized geospatial portal for data discovery, downloading, and sharing intelligence information across the various military, intelligence community, and mission partner network domains. This is a follow-on procurement of contract M00264-21-C-0010.

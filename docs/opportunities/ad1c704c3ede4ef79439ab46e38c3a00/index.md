## Summary

60 MXS/AFREP has s requirement for soldering equipment, fume extraction equipment, and supply Items in accordance with the Statement of Work (SOW). These items will be used to repair aircraft components for all three aircraft systems located here on Travis AFB. The items requested are for brand name or equivalent but must match technical specifications as stated in this SOW. Additionally, these materials are essential for ensuring a safe, organized, and operationally ready work environment for electronics and avionics component repair operations.

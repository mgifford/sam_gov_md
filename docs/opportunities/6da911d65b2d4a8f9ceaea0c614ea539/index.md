## Summary

The Central AL VA Medical Center is seeking a compact automated bench-top molecular diagnostic platform designed for low- to mid-volume laboratories, primarily utilized for HPV testing and STI screening. That offers fully automated molecular testing, integrating sample extraction, real-time PCR amplification, and detection in a single system.

The Government is not soliciting quotes or proposals at this time. 2. Set-Asides: a. Anticipated Set-Aside Strategy: This is not a Set-Aside. 3. Codes for Services or Supplies a. Product Service Code (PSC): DG11 - IT and Telecom—Network: Satellite Communications and Telecom Access Services b. NAICS Code: 517111 – Wired Telecommunications Carriers 4. Description a. (i) Clear and Concise Description of Supplies or Services: Service Title: Local Telecommunication Service Brief Description: 60 COMM requires a contracted service to continue essential local area Telecommunication Services (TS) and features supporting Travis AFB. Site specific equipment connectivity requirements are identified in Performance Work Statement, along with required features. This requirement outlines the provision of comprehensive, 24/7/365 local telecommunication services, including the contractor to shall furnish all necessary labor, materials, and equipment to provide local exchange access and associated features. This includes full compatibility with the existing government infrastructure at no additional government expense. The contractor is responsible for installing and managing all required transmission equipment and cabling to the designated government demarcation points, adhering strictly to all applicable Federal, DoD, and industry standards, and noting that aerial cable installations are prohibited. b. Procedures for Award: The Government intends to award a single source contract directly to the incumbent, Granite Telecommunications, LLC. This action is taken to ensure the continuation of essential, 24/7/365 local telecommunication services for Travis AFB. The legal authority for this non-competitive action is cited as an unusual and compelling urgency under 10 U.S. Code § 3204(a)(2). The critical need arises from the impending expiration of the current contract (FA442725P0001) on March 31, 2026, coupled with an unforeseen delay in the strategic, long-term plan to transition these services to the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA). Anticipated Award Date: On or about: 1 April 2026 c. Trade Agreements Language: Not Applicable d. Technical Data: The Performance Work Statement is included as an attachment. e. Construction Contract Information: Not Applicable. f. Statement Regarding Responsible Sources: All responsible sources may submit a quotation, bid, or proposal, as appropriate, which will be considered by the agency. g. Competitive Status: The government intends to award a contract directly to Granite Telecommunications, LLC in accordance with FAR

12.102 Restricting Competition, and FAR 6.103-2 under the authority at 10 U.S. Code § 3204(a)(2) for unusual and compelling urgency. h. Qualification Requirement: Not Applicable

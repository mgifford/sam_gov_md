# Carlisle Barracks Job Order Contract (JOC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 17:48:19.714-05
- Solicitation Number: W5168W26RA007

## Summary

************ Update 6 March 26************** The purpose of this solicitation amendment is to: 1. Extend the due date from 10 March to 12 March 2026 2. Update paragraph 7.10 in the Statement of Work (SOW) All other items and conditions remain unchanged. ************************************************* This requirement is for Construction services for a broad variety of small minor construction projects at Carlisle Barracks, PA. Work is to be performed in accordance with (IAW) the Statement of Work (SOW) attached to the solicitation. This is a 100% 8(a) Competitive set-aside. The contract awarded as a result of this solicitation will be a single award, Firm Fixed Price (FFP), Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Job Order Contract (JOC) consisting of an ordering period of 60-months. The time for commencement of work and the calendar days allocated for completion of work will be determined at the task order level.

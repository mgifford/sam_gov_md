# NIST CUP-BOILER REPAIR & WELDING SERVICES

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-17 14:43:49.861-04
- Solicitation Number: OFPM_TEAM_SS26_13

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice to request information from industry to help the U.S. Department of Commerce, National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) determine the availability and capability of small businesses and other than small businesses to provide NIST an ASME “R” stamp qualified boiler repair and welding contractor to perform high pressure steam piping and boiler repair and mobile welding operations on a 24/7/365 callback basis for urgent CUP repairs and limited other repairs in site steam manholes and other spaces on a as needed basis. The anticipated period of performance for this requirement is (1) base year plus four (4) option years. Pleas see attached Sources Sought and Statement of Work (SOW) for details.

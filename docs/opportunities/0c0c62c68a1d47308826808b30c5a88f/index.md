# M4/M4A1 Carbine FMS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The U.S. Army Contracting Command - New Jersey (ACC-NJ), on behalf of Project Manager Soldier Lethality, awarded an Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Foreign Military Sales contract to Colt’s Manufacturing Company pursuant to the authority of 10.U.S.C. 2304(c )(1) Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements. This sole source contract is for M4 Carbine, NSN: 1005-01-382-0973, Part Number: 939000; and M4A1 Carbine, NSN: 1005-01-382-0953, Part Number: 12972700 with Firm Fixed Price (FFP), Delivery Orders to be issued from the Requirement Contract W15QKN-26-D-A008. The Contract has five (5) one-year ordering periods. Multiple delivery orders for the Foreign Military services can be placed on this contract over the five (5) years period of performance (POP) starting from the date of contract award.

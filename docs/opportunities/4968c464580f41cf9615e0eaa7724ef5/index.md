- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE

- Posted: 2026-04-09 15:55:00.34-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P4326Q0020

## Summary

Saratoga National Historical Park the contractor will build two American reproduction 6lb cannon carriages. The project will address the deteriorations from being exposed to the year-round weather on the battlefield. The project will require some lumber milling to get the lumber to the proper thickness and also require have some metal hardware fabricated.

# EWP 5087-001 Elkhorn Site & Northfork Church Site in McDowell County, West Virginia

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 12:14:46.538-04
- Solicitation Number: 12FPC326B0002

## Summary

Please see attachments for further information. NRCS is in need of construction services (not considered a commercial service) for erosion control in McDowell County, West Virginia. The erosion control measures will consist of laying wire mesh gabions, prepare foundation on shallow bedrock; rebar anchoring and riprap foundation protection. One of the sites is gabions, but the other is grouted rock riprap. Total small business, firm fixed price.

# 647 CES Architect and Engineer Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-03 15:54:22.974-04
- Solicitation Number: AF2FY26A-E

## Summary

This notice is issued for market research and public announcement purposes to identify qualified architect-engineer (A-E) firms capable of performing professional design services for renovation projects at Joint Base Pearl Harbor-Hickam (JBPHH), Hawaii. This notice is not a solicitation and does not constitute a request for price proposals. Responses will be used by the Government to assess the availability of qualified firms and to help determine the appropriate acquisition strategy.

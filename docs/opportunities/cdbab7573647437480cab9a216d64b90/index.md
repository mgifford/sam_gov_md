# High Temperature Vacuum Furnace with Liquid Nitrogen Tank and Vaporizer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 09:18:42.501-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE4A8-26-Q-0030

## Summary

DLA Weapon Support (Richmond) intends to issue a request for quote (RFQ) in accordance with FAR 13.5. Description of requirements: Vacuum Furnace with Bulk Liquid Nitrogen Tank in accordance with Technical Specification VIBB 23-35-1002. Performance period/completion 283 days after receipt of order. FOB Destination, Inspection at Origin, and Acceptance at Destination. Destination is Warner Robins Air Logistics Complex, Warner Robins, GA 31088. The complete technical data package VIBB 23-35-1002 including Furnace, Training, Technical Data, IUID Tag(s), Shipping, Rigging, Installation, Removal of Equipment, Bulk Liquid Nitrogen Tank, and concrete pad for bulk liquid Nitrogen Tank will be included as a part of the solicitation. Solicitation will be Full and Open Competition procedures. The final contract award decision may be based upon a combination of lowest price technically acceptable (LPTA), past performance and other evaluation factors as described in the solicitation.

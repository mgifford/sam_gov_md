# Travel Management Services

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 04:41:16.193-04
- Solicitation Number: 19N10226Q0011

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy Abuja requires transaction fee-based domestic and regional Travel Management Services (TMS) to support official travel for U.S. Government personnel. The Government intends to award multiple Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts and is seeking quotations from qualified contractors as outlined in this solicitation.

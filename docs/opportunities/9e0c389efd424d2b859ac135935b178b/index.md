# Building Operations & Maintenance

- Agency: LABOR, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-04 12:36:54.185-05
- Solicitation Number: OAA-26-00177

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Labor (DOL) is issuing this Request for Information/Sources Sought Notice (RFI/SSN) in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 10, Market Research, to obtain input from industry regarding the draft Performance Work Statement (PWS) for Operations and Maintenance Services at the Francis Perkins Building (FPB) located at 200 Constitution Avenue, NW Washington, DC 20210. The information received will assist the Government in understanding industry capabilities, refining requirements, assessing risks, and developing an appropriate acquisition strategy consistent with applicable procurement laws and regulations.

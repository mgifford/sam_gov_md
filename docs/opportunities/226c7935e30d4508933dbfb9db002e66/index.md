# Bldg. 200 Energy Repairs/Upgrades

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 14:51:15.75-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8D26SS0001

## Summary

The purpose of this synopsis is to gain knowledge of potential sources for this project including interested Small Business sources. This notice is for planning purposes only. All interested parties should respond. Stewart Air National Guard Base, Newburgh, NY may issue a solicitation and award of a contract for Bldg. 200 Energy Repairs/Upgrades, subject to availability of funds. The magnitude of this project is between $500,000 and $750,000. The project includes, but is not necessarily limited to: Removal and disposal of existing building components, installing motor-operated coiling overhead doors, window systems with insulated glass, insulated roof caps, and low-intensity gas-fired radiant heaters. All interested concerns will provide this office, in writing, a notice stating their positive intent to submit a proposal as a prime contractor no later than 19 March 2026. This notification, which shall not exceed five typewritten pages, must include (1) positive statement of intent to bid as a prime contractor, and (2) a completed and signed Source Sought Information Request Form and (3) A listing of projects completed during the past three years, both for government and private industry. The type of project, dollar value, Contract number, location, and Point of Contact are to be included. Indicate if you were prime or subcontractor, and if subcontractor, provide the name and point of contact for the prime contractor. (4) A brief outline of resources, subcontractors, and key personnel that would be used to accomplish the contract.In the event adequate Small Business contractors are not available for adequate competition for this project, it may be advertised as unrestricted. Information shall be provided via email to: joseph.kugler.1@us.af.mil. Include the following in the subject line- Sources Sought W50S8D26SS0001. No facsimile responses will be honored.

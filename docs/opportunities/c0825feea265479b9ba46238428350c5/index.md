# MXG Air Compressor Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 12:38:24.148-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462026QA204

## Summary

The contractor shall provide all necessary labor, personnel, equipment, tools, material, supervision, and other items and services necessary to perform inspection and maintenance for all equipment listed below in accordance with standard commercial practices, manufacturer's specifications, AFOSH 91-46, AFOSH 91-20-Chapter30-Air Compressors, and this Statement of Work as such that will ensure continuous and safe operation in accordance with all federal, state, and local laws and regulations. Servicing must include, but not limited to, annual MAJOR inspections of all equipment, annual MINOR inspections of all equipment, and Bi-Annual inspections (Every 6 months) of all equipment.

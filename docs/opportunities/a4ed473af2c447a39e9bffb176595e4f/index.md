## Summary

The Multi-Purpose Range Complex (MPRC) is one of the last efforts to complete the FBI Huntsville’s Counter-IED Center of Excellence mission pillar. This project will include demolition of some of the existing structures, sitework to open the range for safe operations, and construction of buildings, structures, and sand pits to support this mission pillar.

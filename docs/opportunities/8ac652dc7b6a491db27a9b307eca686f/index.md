# FBNC Post-Wide Custodial

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-18 12:38:45.467-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124726DA001
- Awardee: CHIMES DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA INC Baltimore MD 21215 USA
- Award Amount: 46073925.73

## Summary

This announcment is for a FFP IDIQ contract for custodial services at various Fort Bragg facilities to maintain a clean and healthy environment for occupants, users, and visitors. Services include daily, recurring, and project-based cleaning for all custodial activities at Fort Bragg, Pope Army Airfield, Simmons Army Airfield, Linden Oaks, and Camp Mackall, North Carolina. This acquisition type is governed under FAR Part 8 Required Sources of Supplies and Services, primarily FAR Part 8.002(a)(2) Services. Services that are on the Procurement List maintained by the Committee for Purchase From People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled (see subpart 8.7). Acquisition from Nonprofit Agencies Employing People Who Are Blind or Severely Disabled. This procurement made IAW FAR 8.002 utilizing the AbilityOne program as a FFP IDIQ contract.

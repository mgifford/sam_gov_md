# JOPES/TransViz

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 16:35:54.731-04
- Solicitation Number: HTC711-26-F-E167
- Awardee: TRILLION TECHNOLOGY SOLUTIONS INC Reston VA 20191 USA

## Summary

USTRANSCOM has awarded a firm fixed price contract, HTC71126CE167, to Trillion Technology Solutions Inc. The contract provides JOPES that formally established policies and procedures that guide conventional operations planning and execution and TransViz provides transportation requirements and plan analysis tool to work with JOPES for global force movements. The contract was competitively procured. The primary location of performance is Scott AFB, with an expected completion date of 30 September 2030, including the base period and four one-year option periods. Additionally, contract includes FAR 52.217-8, Option to Extend Services, for a maximum potential completion date of 31 March 2031. Total contract value is $5,659,048.50. The contracting activity is the U.S. Transportation Command, Directorate of Acquisition, Scott Air Force Base, Illinois.

The U.S. Geological Survey New York Water Science Center has a requirement for the delivery of Borehole Winch System IAW Salients to Coram, NY within 60 days from the date the contractor receives the purchase order. Request for Quote (RFQ) 140G0126Q0100 will be issued on or about 4/06/2026 with written quotes due on 4/09/2026 at 11:00 AM ED. Questions may be emailed to Cynthia Nicanor, Email: cynthia_nicanor@ios.doi.gov, before 4/07/2026 at 11:00 AM ED. Questions received after that time and date may not be responded to.

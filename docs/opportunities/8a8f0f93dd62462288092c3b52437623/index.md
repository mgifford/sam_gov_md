# FY26 Kitchen Exhaust

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-18 16:00:11.204-04
- Solicitation Number: FA302026Q0013

## Summary

The 82nd Contracting Squadron is seeking sources capable of providing all necessary management, tools, supplies, equipment, and labor for the periodic inspection, cleaning, and maintenance of commercial kitchen exhaust hoods and associated ductwork. Services are to be performed in accordance with all applicable local, state, and federal regulations, including the National Fire Protection Association (NFPA) Standard 96. A copy of the current PWS is attached. The requirements in the attachedf document may be changed at the time of solicitation. This is not a solicitation. The Government will not be accepting any quotes, bids, or proposals at this time. Interested parties will not be reimbursed for any costs related to responding to this notice. The information gathered from this notice will be used to determine the appropriate acquisition strategy for a future procurement. The Government is open to questions and suggestions for revisions or clarifications to the potential requirements. Information Requested from Interested Parties Responses to this notice should include the following information: Company Name Point of Contact (Name, Title) Phone Number E-mail Address Unique Entity ID (UEI) and/or DUNS Number CAGE Code Socio-economic Status (e.g., Small Business, 8(a), Woman-Owned, HUBZone, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned) Capabilities Statement: A brief statement (no more than 3 pages) outlining your company's experience and technical ability to perform the services described above. Please include experience with similar government or commercial contracts.

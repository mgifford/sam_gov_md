# F-35 Wheel and Tire Shipping/Storage

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-23 15:46:53.647-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4819SM0002

## Summary

This is a Request For Information (RFI) for market research purposes only. The 325th contracting squadron at Tyndall AFB, FL is conducting market research to identify vendors capable of providing F-35 Wheel and Tire Shipping/Storage Container support kits. the requirement includes: - Eight (8) Tactical ISU-90 lockers - Two (2) Mobile Container Loading Ramps - Sixteen (16) Wheel Storage Transportation (WST XS) units The equipment must support the safe storage, handling, and transportation of F-35 Wheels, tires, rims and wheel accessories for daily operation, TDY, and deployment requirements. Please refer to the attached salient characteristics for full specifications. if interested please indicate whether your company is capable of meeting the requirements outlined in the attached salient characteristics. Again, we are only conducting market research.

# Quadrupole-Orbitrap Hybrid Mass Spectrometer

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF

- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:18:02.018-04

## Summary

The Food and Drug Administration (FDA), Human Foods Program (HFP) is conducting market research to identify small business concerns capable of providing a quadrupole-orbitrap hybrid mass spectrometer system for the Division of Analytical Chemistry, Spectroscopy Mass Spectrometry Branch, located at 5001 Campus Drive, College Park, MD 20740. Delivery is required prior to September 30, 2026. This notice is issued solely for market research and planning purposes and does not constitute a solicitation. Interested parties are invited to review Attachment 1 – Sources Sought Notice and Attachment 2 – Requirement Document for full details and response requirements. Responses are due no later than April 27, 2026 at 10:00 AM Eastern Time. NAICS Code: 334516 — Analytical Laboratory Instrument Manufacturing PSC: 6640 — Laboratory Equipment and Supplies

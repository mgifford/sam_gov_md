# Service and Maintenance for Data Innovations Middleware for Red Lake Hospital

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 15:56:48.697-04

## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT TO ISSUE SOLE SOURCE AWARD The U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS), Indian Health Service (IHS), Bemidji Area intends to award a Firm Fixed Priced Contract on a sole source basis to Data Innovations, LLC, for the Red Lake Service Unit in Red Lake MN. The sole source action will be issued under the authority of Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 13.501(a) - only one source reasonably available. Due to the proprietary nature of the software, no other company can service it. This is the only middleware on the market that can operate with the RPMS system. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code is 518210 – Computing Infrastructure Providers, Data Processing, Web Hosting and Related Services Point of contact for this acquisition is Purchasing Agent, Winona Kitto at winona.kitto@ihs.gov. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive quotes. Interested persons may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement or submit a quote to Purchasing Agent, Winona Kitto at winona.kitto@ihs.gov. Information received in response to this notice will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. Any response to this notice must show clear and convincing evidence that competition would be advantageous to the Government. In doing so, interested sources must submit technical capabilities, and other information that demonstrates their ability to meet the Government's needs.

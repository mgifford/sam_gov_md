# Replacement of 22 Fan Coil Units

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 11:34:55.387-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AU9026Q0004

## Summary

This is a presolicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with FAR part 12 . This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. This project's specification covers the replacement of 22 Fan Coil Units for the US- Embassy, Vienna. The work involves, but is not limited to the following: • Decommissioning of existing FCUs, 2 pipes • Draining to the required extent • Disconnecting cold water- and condensate pipes •Electrical disconnection FCU’s and room controllers • Removal and disposal existing FCU’s and pipes • Execution planning • Delivery and installation 22 FCU’s incl. pipes, valves, actuators and room controllers and insulation • Connection cold water- and condensate pipes • Pipe insulation • Electrical connection • Hydraulic balancing • Commissioning • Documentation All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered. For a quotation to be considered, you must also complete and submit the following: 1. SF-1449 2. Section I, Pricing 3. Additional information as required in Section 3 of the solicitation document 4. Proof of SAM Registration For additional information, soliciation documents and/or technical specifications please contact: TritscherN@state.gov . Anticipated award date May 29, 2026

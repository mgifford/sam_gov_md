# USCGC CALHOUN FQ4 FY26

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 10:26:41.106-04
- Solicitation Number: 70Z08526QLREP0015

## Summary

This requirement is for the Contractor to provide all necessary labor, services, supplies, materials, equipment power, accessories, personnel, and other things as necessary, except otherwise specified to perform dockside repairs to the USCGC CALHOUN (WMSL-759), a 418-foot National Security Cutter in accordance with the solicitation, schedule, and specifications. Work includes, but is not limited to the following: D-001 Stern Boat Launch and Recovery, Level II D-002 Talon Grid (Type 18-08), Inspect and Test D-003 Cargo Handling Elevator, Annual Inspection and Test, Perform D-004 Incinerator, Inspect and Test D-005 Oily Water Separator (OWS) System, Inspect and Groom D-006 Fire Detection System, Inspect and Test D-007 Deck Covering (Slip-Resistant) Flight Deck, Renew D-008 Deck Covering (Slip-Resistant) Hangars, Renew D-009 Deck Covering (Slip-Resistant), Renew D-010 Tenting, Provide D-011 Tanks (Aviation Fuel Service), Clean and Inspect D-012 Tanks (Aviation Fuel Stowage and Overflow), Clean and Inspect O-013 Tanks, Preserve Full (100%) O-014 Tanks, Partial Preserve D-015 Grey Water Holding Tank(s) and Lift Stations, Clean and Inspect D-016 Sewage Holding Tank(s), Clean and Inspect D-017 Tanks (MP Fuel Storage and Overflow), Clean and Inspect D-018 Tanks (Oily Waste), Clean and Inspect O-019 Welding, Provide (General) D-020 Tank Level Indicator (Gray Water Tanks), Install D-021 Temporary Services, Provide - Cutter D-022 Fire Prevention, Provide Amendment 0001 - Note: Period of Performance is 2 SEP 2026 - 21 NOV 2026

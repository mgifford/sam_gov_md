# NI Modules

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 12:28:22.003-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016726Q1050

## Summary

The Naval Surface Warfare Center Carderock Division (NSWCCD) has a requirement for a National Instruments modules. The NAICS code is 541519 and the business size is $34 million. The proposed purchase order is being solicited as on a brand name basis. The proposed action is for NI material, for which the Government intends to solicit and negotiate with compliant products pursuant to FAR 6.302-1(a)(2)(ii). The basis for restricting competition is for the following reasons: NI equipment is the only compatible material, with no valid alternatives. The NSWCCD intends to award a firm fixed-price purchase order using Simplified Acquisition Procedures. Interested parties may identify their interest to this requirement by submitting a quotes and capability statements no later than 08:30 AM, 24 March 2026. Questions must be submitted before 12:00 Noon, 20 March 2026. Answers will be publicized in an amendment to the solicitation.

# Air Handler Motors - KHC

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 19:16:04.005-04
- Solicitation Number: IHS1523238

## Summary

** This is a full and open (unrestricted) solicitation ** The Indian Health Center, Kayenta Health Center located at Highway 160 Mile Post 394.3, Kayenta AZ 86033 has a requirement for air handler motors. The Government will award the contract to the responsible offeror whose proposal represents the best value to the Government, considering evaluation factors such as technical capability, warranty terms, and delivery schedule, as detailed in the Section E of the solicitation document. All questions regarding this solicitation shall be submitted via email to Brandon.Martinez@ihs.gov with subject line, "Air Handler Motors Solicitation" no later than April 6, 2026 at 12:00pm EDT. The Government shall keep the solicitation posted and open for quotes until April 9,2026 @ 12:00pm EDT.

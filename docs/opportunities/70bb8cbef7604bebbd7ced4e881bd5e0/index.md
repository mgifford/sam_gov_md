## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - New England District is issuing a Solicitation Notice for Campground Host #1 Services, Winhall Brook Camping Area, Ball Mountain Lake, South Londonderry, VT. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

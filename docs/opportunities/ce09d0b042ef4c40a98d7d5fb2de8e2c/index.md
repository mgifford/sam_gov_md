# Y1BG--EHRM Infrastructure Upgrades Construction - Syracuse - Project 528A7-22-700

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 12:24:56.89-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C77626B0009

## Summary

Contractor shall completely prepare site for building operations, including demolition and removal of existing structures, and furnish labor and materials and perform work for EHRM INFRASTRUCTURE UPGRADES as required by drawings and specifications. Project scope includes, but is not limited to, the following infrastructure upgrades: Upgrading facility fiber throughout the buildings to each IT closet; Providing redundant fiber runs throughout the facility to each IT closet; Providing redundant fiber mains to the demarcation room from the provider point; Providing Cat 6A cabling from IT closets to all points of use as required; Installation of additional IT outlets and electrical outlets throughout the facility; Provide additional outlets in each IT closet; Upgrade facility power distribution including UPS as required; Upgrade facility HVAC distribution as required; Upgrades to physical security; Abatement of hazardous materials; New construction of Data Center server room. Includes Buildings 1, 2, 16, Rome Community-Based Outpatient Clinic (CBOC), and site.

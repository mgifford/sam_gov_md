# 100 Low Lead Fuel - 2026 Wings Over Solano Air Show

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 19:46:50.566-04

## Summary

The 60th Contracting Squadron at Travis Air Force Base intends to establish a Firm Fixed Price contract to provide 4,000 Gal of 100 LL Fuel in support of the 2026 Wings Over Solano Air Show. See attachments for Statement of Work for exact type and quantities. There is a detailed description of the delivery requirement for this contract. This is a 100% Total Small Business Set-Aside requirement. Please see the attached documents pertaining to this opportunity. Vendors who wish to respond to this should send responses via email no later than 9 April 2026 by 12:00 Pm Pacific Time to both POCs: samuel.de_la_cruz.1@us.af.mil and matthew.kirk.7@us.af.mil.

# OREGON SEAT CUSHIONS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-03 14:29:46.619-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8V26Q0006

## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD SOLE SOURCE The Government intends to issue a sole source, firm fixed price purchase order with COASTAL SEAT CUSHIONS INC located at 183 NE Royce AVE Port Saint Lucie, Florida 34983-1238, United States, under the authority of FAR 13.106- 1(b). The government will acquire the services using the Simplified Acquisition Procedures set forth in FAR Part 13. The North American Industry Classification Systems (NAICS) code for this requirement is 336413. The purpose of this requirement is to acquire Oregon Seat Cushions for the C-17A aircraft crew seats. COASTAL SEAT CUSHIONS INC is the only authorized vendor of Oregon Seat Cushions. This notice is not a formal solicitation. This is not considered a Request for Quotation (RFQ). No solicitation document is available and telephone requests will not be honored. No purchase order will be awarded on the basis of quotation or offers received in response to this notice. Any response to this notice must show clear, compelling, and convincing evidence that competition will be advantageous to the Government. If no affirmative written response is received, the purchase order will be awarded without further notice. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed order based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the government. Information related to the potential availability of other sources that can fulfill this requirement must be received by 2d Lt Christopher Broschart via electronic email at christopher.broschart@us.af.mil.

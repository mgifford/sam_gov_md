# Sources Sought Announcement for NSN 5998-01-713-6420 (ELECTRONIC COMPONEN) on the Advanced Medium Mobile Power System (AMMPS) system.

## Summary

DESCRIPTION—Template The Defense Logistics Agency-Aberdeen, on behalf of the Army Integrated Logistics Supply Center (ILSC) – Supply Chain Management Directorate (SCMD) – Strategic Sourcing Directorate (SSD), intends to acquire Parts to support the Advanced Medium Mobile Power System (AMMPS). Requirements Description: The items to be procured are for the Advanced Medium Mobile Power System (AMMPS). The AMMPS family represents the next generation a standard family of reliable, efficient, light weight, electronically controlled, diesel fueled power systems to support the Brigade Combat Team (BCT) on the 21st Century tactical battlefield. The Government does not possess specifications or detailed drawings for the referenced system. Please note that this is not a Request for Proposal. This notice is provided for planning purposes and is not to be construed as a commitment by the U.S. Government. The following are the Army’s estimated forecasts* for initial production: NIIN Nomenclature Quantity 017136420 ELECTRONIC COMPONEN 5 *Quantities listed are estimations and not a guarantee. Final quantities will be determined at time of solicitation. The Government does not possess the technical data package (TDP) for the parts. The TDP is not available for release. There is no previous buy history for NSN 5998-01-713-6420. It was established as a new NSN to list only the calibrated part number and to be CECOM managed. Items and/or Services to Be Procured: NOMENCLATURE: ELECTRONIC COMPONEN PART NUMBER: A063E172 CAGE: 44940 Requested Response Information: We request that sources who can supply the listed parts or services to submit information to show their capability to manufacture or supply the same or similar parts/services past repair performance or purchases. At a minimum the support information you submit should include, but is not limited to, the following types of documentation: Questionnaire, drawings, test results, information on previous Government and commercial contracts, data rights, etc. In your response, please include your company name, CAGE code, and a technical point of contract with email address and telephone number. Notice, Response Due Date, and Points of Contact: This is NOT a Request for Quote or Proposal.

## Summary

This is an award notice containing a Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition for aerial and/or static displays for Commemortive Air Force, Central Texas (CAF CENTEX). This award was solicited to and negotiated with CAF CENTEX for the following historical air crafts - C-47 Thats All Brother B-25 Yellow Rose C-45 Lonestar Lady U-3 Blue Canoe T-6 Texan BT-13 Slo Poke A-4 Skyhawk Cockpit Trailer This requirement was procured on a single source given the circumstances and unique factors for this requirement. Please refer to the attached Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition for further details regarding the procurement of this request.

# Rental of a steam heat exchanger and associated components

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 13:54:39.116-04
- Solicitation Number: N3904026Q2867

## Summary

Portsmouth Naval Shipyard (PNSY) is seeking to lease a steam heat exchanger and associated components. The system must include a steam heat exchanger; temperature, pressure and flow monitoring capability; and safety, control and isolation valves. The system must be frame/skid mounted with fork truck pockets as well as lifting pad eyes in a footprint no greater than 8 FT x 8 FT and a maximum weight of 4000 LB.

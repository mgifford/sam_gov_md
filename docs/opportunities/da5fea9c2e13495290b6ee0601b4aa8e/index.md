# Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for twenty-two (22) superglue fuming chamber with UV contamination

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-24 13:57:59.475-04
- Solicitation Number: DJA-26-ALAB-PR-0222

## Summary

The Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco, Firearms and Explosives (ATF) intends to award a sole source firm-fixed price Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for twenty-two (22) superglue fuming chamber with UV contamination that are brand new and to be certified to the manufacturer’s Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) specification. OEM is Foster & Freeman. See “Minimum Technical Requirements” for full details. Proposed Contractor: Foster & Freeman USA INC (small business) 20145 Ashbrook Place #190 Ashburn, VA 20147 UEI: LC1TJNRE6KX7 The authority used for the sole source is FAR 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. This announcement constitutes the only notification of the ATF’s intent to solicit on a sole source basis. A formal combined synopsis/solicitation will not occur. All solicitation communication will occur directly with the proposed contractor. This acquisition is for a commercial item or service and is conducted under the authority of the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 12—Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. The notice of award and the written sole source justification will be posted after award.

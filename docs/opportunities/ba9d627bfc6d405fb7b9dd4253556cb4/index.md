## Summary

The project involves a three-phase renovation of multi-stall shower rooms within a high-security correctional environment. The contractor shall be responsible for the dimension verification, existing conditions, demolition, removal, and proper disposal of all existing units and debris. Contractor shall furnish and install eight new shower units and all associated accessories.

# USAF AFNWC GASNT - Request for Information

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 13:19:09.349-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8735-26-GASNT-RFI

## Summary

The United States Air Force (USAF) is seeking information regarding interested parties and qualified sources to support the Global Aircrew Strategic Network Terminal (GASNT) Program Management Office in the design, development, integration, upgrade, and maintenance of the GASNT family of systems. The GASNT program is an ongoing acquisition implemented incrementally and in block upgrades. GASNT Increment 1 (GASNTi1) provides ground-based Extremely High Frequency (EHF) communications. GASNT Increment 2 Block 1 (GASNTi2b1) provides aircrew alerting communications. The Government is considering establishing a new Multiple Award IDIQ contract vehicle in the future to accomplish multiple lines of effort within the GASNT program. The initial focus will be the development and procurement of the next incremental capability for GASNT. Additionally, the IDIQ will address complex integration across the GASNT family of systems and improvements to individual systems. Please refer to the attached Request for Information (RFI) for more details. Please note that this RFI is for market research purposes only and no contract award will result from this posting.

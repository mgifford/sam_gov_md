# Marketplace for Acquisition of Professional Services (MAPS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Consolidate/(Substantially) Bundle
- Posted: 2026-03-20 12:04:16.006-04
- Solicitation Number: PANAPG24P0000006913

## Summary

In accordance with FAR 7.107-5, this posting provides notification of the Department of Army's intent to consolidate RS3 and ITES3S contract requirements for the MAPS acquisition. ACC-APG has conducted market research demonstrating that consolidation of this procurement is both necessary and justified. Consolidating the RS3 and ITES-3S contracts into a single contract vehicle is necessary and justified because this consolidation is projected to yield lower overall prices for the Government, generate significant operational efficiencies, and improve mission performance beyond alternative contracting approaches. By replacing two (2) contracts with one (1), the Government will eliminate duplicative procurement and management efforts, reducing costs and administrative burdens for all parties. This notice is issued for informational and planning purposes only. This announcement does not constitute a solicitation and shall not be construed as a commitment by the Government. Responses in the form of quotes, offers, or proposals are not requested. The Government is under no obligation to solicit or award a contract because of this announcement.

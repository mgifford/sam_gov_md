# Goose Hazard Management Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 16:28:15.207-04
- Solicitation Number: SP4705-26-Q-0002

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Installation Support at Fort Belvoir, VA has a requirement for non-personal services for Goose Hazard Management at the Andrew T. McNamara Headquarters Complex (HQC). The contractor shall provide all management, labor, and trained working dog(s) to provide goose hazard management deterrent services at the facility grounds and pond area. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This procurement is being solicited as 100% Total Small Business Set-Aside under North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) 561710, Exterminating and Pest Control Services. The small business size standard is $17.5M. The Government intends to award a single Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) contract consisting of a 12-month base period and four (4) 12-month option periods. Award will be made on a Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) basis. Partial quotes will not be accepted. A site visit is scheduled for May 5, 2026. Please see the solicitation attachment for full details regarding the site visit, quote submission instructions, and question deadlines. Full details, including the Performance Work Statement (PWS), evaluation criteria, and all applicable provisions/clauses are included in the attachments to this posting. All interested quoters are responsible for downloading and reviewing all attached documents in their entirety.

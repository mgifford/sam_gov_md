# Navy Medical Readiness Logistics Command (NMRLC) - Warehouse Support Services (WSS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 15:45:50.33-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926Q3103

## Summary

Warehouse support, supporting all aspects of warehousing operations. The required support includes maintaining all Civil Engineering Equipment (CEE) in support of NMRLC EXMEDS programs. CEE consists of Civil Engineer Support Equipment (CESE), Civil Engineer End Items (CEEI) Configurable Shelters, Containers, and Expeditionary Tool Kits. See attached Solicitation - N0018926Q3103. This solicitation is available ONLY to qualified service-disabled veteran owned small businesses with an active Seaport contract.

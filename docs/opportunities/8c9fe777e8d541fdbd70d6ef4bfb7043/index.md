# Posit Licenses and Success Support

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-31 12:49:38.231-04
- Solicitation Number: 89603026P0004

## Summary

FERC seeks a continuation of the software licenses and success support. The licenses are being actively used by FERC staff to serve mission and the potential value to FERC of leveraging the significant staff investment in the creation of analytical tools that integrate directly with Posit is deemed significant. FERC requires Posit’s proprietary service in order to continue to use the tools developed to detect market manipulation and fraud, visualize analytical results, and easily share data products with FERC staff.

# Ontonagon, Port Wing, and Cornucopia Maintenance Dredging

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 15:28:04.808-04
- Solicitation Number: W911XK26BA021

## Summary

Mobilize to Ontonagon Harbor in Ontonagon, Ontonagon County, MI, Port Wing Harbor in Port Wing, Bayfield County, WI, and Cornucopia Harbor in Cornucopia, Bayfield County, WI. Dredge harbor locations as shown in plans. Place material dredged from Ontonagon and Port Wing Harbors at nearshore placement site as shown in plans. Place material dredged from Cornucopia (Outer) Harbor at nearshore placement site as shown on contract drawings. Harbors may be dredged in any order. Demobilize when work is complete. Should the applicable options be awarded, mobilize dredge to Cornucopia (Inner) Harbor, dredge between the limits and place material as shown in plans. Demobilize when work is complete.

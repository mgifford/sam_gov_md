# Hotel Lodging for 2026 USTRANSCOM UILS symposium

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-25 11:10:43.485-04
- Solicitation Number: SS-USTRANSCOM-UILS26-01

## Summary

United States Transportation Command (USTRANSCOM) will host the USTRANSCOM International Logistics Symposium (UILS) in June 2026. This event brings together international military and civilian logistics professionals from allied and partner nations. USTRANSCOM seeks to identify a hotel within a 15-minute driving distance the Duane Cassidy conference Center on Scott Air Force Base, IL, to provide single-occupancy lodging and meeting space for international attendees. Please see attached document for dates and specifications.

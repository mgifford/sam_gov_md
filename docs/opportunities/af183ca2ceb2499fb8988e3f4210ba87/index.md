## Summary

Pre-Solicitation Notice Solicitation: 36C25226B0027 Project: 556-23-104 Chilled Water from Bldg. 188 to Bldg. 3 Location: Captain James A. Lovell Federal Health Care Center (FHCC) 3001 Breen Bay Road North Chicago, IL 60064-3048 The Captain James A. Lovell FHCC has a requirement for construction of an expansion line of the chilled water supply and return from building 188 to buildings 1, 3, and 48 as described throughout the contract documents to include drawings and specifications. The contractor shall complete all work as specified in the requirement documents within 360 calendar days from Notice to Proceed (NTP). The North American Industry Classification Code (NAICS) for this procurement is 236220 Commercial and Institutional Building Construction General Construction with a small-business size standard of $45.0 Million. Estimated price range for construction is between $2,000,000 and $5,000,000. Pursuant to Public Law 109-461, this solicitation will be issued as a 100% Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) set-aside. In accordance with VAAR 819.7003(b), at the time of submission of an offer, the offeror must represent that it is a SDVOSB concern, a small business concern under the NAICS code assigned to the acquisition, and is verified for eligibility in the SBA Dynamic Small Business Search Certification database (https://search.certifications.sba.gov/ ). The solicitation (including any/all specifications and drawings) will be posted to SAM.GOV Contracting Opportunities (SAM.gov | Contract Opportunities) on or about June 02, 2026. One (1) pre-bid conference/site walk-through shall be conducted for this project after the solicitation has been posted. The time and date of the site visit will be included in the solicitation details. All interested parties shall have a current registration and current annual On-line Representation and Certifications Application (ORCA) in the System for Award Management (SAM) database https://www.sam.gov prior to award. Vendors are responsible to monitor and download the solicitation and any amendments associated with this solicitation from SAM.gov | Contract Opportunities.

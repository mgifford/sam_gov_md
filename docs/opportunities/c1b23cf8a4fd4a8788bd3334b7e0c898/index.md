# Emergency Operations Center (EOC) operational training support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

This requirement is for contractor-provided Emergency Management (EM) training, Emergency Operations Center (EOC) operational training support, planning assistance, and exercise facilitation in support of MAGTFTC/MCAGCC. The contractor will provide subject matter expertise, instructional services, planning support, and exercise design related to emergency preparedness, response, and recovery, including EOC staff training, Recovery Working Group facilitation, MCCAMS and Critical Infrastructure Protection (CIP) training, Base Defense OPLAN development support, Section Leader training, and Unified Command exercises.

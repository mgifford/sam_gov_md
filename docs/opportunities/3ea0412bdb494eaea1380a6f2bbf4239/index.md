# Request for Application: Initiative to Bolster Used Nuclear Fuel Recycling

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-22 17:33:16.945-04

## Summary

Pursuant to Section 5(c) of EO 14299, DOE is issuing this Request for Application to support or enable privately funded nuclear fuel recycling technologies. Potential resources available from DOE to enable this effort include DOE sites and facilities for testing and potential deployment, DOE used nuclear fuel for prototype testing or recycling, DOE authorization for research activities and prototype testing, or other activities that advance a DOE mission, and expertise at DOE national laboratories.

# J&A and Publication for Sole Source BPA for Coca-Cola Guam

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-09 20:45:22.813-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE302-26-R-0001

## Summary

This notice is provided for informational purposes only to publicize the Brand-Name Justification and Approval (J&A) and shall not be construed as a solicitation or request for offers for this BPA. The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Troop Support Indo-Pacific intends to establish a Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) for the supply and delivery of Coca-Cola beverage products for authorized customers within the Guam Area of Responsibility (AOR). This requirement is being conducted as a brand-name acquisition for Coca-Cola products. Based on market research conducted by the Government, one vendor holds the exclusive distribution rights for Coca-Cola products within the Guam AOR and is the only known source capable of providing the required products to support the Government’s needs in this region. Accordingly, the Government intends to establish a single BPA with the authorized distributor for Coca-Cola products serving the Guam AOR.

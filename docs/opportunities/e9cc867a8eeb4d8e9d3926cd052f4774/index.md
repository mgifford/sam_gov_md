# Solicitation - Sieve Pack Assembly

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 07:36:12.808-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CH25R0195

## Summary

The United States Army Contracting Command - Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) is issuing a solicitation intended to result in a single, firm-fixed price (FFP) contract with available option period for the procurement of the following item. Noun: Sieve Pack Assembly NSN: 6665-01-380-1680 Part Number: 5-15-17033 Unit of Issue: Each (EA) Production Quantity: 268 EA Option Quantity: 268 EA First Article Testing is required unless waived. There is an export-controlled Technical Data Package (TDP) associated with this solicitation, distribution code C. Access to the export-controlled documents requires a current DD 2345. Obtaining an approved DD 2345 may take up to 10 weeks. Once approved, the Data Custodian listed on the DD 2345 may request access to the controlled documents via this posting. Interested parties shall submit one copy of their offer, in accordance with the requirements of the solicitation, to the point of contact identified on this posting no later than 27 April 2026 at 3:00 PM Local Warren, MI time. All offers submitted in response to this solicitation shall be valid for a minimum of 180 calendar days following the offer due date. All questions regarding this solicitation or its associated documents shall be made in writing, directed to the point of contact identified on this posting. The intended solicitation, and any amendments, will be issued electronically. The Government is under no obligation, and is in fact unable, to maintain a mailing list. Interested parties shall periodically access SAM.gov in order to obtain any amendments which may be issued. Failure to obtain any amendments and to respond to them prior to the date and time set for receipt of proposals may render your proposal nonresponsive and result in the rejection of the proposal. NOTICE TO ALL CONTRACTORS: All contractors who provide goods/services to the Department of Defense (DoD) must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM).

# Naval Surface Warfare Center- Carderock Division – Fire Protection

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-26 09:49:23.319-04
- Solicitation Number: W912BU26QNSWC

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all qualified personnel, equipment, supplies, transportation, materials, and supervision necessary to perform inspection, maintenance and operation support services for the Operations Department, Infrastructure Division, NSWCCD as defined in Section 5 of this Performance Work Statement. This shall include inspections, maintenance, modification, and repairs to all fire alarm, protection, extinguishing, and notification systems. The contractor shall maintain a database of all equipment and maintenance records. The Contractor shall perform in accordance with (IAW) the terms and conditions of the contract and consistently with the established standards in this PWS. See PWS and sources sought notice with response requirements attached.

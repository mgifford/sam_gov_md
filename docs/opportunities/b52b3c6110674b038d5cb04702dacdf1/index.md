## Summary

The purpose of this contract is to support NSWCPD Code 333 with the requisite EMC and EMI related EQT activities under its cognizance. The Contractor shall support NSWCPD Code 333 in execution of laboratory testing, test procedure delivery, test report delivery, and test fixture fabrication in contractor facilities in support of ship hardening efforts. The deadline for receipt of questions is 10 days after issuance of the solicitation. Although every effort will be made, the Government makes no guarantee that questions received after the date above will be answered.

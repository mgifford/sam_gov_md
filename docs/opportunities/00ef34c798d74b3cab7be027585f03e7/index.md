- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

This requirement is for a Catholic Worship Director/Musician to provide planning, coordination, rehearsal, and performance of music for Catholic worship services at Geilenkirchen NATO Air Base Chapel IAW Performance Work Statement. An ammendment (FA560626Q00040001) has been issued in accordance with the amended SF 1449 to extend the due date by 2 weeks, to 21 April 10:00AM CEST. Additionally, an ammendment (FA560626Q00040002) has been issued to update the instructions to offerors and evaluation criteria.

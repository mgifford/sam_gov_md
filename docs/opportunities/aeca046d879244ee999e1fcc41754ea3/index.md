## Summary

Repair of two tip-up aircraft hangar doors at Building 1340, Eielson Air Force Base (AFB), Alaska. The work includes repairs to mechanical operating systems; replacement of counterweight systems; door insulation and cladding; installation of heated hangar door thresholds; and all incidental and related work necessary to provide fully functional, operable, and weather-tight hangar doors.

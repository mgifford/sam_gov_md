# FACZG Kitchenette Renovation

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 09:57:16.189-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15941011

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy Zagreb, Croatia has a requirement to modify kitchenette space in the Chancery building, located at U.S. Embassy Compound, Thomasa Jeffersona 2, 10000 Zagreb. The contractor will supply all labor, supervision, and the materials for the project, except the materials listed under Government Furnished Materials (GFM).

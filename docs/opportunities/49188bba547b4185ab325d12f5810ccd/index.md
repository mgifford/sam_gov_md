# BRIDGE SUPPLIES (CLIFF CREEK TRAIL)

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 14:50:40.949-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LS26Q0031

## Summary

This solicitation is for the purchase and delivery of a single 25 ft longitudinal glulam deck panel trail bridge with curbs, timber sills, and all related hardware required for installation. The provided bridge and hardware must match the provided drawings. Offeror must provide a breakdown of items and quantities. Offeror shall submit shop drawings for all bridge components (except timber running planks). Show all dimensions and fabrication details for all cut and bored timber. Bridge shall be delivered and offloaded at the McCall Ranger district storage yard located in McCall, ID: 102 W. Lake Street, McCall, ID 83638. Two weeks’ notice prior to delivery is required. FOB Destination.

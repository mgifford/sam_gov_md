## Summary

This award notice announces the contract award to Ocean Construction Services INC, under solicitation number 70Z04726RPCNI0002, for Design-Build Cutter Engineering & Weapons Training Building Phase 2, at USCG TRACEN Yorktown, VA. The contract was awarded on March 2, 2026, in the amount of $17,785,608.12 for Base Item 0001 and Option Items 0001 and 0002. The procurement supports the Department’s mission to deliver a modernized and fully functional training facility, ensuring advanced instructional spaces and resources to support and execute USCG missions and was conducted in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR). Ocean Construction Services INC. was selected based on best competitive pricing.

## Summary

Pre-Solicitation Notice: Sludge Handling Services This posting is strictly a pre-solicitation notice. The Regional Contracting Office - Bavaria announces its intention to issue a solicitation for sludge handling services at the Wash Rack located in Hohenfels, USAG Bavaria. Requirement Details: This requirement will utilize low price technically acceptable (LPTA) evaluation factors in determining the successful offer. Solicitation Information Competition Status: The government anticipates releasing the solicitation under full and open competition. Expected Release: The Request for Quotation (RFQ) is expected to be posted within the next month. However, the Government reserves the right to delay or cancel the solicitation at any time prior to award. The actual solicitation will be posted separately along with all remaining attachments and detailed instructions at a later date. Where to Find: The RFQ, along with all attachments and detailed instructions, will be available for download at https://sam.gov.

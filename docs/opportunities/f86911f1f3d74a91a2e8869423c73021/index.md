# 149 Wash Rack Pressure Washer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-01 14:29:56.957-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S78-26-SS-KR02

## Summary

The 149th Fighter Wing requirese a stationary pressure washing unit or units to provide water to two separate sprayers. The following specifications are required: The system should be able to output water at or above 175 degrees F The two sprayers are on opposite sides of a building that is approximately 100 Ft Wide and 40ft Tall. Plumbing is already present from a previous system and may be able to be used. Both sprayers must have hose reels with at least 100ft of hose. Each system should be able to output at least 3000 PSI and

3.5 GPM to both sprayers simultaneously. Controls for the system should be mounted locally next to or nearby each sprayer. 120V, 277V and 480V electrical power is available on site. 2 PSIG Natural gas is also available on site. This is a sources sought notice and not a formal request for quotes. *Will be Small Business Set Aside*

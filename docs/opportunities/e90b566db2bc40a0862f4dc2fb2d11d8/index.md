# NYARNG Panoramic Dental X-RAY

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 17:08:26.674-05
- Solicitation Number: W912PQ26QA009

## Summary

The NYARNG Medical command is requesting a Dental Panoramic X-Ray Imaging System (OPG) Planmeca ProOne (Pan/Ceph) or Equivalent. Specifications required brand name OR Equivalent found at https://www.planmeca.com/dental-imaging/panoramic-machines/planmeca-proone/ The system shall be compatible with existing Army dental clinic workflows, digital imaging practices, and medical record systems. The requirement is intended to align with standardized dental radiography equipment currently in use across Army dental treatment facilities. Deliver No later than 09/01/2026.

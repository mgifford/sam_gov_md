# Commercial Internet Service at Vandenberg Space Force Base (VSFB), CA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 16:26:59.623-04
- Solicitation Number: FA461026Q0019

## Summary

The 18th Space Defense Squadron (18 SDS) and U.S. Space Forces – Space (S4S) require an unfiltered network access point to access resources provided by commercial partners that are otherwise inaccessible through standard Department of War (DoW) networks. This CIS capability allows administrators to download patches for locally managed network switches and gives space operators access to commercial resources in support of Space Domain Awareness.

# NRTL Field Evaluation Services (Inspections) NASNI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 17:31:32.057-04
- Solicitation Number: N4523A26Q1308

## Summary

*****Solicitation period extended to 26 Mar 2025***** Solicitation period extended to 20 Mar 2025 The Regional Maintenance Center at Puget Sound Naval Shipyard & Intermediate Maintenance Facility (PSNS&IMF) detachment Naval Air Station North Island (NASNI) intends to award a standalone, Firm-Fixed Price contract to procure Nationally Recognized Testing Laboratory (NRTL) field evaluation services. Noun Name: NRTL Inspections Description: Puget Sound Naval Shipyard and intermediate Maintenance Facility (PSNS & IMF), Bremerton, Washington is required to provide training to military and civilian security forces supporting sensitive operations within the Controlled Industrial Area (CIA) per NAVSEA Instructions. Scope: The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform NRTL field evaluation services as defined in this Performance Work Statement Period of Performance - 30 March 2026 – 1 May 2026 Please complete pricing on attached combined synopsis solicitation document and submit required documents by 20 March 2026, 9:00 am PST. Please ensure that your submittal conforms to all requirements in the C2 26Q1308 Combined Synopsis and Solicitation document and Technical Exhibits located in the PWS. Questions are due by 18 March 2026 at 9:00 am PST. All offerors must be registered with the System for Award Management (SAM) to receive award of government contracts. offerors are advised that SAM representations and certifications can be completed electronically via the System for Award Management website at https://www.sam.gov

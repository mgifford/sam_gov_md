# Clark AB Laundry Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-28 00:12:40.138-04
- Solicitation Number: FA5270BK26_Laundry

## Summary

The purpose of this sources sought Request for Information (RFI) is to identify vendors capable of providing laundry service at Clark AB, Luzon Philippines. This notice is a request for vendor's capabilities, and does not serve as a solicitation. Interested parties should respond with the following information: - Company Name - Mobilization or shipping time required to provide service - Timeframe between pickup and return of laundry - If your company has worked on Clark AB or other APF installations previously - Any other information that would be beneficial to us in determining available laundry service options NOTE: This is a Request for Information (RFI) for market research purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation. The Government does not intend to award a contract on the basis of this RFI or to otherwise pay for the information solicited.

# Laundry and Dry Cleaning Service Amendment 2

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 15:41:55.702-04
- Solicitation Number: W911SF-26-R-A010

## Summary

The Contractor shall operate and manage the Laundry Distribution and Collection Point(s) for the issue, receipt, requisition, exchange, warehousing, and turn-in of linens IAW AR 210-130, Laundry and Dry-Cleaning Operations; AR 710-4, Property Accountability; and other regulatory requirements. The Contractor shall maintain a contractor developed accountability system for linens and other items submitted for L&DC services IAW AR 710-2, Supply Policy Below the National Level; AR 710-4, Property Accountability; and other regulatory requirements. Amendment 0002 is to update the Response due date on the Solicitation SF 1449 document.

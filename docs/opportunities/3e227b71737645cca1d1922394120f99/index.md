# FAA Market Survey /Request for Information / Capability Assessment: Procurement of Alternative Energy Systems Equipment (AESE) and Life Cycle Support

- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-22 11:49:49.047-04
- Solicitation Number: 693KA8-26-RFI-AESE

## Summary

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)'s Air Traffic Organization (ATO), Power Services Group (PSG) is conducting a Market Survey to ascertain current market conditions and vendor capabilities for a follow-on requirement to furnish Alternative Energy Systems (AES) equipment, such as photovoltaic panels, battery energy storage systems (BESS), Lithium Iron Phosphate batteries, hybrid supercapacitors, charge controllers, microgrid controllers, PV array rack systems, and all associated AES cables/connectors supporting National Air Space assets and facilities outlined in the attached Draft Statement of Work (SOW) and Specifications. Please see the attached documents for additional information.

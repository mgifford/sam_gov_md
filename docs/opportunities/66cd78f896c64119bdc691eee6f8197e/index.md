## Summary

This is a COMBINED SYNOPSIS/SOLICITATION for commercial product prepared in accordance with FAR part 12, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. The solicitation number is N0040626QS231 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Flexible Hose. This acquisition is Competitive. This combined synopsis/solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable NAICS Code is 332919, with a Small Business Size Standard of 1000 Employees. A Firm-Fixed Price, Supply type contract will be awarded in accordance with FAR Part 12, Commercial Products and Simplified Acquisition Procedures. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which shall be considered by NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound (FLCPS). This solicitation and any subsequent amendments will be posted to: https://www.sam.gov NAVSUP WSS Transportation is provided at no cost to the Contractor and is mandatory in accordance with the solicitation. Any additional costs, including tariffs, duties, taxes, or customs fees, shall be included by the offeror in the applicable line item(s) of the proposal. Only completed solicitations submitted via email will be accepted. Kindly refer to the attached solicitation for detailed requirements and specifications, applicable clauses and provisions, as well as shipping and invoicing instructions. Quote submissions in response to this solicitation are due no later than 12:00 PM Pacific Standard Time on Friday, March 13, 2026. Please email your quote to the acquisition point of contact(s): Ann Fulton at annarose.m.fulton.civ@us.navy.mil. Any questions received after this deadline may not be answered.

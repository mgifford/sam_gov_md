## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT Title: Building Automation Controllers: Distech EC-Line and Honeywell CIPer/Spyder Line Controllers and Software for Various projects within the NAVFAC Northwest Region. Notice Type: Sources Sought / Notice of Intent NAICS Code: 334519 – Other Measuring and Controlling Device Manufacturing PSC Code: 7J20 – IT AND TELECOM - SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE PRODUCTS (HARDWARE AND PERPETUAL LICENSE SOFTWARE) Attachments: CJA Draft Honeywell_Redacted Company Information Technical Information 1. Introduction The Naval Facilities Engineering Systems Command (NAVFAC) Northwest is issuing this Sources Sought notice as a means of conducting market research to identify parties having an interest in and the resources to support a requirement for Distech Controls EC-Net and Honeywell CIPer/Spyder line controllers and associated software. 2. Background The Government has a requirement for brand-name commercial equipment to ensure compatibility and interoperability with existing, proprietary infrastructure. The Control System Platform Enclave (CSPE) is an area-wide network connecting building-level Direct Digital Control (DDC) systems from several installations in the NAVFAC Northwest region. This system is based on a Tridium Niagara Utility Monitoring and Control System (UMCS) front end and Niagara Java Application Control Engine (JACE) enabled building-level supervisory controllers. To maintain operational integrity and prevent the need for costly and technically prohibitive system modifications, a brand-name justification is being pursued for the following product lines required for all projects connecting to the CSPE: Manufacturer: Distech Product Line: EC Line Controllers and Software Manufacturer: Honeywell Product Line: CIPer and Spyder Line Controllers and Software This requirement does not restrict which firms can install, commission, test, or maintain the controllers, as several sources are known to provide these services. 3. Purpose of this Notice This is a Sources Sought notice issued for market research purposes only and is not a Request for Proposal (RFP). The information obtained from this notice will be used to help determine the most appropriate acquisition strategy. The Government is seeking to identify: Authorized resellers and/or distributors of the specified equipment. Potential sources that can provide brand name or equal" products that meet the exact technical and interoperability requirements of the existing system, 4. Submission Requirements Interested firms are requested to submit a brief capability statement, not to exceed five (5) pages, that includes the following: Company Information: Company Name, Address, Unique Entity ID (UEI) number, CAGE code, and any applicable socio-economic statuses (e.g., 8(a), Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB), Woman-Owned Small Business (WOSB), etc.). Authorized Status: Proof of status as an authorized reseller or distributor for Distech and/or Honeywell. Technical Capability: A brief description of the firm's ability to provide the specified hardware, software, and any associated technical support. "Or Equal" Submissions: If proposing an alternative solution, the submission must include comprehensive technical documentation proving the product is 100% interoperable with the existing Distech/Honeywell systems and the Tridium Niagara framework without the need for additional gateways, protocol converters, or custom hardware/software. 5.

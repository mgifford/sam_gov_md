# Trump Account Summit

- Agency: TREASURY, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-19 11:46:33.312-04
- Solicitation Number: 2032H326F00023

## Summary

The Department of the Treasury (Treasury) is the executive agency responsible for promoting economic prosperity and ensuring the financial security of the United States. As part of this mission, Treasury supports the implementation and communication of Administration initiatives. Trump Accounts are a presidential initiative intended to promote broad-based participation in economic growth and long-term financial engagement among the American public. To formally launch this initiative and align key stakeholders, the President will host a Trump Accounts Kickoff Meeting on January 28, 2026. The event will serve as an official introduction to the initiative and provide an opportunity for senior leadership and invited participants to discuss objectives, implementation considerations, and next steps.

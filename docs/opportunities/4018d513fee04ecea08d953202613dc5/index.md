# Building 51 Painting and Flooring Replacement

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 13:29:29.739-04
- Solicitation Number: 1333ND26RNB190008

## Summary

The National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), Boulder, CO, is seeking a qualified contractor to perform interior painting and flooring replacement in Building 51. The project includes removal and replacement of approximately 1,500 square feet of carpet tile, VCT flooring, cove base, and transition strips, as well as preparation and painting of interior walls and door frames. Work will be performed on an active, secure federal campus and will require coordination with NIST personnel, adherence to safety and security requirements, and accommodation of night and weekend work to minimize operational disruption. The contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision necessary to complete the project in accordance with the Statement of Work. The period of performance is anticipated to be 120 days from Notice to Proceed. Key Dates: List of Individuals for Site Visit Due: Tuesday, April 14, 2026 Site Visit: Thursday, April 16, 2026 Questions Due: Monday, April 20, 2026 Proposals Due: Wednesday, April 29, 2026 A pre-proposal site visit is recommended. Additional details, requirements, and submission instructions are provided in the full solicitation package. 04.01.26 - Please see Amendment 0001 for further details regarding the pre-proposal site visit.

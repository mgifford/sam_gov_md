# Area of Interest (AOI) for Secure Mobile Device Communications (SMDC) Prototype Other Transaction (OT)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 16:47:23.365-04
- Solicitation Number: DISA-OTA-26-R-SMDC

## Summary

AMD 01: The purpose of amendment 01 is to provide attribute notes and extend the solicitation to 22 April 2026, 5:00 p.m., CDT. See attachment AOI SMDC Prototype OT AMD 01 with highlighted changes. The Defense Information Systems Agency Emerging Technologies Directorate Advanced Concepts Division seeks to develop an innovative and user-friendly Retransmission Device to bridge the gap between Commercial Solutions for Classified (CSfC) standards and operational needs. The ideal solution, likely a sleeve or integrated case, will satisfy the new CSfC hardwired connection requirement internally while appearing as a normal case to the outside observer. The goal is to eliminate conspicuous external cables, remove the logistical burden of separate devices, and restore full tactical mobility for users.

# Los Angeles District (SPL), USACE Business Opportunity Open House (BOOH)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-05 19:59:23.137-04
- Solicitation Number: FY26SPLBOOH

## Summary

When: March 18, 2026, 9AM-Noon (Presentations: Noon-1P)(Capabilities Briefings: 12:40P-3:30P) Where: Doubletree by Hilton Phoenix Midtown (4000 North Central Ave, Phoenix, AZ 85012) Why: The primary focus is to connect with our staff with industry and build business relationships. Please register early as attendance is limited. The most recent forecast of LA District opportunities can be found at: https://www.spl.usace.army.mil/Business-With-Us/Small-Business-Program/. There is no charge to attend the BOOH. This SAM posting will be open through April 18th, 2026 to allow attendees the opportunity to download slide presentations (to be posted after March 18) This year, SPL is scheduling presentations and/or will have personnel on hand to discuss: (VA Opportunities in AZ/See W912PL26S0002)(Little Colorado River at Winslow)(Border Infrastructure)(Yuma Proving Ground-YPG, Davis Monthan AFB, Luke AFB, Fort Huachuca Opportunities)(Aircraft Beddown)(SPL and Regional MATOCs and SATOCs)(DB to Budget Project in AZ). More information to follow Link: Click Below for BOOH Registration (Note: Please register individually, not as a group) https://www.eventbrite.com/e/usace-los-angeles-district-business-opportunities-open-house-tickets-1977670969106?aff=oddtdtcreator After the BOOH, SPL intends to hold capabilities briefings with firms interested in AZ based VA opportunities. Please see attachment for details Apart from the SPL BOOH, the Society of American Military Engineers (SAME), Phoenix Post will host their annual Industry Government Engagement (IGE) on March 17th (8A-5P). Personnel from Gov agencies and facilities throughout AZ will be on hand to discuss their upcoming projects. SPL personnel will also be on hand if you would like to engage them about one/more of the projects above. There is a nominal fee to attend the IGE. Please turn to: https://www.same.org/phoenix/ for further information. The BOOH and the IGE are two separate events.

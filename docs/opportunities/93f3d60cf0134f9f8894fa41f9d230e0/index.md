# Aircraft & Trailer Lavatory Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-31 16:38:39.565-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8224-26-0031LS

## Summary

Seeking information from interested Contractors for market research purposes to support a potential need for the following requirement: Contractor shall provide all management, tools, supplies, equipment, and labor necessary to flush, empty, clean, and drain incoming C-130 aircraft and possible 309 AMXG trailer break-room toilets, dispose of waste materials, and prepare the toilet systems, in accordance with the procedures outlined in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). Contractor must be able to respond within 24 hours of a standard call request and within 2 hours for emergency call requests. This work will be done for the 309th Aircraft Maintenance Group at Hill AFB. See the attached sources sought document for full description and response requirements.

## Summary

GS-33F-0027P/1145PC26F0058 is a logical follow-on to Delivery/Task Order GS-33F-0027P/1145PC20F0531 for the continuation of Travel Management Center (TMC) services provided by Omega World Travel, Inc. All required services shall continue without interruption and without any change in scope, in accordance with the contractor’s GSA Schedule contract GS-33F-0027P.

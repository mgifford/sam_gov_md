## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for Janitorial Services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12639526Q0053 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Janitorial services for the Science and Technology (S&T) Laboratory in Coral Gables, FL. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 5617220. The small business size standard is $22.0 M. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

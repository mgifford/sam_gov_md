# Geodesic Radome RFI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-14 10:20:58.097-04
- Solicitation Number: W51AA1GRRFI

## Summary

SEE RFI DOCUMENT FOR DETAILS This Request for Information (RFI) is issued by the Tobyhanna Army Depot (TYAD) to solicit information and capabilities from potential sources for the procurement of a geodesic radome with a diameter of 110 feet and a height of approximately 77 feet. The radome will support the maintenance, calibration, operation, and testing of radar systems. Your response to this RFI, including any capabilities statement, shall be electronically submitted to the technical point of contacts (James Harris and Ricky Saunders) in either Microsoft Word or Portable Document Format (PDF), via email to james.t.harris208.civ@army.mil & ricky.saunders.civ@army.mil no later than 12:00 p.m. Eastern Daylight Time (EDT) on 30 April 2026.

# Lincoln National Forest Prospectus for Campground and Related Granger-Thye Concessions

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-15 16:52:23.504-04

## Summary

This prospectus is being issued to solicit applications for a concession campground special use permit. A permit to provide high-quality public service in the operation and maintenance of Government-owned recreation facilities located within the Lincoln National Forest will be issued. The offering includes approximately 20 sites that include campgrounds, group campgrounds, picnic areas, a day use site, and an equestrian campground. The three-year average gross revenue for this offering is $222,888.53.

# OVERHEAD CONVEYOR BLAST MACHINE for TLSC-E/A

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 08:15:29.769-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV26QA038

## Summary

The 409th CSB, Theater Contracting Center is publishing this presolicitation for the purchase of one (01) each overhead conveyor blast machine, to support TLSC-E/ A and Maintenance Activity Kaiserslautern (MAK) operations in KAISERSLAUTERN (Germany). The overhead conveyor blast machine will be used for the surface treatment (roughening, de-rusting, cleaning) of various steel components. The procurement is essential to ensure the continued successful and efficient execution of the mission-critical component surface treatment and maintenance operations. All vendors who have questions, comments and are interested in providing the equipment specified in the Attachment- “Salient Characteristics Overhead Conveyor Blast Machine DRAFT”, should submit their interest via email to: Ms. Coralia Kelly Email: coralia.a.kelly.ln@army.mil Ms. Judy Biboum Email: judy.i.biboum.civ@army.mil Please include the title " W564KV26QA038- Purchase of one overhead conveyor blast machine" in the subject line of the request, and include the following information: Company name and address Cage Code UEI Point of contact for questions/clarifications Name Telephone number email address The Government will not return any information submitted in response to this notice. All questions must be in writing.

Pyxis ES Medication Dispensing Refrigerator (Brand Name Only) INTRODUCTION/BACKGROUND: The medical-grade refrigerator provides clinicians with secured access to refrigerated medications using the same patient-centered workflow currently available for medications stored at ambient temperatures through the Pyxis MedStation ES. The BD Pyxis ES Refrigerator extends the value of Pyxis MedStation ES to refrigerated medications to enable consistent, secure and safe medication management for all medications across the continuum of care. OBJECTIVE: The Department of Veterans Affairs, VISN 04, Erie VA Medical Center hereby referred to as the Erie VAMC, is requesting the Brand Name only acquisition of the following: Product name: Pyxis ES Medication Dispensing Refrigerator Capacity: 13 cubic foot, RHG-15 Standard Bins Product ID/Part Number: 137549-01 Quantity: 1 EA MAJOR REQUIREMENTS: The Contractor/Vendor must complete the following tasks to meet the needs of this acquisition: Shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and non-personal services necessary to deliver, install and integrate the new Pyxis ES refrigerator. Salient Characteristics for Brand Name Pyxis ES Medication Dispensing Refrigerator. Will be responsible for installing the refrigerators and ensure they are linked with the current Pyxis System. The Contractor must be BD CareFusion certified or work with BD CareFusion to ensure the items are properly installed, functional, and ready for use.

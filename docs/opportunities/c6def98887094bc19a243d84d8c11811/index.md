# M264 M55 Electric Ignition Element

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-19 07:12:09.021-04
- Solicitation Number: N0010426RK

## Summary

NAVSUP Weapon Systems Support (NAVSUP WSS) Mechanicsburg is seeking eligible contractors to furnish effort including labor, material, and facilities as may be required to manufacture the items as specified within this document. The Government intends to solicit as full and open competition. The Government intends to solicit for the procurement of: NSN 1377-01-213-6171, M264, M55 Electric Ignition Element, DWG 10534225; for use on the Bomb Rack Release of the S-3, P-3, and H-60 aircraft. The government does possess the data necessary to perform the required repairs or manufacture of the item. All responsible sources may identify their interest and provide capability statements in response to this notice. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive proposals. However, all responses received prior to the closing date of this sources sought notice will be considered by the Government. A determination by the Government not to compete with this proposed contract based upon responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Approval of a source may be required and WSS will not delay award while Source Approval is pending.

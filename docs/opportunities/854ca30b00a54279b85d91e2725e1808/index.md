## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1284LM26Q0030 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for tree planting services within the Plains Thompson Falls Ranger District. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at https://www.acquisition.gov/far-overhaul. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 115310. The small business size standard is $11.5M. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. See “Attachment 1 for applicable clauses, instructions, and evaluation criteria. Please send any questions regarding this solicitation to joanne.meiers@usda.gov. AMD 0001 Changes the following: 1. Attachment 1 Solicitation Terms and Conditions, Line item 0001AA description changed from 6-inch Container: >199 tpa to Tree Planting 12 x 12 spacing with 18" Scalp (See Attachment 2 Statement of Work, Section 5.0), Solicitation Attachments, Attachment 4 - Summary of Information Chart changed to Attachment 4 - Summary Information Chart AMD 0001, and due date for questions removed (p. 15), 2. Attachment 4 Summary Information Chart AMD 0001, Stock Type for Graves Creek Unit UGC5 changed to Bareroot/Container, and 3. Due date for quotes extended to March 17, 2026, 1700 MT. Changes are highlighted in green. Please sign and return the SF-30 to acknowledge the amendment.

# Internet and Cable Notice

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 12:16:20.577-04

## Summary

** This is not a solicitation. This is a special notice to provide instructions for a solicitation that the Government intends to post** The U.S. Army Contracting Command- Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) intends to post a solicitation to award a five-year contract on Redstone Arsenal, AL for the following items: Cable television and internet services. This is a notice of intent, not a request for competitive proposals, or a solicitation of offers. This is a special notice for all contractors that may be interested in bidding, that in order to successfully bid on the future solicitation, the contractor must already have approval and licensed entry access on Redstone Arsenal. This notice provides instructions on how to obtain entry approval within the attached PWS. The process can take around six months to obtain. The points of contact for this special notice and for any questions, is Katie Wright and Jeffrey Yeager at katharine.c.wright.civ@army.mil and jeffrey.b.yeager2.civ@army.mil. Please reach out to both emails.

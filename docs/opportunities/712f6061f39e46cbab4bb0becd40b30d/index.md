# Beach Equipment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 07:46:25.967-04
- Solicitation Number: FA481426Q0008

## Summary

This requirement outlines the procurement of new commercial-grade equipment to furnish the beach recreation area. The acquisition includes a stainless steel, bi-level commercial charcoal grill with a cooking surface of 800-1200 square inches, intended for in-ground installation. For waste management, commercial receptacles made from recycled plastic are required, each featuring a rain guard and a minimum 32-gallon liner capacity. Seating needs will be addressed by acquiring 6-foot picnic tables made of recycled plastic, including both standard and ADA-compliant versions. All items must be covered by a two-year parts and labor warranty.

# Bulk Propane Supply & Delivery - Dugway Proving Ground - Request for Quotations

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 17:58:51.871-04

## Summary

Solicitation Number: W911S6-26-Q-A009 Title: Bulk Propane Supply for U.S. Army Dugway Proving Ground, UT PSC Code: 9130 - Fuel, Petroleum, Oil, and Lubricants NAICS Code: 221210 - Natural Gas Distribution The Mission and Installation Contracting Command (MICC) at Dugway Proving Ground (DPG), Utah, has a requirement for the supply and delivery of bulk commercial propane. The Government intends to award a single, Firm-Fixed-Price with Economic Price Adjustment requirements contract. The anticipated period of performance is a one-year base period with four (4), one-year option periods. This is a full and open competitive acquisition conducted in accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 12, Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and services necessary to provide bulk propane to various locations across Dugway Proving Ground, as defined in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). The total estimated annual requirement is approximately 1,202,000 gallons. All relevant solicitation documents are available for download with this notice. Offerors are responsible for reading the entire solicitation before submitting a quote.

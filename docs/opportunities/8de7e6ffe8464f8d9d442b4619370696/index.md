# Defense Maritime Solutions - Wartsila Shaft & Rudder Seal Kits for Various Class Vessel's across Coast Guard

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF

## Summary

Pioneer Industries, LLC has been awarded the contract for the supply of shaft and rudder seal kits to the United States Coast Guard (USCG). The parts supplied are original equipement manufacturer (OEM) from Defence Maritime Solutions (DMS) for use on cutters throughout the USCG.

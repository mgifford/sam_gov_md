# Low-Level Radioactive Waste Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:21:49.21-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC25GA001

## Summary

Purpose: Pursuant to the authority of Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 16.703, the United States Government, represented by Army Contracting Command- Rock Island (ACC-RI) establishes a no-costs draft Request for Proposal (RFP) to establish a Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) for the identification, removal, remediation, processing, and disposal associated with Low-Level Radioactive Waste (LLRW) projects. The place of performance will be project-specific for each task order. The Government intends to award the BOA by 01 October 2026. The ordering period for this BOA is a 5 year period. The total duration of the BOA, including all options, shall not exceed 5 years. This requirement is a competitive 8(a) set-aside. The Government reserves the right to amend this solicitation should legislative or regulatory changes impact the viability of the 8(a) set-aside. In such an event, the solicitation may be revised to a Small Business set-aside.

# The Government is seeking interested contractors that are capable of performing Stevedoring Services described in the attached Draft PWS for NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Sigonella site Rota, Spain.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-24 04:14:54.089-04
- Solicitation Number: N6817125QT004

## Summary

The US Government has a requirement for Stevedoring services at NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Sigonella, Rota Site, Spain. We are seeking companies that are capable and interested in performing Stevedoring Services per description in the attached Draft PWS for NAVSUP Fleet Logistics Center Sigonella, Rota Site, Spain. This effort involves providing services to load and stow subsistence and supplies onboard all SURFLANT U.S. Navy vessels and transient units located in Rota, Spain. The contractor shall provide NAVSUP FLC Sigonella Rota site services to include unload, transport, load and stow all subsistence items and supplies from the staging area on the pier to designated location onboard the ship. We are contemplating a 5-Year Agreement that would begin 01 July 2026 to provide services to perform the duties described in this requirement. If your company is interested and capable of performing this requirement, please submit: 1. A response to this email stating your interest. 2. An overview of your capabilities and experience for similar services in the same or related industry. Please note that this is not a Request for Quote or Proposals, we are simply conducting market research efforts. Please submit your responses to Mr. Ivan Moyano at ivan.moyano.ln@us.navy.mil and Ms. Keishla Valenzuela at keishla.a.valenzuela.civ@us.navy.mil by 27 March 2026 at 11:00 Local Rota time, Spain. ***Note that you company will be required to comply with local Spanish labor laws, insurance, regulations and be duly authorized to operate and to do business in Spain.***

## Summary

Pursuant to Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 12.202, the Contracting Officer is issuing a combined synopsis/solicitation for the acquisition of Fee Collection Services for the Bureau of Land Management (BLM) Imperial Sand Dunes Recreation Area (ISDRA) managed by the BLM El Centro Field Office, Imperial County, CA. Interested parties may submit questions relating to this solicitation via electronic mail to the point of contacts listed in section and are due no later than close of business Thursday, May 7, 2026. Quotes are due no later than May 26, 2026, 10 a.m. PT. Quotes shall be submitted via electronic mail to point of contact.

# Test Facility Pump System Replacement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 15:30:33.394-04
- Solicitation Number: FA9101269TFPS

## Summary

22 April 2026 - Extended proposal due date and amended CMMC Article. _________________________________________________________ The United States Air Force (USAF), through AEDC, is seeking proposals from industry to deliver a design and demonstration of operational utility prototype in the form of a raw water supply system, including pumps, supporting infrastructure, and a new free-standing building at Arnold Air Force Base, TN. The objective is to replace the aging system and integrate a new, more reliable and flexible system into the existing site infrastructure. Please see attachments for details.

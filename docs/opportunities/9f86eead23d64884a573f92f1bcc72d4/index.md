# Testing and Calibration of Testing Equipment for USACE Lake Projects Stockton and Harry S. Truman Powerplants

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 12:29:37.523-04
- Solicitation Number: W912DQ26QA057

## Summary

Description of Services Required: The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Kansas City District has an immediate need for Testing and Calibration of Electrical Testing Equipment at Harry S. Truman and Stockton Lake Projects, Warsaw, MO and Stockton, MO respectively. This is a service contract to provide testing and calibration of testing equipment at two (2) Powerhouses. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform inspection, testing, and calibration of testing equipment as defined in the Performance Work Statement (PWS), except for those items specified as government furnished property and services. The Contractor must ensure that all personnel, whether employees or subcontractors, possess current certifications and qualifications required for the testing and calibration of the specified equipment. This includes documented training and certification from relevant standards organizations.

The Department of Veterans Affairs Medical Center, Network Contracting Office (NCO) 6, on behalf of the Salem VA Medical Center, located in Salem, Virginia is conducting market research to determine whether to set-aside an acquisition for Hospital Consumables and accessories: Hydrocolloid Dressing for Salem VA Medical Center (VAMC) for Base plus 4 Option Years. The Department of Veteran Affairs, Salem VAMC seeks a contractor for the delivery of the Hospital Consumables and accessories: Hydrocolloid Dressing. The contractor shall provide items which include all requested items and delivery for items use by the Pharmacy Department.

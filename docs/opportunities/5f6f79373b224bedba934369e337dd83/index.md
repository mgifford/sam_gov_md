# Sale of Scrap Deformed Small Arms Fired Cartridge Casings

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 08:13:40.788-04
- Solicitation Number: QRP26-0004

## Summary

The Fort Knox Qualified Recycle Program is issuing an invitation to bid on the sale of Sale of Scrap Deformed Small Arms Fired Cartridge Casings. This is a Government Sale. The duration of this solicitation is for 1 load. If interested, please open the attached sales solicitation/agreement, complete and return to the Fort Knox Recycle Center. Ensure the solicitation is filled out completely to include a signature, date, address, telephone number, and initials on each page in the designated fields. Most importantly, enter your company's offer price on page 2. Send your bid to Jamie Daley and Anita Overton by email at jamie.l.daley2.naf@army.mil. & anita.l.overton2.naf@army.mil. Respond by 4/15/26 at 2pm Eastern Standard Time. Please call the Fort Knox QRP if you have any questions or concerns. All Bids are welcome.

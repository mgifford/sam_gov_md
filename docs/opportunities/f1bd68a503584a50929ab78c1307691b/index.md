# Local Guard Forces U.S. Mission Santiago, Chile

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 14:09:53.15-04
- Solicitation Number: 19AQMM26R0021

## Summary

The U.S. Department of State (DOS) has a requirement for a qualified Contractor to provide Local Guard Services to support the U.S. Mission in Santiago, Chile. The Government requires a secure environment to conduct their mission. The Contractor shall provide the organizational structure, management, and qualified staff at levels adequate to meet or exceed the requirement contained in the Performance Work Statement. The Contractor shall be required to provide services in a manner that prevent loss or injury to U.S. personnel, dependents, property; destruction of assets; to prevent unauthorized access; and deter potential terrorist attacks. Anticipated period of performance is one base year and four one-year options (to be exercised at the sole discretion of the Government). All contracted personnel, regardless of nationality, will be required to pass a background investigation conducted by the Contractor and a separate background investigation conducted by the U.S. Government to certify their suitability for employment under this contract. Once the solicitation is posted on SAM.GOV, the offerors will have approximately thirty (30) calendar days to submit proposals. All dates are subject to change and any change will be posted on this site. Offerors shall follow instructions to offerors per solicitation guidelines. Site visit presentation will be provided. No site visti presentation will be held at post. PHONE CALLS WILL NOT BE ACCEPTED Offerors shall have an active registration on the System for Award Management at https://www.SAM.gov. Offerors using Joint Ventures shall have a proper registration as a Joint Venture including Unique Entity ID. Offerors shall work with sam.gov to obtain access to the solicitation via registration and should contact sam.gov help desk for assistance. In accordance with 22 U.S.C. § 4864, the Government will give preference to offerors qualifying as U.S. persons or U.S. Joint Venture Persons. The contract will be awarded to the best technically acceptable proposal with the lowest evaluated price. A-001 POSTED 03/31/2026- 19AQMM26R0021- A001 with Exbibits- attachment replaced on 4/01/2026

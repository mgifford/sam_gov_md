# USAFRICOM Dedicated Passenger Air Charter Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-20 13:23:08.546-04
- Solicitation Number: HTC711
- Awardee: PHOENIX AIR GROUP, INC. Cartersville GA 30120 USA
- Award Amount: 67998256.00

## Summary

U.S. Transportation Command (USTRANSCOM) has awarded a firm fixed price contract to Phoenix Air Group, Inc., Cartersville, GA, for Dedicated Passenger Air Charter Services (DPACS) to support United States Africa Command (USAFRICOM) operations. The base year award is valued at $11,880,350.00, and the contract was awarded on February 27, 2026. The contracting office responsible for this award is USTRANSCOM’s Directorate of Acquisition, located at 508 Scott Drive, Scott Air Force Base, Illinois.

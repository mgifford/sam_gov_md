# SOLICITATION: Mowing Services for Whitney and Aquilla Lakes, TX

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 16:44:16.857-04

## Summary

SOLICITATION: This announcement is for a competitive small business set aside, firm-fixed price, single award indefinite delivery indefinite quantity contract, procured in accordance with FAR Part 12 (RFO) Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services to determine best value in accordance with the Comparative Analysis Procedures IAW RFO 12.203 /// Please note this solicitation requires offerors to enter a National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) score in the Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS). The NIST score is required before award. /// Project Scope: The Contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform recreational park and office mowing and trimming at Whitney and Aquilla Lakes. The Contractor shall accomplish all operations safely, in accordance with EM 385-1-1.

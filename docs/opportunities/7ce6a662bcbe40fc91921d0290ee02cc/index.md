# Maritime COMS Notice of Tentative Acquisition Strategy under FTSS V MAC Lot I

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 09:53:07.591-04
- Solicitation Number: N6134026R1014

## Summary

The Naval Air Warfare Center Training Systems Division (NAWCTSD), in accordance with FAR 16.505 (b)(1), intends to provide fair opportunity for award of a Firm Fixed Price Task Order under FTSS V Lot 1 (Unrestricted) in support of Maritime Contractor Operation & Maintenance Services (COMS), Training Systems Maintenance (TSM), and Contractor Maintenance Services (CMS) at NAS Jacksonville, FL and NAS Whidbey Island, WA. The period of performance is 65 months, inclusive of two (2) month mobilization and one (1) five (5) month base year, four (4) twelve (12) month options, and one (1) ten (10) month option. Start of contractor performance (Mobilization) anticipated to be 01 April 2027. This effort will be awarded under FTSS V, Lot I and will be limited to Lot I MAC holders only. A list of FTSS V Lot 1 Points of Contact is included with this notice for possible subcontracting opportunities. Anticipated Site Visits/Pre-Proposal Conference: Site Visits July 2026 Anticipated RFP Release Date: July 2026 Anticipated Award Date: February 2027 Other Information: Predecessor Contract: N6134022F0068, Valiant Global Defense Services Inc The Service Contract Labor Standards Statute is applicable.Be advised that the aforementioned information is anticipatory in nature and is not binding. Further information will be provided, as it becomes known.

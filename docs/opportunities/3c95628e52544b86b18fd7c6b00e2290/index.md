# 732 AMS Airfield CCTV Camera Upgrades - Amendment 3

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 15:53:32.529-04
- Solicitation Number: FA500026QCMRA

## Summary

Amendment 3 - Extends the quqte due date from 22 April 2026 to 23 April 2026 to allow for consideration of the update to Attachment 1 Salient Characteristics, made to be consistent with the update to Attachment 5 Questions & Answers (Question 35). All updates are highlited in the amended COMBO and attachments. The 732nd Air Mobility Squadron (AMS) requires a new, commercially available camera system to upgrade its current system. This upgrade will include 22 new cameras, servers, controls, and monitors to be installed in existing locations for monitoring the flightline, various ramps, and the interior of a freight warehouse in accordance with the attached Salient Characteristics and building schematics for Bldgs 15380, 15431, 15510, and 10364. Installation will be performed at JBER, Alaska and must be completed by 01 September 2026.

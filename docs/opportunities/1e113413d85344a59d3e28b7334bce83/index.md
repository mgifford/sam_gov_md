# MV Breaker Annual Service

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 09:08:59.991-04
- Solicitation Number: 19UK5626Q0026

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy in London has a requirement for MV breaker annual service for a base year and four (4) option years. Only companies or individuals specializing in this field should apply. The Government intends to award a contract/purchase order to the responsible company submitting an acceptable offer at the lowest price. We intend to award the order based on initial quotations, without holding discussions, although we may hold discussions with companies in the competitive range if there is a need to do so. Please note that the RFQ documentation is not ready to be issued. The solicitation will be published on SAM.gov under RFQ #19UK5626Q0026.

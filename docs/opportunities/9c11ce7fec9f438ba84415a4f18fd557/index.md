# B1825 LIGHTNING PROTECTION SYSTEM

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 15:00:27.059-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462626Q0008

## Summary

This requirement is to provide all items, articles, materials, operations, or methods listed, mentioned or scheduled on the drawings and/or as specified herein, including all labor, materials, equipment, and incidentals necessary and required for completion of a lightning protection system (LPS) upgrade and inspection. The LPS system cables shall be up sized to AWG 4/0 (approximately ½”) copper to bring the system up to code, and a small quantity of frayed cable will be replaced. The LPS inspection shall be compliant with the most current edition of NFPA 780. The Contractor shall provide and deliver to the United States Air Force a hard copy and a digital CD of the acceptance testing results and the UL Lightning Protection Inspection Certificate for the facility. The UL Lightning Protection Inspection Certificate shall be certified to NFPA 780 Chapter 8. The Contractor shall have attended and completed the Senior Inspector AMMO 47 or better training or equivalent. Please See attached SOW for complete details.

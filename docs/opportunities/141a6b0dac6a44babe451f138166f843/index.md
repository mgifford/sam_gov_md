# Regional Aircraft Phase Sustainment Center (RAPSC)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 12:39:13.093-04
- Solicitation Number: W912L2-26-R-0001

## Summary

The Arizona Army National Guard (AZARNG) intends to issue a Request for Proposal (RFP) to provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and quality control. The contractor shall provide qualified and professional staff to perform Phased Maintenance Inspections (PMI) I and II annually; and 48-month service and inspections for the H-60 Series Aircraft. The contractor shall work in specialized shops and aircraft hangars associated with the Field level maintenance fixed base operations as well as perform troubleshooting and maintenance repairs on the flight line in support of Army Rotary Wing aircraft operational readiness. The contractor shall perform scheduled and unscheduled services, and corrosion control activities in accordance with (IAW) government directives, policies and technical publications.

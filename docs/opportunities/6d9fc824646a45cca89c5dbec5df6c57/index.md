## Summary

Project No.: CL25342002 Classification Code: 2815 - DIESEL ENGINES AND COMPONENTS NAICS:, 333618 - Other Engine Equipment Manufacturing Title: DLA Land & Maritime is preparing to solicit the NSN(s) (see NSNs listed below) for a Commercial Indefinite Quantity long-term contract. These items are spare parts for the PATRIOT MISSLE SYSTEM and both AM General (34623) and General MOTORS LLC (7X677) are the only approved sources. DLA Land & Maritime does NOT have any technical data with which to procure these items. The purpose of this synopsis is to locate sources interested in becoming approved sources. The solicitation will be synopsized separately at a later date. Interested sources may submit the appropriate information outlined in DLAD Procurement Note L04, Offers for Part Numbered Items at this time and until the solicitation synopsis closes. Alternate offer packages may also be submitted at any other time to be considered for future procurements. The agency has no data with which to evaluate alternate products. Please read about all of the information required before submitting an alternate offer. Responses must be sent to Michael Gallina via email at Michael.Gallina@dla.mil. PLEASE PROVIDE CAGE CODE in response. Respondents to this, sources sought, synopsis will be notified via email when the solicitation is published to DIBBS and ready to download.

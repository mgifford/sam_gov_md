# DLA Disposition Services Industry Day 2026

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 15:14:59.13-04

## Summary

DLA Disposition Services Is holding an Industry Day forum focused on Contracting Industry Engagement. This event is being held in held at the DoubleTree by Hilton in Battle Creek, MI Wednesday May 20th and Thursday May 21st, 2026, from 8:00 am to 4:30 pm EST The Defense Logistics Agency must operate on a wartime footing amid the contested logistics environment in which we live. This industry engagement event is meant to strengthen readiness and accelerate innovation in support of the Warfighter, so that DLA can continue to provide world class support to our uniformed customers. Address: 50 Capital Ave SW, Battle Creek, MI 49017 DLA Disposition Services Contracting topics will include a variety of subjects related to: Small Business Operational Contracting Hazardous Waste Disposal Contracting Public Sales Contracting Useable Surplus Material Government Scrap Contracts

## Summary

The Ouachita National Forest is soliciting miscellaneous building construction services. All work to be performed under the awarded contract will be in accordance with the including specifications and drawings provided for this project(s). This acquisition is a total small business set-aside. The associated NAICS code is 238390 Other Building Finishing Contractors with a small business size standard of $19 million. The product service code is Z1AA – Maintenance Of Office Buildings The period of performance will be for ninety (90) days.

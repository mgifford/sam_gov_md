# removal, purchase and installation of U-shaped salad bar at Camp Zama, JAPAN

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 01:46:23.403-04
- Solicitation Number: FA520926RSALADCZ

## Summary

This is a Presolicitation Notice issued in accordance with (IAW) Revolutionary FAR Overhaul (RFO) FAR 5.1 and 12.201-1(c)(2). THIS NOTICE IS NOT A REQUEST FOR COMPETITIVE PROPOSALS NOR QUOTATIONS. The Government intends to solicit this requirement directly from suppliers IAW RFO FAR 12.201-1(c)(2) for firm-fixed price service contract to purchase and install U-Shaped Salad Bar for its Camp Zama SWR (bldg. 333), Kanagawa Prefecture, JAPAN. Anticipated award date is around 31 Jul 2026 (subject to change). Agency: Department of the Air Force Office: 374th Contracting Squadron, Bldg 620, Yokota Air Base, Fussa-shi, Tokyo, Japan Set-aside: N/Ad NAICS Code: 333241 Description: The contractor shall provide all necessary labor, personnel, supervision, management, tools, equipment, transportation, materials, training and any other items or services necessary to procure and install the U-Shaped Salad Bar at SWR (bldg. 333), and remove and dispose of the existing Salad Bar. Note: This project will be performed in its entirety in the country of Japan. The successful offeror must have valid licenses to perform work in the country of Japan.

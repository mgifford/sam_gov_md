# Testing, Pick up and Disposal of Petroleum, Oil and Lubricant (POL)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 15:08:17.38-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CN-26-Q-A018

## Summary

The Regional Contracting Office Hawaii (RCO—HI) has a requirement for Non-Hazardous Testing, Pick up and Disposal of Petroleum, Oil and Lubricant (POL). The contractor shall provide all the resources necessary to complete this requirement on the island of Oahu, Hawaii. The solicitation will be a 100% Set Aside for Small Business. The award will be conducted under the provisions of FAR Part 12 Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. To be considered for the award, the prospective contractor must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM). It is the responsibility of the prospective offeror to monitor this solicitation for the release of amendments (if any) or other information pertaining to the solicitation. Refer to pages 67-69 on the attached solicitation for Instructions to Offerors. Prospective offerors must complete and submit all required documents to be considered a potential offeror. Questions can be addressed prior to the deadline (April 30, 2026, 10:00 AM HST) to Travis Tonini via E-mail at Travis.C.Tonini.civ@army.mil. All completed forms & supporting documentation must be submitted to Travis Tonini via E-mail at Travis.C.Tonini.civ@army.mil by May 20, 2026 at 10:00 AM Hawaiian Standard Time (HST).

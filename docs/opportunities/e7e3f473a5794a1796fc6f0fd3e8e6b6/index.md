# Special Notice of Non-Competitive Award

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-13 15:02:30.196-04
- Solicitation Number: N6600126D0015

## Summary

This contract will provide technical support for the Naval Information Warfare Center (NIWC) International Positioning, Navigation and Timing (PNT) Division, Code 55790. The support required includes engineering, technical, security, maintenance, material and logistics support to the International Foreign Military Sales (FMS) program for North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) and Friendly United States (US) Countries, and sponsors. Support will be provided to customers that will include the USAF Space and Missile System Center (SMC) Production Corps Foreign Military Branch (PCFB), NAVSEASYSCOM, SPAWARSYSCOM PMWs, and participating countries in the FMS Program in both the Continental United States (CONUS) and Outside the Continental United States (OCONUS).

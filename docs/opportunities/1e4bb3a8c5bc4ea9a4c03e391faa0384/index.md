## Summary

***This notice is for SEAPORT NXG MAC HOLDERS ONLY and is an 8(a) Set-Aside. No other offers will be considered. SeaPort MAC holders will need to register for PIEE accounts in order to receive soliciations and submit proposals/offers.*** Naval Information Warfare Center Atlantic (NIWC Atlantic), Charleston, SC requires support for United States Coast Guard (USCG) Shipboard Command, Control, Communications, Computers, Cyber, Intelligence (C5I) Readiness, Integration, Technical Services. The contractors will be tasked to accomplish the work specified in the performance work statement (PWS). The PWS includes Controlled Classified Information (CUI) and will not be made publically avaiable. In order to obtain access to the PWS, the Offeror shall download, complete, and submit a signed Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) for each individual requiring access to the non-public documents to carson.l.rosenbaum.civ@us.navy.mil. Questions are due to carson.l.rosenbaum.civ@us.navy.mil by 2026/04/10 at 13:00.

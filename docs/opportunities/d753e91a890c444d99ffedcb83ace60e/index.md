# Notice of intent to award sole source contract

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 14:31:10.434-04
- Solicitation Number: 47QSCC26R5006

## Summary

Notice of intent to award sole source contract. GSA intends to award a sole source contract to SupplyCore LLC. The statutory authority allowing other than full and open competition is FAR 6.103-1(a) (GSA Class Deviation RFO 2025-06). The subject contract is for sourcing, warehousing National Stock Number (NSN) items and for delivering these items within 3 business days in INDO-PACOM Japan. The contractor is required to provide a total supply chain solution from sourcing, procuring, warehousing, transporting or arranging for transportation/delivery to the location identified in each Delivery Order (DO). This sole-source contract will have a six (6)-month base period with two (2) three (3)-month option periods, ensuring operational continuity. This is a notice of the intent to award a sole source contract only. Please direct all questions and/or comments regarding this notice to Contracting Officer, Valeriya Orlova (valeriya.orlova@gsa.gov).

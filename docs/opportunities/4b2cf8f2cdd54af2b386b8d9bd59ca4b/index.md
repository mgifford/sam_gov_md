Quoter/Offeror Questions: All questions pertaining to this solicitation and its attachments must be emailed to mary.harding@usda.gov no later than 27 February 2026. Quote Due Date and Time: 5:00 p.m. Central Standard Time on 06 March 2026. All quotes and technical proposals must be emailed to mary.harding@usda.gov by the established deadline date and time. Estimated Magnitude of Construction Cost: Between $250,000 and $500,000 Proposed Start Date: 01 April 2026 - Please note that work cannot occur on bunkhouses or the laundry building during the period 15 April 2026 through 31 October 2026. Required Completion Date: 15 April 2027 Bonding Requirements: Payment and Performance Bonds will be required for this construction project.

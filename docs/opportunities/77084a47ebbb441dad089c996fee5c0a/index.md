# C--Rehabilitate Buzzard Point Park

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 09:17:55.237-04
- Solicitation Number: 140D0426R0027

## Summary

Amendment 0001 - Update closing date of solicitation. Responses to contractor questions is forthcoming. Department of the Interior (DOI), Interior Business Center (IBC), Acquisition Services Directorate (AQD) on behalf of the National Park Service (NPS) intends to issue a single contract for Title I & II Architect-Engineer (AE) services for the Rehabilitation of Buzzard Point Park, a section of park land within Anacostia Park. This solicitation is issued and will be evaluated under the authority of Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Overhaul Subpart 36.102 - Architect-Engineer Services. This is a total small business set-aside under NAICS 541320 - Landscape Architecture. Please review the solicitation and attachments carefully to ensure a complete and timely response.

# Notice of Sole Source WA ANG Recruiting Theater Advertising FY26

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-08 12:11:26.684-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S9E26QS001

## Summary

This is a notification of intent to award a sole source acquisition to the National CinMedia LLC for advertising on movie theaters/screens under their exclusive license for advertising. The location of all these theaters are within the state of Washington. The requirement is for advertising to run on ~32 screens for a period of 5 weeks with a contract amount estimated at $25,000.00.

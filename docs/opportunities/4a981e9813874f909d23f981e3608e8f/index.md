## Summary

Statement of Requirements The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), VISN 15 VA Hospital Medical Centers (VAMCs), has a requirement for the rental of inpatient beds, mattresses, and accessories (See List Below) The requirement will include all inpatient requirements of the facility along with; Spinal Cord Injury Units, and Community Living Centers located at the facilities only. Under the contract the contractor shall provide delivery, set-up, maintenance/service/repair, and pick-up of all items. Key Operator Training will also be needed for all rental items.

## Summary

The U.S. Geological Survey Water Resources Mission Area Observing Systems Division requires the purchase of handheld TEM profiler to be delivered to Storrs CT, within 30 days from the date the contactor receives the purchase order. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which shall be considered by the agency. Quotations shall be submitted to Mindie Dixon at mindie_dixon@ios.doi.gov

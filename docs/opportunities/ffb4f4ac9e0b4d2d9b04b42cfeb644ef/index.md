## Summary

Notice of Intent to Award a Sole Source Contract The U.S. Coast Guard (USCG) hereby provides a notice of intent to award a sole source, firm-fixed-price purchase order to SINTEF Ocean AS. Product/Service: The requirement is for the payment of annual software maintenance service fees for two (2) seat licenses of VERES (VEssel RESponse) and ShipX Workbench. This program is a strip theory, frequency domain program used for the simulation of vessel motions and global loads. Authority: This sole source contracting action is in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 13.106-1(b)(1), which allows for soliciting from a single source when the contracting officer determines that the circumstances of the contract action deem only one source reasonably available. Justification: It is the belief of the government that SINTEF Ocean AS is the only source capable of fulfilling this requirement. VERES is a unique program that combines linear seakeeping analysis, structural loading, and slamming. The software has been in use by the USCG for over a decade, and the agency maintains multiple built-in models of its ships and boats. Market research indicates that no other commercially available software offers the combined capabilities of structural loading analysis, davit motion analysis, crane acceleration evaluation, and comprehensive seakeeping simulations in a single package.

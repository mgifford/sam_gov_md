# REPEATERS SERVICES FOR RADIO COMMUNICATION BETWEEN CSO TRANSPORTATION DISPATCHER AND GSA SCHOOL BUSES DOWEA MID ATLANTIC DISTRICT FIELD SITE -PR FORT BUCHANAN, PR 00934

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 10:35:37.118-04
- Solicitation Number: HE125426QE020

## Summary

The Department of War (DoWEA) Education Activity (formally known as DoDEA)Mid-Atlantic District Field Site, Fort Buchanan, Bayamon, and Ramey, Aguadilla, Puerto Rico requires the procurement of two antenna repeaters that will connect a modern VHF communications system located at both locations. The requirement will also include service calls for Repair/Replacement parts for base station radios. Please download a copy of the attached draft Performance Work Statement (PWS), which contains more details about this service requirement.

# Mobilizer & Wrecker Crane Cover

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 13:49:12.311-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S7X26QA005

## Summary

This solicitation is for Mobilizer and Wrecker Crane Covers that meet the following: Quantity: Equipment Covers 1. 12 EA Covers for M832 mobilizers - The M-832 Mobilizer (Part Number 209666) - Dimensions of mobilizer: 96" W x 96" H x 407" L (including towbar) 2. 1 EA Cover wrecker crane - M1089A1P2 FMTV Wrecker (Family of Medium Tactical Vehicle), 5 Ton MHE (Material Handling Crane) Crane - Dimensions of crane: 96” W x 111” H X 379” L Characteristics: Equipment Covers Will need to be easy to use or install with max of 2 people Covers will be made from water resistant material to keep the equipment dry to reduce the risk of corrosion and water damage. Covers will provide UV Protection protecting rubber components and slowing paint wear. Covers will provide Anti-condensation capability to prevent condensation and mold growth under cover. Customized to fit and made to cover the M832 and wrecker crane. Send all questions and offers on stated close date to 184WG.MSG.CONTRACTING@us.af.mil with the title "Mobilizer & Wrecker Crane Cover

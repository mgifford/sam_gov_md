# TAC VEH Fire Suppression System (FSS) and Air Conditioner (AC) maintenance and repair services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 17:50:31.714-05
- Solicitation Number: M0068126Q0034
- Awardee: null RCO-MCIWEST Twentynine Palms, CA 92055-5001

## Summary

This is a service contract to provide Fire Suppression System (FSS) and Air Conditioner (AC) maintenance and repair services on armored tactical wheeled and tracked vehicles with a FSS and AC System at Marine Air Ground Task Force Training Command (MAGTFTC), Twentynine Palms, CA. The Government shall not exercise supervision or control over the Contractor Employees and the Contractor shall be accountable for the maintenance, repair, refilling of FSS/AC systems to the Government.

# Sources Sought CATV and Internet Services Schofield Barracks Hawaii

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-08 22:16:31.522-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CNCable

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice only. This is not a solicitation announcement or a Request for Quote (RFQ). This announcement is for information and planning purposes only and is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government, implied or otherwise to issue a solicitation or ultimately award a contract. This is a market research tool being utilized to determine the availability of sources prior to issuing an RFQ. The 413th Contracting Support Brigade, Regional Contracting Office-Hawaii is performing market research in regards to parties capable of providing cable television and internet services at approximately thirteen locations on Schofield Barracks, Hawaii. The requirement is to provide internet services and cable television programming with Command Channel (on-installation channel(s) for use by the installation), excluding housing units in accordance with applicable United States Army and local regulations. Contractor shall be responsible for providing all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, vehicles and supervision necessary to install, maintain, repair, etc equipment pertinent to providing cable television (CATV) programming and internet services. Period of Performance will be for one year of operation from 1 May 2026 – 1 May 2027. Potential sources must submit their capabilities in writing. The Capabilities Statement shall contain the Company's experience providing CATV and internet services within the past three years, unique capabilities and resources, and the Company's size status under the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 515210. Responses must be submitted via email to MAJ Douglas G. Dorward at douglas.g.dorward.mil@army.mil by April 16, 2016 1:00PM Hawaii time.

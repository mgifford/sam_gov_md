# FCI MCKEAN TRAINING AMMUNITION NEEDS

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 13:01:30.297-04
- Solicitation Number: 15B20926Q00000011

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Prisons, Federal Correctional Institution, McKean, intends to issue solicitation 15B20926Q00000011 for Training Ammunition FY 26. This solicitation is restricted, 100% Small Business. All quotes should include shipping costs, FOB destination. Please notate any additional Excise tax that will be assessed. Deliveries will be made to FCI McKean’s Armory Only. Located at 6975 Route 59, Lewis Run, PA 16738. The contract type is a Firm Fixed Price. The Government will award to the responsible offeror whose bid, conforming to the solicitation, are considered to be most advantageous to the Government, with both Price, and Delivery time considered. The solicitation will be available Wednesday April 8, 2026 at 12:00 PM EST and will be posted to the General Services Administration Federal Business Opportunities website. www.sam.gov. All future information about this acquisition, including solicitation amendments, will be distributed through this site. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring this site to ensure they have the most up to date information about this solicitation. The closing date for this solicitation will be April 20, 2026, at 12:00 PM EST. Payment will be made using Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) through a purchase order. The North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code applicable to this requirement is 332992. The point of contact for all information regarding this solicitation is Matthew Morris, Procurement and Property Specialist, at the address listed below and telephone number (814)362-8900 extension 3420 and Cory Sudbrook, Supervisory Contract Specialist telephone number, (814)362-8900 Extension 3419. Completed offers must be emailed to m4morris@bop.gov and csudbrook@bop.gov or faxed to (814)363-6815. Emails are preferred. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please call to verify. The anticipated award date is April 24, 2026.

# Tactical Ground Mobility (TGM) - Enhanced Driving Skills Courses of Instruction (COI) Single-Award Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity Ordering Contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-13 20:34:24.171-04
- Solicitation Number: H9224026Q2003

## Summary

It is the Government’s intent to award a Firm-Fixed-Price Single-Award Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Ordering Contract. The ordering contract will have five (5) one (1) year ordering periods with a total capacity of $5,000,000.00. The objective of this requirement is to obtain services to develop and deliver Tactical Ground Mobility (TGM) Enhanced Driving Skills Courses of Instruction (COI). Contractor shall provide the personnel, management, materials, equipment, facilities, land, and vehicles (not otherwise provided by the Government) necessary to develop, manage, and instruct basic and advanced TGM EDS Training COI ensuring students receive sufficient TGM EDS Training and attain the necessary certifications. Training will be conducted in Contiguous United States at a contractor’s leased or owned facility/land. Training will be conducted in the Contiguous United States at a contractor provided facility/land. The Contractor provided owned/leased facility and land shall have appropriate student berthing/lodging location(s) for twelve (12) students and five (5) support personnel that are within 25 miles of the training site. This is a 100% Small Business set aside. Only SBA certified Small Business or Small Business Joint Venture firms that are certified under the applicable North American Classification System (NAICS) code size standard listed below at time of quote submission are eligible for award. Small Business or Small Business Joint Venture SBA certification status will be verified at https://search.certifications.sba.gov/?GoTo=Certs%.

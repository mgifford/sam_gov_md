- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 15:02:26.049-04

## Summary

This solicitation is for the ILARNG, the Contractor shall furnish all personnel supervision, parts, tools, materials, and transportation necessary to provide 3,615 prepared meals for the 2118th Asphalt Detatchment between 30 May - 16 June 2026 at the Crestwood Armory, located 13838 S Springfield Ave, Crestwood, IL. 60472. Follow the statement of work for all instructions and schedule of meals (breakfast and dinner only).

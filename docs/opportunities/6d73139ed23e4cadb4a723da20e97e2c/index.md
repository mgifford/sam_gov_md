# Butterfly Valves

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 13:46:07.395-04
- Solicitation Number: FA910126QB020

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation. The subject requirement is for a commercial product; as such, this solicitation is prepared in accordance with (IAW) the format in Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) part 12, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This solicitation is not an authorization to begin performance, and in no way obligates the Government for any costs incurred by the vendor associated with developing a quote. The Government reserves the right not to award a contract in response to this RFQ. Prior to commencement of any activities associated with performance of this requirement, the Government will issue a written directive or contractual document signed by the Contracting Officer with appropriate consideration established. The purpose of this solicitation is for the Government to procure twelve (12) 24-inch butterfly valves with manual operators and with non-modulating electrical actuators (IAW) Attachment 1 – Butterfly Valve Equipment Specifications and the American Water Works Association (AWWA) standards. Delivery is required to Arnold AFB in Tennessee.

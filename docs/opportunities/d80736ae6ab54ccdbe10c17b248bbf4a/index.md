## Summary

Description This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0355 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Water System Improvements, including installing new water well, decommissioning existing water well, replacing water system controls as well as replacing the treatment system. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 237110. The small business size standard is $45.0M. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

# Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO): 101 Training for Industry

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-11 09:57:52.455-04

## Summary

Updated 11 March 2026: This event is being held in person and is open to industry, registration is required. Virtual option is also available via MS Teams. Event will also be recorded and recording will be shared post event. ----- Description: Please join Subject Matter Expert(s) from the Army Contracting Command- Detroit Arsenal as we host Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO): 101 Training for Industry. Commercial Solutions Openings are a primary tool that ACC-DTA is using for agile acquisition and innovation. This event is being held on March 13, 2026, from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM EST and is open to Industry, registration is required. The event will be held at the Velocity Event Center, located at 6633 18 Mile Road Sterling Heights, MI 48314. Formal registration is required. Event Agenda: 0730- Registration 0800- Opening Remarks / Ground Rules 0815- CSO 101 Training Begins 1200- Event Concludes Participants Industry representatives, including both established and emerging companies. Organizations with limited government contracting experience are welcome. RSVP is required no later than 5:00 p.m. EDT on March 12, 2026, at the following link. https://forms.osi.apps.mil/r/B3R2eT7tu6

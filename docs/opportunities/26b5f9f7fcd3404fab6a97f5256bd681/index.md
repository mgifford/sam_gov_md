# USDA Intent to Award Destrehan, LA

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-23 15:00:00.388-04
- Solicitation Number: 57-6395-16-003V1

## Summary

Notice of Intent to Lease Space Using Other Than Full and Open Competition The United States Government intends to exercise a Renewal Option using other than full and open competition for an existing lease for the U. S. Department of Agriculture that is expiring in Destrehan, LA. The Government requests office and related space yielding a minimum of 10,109 to a maximum of 10,641 ANSI/BOMA office area and 12 adjacent parking spaces. The space must be located with the delineated area below, be contiguous, and all services, supplies, utilities, partitioning and tenant alternations must be included as part of the rental consideration. Delineated Area: Northern Boundary – US Hwy Interstate 10 Southern Boundary – Not to extend south beyond Hwy 48 (East Bank River Road) Eastern Boundary – St. Rose, LA (not to extend beyond St. Charles Parish Line) Western Boundary – Western Boundaries of Norco (not to extend beyond Apple St.) To submit a location for inspection, contact this office by 9:00 a.m. Central Standard Time on May 1, 2026. (Submissions by other than the owner or manager of a property must be accompanied by a letter from the owner granting permission to make a general offering of the space). Interested parties should send expressions of interest to: Andrew Johnson Realty Specialist USDA, APHIS, MRPBS, 250 Marquette Ave, Suite 410 Minneapolis, MN 55401 Phone: 612-336-3277 Fax: 612-336-3553 Email: andrew.johnson2@usda.gov

# Small Micro Reactor

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 11:07:10.736-04
- Solicitation Number: FA824026R3001

## Summary

The purpose of this Request for Information (RFI) is to assist the Government in identifying potential offerors for understanding the company’s capability to design, license, fuel, construct, and deploy Small, Micro, or Modular Reactor (SMR) technologies in compliance with applicable regulatory, safety, environmental, and security requirements. The information collected will help assess industry confidence in achieving and installing a commercially viable Nth-of-a-Kind (NOAK) capability.

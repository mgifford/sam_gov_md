# Rogue River-Siskiyou National Forest - Prospectus for Campground & Related Granger-Thye Concessions

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-15 17:15:12.986-04

## Summary

This prospectus is being issued to solicit applications for a concession campground special use permit. One permit to provide high-quality public service in the operation and maintenance of Government-owned recreation facilities located on the High Cascades Ranger District of the Rogue River-Siskiyou National Forest. The three-year average gross revenue for this opportunity is $252,791.94 (2023-2025). See attached document for additional information.

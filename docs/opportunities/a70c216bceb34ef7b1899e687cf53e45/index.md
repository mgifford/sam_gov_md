# B51 Generator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 12:01:52.469-04
- Solicitation Number: FA460026Q0022

## Summary

DESCRIPTION OF REQUIREMENT This requirement is for (1) 100 kW generator, (1) automatic transfer switch, (1) manual transfer switch, and all other fittings and connectors for a complete system. This item is needed for the uninterrupted operation of the B51 Traffic Check House at the STRATCOM Gate including barriers, DBIDS support equipment, and search pit. Future provisions are included for gate/barrier and search pit upgrades. QUESTIONS DUE DATE AND TIME Offerors are required to complete all clauses/provisions that require fill-ins and submit them with their quotes or ensure their representations and certifications are up to date in SAM.gov. Questions for solicitation are due by 19 March 2026 at 11:00 AM CDT. OFFER DUE DATE AND TIME Monday, 30 March 2026 at 11:00 AM CDT.

# 2026 Arkansas Regional Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-16 11:34:44.812-04
- Solicitation Number: W9127S

## Summary

**UPDATE: Our Annual Arkansas Regional Industry Day Event has been scheduled for April 21, 2026 through April 22, 2026.** The US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) Little Rock District proudly presents the Arkansas Regional Industry Day. This event will provide industry the priceless opportunity to network with Government personnel as well as other large and small business contractors. Industry Day will also provide an invaluable forum to discuss upcoming business opportunities. Businesses will also have the opportunity to meet one-on-one with various Government agencies and contractor personnel. This event is open to all industries including: • Construction • Architecture & Engineering Survey • Initial Outfitting • Supplies & Services • and more! Government Agencies expected to be invited: USACE: Tulsa District, Kansas City District, Memphis District, Galveston District, Ft Worth District, Vicksburg District, St. Louis District. Registration will open March 16, 2026 at 12:00PM CT. If you have any questions or concerns, please send an email to ceswl-industryday@usace.army.mil. INTERESTED VENDORS: Please contact Lee Powers via email at LPowers@avcor-usa.com for all inquires regarding vendor booths.

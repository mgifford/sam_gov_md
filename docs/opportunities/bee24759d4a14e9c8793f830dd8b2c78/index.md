# Dallas Port-a-Potty Rental for Memorial Day

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-21 13:36:23.1-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C78626Q50169

## Summary

Please refer to attachment for full details. The Contractor shall provide all labor, tools, materials, and equipment necessary to provide (deliver, put in place, service, and remove) portable restrooms at different spots located on: Dallas-Fort Worth National Cemetery, 2000 Mountain Creek Parkway, Dallas, TX 75211. Required items/services: Nine (9) Portable Restrooms (aka: Deluxe Porta Potty with Sink and Flush) with Hand Sanitizer and Toilet Paper. Six (6) ADA Compliant Portable Restrooms (aka: Portable Toilet) with Hand Sanitizer and Toilet Paper. Ten (10) 2-Station Portable Sink, Freestanding Portable restrooms and sinks must be delivered and setup by 3pm on Friday May 22, 2026 and removed by 3pm on Tuesday May 26, 2026. All deliver and removal times should be coordinated with the COR or Cemetery Director to ensure no interruption to cemetery burial operations.

# Travis AFB - Starbucks HVAC Air Handler Replacement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 16:08:29.833-04
- Solicitation Number: FA442726QZ037

## Summary

1. This pre-solicitation notice is in accordance with FAR 5.101(c). This is not a Request for Quotation (RFQ), a Request for Proposal (RFP), or an Invitation for Bid (IFB). The Government is not soliciting quotes or proposals at this time. 2. Description: The 60th Civil Engineer Squadron (CES) at TAFB requires a HVAC Air Handler Replacement. The contractor shall provide all labor, equipment, tools, material, supervision, and any other items necessary to properly remove and replace old Carrier air handler unit with a new AHU unit with estimated 3,500CFM capacity to meet the heat/cool load requirements desired. See Statement of Work for full requirement details. 3. Procedures for Award: The Government intends to issue a Request for Quotation (RFQ) and award a firm-fixed-price Purchase Order. The award basis is anticipated to include Past performance and Price. The Government anticipate to award on or about 02 May 2026. Trade Agreements Language: Not Applicable.

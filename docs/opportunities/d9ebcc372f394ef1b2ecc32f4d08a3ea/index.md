# J046--Humidifier RO System Maintenance and Service

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 07:48:11.271-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24826Q0596
- Awardee: null

## Summary

This Amendment 0001 is issued to cancel Solicitation 36C24826Q0596 in its entirety. The Government has determined that cancellation is necessary due to insufficient information regarding the existing system configuration and site conditions required to support vendor proposal development. Specifically, during the scheduled site visit, the Government was unable to identify and verify the location of the existing Humidifier Reverse Osmosis (RO) Water Treatment System. As a result, the Government is unable to provide adequate responses to vendor questions related to system configuration, equipment details, and operational requirements. Due to these uncertainties, proceeding with the solicitation would not allow for fair and accurate competition. The Government intends to reassess the requirement, locate and verify the existing system, and obtain the necessary technical information. A revised solicitation may be issued at a later date once sufficient details are available. IMPORTANT NOTES TO OFFERORS - This solicitation is hereby canceled, and no award will be made under Solicitation 36C24826Q0596. - Any proposals submitted will not be evaluated. - Offerors are encouraged to monitor SAM.gov for any future re-solicitation of this requirement.

## Summary

The U.S. Government has awarded a succeeding lease using other than full and open competition for an existing lease for the Veterans Heath Administration (VHA) that is expiring in Clifton Park, NY. This procurement is being conducted in accordance with FAR 6.302-1-Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. NAICS Code 531120 applies to this procurement. This notice of award follows the intent to forego requests for competitive quotes. The lease is for 10 years (5 f,irm 5 Soft) from beginning On January 8, 2026 to January 7, 2036. The Clifton Park CBOC is awarded for 4400 rsf at the present location. The awarded amount ofor this lease is $85,057.00 annually.

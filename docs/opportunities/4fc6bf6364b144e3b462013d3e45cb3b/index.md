# Parachute Training Support Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 15:18:07.306-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700126Q0046

## Summary

Synopsis for Parachute Operations Aircraft Services and Support 2d Reconnaissance Battalion has a requirement for Parachute Operations Aircraft Services and Support near Camp Lejeune, NC. It is the Government’s intention to issue a firm fixed price contract. The proposed acquisition will be unrestricted. The associated North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this requirement is 481219 -Other Nonscheduled Air Transportation. Vendors interested in this requirement must be listed on the DoD Paratroop Commercial Operator Listing, managed by Air Mobility Command, Commercial Airlift Division, HQ AMC/A3B. The Government will solicit and award this contract using Simplified Acquisition Procedures in accordance with FAR part 12. Basis for Award: Award will be made using best value: technical capability, price and other factors considered. The Government intends to make an award to the vendor whose proposal represents the best value to the Government based on (1) vendor’s technical capability, (2) price, and (3) Past Performance using the Supplier Performance Risk System (SPRS). Technical capability will be based on the demonstrated ability to meet the technical requirements. The solicitation will contain the complete Performance Work Statement (PWS). The solicitation No. M6700126Q0046 will be available for download on Sam. Gov or about 1 April 2026. It is the responsibility of the quoter to continually view the website for the solicitation and any amendments. Contractors interested in doing business with the government must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at https://www.sam.gov/ and agree to implement the Department of Defense web-based software application Wide Area Workflow Receipt and Acceptance (WAWF-RA) at https://wawf.eb.mil/. All responsible business may submit an offer, which will be considered.

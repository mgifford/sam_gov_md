## Summary

This Contractor is to furnish all labor, supervision, materials, and equipment necessary to accomplish microbial, chemical, and water quality testing of potable water. Service will include testing for legionella, bacteria, disinfection-byproducts, lead & copper analysis, and other water quality testing as required (e.g. PFAS). Water samples will be collected at the George E Wahlen Veterans Medical Center by Contractor staff, in bottles provided by the Contractor, then delivered to the lab for testing by the Contractor.

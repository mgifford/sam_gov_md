# Tulsa Resident Office HVAC Ductwork Repair or Replacement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 09:52:58.709-04
- Solicitation Number: W912BV26QA056

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers Tulsa District has a requirement at the Tulsa Resident Office for the inspection, repair, and/or replacement of the supply and return air HVAC ductwork and associated appurtenances for two HVAC units. The total area of the building is approximately 3250 square feet. The contractor will perform a comprehensive HVAC load analysis, assess existing ductwork, and repair or install new existing ductwork to ensure performance and compliance standards are met. This is a 100% Total Small Business Set Aside. NAICS Code 238220 PSC J041. The full Solicitation document containing the Instructions to Offerors can be found in the PIEE Solicitation Module: https://piee.eb.mil.

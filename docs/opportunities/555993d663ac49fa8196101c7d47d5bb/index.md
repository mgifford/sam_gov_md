- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation

## Summary

US Embassy in Bratislava, Slovakia requests price quotation for the reconstruction of bathroom facility in the U.S. Embassy building in Bratislava to meet the prescribed American Barriers Act (ABA) requirements. The service specifications are provided in the attached document 19L01026Q0002 and in Attachments 2 and 3. To meet solicitation requirements, interested companies must be registered in Slovak Business Registry.

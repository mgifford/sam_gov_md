# PMW 120 Industry Day Announcement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:58:29.677-04
- Solicitation Number: NAVWAR-PMW120-INDUSTRYDAY-26

## Summary

UPDATE: 12 March 2026 - Event Dates Day 1 - Unclassified - 29 April 2026. Day 2 - Classified 30 April 2026. Final details will be sent to recipients that submitted a request to attend prior to the cutoff. UPDATE 10 February 2026 - See new Question and Answer attachment. The Program Executive Office, Command, Control, Communications, Computers and Intelligence (PEO C4I) Battlespace Awareness and Information Operations Program Office (PMW 120) plans to host an Industry Day in April 2026 in San Diego, CA. This event will provide industry partners with insight into the PMW 120 mission, an overview of its programs, capabilities, and opportunity areas for Industry involvement. The goal of this event is to create meaningful engagement between the PMW 120 program office representatives and prospective industry partners through targeted briefing and direct networking sessions. See attachment for more details.

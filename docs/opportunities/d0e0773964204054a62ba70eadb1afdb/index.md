# Logistical Support Services for the U.S. Mission in Paris – France

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 12:25:36.368-04
- Solicitation Number: 19FR6326Q0063

## Summary

The Embassy of the United States in Paris, France, will release a solicitation for logistical support services. Please note this solicitation is not ready to be issued as of this date. The Embassy of the United States in Paris, France will launch this solicitation on the SAM.gov website when it is available. The resultant contract will be IDIQ (indefinite delivery indefinite quantity) contract, to be potentially awarded to multiple companies, under which will be issued task orders. The contract will be for a base year with four one-year options if exercised by the Government. All contractors have to be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to submitting an offer pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. Therefore, prospective offerors are encouraged to register prior to the submittal of quotations/proposals. The guidelines for registration in SAM are available at: https://www.sam.gov Electronic Submissions/Responses: This opportunity allows for electronic responses. Please submit your offer to the ParisContracting@state.gov address prior to the response date and time.

# 2026 Arkansas Regional Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-16 12:01:59.306-04
- Solicitation Number: W9127S

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) - Little Rock District is hosting its annual Industry Day event on April 21-22, 2026 in the Magnolia Ballroom at the Hilton Garden Inn West located at 10914 Kanis Road, Little Rock, Arkansas. This two-day event will provide our industry partners the opportunity to network with several government agencies, as well as other large and small business contractors. This event consists of a forum to address upcoming business opportunities and will also provide training and panel discussions on important contracting topics. Registered participants will have the opportunity to meet one-on-one with representatives from the Little Rock District, Memphis District, Little Rock Air Force Base and Small Business Administration. This event is open to all industries including: Construction, Architecture & Engineering Survey, Initial Outfitting, Supplies & Services, and other contracting opportunities. This event is open to all industries including: • Construction • Architecture & Engineering Survey • Initial Outfitting • Supplies & Services • and more! The agenda for this event is forthcoming. Registration will open March 16, 2026 at 12:00PM CT. If you have any questions or concerns, please send an email to ceswl-industryday@usace.army.mil.

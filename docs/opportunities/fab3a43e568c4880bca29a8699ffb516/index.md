## Summary

Please also change the description box to say: An unrestricted, firm-fixed price (FFP), standalone contract to provide preventive and corrective maintenance in support of the Defense Logistics Agency - Energy (DLA-E) within the Pacific Ocean Division- Far East District (POF). Specifically, work will be performed in South Korea. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this acquisition is 561210 Facilities Support Services with a small business size standard of $47M. The applicable Product Service Code (PSC) is ZINA Support - Maintenance of Fuel Supply Facilities. The contract performance period is 3 years (one 12-month base and two 12-month option) and Revolutionary Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Overhaul (RFO) 52.217-8, Options to Extend Services. The contractor shall deliver comprehensive maintenance services, ensuring the continued operational readiness of vital petroleum facilities and systems.

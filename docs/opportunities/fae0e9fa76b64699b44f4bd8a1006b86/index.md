## Summary

NSN 5340-014538395, Bracket Mounting. This material is being procured in accordance with IAW Basic Drawing NR 53711 7016077. The required quantity is 8 EA with a 100% quantity option to be shipped to DODAAC SW3117 DLA Distribution Center with a required delivery of 315 days. FOB Origin (First Destination Transportation applies) and Inspection and Acceptance at Destination are required. This solicitation is set-aside for small business. The material is not commercial; therefore the Government is not using the policies contained in FAR Part 12 in its solicitation for this item. However, interested suppliers may identify to the Contracting Officer their interest and capability to satisfy the Government's requirement with the commercial item within 15 days of this notice. This solicitation will be available on the Internet at https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil// after the issue date of 03/26/2026. Hard copies of the solicitation are not available.

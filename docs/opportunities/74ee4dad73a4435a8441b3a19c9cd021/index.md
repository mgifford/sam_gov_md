# Request for Quotes (RFQ) to upgrade security lights at U.S. Government residence 10/316.

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-10 05:52:20.793-04
- Solicitation Number: PR15755127

## Summary

The proposals from General Contractors to upgrade security lights at U.S. Government residence 10/316 includes: Supply and installation of double tube weatherproof light fittings with 2 tube lights on each at 3 meters heights (Phillips, FSL or radiant brand). Supplying and installation of 2.5 mm by 3 core armored cables (about 50 meter) as well as associated accessories (watertight junction boxes, compression glands, flex cables, wire nuts, etc.) to tap power from the nearest light pole to the nearest light point. Supplying and installation of 250 meters by 2.5 mm single core cables (RGB) and associated accessories (20 mm PVC pipes, copulins, nipples, round boxes, round covers). Wall chasing works all along the 250 meters stretch. Masonry works, gardening works and painting for restoration of affected walls and the lawn. Trenching will be done to 70 cm deep and a caution tape will be laid 10 cm above the cable. Work shall be carried out in conformance with this Statement of Work (SOW) and Specifications, General Conditions, and the Contract Terms and Conditions. If there is a conflict between the documents, the Contractor shall follow the more stringent requirement.

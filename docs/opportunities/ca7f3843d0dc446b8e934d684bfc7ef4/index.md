# Valve, Alarm, Automatic Sprinkler

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-17 12:43:30.601-04
- Solicitation Number: NSN4210005759297

## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice only. This is NOT a Request for Proposal, Request for Quote or invitation for Bid. DLA Troop Support is conducting market research seeking to identify possible manufactures that can supply items similar to form, fit, and function of the Valve, Alarm, Automatic Sprinkler NSN 4210-00-575-9297. Interested contractors are requested to review the item description carefully to identify any protentional manufacturing challenges and formulate any questions. The Government does not have in its possession the technical data for this item. The intent of this Sources Sought Notice is to gather information that will identify sources for the possibility of a solicitation for the referenced item under a Firm-Fixed Price Contract. Interested vendors should submit a complete data package for the item on or before May 18, 2026, to Damian Panto, DLA Troop Support – Construction & Equipment; damian.panto@dla.mil. This Sources Sought Notice is issued for the purpose of Market Research only and the Government is in no way obligated to purchase any material as a result of this inquiry. The Government will NOT pay for any information submitted by respondents in response to this Sources Sought Notice. If a solicitation or solicitations would result, it/they would be synopsized on the Contract Opportunities website, and it would be the offeror's responsibility to monitor the Contract Opportunities website for any such notices.

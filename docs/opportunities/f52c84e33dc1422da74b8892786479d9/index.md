# Facility Environmental Compliance, Planning, and Resiliency Technical support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-20 13:31:23.297-04

## Summary

The Facility Environmental Compliance, Planning, and Resiliency Technical support services assists in meeting FSD’s mission to ensure the 24/7/365 readiness of the Pentagon Reservation for DoW and National Leadership to execute their mission responsibilities, and provide the DoW workforce, predominantly in the National Capital Region (NCR) at supported locations, safe, secure, healthy, resilient, energy efficient and operationally effective working environments. Support efforts include multimedia environmental compliance, resiliency, natural resources, incident response and recovery, planning, and communications.

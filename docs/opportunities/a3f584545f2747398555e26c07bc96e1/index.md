# Asphalt Paver Rental for Crestwood Annual Training

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 10:07:42.125-04
- Solicitation Number: W91SMC26QA005

## Summary

The 2118th Asphalt Detachment requires the short-term rental of one (1) commercial-grade crawler asphalt paver to complete the paving of a Physical Training Track during Annual Training at the Crestwood Armory in Robbins, IL. This is a “dry lease” requirement where the Government will provide the operator and fuel. Solicitation has been ammended to update the PoP on page 7 to 8-12 June and to update the model of the Leeboy paver to 8608 that was on page 4. The ammendment has been attached as well as the summary of changes. Also uploaded questions and answeres.

# SENSEI (Sensing for Interdiction) Electro-Optic Infrared System Technical Refresh For Counter Unmanded Arriel Systems (C-UAS)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-23 13:40:05.516-04
- Solicitation Number: N0016426SNB68

## Summary

PURPOSE This special notice is to inform vendors of a Request for Information (RFI). The purpose of this RFI is to collect information from the general public about SENSEI topics in the RFI. Based off the information collected from the RFI, the Government may send out a Request for Solutions (RFS) with the intent of awarding a Project Order under the Expeditionary Missions Consortium (EMC2) Other Transaction Agreement (OTA) in accordance with 10 U.S.C. § 4022. BACKGROUND This Request for Information (RFI) is issued by the Expeditionary Missions Consortium – Crane (EMC²) in support of the Naval Surface Warfare Center for the specific SENSEI topic. The Topic Sponsor desires to collaborate with EMC² Members and industry to discuss the topic and possibly shape future EMC² Request for Solutions (RFS). All interested parties need to become members of EMC2 to see the RFI and be able to receive the necessary documents that go along with it. Some of the documents are Distro C and D and will need to be requested to fill out your submission. All interested parties should also visit the EMC2 website at https://emccrane.org/ to become a member of the consortium to view the future possible RFS.

## Summary

This Request for Information (RFI) is not a Request for Proposal (RFP), Invitation for Bid, or a solicitation announcement. The Air Force District of Washington, Contracting Directorate (AFDW/PK), Joint Base Andrews, Maryland, is conducting market research to identify potential sources capable of providing Space Integrated Modeling, Simulation, & Analysis (SIMSA) Follow-on services for the United States Space Force (USSF) and its mission partners. Please review the RFI_SBIR III SIMSA Follow-on FA701426Q0019, Performance Work Statement, along with the SBIR Phase III RFI Background Information and Vendor Questions document for additional details regarding the requirement. Responses are required for the following: Section D of the RFI_SBIR III SIMSA Follow-on FA701426Q0019 All questions included in the SBIR Phase III RFI Background Info and Vendor Questions document

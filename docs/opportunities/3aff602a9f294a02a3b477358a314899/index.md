# Course Development and Delivery Services?

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-25 12:39:29.788-04
- Solicitation Number: FA300226R0007

## Summary

The 338th Enterprise Sourcing Squadron (ESS), at Joint Base San Antonio (JBSA) ? Randolph, Texas (TX), is planning to issue a solicitation for nonpersonal services on a Multiple Award Contract (MAC) vehicle providing comprehensive Course Development and Delivery Services with Professional Support Services to meet current and emergent course development and delivery and mission requirements. The proposed solicitation identifier is FA300226R0007. Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts will be awarded providing a suite of contractors for TO competitions to meet Air Force needs within the Continental United States (CONUS), Alaska and Hawaii.The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code 611710 - Educational Support Services, with a small business size standard of $24 million, Service Code: U009, Education/Training- General. The period of performance will consist of one (1) five-year basic ordering period, and 2-year ordering period option; a total ordering period of seven (7) years. The proposed solicitation will be a competitive Woman Owned Small Business (WOSB) set-aside. The above description of work is for the purpose of general information about the solicitation and consolidation efforts only and is not intended to encompass the entire scope of work. THIS IS A SYNOPSIS AND CONSOLIDATION NOTICE. THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION. It is anticipated that the solicitation will be made available electronically only via SAM.gov and the PIEE Solicitation module as soon as practicable. Offerors are responsible for routinely checking SAM.gov website and the PIEE Solicitation module for any possible solicitation amendments that may be issued. This notice does not commit the Government to award a contract. Points of contacts: Mr. Robert Widdoss, Contracting Officer at robert.widdoss.1@us.af.mil and Ms. Audra Cardona, Contract Officer at audra.cardona@us.af.mil.

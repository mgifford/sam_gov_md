# Department of Commerce Strategic Sourcing Initiative - Operational and Program Support Services

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 15:58:46.768-04
- Solicitation Number: 11242024

## Summary

The U.S. Department of Commerce (DOC), Office of Acquisition Management, Shared Services Procurement Office (SSPO), provides the following update regarding the Operational Program Support (OPS) requirement previously announced under RFI-11242024 on SAM.gov. The Government has revised its acquisition strategy and will no longer pursue this requirement as an open market procurement. The OPS requirement is now being competed exclusively among eligible contractors under the General Services Administration (GSA) One Acquisition Solution for Integrated Services Plus (OASIS+) Governmentwide Acquisition Contract (GWAC) in accordance with FAR 16.505 ordering procedures. Phase I (Opt-In Eligibility Confirmation) has been issued to eligible OASIS+ SDVOSB contract holders within the applicable domain through the OASIS+ ordering platform. This notice is provided for informational purposes only. The Government is not seeking responses, capability statements, or questions regarding this update.

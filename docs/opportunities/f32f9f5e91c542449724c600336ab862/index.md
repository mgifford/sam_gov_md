## Summary

SPECIAL NOTICE: Department of Veterans Affairs (VA), John D. Dingell VA Medical Center, 4646 John R. St., Detroit, Michigan 48201, is soliciting and intends to award a firm fixed price contract for DEMCA FY26 Membership Dues. NAICS Code 541990, size standard $19.5 Million. The Government intends, in accordance with FAR 6.302.1 (only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements), to negotiate on a sole source basis with Detroit East Medical Contract Authority (DEMCA), Inc. (10200 Erwin St., Detroit, MI 48201), for this procurement due to the specialized nature of this procurement. The Contractor shall provide all parts, labor, equipment, materials, licensure, supervision, necessary to service the requirement located at the John D. Dingell VA Medical Center, Detroit, Michigan, for the period of May 09, 2026 May 8, 2027, in strict accordance with the statement of work, all schedules, and terms and conditions of the resultant contract. No solicitation package is available. A determination not to compete is solely within the discretion of the Government. Any response to this notice must show clear and convincing evidence that competition would be advantageous to the Government. Responses must be received NLT 12:00pm Eastern on 3/25/26. All responses must be emailed to Alexis.Alexander1@va.gov.

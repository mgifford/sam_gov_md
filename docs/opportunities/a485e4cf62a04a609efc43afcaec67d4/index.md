# FCC HAZELTON INMATE BOOT ORDER

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 13:51:29.331-05
- Solicitation Number: 15B12126Q00000015

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Prisons, Federal Correctional Complex Hazelton, West Virginia, intends to issue a solicitation for the requirements of: INMATE BOOTS as prescribed in FAR subpart 12.6 under NAICS Code 316210. This solicitation is restricted to 100% Small Business, FOB Destination, delivery date is estimated to be 14 days ARO. Deliveries will be made to the Federal Correctional Complex Warehouse, 1640 Skyview Drive, Bruceton Mills, WV 26525. The contract type is a firm-fixed price. The Government reserves the right upon award to make formal single, multiple awards or no awards. The Government will award to the responsible offeror(s) whose offer, conforming to the solicitation, which is considered to be most advantageous to the Government, with past performance, price and delivery time frame considered. Evaluation factors as prescribed by 52.212-2 will be applied for determination of winning bid. The solicitation will be made available on or about March 6, 2026, and will be posted to the General Services Administration’s Federal Business Opportunities website: https://sam.gov/. All future information about this acquisition, including solicitation amendments, will also be distributed through this site. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring this site to ensure they have the most up-to-date information about this solicitation. The anticipated date for receipt of quotes is March 17, 2026, at 12:00 pm. EST. A signed SF 1449, including vendor information, must be submitted to be considered a viable quote. Payment will be made using Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) or Government Purchase Card depending on dollar amount of individually awarded orders. Each offer is required per 52.212-3 to complete their representations and certifications via the Online Representations and Certifications Application (ORCA). The point of contact regarding this solicitation is Charlie Hall, Contract Specialist, FCC Hazelton, 1640 Skyview Drive, Bruceton Mills, West Virginia 26525. E-mail contact: Charlie cehall@bop.gov. Completed offers must be delivered via email to HAX-BusinessOfffice-S@bop.gov. (backup email for bids is cehall@bop.gov . A confirmation email will be sent when bids are received. Fax or mailed quotes will not be accepted. The anticipated award date is March 20, 2026 (subject to change).

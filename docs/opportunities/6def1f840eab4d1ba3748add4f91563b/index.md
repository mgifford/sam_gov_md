# Notice of Intent to Sole Source - Dillard Engines Service

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 17:24:06.255-04
- Solicitation Number: W912P726QA004

## Summary

The proposed contract action is for a maintenance/service on both Port and Starboard CAT ACERT C032 Marine Engines with a certified Caterpillar marine technician/mechanic. All replacement parts, fluids, etc. shall be manufacturer recommended or marine grade equivalent. The US Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), San Francisco District intends to solicit with Peterson Power Systems, Inc. under the authority of FAR 12.102(a). Interested persons may identify their interest and capability to respond to these requirements. This notice of intent is not a request for quotes; however, all information received after date of publication of this synopsis may be considered by the Government. If interested, please provide the following information: Organization Name; Address; Point of Contract for information with telephone number and email address; Cage Code; and Capabilities information/statement showing the organization has the ability and jurisdiction to fulfill the Government requirement. The contractor must be able to comply with all requirements described herein to be considering capable. Send the required information to Logan Champlin (logan.champlin@usace.army.mil) and Nairi Freeman (nairi.freeman@usace.army.mil). Description of Services: See attached Scope of Work.

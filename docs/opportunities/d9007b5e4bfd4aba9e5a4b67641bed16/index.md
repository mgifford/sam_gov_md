# C-Band Doppler Radar Life Cycle Replacement Parts

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-22 17:07:04.675-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S626CBAND

## Summary

THIS IS A Sources Sought Notice ONLY. The U.S. Government desires the procurement of proprietary C-Band Doppler Radar components from Enterprise Electronics Corporation (EEC), the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM), for life cycle replacement. The Mission Installation Contracting Command (MICC) Dugway Proving Ground (DPG) currently intends to award a contract on a sole source basis, but is seeking vendors that may be able to supply the required components to support a competitive procurement. We encourage all small businesses, in all socioeconomic categories (including 8(a) Business Development Program, Small Disadvantage Business, Historically Underutilized Business Zone, Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned, Women-Owned Small Business concerns), to identify their capabilities in meeting the requirements at a fair market price. See Sources Sought Notice in Full Text (attached) for details and equipment list.

Notice to all interested parties: The site visit is rescheduled for 28 January 2026 at 1:00 P.M. Eastern Time. Visitor request forms are due no later than 5:00 P.M. Eastern Time 22 January 2026. See Page 96, Section L #11 of the attached RFP for specific details. This is a 100% 8(a) small business set-aside to establish a multiple-award indefinite delivery indefinite quantity (IDIQ) firm-fixed price construction contract. The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, quality control and other items and non-personal services necessary to perform facilities alteration, repair, maintenance, & minor construction of infrastructure, facilities, and interior/exterior utility systems under a multiple award task order contract (MATOC). Requirements under this contract will include alteration, repair, maintenance, minor construction, demolition of interior and exterior real property, utility systems, grounds improvements, pavements, installation of new equipment, and any other work covered by the Unified Facilities Guide Specifications (UFGS), and the design and engineering work required of such requirements for the Letterkenny Army Depot (LEAD) and any of its tenant organizations or customers. This contract shall not be used for construction work that meets the threshold or requirement for Military Construction (MILCON) appropriations. See the attached RFP and Attachments.

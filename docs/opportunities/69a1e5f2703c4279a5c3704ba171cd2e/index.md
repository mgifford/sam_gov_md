# Mobile Tactical Cannon (MTC) Resupply Capability - Call for Solution

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 07:07:08.402-04
- Solicitation Number: MOSAIC-26-01

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command – Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA), is seeking an innovative, flexible, and efficient Resupply Capability (RC) to transport and deliver 155mm artillery ammunition to the new Mobile Tactical Cannon (MTC). In accordance with Open Solicitation Procedures detailed within Solicitation W50RAJ-26-S-0001, the Government intends to award one or more Other Transaction (OT) for Prototype Project pursuant to 10 USC 4022 (formerly 10 USC 2371b). The Government further reserves the right to award to all, part, or none of the companies who submit proposals. This Call for Solution (CFS) will utilize CSO procedures in accordance with 10 U.S.C. 3458. The Call for Solution document, outlining the full problem statement, required solution attributes, submission requirements, and evaluation phases can be found in the "Attachments" section below. The deadline to submit questions for Phase 1 is April 28, 2026 at 3:00PM ET. The following shall be utilized in the email subject line: " Call for Solution – MTC Resupply Capability Solutions 26-01" The Munitions Open Solicitation for Advanced and Innovative Capabilities (MOSAIC), Notice ID W50RAJ-26-S-0001, that this Call for Solution is associated with can be found at: https://sam.gov/workspace/contract/opp/f64ed1039050411cb59512ca84ca18c2/view Please review the MOSAIC posting and associated attachments, in addition to the attachment to this call, for further information on how MOSAIC is structured.

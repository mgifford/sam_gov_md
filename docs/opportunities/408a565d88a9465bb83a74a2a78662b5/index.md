# Demolition Services B15 and B54

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 13:03:36.558-04
- Solicitation Number: FA309926Q0008

## Summary

The 47th Contracting Squadron at Laughlin Air Force Base, TX, has a requirement for the demolition of two (2) buildings: Building 15 and Building 54. The work includes, but is not limited to, the complete demolition of the structures, proper disconnection and capping of all associated utilities, removal and disposal of all debris, and complete restoration of the sites. Site restoration shall include backfilling, grading, seeding, and installation of a new security fence. This project is in close proximity to an active airfield, and contractors will be required to adhere to strict safety and security protocols. This is a commercial acquisition conducted under FAR Part 12. A pre-award site visit is scheduled. Please see the solicitation for complete details, submission instructions, and all applicable dates. Site Visit: 22 April 2026, 09:00 a.m. CDT; See Schedule for Details See Schedule for All Details.

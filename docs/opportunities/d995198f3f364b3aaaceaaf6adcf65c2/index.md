## Summary

Title: DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap – Term Contract (National Capital Region) Notice Type: Solicitation Solicitation Number: 2-1-QSC-T-26-059 Agency / Office: General Services Administration (GSA) Federal Acquisition Service (FAS) Personal Property Management IV Description The U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) is issuing this Invitation for Bids (IFB) for the sale of DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap generated by multiple Federal agencies located throughout the National Capital Region (NCR), including the District of Columbia, Maryland, and Virginia. This solicitation establishes a term sales contract under which the successful bidder (Buyer) shall purchase scrap material and perform removal as a condition of sale. Scrap material includes, but is not limited to: Steel Aluminum Copper / Brass Wire Other ferrous and non-ferrous metals Electronic waste (E-waste), ADP equipment, and data-bearing devices are excluded from this contract. All property is sold strictly for recycling as material content only in accordance with 41 CFR 102-38. Resale as usable property is prohibited. Contract Structure Base Period: One (1) Year Option Periods: Two (2) One-Year Options Maximum Contract Term: Three (3) Years Extension: Up to six (6) months at Government discretion Pricing Structure This is a removal-based sales contract. Bidders shall submit firm-fixed unit prices for defined removal event types. Pricing shall not be based on weight, market indices, or material composition. Mandatory Pre-Bid Inspection Pre-bid inspection is mandatory. All bidders must physically attend the: GSA Personal Property Center (PPC) 7942 Angus Court Springfield, VA 22150 Bidders must obtain verification of attendance on Appendix A – Site Verification & Bidder Certification Form. Failure to attend and obtain required verification will result in the bid being determined non-responsive. Inspection scheduling shall be coordinated in accordance with the Site Access and Inspection Procedures document located in the Documents section. Participating Agency Locations General Services Administration (GSA) – Mandatory Inspection Location GSA Personal Property Center / Surplus Sales Center 7942 Angus Court Springfield, VA 22150 U.S. Department of State (DOS) 2201 C Street NW Washington, DC 20520 Social Security Administration (SSA) 6401 Security Blvd Baltimore, MD 21235 U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) 6500 Sheriff Road Landover, MD 20785 Important: All bidders are responsible for reviewing the IFB and all associated documents for complete requirements, site coordination procedures, and points of contact.

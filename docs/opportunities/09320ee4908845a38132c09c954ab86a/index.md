# Motorola Solution Accessories

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-08 12:04:21.953-04
- Solicitation Number: N6471026Q0005

## Summary

STATEMENT OF WORK Motorola Solutions Radio Equipment and Accessories Requisition: N647106007H422 Ship/Activity: Strategic Weapons Facility, Pacific (SWFPAC)

1.0 Background:SWFPAC requires the procurement of Motorola Solutions Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) radio equipment and accessories that are fully compatible with the existing radio system deployed across the installation. This equipment is replacing end-of- life communications security equipment currently in use at SWFPAC. All equipment and accessories must integrate seamlessly with the current equipment and configured to ensure reliable, uninterrupted communication for security personnel supporting Strategic Weapons Security operations.

2.0 Scope: The Motorola Solutions accessories are required for extended security operations at SWFPAC for the protection of Strategic assets. The radios currently deployed across Strategic Weapons Security operations require Motorola Solutions OEM product accessories to ensure proper fit, power regulation, firmware compatibility, and secure communication functionality. All radio equipment and accessories shall be new, non-refurbished, produces and sourced through an authorized Motorola Solutions distributor.

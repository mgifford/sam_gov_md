# PNA Network Analyzer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 08:59:16.655-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700426P0011

## Summary

The Marine Corps Logistics Command (MARCORLOGCOM) Contracts Department, Albany, GA intends to solicit and contract with Keysight Technologies Inc (CAGE 7ZXR5) of Colorado Springs, Colorado on a Sole-Source basis for the purchase of the PNA Network Analyzer for Marine Depot Maintenance Command. The North American Industry Classification Standard (NAICS) code assigned is 334515 - Instrument Manufacturing for Measuring and Testing Electricity and Electrical Signals. No solicitation will be posted on this website for this requirement. A Justification and Approval Document is attached to this notice. Any potential source may contact the individual indicated within this notice to discuss their ability to offer the required services for future acquisitions.

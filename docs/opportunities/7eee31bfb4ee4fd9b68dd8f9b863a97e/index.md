# USSPACECOM REDCON Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-10 18:16:01.547-04
- Solicitation Number: FA251826CA001

## Summary

Contractor support is required to provide technical, engineering, and executive staff support to the US Space Command (USSPACECOM) Program Management Office (PMO). The PMO manages the design, construction, and mission equipment integration for the USSPACECOM Command & Control Facility (C2F) design, construction, and transition-to-occupancy. The scope of design and construction includes integration of all USSPACECOM requirements, including information technology (IT) and security. Project execution includes the IT and security components of design and construction, as well. As the USSPACECOM C2F Requirements Owner, the PMO requires this support to assist in establishing technical and engineering expertise to successfully accomplish assigned tasks.

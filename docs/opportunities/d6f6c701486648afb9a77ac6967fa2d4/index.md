## Summary

This action is to establish a contract for the acquisition of the UH-1N Helicopter-Starter Generator, NSN: 2925-00-465-6344GA, Part Number 23046-020. The DLA/AWB Contracting Office intends to award a single definitive contract for the acquisition of the UH-1N Helicopter-Starter Generator. This sole source effort will be IAW FAR 6.302-1, Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements, under which the Government intends to solicit and negotiate only with Safran Power USA, LLC, Cage (3CPE0). The Government does not own the rights to the data for the manufacturing of this item. Requirements: NSN:2925-00-465-6344GA PN: 23046-020 Noun: Starter Generator Quanity: 44EA Required Delivery: ARO 90-Days Request for Quote SPRWA1-26-Q-0010 will be posted for 45 days. IAW FAR 5.207(c)(16)(ii), all responsible sources may submit a bid, proposal or quotation which shall be considered by the agency.

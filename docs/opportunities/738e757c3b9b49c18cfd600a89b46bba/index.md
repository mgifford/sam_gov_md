# USDA Forest Service LMIR BPA Call for Lolo NF Stocking Surveys AMD 0001

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-10 17:30:55.724-04
- Solicitation Number: 1284LM26Q0035

## Summary

This notice is for a BPA Call Order to be issued against an existing Land Management Integrated Resources (LMIR) Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA). To respond to this solicitation, contractors must have an existing LMIR BPA that includes Stocking Surveys within Lolo National Forest, Missoula and Powell Counties, Montana. Please see the attached BPA Call Order Form for the schedule of items and submittal instructions. Please send any questions to joanne.meiers@usda.gov no later than 3/11/2026, COB. NOTE: Original Documents pertaining to the LMIR National Blanket Purchase Agreement can be found in the attachments located at: https://sam.gov/opp/8f26188b0e7445b3b4f9bb71484448f4/view. There is an open and continuous BPA with a ceiling amount of $50 million per contractor that allows USDA FS to accept new responses for the life of the BPA (LMIR BPA on SAM.gov). The LMIR BPA has a continuous pre-solicitation sharing that allows contractors to join the BPA at any point in time while the BPA is active. If your firm would like to join, please contact the LMIR BPA Team at SM.FS.lmirbpa@usda.gov Amendment 1 corrects line item 2a estimated quantity from 414 to 207 plots. BPA contractors must sign and return the SF-30 with their quote to acknowledge the amendment.

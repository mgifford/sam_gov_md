# Elevator Repair Building 129

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 08:39:27.552-04
- Solicitation Number: FA5606ElevatorRepair

## Summary

This requirement is for the repair of an elevator on Spangdahlem Air Base at Building 129. The intention is to award to the vendor who is deemed responsible, responsive, and represents the best value to the Government based on selection of the lowest total price that is deemed technically acceptable. In accordance with FAR 12.201-1 (c) (2) Contracting Officer has chosen to solicit quotations directly from suppliers. When soliciting directly- (i) Post a presolicitation notice in the GPE according to subpart 5.1, unless an exception in 5.101(b) applies. (ii) Solicit quotations from at least three sources; and (iii) Whenever practicable, solicit quotations from two sources not included in the previous solicitation. In case you are interested in receiving the solicitation please feel free to contact the Primary or the Secondary Point of Contact.

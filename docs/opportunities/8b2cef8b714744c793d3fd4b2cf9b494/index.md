# J046--Intent to Sole Source: Repair and Maintenance for Phigenics Water Quality Monitoring System at Bedford VA Medical Center

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-18 09:51:42.641-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24126Q0347
- Awardee: null

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs,Veterans Health Administration (VHA)Contracting Office Intends to issue a Sole Source Award to Small Business Phigenics to provide Repair and Maintenance Service for a Phigenics Water Monitoring System at the Bedford VA Medical Center. The intent of this procurement is to award based on a Sole Source requirement. This purchase will be made under simplified acquisition procedures as authorized by FAR13-501(a). This notice is neither a request for quote nor a solicitation of offers. All responsible sources may submit a capabilities statement for possible consideration in future procurements by the government. Submit capabilities statements to Yolanda.rankin@va.gov no later than 12:00pm 25 March 2026.

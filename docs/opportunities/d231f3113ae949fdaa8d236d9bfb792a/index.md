## Summary

Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0172 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for reagents compatible with the Rhapsody high-throughput (HT) microwell system. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 325413. The small business size standard is 1250. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

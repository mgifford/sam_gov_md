# 7 Separate Sites Phone Services

- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 15:24:26.302-04
- Solicitation Number: N0018926RL014

## Summary

COMBINED SYNOPSIS/SOLICITATION The Naval Supply Systems Command (NAVSUP) Fleet Logistics Center (FLC) Norfolk Contracting Department, Norfolk, VA 23511-3998, intends to award up to seven firm fixed IDIQ contract for commercially available integrated voice services via Central Office based, dial tone facilities to defense activities., using the policies and procedures of FAR Part 15 and FAR 16.505. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Subpart 12.6, Streamlined Procedures for Evaluation and Solicitation of Commercial Items, with additional information included in this notice. This requirement will be solicited as full and open competition. The NAICS code applicable to this procurement is 517111, Wired Telecommunication Carriers and the size standard is 1,500. BACKGROUND: NCTAMS LANT provides critical base level communications to base users and supported commands and is integral to the mission for all users onboard Naval Air Station (NAS) Key West, Naval Station (NAVSTA) Great Lakes, NAVSTA Norfolk, Joint Expeditionary Base (JEB) Little Creek, Naval Support Activity (NSA) Mechanicsburg, NSA Philadelphia, and Philadelphia Navy Yard (PNYA) Philadelphia by providing critical commercial telephone services to all users and supported commands. Those commands have many critical National Defense missions to support operational and readiness requirements for Department of War (DoW), Department of Homeland Security (DHS), National Guard units, federal agencies and allied forces. Please read the attached Solicitation for additional information. The solicitation, any documents related to this procurement, and all amendments issued will be in electronic form. The solicitation is expected to be available on or about 23 April 2026 on Sam.gov. The SAM.GOV website has a “submit bid” button next to the solicitation number. DO NOT use the “submit bid” button on SAM.GOV. Please submit all proposals and Questions to james.p.keegan7.civ@us.navy.mil and samantha.a.ballance.civ@us.navy.mil. QUESTIONS: Vendors may submit questions regarding clarification of solicitation requirements via email to james.p.keegan7.civ@us.navy.mil and Samantha Ballance at samantha.a.ballance.civ@us.navy.mil.

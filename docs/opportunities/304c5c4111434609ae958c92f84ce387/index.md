# Umatilla National Forest - North Zone Spring Tree Planting

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 19:01:27.028-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240BE26Q0025

## Summary

This contract solicitation is to secure services for coniferous tree planting of Government furnished tree seedlings by hand tool (hoedad) method on 283 acres of National Forest Lands. Conifer tree species to be planted include Western Larch (LAOC) and Ponderosa Pine (PIPO). There is only one stock type that will be planted under this contract; 160-plugs. Planting will occur across an elevational gradient of approximately 1,000ft – 1,500ft. Amendment 001 changes the project period of performance to May 4, 2026 to May 22, 2026. Amendment 002 adds Elbow units. All documents are revised and posted.

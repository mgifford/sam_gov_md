# Screws and Nuts

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 13:46:58.161-04
- Solicitation Number: 26-65-1095

## Summary

DLA Aviation Richmond intends to issue a solicitation for Screws and nuts Parts with contract completion required in 90 days ARO. Complete technical data package DLA-A-VICA 26-65-1095 including Screws and Nuts per spreadsheet and Shipping will be included as a part of the solicitation. Request for Proposal SPE4A8-26-Q-0031 is anticipated to issue on April 13, 2026, and close on May 13, 2026 FOB will be at Destination/ Inspection and Acceptance at Destination. The solicitation will be 100% small business set aside. Award will be the result in a Firm, Fixed Price Contract. Please mail the solicitation package along with the compliance statement and descriptive literature. This solicitation and award will be made under FAR 13.5 procedures. A copy of the solicitation will be available via the DLA DIBBS website at https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil/Solicitations/ Solicitations are in Portable Document Format (PDF). To download and view these documents, use the latest version of Adobe Acrobat Reader. This software is available free at http://www.adobe.com.

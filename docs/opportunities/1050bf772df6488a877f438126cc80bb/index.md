## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Network Contracting Office 16, plans to request proposals for Transplant Reference Laboratory Testing for the Michael E. DeBakey Medical Center in Houston, TX (MEDVAMC). The solicitation being considered is a firm-fixed-price IDIQ contract with a five-year ordering period. The relevant NAICS code is 621511, Medical Laboratories. This solicitation will be issued on a full and open basis, with an anticipated release date of around April 15, 2026. The contractor will be responsible for providing all necessary personnel, equipment, supplies, tools, materials, and supervision to perform histocompatibility tests and other transplant-related immune evaluation tests.

# Greenhill Software Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-07 13:25:05.116-04
- Solicitation Number: F2MTN76083AW07

## Summary

This is a NOTICE OF PROPOSED CONTRACT ACTION (NOPCA) / SOURCES SOUGHT SYNOPSIS. This is NOT a solicitation. The Air Force Life Cycle Management Center, Cryptologic and Cyber Systems Division Contracting Branch, (AFLCMC/HNCK), Joint Base San Antonio Lackland intends to award, on a sole source basis, a Fixed Price Contract for the following technical support services: Annual Technical Support for Green Hills Professional Integrated Development Environment Windows hosted network floating license; PoP: 1 Jun 2026 through 31 May 2027 Annual Technical Support for Green Hills for Professional Bundle 1 product line license; PoP: 1 Jun 2026 through 31 May 2027 The source of this technical support is Green Hills Software, Inc., CAGE: 04LG3. Detailed technical data for the Annual Technical Support for Green Hills Professional Integrated Development Environment Windows hosted network floating and Professional Bundle 1 product line licenses are not available from the Government. The proposed contract action is for services for which the Government intends to solicit and negotiate with only one source: In Accordance With (IAW) 10 U.S.C. 2304(c)(1), as implemented by FAR 6.103-1, only one responsible source. The Government intends to make an award by the end of May 2026. If a firm believes it is capable of meeting the Government’s requirement, it may identify its interest and capability to the Contract Officer, Rachel Sexton, rachel.sexton@us.af.mil and Contract Specialist, Victor Quiroz, victor.quiroz@us.af.mil within five (7) business days of this publication (by 3:00 PM CST). Any interested source is invited to submit a written qualification package for review and approval to include documented approval for performing these services from Green Hills Software Inc. The written qualification package must include the following information: 1) Contractor name and phone number 2) A brief statement detailing capabilities to perform these services 3) A list of names and phone numbers for which you performed these services 4) Type of business, and whether they are large, small, any socio-economic classification, and whether they are U.S. or foreign owned All data received in response to this announcement marked or designated as corporate or proprietary information will be fully protected from release outside the Government. The Government will not pay for any materials provided in response to this synopsis and submittals will not be returned to the sender.

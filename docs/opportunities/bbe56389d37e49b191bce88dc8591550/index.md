# On-Site Drug Lab equipment and reagents

- Agency: ADMINISTRATIVE OFFICE OF THE US COURTS
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-23 14:57:35.638-04
- Solicitation Number: USCA26R0001_0000

## Summary

THIS IS A DRAFT. The Judiciary intends to release the solicitation on or about March 4, 2026, with a 15-day response time. Award is intended to be made on or about April 15-20, 2026. (dates are subject to change) This notice provides a preliminary notice that the Judiciary intends to make a single award IDIQ from this solicitation. The period of performance for the IDIQ contract is for a seven-year ordering period with a three-year optional ordering period, and a six-month optional ordering period (under Clause 2-90C). The contractor shall provide, to individual United States Probation and Pretrial Services Offices (USPPSO), located nationwide, automated analyzers, forensic use immunoassay reagents, controls, calibrators, all supplies and consumables necessary to operate the analyzers; maintenance of the analyzers; training; water systems; quality control, and a data management system with the capability to export results to all USPPSO laboratories. Interested parties must be registered in SAM.GOV in order to be awarded a contract. All contractors have to be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to contract award. The Judiciary is not subject to the FAR or small business regulations for open market procurements and thus this will not be set aside for small business.

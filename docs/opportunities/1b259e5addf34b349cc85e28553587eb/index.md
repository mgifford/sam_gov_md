# Army Prepositioned Stocks - 4 (APS-4) Korea

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-09 09:48:25.016-04
- Solicitation Number: W52P1J-12-G-0061_0001_C1

## Summary

The contractor is responsible for maintaining APS-4 at Camp Carroll, Waegwan, Korea, Camp Humphries, Korea and Sagami General Depot, Japan. APS-4 consists of prepositioned sets, operational project stocks, sustainment stocks, activity sets and watercraft. Its primary purpose is to reduce the initial amount of strategic airlift required to support a continental U.S. based force projection for the Army and to sustain soldiers until sea lines of communications are established. The APS-4 program is a service provided under the Enhanced Army Global Logistics Enterprise (EAGLE) program which is an Army preferred source.

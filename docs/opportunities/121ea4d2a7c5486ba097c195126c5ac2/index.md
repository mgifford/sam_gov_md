# Low SWaP-C TAPPAS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-20 13:21:26.933-04
- Solicitation Number: SpRCOTAPPAS0420

## Summary

Space Rapid Capabilities Office (SpRCO) is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) to identify potential vendors with mature technologies or products suitable for a Direct to Phase II (D2P2) Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) project focused on Low Size, Weight, Power, and Cost (Low SWaP-C) Tactical Awareness Payload for Proliferated, Agile Satellites (TAPPAS). This RFI is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation or a promise to issue a solicitation in the future. Respondents who qualify for SBIR projects will be notified when the public SBIR solicitation will come out for response. Additional requirements are provided in the CUI attachment. Responses should be concise and limited to a maximum of four (4) pages, including any attachments. Questions will be received until 1 May 2026. Submit responses electronically to jennifer.wilterdink.1@spaceforce.mil and responses should be classified no higher than CUI with the subject line "RFI Response: Low SWaP-C TAPPAS” on an unclassified network. This RFI is for informational purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation. The Government is not obligated to issue a solicitation or award a contract as a result of this RFI. All information received in response to this RFI will be treated as information only and will not be considered as proposals. Responders will not be entitled to payment for information furnished in response to this RFI.

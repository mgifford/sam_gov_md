# ELECTRONIC CHAIN HOIST

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 11:11:28.243-04
- Solicitation Number: N0040626Q0045

## Summary

Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound (FLCPS) intends to solicit for ELECTRIC CHAIN HOIST AND CHAIN HOIST WITH CLAMP. The Part number for the Electric Chain Hoist is 3565-30SH-3. The part number for the Chain Hoist With Clamp is MIL-H-904. BRAND NAME: This requirement is sole sourced to STUART RUSH/COLUMBUS MCKINNON. Interested companies may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement, which will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. A determination not to compete this action based upon responses received to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. The Government will evaluate the offeror’s price for reasonableness and past performance as part of the responsibility determination in accordance with RFO 12.203. All responsible sources may submit a quotation, bid, or proposal, as appropriate, which will be considered by the agency. Quotes will be evaluated in accordance with RFO 12.203, the Government will select the most advantageous offer based on the following evaluation factors: technical including delivery date, price, and past performance in no order of relative importance. Anticipated Award Date: April/2026 Contracting Officer: Taura Helms, taura.s.helms.civ@us.navy.mil

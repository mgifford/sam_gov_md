# Offutt AFB FY26 Air Show: TBM Avenger for Static Display

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-26 14:26:19.912-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4600_TBM_Avenger_Static

## Summary

The 55th Contracting Squadron has an ongoing requirement to acquire a TBM Avenger aircraft for static display at the 2026 Offutt Air Force Base "Defenders of Freedom" Air and Space Show. The show will be held at Offutt AFB, Nebraska on August 8 and August 9, 2026. Additional details can be found in the attached Statement of Work. If you are able to provided the specified aircraft for static display on the specified dates, please contact the listed point of of contact.

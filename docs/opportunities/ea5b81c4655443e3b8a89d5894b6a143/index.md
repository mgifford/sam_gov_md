# MARFORSOC Industry Day

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-06 13:27:17.235-05

## Summary

WHAT: Marine Forces Special Operations Command (MARFORSOC) Industry Day WHERE: Hampton Inn, 1248 NC Hwy 210, Sneads Ferry, NC 28460 WHEN: Thursday, March 19, 2026 PURPOSE: To provide information on upcoming MARFORSOC contracting opportunities. EVENT ATTENDANCE: All vendor companies must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM). Attendance is on a first-come, first-served basis, and registration will close once capacity is reached. There is no cost to attend, but pre-registration is mandatory. Companies may bring up to two representatives. Walk-ins without pre-registration will not be allowed entry. SESSION DETAILS: Two sessions are available to accommodate more attendees: Morning Session: 0830–1130 Afternoon Session: 1230–1530 Both sessions will cover the same content, and attendees may register for only one session. Participants must indicate their preferred session when registering. HOW TO REGISTER: To register, visit the Eventbrite link below and complete your registration by selecting the “Registration” button. Registration closes on March 18, 2026. Eventbrite is the only accepted method of registration—no phone calls, please. Only 1 ticket is allowed per Company. Each ticket allows up to two individuals to attend. Registration Links: Morning Session: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/marforsoc-industry-day-morning-session-tickets-1984636058877?aff=oddtdtcreator Afternoon Session: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/marforsoc-industry-day-afternoon-session-tickets-1984637777016?aff=oddtdtcreator

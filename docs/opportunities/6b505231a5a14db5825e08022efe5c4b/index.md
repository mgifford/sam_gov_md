# Brand-Name Class Justification and Approval (J&A) for Hardware Renewal Warranty Support and Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-10 13:25:29.55-04
- Solicitation Number: JA26-025

## Summary

The nature of this action is to procure annual hardware renewal warranty support (hereinafter referred to as “support”) and maintenance for previously purchased proprietary, commercial off-the-shelf, brand-name hardware already integrated within the existing mission partner infrastructure. Scope is limited to the defined duration and ceiling of this J&A for hardware support and maintenance. This J&A does not cover the procurement of new hardware. Scope is restricted to hardware support and maintenance only (to include subscription or license renewals if appropriate). It is noted that the ordered hardware support or maintenance can be in effect longer than the duration of this class J&A as long as contract/order is issued during the covered period. Contract/orders may also include option periods.

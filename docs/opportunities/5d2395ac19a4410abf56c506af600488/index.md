PROCUREMENT TITLE: Medical Surgical Unit (MSU) Operation and Maintenance Training INTENT TO AWARD: The Department of Veterans Affairs (NCO 19), 6162 S. Willow Drive, Greenwood Village, CO 80111, intends to award one (1) purchase order on a sole source basis, to FORTS Logistics, LLC., for MSU Operation and Maintenance Training. BACKGROUND AND DESCRIPTION: The Western Colorado Health Care System (WCHCS) located at 2121 North Ave, Grand Junction CO, 81501 has a requirement for training facility personnel on the operations and maintenance of the VA s Office of Emergency Management (OEM) FORTS Medical Surgical Unit (MSU) which has been deployed at WCHCS. In preparation of project 575-19-101 Renovate Urgent Care Project a MSU was deployed to the WCHCS main campus to house the Emergency Department during construction. The Facilities Management Services (FMS) and Biomed team will be completing required maintenance and repairs on the trailers ensuring they remain operational during the estimated 18-month project duration. The WCHCS FMS team requires the necessary technical information and knowledge to ensure proper operation of the MSU mechanical and electrical systems for the duration of the project NAICS: 561210 Facilities Support Services, $47 Million PSC: U099 Education/Training - Other AUTHORITY: This procurement will be issued under the authority of RFO Part 12 Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services.

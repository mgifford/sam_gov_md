# Catholic RE Coordinator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 12:07:17.788-04
- Solicitation Number: FA489726Q0013

## Summary

The 366th Contracting Squadron, Mountain Home AFB, Idaho, is seeking contractor support to serve as the Catholic Religious Education (RE) Coordinator for the 366th Fighter Wing Chapel. The contractor shall provide all personnel, management, and services necessary to plan, develop, and execute a comprehensive Catholic Religious Education and Sacramental Preparation program. Support includes coordinating curriculum, scheduling and facilitating classes, maintaining program records, and assisting the Chaplain Corps in meeting the spiritual and pastoral care needs of Catholic personnel and their families assigned to Mountain Home AFB. Amendment #1 to this solicitation, issued 18 March 2026 by the 366th Contracting Squadron, Mountain Home AFB is to attach the questions and answers. All other specifications remain unchanged from original posting.

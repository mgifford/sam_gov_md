# S&RTS Rotterdam, Netherlands

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-17 13:48:11.582-04
- Solicitation Number: TRANSCOM26R003

## Summary

Provide Stevedoring and Related Terminal Services (S&RTS) at the Port of Rotterdam, Netherlands, primarily at the UniPort Terminal, in connection with the import and export of Defense Transportation System (DTS) sponsored cargo. all tasks shall be completed per requirements stated through the Performance Work Statement (PWS) and in a manner consistent with the “Best Commercial Practices.” ARTRANs has identified the UniPort Terminal in the Port of Rotterdam as the primary terminal to support DoW terminal and vessel operations for this contract. Contractor shall have the ability to provide services, labor, and equipment in accordance with this contract primarily at the UniPort Terminal and other designated terminals located within the Rotterdam port, as required.

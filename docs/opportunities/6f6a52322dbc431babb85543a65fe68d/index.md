## Summary

The contractor shall provide all personnel, management, supervision, tools, equipment, materials, transportation, and services necessary to perform preventive, corrective, and emergency maintenance on government-owned medical and dental equipment at the Armed Forces Retirement Home, Gulfport Campus (AFRH-G).

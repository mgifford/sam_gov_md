# Aviano RFQ FA568226Q8011 - Architect Engineer Services.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 06:07:51.153-04
- Solicitation Number: FA568226Q8011

## Summary

The 31st Contracting Squadron requires an Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Contract vehicle to provide Title I, Title II, and Other Architect & Engineering (A-E) Services to support Aviano Air Force Base and Geographical Separated Units (GSU) located within the Italian territory. The IDIQ contract will be utilized to support facility sustainment, restoration, modernization and sporadically new construction projects for the 31st Fighter Wing and all mission partners.

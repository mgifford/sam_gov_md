## Summary

This is a Sources Sought Notice only. The Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Network Contracting Office (NCO) 21 is performing market research to determine if there are any vendors, including Large Businesses, Small Businesses, Service-Disabled Veteran Owned, Veteran Owned, Women Owned, and other Small Businesses, that can provide the Leica Arveo 8 Digital Visualization Microscope for Neurosurgery and the Leica M530 OHX Premium Optical Surgical Microscope, or equal products. Vendors are also requested to provide information about any Federal Supply Schedule (FSS), National Acquisition Center (NAC), or Strategic Acquisition Center (SAC) contracts they may have for these products. Responses to this Sources Sought Notice will be utilized for market research purposes. The requirement entails providing the Leica Arveo 8 Digital Visualization Microscope for Neurosurgery, as well as the Leica M530 OHX Premium Optical Surgical Microscope for Neurosurgery, to the Veterans Affairs Medical Center in Palo Alto. This information will assist the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Network Contracting Office (NCO) 21 in identifying potential vendors capable of fulfilling this requirement. There is no solicitation document available at this time. The applicable NAICS code for this requirement is 339112, with a Small Business size standard of 1,000 employees. Please ensure that your registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) (www.sam.gov) reflects this NAICS code if you are interested in this requirement. If you are responding as a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) or Veteran-Owned Small Business (VOSB) vendor, you must be verified in the Small Business Administration s (SBA s) Small Business Search (SBS) database (https://search.certifications.sba.gov/) and your verification must indicate this NAICS code. Only firms that are both interested and capable should send their information, capability statements, and any questions by email to Mohammed.hussain2@va.gov no later than 15:00 PM Pacific Standard Time, March 25, 2026.

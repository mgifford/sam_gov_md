## Summary

This is a SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT ONLY. It is neither a solicitation announcement nor a request for proposals or quotes and does not obligate the Government to award a contract. Responses to this Sources Sought Announcement must be in writing. The purpose of this Sources Sought Announcement is for market research to make appropriate acquisition decisions and to gain knowledge of potential businesses interested and capable of providing the services described below. Background: The Puget Sound VA Medical Center Nutrition & Food Services, located in Seattle, WA is seeking a contractor to provide delivery & installation of two Electric 10-Full-Size Sheet Pan Combi Ovens (Alto-Shaam Prodigi Pro 10-10 (E) or equivalent). The Ovens must meet the salient characteristics below: The ovens shall have maximum dimensions of 4 W x 3 6 D x 6 3 H. The ovens shall have a minimum capacity to hold 10 full-size sheet pans. The ovens must have four legs or a stand with four legs so that the ovens are at least two feet off the ground to allow for ergonomic operation & easy cleaning underneath the equipment. The ovens must meet National Sanitation Foundation (NSF) requirements & only be constructed from Food & Drug Administration (FDA)-approved materials. Interior & exterior surfaces must be made from stainless steal to allow for easy cleaning. Oven doors shall have glass panes to observe food in cooking chambers. The ovens must have 440-480v/50/60/3-ph, 26.9-29.4 amps. The ovens must have Turbo Mode & 40A 5026972 installation kit or equivalent. The ovens must use computerized technology to cook items using steam & convection functionality. The ovens shall have a programmable touchscreen panel that can be loaded with specific & custom recipes. The ovens must have sensors & probes to manage climate & cooking of items in the oven. The ovens must have automated tablet-based cleaning cycles. The ovens must have a computerized cleaning & descaling system. A water softener or filtration shall be installed to the existing water line to ensure that build-up does not prevent the equipment from working properly. The ovens shall meet energy star requirements.

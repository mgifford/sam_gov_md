# Bridge Contract for Preventative Maintenance and Repair Services

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-11 10:35:57.765-04
- Solicitation Number: 70B01C26F00000160

## Summary

The proposed order will be firm-fixed-price with a total estimated value of $1,240,943.29 for a period of performance of March 9, 2026 – July 26, 2026. The previous order expired on February 28, 2026, and a competitive follow-on acquisition is in progress. This bridge action is required to ensure continuation of mission-critical services while the competitive follow-on procurement is completed. The period of performance has been limited to the minimum duration necessary to complete the competitive acquisition process. This order provides all labor, supervision, tools, materials, parts, equipment, transportation, licenses, permits, certifications and management necessary to provide for equipment and system sustainment, maintenance and repairs at the government facilities in the Southern California Region.

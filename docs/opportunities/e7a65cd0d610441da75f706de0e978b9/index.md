# Construction of B-21 Radio Frequency (RF) Hangar

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 14:41:20.641-04
- Solicitation Number: W912DQ26RA019

## Summary

CONTRACT INFORMATION: The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) Kansas City District (NWK) intends solicit, negotiate, and award a one-time Unrestricted, Firm-Fixed-Price, Design-Bid-Build, construction contract to construct a Radio Frequency/Measurements Hangar Facility with one aircraft position and all associated back shops, material storage, and administrative and facility support spaces. at Whiteman Air Force Base (WAFB), MO. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 236220 Commercial and Institutional Building Construction.Estimated Magnitude of Construction: Between $50,000,000 and $100,000,000A bid bond will be required with your proposal in the amount of 20% of the offeror proposed price or $3,000,000.00 whichever is less. Payment and Performance Bonds will be required for the full amount (100%) of the awarded contract. The requirement is an Unrestricted/Full and Open competitive acquisition; both small and large businesses are eligible to respond.The actual solicitation will be posted and available on SAM.gov on or about 2 April 2026. Proposals will be due approximately 75 days later. The solicitation and any issued amendments will include the proposal submission and evaluation criteria, and shall establish the actual proposal due date.Viewing and download the solicitation requires registration at the SAM.gov website at https://sam.gov/. Downloads and instructions are available through this website. Any amendments will only be available from this website. Offerors are responsible for checking the website frequently for any update(s) to this Pre-Solicitation Announcement or amendments to the Solicitation.A site visit will be held on or about 20 April 2026. The deadline for submitting site visit registration is approximately 7-10 days prior to site visit on or about 10 April 2026. Contact David Miller at david.f.miller@usace.army.mil to register for the site visit.Synopsis of the Scope of Work: The scope of this contract is to construct a Radio Frequency/Measurements Hangar Facility with one aircraft position and all associated back shops, material storage, and administrative and facility support spaces. The facility will consist of both unclassified and classified spaces for a total square footage of 5,345 square meter (57,532 square feet). Structure will feature a new reinforced concrete foundation by replacing the paved area east of the existing Phase Hangar (Building 9), a metal roof, metal siding panels on steel framing and girts above a masonry cavity wall and Concrete Masonry Unit wall assembly around the base of the facility, and powered steel sliding hangar doors. The project will also include supporting facilities such as utilities, pavements, site improvements, and other necessary infrastructure to have a complete and usable facility.

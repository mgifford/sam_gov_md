Project Information: Work includes general construction, alterations, roads, walks, grading, drainage, and construction and certain other items as required by the contract drawings and specifications. Renovate the existing historic Laundry Facility in Building 18 on the Tuscaloosa VA Campus, and construct a new generator enclosure, and a fuel tank enclosure building. New traffic control gate arms will be provided with this project to control traffic. In addition to the above requirements, Contractor shall abide by all VA safety rules and regulations, and ensure work is compliant with applicable NFPA and NEC codes. Project Goals: Renovate Building 18 to meet current operational, safety, code, and accessibility requirements. Maintain uninterrupted laundry operations throughout construction. Preserve the historic integrity of the building while modernizing infrastructure. Improve structural stability, energy efficiency, security, workflow, and overall functionality. Construct new generator and fuel tank enclosure buildings compliant with PSRDM and VA standards. Scope of Work: The Contractor shall provide all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision to complete: Full renovation of Building 18 including interior spaces, restrooms, basements, structural supports, waterproofing, and MEP upgrades. Abatement of lead-based paint and management of any hazardous materials. Relocation and installation of electrical equipment to a new building. Construction of a generator enclosure and a blast resistant above ground fuel tank enclosure. Replacement of windows/doors and refurbishment of select historic units. Installation of two HVAC units and required physical security protections. Exterior site work including grading, drainage, utilities, and traffic control systems. All tasks shown or implied on drawings, specifications, and referenced standards. Contractor shall reference all plans, drawings, and VA specifications.

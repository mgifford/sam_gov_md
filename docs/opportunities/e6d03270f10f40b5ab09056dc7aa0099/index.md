# Laurel Bridge Compass Routers, Software Licenses and Installation

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-13 10:02:43.001-04
- Solicitation Number: VA-26-00047245

## Summary

The VA, OIT, Health, Clinical Imaging, has a requirement for renewal of existing maintenance license subscriptions for compass routers, Lighthouse licenses, and support services current fielded brand name Laurel Bridge products that supports imaging facility operations and technical development/implementation. The base period will include the Laurel Bridge operational DICOM compass routers and Lighthouse perpetual licenses maintenance supporting enterprise-wide image data activities among VA facilities, community providers, and Department of War (DOW). Required software maintenance and support includes periodic updates, enhancements, and corrections to the software as well as technical/help desk support.

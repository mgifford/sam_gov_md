# Amendment 0004 to Solicitation Number SPE608-26-R-0200 3.7 Domestic Ship's Bunkers Program

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

Amendment 0004 to Solicitation SPE608-26-R-0200 is being issued to add requirements 1. ADDITION A. Successive Closing Dates The solicitation is hereby amended to add the following provision: This is an open continuous solicitation with successive closing dates. The Government anticipates receiving new requirements throughout the period of performance, ending December 31, 2028. Solicitation SPE608-26-R-0200 will remain open for the addition of new line items. As requirements arise, the Government will issue amendments establishing closing dates and affording all Offerors an opportunity to compete. B. Additional Requirements 1) Closing Date for This Amendment: Offers responding to this amendment are due Tuesday, April 21, 2026, 3:00 PM EST, Fort Belvoir, Virginia, local time. 2) Required Quality Assurance Provisions are included. See Attachment D a) C16.23 FUEL, NAVAL DISTILLATE (F76) (DLA ENERGY FEB 2026) b) C16.01 TURBINE FUEL, AVIATION (JP5) (DLA ENERGY FEB 2026) 3) Line Items Added: Item: 0058 Location: Ponce, Puerto Rico Mode of Delivery: Barge Product: Fuel Navy Diesel, F76 Quantity: 77,500,000 Gallons Base Reference: Platts ULSD FOB US Gulf, Waterborne Base Reference Price: $3.9058 Per Gallon Base Reference Date: 17 MAR 2026 Item: 0059 Location: Ponce, Puerto Rico Mode of Delivery: Barge Product: Turbine Fuel, Aviation, JP5 Quantity: 31,000,000 Gallons Base Reference: Platts Oilgram Price Report US Gulf Jet 54 Waterborne Base Reference Price: $4.1233 Per Gallon Base Reference Date: 17 MAR 2026 C) Submission Requirements Offerors shall submit Attachment B - Offer Data Sheet and include the following with each item offered: Certificate of Analysis (COA) / Certificate of Quality (COQ) Commitment Letter from the Supplier Offers shall be submitted electronically to: DDFBunkers@dla.mil 3) TERMS AND CONDITIONS Except as provided herein, all other terms and conditions of Solicitation SPE608-26-R-0200 remain unchanged.

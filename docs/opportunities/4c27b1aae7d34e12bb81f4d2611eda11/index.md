## Summary

STATEMENT OF WORK The Contractor shall provide all tools, equipment, material, permits, labor, and supervision required to completely perform the required construction work for the subject project. All work shall be completed in accordance with the project drawings and specifications. The scope of work involves renovation for site security in the main hospital, CLC, Utility Plant, Admin bldg. 3, parking garage and the VA Cincinnati Site. Work includes, but is not limited to, general construction, alterations, electrical, telecommunications, and site work, necessary for demolition and upgrades of security gates, card readers, fencing, walls, ceilings, piping and other items necessary for completion and installation of new work. Due to the utility/roadway shutdowns required, some of this work will have to be performed outside of normal facility business hours. A full list of requirements and further details will be provided in the project drawings and specifications. This is not a solicitation. The government anticipates issuing a solicitation on or about March 13, 2026. *** End Presolicitation Notice ***

# D--Consolidation D&F: NCI CTRP-CSE & CTRO-SS

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-20 08:35:58.693-04
- Solicitation Number: DOIDFBO260044

## Summary

The Acquisition Services Directorate (AQD) on behalf of National Institute of Health (NIH), National Cancer Institute (NCI), Center for Biomedical Informatics and Information Technology (CBIIT) anticipates issuing two Time-and-Materials (T&M) Task Orders against the internal AQD-issued Biomedical Information Technology, Software Development, and Informatics Support (BITSDIS) Indefinite Delivery-Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) to provide a variety of biomedical informatics support services for the NCI CBIIT's Clinical Trials Reporting Program (CTRP). This Determination and Findings (D&F) is executed in support of both Task Order 140D0426R0052 - CTRP-CSE and Task Order 140D0426R0055 - CTRO-SS.

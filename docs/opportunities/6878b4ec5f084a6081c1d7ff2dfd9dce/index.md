# GEX DPC Data Standards

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 12:39:00.07-04
- Solicitation Number: SP470926Q1003

## Summary

Amendment 001 is issued on 3/26/2026 to provide Answers to Questions submitted in by the Question and Answer due date provided in the solicitation. This is a follow-on acquisition to acquire professional support services to provide data maps to transform the data to and from legacy formats in use in other systems, develop reporting capabilities to analyze data quality and use, and analyses of data requirements to support business processes for the Procurement Data Standard (PDS) and Purchase Request Data Standard (PRDS) Development Global Exchange (GEX).PDS and PRDS Development GEX Task Order requires contractor support in the implementation, and troubleshooting of the Procurement Data Standard (PDS) and Purchase Request Data Standard (PRDS) in furtherance of the requirements for a Uniform Contract Writing System set forth in Section 862 of the 2013 National Defense Authorization Act.

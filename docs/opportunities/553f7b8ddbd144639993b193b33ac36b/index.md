## Summary

2032H326N00004 Subscription Services for Global Access to a World Trade Database. Period of Performance: Base plus 4 Option Periods The purpose of this System for Award Management (SAM) announcement is to seek business sources capable of competing for this requirement. GENERAL SCOPE The Office of Terrorism and Financial Intelligence (TFI) requires an enterprise-wide license to a commercial global trade database subscription service. Acquiring this type of subscription service is crucial for TFI researchers, who report this data to Treasury senior policymakers. The purpose of this requirement is for the selected contractor to provide the following subscription services to users in TFI: Enterprise-wide license for global access to a comprehensive trade data database, including all HS Codes at the Tariff Line Level, maintained in real-time for a variety of countries The ability for data delivery by FTP and API Dedicated customer support to resolve issues and offer trainings (when needed) The database must include information for the following regions and countries: Asia and Middle East Including China, Iran, Iraq, Kazakhstan, Russia, Saudi Arabia, Myanmar, etc. Europe Including Slovakia, Malta, etc. Africa Including Zambia, Nambia, Madagascar, etc. Responses to this Sources Sought may be provided through a capabilities statement. This Sources Sought is for information and planning purposes only and shall not be construed as a solicitation or an obligation on the part of the Treasury to issue a request for proposal or award a contract. Treasury does not intend to award a contract on the basis of responses nor otherwise pay for the preparation of any information submitted. Contractors responding to this Sources Sought should submit the information necessary for the Treasury to make a determination that the source is capable of satisfying the Government’s anticipated need. Treasury reserves the right to contact individual companies for additional information. This notice does not obligate the Treasury to provide acknowledgment or receipt of all responses received resulting from this notice. Should such a requirement materialize, no basis for claims against the Treasury shall arise as a result of a response to this Sources Sought or the Government's use of such information as either part of our evaluation process or in developing specifications for any subsequent requirement. Responses should be identified with Sources Sought #2032H326N00004 and are due no later than Monday, April 27, 2026 at 5:00 PM EST. All interested parties must submit their responses via email to: Adam Walsh, Contracting Specialist via email at Adam.Walsh@treasury.gov. Questions (oral or written) regarding this Sources Sought will NOT be accepted, nor responses to such questions provided.

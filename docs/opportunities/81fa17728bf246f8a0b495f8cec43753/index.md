# Air Supply Initiator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 12:47:06.612-04
- Solicitation Number: FA821325R3035

## Summary

The Air Supply Initiator is used on the Air Launched Cruise Missile (ALCM), and is a component that initiates the release of compressed air to then pressurize and enter into the missile fuel tanks, setting off launch procedures of the missile. Delivery on this contract is 24 months ARO. This contract will be a single award to procure an estimated total quantity of 200 units.

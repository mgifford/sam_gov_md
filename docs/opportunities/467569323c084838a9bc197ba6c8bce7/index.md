# Sources Sought - Temporary Cooling Services for the DC Armory

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-09 18:17:04.148-04
- Solicitation Number: W912R1-26-Q-4001

## Summary

This is a Request for Information (RFI) and Sources Sought Notice for market research purposes only. The United States Property & Fiscal Officer (USPFO) Contracting Office, on behalf of the District of Columbia Army National Guard (DCARNG), is seeking information regarding the capability and availability of commercial sources to provide temporary climate control services. The contractor shall provide all necessary equipment, labor, and materials to deliver and operate a temporary cooling system for the DC Armory Drill Floor (370’ long x 196’ wide x 88’ tall). The objective is to maintain temperatures between 70-74 degrees within the facility. This notice is intended to identify potential sources, including small businesses, capable of meeting the government's requirements. Please contact the Contracting Officer, LTC Paul Caldwell at joseph.p.caldwell.mil@mail.mil and the Contract Specialist, MAJ Kyle Hartleben at kyle.d.hartleben2.mil@army.mil with any inquires or interest in response to this Sources Sought. Please include your CAGE Code, Business Size and Socioeconomic Designation if applicable. Please provide supporting information demonstrating your company is fully capable of meeting the government's requirement to include past federal contract numbers for simliar services.

# SmartSketch software support renewal

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 09:40:00.356-04
- Solicitation Number: HC102826R0022

## Summary

The Government intends to issue a sole source order to Intergraph Corporation (“Hexagon”) for SmartSketch software support renewal for Puget Sound Naval Shipyard & Intermediate Maintenance Facility. The period of performance is Date of Award through March 31, 2027. The Redacted Brand Name Class Justification & Approval for Software Support is also posted along with the solicitation. Responsible sources may submit a completed and signed solicitation in response. If you are a reseller, you must also submit a letter or email from the original equipment manufacturer confirming your authorized reseller status along with your completed solicitation. Please submit by 11AM CST on April 08, 2026 via email to alexis.n.corley.civ@mail.mil.

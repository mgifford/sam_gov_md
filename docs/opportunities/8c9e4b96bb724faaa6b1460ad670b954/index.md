## Summary

This is a SOURCES SOUGHT/REQUEST FOR INFORMATION (RFI) only and shall not be construed as a solicitation or as an obligation on the part of the Government. This notice does not obligate the government to award a contract or otherwise pay for the information provided in the response. The Government will not be liable for payment of any response preparation expenses. Interested parties are responsible for adequately marking proprietary or competition sensitive information contained within their response known and labeled accordingly. Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) Strategic Information Technology (IT) Division operating under Army Contracting Command - Aberdeen Proving Ground (ACC-APG) Senior Contracting Official's authority is investigating the market capability to provide sustainment support for Product Lead Logistics Information Systems (PL LIS) Radio Frequency-In-Transit Visibility (RF-ITV) program. RF-ITV is an Army system that provides global in-transit visibility for the identification, movement, and tracking of assets for unit move and sustainment cargo for the entire Department of War. Support of RF-ITV includes maintaining the global active radio frequency identification (aRFID) equipment, supporting the day to day operations for the RF-ITV Production, COOP, Trainer, and Development/Test Environments to include all cybersecurity, engineering, and system administration needed for the hardware and software, 24/7 service desk supporting both the RF-ITV and TC AIMS systems, program management, agile software development for all servers, portals, and applications used on the program.

## Summary

STATEMENT OF WORK (SOW)

1.0 Scope of Work: The objective of this project is to purchase and successfully install a new cone beam computed tomography (CBCT) imaging unit at Harry S Truman VA Medical Center. This includes ensuring the system is fully operational with the necessary software, training staff on its use, and providing ongoing technical support to maintain optimal functionality.

2.0 Background: The current Carestream 9000 imaging unit at Harry S Truman VA Medical Center has reached its End of Life and is beyond economic repair. In recent months, the unit has experienced frequent downtimes, resulting in numerous patient appointment cancellations and delays. This has significantly impacted patient care services and operational efficiency. The acquisition of a new brand name or equal Carestream 9600 unit is critical to restore reliable diagnostic imaging services. CONTRACTOR REQUIREMENTS

Provide comprehensive training for relevant medical and technical personnel on the operation of the new unit.

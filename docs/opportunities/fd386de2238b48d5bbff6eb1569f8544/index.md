The Contractor shall provide all preventive and corrective maintenance necessary to ensure optimal performance, reliability, and longevity of the covered equipment. Priority Remote Service Response - Requires connectivity to the Remote Services network. - Provides prioritized remote diagnostic and troubleshooting support. c. Labor and Travel Coverage - All labor and travel expenses covered during standard service hours: Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, excluding federal holidays. d. Response Times - One-hour initial response time. - Four-hour on-site response time when required. e. Software Enhancements - Provision of operating system software enhancements that do not require hardware changes, excludes software upgrades. i. Technical and Clinical Support - Unlimited technical support 24/7. - Clinical applications support Monday–Friday, 8:00 AM–5:00 PM. j. Remote Service Support - Includes Remote Desktop support. k. Remote Proactive Monitoring - Requires Remote Services connection. - Provides continuous monitoring and proactive issue detection. l. Reporting - Reports including PSI eligibility, service history, and PM summaries. m. Provide service and maintenance support on the existing MR Elastography hardware/software. - Ongoing maintenance and service support for the Elastography hardware/software already licensed on the system. - Troubleshooting and issue resolution related to Elastography functionality as part of the service agreement. 3.

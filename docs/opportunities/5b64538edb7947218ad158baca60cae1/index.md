## Summary

This solicitation is for the replacement of parts and sustainment of the Giant Voice Notification System at Misawa Air Base in Japan. The attached documents: a) RFQ; b) Brand Name Justification; c) and Statement of Objectives; detail the requirements for the solicitation and the specifications for the parts needed.

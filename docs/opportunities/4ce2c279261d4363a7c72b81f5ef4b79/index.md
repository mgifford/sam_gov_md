# NOI - Insulated-Sensing Myocardial Navigation Guidewire, Initial R&D and In Vivo Test

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 12:17:31.736-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N98026Q00214

## Summary

The National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute (NHLBI) intends to award a sole source contract for the design, fabrication, and testing of insulated-sensing intramyocardial electrosurgical guidewire prototypes. The effort includes engineering development, bench validation, and delivery of refined prototypes for preclinical in vivo evaluation. This Notice of Intent is not a request for competitive proposals; however, interested parties may submit capability statements for consideration.

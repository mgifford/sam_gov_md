CLOUD VA INDUSTRY DAY: GASTROSCOPE/COLONOSCOPE SYSTEMS Please join the Department of Veterans Affairs, Veteran Integrated Service Network 23 for an onsite Industry Day as it relates to the St. Cloud VA Medical Center s need for gastroscope/colonoscope systems. This invitation is open to all vendors who believe they might have the capability to provide the necessary instrumentation to the St. Cloud VA Medical Center. The purpose of this industry day is to conduct market research as well as to survey the market to ascertain whether-or-not there are other sources willing and capable of providing the needed supplies and services. This Industry Day will also allow potential contractors/vendors to submit a non-binding statement of interest and possible documentation demonstrating their capabilities. The interested parties will visit the medical center during their scheduled time to demonstrate the capabilities of their products.

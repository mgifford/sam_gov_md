# Articulating Boom Lifts

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 02:23:54.414-04
- Solicitation Number: 19TX1026Q0003

## Summary

This notice is issued for informational purposes only and does not constitute a request for quotations. No responses are requested at this time. The U.S. Embassy in Ashgabat, Turkmenistan intends to issue Request for Quotation (RFQ) No. 19TX1026Q0003 on or about May 1, 2026, via the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) website. All responsible sources may submit a quotation once the RFQ is released. The U.S. Embassy Ashgabat intends to issue a solicitation for the procurement of one (1) brand new Articulating Diesel Boom Lift and one (1) brand new Articulating Electric Boom Lift, complete with all associated equipment, training, and support services for the U.S. Embassy Ashgabat, Turkmenistan. Only new equipment will be accepted; refurbished or used units are not acceptable. The requirement will include delivery to U.S. Embassy Ashgabat, Turkmenistan. All technical requirements, specifications, evaluation criteria, and submission instructions will be provided in the forthcoming solicitation. The anticipated contract will be a firm-fixed-price purchase order awarded to the lowest-priced technically acceptable responsible offeror. The Government may award the contract based on initial quotations without discussions. Important Notice: All contractors must be registered and maintain an active registration in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) with a valid Unique Entity Identifier (UEI) prior to contract award, pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. There is no fee to register for SAM.gov. Detailed information, user guides, and webinars are available at https://www.sam.gov. Additional Information: No paper copies of the RFQ will be issued. All documents will be available electronically via SAM.gov. Please note: Questions from vendors regarding this requirement will only be accepted after the official RFQ is issued. No inquiries will be entertained prior to the release of the RFQ.

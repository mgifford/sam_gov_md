# LAWN TRACTOR HEATED CAB AND SNOWBLOWER

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 12:57:59.763-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U3037

## Summary

Amended to include set aside code. This vehicle is equipped with a gasoline-powered, 25-horsepower, 2-cylinder, liquid-cooled engine as a minimum, featuring electronic fuel injection that requires no choke. It includes a replaceable engine oil filter and a hydrostatic transmission with four-wheel drive. The transaxle is fitted with a differential lock, and the vehicle operates with a mandatory 2-pedal direction control, eliminating toe and heel control. It comes with hydraulic power steering, a cast iron front axle, and internal wet brakes. The attachment system features a 2-function hydraulic lift and hydraulic quick disconnects for a snow blower attachment. For operator comfort and convenience, the vehicle is outfitted with an adjustable suspension seat with a seat belt, an hour meter, front and rear lights, backup lights, tilt steering, a cup holder, a toolbox, and cruise control.The vehicle is fitted with R3 turf tires on both the front and rear. It includes a 47-inch, two-stage, front-mounted quick-attach snowblower and a fully enclosed cab with heat and a windshield wiper. Additionally, it comes with rear tire chains, rear wheel weights with all necessary brackets, and IUID labels.The unit will be delivered fully assembled and operational, complete with all operator, service, and parts manuals. The preferred delivery date is 120 days after the order is placed, with delivery to Fort Drum, NY.

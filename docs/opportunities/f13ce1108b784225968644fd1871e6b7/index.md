# Los Padres National Forest - Prospectus for Campground & Related Granger-Thye Concessions

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-20 16:58:20.934-04

## Summary

This prospectus is being issued to solicit applications for a concession campground special use permit. One permit to provide high-quality public service in the operation and maintenance of Government-owned recreation facilities located on the Monterey, Santa Lucia, Santa Barbara, Mount Pinos and Ojai Ranger Districts of the Los Padres National Forest. The three-year average gross revenue for this opportunity is $3,292,716.00 (2021-2023). See attached document for additional information.

# Removal and Installation of Decals on FHFD Apparatuses

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 13:05:50.585-04
- Solicitation Number: W9115126QA025

## Summary

MICC Fort Hood intends to award a Firm-Fixed-Price Contract for Removing and Installing Decals on all Fort Hood Fire Department (FHFD) Apparatuses. The North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code for this procurement is 323111 – Commercial Printing (except Screen and Books). This requirement is a full and open acquisition. Please see attached Statement of Work (SOW) for full requirement details. All questions must be submitted no later than 2:00 PM Central Daylight Time on April 13th, 2026, to both the Contract Specialist and the Contracting Officer at the above email addresses. Email is the preferred method for questions. Requests shall be marked with solicitation number W91151-26-Q-A025. Please see updated Q&A titled "W9115126QA025 Questions and Answers 01APR26.pdf."

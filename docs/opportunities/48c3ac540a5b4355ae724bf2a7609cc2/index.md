# Water Treatment Facility

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 01:16:44.567-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LN26Q0003

## Summary

Contractor will provide all labor, equipment required to complete improvements to the existing water treatment facility at the Monument Fire Center, Monument, Colorado. The water treatment facility supplies potable drinking water to the fire center staff. Due to the periodic presence of soluble lead in the well water, Optimal Corrosion Control Treatment (OCCT) is required to comply with Colorado Department of Public Health and Environment (CDPHE) regulations.

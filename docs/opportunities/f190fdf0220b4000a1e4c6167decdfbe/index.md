## Summary

Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI), on behalf of Rock Island Arsenal Joint Manufacturing and Technology Technology Center plans to hold an Industry Day on June, 4th 2026. It is tentatively scheduled to be held at Rock Island Arsenal. This posting is to notify Industry of the Government's intent to hold the Industry Day to assist with planning and travel arrangements.

## Summary

The Department of the Interior, National Park Service, is requesting quotes (RFQ) for Alt Helipad Site Rental Equipment Sequoia National Park. The Contractor shall provide all mobilization, labor, supervision, materials, equipment, transportation, site rehabilitation, clean-up, and demobilization. This procurement is set-aside for Small Business (SB) concerns only. Offers received from concerns that are not Small Business concerns shall be considered nonresponsive and will be rejected. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code is 532120; the small business size standard is $47.0 million. All questions for this requirement must be sent via email to Brian Roppolo at brian_roppolo@ios.doi.gov no later than 2 April 2026. All questions will be answered on a future amendment posted on www.SAM.gov. QUOTES ARE DUE AT 12:00 P.M. PACIFIC STANDARD TIME (PST) ON THURSDAY, 16 APRIL, 2026. Only emailed proposals will be accepted. The government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation.

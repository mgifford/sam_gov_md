# US Secret Service Virtual Industry Day

- Agency: HOMELAND SECURITY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-20 12:39:09.21-04
- Solicitation Number: 03262026

## Summary

THE MARCH 26, 2026 USSS VIRTUAL INDUSTRY DAY IS NOW CANCELED. SINCE THEIR IS A PARTIAL GOVERNMENT SHUTDOWN AFFECTING DHS, USSS WILL NOT CONDUCT AN INDUSTRY AT THIS TIME. To introduce the USSS community to a host of vendors in an effort to increase large and small business support and expand means of acquiring goods/services, as well as inform vendor communities of major acquisitions and possible contract opportunities. After the presentations, the Government will accept questions. If additional information is needed for certain questions, the Government will record them and attach responses via SAM.gov after the event. During the Virtual Industry Day Session, each Industry Partner will be asked to fill out a Form with its list of attendees. It is the Government’s intent to release all attendees’ names and contact information to SAM. If you would like to opt out of having your information released, please mention that in your firm’s RSVP request email. This event requires registration. After you register, you will receive a confirmation email message with instructions on how to join the event. The registration link is attached.

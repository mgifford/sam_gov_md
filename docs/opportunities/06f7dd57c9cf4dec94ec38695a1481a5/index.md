- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-08 18:40:04.809-04

## Summary

This notice is prepared IAW RFO FAR 5.1 and 12.201-1(c)(2). THIS NOTICE IS NOT A REQUEST FOR QUOTATIONS. The Government intends to solicit this requirement directly from suppliers IAW RFO FAR 12.201-1(c)(2) for firm-fixed price supply contract to purchase three (3) each of Maintenance Trucks, Panel Vans for Sagami Sub Fac Engr, DPW. The anticipated award date is on or about 15 May 2026 (subject to change). Description of the requirement: The contractor shall provide three (3) each Maintenance Trucks, Panel Vans including transportation and any additional equipment or materials necessary to deliver the required products. Note 1: This procurement will be performed entirety in the country of Japan. The successful offeror must have valid licenses work in the country of Japan.

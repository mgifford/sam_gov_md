# RFQ - Sign-Making Equipment Package (Cutter, Applicator Table, and Supplies)

## Summary

Amended to change NAICS code. This RFQ is for a complete package of sign making equipment and supplies. The required items include one Graphtec FC9000 series vinyl cutter plotter, one Roll X Go flatbed applicator table, one Omega

8.0 Software license, various widths of R Tape AT65 clear application tape, and multiple rolls of 15 inch and 30 inch 3M 7125 Gerber 220 series punched vinyl in assorted colors including White, Black, Tomato Red, Russet Brown, and Duranodic.

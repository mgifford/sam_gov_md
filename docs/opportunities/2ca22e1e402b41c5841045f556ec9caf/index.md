## Summary

Shelf Stocking, Receiving/Storage/Holding Area (RSHA), and Custodial Services at the Miramar MCAS Commissary. The Government anticipates issuing a solicitation for shelf stocking, receiving/storage/holding area (RSHA), and custodial services at the Miramar MCAS Commissary. The Contractor shall furnish all personnel, supervision, equipment, tools, supplies, materials, and other items and services necessary to perform these requirements. This notice is issued for informational and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation. Solicitation documents and attachments will be released at a later date with the formal solicitation.This requirement was previously synopsized under Notice ID Number HQC00826RE0003 and will now be issued as an RFQ under HQC00826QE0007. Note- This is a pre-solicitation notice (synopsis) and is not a solicitation. No proposals or quotes are being requested at this time.

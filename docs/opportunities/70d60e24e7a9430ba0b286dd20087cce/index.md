# Notice of Consolidation for DFC Enterprise IT Operation Services

- Agency: UNITED STATES INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT FINANCE CORPORATION
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-14 16:31:49.964-04

## Summary

This notice is made in accordance with FAR 7.107-5(c) to publicize DFC's determination that consolidation of Enterprise IT Operation Services is necessary and justified. This notice is neither a formal solicitation nor a request for competitive proposals. No solicitation document is available and email/telephone requests will not be honored. No award will be made on the basis of unsolicited quotations or offers received in response to this notice. This notice is for informational purposes only.

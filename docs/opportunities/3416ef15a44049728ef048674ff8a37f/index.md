## Summary

The contractor shall provide all supervision, labor, tools, equipment, vehicles, transportation and chemicals necessary to provide Weed and Feed lawn care services. Lawn care services shall maintain healthy grass, trees, shrubs and plants and present a clean, neat and professional appearance. Safety devices shall be utilized at all times on all equipment.

# Agricultural Chemicals

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 15:41:31.371-04
- Solicitation Number: 1232SA26Q0523

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0523 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for agricultural chemicals for research farming. Beltsville Agriculture Research Center located in Beltsville, MD looks to purchase restricted use Herbicides for the use in its agriculture research and production program. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 325320. The small business size standard is 1,150. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

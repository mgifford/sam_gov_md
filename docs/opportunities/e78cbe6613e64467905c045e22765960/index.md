# Inconel 625 Scrap

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 12:54:11.246-04
- Solicitation Number: 2-1-QSC-I-26-208-001

## Summary

This requirement is for the sale and removal of approximately 22,600 pounds of Inconel 625 scrap located at Anniston Army Depot in Anniston, Alabama. The material will be loaded into the purchaser’s conveyance by Government personnel. Due to loading limitations, vehicle height must not exceed 12.5 feet, as depot equipment cannot accommodate taller conveyances. The property is offered for sale as-is, where-is, with no warranties expressed or implied. CONDITION OF THE PROPERTY IS NOT WARRANTED. PICTURES ARE NOT WARRANTED. This property is located on a secure military installation. Inspection and pickup are by confirmed appointment only, Monday through Thursday, excluding federal holidays. Access requirements include valid government-issued photo identification, compliance with installation security procedures, and escort while on site. All personnel and vehicles are subject to inspection. Individuals with disqualifying background conditions may be denied access. The purchaser is responsible for coordinating all access and removal activities in advance with the designated site points of contact.

# N5523626Q0060 - Joint Oil Analysis Program (JOAP) Lab Titrator and Autosampler

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-16 17:15:01.754-04
- Solicitation Number: N5523626Q0060

## Summary

Southwest Regional Maintenance Center (SWRMC), Code 410A Procurement, intends to award a sole source requirement to Hanna Instruments Inc., to provide calibration and maintenance services for Joint Oil Analysis Program (JOAP) Lab Titrator and Autosampler. This requirement will be processed in accordance Part 12. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 811210 Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance. This notice is neither a request nor a solicitation of offers; however, all information received after the date of publication of this notice will be reviewed by the Government for future considerations. Information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether conducting a competitive procurement is in the best interest of the Government. Responses must provide clear and concise documentation indicating the vendor’s bona fide capability to provide this service or equivalency. Responses must include company name, address, telephone number, email address, CAGE Code, and business size. Certifications in the System for Award Management must be current and active to be eligible for contract award. All information should be delivered via email to Ilda Trejo, ilda.c.trejo.civ@us.navy.mil and Dayen Lagunas, dayen.lagunas.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 11:00 AM (PST), 17 April 2026.

## Summary

Sources Sought Notice 2026-SPRDL126R0093 Item Name: Transfer Transmission NSN: 2520-01-469-9893 Government Part No: (19207) 57K3505 Minimum Guaranteed Quantity: 63 EA Estimated Maximum 3-year Quantity: 945 EA End Item: High Mobility Multipurpose Wheeled Vehicle (HMMWV) The Government will provide a Technical Data Package (TDP) when available. The Transfer Transmission Assembly is a drivetrain component for the HMMWV. This assembly functions as a secondary gearbox that directs power from the engine and main transmission to both the front and rear axles of the vehicle. This distribution of power is what enables the HMMWV's four-wheel-drive capability. The transfer case allows the driver to switch between different gear ranges.

# Air Delivery and Low-Level Static Line Training Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 16:12:15.147-04
- Solicitation Number: M00681Q0037

## Summary

The purpose of this acquisition is to provide, a Firm Fixed Price (FFP), performance-based acquisition (PBA),service contract. 1st Distribution Support Battalion, 1st Marine Logistics Group, I MEF is responsible for providing it’s Marines with Air Delivery (AD) and Low-Level Static Line (LLSL) Progression and Sustainment Training. The period of instruction will build upon current resident AD and LLSL capabilities to support future deploying Marine Expeditionary Units (MEU), I MEF contingency operations, major combat operations, and the battalion’s requirements within the prioritized Combatant Command Operational and Conceptual Plans. This is to be accomplished through a combination of contracted facilities, aircraft support, battalion-provided training, and contracted drop zone use in a geographic location that can support all cargo and personnel parachute requirements listed in the Performance Work Statement. The training will be completed over the course of approximately two years from 1 May 2026 to 30 April 2028. Prospective contractors pay special attention to Addendums 52.212-1 and 52.212-2 as they describe Instructions to Offerors and Evaluation Criteria.

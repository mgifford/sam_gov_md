# Lionsgate

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 11:36:15.543-04
- Solicitation Number: 26-1012

## Summary

The Department of War Activity (DWIA) intends to award a single source purchase order to LIONSGATE FILMS INC., CAGE Code: 5BS75, located at 2700 Colorado Avenue, Suite 200, Santa Monica, California, 90404-5502, United States. The intent of this award is to acquire proprietary television and movie programming content, including the broadcast distribution rights for a mixture of prime time and non-prime time television programming for AFN outlets overseas.

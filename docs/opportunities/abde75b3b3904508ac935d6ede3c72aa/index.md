## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Weapons System at Norfolk Naval Shipyard (NNSY) intends to award a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Single Award Contract as Brand Name Specific with Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) Evaluation Factors. The required item is for commercial items prepared in accordance with the information in FAR Part 13 and FAR Subpart 12.6, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. The Contract Line Item Number (CLIN) is as follows: CLIN 0001 QTY 79 EACH MATERIAL: BATPOWER PROE 2 ES15B SURFACE EXTERNAL BATTERIES PART NUMBER B076VYP2VN ORDERING DATA IS ATTACHED TO SOLICITATION MANUFACTURER: BATPOWER The requirement will be solicited as a total small business set aside. The solicitation document incorporates provisions and clauses. The anticipated delivery date for this acquisition is 2 weeks.

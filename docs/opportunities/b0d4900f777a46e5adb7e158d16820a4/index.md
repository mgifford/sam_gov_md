# Road Maintenance Service for the Umatilla National Forest

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 18:05:01.069-05

## Summary

The Umatilla National Forest, Pendleton, OR requires roadway surface and vegetation maintenance on an estimated 269.7 miles of road in the south zone, Heppner OR; and 165.6 miles in the north zone, Walla Walla, WA. See ATCH-2_1240BE26Q0002 _PWS: Performance Work Statement, Roadway Maintenance Service for the Umatilla National Forest. Amendment 0001: Changes the Quality Assurance Surveillance Plan to include the Roadway Vegetation Maintenance diagram, "Brushing".

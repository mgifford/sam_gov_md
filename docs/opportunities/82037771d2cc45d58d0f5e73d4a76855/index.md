# Ammunition Order for USP Atwater

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 12:11:37.439-04
- Solicitation Number: 15B61826Q00000006

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Prisons, United States Penitentiary, Atwater, Ca intends to issue a single award to a qualifying entity for the provide various small arms ammunition that meets or exceeds the specs listed below. Vendors must be able to sell and deliver the ammo in the state of California. All ammunition must be new in box. No refurbished ammunition or loose ammunition will be accepted. We are seeking name brand or equivalent. All quotes should include shipping costs, FOB destination. Please note any additional Excise tax that will be assessed. The solicitation document and incorporated provisions and clauses are those in effect through Federal Acquisition Circular 2026-01. The North American Industrial Classification System (NAICS) code applicable to this requirement is 332992. The solicitation is 100% set-aside for small business. The small business size standard for this NAICS code is 1,300 employees. Quotes will be evaluated to which offer(s) conforms to the Specifications and is the most advantageous to the Government, with both Price and Delivery time considered. Each potential vendor is required per FAR 52.212-3 to complete their representation and certifications via the Online Representation and Certification Applications (SAM) at http://www/sam.gov. The pre-solicitation will be available on or after April 14, 2026 with a closing date of April 28, 2026, and no later than 9:00 a.m., Pacific Standard Time. This solicitation will be distributed solely through beta.SAM.gov http://www.beta.sam.gov/. Hard copies of the solicitation document will not be made available. The site provides downloading instructions. All future information about this acquisition, including solicitation amendments, will also be distributed solely through this site. Interested parties are responsible for monitoring this site to ensure that they have the most up-to-date information about this acquisition. Vendors that would like to provide an offer must include a signed copy of page 1 of the SF-1449 RFQ #15B61826Q00000006 with a Unit Price and Total for each of line items found on page 4 of the SF-1449. 54 Cases - 9B9 FEDERAL CLASSIC 9MM 115GR JHP HI-SHOK STANDARD VELOCITY – (NO +P or +P+) NAME BRAND OR EQUAL TO 9BP FEDERAL 9MM 115GR HI-SHOK JHP. NEW IN BOX, BRAND NAME MANUFACURE, NEW BOXER PRIMED BRASS. CASES OF 1,000 WITH BOXES OF 50. NO LOOSE AMMUNITION. 40 Cases - 5.56X PMC 5.56 55GR FMJ-BT X-TAC XM193 NAME BRAND OR EQUAL TO PMC 5,56 55GR XTAC XM193. NEW IN BOX BRAND NAME MANUFACTURE, NEW BOX PRIMED BRASS. CASES OF 1,000 WITH BOXES OF 20. NO LOOSE AMMUNITION.\

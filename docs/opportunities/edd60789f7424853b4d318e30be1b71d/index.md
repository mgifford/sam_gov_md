# FCC BUTNER ICE MACHINES AND DISPENSERS

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 09:44:54.124-04
- Solicitation Number: 15B10626P00000233

## Summary

The Federal Correctional Complex at Butner, NC is requisitioning 9 brand name or equal Koolaire KDP0500A Ice Makers and 9 brand name or equal Manitowoc SPA312 Ice Dispensers. This requirement will be awarded following FAR Overhaul 12.201-1(c)(2). Following this presolicitation, FCC Butner will solicit quotations from at least 3 sources and award based of off lowest price techincally acceptable. The anticipated date of award is April 10, 2026. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. Products proposed must meet atleast the following qualifications to be eligible for award: The Ice Maker must have the following qualities: approximately 30" W and 24" D to fit the accompanying dispenser. production capacity around 502 lb/24 hours no digital screens no water cooled condenser smudge & fingerprint resistant stainless like finish dice size cubes 115V Energy Star certified at least industry standard warranty The Ice Dispenser must have the following qualities: vending ice dispenser approximately 30" W x 32" D to fit the accompanying ice maker approximately 180-lb capacity for dice ice bucket filling stainless steel exterior 115V at least industry standard warranty

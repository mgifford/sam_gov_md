# Grounds Maintenance Services, West Hill Dam, Uxbridge, MA

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 14:38:22.681-05
- Solicitation Number: W912WJ26QA068

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers – New England District is issuing a Pre-Solicitation Notice for Grounds Maintenance Services, West Hill Dam, Uxbridge, MA QUESTIONS REGARDING THE PROCUREMENT WILL NOT BE ANSWERED DURING THE PRESOLICITATION PHASE. SUBMIT QUESTIONS TO THE CONTRACT SPECIALIST AFTER THE SOLICITATION AND ASSOCIATED DOCUMENTS HAVE BEEN POSTED. THE WORK CONSISTS OF: Furnish all equipment, materials and labor to perform the grounds maintenance services at the West Hill Dam Project, Uxbridge, MA. The West Hill Dam project mowing includes approximately 9 acres of weekly mowing areas and 0.25 acres of biweekly mowing areas. This is not a request for quotes at this time. The solicitation requirements and contract documents, to include instructions for submission, and the response date will be available on or about March 23, 2026. Neither telephonic, mailed nor faxed requests for hard copies of this solicitation will be accepted. The solicitation will only be available online; in order to download the solicitation offerors must access the Contract Opportunities website (www.SAM.gov). This procurement is set-aside for small business vendors. The applicable NAICS code is 561730 with a Small Business Size Standard of $9.5 Million. ALL VENDORS MUST HAVE AN ACTIVE REGISTRATION IN SAM.GOV AT THE TIME OF SUBMISSION IN ORDER TO BE CONSIDERED. ANY VENDOR THAT DOES NOT HAVE AN ACTIVE SAM.GOV REGISTRATION WILL BE REJECTED AS NON RESPONSIVE.

# Selfridge ANGB, MI - Paving Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 12:35:19.268-04
- Solicitation Number: GNFR-FC-SEL-26-26229

## Summary

Project replaces approximately 17,000 sf of existing concrete and asphalt pavement of the commissary loading area. It consists of removal and discarding existing pavement, assessment and replacement of existing subgrade, installation of underground drainage system, regrading and installation of new pavement. In order to participate in this solicitation, please refer to link provided. Any and all amendments will be posted on our website, and must be acknowledged, when submitting a proposal. https://odin.aafes.com/ConstructionSolicitations/

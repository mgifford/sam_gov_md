## Summary

SECTION I — DESCRIPTION / STATEMENT OF WORK

1.1 Objective The US. Embassy, Abuja, (the Government) requires a qualified contractor to provide Hydraulic Forklift Operator Training, including hands-on practical instruction with Narrow Aisle Electric Forklifts, for twenty-eight (28) Government personnel. Training must comply with all applicable Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA) standards, specifically 29 CFR 1910.178 — Powered Industrial Trucks. (Please see the attached solicitation for details).

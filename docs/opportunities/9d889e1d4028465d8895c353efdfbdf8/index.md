# A30 FiberOptic Waterfront Cabling Support

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 07:23:52.743-04
- Solicitation Number: N0017826R3009

## Summary

***THIS IS A SEAPORT-NXG REQUIREMENT. ONLY SEAPORT-NXG MULTIPLE AWARD CONTRACT CONTRACTORS ARE ELIGIBLE FOR AWARD. All SEAPORT MAC holders will need to register PIEE accounts in order to now receive solicitations and submit proposals/offers.*** The Naval Surface Warfare Center Dahlgren Division (NSWCDD) has a requirement for fiber optic networking support for the U.S. Navy. This includes Research and Development of new technical capabilities, Ship Construction support for all classes of Navy Warships, Operations and Maintenance of fielded fiber optic cable systems and plants.

## Summary

Solicitation Number: THERE IS NO SOLICITATION AT THIS TIME Notice Type: RFI / Sources Sought Requisition Number: RFISS-168- Bldg_Subfloor Removal Epoxy Coating Synopsis: The Naval Undersea Warfare Center Division, Keyport is issuing this sources sought notice as a means of conducting market research to for the replacement of epoxy flooring at a certain Bldg. rooms 127A, 128A and the paint booth. The project will require the contractor to grind existing flooring, patch cracks in existing concrete and apply a new epoxy floor coating. The Contractor shall provide labor, materials, and equipment, as required, to accomplish the following work in accordance with the statements here. 1.Grind and remove existing epoxy floor coating, 2. Patch all cracks and expansion joints with a compatible filler, and 3.

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1240LU26Q0054 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the Coeur d'Alene Dispatch Electrical Improvements, which involves disconnecting and relocating the 3 previously installed power poles to feed existing desks and reroute power circuits from panel to ceiling space and down power poles. North American Industry Classification (NAICS): 238210 Electrical equipment and appliance Installation. Size Standard is $19.0 M. Set Aside: 100% small business set-aside. Project Magnitude –$25,000 - $100,000 Payment Bonds (FAR 52.228-15): required at 100% of original contract price. Provide quote by: 12:00 PM, Mountain Time on April 20, 2026. For solicitation questions email the Administrative Contracting Officer Daniel Franco at daniel.franco@usda.gov. Questions are due no later than April 10, 2026, at 12:00 PM Mountain Time. This will ensure enough time to respond before the solicitation period ends. Please include the solicitation name and number as the subject line of the email (1240LU26Q0054, Coeur d'Alene Dispatch Electrical Improvements).

## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT ANNOUNCEMENT Navy Supply Command Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound (NAVSUP FLCPS) is posting a Sources Sought notice as a means of conducting market research to identify any interested vendors for the procurement of OEM Kingsbury Inc. - Thrust Bearing Assembly Parts for the USS Blue Ridge, in support of SRF-JRMC. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code assigned to this procurement is 332991, with a small business size standard of 1,250 employees. The results of this market research may contribute to future procurement process decisions. NO feedback or evaluations will be provided regarding any submission against this Sources Sought notice. Please provide responses to this announcement no later than the posted closing date. DESCRIPTION OF REQUESTED SUPPLIES: OEM Kingsbury Inc. - Thrust Bearing Assembly Parts.

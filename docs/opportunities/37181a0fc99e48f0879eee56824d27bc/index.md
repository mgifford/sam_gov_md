- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-03-19 12:15:23.488-04

## Summary

This procurement is for the M11 Small Arms Storage Rack. The proposed solicitation is intended to result in the award of a Single, Firm-Fixed Price, One-Time Buy w/100% Option Contract for the estimated supplies specified in the schedule. The resulting contract will establish pricing to be utilized for the period specified in the schedule. The Government does not intend to use FAR Part 12 for the acquisition. Procurement is competed under Full and Open Competition Procedures using FAR Part 15 – Contracting by Negotiations. Noun: M11 Small Arms Storage Rack NSN: 1095-00-897-8755 P/N: 8429990. The TDP is EXPORT CONTROLLED, Distribution D Joint Certification Program (EXPORT CONTROL): You must be certified under the United States/Canada Joint Certification Program (JCP) (https://www.dla.mil/HQ/LogisticsOperations/Services/JCP/) to be granted access to the technical data (TDP) for this solicitation.

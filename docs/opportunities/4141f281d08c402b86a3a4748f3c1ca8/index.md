## Summary

Revised Description from Original Notice Published : 3-27-26 (See Attachment) This performance-based shelter program is to replicate and enhance existing, successfully developed prototype shelter systems. Building on this proven foundation, the contractor will integrate cutting-edge technologies onto military and commercial shelter, trailer, vehicle and transit case platforms, rapidly addressing urgent requirements for Quick Reaction Capability (QRC) C5ISR solutions. The contractor will be responsible for integrating, delivering, fielding, training on, and demonstrating the CSPI systems, significantly enhancing intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) capabilities for selected programs. This is a follow-on production FAR Based award. A Single -Award Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite- Quantity (IDIQ) will be utilized. Under - Title 10, United States Code (U.S.C.), Section 4022(f) Orders will be issued on a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) and Cost No Fee basis.

## Summary

The Naval Warfare Center Aircraft Division, Lakehurst, NJ awarded a Firm Fixed-Price Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to D. Gillette Industries (CAGE: 68ME8) on 15 April 2026 for the procurement of QEC-NP2000 Prop Dollies to support US Navy E-2D Requirements, FMS E-2D Requirements US Navy C-130 Requirements and USAF C-130 requirements. This procurement was synopsized and solicited under N6833525R0425.

## Summary

Compact assembly consisting of high-pressure pump w/a powerful series-wound motor. Powerful 1/0 kW series wound motor, horizontal with high overload capacity. Spring loaded side support casters Electromagnetic disk brake mounted on motor shaft. Automatically activated brake can be released with the handle in the upright position, allowing the operator to maneuver the truck with the crawl speed switch. Stability Casters) Battery / Charger: (2) 12 V 130 Ah with integrated charger (Maintenance free) Drive Wheel: Polyurethane tires as standard, dia. 9.06 inch x 2.76 inch wide. Load Wheel: Polyurethane tires as standard, dia. 3.23 inch x 3.94 inch wide. Delivery and standard warranty included.

## Summary

In accordance with FAR 6.302-1(a)(2) Department of State, U.S. Consulate General in Guadalajara, intents to award a Sole Source, Firm-fixed price, Purchase Order to the company ESTRATEGIA EN SEGURIDAD PRIVADA Y TRANSPORTE MATOH for the acquisition of Residential Alarm Services, which includes installing security alarm systems, providing 24/7 monitoring services, emergency repair and alarm notification as well as other type of security services. This is not a request for competitive quotations. However, interested parties may submit their capabilities and qualifications for future requirements in writing to the email address: GDLGSOProcurement@state.gov

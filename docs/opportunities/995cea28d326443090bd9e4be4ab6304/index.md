# M106 Canister Bodies

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 13:22:57.736-04
- Solicitation Number: 030840

## Summary

The Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) hereby issues Synopsis W519TC-26-Q-0021 for the procurement of 17,500 each of Canister Bodies for the M106 Grenade. This requirement is expected to result in a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract to the offeror whose quote is in compliance and adheres to all the terms and conditions of the solicitation with the lowest total evaluated price. This is for the procurement of a non-commercial item, Canister Bodies, under NAICS 322219 -- Other Paperboard Container Manufacturing. The Canister Bodies must be in accordance with attachment 0001 Drawing 13-19-1011C dated 4 March 2026. First Article Testing (FAT) will be required at a quantity of ten (10) each. FAT samples shall be delivered 30 days after contract award. All attachments will be linked to the forthcoming solicitation W519TC-26-R-0021. Inspection and Acceptance/Delivery of Canister Bodies FAT and production quantity will be done at Destination. US Army, Pine Bluff Arsenal Central Shipping Receiving Point (CSRP) Bldg 53-990, 506th Street Pine Bluff, AR 71602 The Point of Contract for this action is Austin Acree, at austin.g.acree.civ@army.mil

# GRSM Recreation Building at LeConte Lodge Demoliti

- Agency: INTERIOR, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 14:36:06.477-04
- Solicitation Number: 140P5326R0008

## Summary

The National Park Service (NPS) is issuing a combined synopsis/solicitation for services required to demolish the recreation building from the Mt. LeConte Lodge area. Location of Recreation building is on Mt. LeConte. This site is a remote location and limited access by way of trails. Airlift services are anticipated for Contractors mobilization of equipment and materials, as well as the removal of demolished materials and demobilization. TYPE OF CONTRACT: Firm Fixed Price This proposed acquisition will be 100% small business set-aside procurement. The NAICS code is 238910 Site Preparation Contractors $19M Period of Performance 4/15/2026 to 8/1/2026 SYSTEM FOR AWARD MANAGEMENT (SAM): Federal Acquisition Regulations require that federal contractors register in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at https://www.sam.gov and enter all mandatory information into the system. Award cannot be made until the contractor has registered. Interested Offerors are encouraged to ensure that they are registered in SAM prior to submitting their quote. THESE REGISTRATIONS WILL BE COMPLETED BEFORE TIME OF AWARD. Site Visit: Offerors are highly encouraged to perform site visit at any time to inspect the site and fully understand the requirement and conditions. Vendor Questions cut-off date: March 20, 2026, 5:00 EST send via-email to Ashley_Warcewicz@nps.gov Request for Proposals closes April 10, 2026, 5:00PM EST send via-email all proposals to Ashley_Warcewicz@nps.gov The contracting office will not provide hardcopies or email copies of solicitation. No Phone calls, please.

# USDA-RD Finance Office Support

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-24 10:07:12.242-04
- Solicitation Number: 12C0BA26Q0003

## Summary

The United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) Rural Development (RD) Finance Office (FO) is issuing this request for information (RFI) to solicit feedback from industry and potential sources related to USDA RD FO’s need for support with report generation, process automation, process improvement, and data analysis for FO responsibilities like Budget Formulation, Budget Execution, Credit Reform Act of 1990 Activities, and Accounting. USDA RD FO expects to award a multi-year Blanket Purchase Agreement (BPA) to one or more sources for this initiative.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

## Summary

The Statement of Work (SOW) defines the requirements for the Contractor to engineer, furnish, install, and test (EFI&T) the upgrade of premise wiring infrastructure to Cat-6 for the building’s 246 (B246) and 414 (B414) at Columbus AFB, MS. The Contractor shall also be required to remove existing outdated premise wiring in this building.

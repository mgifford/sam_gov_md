# Colorado Springs Airtanker Electrical Project

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 14:36:54.986-04
- Solicitation Number: 1240LT26Q0043

## Summary

Solicitation 1240LT26Q0043 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for supplying all labor, materials, equipment and miscellaneous items for the purpose of installing electrical power to the loading pit(s) at the Colorado Springs Airtanker Base. Electrical power is needed for micro motions meters which are required by policy for the loading of retardant on the agency contracted air tankers and all electrical work needs to be weatherproof due to outdoor exposure. Refer to the Statement of work for the full electrical project requirements. See the attached Solicitation and applicable documents for full project details and requirements. NOTE: A site Visit will be held on March 26th, 2026. Refer to Solicitation for details and requirements.

# Non Clinical Case Management

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-10 10:01:06.256-04
- Solicitation Number: W912LN26RA013

## Summary

This is a Pre-Solicitation Notice for the VTARNG / USPFO-VT, Purchasing and Contracting, to provide Non-Clinical Case Management Services. The full solicitation will be posted on the Contract Opportunities website at www.sam.gov on or about 26 April 2026.The contract type is anticipated to be a Firm Fixed Price and will consist of one (1) four month base period from 12 JUL 26 - 11 Nov 26, one (12) month option period from 12 NOV - 26 - 11 NOV 27, and one (1) eight month option period from 12 NOV 27 - 11 JUL 28 year This acquisition is 100% set-aside for Service Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business (SDVOSB) concerns. The applicable NAICS code for this requirement is 541611, Administrative Management and General Management Consulting Services with a Small Business Size Standard of $24,500,000.The VTARNG Office of the Deputy State Surgeon has a requirement to provide Non-Clinical Case Management Services for VTARNG service members (SM) thereby promoting fitness and personal wellness for operational readiness and deployability. Non-Clinical case Managers coordinate all aspects of the care of individual patients. They ensure proper utilization of services and resources and provide assistance within, between, and outside of VTARNG facilities. The contractor shall provide services during the normal duty day, typically between the hours of 0600-1700, Monday through Friday, except on recognized US holidays or when the Government facility/installation is closed due to local or national emergencies, administrative closings, or similar Government-directed facility/installation closings. Performance shall be at Green Mountain Armory located at 789 Vermont National Guard RD BLDG 7 Colchester, VT 05446. Contractors must be registered in the System for Award Management (SAM) at www.sam.gov.

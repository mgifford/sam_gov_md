# 41Px Kit A Modernized Electronic Warfare (EW) Modification

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 09:24:47.163-04
- Solicitation Number: N0001926F0301

## Summary

The Joint Strike Fighter (JSF) Joint Program Office (JPO) at Arlington, VA announces its intention to procure, on a Sole Source basis, the services necessary to provide the Mods and Retrofits program with material associated with furnishing kits for 41Px Kit A for the Naval Air Systems Command (NAVAIR). These kits will be furnished by LM Aero of Fort Worth, TX under a Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) N0001924G0010 under a new delivery order N0001926F0301. The delivery order will be Firm-Fixed-Price with a delivery schedule from date of award until 31 March 2032.

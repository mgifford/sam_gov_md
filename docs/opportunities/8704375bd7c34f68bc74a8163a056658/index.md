# DRAFT Solicitation for Feedback - LOCK AND DAM 25 NEW 1,200 FT LOCK NAVIGATION AND ECOSYSTEM SUSTAINABILITY PROGRAM (NESP)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 09:08:39.276-04
- Solicitation Number: W912P926RA004

## Summary

DRAFT SOLICITATION FOR PUBLIC FEEDBACK -------- Feedback shall be provided via email to NESPLock25Project@usace.army.mil with the subject line: DRAFT Solicitation Feedback - W912P926RA004 - NESP LD25 1200 ft Lock --------- The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), St. Louis District, has an upcoming solicitation for constructing a new 1,200 ft Lock for Lock and Dam 25. Lock and Dam 25 is located approximately 61.5 river miles upstream from St. Louis and 241.5 miles above the mouth of the Ohio River. USACE is requesting review and feedback from interested vendors by the response date and time of this notice to be considered prior to the actual solicitation. (NOTE: The instruction to request the Plans is within the Solicitation at the top of the List of Drawing Sheets on page 4.)

# Dover AFB - Catholic Education Coordinator

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 15:20:50.678-04
- Solicitation Number: FA449726Q0012

## Summary

The Dover Air Force Base Chapel has a requirement for a Catholic Religious Education Director. The selected contractor shall be responsible for the management and instruction of the religious education program for all age groups, in coordination with the base Chaplain. Market research, in the form of a previously posted sources sought notice, has concluded. This effort identified at least three (3) qualified sources capable of meeting the requirement. Based on these findings, the contracting officer intends to proceed with an oral solicitation to these identified sources.

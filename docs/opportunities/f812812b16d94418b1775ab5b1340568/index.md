# Cemetery Grounds Maintenance Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 15:48:38.663-04
- Solicitation Number: PANAPG-26-P-0000-042954

## Summary

Services include full maintenance of the cemetery and surrounding grounds, such as landscaping, tree trimming and removal, weed control, fertilization, and cleaning of headstones. Additional duties include maintaining pathways, parking areas, fencing and site features; trash removal; excavation for interments; mowing, aeration, overseeding; headstone alignment and installation; and overall grounds beautification.

## Summary

The contractor shall provide Brief 101 modules, Introductory Concepts, Brief Mapping, Brief Trimming, Minimum Definitions of Success and Application Scenarios. The instructors shall be Brief Belt certified, have 2+ years’ experience in supporting U.S. Government organizations and deliver the training sessions, participant certifications, course materials and Brief Bridge Access.

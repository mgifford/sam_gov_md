## Summary

Contractor shall provide specifications, rough-in drawings, or construction cut sheets for review by architects/contractors/owners and for the use by the necessary trades performing the installation. The vendor will make a complete delivery of all deliverables listed in item 3. contracting technicians will arrive onsite to assemble all acquired sinks. FMS personnel of Martinsburg VAMC will remove the current sinks and install the hardlines for utility services. Upon installation, contracting personnel will validate proper operation of the new sinks per equipment manufacturer instructions. Upon successful installation, the customer (SPS) will then validate the equipment with their SOP. Upon completion of the contractor s validation and customer validation, unit will be in service for operation. Documentation will be provided to SPS and Biomed recording all work done. The warranties on all equipment will not start until the successful installation and VA validation. Vendors will comply with all applicable federal government requirements.

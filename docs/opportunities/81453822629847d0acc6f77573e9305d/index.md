## Summary

The Louis Stokes VA Medical Center, Cleveland, Ohio located at 10701 East Boulevard, Cleveland, Ohio 44106 is seeking qualified contractor support from a QEI-1 certified elevator inspector to provide all labor, materials, equipment, transportation, and supervision necessary to perform Annual, Semi-Annual, (30) Day Follow Up inspections, and a Five (5) Year Full Load Test, on the (19) Traction Elevators and (13) Hydraulic Elevators located at the Wade Park VA Medical Facility and Fisher House. This will be a base plus four (4) Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) contract. The Base Year Period of Performance will be from 5-1-2026 through 4-31-2027. This is a Total Small Business Set aside for Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Businesses. If you qualify as a SDVOSB you must be registered with the SBA. Address pricing on pages 5 & 6. Address VARR 852.211-72 Technical Industry Standards on page 24. Responses must include a printed copy of your ASME - QEI-1 – certification. Address VARR 852.219-73 VA Notice of Total Set-Aside for Certified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses pages 25 - 27. “(iv) The business has been verified for ownership and control pursuant to 38 U.S.C. 8127 and 13 CFR 128, and is certified and listed in the SBA certification database at https://veterans.certify.sba.gov/;” Responses must include a printed copy of your SBA SDVOSB certification. Address VARR 852.219-75 VA Notice of Limitations on Subcontracting—Certificate of Compliance for Services and Construction on pages 28 & 29. Contractor must fill out certification data under paragraph (d) on page 29. Questions must be emailed NLT 03/31/2026 1500 EST to:thomas.bellamy@va.gov. Responses to the RFQ are due NLT 04/10/2026 at 1500 EST; email RFQ responses to: thomas.bellamy@va.gov.

The objective of this project is to replace the existing Tube and Shell heat exchanger bundle Manufactured by Armstrong, (Serial number 539028 Model WSX-67-2) Tube side MAWP, AT 150 PSI, AT 300 F, MDMT 35 F, AT 150 PSI, Shell Side MAWP150 PSI, AT 300F, MDMT 35F, AT 150 PSI, Flo-rite Temp water heater Model 665DW, seral number 7470, at 40-140 F at 63 GPM, water OP 20- 150 PSIG ,Water MAP 200 PSIG, Steam OP 2-15 PSIG, Steam MAP 150 PSIG suggested replacement with new plate and frame heat exchanger manufactured by Spirax Sarco, includes Skid with the Brain - Parallel Single Temperature Heat Exchanger, this is a Package/Bundle model (AEH-6E-PT-DHW-PN) or equivalent that would meet current spec of existing unit in new condition, this project includes the replacement of any worn out or damaged copper lines, piping and fittings with 5 feet radius of the old unit to be replaced to ensure proper fit and operation of the new unit . This project aims to ensure long-term reliability, improve energy efficiency, and maintain optimal heating performance for the facility.

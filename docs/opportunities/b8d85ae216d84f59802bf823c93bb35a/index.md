# DDAG POLYETHYLENE Foam

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-19 08:16:02.152-04
- Solicitation Number: SP330026Q0091

## Summary

Amendment 0001 was issued to extend delivery date from 7 days to 30 days. This Request for Quotation (RFQ) is being issued to establish a firm-fixed-price (FFP) purchase to provide 50 bundles of polyethylene foam for DLA Distribution Albany, GA (DDAG). The Government intends to award one (1) purchase order as a result of this RFQ and will be issued on a Standard Form (SF) 1449. This acquisition is not a Defense Priorities and Allocations System (DPAS) rated procurement. This acquisition is being solicited 100% Service Disabled Veteran owned Small Business Set-Aside (SDVOSB). The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this project is 326150 and the size standard is 750 employees. The Product or Service Code (PSC) for this acquisition is 8135.

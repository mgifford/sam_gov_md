# Side-by-Side UTV - 6 person, Hard Cab, HVAC, w/ Power Dump Box - Genoa, WI

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 15:13:08.554-04
- Solicitation Number: W912ES26QA063

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers - St. Paul District requires a Contractor to supply one side-by-side utility terrain vehicle (SxS UTV) capable of fitting 6 people, with a hard cab, heating/AC, a power dump bed, and a 4,500 lb front mount winch. Full specifications are listed in the RFQ statement of work. Contractor will deliver UTV FOB(D) to Lock and Dam 8 in Genoa, WI. Delivery is desired by 26 May 2026. This solicitation is 100% set-aside for small businesses under NAICS 336999 with an SBA size standard of 1,000 employees. Offerors are requested to submit documentation that the equipment they are offering fully meets requirements (i.e. a spec sheet) and Country of Origin information. Offerors must be fully registered in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) to be eligible for contract award. Offerors are requested to submit their quote documents through PIEE.

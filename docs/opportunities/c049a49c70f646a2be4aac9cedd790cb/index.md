# IDIQ for Columns and QUECHERS kits for FSIS Laboratories

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 13:53:49.205-04
- Solicitation Number: 123A9426Q0015

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 123A9426Q0015 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for solid phase extraction columns, QUECHERS salt kits, and QUECHERS salt packages for all U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA), Food Safety and Inspections Service (FSIS) laboratories. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 334516 - Analytical Laboratory Instrument Manufacturing. The small business size standard is 1,000 employees. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

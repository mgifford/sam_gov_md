- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 22:26:02.517-04

## Summary

Infield dirt for softball fields Field A and Field B on Misawa Air Base. The project includes excavation, drainage/water run off capabilities, new soil delivery, compaction, leveling, old soil removal and disposal, repair/replacement and site clean-up. This a Balance of Payments Program exempt requirement. Responses to RFQ No. FA520526QB043 that are not for construction materials manufactured in Japan will not be considered for award

# A&E IDIQ Sources Sought

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 07:15:27.754-04
- Solicitation Number: FA561326R0004

## Summary

Amendment (01): The purpose of this Sources Sought Amendment (01) is to provide a revised Draft SOW dated 15th April 2026. All changes have been highlighted yellow in the Attachment named "Atch 2 - A-E IDIQ Revised Draft SOW 15 April 2026". Introduction: This notice officially begins dialogue with industry for the re-acquisition of follow-on Architecture and Engineering (A-E) Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contracts in support of the Kaiserslautern Military Community (KMC). The sources sought / request for information is a market research tool used for the purposes of preliminary acquisition planning and does not constitute a Request for Quote (RFQ), Request for Bid (RFB) or Request for Proposal (RFP). The intent of this notice is to identify potential offerors and is not a commitment of the Government for any purpose other than market research. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this announcement or any follow-up requests. Please ensure if you are anticipating submitting a proposal when the soliciation is posted, that your company is registered in SAM.gov. Directions on registering in SAM.gov have been provided in the attachments listed below. Respondents will not be notified of the data collected in result of this market research and any information submitted to this request by respondents is voluntary. Information listed in this sources sought / request for information is subject to change and updates will be posted as more information becomes available. Please send any responses to any of the attachments listed below in PDF format via Email, to both the following individuals: Ms.Sibel Wright, Contracting Officer, sibel.wright@us.af.mil Ms. Fatima Berlitz, Contract Specialist, fatima.berlitz.de@us.af.mil Requirement Description: The 86th Civil Engineering Squadron (86th CES) has a requirement for follow-on A-E IDIQ contract. It is anticipated the government will award to no more than eight (8) Firm-Fixed Price (FFP), IDIQ contracts, each with a base ordering period of 5 year and one (1) 5 year option period. The estimated maxium contract value othe IDIQ is $59,000,000. These IDIQ contracts will support the 86th CES located at Ramstein AB, Germany. All work will consist of A-E services for facilities and geographically separated units (GSU) and other locations supported by the KMC. Work is anticipated to be performed will be within the North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Codes of 541330 Engineering Services, 541310 Architectural (except landscape) services, and 541320, Landscape Architectural Services.

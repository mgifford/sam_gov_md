# Invitation to Bid for Miscellaneous Non Ferrous Scrap Metals (Roll-Offs) at Naval Base San Diego

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 14:06:42.454-04
- Solicitation Number: S010-26JA

## Summary

Good afternoon, The Navy Region Southwest Qualified Recycling Program (QRP) is issuing an invitation to bid on the sale of Miscellaneous Non-Ferrous Scrap Metals located at Naval Base San Diego (San Diego, CA). This is a Government Sale. If interested, please open the attached forms for information and instructions on how to submit your bid. Bids must be sent to our office using the bid sheet form provided with this email. Bid sheet must be sent in ink or typed, clearly legible, and signed by the person sending the bid. Bids are due no later than 12 P.M. (Pacific Time) March 24th, 2026. If you need pictures, please contact our office. If you have any questions regarding this bid, please contact our office at 619-556-9475 or email questions to cnrsw-qrp-sales@us.navy.mil.

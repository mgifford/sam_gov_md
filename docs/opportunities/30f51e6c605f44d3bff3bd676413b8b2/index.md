## Summary

Repair Industrial Water Pump Control Unit in Water Pump Station Bldg. 102-6 at Sagami General Depot. The contractor shall provide all necessary labor, personnel, supervision, management, tools, equipment, transportation, materials, and any other items or services necessary to repair industrial water pump control unit for water pump station in building (Bldg) 102-6, Sagami General Depot (SGD).

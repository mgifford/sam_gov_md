## Summary

This requirement is for firm-fixed-price recreation site cleaning services for RSFO Recreation Sites. The contractor shall provide all labor, supplies, supervision, tools, materials, equipment, and transportation necessary to clean and maintain designated recreation facilities. Work includes maintaining vault toilets, picnic tables, fire rings/grills, and surrounding site areas at White Mountain Petroglyphs, Killpecker Sand Dunes, and Three Patches Day Use to ensure the sites remain clean, sanitary, safe, and presentable for public use.

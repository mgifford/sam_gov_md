# SIEMENS industrial control hardware and associated equipment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 10:01:32.575-04
- Solicitation Number: N6449826Q5103

## Summary

The Naval Surface Warfare Center - Philadelphia Division (NSWCPLD) Philadelphia, PA site intends to solicit on a brand name basis for SIEMENS industrial control hardware and associated equipment. The items being procured consist of SIEMENS ET200-SP series Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), SIEMENS IE/PB Link Protocol Conversion Gateways, and supporting hardwired modules for use with the ET200-SP PLC. The part numbers, descriptions, and quantities are as follows: Part Number Description Qty 6ES7-510-1DK03-0AB0 ET200SP CPU 1510SP-1 PN 130 6ES7-131-6TF00-0CA0 Digital input module NAMUR, 8 channel 170 6ES7-132-6BF01-0BA0 Digital output module, 8 channel 60 6ES7-132-6GD51-0BA0 Relay output module, 2 channel 20 6ES7-134-6FB00-0BA1 Analog input module, voltage, 2 channel 30 6ES7-134-6GB00-0BA1 Analog input module, current 2/4 wire, 2 channel 480 6ES7-134-6JD00-0CA1 Analog input module, RTD, 2 channel 25 6ES7-135-6GB00-0BA1 Analog output module, current, 2 channel 10 6ES7-137-6AA01-0BA0 Serial module, RS422/485 5 6ES7-193-6BP00-2DA0 Base module for ET200SP, BU type A0+D2, unit count 10 80 6GK1-411-5AB10 IE/Profibus gateway 87 All units of all items will be awarded to one offeror. Offers, therefore, must be on the basis of furnishing all units of all items to the specification(s) detailed in this solicitation. The government anticipates award of a firm fixed priced contract from this solicitation. The resulting contract will be awarded to the low priced offeror who is deemed responsible in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 9, as supplemented, whose proposal conforms to the solicitation’s requirements (to include all stated terms, conditions, representations, certifications, and all other information required by evaluation factors of this request for proposal) and based on the evaluation factors is considered technically acceptable. Technical acceptability is determined by the offeror demonstrating the material/service being provided meets the solicitation requirements. The low offer will be analyzed to determine if the offeror’s proposed price is fair and reasonable utilizing price analysis techniques in accordance with the guidelines in FAR 12.204. NOTE: All supplies must be new and factory sealed. Documentation that supplies are from an authorized distributor is required. This notice shall not be construed as a contract, a promise to contract, or as a commitment of any kind by the Government. The Government is NOT seeking or accepting unsolicited proposals The anticipated date of RFQ posting is around May 2026.

# Hawaii Radio Advertising Campaign

- Posted: 2026-04-01 21:02:42.597-04

## Summary

Non competitive award for Hawaii Air National Guard (HIANG) Radio Advertising Campaign on the Islands of Hawaii, Maui and Oahu. Pacific Radio Group, Inc. remains the sole source capable of fulfilling the HIANG's Recruiting and Retention's minimum requirements, as no other suppliers can offer a comparable solution of advertising on the Islands of Hawai'i, Maui, and Oahu to the target demographic (18-30 year olds).

# Classroom Fixed Seating

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 07:07:33.803-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8S26QA003

## Summary

Project Overview: Total Quantity: 33 seats. Location: 180th Fighter Wing, Swanton, OH. Required Services: Full-service project including the removal and disposal of existing chairs and the professional installation of new seating. Project Sequence: The work will proceed by removing old chairs, allowing for carpet replacement by others, followed by the final installation of the new chairs. Product Specifications: We are seeking fixed seating solutions with the following features: Mounting: Floor-mounted with powder-coated steel standards. Construction: Durable plastic outer shell for the back and seat pan. Comfort: Upholstered inner back and seat cushions. Writing Surface: Integrated folding tablet arm with a plastic laminate top. Quality: Standard commercial-grade finishes and construction.

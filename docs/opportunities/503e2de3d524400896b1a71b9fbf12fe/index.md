# NAVSEA Base Inspector Program Training Certification

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 10:54:38.809-04
- Solicitation Number: N5523626Q0041

## Summary

Southwest Regional Maintenance Center (SWRMC), Code 410A Procurement, intends to award a sole source requirement to Association for Materials Protection and Performance (AMPP) to provide NAVSEA BASIC PAINT INSPECTION (NBPI) Training and Certification. The requirement will be processed in accordance with FAR part 12. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 611430 - Professional and Management Development Training. This notice is neither a request nor a solicitation of offers; however, all information received after date of publication of this synopsis will be reviewed by the Government for future considerations. Information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether conducting a competitive procurement is in the best interest of the Government. Responses must provide clean and concise documentation indicating a vendor's bonafide capability to provide this product or equivalency. Requests must include company name, address, telephone number of the requester, e-mail address, Cage code, and business size. All information should be delivered via e-mail to silvana.miranda.civ@us.navy.mil and dayen.lagunas.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 11:00 AM (PST), 06 April 2026. Certifications in the System for Award Management must be current and active to be eligible for contract award.

# DOT/Maritime Mixed Metals Term Contract

- Agency: GENERAL SERVICES ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 11:08:11.839-04
- Solicitation Number: 2-1-QSC-T-26-052

## Summary

Invitation for Bids (IFB) 2-1-QSC-T-26-052 DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap Term Contract U.S. Department of Transportation – Maritime Administration (MARAD) Hampton Roads Region, Virginia The U.S. General Services Administration (GSA) is issuing this Invitation for Bids (IFB) for the sale and recurring removal of Government-generated DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap located at facilities operated by the U.S. Department of Transportation Maritime Administration (MARAD) across the Hampton Roads region of Virginia, including Norfolk, Newport News, Portsmouth, and Williamsburg. This solicitation establishes a term contract for the sale of scrap property, with removal required as a condition of sale. The successful bidder shall remove scrap material on a recurring, as-needed basis in coordination with the Agency Government Representative (AGR), and in accordance with all site-specific operational, maritime, and security requirements. OVERVIEW Commodity: DEMIL A Mixed Metals Scrap Locations: Multiple MARAD facilities (Hampton Roads Region, VA) Contract Type: Term Contract (3-year base with up to two 1-year options) Estimated Volume: Approximately 60,000 pounds annually (not guaranteed) Disposal Requirement: Material must be recycled for material content only SOLICITATION ACCESS This solicitation is available for review and bidding on SAM.gov and GSA Auctions. GSA Auctions Link: https://gsaauctions.gov/auctions/preview/359615 All solicitation documents, including the IFB, Surveillance Plan, and Appendices, are located in the Documents section of the GSA Auctions listing. SUBMISSION INFORMATION Submission Method: Email (sealed bid submission) Bid Submission Deadline: May 8, 2026 – 5:00 PM Central Time Bid Opening: May 12, 2026 – Time provided via email notification KEY REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY) MANDATORY Pre-Bid Site Inspection Required (IFB Section 3.3) Submission must include: Appendix A – Site Verification & Bidder Certification Appendix B – Bid Sheet & SF 114 Appendix C – Container Execution Narrative Pricing is based on a percentage of a published market index Certified weight tickets are required; contractor-owned scales are not permitted Performance Guarantee of $2,500 required post-award All amendments must be acknowledged Failure to meet these requirements may result in the bid being deemed non-responsive. SITE ACCESS / SECURITY MARAD facilities are secure maritime and federal installations.

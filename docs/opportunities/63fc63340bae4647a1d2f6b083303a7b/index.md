# Market Research for Long Range Fires Capabilities

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-15 10:59:05.39-04
- Solicitation Number: 2026-L-0001

## Summary

The Department of War's Strategic Capabilities Office (SCO), specifically the Long Range Fires (LRF) Portfolio, is inviting industry partners to showcase capabilities for countering near-peer adversaries. We are seeking game-changing technologies that are at a prototype stage or ready for near-term demonstration. We strongly encourage responses from both traditional and non-traditional defense organizations. Scope of Interest: This request focuses on tangible systems ready for evaluation, including: Offensive and Defensive Prototypes Kinetic and Non-Kinetic Applications Adaptations of existing or emerging commercial technologies ready for demonstration Vetting Process: This preliminary notice serves to identify and vet interested parties with demonstrable solutions. The full Request for Information (RFI) will be provided only to vetted respondents. This process will also establish a secure channel for any necessary classified discussions related to prototype evaluation and demonstration. To express interest and begin the vetting process, please email LRF-RFI@SCO.MIL with the following information: Company Name Company Unique Entity ID (UEI) number Company CAGE Code Company Point of Contact (Full Legal Name) POC Title POC Full SSN OR POC Citizenship Status POC Unclassified Email Address POC Unclassified Phone Number If all requested items are not provided, no further RFI details will be released.

## Summary

Requirement: Replace-in-kind approximately fifty (50) linear feet of corroded/degraded 2” gauge liquid natural gas (LNG) line extending from the city meter to the emergency generator. This work is located at the USCG Station Morro Bay, CA 93442. The facility is located at USCG STATION MORRO BAY - 1279 Embarcadero, Morro Bay, CA 93442 Required Bidding Documents Please reference the entire Scope of Work Document and provide in your bid: 1) full detailed pricing schedule, separating labor and materials 2) license(s) to perform work in California 3) past performance of similar jobs, if available Project Representatives: Owen Gibson (owen.m.gibson@uscg.mil) Jack Fielden (Jack.A.Fielden@uscg.mil) Contracting Officer: Ryan H. King (ryan.h.king2@uscg.mil) Evaluation under FAR 52.212-2 Evaluation—Commercial Products and Commercial Services: (a) The Government will award a contract resulting from this solicitation to the responsible offeror whose offer conforming to the solicitation will be most advantageous to the Government, price and other factors considered. TECHNICAL & PAST PERFORMANCE, DELIVERY SCHEDULE, AND PRICE Technical and past performance, when combined with delivery schedule, are more important than price alone.

# CROWN - FC5715-40 4,000 LB. CAPICITY, 4-WHEEL, Electric SIT-DOWN COUNTERBALANCE TRUCK

## Summary

This description summarizes the requirements for a high-performance, electric sit-down forklift and its supporting infrastructure to ensure a competitive and fair bidding process. Equipment and Performance Specifications Vehicle Type: 4-wheel electric sit-down counterbalanced forklift with a minimum 4,000 lb. lifting capacity. Mast Assembly: Triple-stage mast with a maximum lift height of approximately 188", a collapsed height of 83", and a minimum free lift of 32". Hydraulics and Attachment: Integrated high-visibility sideshifting fork positioner. 48" standard forks on a 38" wide carriage. Two-function auxiliary hydraulics (4-lever/spool) with internal carriage plumbing. Drivetrain and Steering: AC traction and hydraulic motors with on-demand electric steering and a specialized cooling package for high-demand (dock) performance. Tires: Non-marking lugged cushion rubber tires on both drive and steer axles. Power System and Charging Battery: 36V Lead Acid battery (min. 1100AH) equipped with a wireless battery monitoring system (e.g., WiiQ or equivalent) to track health and cycles. Charger: High-efficiency, 3-phase high-frequency charger (208/240V) compatible with the specified battery and monitoring system. Configuration: Lift-out battery removal with standard door retainers and safety cutoff switches. Ergonomics and Safety Operator Compartment: Full-suspension vinyl seat with high-visibility orange seatbelt, integrated armrest with fingertip bionic controls, and dual square plastic mirrors. Safety Features: Smart reverse travel alarm, forward and reverse blue floor spotlights, and LED front worklights mounted to the overhead guard. Accessories: Includes an operator cooling fan and "Work Assist" storage bins/trays. Fleet Management and Telematics System: Integrated fleet management hardware with a minimum 7” touchscreen display. Subscription: 60-month service contract for wireless monitoring, impact sensing, and operator checklists. The vendor is responsible for all initial activation and service fees associated with the 5-year term. Logistics and Support Delivery: Inclusive of freight to Oklahoma City, OK, local dealer delivery, and professional assembly. Compliance: Units must undergo a comprehensive "Pre-Flight" dealer inspection and safety test prior to handover.

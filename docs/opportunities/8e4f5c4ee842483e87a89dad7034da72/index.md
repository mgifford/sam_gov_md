# K-6 Learning Portfolio

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-13 12:25:14.794-04
- Solicitation Number: HE125426QE029

## Summary

K-6 Learning Portfolio The Contractor shall commercial off-the-shelf, 100% cloud-based digital art portfolio to capture K-6 student work in elementary art classes systemwide. The tool allows students and teachers to collect, store, share, and assess student work. DoWEA requires virtual professional learning for K-6 teachers. In addition to the K-6 students, K-6 staff and the students’ parents/sponsors also have access to the platform. Note: Attachments identified in this solicitation contain requirements that are integral to the solicitation and must be addressed by Offerors, as applicable.

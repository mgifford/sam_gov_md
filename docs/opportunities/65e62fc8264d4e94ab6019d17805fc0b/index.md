# Emergency MEDEVAC Under BPA HT940222A0001

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-04-22 12:03:27.2-04
- Solicitation Number: JA-FY26-0627

## Summary

The Defense Health Agency (DHA), DHA Contracting Activity (DHACA), Managed Care Contracting Division (MC-CD), awarded a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Task Order under Blanket Purchase Agreement HT9402-22-A-0001 to provide an emergency medical evacuation of a DoD civilian from OCONUS to CONUS. This BPA Call/Task Order was issued as a sole-source action accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 6.302-2, Unusual and Compelling Urgency. The period of performance and evacuation took place on February 8, 2026. See the attached Justification and Approval.

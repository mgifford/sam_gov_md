# AIR Card® Information Session for POS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-08 11:27:35.47-04

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency Energy (DLA Energy) will host an AIR Card® Information Session for POS teams / AIR Card® Processors ONLY. Upcoming changes on the AIR Card® Program will be discussed and POS teams / AIR Card® Processors will have the opportunity to provide feedback to DLA Energy and voice their concerns and questions about the new changes under the AIR Card® Program. When: April 13th, 2026, starting at 9:30AM EST to 11:30AM EST Where: This will be a virtual event, credentials will be provided closer to the event. Registration is required What: Topics to be discussed include: AIR Card® invoicing procedures, Fully Itemized Price and signed delivery ticket. Who: AIR Card® POS teams and Processors ONLY

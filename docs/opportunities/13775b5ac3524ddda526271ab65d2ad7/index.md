# Ready to Eat Cereal Products for Domestic Food Assistance

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-03 15:06:15.006-04
- Solicitation Number: 12-3J14-26-B-0209

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Proposals are being requested and separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 12-3J14-26-R-0209 is issued as a Request for Proposal (RFP) for Ready to Eat (RTE) cereal for domestic feeding programs. This acquisition is not set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 311230, manufacturing of oat, mixed grain, and breakfast cereal. The small business size standard is 1,300 employees or less. This acquisition is an unrestricted requirement. All responsible sources may submit a proposal which will be considered by the agency.

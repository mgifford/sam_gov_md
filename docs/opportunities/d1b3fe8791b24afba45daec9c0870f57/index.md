# SM-3 Sustaining Engineering and Product Support IDIQ Ceiling Increase

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-18 17:19:43.429-04
- Solicitation Number: 26-MDA-12312
- Awardee: RAYTHEON COMPANY Tucson AZ 85756 USA
- Award Amount: 11742110000.00

## Summary

Raytheon, Tucson, Arizona is being awarded a noncompetitive overall ceiling increase of $8,410,280,000 to a previously awarded indefinite-delivery/indefinite-quantity (IDIQ) contract (HQ0851-21-D-0001). The value of the IDIQ ceiling is increased from $3,331,830,000 to $11,742,110,000. Under this modification, the contractor will provide the management, material, and services associated with the sustaining engineering and product support services for the Standard Missile-3 Block missile variants for the U.S. and Foreign Military Sales partners. The work will be performed in Tucson, Arizona and Huntsville, Alabama. The performance period is from Oct. 30, 2020 through Oct 29, 2029. The Missile Defense Agency, Huntsville, Alabama, is the contracting activity.

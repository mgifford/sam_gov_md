# AFP for Exploration Park at JSC

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 19:27:29.042-04
- Solicitation Number: AFP-JSC-EXP-2026

## Summary

This Announcement for Proposals (AFP) solicits proposals for lease of all or a portion of available undeveloped land at NASA Johnson Space Center (JSC). (Attachment A, Land Map; hereinafter the Available Land) The Available Land is located outside of the controlled access area of JSC and adjacent to JSC’s main campus. The size of the total parcel is approximately 183.7 acres and is accessible by Saturn Lane. This zone may be sub-divided with potential for phasing and expansion. This locality has potential for separation of facilities if commercial entities have privacy concerns. The Available Land will be available at award in its current condition for one 20-year base period and two 20-year option period(s). Key Dates: Site Visit : 18 February (Requests must be received NLT 16 February) Q&A for submitted questions must be received NLT 23 February AFP closes 30 April AFP UPDATED ON 2/11/26 TO REFLECT THE FOLLOWING: AFP CLOSE DATE EXTENDED TO 15 May 2026 AFP UPDATED ON 3/31/26 TO REFLECT THE FOLLOWING: UPLOADED Q&A RESPONSES AND UTILITY PATH

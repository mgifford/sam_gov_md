## Summary

NOTICE: This market survey is being conducted to identify potential sources that may possess the expertise, capabilities, and experience to meet the requirements for qualification and resources to provide us with Matrix Array Ultrasonic Testing NDI System for use by United States Air Force (USAF) personnel. The level of security is unclassified only and documents involved are no higher than Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI). Contractors/Institutions responding to this market research are placed on notice that participation in this survey may not ensure participation in future solicitations or contract awards. The government will not reimburse participants for any expenses associated with their participation in this survey. Description of Matrix Array Ultrasonic Testing NDI System The Government is conducting market research to identify potential sources that possess the expertise, capabilities, and experience to meet qualification requirements for Matrix Array Ultrasonic Testing NDI System for Tinker Air Force Base (AFB). The intent is for the Government to purchase one Matrix Array Ultrasonic Testing NDI System with training at the Oklahoma City–Air Logistics Complex (OC-ALC).

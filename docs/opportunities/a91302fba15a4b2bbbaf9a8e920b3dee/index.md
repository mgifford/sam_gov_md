# Ochoco National Forest Planting Supplies -Tiller Netting, Rigid seedling tubes

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 15:15:45.474-05
- Solicitation Number: 1240BG26Q0006

## Summary

400,000 feet of Tiller Netting and 200,000 rigid seedling tubes. Desired products are described in the product characteristics below: Tiller Netting Lightweight, all-purpose netting for protection against deer, rabbits, and rodents. Non-oriented elastic netting that expands as the seedling grows, to prevent restriction to lateral growth. Product must biodegrade over a period of 12-18 months. Must be delivered in rolls of 5,000 feet or more and allow for loading/unloading with a forklift. 4" x 18" rigid seedling tube Will also accept 4� x 20� or 4� x 24� seedling tubes, although 4� x 18� is the preferable size. Rigid tubes constructed of polyethylene and polypropylene material. Tubes must have diamond mesh. These tubes prevent browse damage from wildlife and cattle if properly installed. Product must photo degrade when exposed to sunlight over a period of 3-5 years. Must be delivered in boxes of 250-500 and secured on pallets to allow for loading/unloading with a forklift.

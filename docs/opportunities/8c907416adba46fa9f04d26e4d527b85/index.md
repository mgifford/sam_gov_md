## Summary

The task/delivery order bridge is to provide subject matter expertise to support the planning, development, and delivery of uninterrupted multinational CFBLNet services and capabilities for a global user base. CFBLNet is a critical component of the Mission Partner Environment (MPE) which includes networks and projects of the Mission Partners Capabilities Office (MPCO) technical services portfolio.

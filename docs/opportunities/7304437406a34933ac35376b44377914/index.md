# Solid and Medical Waste Pick Up

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 18:02:13.504-04
- Solicitation Number: 75H71526Q00014

## Summary

The Contractor shall furnish all personnel, equipment, supplies, facilities, transportation, tools, materials, supervision, and other items necessary to perform services as described in the PWS. The contractor will provide and deliver an 8-yard dumpster for use. It will be placed in the fenced area in the rear of the clinic. The contractor will empty the contents of the dumpster on a weekly basis.

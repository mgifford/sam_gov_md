# Notice of Intent to Sole Source MCSATS Repair/Modernization/Upgrade

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-20 11:25:16.505-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8C26SS0002

## Summary

The USPFO for Nevada, 152 MSG/MSC Office is to award a firm-fixed price contract on a sole source basis to SHOOTING RANGE INDUSTRIES LLC, dba MILO Live (Cage: 6VM39). The statutory authority which permits other than full and open competition for this acquisition is 10 U.S.C. § 3204(a)(1) or 41 U.S.C. 1901 for only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements, as implemented by RFO FAR 12.102(b), Table 12-1. Therefore, a Request for Quotation (RFQ) solicitation from a single source will be issued to SHOOTING RANGE INDUSTRIES LLC to provide comprehensive modernization of the existing shooting range. This project requires the contractor to manage all aspects of the upgrade, including providing all labor, tools, materials, and transportation. The target systems and Control Computer, which are proprietary to the manufacturer, MILO Live. The project also encompasses significant infrastructure upgrades, including roofing, repairing dimmable lighting system, and ensuring HVAC system operations. The contractor will be responsible for complete installation services, system testing, operational training, and all site logistics, including power coordination and waste disposal, making MILO Live the only vendor capable of meeting these integrated and specific requirements for the Nevada Air National Guard (NVANG). This acquisition is unrestricted. The designated North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 332311, Prefabricated Metal Building and Component Manufacturing, with a size standard of 750 employees. Please note that this notice is not a Request for Quotations (RFQ); however, any response to this notice must show clear, compelling, and convincing evidence that competition will be advantageous to the Government. Responsible parties who believe they can responsibly meet the requirements are invited to submit in writing a complete information describing their ability to provide the product/services no later than 1000 AM Pacific Daylight Time, 27 March 2026. Interest/capability statements may be sent to Alex Norris at alex.norris@us.af.mil and Wendy Cano at wendy.cano_perez@us.af.mil. No telephone responses will be accepted. No solicitation document is available for this requirement. The U.S. government will not pay for any information or administrative cost incurred in response to this Notice. All costs associated with responding to this Notice will be solely at the expense of the interested party. A determination not to compete with the proposed requirement based upon the responses to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government.

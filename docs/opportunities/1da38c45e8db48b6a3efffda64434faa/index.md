## Summary

PRE-SOLICITATION SYNOPSIS General Information Solicitation Number: W912BU26QA017 (Note: listed as W912BU26RA017 in the SSP, but updated to match the SF1449) Project Title: NAVY MISSION SUPPORT SATOC - Mission Critical Facilities and Equipment Support Agency: U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE), Philadelphia District Action Code: Pre-Solicitation Set-Aside: 100% Small Business NAICS Code: 561210 – Facilities Support Services (Size Standard: $47,000,000) Product Service Code (PSC): Z1AZ – Maintenance of Other Administrative Facilities Description of Requirement The USACE Philadelphia District intends to issue a Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) Request for Quote/Proposal for a Single Award Task Order Contract (SATOC) to support mission-critical facilities and equipment at the Naval Surface Warfare Center in Bethesda, Maryland (Navy Carderock Division). The Contractor shall provide all supervision, labor, supplies, materials, equipment, and tools necessary for preventative maintenance, inspection, and repairs for systems and equipment within mission-critical buildings. The Contractor will receive service tickets through the service desk to execute all requirements, confirm the operational readiness and performance of critical systems to prevent failures, and support the Base in the event of natural and man-made disasters. Contract Terms Contract Type: Firm-Fixed-Price SATOC Period of Performance: Five-year ordering period (60 months / max 1,825 days) Maximum Contract Capacity: Construction Budget $5,000,000- $10,000,000 Minimum Guarantee: $10,000.00 Synopsis Period: April 21, 2026 – April 24, 2026 (4 Days) Estimated Solicitation Issue Date: On or about April 24, 2026 Estimated Proposal Due Date: May 9, 2026 (15 days from issuance)

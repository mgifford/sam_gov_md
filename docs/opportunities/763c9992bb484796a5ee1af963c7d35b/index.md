# 500 Gallon Water Trailers

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-26 16:09:59.35-04
- Solicitation Number: PANMCC-26-P-0000-039307

## Summary

THIS IS A SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE TO REQUEST INFORMATION ONLY The Mission and Installation Contracting Command (MICC), Fort Benning, Georgia is conducting market research to gain knowledge of potential, qualified sources interested in providing around 20 qty to 45 qty 500 Gallon Water Trailers. This sources sought is issued to conduct Market Research in accordance with the Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 10. Any information submitted by respondents is strictly voluntary. The Government will not be responsible for any cost incurred in response to this Sources Sought Notice. This market research does not restrict the Government to a specific acquisition approach. This acquisition falls under Classification Code F Resources and Conservation, North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code 336212 Truck Trailers Manufacturing. The Water Trailer must be 500 gallons oval straight barrel, no slope and must be made of 10-gauge type 304L stainless steel. These water trailers will be towed with allowable speeds of 80 km/h highway and 48 km/h cross-country. A capability statement that demonstrates the knowledge and ability to perform the requirements as stated in the PWS. The business name, to include address and phone number. The name, phone number and email address of the point of contact. CAGE Code and UEI number. Please send responses to: lashun.m.littleton.civ@army.mil. Please ensure that PANMCC-26-P-0000 039307 is put in the Subject Line of your email response. Responses must be received no later than 10 April 2026 at 1600 hours local time. Please address any questions reference this Sources Sought to:lashun.m.littleton.civ@army.mil

# 6835--Medical Gas and Cylinders Bath VA Pre-Solicitation Notice

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-31 13:56:25.579-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24226R0054
- Awardee: null

## Summary

The VHA, VISN 2, Bath VA Medical Center require a contractor to provide medical gases to the VA medical center facilities. The Contractor shall be responsible for providing, filling, transporting, handling, servicing, maintaining, delivering, picking up and storing medical gases and cylinders for all facilities located within VISN 2, Bath VA Medical Centers catchment area. All equipment and materials required to perform on the contract shall be provided by the Contractor (other than government-furnished property).

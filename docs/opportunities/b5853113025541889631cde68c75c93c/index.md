# PACIFIC ISO CONTAINER SUPPORT REQUIREMENT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-25 20:16:20.485-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE602-26-R-0701

## Summary

This is solicitation SPE602-26-R-0701 for the Pacific ISO Container Services Requirement. The requirement is for the transportation, repair, and maintenance of leased Intermodal (ISO) Bulk Fuel Containers in support of the Department of Defense (DoD) operations in the state of Hawaii. The NAICS code for this procurement is 484230. The Total Period of Performance is April 01, 2026, through March 31, 2031. There will be one (1) base year and four (4) one-year options. The requirement includes five (5) distinct line items: Intermodal Container Transport (To/From JBPHH, To/From Port, and Intra-Island) ISO Container – Integrity Testing ISO Container – Cleaning ISO Container – Maintenance/Repair ISO Container – Inspection DLA Energy is seeking to increase Small Business participation in our solicitations. For this solicitation, 100% is set-aside for small business concerns. If you are a designated small business in accordance with FAR 52.212-1, please ensure your System for Award Management (SAM) designation is correct. Please see attachments for RFP, Attachments, and any subsequent amendments. If you have questions, please email Mr. Jones Hysons at Jones.Hysons@dla.mil and Ms. Birgitta Lapoint at Birgitta.Lapoint@dla.mil.

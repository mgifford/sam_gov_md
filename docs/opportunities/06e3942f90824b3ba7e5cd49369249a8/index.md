## Summary

Combined Synopsis/ Solicitation for Commercial Service 60TH CONTRACTING SQUADRON 350 HANGAR AVE, BLDG. 549 TRAVIS AFB, CA 94535-2632 SUBJECT: Request for quotation (RFQ), FA442726Q0008, Local Telecommunication Service, Travis AFB, CA 1. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products or commercial services prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. 2. Solicitation number FA442726Q0008 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ), for Local Telecommunication Service at Travis AFB. 3. This acquisition is a set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. 4. This is not a Defense Priorities and Allocations System (DPAS) rated acquisition. 5. Discription: The 60th Communications Squadron requires a contractor to provide comprehensive local and long-distance telecommunication services for Travis AFB. The contractor must supply all personnel and materials to ensure reliable, 24/7 service that is fully compatible with the existing government infrastructure. Should a new contractor be selected, they are entirely responsible for planning and managing the transition from the previous provider to guarantee a smooth cutover. 6. Date(s) and place(s) of delivery and acceptance. The performance will take place at Travis AFB, with an anticipated performance period of one base year, four option years and a 6-month extension. 7. See Attachment SF 1449 and corresponding attachments for the full detail of this Solicitation. 8. The date, time, and place for receipt of offer. RFQ Response Deadline: 4 May 2026 12:00 PM PDT. Offers received after the solicitation close date/time will not be accepted unless requested by the CO. Submission Method: Offers must be submitted electronically via email to both Point of Contacts Questions: All questions concerning this solicitation must be submitted to the both Point of Contacts no later than 24 April 2026 12:00 PM PDT. Any inquiry submitted past this deadline will not be accepted. 9. Point of contact. Contract Specialist: Don Salgado, don_phillip.salgado@us.af.mil Contracting Officer: SSgt Alexander Love Gaunt, alexander.love_gaunt@us.af.mil Organization Address: 60th Contracting Squadron (60 CONS), 350 Hangar Avenue, Bldg 549, Travis AFB, CA, 94535 10. This acquisition is a total Small Business Set-Aside. 11. The Product Service Code for this acquisition is DG11 - IT and Telecom—Network: Satellite Communications and Telecom Access Services. The NAICS code for this acquisition is 517111 – Wired Telecommunications Carriers. 12. Description: a. This solicitation details all required tasks, standards, and deliverables. b. Reserved. c. The items are not subject to the WTO GPA and/or a Free Trade Agreement by citing the FAR Clause and Required language shown at FAR 5.101(4)(iii). d. Technical Data: All necessary information is contained within this solicitation and its attachments. e. Reserved. f. All responsible sources may submit a quotation and will be considered. g.

The United States Fish and Wildlife intends to award a firm-fixed-price, purchase order on a sole-source basis to CONSERVATION LOGIC LLC, located at 2800 COTTAGE WAY, ROOM W-2606 SACRAMENTO CA 95825-1846 US, for the purchase of Data review, statistical analyses, and report/manuscript writing for SARP. The Government intends to negotiate with only one (1) source under the authority of FAR subpart 13.106-1(b)(1)(i) and (ii), for purchases not exceeding the Simplified Acquisition Threshold. Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services will Satisfy Agency Requirements.

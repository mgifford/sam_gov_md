# Sources Sought_Front Sight Assembly_W912CH-26-Q-0039

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-23 10:55:51.233-04
- Solicitation Number: W912CH-26-Q-0039

## Summary

Army Contracting Command - Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) is conducting a Market Survey to determine the level of interest among qualified Contractors for the production of Front Sight Assembly, NSN:1005-01-664-9388. Suppliers capable of manufacturing this item must indicate so in response, by email, to Ashley Treier, ashley.s.treier.civ@army.mil. The purpose of this market survey is to identify potential offerors and gather information for planning purposes about capabilities of each industry source as defined in Federal Acquisition Regulation 15.201(e). The input will help ACC-DTA ascertain the interest, capability, and capacity of all potential offerors. The market survey does not constitute a solicitation and shall not be construed as commitment by the Government. No telephone responses will be accepted.

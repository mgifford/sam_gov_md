## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs will be soliciting for vendors that can provide Xenon Surgical Light Source and Accessories for the Salem VA Health Care System, located in Salem VA. A Request for Quotation will be issued on or about 21 April 2026. This is a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Small Business Set-Aside. To be eligible for award, the contractor must be registered in in the SAM database at http://www.sam.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) Code is 339113 Surgical Appliance and Supplies Manufacturing, the Size Standard is 800 employees, and the Product Service (PSC) Code is 6515 Medical and Surgical Instruments, Equipment, and Supplies. Contractors interested in providing this service should monitor Contract Opportunities for the solicitation #36C24626Q0532.

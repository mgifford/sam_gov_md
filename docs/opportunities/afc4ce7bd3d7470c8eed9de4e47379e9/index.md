SUBJECT: Notice of Intent Synopsis NAVSUP-WSS is seeking eligible contractors to furnish effort including labor, material, and facilities as may be required to Evaluate, Repair and/or Modify the items as specified Point of Contact(s): Sarah Haley, Contracting Specialist at sarah.r.haley2.civ@us.navy.mil.

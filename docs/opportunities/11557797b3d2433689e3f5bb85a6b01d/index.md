# 2815 OVERHAUL KIT

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 13:57:18.702-04
- Solicitation Number: N0040626Q0141

## Summary

Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound (FLCPS) intends to solicit for OVERHAUL KIT supplies: OVERHAUL KIT FOR ENGINE, 36SS6V-AM, part number 1P341434KIT. This requirement is sole sourced to Fincantieri Marine Systems North America due to manufacturer DWG No.-36SS6V-AM MDL 2894F01 that was solely developed by the OEM that has proprietary data and information. A determination not to compete this action based upon responses received to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. The Government will evaluate the offeror’s price for reasonableness and past performance as part of the responsibility determination in accordance with RFO 12.203. Drawing, references and TGIs will not be provided or furnished as part of this solicitation due to distribution restrictions. Drawings, references and TGIs are available to quoters listed on the Joint Certification Program (JCP) website. Quoters may request these by providing their CAGE code and DD-2345 for their data custodian. For instructions on how to register for JCP please go to https://www.dla.mil/Logistics-Operations/Services/JCP/ .

Unique characteristics that limit availability to only one source, with the reason no other supplies or services can be used: There are several RMD laser marking devices on the market. However, RMD laser etchers are not universally compatible with all RMD tracking systems. Compatibility must be verified with the RMD tracking systems manufacturer. The CensiMark Laser Etcher is verified by the RMD tracking system manufacturer, Censi Technologies, Inc., as being the only etcher that is compatible with the current NIHCS Censitrac tracking system.

## Summary

13 April 2026- The purpose of this amendment is to set a deadline for questions and update the Contract Specialist Point of Contact (POC). 1) Questions for this requirement are due Wednesday, 15 April 2026, 14:00 (2 PM) MDT. 2) Contract specialist POC is Ms. Juliane Indelicato 6 April 2026- In accordance with DAF Contracting Compass Part 32, section Release of Solicitations in Advance of Funding Availability - Notice to Offeror(s)/Supplier(s): Funds are not presently available for this effort. No award will be made under this solicitation until funds are available. The Government reserves the right to cancel this solicitation, either before or after the closing date. In the event the Government cancels this solicitation, the Government has no obligation to reimburse an offeror for any costs. Services include the range of paramedic, EMT, and ambulance services provided in the local community, including connectivity to area-wide 911 dispatch. Emergent patients will only be transported to the New Mexico Veteran Affairs Health Care Services (NMVAHCS) Emergency Department (ED) except those excluded, (trauma, Pediatrics, Obstetrics, or civilian patients choice), or diverted.

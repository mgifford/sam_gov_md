DRAFT Statement of Work Background The Northern Arizona VA Health Care System (NAVAHCS), located in Prescott, Arizona, requires a secure and reliable point-to-point communication circuit with the Arizona Department of Public Safety (AZ DPS) to access the Criminal Justice Information System (CJIS). This connectivity is mission-critical for law enforcement operations and must preserve CJIS security and integrity. The circuit is mandated by AZ DPS to be delivered via metro ethernet, operated in conjunction with onsite equipment as specified herein, and the service provider must coordinate access consistent with FBI CJIS security requirements. Scope The Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, transport, and services necessary to deliver and maintain the CJIS access circuit and supporting internet port to NAVAHCS Police, in accordance with VA and CJIS security policies. Contractor shall provide the following required services: Secure and Reliable Circuit: Must provide a secure, reliable, point-to-point circuit capable of handling sensitive law enforcement data and supporting the specified bandwidth. Local Access Circuit: 10 Mbps dedicated local access. Internet Port: 2 Mbps iQ Internet Port. Transport: Circuit provisioned via metro ethernet terminating at AZ DPS CJIS. Onsite Integration: Circuit must operate in conjunction with a standalone CPU and Cisco ASA 5506X router at NAVAHCS Police, without redesign that would introduce unacceptable delay or risk to CJIS access continuity. Coordination: Provider must coordinate provisioning and ongoing access consistent with FBI CJIS security requirements for CJIS system access. Provider must coordinate CJIS access with AZ DPS and maintain FBI CJIS security requirements for the service. Service Configuration: Provider must support the specified 10 Mbps local access and 2 Mbps iQ Internet Port service configuration as part of the CJIS solution. Contractor Qualifications & Eligibility Demonstrated Capability: Demonstrated capability to provide the specified metro ethernet CJIS access, bandwidth, and associated internet port. Compliance: Compliance with VA security and privacy requirements for handling VA sensitive information. Secure Infrastructure: Infrastructure that supports secure transmission and storage of VA/CJIS data. Documentation: Documentation evidencing compliance with applicable VA and federal security standards. Deliverables Service activation & configuration documentation Completed Security Checklist and CSCA reports Performance & compliance reports as required by VA.

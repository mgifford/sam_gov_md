Responses to this notice will be used for information and planning purposes. No quotes are being requested or accepted at this time with this notice. The VANEOHS Vascular Clinic is replacing their existing fleet of Vascular Non-Invasive Diagnostic Machines across all clinic locations. This Vascular Clinic equipment initiative requires seven (7) VascuLab GSX/VL4060-GSXB systems and all related equipment OR EQUIVALENT. This request for the listed equipment included in this SOW (ATTAHCED) directly support this equipment initiative. This equipment is necessary to deliver high-quality eye care for the VANEOHS Veterans visiting all Vascular Clinic locations. Core equipment is essential for Vascular care and has a significant effect upon our ability to accurately evaluate and diagnose vascular patients, provide state-of the art technological expertise and allow for optimized efficiency and ergonomics for our Vascular clinics. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code being considered for this procurement is 339112 Surgical and Medical Instrument Manufacturing and the PSC is 6515 Medical and Surgical Instruments, Equipment, and Supplies.

## Summary

The Contractor shall install new epoxy flooring in Operating Room 2 and install new safety padding flooring in room C-5018 at the Richard L. Roudebush VAMC campus as detailed in the Technical Requirements section, project bid documents, and VHA Master Construction Specifications. Completion of this project will restore reliable flooring to OR 2 sterile environment, and provide seamless room padding protection for critical patient safety within the inpatient mental health unit.

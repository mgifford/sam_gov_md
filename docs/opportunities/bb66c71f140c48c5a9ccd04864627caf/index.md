## Summary

This RFI with sources sought is for market research purposes only. Please view the two attachments they will provide additional information on this requirement. The Buckley Space Force Base Radar, Airfield & Weather Systems (RAWS) unit requires exacting test sets to perform essential maintenance on Air Traffic Control and Landing Systems (ATCALS). To maintain Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) certification, these systems must undergo a flight inspection every eighteen months. During these inspections, critical parameters such as reflected radio frequency (RF) and cable phasing must be precisely measured and adjusted. Headquarters Air Force Flight Standards Agency (HQ/AFFSA) has identified the specified test requirements as uniquely suited for the ATCALS mission. The Government is requesting a response to the following: What is the fastest delivery time after award you could provide each of these items? (Please reply citing the delivery time to Buckley Space Force Base, Colorado.) Can your company provide the supplies detailed in the attached Statement of Need? If yes, please provide a brief statement explaining your technical capability. Your response must specifically address how your proposed equipment complies with applicable Air Force regulations and ensures full compatibility/integration with our current system of radios. Please also highlight one recent, similar supply delivery you have completed. Based on the attached Statement of Need, can your company provide a Rough Order of Magnitude (ROM) or an estimated cost range to deliver these supplies? (Note: This estimate is strictly for government planning and market research purposes, and does not constitute a formal quote or proposal.) Do you have a pre-priced contract vehicle with these items on your schedule? If so, please provide the contract/schedule number for validation. Do you offer a bulk quantity discount? If so, at what quantity(ites)? Is a warranty included? If so, what is the standard commercial warranty? What are your extended warranty options? Company name, address, point of contact, telephone number, e-mail address, CAGE/DUNS and pre-priced contract vehicle number if applicable.

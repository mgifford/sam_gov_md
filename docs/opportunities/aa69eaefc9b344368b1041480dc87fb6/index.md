# Synopsis for Revitalization of the Uninterruptable Power Supply

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-07 11:46:14.652-04
- Solicitation Number: W519TC-26-Q-RUPS

## Summary

This is a PRESOLICITATION NOTICE for Solicitation W519TC-26-Q-RUPS. The U.S. Army Contracting Command - Rock Island (ACC-RI) intends to issue a Request for Quote for a requirement to procure revitalization of the Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) system that supports the Headquarters’ facility building on the Rock Island Arsenal in Rock Island, Illinois. All work must be performed by service professionals certified by Schneider Electric. Any resulting procurement will be awarded as a Firm-Fixed-Price (FFP) 100% Small Business Set Aside. The small business size standard is $34 million. Work required under the solicitation will be perform via FOB Destination at Rock Island Arsenal, Rock Island Illinois. Please note: This notice does not constitute an RFQ. Responses to this announcement are not quotes and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. This is not a guarantee of a future RFQ or an award. This should not be construed as a commitment of any kind by the U.S Government. The Points of Contact for this notification are Sydney Johnson, sydney.e.johnson18.civ@army.mil, and Kathleen Ward, kathleen.m.ward20.civ@army.mil.

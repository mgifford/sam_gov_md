# Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-27 11:46:56.834-04
- Solicitation Number: W81K0026QA115

## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT: The Medical Readiness Contracting Office - West, JBSA Fort Sam Houston, TX intends to award on a Limiting Competition sole source basis, a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) contract to Neuromodulatory Devices & Applications, Inc. under the authority of Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 13.106-1(b) (1), for the purchase of Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) Systems in support of the Department of Behavioral Health, Dwight David Eisenhower Medical Center (DDEAMC), Fort Gordon, GA. The NAICS code is 334510, Electro-medical and Electrotherapeutic, Product Service Code 6515, Medical and Surgical Instruments, Equipment, and Supplies with a size standard of 1,250. This notice is neither a request for quotes nor a solicitation of offers. However, all responsible sources may submit a capability statement, proposal, or quotation, which shall be considered by the agency for future requirements. Note: Interested vendors, please respond to this notice on or before 1 Apr 2026. An award will be made on or around 15 Apr 2026.

# U.S. Embassy in Tegucigalpa – Delivery of Bottled Water to US Government Facilities

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-16 20:27:39.147-04
- Solicitation Number: 19H08026Q0031

## Summary

The U.S. Embassy of Tegucigalpa, Honduras has a requirement for supply of bottled water in different presentations for US Government-owned or leased residences and functional properties in Tegucigalpa and San Pedro Sula, Honduras. The contractor shall deliver the different bottled water as requested to the for US Government-owned or leased residences and functional properties in Tegucigalpa and San Pedro Sula, Honduras. The anticipated performance is for a base period of twelve months and four (4) one-year periods at the option of the Government. All responsible sources may submit an offer, which will be considered. Please see the RFQ for the quotation due date and time. The Government may award the contract based on the initial offer without discussion. The resultant contract will be a firm fixed price type contract. Electronic Submission/Responses: This opportunity allows for electronic responses. Please submit your offer to tgubids@state.gov prior to the response date and time in the solicitation. All contractors have to be registered in the SAM (System for Award Management) Database https://www.sam.gov prior to contract award pursuant to FAR provision 52.204-7. Therefore, prospective offerors are encouraged to register prior to the submittal of quotations/proposals. The guidelines for registration in SAM are also available at: https://www.fsd.gov/fsd-gov/learning-center-system.do?sysparm_system=SAM

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for construction services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 36, as supplemented with additional information included in this notice. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; quotes are being requested, and no other written solicitation will be issued. Instructions to quoters, evaluation criteria, and all applicable FAR Provisions and Clauses are attached. Solicitation Number: 70Z02926QNEWO000003 Type: Request for Quotation (RFQ) NAICS Code: 238220 PSC Code: Z2EZ Set Aside: 100% Small Business Contract Type: Firm-Fixed Price Construction Contract Award Basis: Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA) This will be a firm-fixed priced purchase order awarded to the lowest priced technically acceptable offer. The evaluation factors are: - Price - Technical Acceptability - Past Performance Make sure to provide your price, technical approach, and past performance with your quote. The Government will evaluate the past performance of each offeror to determine the degree of confidence that the offeror will successfully perform the contract requirements. Evaluation will consider the relevance, quality, and timeliness of prior work as demonstrated through submitted references and independent government sources. Offerors with no relevant past performance will receive a neutral rating. Past performance will be rated and factored into the overall evaluation as specified in the solicitation. Offerors, if they wish, can also provide information on problems encountered on the identified contracts and the offeror’s corrective actions. The Government shall consider this information, as well as information obtained from any other sources, when evaluating the offeror’s past performance. The source selection authority shall determine the relevance of similar past performance information. SAM.gov Registration: Active account required. Project Location: U.S. Coast Guard Station Destin 2000 Miracle Strip Pkwy Destin, Florida 32540 Project Description: The United States Coast Guard is seeking quotations from qualified contractors to perform comprehensive maintenance and repair services for the generator and fuel tank system at USCG Station Destin, Destin, Florida. Work includes, but is not limited to: Replacement of emergency generator battery (Kohler 100REOZJF) with specified model and connection to charger. Replacement of muffler exhaust insulation blankets with 3” thick Type 1 calcium silicate and aluminum jacket. Removal and disposal of abandoned underground fuel lines; capping and sealing openings. Modification of fuel trough-trench and installation of piping at ground level. Removal of rust and painting of the 1,000-gallon fuel tank with state-approved coating system. Installation of OSHA-required placards and electrical grounding per NEC. Pressure testing of primary and secondary tanks by a Florida registered precision tank tester. Sealing of concrete walls/dike with state-approved elastomeric polysulfide joint sealant. Installation of fuel containment pans. Electrical connections between generator, ATS, and panels per NEC and manufacturer requirements. Generator operational testing and informal training for station personnel. Compliance with all federal, state, and local environmental, safety, and security regulations. Performance Period: 30 calendar days from Notice to Proceed.

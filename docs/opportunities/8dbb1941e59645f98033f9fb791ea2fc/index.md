# NSN 3990-00-930-1480 // Cargo Pallet Cover

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 13:40:32.758-05
- Solicitation Number: SPE8EG26Q0003

## Summary

Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Troop Support Philadelphia intends to issue a full and open Request for Quote (RFQ) for the acquisition of one NSN, 3990-00-930-1480 (Cargo Pallet Cover), under solicitation number SPE8EG-26-Q-0003. This item is manufactured to a Commercial Item Description (CID), A-A-55437 Type I. The acquisition is for a Firm-Fixed Price Indefinite Delivery, Indefinite Delivery (IDIQ) single award long-term contract (LTC) with the option years priced out at the time of award. The procedures at FAR Part 13.5, �Simplified Procedures for Certain Commercial Products and Commercial Services� and FAR Part 12, �Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services� will be utilized. A price only approach will be utilized. Any resultant contract will be awarded to the responsible offeror, offering the lowest evaluated price, taking no exceptions to the terms and conditions set forth in the solicitation including offering an item that complies with the applicable item description. The resultant contract will include a two (2) year base period and four (4) two-year option periods. The contract maximum will be $5 million. Delivery terms will be Freight on Board (F.O.B.) Origin. Inspection & Acceptance (I/A) will take place at Destination. The synopsis should NOT be considered a Request for Proposal (RFP), Request for Quotation (RFQ), or an Invitation for Bid (IFB). The Government is in no way obligated to purchase any material as a result of this notice. The estimated release date of this solicitation is March 10, 2026. This date is subject to change. A copy of the solicitation will be made available through the DLA Internet Bid Board System (DIBBS), https://www.dibbs.bsm.dla.mil/. All responsible sources will be able to submit a proposal which will be considered by DLA Troop Support. NOTE: Copies of the solicitation package will not become available until the solicitation issue date.

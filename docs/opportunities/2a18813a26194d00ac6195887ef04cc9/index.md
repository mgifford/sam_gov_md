# Superior National Forest Vault Toilet Pumping

- Agency: AGRICULTURE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-23 11:05:39.248-04
- Solicitation Number: 12444526Q0021

## Summary

The contractor shall provide all management, supervision, labor, personnel, transportation, materials, equipment, and supplies, except as otherwise specified, necessary to provide services at various vault toilets on the Superior National Forest located at various sites around the areas near Aurora, Virginia, Cook, Crane Lake, Echo Trail, Ely, Fernberg Trail, Isabella, Tofte, Grand Marais, and the Gunflint Trail. All work shall be performed in accordance with this statement of work (SOW) and all federal, state, and local laws and regulations.

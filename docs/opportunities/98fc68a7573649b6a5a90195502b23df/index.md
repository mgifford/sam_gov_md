# Grand Jury Court Reporting Services; U.S. Attorney's Office, Eastern District of Texas

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 14:42:40.935-05
- Solicitation Number: 15JA0526Q00000031

## Summary

The Executive Office for United States Attorneys (EOUSA) on behalf of United States Attorney’s Office, Eastern District of Texas (USAO-TXE), contemplates the Multiple Award of an Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract for grand jury and/or depositions court reporting services. The award of this IDIQ will assist USAO-TXE in fulfilling the mission for United States Attorneys to serve as the nation’s principal litigators under the direction of the Attorney General. Anticipated award date is in March 2026.

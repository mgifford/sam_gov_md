# Request For Information - Adaptive Central Receive (ACRx) Antenna Installation

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-30 16:02:13.537-04
- Solicitation Number: SHOP-PR-26-000287

## Summary

The Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) Procurement Section is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) to request information from all prospective vendors in regards to Adaptive Central Receive (ACRx) Antenna Installation services. Submit responses to this RFI by email to contracting point of contact Dylan Hankins at dehankins2@fbi.gov no later than April 7, at 12:00 PM Central Time. Please limit responses to no more than ten (10) pages, to include cover pages, appendices and attachments. All proprietary/company confidential material shall be clearly marked on every page that contains such. The FBI reserves the right to not respond to any or all emails or materials submitted. All current FBI requirements identified herein are subject to change at any time, without further notice being required.

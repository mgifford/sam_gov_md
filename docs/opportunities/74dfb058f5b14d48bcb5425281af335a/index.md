RFP Number: FA930126R0005 Issue Date: 10 March 2026 Proposal Due Date: 10 April 2026, 11:00 AM, PDT NAICS Code: 561210 Contract Type: Firm-Fixed-Price General Description: The contractor shall furnish all labor, supervision, facilities, vehicles, transportation, equipment, supplies, tools, materials, and other items and nonpersonal services (except those identified as Government-furnished) to perform MMO services as defined in the Performance Work Statement.

# Protestant Musician/Organist

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 06:55:41.762-04
- Solicitation Number: M00264-26-Q-0016

## Summary

Command Religious Program requires Contractor support for the delivery of musical support as the Musician, including Choir director and organist for Liturgical (General) Protestant worship services and for special/seasonal services, as well as funeral and memorial services. With this requirement, Liturgical Protestant and/or Contemporary Congregation requests a Musician/Organist for their 1030 Worship Service. The period of performnace will be a base year and four (4) 12 month option periods.

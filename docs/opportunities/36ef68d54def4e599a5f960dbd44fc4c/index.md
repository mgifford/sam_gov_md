## Summary

INTENT TO SOLE SOURCE THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION OR REQUEST FOR OFFERS. NO SOLICITATION PACKAGE IS AVAILABLE AND TELEPHONE REQUESTS WILL NOT BE HONORED AND NO SOLICITATION WILL BE POSTED TO CONTRACT OPPORTUNITIES. PROCUREMENT TITLE: ECLIPES SOFTWARE MAINTENANCE, REPAIRS, AND SUPPORT: FOR THE VARIAN TRUEBEAM LINEAR ACCELERATOR INTENT TO AWARD: The Department of Veterans Affairs, VISN 01, Jamaica Plain VA Medical Center, 150 South Huntington Avenue, Boston, MA 02130-4817, intends to award one (1) Contract to the OEM named VARIAN MEDICAL SYSTEMS, INC, for Eclipse Software Maintenance, Repairs, And Support: For The Varian Truebeam Linear Accelerator Product. BACKGROUND AND DESCRIPTION: This requirement is for Eclipse software maintenance, repairs, and support for the Varian Truebeam Linear Accelerator. The eclipse software is proprietary software that optimizes the radiotherapy treatment plan based on a physician’s dose instructions and information about the size, shape, and location of a tumor to be treated. It’s used in conjunction with the VA-owned Varian Truebeam linear accelerator. The procurement is for a Base Year plus four Option Years, see attached SOW for full details and performance periods. NAICS: 811210 - Electronic and Precision Equipment Repair and Maintenance, size standard $34M PSC: J065 - Maint/Repair/Rebuild Of Equipment- Medical, Dental, And Veterinary Equipment And Supplies AUTHORITY: The authority for this procurement will be via FAR Part 12 - Acquisition of Commercial Products and Commercial Services. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed award upon response to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Contracting Officer. INTERESTED PARTIES: Any Contractor who believes that they have experience and capability to perform services described in the attached Statement of Work (SOW) may express interest by submitting a written capability statement or letter of interest to pearl.wray@va.gov by 14:00 EST, by 4/7/2026. Any questions related to this notice can also be sent to pearl.wray@va.gov.

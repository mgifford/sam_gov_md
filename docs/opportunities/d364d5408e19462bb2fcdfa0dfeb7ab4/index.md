# Inspection, Maintenance and Repair of Hydraulic Spud System on Crane Barge

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-17 14:57:44.024-04
- Solicitation Number: W912ES26QA042
- Awardee: GTS-MC LLC 8902 RAMBIL RD Eau Claire WI 54703 USA
- Award Amount: 79460

## Summary

The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers is seeking a Contractor to perform a complete diagnostic inspection, maintenance and repairs on the spud system of the USACE Crane Barge Leonard. The CB Leonard was constructed in 1998 and is equipped with a 75-ton crane. The spud system uses hydraulic pumps and electric winches to lower and raise a pair of ?spuds?, each weighing 28,500 lbs, to and from the river bottom, to hold the barge in place against the current during crane operations. The Contractor will perform a full inspection of all system components, perform maintenance and repairs necessary to eliminate all system chattering and excessive vibration (notable in one spud), and replace both inoperable deck control stations with new, fully functional controls. The work will take place aboard the CB Leonard, at Fountain City Service Base (FCSB) or a location to be determined (TBD) along the Mississippi River within a 100-mile radius of FCSB. Work will take place Monday ? Thursday, 0700 ? 1730, except Federal Holidays. Work must be completed no later than 15 June 2026. This solicitation is 100% set-aside for small businesses under NAICS 811310, with a small business size standard of $12,500,000. Offerors must be fully registered in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) to be eligible for contract award. Quoters are strongly encouraged to visit the barge and inspect the equipment prior to quoting.

# Test Evaluation and Support Team 3 (TEST3) Period of Performance Extension

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-24 18:08:21.693-04
- Solicitation Number: 80JSC022DA003
- Awardee: SIERRA LOBO INC Fremont OH 43420 USA
- Award Amount: 266000000.00

## Summary

NASA Johnson Space Center White Sands Test Facility (JSC WSTF) extended the Test Evaluation and Support Team 3 (TEST3) contract with Sierra Lobo, Inc. for up to two years (1-year base with 1-year option) to allow sufficient time for successful completion of two large-scale construction projects at WSTF funded by One Big Beautiful Bill Act (OBBBA). This extension supports the effective utilization of the OBBBA funds by ensuring that the projects are conducted timely and are overseen and implemented by the experienced TEST3 contractor workforce who are already familiar with the site’s operational needs and project plans. The statutory authority permitting other than full and open competition is 10 U.S.C. 3204(a)(1), as implemented by FAR 6.103-1, Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements. See Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition (JOFOC) for further details.

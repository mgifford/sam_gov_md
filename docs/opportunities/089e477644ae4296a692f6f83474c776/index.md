# This effort is to acquire one (1) ANA Energy EBOSS 70-45 Hybrid Generator with a Dual Axle Surge Trailer, including a 3" Ring Hitch Assembly as well as one (1) Solar Panel Package to support the generator.

- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-14 07:44:11.096-04
- Solicitation Number: 6973GH-26-Q-00073

## Summary

The FAA has a need for Procurement of Alliance North America (ANA) EBOSS Brand Generator (Model 70-45) in Support of Customs and Border Protection (CBP) Remote Video Surveillance System (RVSS). This effort is to acquire one (1) ANA Energy EBOSS 70-45 Hybrid Generator with a Dual Axle Surge Trailer, including a 3" Ring Hitch Assembly as well as one (1) Solar Panel Package to support the generator. (1) One Part Number BOSS70-004 - EBOSS 70-45 HYBRID DUAL AXLE ELEC TRAILER 2-5/16" BALL HITCH ASSEMBLY. This asset will need to have the EBOSS 70-45 HYBRID CWP SOL ACCESSORY PACKAGE. Part Number BOSS70-A009. No substitutions are authorized regardless of fit, form, and function.

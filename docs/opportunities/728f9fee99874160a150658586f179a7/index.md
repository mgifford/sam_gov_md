## Summary

This is a notice of intent to exercise FAR 52.217-8, Option to Extend Services in accordance with the terms and conditions in Contract No. W912KC25PA017, awarded to Williams Scotsman Inc., DBA Willscot on August 26, 2025. This is a firm-fixed-price purchase order for the lease extension of five (5) each, 40 Foot Refrigerated Containers with 3 Phase Power- Hi Cube that includes a Remote Monitoring System. The value of this contract action is $25,900.00 increasing the total contract value to $80,780.85. The anticipated period of performance for the extension is April 10, 2026 to July 31, 2026 for the containers located at Fort Indiantown Gap, Annville, Pennsylvania. This will extend services for four months (28-day billing cycles). The contractor will continue to lease the five (5) refrigerated containers to the Government. The contractor will comply with all applicable federal, state, and local regulations, standard industry terms, and provide 24/7 emergency repair services. The Government has determined that the option is the most advantageous method of fulfilling the requirement. This synopsis is for notification purposes only and is not a request for quotes.

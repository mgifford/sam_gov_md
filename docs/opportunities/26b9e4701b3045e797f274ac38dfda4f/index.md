SOURCES SOUGHT – CLASSIFIED IT DESTRUCTION THIS IS A MARKET SURVEY FOR INFORMATION ONLY. THIS IS NOT A SOLICITATION FOR PROPOSALS AND NO CONTRACT WILL BE AWARDED FROM THIS PRESOLICITATION NOTICE. PLEASE DO NOT REQUEST A COPY OF A SOLICITATION, AS ONE DOES NOT EXIST. - REQUIREMENTS – Requirement is for classified IT destruction. *** Please see attached Statement of Work (SOW)*** FOB Destination Edwards AFB, CA 93524. Identification of business size (small or large) and identify the socioeconomic disadvantaged category, as defined the Federal Acquisition Regulation FAR Part 19 and also currently listed in the System for Award Management (SAM).

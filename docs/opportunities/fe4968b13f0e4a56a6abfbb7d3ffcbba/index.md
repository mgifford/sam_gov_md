## Summary

The contractor will provide all vehicles, personnel, management, supplies, equipment, and necessary reporting for the provision of non-emergent wheelchair transportation services. The contactor will make these non-emergent wheelchair transportation services available to the VA as needed, 24 hours a day, seven days a week (24/7), including weekends and holidays.

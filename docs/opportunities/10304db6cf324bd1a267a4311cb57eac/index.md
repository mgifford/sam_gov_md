- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE

- Posted: 2026-04-22 09:47:34.92-04

## Summary

The Military Sealift Command has a requirement for the Regular Overhaul and Dry Docking (ROH/DD) for the USNS LEROY GRUMMAN (T-AO 195). This is an East Coast or Gulf Coast Availability. Major work items associated with this availability include: General Ship Services, Clean and Gas Free Tanks, Cofferdams, Voids and Spaces, Hazardous Waste Disposal, Cargo Potable Deck Replacement, Tank Hatch Preservation, Superstructure Blast and Paint, Window Regasketting and Steel Repair, No 4 Port and Stbd Ballast Tank Preservation, AFFF Piping, Cargo Petroleum Tank Preservation, High Pressure Air Compressor Services Dry Docking and Undocking, Propeller Maintenance, Overhauling Sea Valves and Underwater Hull and Freeboard Preservation.

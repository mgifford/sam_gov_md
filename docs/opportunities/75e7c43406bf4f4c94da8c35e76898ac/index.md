# Multiple Award Task Order Construction Contracts (MATOC) at Tobyhanna Army Depot

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 14:05:37.721-05
- Solicitation Number: W51AA126RA0002

## Summary

TOBYHANNA ARMY DEPOT CONSTRUCTION MATOC FY26 The intent of this requirement is to award Indefinite Delivery-Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) multiple award task order contracts (MATOC) for maintenance, repair, and construction services in support of Tobyhanna army depot (TYAD) consisting of a five-calendar year ordering period. The total maximum ceiling amount is $200,000,000. This action is being solicited as a total small business set-aside under NAICS code 236220, $45 million is the small business size standard. The government�s intent is to award a contract to all qualifying offerors. At the task order level, the contracting officer may set-aside an individual task order for other socio-economic programs. All task orders will be issued as a firm fixed price award and may require performance and payment bonds. The task orders in this contract may range from $25,000 to $10,000,000 but may be as large as $100,000,000 for one task order. The expected durations for these projects are 365 calendar days or less, however, the duration may also exceed 365 calendar days. Associated information, notices, and amendments related to this solicitation will be updated to this posting via https://www.sam.gov under the contracting opportunities application. Note: a bid guarantee is not required for this solicitation but may be required on future task orders.

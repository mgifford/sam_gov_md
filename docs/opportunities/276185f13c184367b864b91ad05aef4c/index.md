Please provide your capability statement, I will accept responses for this Sources Sought until the 17th of March 10, 2026, at 14:00, thanks. STATEMENT OF WORK Intercom System, C.W. Young VAMC

1.0 INTRODUCTION: The C.W. Young VAMC in Bay Pines, Florida requires an IP Intercom System. This system requires three (3) separate zones consisting of forty-two (42) locations in total. These locations will require ten (10) handheld/phone stations, thirty-three (33) wall stations, and one (1) server as identified in the provided device list and floor plan drawing.

2.0 SCOPE OF WORK: The Contractor shall supply all supervision, labor and materials to remove and dispose of the old intercom system, DEMO all conductors and abandoned raceways that are part of the old system back to the source. All penetrations must be fire caulked and seal off any holes left from the demo. The installation shall consist of a Zenitel ICX Intelligent Communications System with Cleanroom Intercom Station IP-CROR and IP Desk Master with handset V2 (or equivalent) at the Bay Pines Veterans Medical Center in Bay Pines, Florida.

3.0 GENERAL INFORMATION

3.1 Place of Performance C.W. Young VAMC 10000 Bay Pines BLVD Bay Pines, Florida 33744

3.2 Hours of Operation Surgical and Imaging hours of operation are from 7:00 am to 4:30 pm, Monday through Friday. Construction will be limited to after Surgical and Imaging hours of Operations.

3.3 Federal Holidays Do not schedule work on Federal Holidays or days observed in lieu thereof. The Federal holidays listed below are observed: New Year s Day Labor Day Martin Luther King s Birthday Columbus Day Presidents Day Veterans Day Memorial Day Thanksgiving Day Juneteenth Christmas Day Independence Day

4.0 STATEMENT OF WORK:

4.1 The contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, employee training, services, insurance, and equipment necessary to perform the designated work and shall be responsible for the professional quality, technical accuracy, timely completion, and restoration of the area. The requested type of intercom system/speakers is Zenitel. For any equal or better products, the contractor will provide a specification sheet detailing the equal or better product. The contractor will remove and dispose of any, and all debris resulting from this project. The contractor is responsible for any painting required as a result of this demo and installation. The painting will match the existing paint in the rooms. CAT6A cabling is to be used for this project. The contractor is responsible for any drywall repairs/patching required (as a result of this installation) to return each room to its original condition. The contractor will follow all Government, State, and Local guidelines.

Training of local maintenance staff as needed. 5.3.3. Warranty and operating manual are provided to the COR/Maintenance Personnel.

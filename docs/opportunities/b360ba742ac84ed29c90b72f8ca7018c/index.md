# Request For Information: Nuclear Command, Control, and Communications (NC3) Very Low Frequency/Low Frequency (VLF/LF) system

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-24 13:56:08.629-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8735-26-CVRi1-RFI

## Summary

The United States Air Force Materiel Command (AFMC), Air Force Nuclear Weapon Center (AFNWC), in support of the Very Low Frequency Enterprise Program Office, is seeking information on Industry improvements and capabilities from interested contractors who could procure, integrate, and develop the Nuclear Command, Control, and Communications (NC3) Very Low Frequency/Low Frequency (VLF/LF) system, its ancillary components, and develop, procure, and integrate portable configurations for current and future U.S. military aircraft. Please refer to the attached Request for Information (RFI) for more details. Please note that this RFI is for market research purposes only and no contract award will result from this posting.

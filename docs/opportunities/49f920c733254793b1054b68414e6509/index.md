# Z1DA--Pharmacy Level 3 Bullet Resistant Glass Installation

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-04 10:56:33.124-05
- Solicitation Number: 36C24626Q0365

## Summary

The Department of Veteran s Affairs will solicit for vendors capable of providing Level 3 Bullet-Resistant Glass and Installation for the Western NC Veterans Health Care System, Charles George VA Medical Center, located in Asheville, NC. This will be awarded as a Firm-Fixed contract for One (1) Job. A Request for Quotation, RFQ, will be issued by March 5, 2026. To be eligible for the award, the prospective contractor must be registered in SAM database at http://www.sam.gov and comply with the Evaluation for Basis of Award at the time the quote is submitted. The prospective contractor must also have updated and correct information in the VA Financial Service Center s Customer Engagement Portal. If you are an SDVOSB, VOSB or Small Business and plan on subcontracting, please provide a plan to show how your company provides 51% of the requirement. The North American Industry Classification Code, NAICS, is 561210 (Facilities Support Services), Product Service Code is Z1DA (Maintenance of Hospitals and Infirmaries) and the Size Standard is $47 Million. Interested contractors should monitor Contract Opportunities, SAM, for solicitation number 36C24626Q0365. Point of Contact: Satasha Stewart Satasha.Stewart@va.gov *Please direct all questions to the above email address. Phone calls will not be accepted.

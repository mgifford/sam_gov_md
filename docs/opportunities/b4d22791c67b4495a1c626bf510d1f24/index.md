# Portable Latrines & Hand Washing Stations

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-21 08:56:25.646-04
- Solicitation Number: W9124926RA012

## Summary

This is a non-personal services contract to provide the rental, delivery, and servicing of portable latrines and hand wash stations in support of personnel across multiple 15th Signal Brigade Training Areas. The contractor shall provide, install, service and stock a total of 84 portable latrines and 37 hand washing stations distributed across the locations specified in this SOW.

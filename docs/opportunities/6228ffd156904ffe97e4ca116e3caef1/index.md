# IMPLAN Economic Impact Modeling Software Licenses

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-04 11:18:40.791-05
- Solicitation Number: 140602-26-00XX

## Summary

The National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) intends to award a sole source contract to IMPLAN Group LLC for Economic Impact Modeling Software Licenses. The Government intends to procure this requirement per FAR Part 13 and ultimately award a firm-fixed-price purchase order to IMPLAN Group LLC. The performance period is anticipated to begin on June 13, 2026, and end on June 12, 2027. The associated North American Industrial Classification System {NAICS) code for this procurement is 513210 - Software Publishers, with a $47 million size standard. This notice of intent is not a request for competitive proposals; no solicitation is forthcoming. Any party interested in submitting questions or concerns regarding this notice of the Government's intent to sole source may contact the Contract Officer, Margaret Williams, at margaret.williams@noaa.gov by March 18, 2026, at 12:00 P.M. Eastern Time. NOAA will not be responsible for any costs incurred by responding to this notice.

# Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 04:56:33.887-04
- Solicitation Number: FA560626QA015

## Summary

This requirement is to establish a 5-year service contract with one base year and four option years to provide a comprehensive maintenance and repair program for all mission-essential Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) systems and associated battery strings supporting Spangdahlem Air Base. All UPS systems are provided as an attachment along with the PWS for the requirement.

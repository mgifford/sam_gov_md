## Summary

Notice 08 April 2026 Amendment 0001 Issued. Revised proposal due date and time to 20 April 2026 at 12:00pm Mountain. Notice 06 April 2026 - An amendment will be issued to provide submitted questions and answers. Proposal due date will be extended to 20 April 2026. This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation; offers are being requested, and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 127EAX26Q0009 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Baptist Camp Bridge Maintenance This project will consist of repairing wearing surface, applying wood preservative, improving drainage, replacing timber posts, repairing asphalt and replacing object markers on the Baptist Camp Bridge located in the Tonto National Forest in the Payson Ranger District. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 237310 The small business size standard is $45M This acquisition is set aside for SBA Certified Women-Owned Small Businesses (WOSB).

# Sources Sought - Polyalphaolefin (PAO) chillers and remote manifolds diagnosis and repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-22 20:43:03.177-04
- Solicitation Number: 15797

## Summary

SOURCES SOUGHT SYNOPSIS Polyalphaolefin (PAO) chillers and remote manifolds diagnosis and repair NAICS: 811310 PSC: J041 NOTICE: This is not a solicitation. The purpose of this Sources Sought Synopsis (SSS) is to conduct market research to determine responsible sources capable of providing diagnosis and repair of communication faults within Polyalphaolefin (PAO) chillers and remote manifolds. This Firm Fixed-Price procurement shall be performed at Hill Air Force Base (HAFB). SCOPE: The contractor shall provide all necessary personnel and equipment to perform the following tasks: • Troubleshoot: Investigate and identify the root cause of the communication faults between the PAO chillers and the remote manifolds. • Repair: Implement the necessary repairs to restore full communication and functionality to the affected systems. Please see attached Statement of Work (SOW) for detailed specifications. INSTRUCTIONS: 1. The Government requests that interested parties respond to this notice, if applicable, and identify your capability of providing for this requirement. Please also provide the following information: Company Profile: A. Small Business Status B. Company’s applicable registered NAICS codes C. Number of employees D. CAGE code E. SAM Unique Entity ID F. Location G. Point of Contact Name, Telephone, Email address H. Please advise if an alternate NAICS/PSC code than those identified above would be more appropriate for our requirement. 2. Responses are due NLT 11:00 AM EST, 06 May 2026. 3. No reimbursement will be made for any costs associated with providing information in response to this synopsis or any follow-up information requested. 4. The Government will use the information obtained via this SSS to determine the best acquisition strategy for the procurement of IUID Hardware Support Services. 5. Questions regarding this market survey should be addressed to Ms. Kathryn McKay at kathryn.mckay@us.af.mil or TSgt Jonathan Graham at jonathan.graham.11@us.af.mil.

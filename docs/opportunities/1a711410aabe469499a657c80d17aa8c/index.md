# Legal Case & Document Management System

- Agency: DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA COURTS
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-30 11:22:16.284-04
- Solicitation Number: DCSC-26-RFP-60

## Summary

The District of Columbia Courts, on behalf of the Office of the General Counsel, seeks a qualified vendor to provide a cloud-hosted, configurable commercial-off-the-shelf (“COTS”) Legal Case and Document Management System, including necessary software, licenses, warranties, and related services to include installation, document migration, implementation, on-site post-implementation support and training, and maintenance and support as specified in Section C (Description/Specifications/Work Statement).

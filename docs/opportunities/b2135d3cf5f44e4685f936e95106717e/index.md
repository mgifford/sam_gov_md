# Sources Sought: ADR Vehicular Equipment Kits Survey_2025

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-27 13:26:41.226-04
- Solicitation Number: ADR-Vehicular-Equipment-Kits_2025

## Summary

This is a SOURCES SOUGHT NOTICE/MARKET RESEARCH SURVEY to request information for Government planning purposes only. The Army Contracting Command – Detroit Arsenal (ACC-DTA) is seeking to gain knowledge of interest, capabilities and qualifications of various members of the community, to include both small and large businesses, for production of the item identified in the document attached. Responses to this Sources Sought Notice/Market Research Survey will assist ACC-DTA in planning its acquisition strategy for a potential future acquisition of ADR Vehicular Equipment Kits. The ADR Vehicular Equipment Kits should include, but are not limited to the following components: Comprehensive repair tools and equipment Safety gear for personnel Replacement parts for various vehicle types Instruction manuals and training materials Fire bottle, MSDS placard, and various bracketry to make the vehicle EU compliant.

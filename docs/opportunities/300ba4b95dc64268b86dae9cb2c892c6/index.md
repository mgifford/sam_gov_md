# Rockwell BOA Order for Common Compressor Controller (NC3)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 09:44:47.142-04
- Solicitation Number: N6449826Q5071

## Summary

This solicitation notice is to document that solicitation N6449826Q5071 was issued to Rockwell Automation, Inc. on 18 March 2026. The Department of the Navy, Naval Surface Warfare Center, Philadelphia Division (NSWCPD). Intends to negotiate and award a Basic Ordering Agreement (BOA) Order under BOA N6449824G4001 to Rockwell Automation Inc., 1 Allen Bradley Drive, Cleveland, OH 44124 (Rockwell) for the procurement of Common Compressor Controller materials. The Government intends to make an award in the 2nd quarter of Fiscal Year 2026. This is part of the follow-on procurement for this requirement. Pursuant to 10 U.S.C 2304(c)(l), Rockwell is the only responsible source capable of fulfilling the Navy’s requirements for the specified materials. The proposed BOA Order award is for supplies for which the Government intended to solicit and negotiate with only one source under the authority of FAR 6.302. This notice is not a request for competitive proposals. Interested parties, however, may identify their interest and capability by responding to the notice. The Government will consider all information received prior to the closing date of this notice. Information received will normally be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement in the future. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed contract action based upon responses to this notice is soley within the discretion of the Government. Responses shall be made in writing by email to the attention of Kimesha Speller via email at Kimesha.L.Speller.civ@us.navy.mil by 5:00 PM Eastern on 30 April 2026.

# RFI sUAS Cable Connectors

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-26 13:38:27.284-04

## Summary

Tobyhanna Army Depot (TYAD) is issuing this Request for Information (RFI) as a part of its market research to identify American companies based in the United States of America with the interest and capability to manufacture and supply commercial-grade connectors. These connectors are required to support the development, maintenance, and operation of various electronics used in small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS). This RFI is for market research and planning purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation for proposals. This is an RFI only. This RFI is for planning and market research purposes and does not constitute a solicitation for proposals, proposal abstracts, or quotations. The Government will not award a contract on the basis of this RFI.

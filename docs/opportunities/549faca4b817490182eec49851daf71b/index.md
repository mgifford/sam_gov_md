# Solicitation_Automated Endoscope Leak Tester

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-09 09:47:53.463-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25526Q0245

## Summary

The Harry S Truman Memorial Veteran’s Hospital is in need of Automatic Endoscopic Leak Testers in accordance with this Statement of Requirement. The purpose of this equipment is to improve the accuracy and efficiency of leak detection processes and reduce downtime due to undetected leaks. The Contractor shall deliver, install, and perform start-up testing for the Automatic Endoscopic Leak Testers and accessories.

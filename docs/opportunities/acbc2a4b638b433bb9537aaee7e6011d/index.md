# AIR Card® Individual Sessions

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-09 14:40:16.298-04

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agency Energy (DLA Energy) will host an AIR Card® Information Session at the DLA World Wide Energy Conference for Into-Plane contract holders / POS teams / AIR Card® Processors ONLY. Upcoming changes on the AIR Card® Program will be discussed and those attending will have the opportunity to provide feedback to DLA Energy and voice their concerns and questions about the new changes under the AIR Card® Program. You must register to attend the Conference in order to register for a meeting. When: April 21st, 22nd, 23rd, 2026 Where: This will be at the DLA World Wide Energy Conference held at the Hyatt Regency in Crystal City, Arlington, VA. Registration is required. What: Topics to be discussed include: AIR Card® invoicing procedures, Fully Itemized Price and signed delivery ticket. Who: Into-Plane contract holders / AIR Card® POS teams / Processors ONLY

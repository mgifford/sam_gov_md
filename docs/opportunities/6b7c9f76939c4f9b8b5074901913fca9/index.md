# BA-30 Parachute System

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 11:53:28.318-04
- Solicitation Number: A26-087

## Summary

The Air Force intends to solicit and issue a sole source contract to Butler Parachute Systems under the authority of FAR 6.103-1(b) for the following: The 76th AMXG is requesting utilization of funds for the purchase of BA-30 Parachute System. The requested equipment specifications are outlined below. a. Item one – BA-30 Parachute System a) BA-30 Parachute System P/N: 11090 (Qty: 28) b) Loft kit P/N: 801161 (Qty: 1) c) Consumables Kit P/N: 801160 (Qty:1) d) Rigging Kit P/N: 801156 (Qty: 1) e) CYPRESS Rigging Kit P/N: AADCRPK (Qty: 1) A Firm Fixed Price type contract is contemplated. Estimated Period of Performance: 60 days ARO System for Award Management (SAM) is required to receive any award. Contractors can register electronically at https://www.sam.gov/portal/public/SAM/. The NAICS code for this requirement is 314999 with a size standard of 550 EMP. Interested persons may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement by submitting a proposal. Electronic procedures will be used for this solicitation through SAM.gov at https://www.SAM.gov/. No telephone requests. See Note 22. Authority is 10 USC 2304 (c)(1). For further information contact: Karsyn Smith Contract Specialist

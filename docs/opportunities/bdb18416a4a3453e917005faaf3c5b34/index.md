# MCAS Beaufort Washer and Dryer Contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-25 12:02:15.884-04
- Solicitation Number: M6700126Q0022

## Summary

NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD SOLE SOURCE Contracting Department, Marine Corps Installations East, Camp Lejeune, NC intends to issue a sole source contract to Marco Federal Services, LLC 217 N Resler Dr El Paso, Texas 79912. This procurement, for Marine Corps Air Station Beaufort, is being conducted under the authority of FAR Part 19.108-4(c)(3). The period of performance for this requirement is May 3, 2026 to May 2, 2031. This contract includes maintenance and repair of Contractor-owned and Government-owned washer and dryer machines. The participant must provide all necessary management, tools, supplies, equipment, and labor. All utility connections for Contractor owned machines and Government-owned machines are the participants’ responsibility and expense. All installation work must adhere to local, state, and federal plumbing and electrical codes. This notice of intent is not a request for quotes; however, all responsible sources may submit a quotation and technical capabilities, which, if timely received may be considered. All responses must be submitted to the Government point of contact at the email address shown below. A determination by the Government not to open the requirement to competition, based upon responses to this notice, is solely within the discretion of the Government. The Point of Contact (POC) for this requirement is Tammie Cassell @ tammila.cassell@usmc.mil, phone 910-451-6357, and facsimile @ 910-451-7845.

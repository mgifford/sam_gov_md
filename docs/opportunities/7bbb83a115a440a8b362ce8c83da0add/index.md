# 2026 HAFB Air Show Lodging

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 12:05:48.02-04
- Solicitation Number: FA820126Q0010

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items and services prepared in accordance with the format in FAR part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Offers are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Hill Air Force Base is seeking 120 non-smoking rooms in a facility located between Ogden, UT and Layton, UT for authorized personnel assigned to or supporting the Hill AFB's Air Show/Open House at the current government rate. The Government's preference is to make an award to a maximum of two (2) locations. See included attachments for additional details.

# 146 MXG MOVING SERVICES - Office Furniture and Equipment

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 11:47:34.632-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S7526QA014

## Summary

This announcement is for information and planning purposes. It is not to be construed as a commitment by the Government, implied or otherwise, to issue a solicitation or award a contract. The California Air National Guard, 146th Airlift Wing (146th AW) Channel Islands ANG Station, Port Hueneme has a requirement entitled: 146 MXG Moving Services – Furniture and Equipment. The 146th AW intends to solicit quotes from at least three (3) interested sources in accordance with RFO FAR 12.201-1 Simplified procedures. This will be 100% set-aside for a Small Business competition that is intended to result in award of one (1) firm-fixed price contract with a 14-day period of performance to occur late July / early August 2026. The general scope of services requires the Contractor to provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and quality control necessary for the non-personal services of relocating office furniture and equipment from various temporary storage areas to permanent offices and shop areas. The place of performance is the California Air National Guard Station, 146th Airlift Wing Channel Islands, 100 Mulcahey Dr, Port Hueneme, California. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this project is 484210 Used Household Goods and Office Moving with a small business size standard of $34.0 Million. This Sources Sought/Presolicitation Notice is not a Request for Quote (RFQ), Request for Proposal (RFP), or an Invitation for Bid (IFB). This Notice is published for this proposed action in accordance with RFO FAR 5.101(a). The 146 MXG Moving Services performance work statement will be detailed in the RFQ when distributed. Quotes will be due no less than 10 days from the date the solicitation is issued. The definitive due date and time will be stated in the solicitation and any associated amendments that may be issued. No CDs or hard copies of the solicitation will be available. Interested vendors are responsible for checking https://SAM.gov for any update(s) to this Notice. The Government is not responsible for any loss of Internet connectivity or for any vendor’s inability to access any documents posted on the above-mentioned website. To conduct business with the Department of War, a Contractor must provide registration information to the System for Award Management (SAM). All offerors must be actively registered at SAM prior to receiving an award. Registration instructions may be obtained, and online registration may be accomplished at https://SAM.gov. This announcement is for information and planning purposes. If your firm is interested in this requirement and would like to receive the RFQ when distributed, email your interest and firm’s contact information with SAM UEI# to toni.wasgatt@us.af.mil and william.tucker.32@us.af.mil on or before 12:00pm PDT on 17 April 2026.

# Invitation to Bid for Miscellaneous Non-Ferrous Metals in 4x6's at Naval Air Station Fallon

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 12:47:43.077-04
- Solicitation Number: S009-26

## Summary

The Navy Region Southwest Qualified Recycling Program (QRP) is issuing an invitation to bid on the sale of Miscellaneous Non-Ferrous in 4x6’s located at Naval Air Station Fallon (Fallon, NV). This is a Government Sale. If interested, please open the attached form for information and instructions on how to submit your bid. Bids must be sent to our office using the bid sheet form provided with this email. Bid sheet must be sent in ink or typed, clearly legible, and signed by the person sending the bid. Bids are due no later than 12 P.M. (Pacific Time) March 16, 2026. If you have any questions regarding this bid, please contact our office at 619-556-9475 or email questions to cnrsw-qrp-sales@us.navy.mil

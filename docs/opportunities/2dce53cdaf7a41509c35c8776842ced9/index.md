# Salisbury Veterans Center RLP for Office Space

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-30 11:24:27.512-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C24526R0059

## Summary

Veterans Health Administration is in need of a maximum of 2534 ABOA SF, not to exceed 3000sf of contiguous space in Salisbury, MD for a Veterans Center. The term of the Lease is 20 years, 5 Firm. The location Proximity to gun stores/ranges, pawn shops, adult entertainment clubs, liquor stores, medical (or otherwise) marijuana distributors or methadone clinics are precluded within a walkable mile of the Vet Center. Amenities that are rated as unfavorable include: childcare, community or recreation center for children, K-12 school, bars, or other establishments with alcohol or pornography distribution, dog kennels, and adjacent to single family housing. Successful Offerors will be selected based on Building Location, Space Concept Design, Parking Plan, Technical Capabilities, Program Management & Response Plan, Emergency Preparedness Plans, Past Performance and Price. Please email all questions to tammy.buckwalter@va.gov

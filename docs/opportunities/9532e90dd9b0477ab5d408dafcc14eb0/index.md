## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1240LP26Q0040 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for Vault Toilet Pumping Services for the Yampa Ranger District located on the Medicine Bow Routt Thunder Basin National Forest. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 562991 The small business size standard is $9 Million. This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency. Please refer to the attachments tab for RFQ details. All Questions must be submitted to curtis.r.landreth@usda.gov no later than March 26, 2026 by 12 pm MST.

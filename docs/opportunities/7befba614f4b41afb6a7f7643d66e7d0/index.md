# SPS Waste Water Parts and Maintenance

- Agency: VETERANS AFFAIRS, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-10 13:04:10.236-04
- Solicitation Number: 36C25026Q0188

## Summary

The Department of Veterans Affairs, Veterans Health Administration, Network Contracting Office 10 (NCO 10), Ann Arbor, MI 48105, intends to negotiate a sole-source, firm fixed-price contract for maintenance, parts and installation for the Evoqua Water Sterilization System at the Saginaw VA Medical Center, to Evoqua Water Technologies, LLC, 10 Technology Drive, Lowell, MA 01851 in accordance with FAR 13.106-1(b). This acquisition is for the Aleda E. Lutz VA Medical Center, 1500 Weiss Street, Saginaw, MI 48602. The government intends to award the contract on or about April 30, 2026, to Evoqua Water Technologies, LLC, 10 Technology Drive, Lowell, MA 01851, DUNS 129138421. This notice satisfies the FAR 5.201(b)(1) requirement requiring publication of the proposed contract action. This notice of intent is not a request for quote, and no solicitation shall be made available. In accordance with FAR 5.207(c)(16)(ii), contractors may submit a capabilities statement or quotation which shall be considered by the agency. The government shall not pay for any costs incurred by responses to this notice

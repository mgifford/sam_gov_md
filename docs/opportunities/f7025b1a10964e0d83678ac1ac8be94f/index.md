# Furniture Requirement

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-17 11:54:26.822-04
- Solicitation Number: FA255026Q0011

## Summary

This is a new requirement to procure office furniture and install it across three rooms on Schriever Space Force Base. Important details listed below, but please see the Solicitation Document for FULL instructions and details. Site Visit: 8 April 2026 at 9:00 AM MT. You must notify BOTH the listed points of contact on this solicitation no later than 7 April 2026 at 10:00 AM MT that you or a representative of your company will be attending and/or need access to base. RFIs/Questions: Due no later than 13 April 2026 by 10:00 AM MT. Solicitation Quotes: Due no later than 20 April 2026 by 10:00 AM MT. Nonmanufacturer Rule: The manufacturer the vendor resells/deals the product for, the vendor shall provide the manufacturer's company name and manufacturer's UEI/CAGE code. Failure to meet the nonmanufacturer rule may result in the vendor being deemed ineligible for contract award. (Page 19, point f). The contractor must be a small business. The manufacturer, if one is utilized, must also be a small business. Please refer to page 18-22 for instructions on what your quotes should contain. Refer to page 21 for basis of awrd and how quotes will be evaluated. Additionally, refer to all provided attachments within this solicitation. Amendment 0001 - Provide an updated Statement of Work with incorrect verbiage removed from it. Old verbiage spoke to the Office Workplace Furniture (OWF), which is not being utilized within this requirement. Amendment 0002 - Provide a quote due date extension by 7 calendar days due to the delay in RFI response. New Due Date is 27 April 2026 at 10:00 AM MT.

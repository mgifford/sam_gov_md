# Animal Care and Laboratory Support Services to the Vaccine Research Center

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 15:20:49.358-04
- Solicitation Number: 75N93024R00029

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide qualified personnel, materials, equipment, and facilities for the housing and care of NHPs to support VRC TRP, investigators and collaborators developing vaccines and therapeutics for infectious diseases. This contract will be administered and used primarily to support NIAID VRC research, however, the Government may request the Contractor to support related NIAID research to respond to changing priorities as scientific and public health needs shift, including for rapid responses to public health emergencies and emerging and re-emerging disease threats both domestic and foreign.

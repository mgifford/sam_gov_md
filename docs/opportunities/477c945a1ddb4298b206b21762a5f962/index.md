# WCF Towels/Wipes Rental & Laundering

- Agency: TREASURY, DEPARTMENT OF THE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-02 17:18:12.514-04

## Summary

Notice of No Award – RFQ #2031ZA26Q00019 The Government has completed its evaluation of quotations received in response to RFQ #2031ZA26Q00019 for Towel Rental and Laundering Services. This solicitation will not result in an award. The Government intends to revise the acquisition strategy and re-solicit this requirement on an unrestricted (full and open competition) basis. The new solicitation will be posted on SAM.gov. The Government appreciates the interest and effort of all vendors who submitted quotations.

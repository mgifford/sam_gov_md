# IDIQ - Laboratory Gloves

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 12:12:26.27-04
- Solicitation Number: PR-000784

## Summary

The FBI intends to award a single award, Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract for latex and nitrile laboratory gloves, for the Laboratory Division (LD). This requirement is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. Please see the attached RFP for complete details and instructions. Questions pertaining to the solicitation are due by 5:00 PM/EST on Friday, May 1st, 2026. All questions shall be submitted using the enclosed Offeror Question Submission Attachment. Proposals must be received by 5:00 PM/EST on Friday, May 8th, 2026.

# X-ray Diffractometer Maintenance for Empyrean Diffractometer

- Agency: NATIONAL AERONAUTICS AND SPACE ADMINISTRATION
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-10 08:25:28.656-04
- Solicitation Number: 80NSSC26926952Q

## Summary

Synopsis: NASA/NSSC has a requirement for X-ray Diffractometer Maintenance for Empyrean Diffractometer. NASA/NSSC intends to issue a sole source contract to Malvern Panalytical Inc: 2400 Computer Dr, STE 201 | Westborough, MA 01581 under the authority of FAR 13.106-1(b)(1)(i). It has been determined that Malvern Panalytical Inc is the sole provider of X-ray Diffractometer Maintenance for Empyrean Diffractometer. NASA/NSSC will be the procuring center for this effort. Performance will be located at NASA/ Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC). The Government intends to acquire commercial items and services using the provisions, clauses and procedures prescribed in FAR Part 12 and FAR Part 13. The NAICS Code for this procurement is 811210. Interested organizations may submit their capabilities and qualifications to perform the effort in writing to the identified point of contact no later than 3:00 p.m. Central Standard Time on 3/12/2026. Such capabilities/qualifications will be evaluated solely for the purpose of determining whether or not to conduct this procurement on a competitive basis. A determination by the Government not to compete this proposed effort on a full and open competition basis, based upon responses to this notice, is solely within the discretion of the government. Oral communications are not acceptable in response to this notice. NASA Clause 1852.215-84, Ombudsman, is applicable. The Center Ombudsman for this acquisition can be found at http://prod.nais.nasa.gov/pub/pub_library/Omb.html . Primary Point of Contact: Cody Guidry Procurement Specialist Cody.d.guidry@nasa.gov Secondary Point of Contact: Shari Trigg Contracting Officer Shari.trigg@nasa.gov

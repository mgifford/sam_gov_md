# K-12 Visual Arts Resource Platform and Professional Learning Platform

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 11:13:06.494-04
- Solicitation Number: HE125426QE018

## Summary

DoWEA requires a K-12 Visual Arts Resource Platform and a Visual Arts Professional Learning Platform that is a mature, fully developed product capable of fully functioning on the first day of contract performance. The platform must be fully developed, fully operational, and require minimal to no modifications upon deployment. It shall include a comprehensive bank of art-specific instructional materials and have teacher-specific tools to guide improvement in art education. DoWEA also requires professional learning via a mature, fully developed K-12 visual arts professional learning platform that (1) provides differentiated, on-demand educational opportunities in the visual arts and visual arts pedagogy and (2) includes a comprehensive bank of visual arts-specific asynchronous courses/webinars and/or digital modules that promote improvement in arts education and that arts educators can access at their convenience. Note: Attachments identified in this solicitation contain requirements that are integral to the solicitation and must be addressed by Offerors, as applicable.

## Summary

The Department of Strategic Wargaming (DSW) requires skills provided by contracted wargame designers and developers to meet the growing demand for academic wargames serving as experiential learning mechanisms and analytical wargames assisting Army Service Component Commands (ASCCs) and other government agencies in defining complex problems and deriving insights for potential solutions.

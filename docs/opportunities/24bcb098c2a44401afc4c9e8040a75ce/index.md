# Ferrous and Non-Ferrous Metals and High Performance Plastics

- Agency: ENERGY, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-19 17:33:46.649-04
- Solicitation Number: 2026-KG-10

## Summary

Background The Nevada National Security Site (NNSS) is a U.S. Department of Energy, National Nuclear Security Administration (NNSA) installation, operated by Mission Support and Test Services, LLC (MSTS or CONTRACTOR), comprising approximately 3,561 square kilometers (1,375 square miles) of federally owned land located in southeastern Nye County, Nevada. Located approximately 105 kilometers (65 miles) northwest of Las Vegas, Nevada, the NNSS is accessed from U.S. Highway 95, which roughly forms the southern boundary of the site. The MSTS also operates the North Las Vegas Facility (NLV); the Remote Sensing Lab at Nellis AFB, North Las Vegas, NV (RSLN); the Remote Sensing Lab at Andrews AFB, Maryland (RSLA); Special Technologies Lab at Santa Barbara, CA (STL); Livermore Operations at Livermore, CA (LO); and Los Alamos Operations at Los Alamos, NM (LAO). Objective MSTS shall provide a Subcontractor to supply metal and plastic materials on an as-needed basis via task orders for a two-year base ordering period, with three (3) additional one-year options for renewal (totaling up to 5 years). Description of Work MSTS (NLV) facility and (NNSS) Site are seeking a strategic supply partner for a diverse portfolio of upcoming projects. We require a comprehensive supplier capable of providing a wide variety of ferrous and non-ferrous metals, as well as high-performance engineering plastics. Our needs include, but are not limited to: Alloy Steel Aluminum Brass Bronze Copper Plastics Stainless Steel Steel Tantalum Titanium Tungsten Reliable sourcing, consistent quality, and, if available, secondary processing services are key requirements. All shipments must be accompanied by Certificate of conformance (CoC) and material certifications at the time of delivery. Responses to this notice should include the following: Company name and Unique Entity Identifier (UEI) to confirm company is registered in the System for Award Management (SAM.gov) Company business size for NAICS code 332999. Capability statement and/or brochure or applicable past performance information that demonstrates that the service can be provided by your company. A list of all available ferrous and non-ferrous metals, as well as high-performance engineering plastics that can be supplied by the company. Please include all manufacturers available. General Conditions The submission of a response to this opportunity does not create any implied or express contract between an Offeror and MSTS obligating MSTS to review or evaluate the sources sought or to make an award in accordance with the evaluation and selection criteria specified herein. The sources sought and the information specified herein are strictly for the convenience of MSTS. By submitting a response to this opportunity, Offerors acknowledge and agree that they shall not be entitled to any compensation, damages or remuneration of any kind whatsoever in the event that MSTS fails to follow, or deviates from, these sources sought.

## Summary

The Defense Logistics Agnecy (DLA) in support of the United States Air Force Life Cycle Propulsion Sustainment International Engine Branch, awarded contract SPRHA1-26-D-0001 to Pratt & Whitney (CAGE 52661). This contract is the award resulting from solicitation SPRTA1-23-R-0196. Through orders placed against the contract, DLA will procure the remanufacture of F100-PW-100/200/22E/229/229EEP engine modules to include the Inlet Fan, Core, Fan Drive Turbine, High Pressure Turbine, and Gearbox. The contract is a 10-year Firm Fixed Price Requirements contract with one 3-year base, one 3-year option, and one 4-year option. The effort is 100% Foreign Military Sales. Redacted J&A is posted as an attachment.

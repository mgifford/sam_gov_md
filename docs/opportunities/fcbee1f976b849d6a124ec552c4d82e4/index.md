## Summary

6 April 2026 Please be informed that the subject Request for Quote (RFQ) has been amended. The purpose of this RFQ amendment is to extend the RFQ due date and time. In accordance with this RFQ amendment, quotes are now due 9 April 2026 at 10:00 AM Mountain Time. The following changes are also being incorporated into the current RFQ amendment: 1. Revise Attachment 1 - Statement of Need 2. Post Government answers to industry questions Please review the amended RFQ in its entirety. An acknowledgment of the amended RFQ is required with RFQ submission. ----------------------------------------------- (i) This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1305M26Q0071 is issued as a request for quotation (RFQ) for a time-and-materials purchase order providing compressed gases to the Global Monitoring Laboratory (GML) in Boulder, CO. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. (ii) A list of line-item numbers can be found in the SF1449 within the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071, and items, quantities, and units of measure can be found in the attachment labeled Attachment 2 - Pricing Schedule. (iv) Dates and place of delivery and acceptance and f.o.b. point can be found in clauses CAR 1352.246-70 and CAR 1352.270-70 within Section 3 - Contract Terms and Conditions of the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071. (v) A list of solicitation provisions that apply to the acquisition can be found in Section 5 - Provisions within the attachment labeled RFQ (CSS) 1305M326Q0071.

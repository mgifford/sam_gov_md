## Summary

The 59th Medical Wing True North Religious Support Team (RST) in San Antonio, Texas has a requirement for non-personal services as described in this PWS. This a requirement for a 2-night, 3-day family retreat on the dates of 13-15 March 2026. During the retreat the chaplain shall provide training each day for the couples on developing strong relationships. This training shall be conducted in the conference rooms and shall include topics on spiritual and emotional connection, trust, and communication.

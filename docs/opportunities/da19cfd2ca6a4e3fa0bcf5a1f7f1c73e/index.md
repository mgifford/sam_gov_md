# MARKET SURVEY CAPABILITY ASSESSMENT FOR VARIOUS AIRCRAFT SYSTEM TRAINING COURSES

- Agency: TRANSPORTATION, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-18 08:21:29.185-04

## Summary

The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), Mike Monroney Aeronautical Center (MMAC), Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, has a requirement for Level 1 Aircraft Systems Training for Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Aviation Inspectors (19 different Aircraft). The FAA contemplates award of a Fixed Price - Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity type contract with 1-year base with 4 one year option periods. The award will be given to the vendor with the lowest price technically acceptable proposal. Multiple awards will be considered. NO PHONE CALLS PLEASE. ALL QUESTIONS MUST BE IN WRITING AND EMAILED TO THE CONTRACT SPECIALIST. CONTACT INFORMATION BELOW. All interested sources should first attempt to download the attached SIR document(s). If downloading is not possible all request for copies of the SIR document(s) must be made in writing to the attention of Joe Oglesby, Contracting Specialist (AAQ-740), via email at joe.oglesby@faa.gov. Technical and cost proposals must be received by the FAA no later than 1 P.M. CST, April 3, 2026. Bids can be electronically submitted to joe.oglesby@faa.gov. In accordance with SIR Section L provision 3.2.2.3-14 entitled, "Late Submissions, Modifications, and Withdrawals of Submittals," any proposals received after the due time and date of 1:00 P.M. CST, April 3, 2026, will not be considered. In addition, if all requested information is not furnished in the offeror's proposal, the offeror's proposal may be determined to be non-responsive and ineligible for consideration for contract award.

## Summary

Award Notice for Project Number: 636A6-22-104 Replace Boiler Plant Brine Tank. Contractor shall provide all labor, materials, tools, equipment, etc. necessary to replace the two brine generation tanks and one brine tank located in Building 11 with a system utilizing free-standing poly tanks. Work includes general construction, alterations, roads, walks, grading, drainage, necessary removal of existing structures and construction, temporary equipment, temporary power, temporary dust and entry barriers, mobilization and demobilization, site restoration and cleanup in accordance with the contract documents. Period of Performance for this work is 365 Calendar Days from issuance of Notice to Proceed (NTP), to include material lead time.

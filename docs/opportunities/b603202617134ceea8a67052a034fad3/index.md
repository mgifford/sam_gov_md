# Industry Day for the Army MIOSS Program

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-12 10:37:09.254-04

## Summary

Army Contracting Command - Orlando (ACC-Orlando), in support of Capability Program Executive Simulation, Training, Test & Threat (CPE-ST3) and Combined/Joint Training Readiness Support (CJTRS), is holding an Industry Day for the Medical Simulation Training Center (MSTC) Instructor Operator Support Services (MIOSS) program. This event will be held at Fort Stewart, Georgia on, Tuesday, 31 March 2026. The event will begin at 09:00 am at Bldg number 3002. This event will be limited, due to space, to only 2 personnel from each contractor. Please send your response on if your company will be attending along with the names of the two (2) personnel from your firm who will be attending to: usarmy.orlando.peo-stri.mbx.mioss@army.mil To access Fort Stewart for those needing a pass: Online Pre-registration Visitor Pass Use link https://pass.aie.army.mil/steps/branch_selection or click above “Online Pre-registration Visitor Pass” to obtain a general passes up to 60 days based on reason for visit. Individuals will receive status of their requested pass via text messages to inform if their pass is approved, denied, or system error issues. Pass is then linked to individuals’ driver’s license or can be picked up in person at the VCC during operating hours 0600-1800. For more information call: 571-801-0273 or 571-801-0277.

# 100% ISBEE Set-Aside, combined synopsis/solicitation to provide Medical Technologist or Medical Laboratory Technician Service to the I H S Pine Ridge Hospital located at 607 Indian Health Road, Pine Ridge, Sou

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 14:36:19.848-04
- Solicitation Number: 75H70626Q00037

## Summary

The purpose of this contract is to establish a multiple option contract to provide Medical Technologist services to the IHS Pine Ridge Service Unit (PRSU) Hospital for direct patient care to the Laboratory Department at the Pine Ridge I H S Hospital and Outlying Health Centers requires a minimum of one (1) and a maximum of ten (10) Medical Technologist or Medical Laboratory Technician with skills in general laboratory medicine (moderate & high complexity) whose mission is to provide full-scope, quality laboratory services to patients of the Oglala Sioux Tribe. Based on historical workload requirements at the Service Unit, base period requires 5,400 service hours and each option period requires 3,600 service hours. Contract Delivery type will be a Firm Fixed Unit Price Performance Based Service contract.

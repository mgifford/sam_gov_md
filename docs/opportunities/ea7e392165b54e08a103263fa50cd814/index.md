The 148th Fighter Wing has a requirement for two (2) F-16 Internal Vent and Pressure Valves to be diagnosed and repaired. Details can be found below: Internal Vent and Pressure Valves Quantity: 2 Original Manufacturer: Parker-Hannafin NSN: 4810-01-054-9843 ------------------------------------------------------------------- Please respond to the following questions: 1.

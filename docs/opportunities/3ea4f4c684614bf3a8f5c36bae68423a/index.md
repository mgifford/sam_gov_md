# TDS T-6 Immersive Training Devices (ITDs) Metal Parts

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-27 11:00:15.891-04
- Solicitation Number: FA301626Q0040

## Summary

Immersive Training Devices (ITDs) which will be used as a cost-effective simulator to instruct users on T-6A flight operations. The T-6A ITD will consist of a T-6A Power Control Lever (PCL), a T-6B flight stick, and a T-6A simulator landing gear control. All these components, including the custom-made mount, will be designed and manufactured in-house with the metal parts.

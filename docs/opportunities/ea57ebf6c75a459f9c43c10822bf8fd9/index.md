# Pre-Solicitation Notice - SPE3S1-26-R-0005 - Rations National Contract (RNC) Bakery Items for MRE Assembly

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-24 14:37:07.544-04
- Solicitation Number: SPE3S1-26-R0005

## Summary

Presolicitation notice to industry. Solicitation for Rations National Contract (RNC) Bakery Components will soon be published. Bakery components are included in the assembly of the Meal, Ready-to-Eat (MRE). This acquisition is for the bakery components utilized in the MRE program. Applicable NAICS codes for this acquisition are as follows: 311821 - Cookie and Cracker Manufacturing 311812 - Commercial Bakeries 311830 - Tortilla Manufacturing

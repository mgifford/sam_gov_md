# 48 foot Reefer Trailer Lease

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-09 08:52:55.386-04
- Solicitation Number: M0026326QD011
- Awardee: null 29905-9001

## Summary

One (1) 48 foot Refrigerated Trailer (Reefer) Lease The contractor shall provide refrigerated (Reefer) trailer for lease for a period of five (5) months, from 1 May 2026 through 30 September 2026. Specifications: Trailer length: must be 48 feet Width: shall be minimum 8 feet, maximum 10 feet Unit must be capable of operating as both: o Refrigerator (30F) o Freezer (down to 0F) Trailer must support easy loading/unloading, with the ability to be picked up, dropped off, and repositioned as needed Rear doors: Roll-up style required Height: Standard dock height compatible Power Requirements: Voltage requirements no less than 240V, 60 amps, 3-phase, 4-wire electrical cord Ability to be powered by diesel or gas Maintenance and Reliability: Contractor shall perform all preventive and corrective maintenance at no additional cost In the event of equipment failure or inability to maintain required temperatures, the contractor shall provide a replacement unit within 48 hours Delivery and Pricing: Pricing shall be all-inclusive, to include: o Delivery o Setup o Pickup/removal

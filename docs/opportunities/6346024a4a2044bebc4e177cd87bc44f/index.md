## Summary

The Naval Sea Systems Command (NAVSEA) awarded contract N00024-26-C-6234 to General Dynamics Mission Systems for the development, integration, testing, production, and installation of Multifunction Modular Mast (MMM) Payload Sensor systems. This cost-plus-fixed-fee (CPFF), cost-plus-incentive-fee (CPIF), firm-fixed-price (FFP), and cost type contract was competitively procured through full and open competition.

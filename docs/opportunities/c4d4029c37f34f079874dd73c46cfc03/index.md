## Summary

The subject requirement is for the acquisition of NSN 6116013697679 (FUEL CELL, ELECTRICAL). CAGE MANUFACTURER PART_NUMBER 21070 TELEDYNE INSTRUMENTS INC C06689-B3M DLA L&M proposes to enter into fixed-price contract(s) with an annual price adjustment provisions to adjust out-year contract prices.

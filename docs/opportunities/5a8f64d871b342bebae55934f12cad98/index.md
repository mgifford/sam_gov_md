# VJ Tech Computed Tomography System Preventative Maintenance

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-26 15:12:20.713-04
- Solicitation Number: FA812626Q0029

## Summary

The Contractor shall provide all labor, tools, equipment, test equipment, material, parts, quality control, transportation and inspection manuals necessary to perform preventative maintenance (PM) for the VJ Tech Computed Tomography System in accordance with the performance work statement (PWS). The Contractor shall perform four (4) PM per year on the equipment listed within the Appendix A.

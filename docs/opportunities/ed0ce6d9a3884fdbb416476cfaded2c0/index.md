## Summary

FAR 8.405-6(a)(1)(i)(B) Limiting Sources. Limited-Sources Justification - Electricity Services for Irvington Site Office. A new Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Task Order, W9127826FA070, awarded under the procedures outlined in the Revolutionary Federal Acquisition Regulation Overhaul (RFO) Part 41 “Acquisition of Utility Services” is contemplated, GSA Contract Number: 47PA0425D0028. This contract will be funded using non-expiring Operation & Maintenance General (O&MG) funding. Provide electrical utility services for the Irvington Project Site Offices. Electric services will be provided for a period of ten (10) years.

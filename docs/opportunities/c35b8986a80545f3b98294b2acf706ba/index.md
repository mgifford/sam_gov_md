# Bridge Extension: Support Services for the Office of the Secretary of War (OSW), Office of the Assistant Secretary of War for Critical Technologies (OASW(CT))

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Justification
- Posted: 2026-03-19 07:55:45.986-04
- Solicitation Number: AJM03112026

## Summary

The purpose of this notice is to post the Justification for Other Than Full and Open Competition (JOFOC). Washington Headquarters Services, Acquisition Directorate (WHS/AD) awarded a Firm Fixed Price (FFP) Bridge Contract to Science Applications International Corporation (SAIC) for Specialized Technical and Program Management Support Services for the Office of the Secretary of Defense, Office of the Assistant Secretary of Defense for Critical Technologies (OASD(CT)). This contract was awarded sole source under the authority of Federal Acquisition Regulation 12.1, Restricting Competition. This notice provides a copy of the approved justification in accordance with FAR 6.103-1.

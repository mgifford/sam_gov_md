# SFS Drone Gun

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-12 13:30:27.145-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8N26QA015

## Summary

This Solicitation is issued as a Request for Quotation, solicitation number W50S8N26QA015 in accordance with FAR parts 12 and 13, and is in accordance with provisions anc clauses effective in Federal Acquisition Circular (FAC 2025-06 effective 1 Oct 2025). The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) number is 334511 and the small business size standard is 1,350 employees. the proposed acquisition is 100% set-aside for Small-Business. CLIN 0001- Drone Gun, portable handheld drone countermeasure. Must meet the following minimum salient characteristics: Drone Gun Salient Characteristics Performance Effective Range Up to 1km (distance ratio dependent, >1:1) Operating Time >1 hour (Active Disruption) Start-Up Time <3 seconds Product Specifications Effective Frequencies 2.4GHz ISM, 5.2GHz ISM, 5.8GHz ISM, 1.2GHz, 915MHz ISM GNSS: GNSS (L1/L2/L5), GLONASS, GLONASS, BeiDou, Galileo, NAVIC, QZSS Operating Modes All Bands Mode (default) All Bands Mode with GNSS disruption Selectable Bands Mode Selectable Bands Mode with GNSS Battery and Power Battery NATO-standard military grade battery Chemistry Rechargeable lithium-ion battery Nominal Voltage 10.8V Compatibility PRC-152 and PRC-163 batteries Environment Operating Temperature Tested to -32ºC to +49ºC / -25.6ºF to +120.2ºF Ingress Protection Rated to IP67 Compliance Certifications TAA and Berry compliant MIL-STD-810H Manufactured in an ISO9001 compliant facility - Requested delivery period is within 90 days ADC. - Award will be based on lowest price. - Shipping cost must be included in price. Delivery address is: 174 SFS Squadron 6001 E Molly Rd. Syracuse, NY 13211

# Sole Source Justification - InfoPro/Risk.net renewal for 15 users

- Agency: TREASURY, DEPARTMENT OF THE

- Posted: 2026-04-01 11:25:23.693-04

## Summary

This is a sole-source justification in accordance with FAR 5.703(b)(3), the award is a firm-fixed price contract. InfoPro Digital LLC is a sole-source vendor for Risk.net and for that reason, it was not competively competed without any resellers available. The period of performance includes a 12-month base period from 03/28/2026 - 03/27/2027 for 15 users with 4 additional optional users. The total contract value for the award is $140,505.00.

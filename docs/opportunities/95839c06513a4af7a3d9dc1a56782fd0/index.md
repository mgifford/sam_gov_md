# Museum Staffing Fort Riley KS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-15 13:09:30.296-04
- Solicitation Number: W911RX26QA022

## Summary

This requirement is for Museum Staffing Services. The contractor shall furnish all personnel, equipment, supplies, transportation, supervision, and perform duties as a customer service representative (receptionist) and tour guide as defined in the Performance Work Statement (PWS). The contractor shall provide a total of two customer service representatives (receptionists) for buildings 205 and 207. The contract will consist of a base period of (1) year and (4) 1-year option periods. The period of performance will be 15 May 2026 to 14 May 2031. This requirement is considered commercial and the contracting vehicle will be an Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ).

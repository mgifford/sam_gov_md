## Summary

The Office of Strategic Planning and Analysis (NA-183) conducts strategic planning and resource advocacy to sustain and modernize a safe, secure, and effective stockpile for the United States nuclear deterrent on behalf of the Office of Defense Programs (DP). NA-183 leads the integration of DP planning processes for infrastructure, workforce, long-range budgeting, and nuclear security enterprise capabilities as part of the development and publication of the Stockpile Stewardship and Management Plan (SSMP). NA-183 also leads efforts for monitoring the Nuclear Industrial Base along with Committee on Foreign Investment in the United States (CFIUS) activities for DP. This contract will support the development of DP strategic plans, the annual development of the SSMP, industrial base monitoring at the enterprise level, and CFIUS activities

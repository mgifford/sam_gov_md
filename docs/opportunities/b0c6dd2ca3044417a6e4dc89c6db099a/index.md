## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial items prepared in accordance with the format in FAR Part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotations are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 1232SA26Q0252 is issued as a Request for Quotation (RFQ) for the USDA-ARS, Eastern Regional Research Center, in Wyndmoor, PA Phone Systems Support. The ERRC requires maintenance and support services to its VoIP Mitel telephone system. This acquisition is set-aside for small business concerns. The applicable North American Industry Classification Standard Code is 811210 The small business size standard is $34M This acquisition is a Total Small Business Set-Aside. All responsible sources may submit a quotation which will be considered by the agency.

# Paramedic Certification Training

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-08 11:12:23.934-04
- Solicitation Number: FA8003NRPRFI

## Summary

This is a non-personal services contract to provide National Registry Paramedic (NRP) Certification courses. EMT Paramedic Certification course shall certify up to one hundred and twenty-five (125) AFMS Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs) annually to the NRP level in accordance with (IAW) the established standards set by the National Registry of Emergency Medical Technicians (NREMT) Paramedic certification guidelines and the National EMS Education Standards and Paramedic Instructional Guidelines. Course length for each student shall not exceed 139 calendar days. **Follow instructions outlined in attached RFI.**

# PREMISE WIRING B145

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-20 15:48:35.406-04
- Solicitation Number: FA462626Q0009

## Summary

Engineer, furnish, install, and test (EFI&T) a premises wiring upgrade to new plenum-rated Cat-6 and OM4 multimode (MM) fiber cabling for Building 145 (B145) at Malmstrom AFB, MT. The Cat-6 and OM4 MM fiber drop layouts are shown in Attachment A, Layout Drawings. The upgrade shall include installation and replacement of communications (comms) racks/cabinets, installation of new AC power with power distribution unit (PDU), and comms rack/cabinet grounding. The Contractor shall remove all existing outdated and abandoned-in-place (AIP) Cat-3/5 and MM fiber cabling, along with any residual materials, from the building and dispose of them accordingly. See Attached SOO for full details.

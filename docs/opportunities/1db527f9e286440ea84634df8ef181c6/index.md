## Summary

United States Coast Guard (USCG) Air Station Traverse City is seeking a contractor to replace the air conditioning plant. Refer to the Scope of Work for requirements. The site address is: U.S. Coast Guard Air Station Traverse City 1175 Airport Access Road Traverse City, MI 49686-3513. The Project Point of Contact is MKC Charles Osborne who can be contacted at (810) 441-8247 or via e-mail at Charles.R.Osborne@uscg.mil. Site visits are highly encouraged and recommended for the development of an accurate bid package. The period of performance shall not exceed 15-APR-2026 unless otherwise approved by the Contracting Officer. Refer to the Scope of Work for details. This procurement is estimated to be below $25,000.00 The applicable NAICS is 238220 Plumbing, Heating, and Air-Conditioning Contractors with a Small Business Size Standard of $19.0M. All relevant solicitation documents, including specifications and drawings will be available for download exclusively from the Internet at this website. Interested parties should utilize the website's Register to Receive Notification function by registering under the solicitation number 70Z03026QCLEV0018. THE DUE DATE FOR OFFERS WILL BE ESTABLISHED UPON ISSUANCE OF THE REQUEST FOR QUOTE. Any amendments issued to this solicitation will be posted solely on SAM.gov.

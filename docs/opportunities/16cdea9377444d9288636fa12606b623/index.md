# Notice of Intent to Sole Source to Accurate Circuit Engineering, Inc.

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-22 14:57:15.475-04
- Solicitation Number: N66001-26-Q-6125

## Summary

The Department of the Navy, Naval Information Warfare Center Pacific (NIWC Pacific) hereby publicizes its intention to solicit, negotiate, and award a Firm-Fixed-Price purchase order on a noncompetitive basis with Accurate Circuit Engineering, Inc. of Santa Ana, California, pursuant to FAR 12.102. The requirement is for the purchase of the following: Item 0001: Antenna Printed Circuit Board Manufacturer: Accurate Circuit Engineering, Inc. P/N: TRX64_V1 QTY: 10 Item 0002: Stencil/Setup Manufacturer: Accurate Circuit Engineering, Inc. QTY:1 Item 0003: 10 sets of components Manufacturer: Accurate Circuit Engineering, Inc. QTY: 1 Item 0004: 10 pcs Assembly Manufacturer: Accurate Circuit Engineering, Inc. QTY: 1 Based on an approved Justification for Non-Competitive Procurement (JNCP) the agency has identified only one responsible source, and no other supplier will satisfy agency requirements. A redacted JNCP is attached to this Notice of Intent. A determination by the Government not to compete based upon responses to this notice is solely at the discretion of the Government. This notice is not a request for competitive quotes; however, interested parties may identify their interest and capabilities in response to this notice, and must clearly demonstrate the firm?s capability to meet the Government?s minimum technical and delivery requirements without unacceptable risk to quality, performance, or schedule. Capability statements, comments, questions, or concerns regarding this notice shall be submitted via e-mail. The point of contact for this announcement is Kylie Nevells at kylie.m.nevells.civ@us.navy.mil. Be advised that the aforementioned information is anticipatory in nature and is not binding. This notice is for informational purposes only and is not a request for competitive quotations. All responding vendors must be registered to the System for Award Management (SAM). Information can be found at https://www.sam.gov. Complete SAM registration means a registered UEI and CAGE Code numbers. Any comments, questions or concerns regarding this notice may be submitted via e-mail to kylie.m.nevells.civ@us.navy.mil by 27 April 2026 at 0800 Pacific. Questions by phone or fax will not be considered.

# High Voltage Switches

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 13:05:18.403-04
- Solicitation Number: Switches0001

## Summary

Amendment 1 - Extension of the Proposal Due Date from 27 March, 2026 to 13 April, 2026. This is a requirement for supplies for High Voltage Switches and Switchgear. It is brand name for S&C Electric brand Switchgear, Power Fuses, and Fuse Holders, and other miscellaneous related items. The 21st Contracting Squadron is issuing a solicitation for High Voltage Switches and Switchgear on Peterson Space Force Base (PSFB), CO. The supplies required must be in accordance with Table 1 in the attached Solicitation FA251726Q0010. Type of Contract and NAICS: The resulting award will be a firm-fixed price purchase order and will be made to the responsible offeror whose quote conforms to the solicitation requirements. The North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code for this service is 335313 – Switchgear and Switchboard Apparatus Manufacturing , and the small business size standard is 1,250 Employees. Type of Set-Aside: This acquisition is a 100% total small business set-aside. Registrations: Offerors shall have and shall maintain an active registration in the System for Award Management (SAM) database at http://www.sam.gov to be eligible for a Government contract award. If at the time of award an Offeror is not actively and successfully registered in the SAM database, the Government reserves the right to award to the next prospective Offeror. It is the Contractor's responsibility to monitor the SAM.gov website for information, updates, and amendments regarding the solicitation. Points of Contact: Contract Specialist: Kazuo Colunga (719) 556-9088, Kazuo.colunga@spaceforce.mil Contracting Officer: SSgt Kaylie Anderson, (719) 556-6673, kaylie.anderson@spaceforce.mil Questions: Please submit questions related to this solicitation to Kazuo Colunga at kazuo.colunga@spaceforce.mil and SSgt Kaylie Anderson at kaylie.anderson@spaceforce.mil no later than Monday, 23 March 2026, at 3:00 PM MDT. Due Date/Time of Offers: Solicitation closes and quotes are due to Kazuo Colunga via email (kazuo.colunga@spaceforce.mil ) and SSgt Kaylie Anderson (kaylie.anderson@spaceforce.mil ) no later than Monday, 13 April 2026, at 2:00 P.M. MDT. See the solicitation and attachments for more information.

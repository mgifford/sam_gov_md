# Regulated Medical Waste (RMW) Disposal and Incineration Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Award Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-26 09:52:17.302-04
- Solicitation Number: HT942626DE002
- Awardee: CURTIS BAY ENERGY INC Curtis Bay MD 21226 USA
- Award Amount: 947830.00

## Summary

This requirement is for severable, non-personal services in support of USAMRIID, WRAIR, and NMRC-Frederick, and any tenant of Fort Detrick and/or USAMRDC, requiring services from a source with extensive knowledge and capability corresponding to the transportation, disposal and incineration of Regulated Medical Waste (RMW) in accordance with current Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), and the Maryland Department of the Environment (MDE) standard and policies.

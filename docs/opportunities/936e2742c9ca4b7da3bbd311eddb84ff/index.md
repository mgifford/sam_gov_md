## Summary

KMRD Hyder Seasonal Housing Facility This is a non-personal services contract to provide a housing facility for the Forest Service Ketchikan-Misty Fjords Ranger District (KMRD), Tongass National Forest (TNF), Alaska. Background and Purpose: KMRD owns and operates the Fish Creek Wildlife Observation Site in Hyder, Alaska. Hyder is a remote Southeast Alaska community with approximately 50–100 residents, located about 75 air miles from Ketchikan, Alaska, and 2 miles from Stewart, British Columbia. KMRD staffs the Fish Creek Wildlife Observation Site annually from July through September. During this operational season, KMRD employees must reside in Hyder; however, KMRD currently does not have adequate facilities to house these employees and therefore requires the rental of suitable seasonal housing. Description: The contractor shall furnish all labor, supervision, management, tools, materials, equipment, facilities, transportation, and other items and non-personal services necessary to provide a housing facility in Hyder, Alaska, for use by employees of the Forest Service Ketchikan–Misty Fjords Ranger District. Place of Performance: The housing facility shall be located in Hyder, AK 99923. Period of Performance: The period of performance shall be for 1 Base Period of 12-months and 4 Option Periods of 12-months each. Contract Type: The Government will award a Firm-Fixed Price, Requirements, Non-Personal Services Contract with up to a 5-Year Ordering Period. Amendment 0001: This solicitation has been amended to address offeror questions; revise the PWS, Schedule of Items & Price Sheet, and Provisions & Clauses; and extend the response due date to 20 April 2026.

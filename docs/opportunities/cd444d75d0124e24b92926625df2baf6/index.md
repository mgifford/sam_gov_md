# Heavy-Duty Professional Grade Lowboy Equipment Trailer

- Agency: JUSTICE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-20 14:26:40.424-04
- Solicitation Number: D-26-MI-0076

## Summary

The DEA has a requirement for the refurbishment and welding of lowboy equipment trailer. The DEA intends to award a firm-fixed price sole source purchase order to Josam Frame and Aligment, 8849 Exchange Dr., Orlando, FL 32809, for the purpose of trailer repair services. This acquisition is being pursued on a sole source basis in accordance with 13.106-1(b)(l)(i) Only One Source Reasonably Available. The NAICS code for this acquisition is 811111. A determination by the Government not to open the requirement to competition based on the response to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. Information received will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. This notice is NOT a request for proposal. However, all capability statements received by April 23, 2026 at 2:00 PM EST will be considered by the Government. Any source capable of providing the trailer repair services and is interested in submitting a capabilities statement may do so electronically to Ms. Angel Barkley at angel.d.barkley@dea.gov.

# EXHAUST SILENCER

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 12:32:28.587-04
- Solicitation Number: N0040626Q0119

## Summary

Fleet Logistics Center Puget Sound (FLCPS) intends to solicit for EXHAUST SILENCER, EXHAUST BELLOWS ASSEMBLY, AND EXHAUST ASSEMBLY. The Exhaust Silencer Part Number is 501-C 5106-1-S-29004. The part number for the Exhaust Bellows Assembly is GA304717. The part number for the Exhaust Assembly is GA304718. BRAND NAME: This requirement is BRAND NAME to GT EXHAUST SYSTEMS INCORPORATED. Interested companies may identify their interest and capability to respond to the requirement, which will be considered solely for the purpose of determining whether to conduct a competitive procurement. The determination to compete this action based upon responses received to this notice is solely within the discretion of the Government. The Government will evaluate the offeror’s price for reasonableness and past performance as part of the responsibility determination in accordance with RFO 12.203. Quotes will be evaluated in accordance with RFO 12.203, the Government will select the most advantageous offer based on the following evaluation factors: technical including delivery date, price, and past performance in no order of relative importance. NOTE: The customer is requesting expedited shipping on or before 4 May 2026. The expedited delivery will be the evaluation factor. Anticipated Award Date: April / 2026 Contracting Officer: Taura Helms, taura.s.helms.civ@us.navy.mil

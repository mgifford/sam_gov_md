# Demolish Vacant Barracks Buildings 532 and 132H Naval Station Great Lakes

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-06 08:38:23.078-05
- Solicitation Number: N4008526R0090

## Summary

3/6/26 Amendment 0002 issued. 3/2/26 Amendment 0001 issued. The Contractor shall furnish all labor, tools, transportation, supervision, materials, inspections, equipment, and safety compliance necessary to perform all work required to completely demolish and remove Building 532, a vacant 3-story student barrack building with a partial basement, onboard Naval Station Great Lakes, IL. The demolition for building 532 comprises the entire building including all foundation walls, foundation footings, all individual column footings, and associated utilities. The Contractor shall furnish all labor, tools, transportation, supervision, materials, inspections, equipment, and safety compliance necessary to perform all work required to completely demolish and remove Building 132H, a vacant 4-story student barrack onboard Naval Station Great Lakes in Great Lakes, IL. The demolition for building 132H comprises the entire building including all foundation walls, foundation footings, all individual column footings, and associated utilities.

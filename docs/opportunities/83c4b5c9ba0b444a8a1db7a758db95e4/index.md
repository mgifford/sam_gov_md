# Airsoft Training Equipment

- Agency: STATE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-01 12:45:56.168-04
- Solicitation Number: 191NLE26Q0059

## Summary

The mission of the Bureau of International Narcotics and Law Enforcement Affairs (INL) is to minimize the impact of international crime and transnational narcotics trafficking on the United States, its citizens and partner nations. An important component is support to the Haitian National Police (HNP) School in training cadets on urban combat; the following material requested below will support the HNP School in providing urban combat training to cadets through use of Airsoft training equipment.

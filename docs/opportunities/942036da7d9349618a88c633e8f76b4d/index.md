# ZERO TURN LAWN MOWER 60 INCH

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-16 08:19:00.579-04
- Solicitation Number: W911S226U3038

## Summary

This machine is powered by a minimum 27-horsepower, 2-cylinder gasoline engine that features electronic fuel injection, eliminating the need for a choke. The engine is also equipped with a replaceable oil filter. It includes a 60-inch fabricated mowing deck with greaseable spindles and a hydrostatic transmission with a cooling fan. For safety and maintenance, the mower has ROPS bar protection, a hand-operated parking brake, and an easy-access floor plate for mower deck spindle access.The frame features front and rear mounted suspension with shock absorbers, and the fuel tank has a capacity greater than five gallons. For operator comfort and convenience, there is an adjustable suspension seat with a seat belt, a fuel level gauge, a USB port, an hour meter, and a cup holder.The unit will be delivered with IUID labels, fully assembled, and operational. All operator, service, and parts manuals will be included. The preferred delivery date is 120 days after the order, with delivery to Fort Drum, NY.

# THE HHS ACQUISITION SOLUTIONS DAY

- Agency: HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-20 11:17:46.3-04
- Solicitation Number: 2026-05-18

## Summary

Registration is now open for HHS Acquisition Solutions Day! Registration link: https://tinyurl.com/OMASSolutionsDay Join us on May 18 where HHS Office of Acquisition Management Services (OMAS) will share the latest on what’s changing and how OMAS is working with industry partners to support the mission. Why attend? Get an up-to-date view of OMAS priorities and what’s new Learn how OMAS is engaging with industry partners and what that means for you Hear directly from acquisition leaders shaping the path forward Where & When: The 5600 Conference Center, 5600 Fishers Ln, Rockville, MD 20852 General attendance (in-person & online): 9:45 – 11:50 am ET Invite-only afternoon session (in-person only): 1:00 – 2:30 pm ET During registration, you may indicate interest in attending an invite-only afternoon breakout session focused on sharing the industry’s perspective on what’s working now, and what could work better in the future. Space for the breakout discussion is limited, and you will receive a separate confirmation if selected. We highly encourage you to sign up for HHS SBCX (https://osdbu.hhs.gov) in the meantime! Individuals registered within the HHS SBCX portal will be given priority when invitations are distributed for the invite-only portion of the afternoon session.

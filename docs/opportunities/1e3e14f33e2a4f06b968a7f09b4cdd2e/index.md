# Sources Sought- Audio Visual Maintenance- Gulfport, MS

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-30 07:07:51.914-04
- Solicitation Number: N6883626SSAV2026

## Summary

The purpose of this sources sought Request for Information (RFI) is to identify vendors capable of providing maintenance, repair, relocation, and installation of Audio Visual (AV) equipment in support of NCTC, Gulfport, MS . This notice is a request for vendor's capabilities, and does not serve as a solicitation. The surveillance systems located within two buildings currently consist of an estimated 58 surveillance cameras of varying makes and models, 4 DVR recording units, multiple NEMA-rated environmental enclosures, DVD burners, and UPS battery backup systems. Quantities, manufacturers, and models of installed equipment may change throughout the period of performance as a result of replacement, modernization, or additional equipment acquisitions. Interested parties should respond with the following information: - Company Name - Company Cage and Business Size - Capability of completing service and maintenance calls in Gulfport, MS. - Any other information that would be beneficial to us in determining available options NOTE: This is a Request for Information (RFI) for market research purposes only and does not constitute a solicitation. The Government does not intend to award a contract on the basis of this RFI or to otherwise pay for the information solicited.

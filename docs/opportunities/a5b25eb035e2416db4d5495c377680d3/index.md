NO PHONE CALLS. This is a Brand Name or Equal sources sought notice only and it is not a solicitation announcement. No questions will be answered by the government for this notice. The purpose of this sources sought notice is to gain knowledge of qualified potential contractors and their socio-economic size classification in accordance with NAICS code 339113 - Surgical Appliance and Supplies Manufacturing The Omaha VA Health Care System at Omaha, NE is seeking a supplier to provide the following: AMSCO Drying Cabinet Model-DCC201201 Door Swing Restricting Bracket for AMSCO Cabinet Part Number: DCDOORBR Installation: Part Number-SE101812 Essential/significant physical, functional, or performance characteristics. Salient characteristics are those characteristics that make an item equal to the brand name item we specify. Specifically, salient characteristics are those characteristic of the brand name item that the VA needs. 38 wide cabinet 9 XL full width shelves Scope hanger for up to 4 scopes Touch screen Timer countdown for each shelf. Advises then the devices are nearing their user-selected drying limits. HEPA filtered Air Purge system Air Purge connector for Davinci devices Dual filtration removes 99.97% of particulates from the air Exact digital control up to 58 degrees C / 136.4 degrees F internal cabinet LED lighting; 1 per shelf Door alarm sounds if door left open for 45 seconds to help minimize heat loss Tempered glass doors to allow for operator to easily view the devices being dried Shelves hold up to 25 lb/load Electrical: 120 Vac, 60Hz, 20 Amp standard duplex receptacle Cabinet Dimensions: 38 W x 31 D x 78 H door swing restricting brackets - two brackets per door Removeable, stainless steel drip pan Stainless steel - Easy-to-clean stainless-steel construction Any brand equivalent equipment must be compatible with existing Steris equipment used at Omaha VAMC.

# B158 CRAC Repair

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-22 15:15:01.866-04
- Solicitation Number: W50S8S26QA005

## Summary

This solicitation is strictly restricted to a sole source vendor in accordance with FAR 6.302-1 (Only one responsible source and no other supplies or services will satisfy agency requirements). The contractor shall provide all personnel, equipment, tools, materials, supervision, and quality control necessary to perform refrigerant circuit repairs on two (2) Liebert cooling units (Model: DS105AUA1SI036S) at the 180th Fighter Wing facility, as defined in this Performance Work Statement. Contractor shall be minimum level 1 certified with Vertiv (manufacturer) to service equipment.

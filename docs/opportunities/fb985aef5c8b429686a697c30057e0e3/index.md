# Congressional Information Web Service

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 16:19:58.891-04

## Summary

To obtain a subscription to a web service that will provide email alerts and timely and searchable information about congressional news and schedules, in-depth information on the policy-making process – especially the federal budget and appropriations process, legislative tracking, a searchable Congressional Record, congressional member profiles, and a documents manager for this information.

# Administrative Pastoral Life Coordinator (Kaiserslautern, Germany)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-06 03:31:05.126-04
- Solicitation Number: W564KV26QA013

## Summary

The contractor shall provide Catholic Administrative Pastoral Life Coordination services to the USAG Rheinland-Pfalz Religious Support Office (RSO), serving both the Baumholder Military Community (BMC) and the Kaiserslautern Military Community (KMC). This is a non-personal services contract between the USAG Rheinland-Pfalz RSO and the Contractor to perform tasks and provide deliverables identified herein. The contractor shall become knowledgeable of the Catholic Community and Garrison Religious Support program through the review of program schedules, Concept of Operations, etc. The contractor shall attend and participate in required meetings scheduled by the Parish in order to gain and provide information pertaining to the Catholic Administrative Pastoral Life Coordination tasks. The contractor will develop and provide a monthly Catholic Community calendar with important religious events as well as closures. The Contractor shall create and provide a weekly bulletin and announcements; and assist the Community Event Coordination and Publicity program. The Contractor shall maintain the Catholic Community Registration as well as collect and support the Sacramental Records.

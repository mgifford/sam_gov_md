## Summary

This procurement is unrestricted. The Bureau of Land Management (BLM), Northwest Oregon Office, intends to issue a Request For Quote for a one (1) firm fixed price commercial items services purchase order for outfitting one 2026 Ford F-150 Law Enforcement (LE) Vehicle, BLM Northwest Oregon conducted under FAR Part 13.. The performance period is from date of award through 09/01/2026. The NAICS code is 811198 and the small business size standard $10M. Solicitation is anticipated to be issued on or about 03/11/2026. The basis of award will utilize the lowest price technically acceptable and relevant experience. All responsible sources may submit a proposal which shall be considered by the agency.

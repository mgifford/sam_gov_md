# Notice of Intent– Brand Name Requirement for ELK Intrusion Detection Systems

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-04-22 16:54:32.792-04
- Solicitation Number: W912QR-26-ELKIDS

## Summary

This is not a solicitation or request for quote. The purpose of this notice is to give notification of the Government’s intent to include brand name requirements for Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) in future solicitations executed by the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) for construction projects at Ft. McCoy and all Army Reserve facilities that have alarm signals that report to the Ft. McCoy Army Reserve monitoring station for Fiscal Years 2026 through 2028. The statutory authority permitting Other than Full and Open competition is 10 United States Code (U.S.C.) 3204(a)(1), as implemented by RFO FAR 6.103-1 “Only One Responsible Source and No Other Supplies or Services Will Satisfy Agency Requirements.” This authority is applicable to brand name justifications in accordance with RFO FAR 6.103(d). USACE intends to specify the ELK IDS panel for their alarm monitoring system as part of future construction contracts located at Army Reserve facilities. This ELK alarm panel is the current approved alarm system that can transmit signals over the Army Reserve Network (ARNET) and meets all of USARC’s cybersecurity requirements. In order to connect to the existing monitoring station, the IDS system must be compatible and approved with the monitoring station. The government will not purchase the equipment directly, but rather brand name specifications will be included in solicitations that will lead to new construction contracts. The brand name components include: Panel Enclosure – ELK – TROVE2 Security Panel – ELK – M1 GOLD (Includes Transformer) Keypad – ELK – M1KP Battery – ELK – ELK-1280 ELK - 16 Input Zone Expander – M1XIN ELK - Telephone Line Surge Protector – 951 ELK - 8 Zone Output Expander – M1XOVR ELK - RS-485 Data Bus Hub – M1DBHR ELK - Circuit Board – M1VO ELK - Ethernet Expander – M1XEP ELK - Serial Port Expander – M1XSP ELK - Speaker - ELK-1RT ELK - Siren Driver – ELK-44 This is not a solicitation or request for quote. Responses to this notice will be used to determine the availability of this type of service. All responsible sources may respond to this synopsis and all responses will be considered by the agency. Responses should appear on company letterhead and include, at minimum, affirmation of active registration in the System for Award Management (SAM), your Unique Entity ID (UEI) number, any applicable processed credentials and qualifications, and any applicable pricing data or information. All responses should include a point of contact, telephone number, and electronic mail address. Additionally, respondents should indicate whether they are a large business, small business, small disadvantaged business, 8(a) concern, women-owned small business, HUBZone, service disabled veteran-owned small business, or qualify as socially or economically disadvantaged and whether they are U.S. or foreign owned. Responses should be furnished electronically to charity.a.mansfield@usace.army.mil no later than 12:00 PM Eastern Time Friday, 8 May 2026. Your response must include W912QR-26-ELKIDS in the subject line of your email.

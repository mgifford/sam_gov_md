# General or Specialized Construction Services

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-04-16 06:28:43.754-04
- Solicitation Number: N6945026RXXXX

## Summary

THIS NOTICE IS A REQUEST FOR INFORMATION (RFI) ONLY AND DOES NOT CONSTITUTE A REQUEST FOR EITHER A QUOTE OR PROPOSAL, OR AN INVITATION FOR A BID. THE INTENT OF THIS RFI IS TO IDENTIFY POTENTIAL U.S. SMALL BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION CERTIFIED 8(A) BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM PARTICIPANTS FOR CONSTRUCTION SERVICES AT NAVAL AIR STATION (NAS) JACKSONVILLE AND ITS AREAS OF RESPONSIBILITY (AOR). NO SOLICITATION, SPECIFICATIONS OR DRAWINGS ARE CURRENTLY AVAILABLE. The purpose of this RFI is to gain knowledge of qualified and interested participants under the U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA) certified 8(a) business development program. Potential sources should have current relevant qualifications, experience, personnel, Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification, equipment, and capability to perform general or specialized construction services. Preferred 8(a) participants will have capability of performing trade work with in-house staff. In accordance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) 15.101(c), responses to this notice are not offers and cannot be accepted by the Government to form a binding contract. Respondents will not be notified of the results of the evaluation and any information submitted by respondents to this notice is strictly voluntary. The Government will use the RFI responses along with other market research to make various determinations regarding the procurement of construction activities at NAS Jacksonville. Contracting Officers may decide to pursue single award or stand-alone IDIQs for General or Specialized Construction Services from responses to this RFI; however, this RFI is not a guarantee of any future opportunities. The estimated maximum contract value will vary depending on project or IDIQ determinations but may range from $200,000 to $5,500,000. Any IDIQ will have subsequent task orders ranging from $200,000 to $1,900,000 and will be issued as firm-fixed-price task orders. The applicable North American Industry Classification System (NAICS) code will be determined based on applicable construction projects awarded but will primarily be related to the Sector 23 - Construction codes which have various Small Business Size Standards ranging from $19M to $45M. Description of Work - The Contractor shall be able to provide labor, supervision, materials, equipment, tools, parts, supplies, and transportation, to perform all the services described in the specifications for each contract/task order. Work associated with general construction projects includes specific requirements for renovation, alteration, repair and demolition services (including but not limited to asbestos, lead paint and mold re-mediation) while specialized work may be issued for electrical, roofing, and mechanical. The Contractor shall provide a staff with the necessary management expertise to assure performance objectives and standards are met and permitting to comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws and regulations; Accident Prevention & Activity Hazard Analysis; Environmental Protection; Waste Management. Submission Requirements - Responses are limited to 5 pages in length, 5MB in size and should only include the information requested in the questionnaire attachment titled “RFI_Questionnaire” and a Capability Statement. Responses must be submitted in electronic form (Adobe PDF format) and may be attached to an email addressed to: Ms. Monica Gummerus at Monica.a.gummerus.civ@us.navy.mil and Mr. Stephen Sparks at stephen.b.sparks2.civ@us.navy.mil. The due date for responses is 1:00 PM ET, 30 April 2026. Please identify in the subject line of your response: “RFI NAVFAC SE NAS Jacksonville – [COMPANY NAME]”. NO REIMBURSEMENT WILL BE MADE FOR ANY COSTS ASSOCIATED WITH PROVIDING INFORMATION IN RESPONSE TO THIS RFI OR ANY FOLLOW-UP INFORMATION REQUESTS. NO APPOINTMENTS FOR PRESENTATIONS WILL BE MADE AT THIS TIME.

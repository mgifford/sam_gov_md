# NATO Business Opportunity: Information Environment Assessment Capability (IEAC) Audience Research

- Agency: COMMERCE, DEPARTMENT OF
- Type: Special Notice
- Posted: 2026-03-09 14:54:07.675-04
- Solicitation Number: IFB-424348-IEAC

## Summary

The NATO Communications and Information Agency (NCIA) intends to issue an Invitation for Bid (IFB) for Information Environment Assessment Capability (IEAC) Audience Research. To bid on this project, potential U.S. prime contractors must 1) maintain a professionally active facility (office, factory, laboratory, etc.) within the United States and 2) register with NCIA’s eProcurement tool, Neo: https://www.ncia.nato.int/business/procurement/neo-eprocurement The reference for the IFB is IFB-424348-IEAC and all correspondence concerning the IFB should include this reference. SUMMARY OF REQUIREMENT Please see attached. BECOMING ELIGIBLE TO BID A Declaration of Eligibility (DOE) is NOT required for participation. Indicate your interest by emailing the Point of Contact listed in the attachment to request being added to the bidder’s list and receive the solicitation. IMPORTANT DATES: NCIA distributes the IFB (planned): 30 April 2026 Bid closing (anticipated): 15 June 2026 Contract Award (estimated): 30 November 2026

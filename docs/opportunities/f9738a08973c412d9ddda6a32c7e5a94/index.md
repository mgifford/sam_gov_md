# Purchase of High Speed Envelope Inserter

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-10 12:01:42.747-04
- Solicitation Number: SP700026Q1006

## Summary

Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) Document Services seeks to purchase the following equipment and associated services: One High Speed Envelope Inserter. This purchase includes delivery and installation of the equipment, maintenance and repair of the equipment, Information Technology (IT) Security Accreditation and technical support for the equipment, Training on use and daily upkeep/operations of the equipment, and Documentation on material and supplies with provided hardcopy/digital User Manuals. The purchase will be for an up to 60-month contract with one base period of 10 months, four (4) one year option periods, and one two (2) month option period for maintenance. Device will be located at Robins AFB, GA. All information can be accessed utilizing the PIEE SOLICITATIONS App at: https://.piee.eb.mil/

# 24 in. Cold Planer

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 10:25:57.532-04
- Solicitation Number: FA4855Reag0002

## Summary

Notice: The anticipated solicitation method for this requirement is direct solicitation of quotations from vendors. The Government does not intend to issue a formal written solicitation. Quotations will be requested via oral and/or email communication. Interested vendors are encouraged to respond to this notice with their capability and contact information by 30 April 2026, at 1:00PM MST to be considered for the upcoming solicitation. Cannon AFB is requesting a 24 inch Cold Planer compatible with CAT 299C high-flow skid steer that must meet the below specifications: Depth of cut – 0-6 inches Width of cut – 24 inches Number of teeth – 60 Side Shift – 24 inches (hydraulic) Tilt – 16 degrees Hydraulic requirement – 22-56 GPM/3000-6525 psi Depth and tilt control – Hydraulic Factory Installed High Flow Couplers Wire Harness Cat "C" Series 8 Pin Case Drain Coupler (female)

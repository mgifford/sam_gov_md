# Langley Sports and Fitness Center Massage Therapy (NAF)

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-24 15:01:22.429-04
- Solicitation Number: FA480026Q0031

## Summary

The contractor shall furnish all management, transportation, tools, supplies, equipment, and labor necessary to perform massage therapy services on Joint Base Langley-Eustis (JBLE), Langley Air Force Base (LAFB), Virginia (VA) in a manner that shall ensure continuous and reliable service to JBLE patrons. Contractor responsibilities include but are not limited to providing massage tables and chairs, standard intake forms, service-ready linens, a printer for administrative documentation, cleaning and sanitation supplies to maintain a professional, safe, and hygienic service environment for patrons of Joint Base Langley-Eustis.

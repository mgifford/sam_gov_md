If the concern is the manufacture and the sole distributor, please provide documentation to that effect. Please see the requirements below: Salient Characteristics/Statement of Need or Work VISN10-515 WYOMING HEALTHCARE CENTER ENDOSCOPY REPROCESSING SINK General: This request is to replace the Endoscopy Reprocessing Sink at the Wyoming Clinic, a CBOC to the Battle Creek VAMC, for the Sterile Processing Service (SPS). The current equipment was original to the building and is noncompliant with VHA Directive 1116(2), and the Association for the Advancement of Medical Instrumentation (AAMI) standards set forth by the Joint Commission, AMMI ST91. This has been deemed a Priority 1 replacement (Mission Critical) for the Gastrointestinal (GI) clinic s reprocessing area within SPS during the recent site visit and audit from the VISN 10 Chief Sterile Processing Officer (CSPO). The C&A requirements do not apply, and a Security Accreditation Package is not required. Warranty Requirements: General manufacturer warranty that products are free from defects in materials and workmanship. All defects will be replaced or repaired by the manufacturer at no cost to the buyer. The manufacturer directly offers a three-year warranty on the lifters on the adjustable height sink made by PureSteel Healthcare (requesting specific sink, documentation will be included in FORCE package).

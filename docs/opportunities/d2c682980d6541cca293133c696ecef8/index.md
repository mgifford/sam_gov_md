# ONR Internships, Outreach, and Website Support contract

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-03-17 17:26:29.541-04
- Solicitation Number: N0001426R0001

## Summary

N0001426R0001 DRAFT SECTIONS L, M, PWS’ FOR IDIQ AND FIRST TO The Office of Naval Research (ONR) is posting a DRAFT Section L, M, and Performance Work Statements for the IDIQ and First Task Order, and the DD 1423 for the first Task Order for the ONR Internships, Outreach, and Website Support contract to SAM.gov. This is NOT a formal RFP request and the Government will not reimburse contractors for their time spent on a response. The following information is provided in advance of the formal RFP post: The formal RFP will be posted for 8(a) concerns only. Prior market research indicated Tribal owned only but the strategy has changed to allow for an 8(a) competition. Formal RFP responses will need to use the PIEE Solicitation Module. Offerors shall follow the PIEE registration instructions below to register for an account. Registration Instructions: https://www.acq.osd.mil/asda/dpc/ce/cap/docs/piee/PIEE_Solicitation_Module_Vendor_Access_Instructions.pdf. For assistance with the Solicitation module, contact the PIEE helpdesk at disa.global.servicedesk.mbx.eb-ticket-requests@mail.mil or (866)-618-5988. The following information is provided regarding the draft RFP: This draft is posted for industry comments on Section L and M. Attachments to the RFP are not posted and will be posted with the formal RFP. This single award IDIQ will be awarded on/around 12 Jan 2027. In order to meet the IDIQ minimum, offerors will submit a proposal for the First Task Order as part of their submission. Interested contractors should review the sections and associated PWS’ and submit any questions on the requirement which seek to clarify requirements. Comments or questions on this requirement should be sent to Ms. Vivian Truman at vivian.m.truman2.civ@us.navy.mil no later than 24 Mar 2026 (Tues) by 1500 Eastern.

## Summary

This is a combined synopsis/solicitation for commercial products prepared in accordance with FAR part 12. This announcement constitutes the only solicitation. Quotes are being requested and a separate written solicitation will not be issued. Solicitation number 36C10X26Q0123 is issued as a Request for Quote (RFQ) for Automated Coverslipper Machines. This acquisition is unrestricted. This solicitation incorporates provisions and clauses by reference. The full text of provisions and clauses may be accessed electronically at www.acquisition.gov. Line Items: Refer to Attachment D - Price Cost Schedule for CLINs, quantities, and units of measure. Description of Requirements: Quoters shall provide NX EQ automated coverslipper machines that meet the salient characteristics listed in Attachment B Product Description. Only new OEM equipment is acceptable. No refurbished, remanufactured, or gray market items will be considered. Delivery and Acceptance: Delivery is FOB Destination. Specific delivery locations and dates will be identified in individual orders issued under the contract.

# Maintenance Utility Truck and Maintenance Cargo Truck for DPW, USAG-Japan

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Presolicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 00:01:48.772-04
- Solicitation Number: FA520926Q0024

## Summary

This notice is prepared IAW RFO FAR 5.1 and 12.201-1(c)(2). THIS NOTICE IS NOT A REQUEST FOR QUOTATIONS. The Government intends to solicit this requirement directly from suppliers IAW RFO FAR 12.201-1(c)(2) for firm-fixed price supply contract to purchase for 3000 kg carry capacity Maintenance Utility Truck and 3500 kg carrying capacity Maintenance Cargo Truck in support of DPW, USAG-Japan. The anticipated award date is on or about 30 Apr 2026 (subject to change). Description of the requirement: The contractor shall provide the maintenance truck utility and maintenace cargo truck including transportation and any additional equipment or materials necessary to deliver the required products. Note 1: This procurement will be performed entirety in the country of Japan. The successful offeror must have valid licenses work in the country of Japan

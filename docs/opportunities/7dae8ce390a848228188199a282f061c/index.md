Overview The United States Space Force (USSF) Space Systems Command, Enterprise Sourcing Division (SSC/PKA) has released three (3) Request For Quotes (RFQs) of the six (6) projected Commercial SSSV Constellation Blanket Purchase Agreements (BPAs): Administrative Support, Cybersecurity Support, and Acquisition Support. SSC/PKA projects to release the remaining three (3) Commercial solicitation during calendar year 2026 (CY2026) for: Financial Management Support, Engineering Support, and Security Support. All RFQs will be posted on GSA eBuy only, and a Special Notices For Information Purpose Only regarding the posted solicitations on eBuy to each respective BPA will be posted on SAM.gov to inform industry of potential teaming or subcontract opportunities and encourage Teaming or Subcontract Arrangements with General Services Administration (GSA) Multiple Award Schedule (MAS) Holders. Below are the remaining three (3) projected SSSV program Constellation BPA timeline for the release of RFQs to establish multiple BPAs for each of the constellations outlined in the RFI against the GSA MAS program, also known as the GSA Federal Supply Schedules (FSS). The Government’s objective is to release the next in line projected RFQ, Finance Management Support, in CY2026 Quarter 2 (Q2). Below are the remaining projected Constellations in the likely order of release and with their planned details for planning purpose only.

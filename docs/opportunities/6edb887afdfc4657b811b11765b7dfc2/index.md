## Summary

Enclosed is a Request for Quotations (RFQ) for the annual preventive maintenance of the automatic transfer switch at the U.S. Consulate General Merida. Please refer to the attached files for detailed information regarding the request, delivery, and payment terms. The award will be based on initial quotations without discussions; however, discussions may be held with companies in the competitive range if necessary.

# Remanufacture of TF-33 Low and High-Speed Compressor Stators - MAC IDIQ

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Solicitation
- Posted: 2026-04-02 16:55:39.221-04
- Solicitation Number: FA812126R0004

## Summary

The Air Force Sustainment Center (AFSC) is contemplating to procure the remanufacture of the TF33 Stators and Shrouds cited above for a 5-year Firm-Fixed Price (FFP) Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) Multiple Award Contract (MAC) with 2 Year Basic with 3 One Year Options. Qualification Requirements: Sources must be qualified prior to being considered for award. To be eligible for contract award, the offeror must be an approved source IAW RFO 52.209-1, Qualification Requirements. The Government is not required to delay contract award to review pending Source Approval Requests (SARs). Therefore, offerors are encouraged to submit SARs as soon as possible. If the Government has not completed review of a SAR when the contract is awarded, the SAR will be retained, and the source will be reviewed as a possible source for future awards.

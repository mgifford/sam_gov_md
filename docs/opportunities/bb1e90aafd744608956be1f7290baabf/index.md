# Notice of Intent to Renew Lease - Durant, OK

- Agency: DEPT OF DEFENSE
- Type: Sources Sought
- Posted: 2026-03-12 11:45:56.581-04
- Solicitation Number: DACA56-5-26-1005

## Summary

The U.S. Government currently occupies retail/office and related space in a building under lease that will be expiring. The Government is considering alternate space if economically advantageous. In making this determination, the government will consider, among other things, the availability of alternate space that potentially can satisfy the government’s requirements, as well as costs likely to be incurred through relocating, such as physical move costs, replication of tenant improvements and telecommunication infrastructure, and non-productive agency downtime. The Government’s desire is for a full service lease for approximately 1000 rentable square feet of retail/office space for an Armed Forces Career Center for a term not to exceed five (5) years with Government termination rights and located within a defined delineated area: within the city limits of Durant, OK. Non-exclusive on-site parking for up to three (3) government vehicles must be provided for on a 24 hours 7 days a week basis. Offered space must meet Government requirements per the terms of the Government Lease. Interested respondents may include building owners and representatives with the exclusive right to represent building owners. Representatives of building owners must include the exclusivity agreement, representation letter, listing contract or other executed agreement granting the exclusive right to represent the building owner with their response to this advertisement.

## Summary

This effort is for the delivery of aviation security screening equipment to three (3) international (OCONUS) airports; Nadi international Airport (NAN), Nadi, Fiji; Cassidy International Airport (CXI), Kiritimati, Kiribati; and Faleolo International Airport (APW), Apia, Samoa. The contractor will be required to supply all components, ship, deliver, install, and integrate (if applicable), and test and commission the equipment.

## Summary

Solicitation Description: The Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, tools, and equipment required for reconstruction of parking lot P111 (56,567 SQ. FT) and roadways adjacent (8050 SQ. FT). The project involves a phased reconstruction of an employee parking lot on the eastern half of the campus. The survey, removal, and replacement of the asphaltic course. Removal and re-compaction, grading of base gravel coarse (P111) is necessary prior to replacement of asphaltic layer. The roadways/entrances to the parking lot shall also be reworked in the same manor. A total of 158 parking spaces shall be stripped as existing conditions show. All work completed in accordance with the specifications and contract drawings. Please see all attachments for more details.

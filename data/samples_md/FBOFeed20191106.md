The procurement is for an annual service contract for the repair, service and maintenance of the York Chillers and Controls at Bldg. 2, JWDSRC in Stoneville, MS.  Johnson Controls will provide the service for the (2) York 500 Ton Chillers and the METASYS BAS Controls at the USDA-ARS, Stoneville, MS for a one-year period. The systems that Johnson Controls will maintain are the York Chillers and METASYS BAS Controls. The York Chillers and the METASYS BAS Controls work together as one component and only qualified York/BAS technicians can operate and repair the METASYS BAS Controls. Without Johnson Controls servicing the METASYS BAS Controls, the York Chillers would not operate. Johnson Controls is the only vendor that can provide the updates to the METASYS BAS Controls, no other vendor has rights to the software. Johnson Controls is the only known vendor that has the technicians allowed to service York/BAS units and systems. Johnson Controls of Memphis, TN is the only dealer for this area represented for York.
